Creating a safer, stronger community

The Safer Telford & Wrekin Partnership is the local Community Safety Partnership. Which is a partnership between agencies in Telford and Wrekin to work together to reduce and tackle crime and anti-social behaviour.

There are a number of partners that work together which include Telford and Wrekin Council, Police, Probation, PCT and Shropshire Fire and Rescue Service. There is a full list of partners on the About Us page.

We have developed this website to provide you with information on what we are doing locally, provide you with personal safety and crime prevention advice, where to go to get support and reporting a problem in your neighbourhood.

Telford and Wrekin is a safe place to live and work, often the fear of crime is much greater than the actual crime that occurs.
