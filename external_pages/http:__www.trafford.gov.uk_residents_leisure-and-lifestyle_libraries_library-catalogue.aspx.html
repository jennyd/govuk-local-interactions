Library catalogue

Trafford libraries online catalogue contains details of books, DVDs and Talking Books in stock throughout the borough. The catalogue can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. The catalogue search facilities are open to anyone.

To make full use of the catalogue you will need your borrower number and PIN. Your PIN can be obtained from a member of staff at any Trafford library (you will be asked to show ID). Once you have your PIN you can:

  • Log in to your account using your borrower number and PIN
  • Renew books
  • View your current loans and requests
  • Place reservations on items in stock.
