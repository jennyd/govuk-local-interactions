Car parks in Sandwell

We manage a number of car parks around Sandwell. Some are free to park in, some are pay and display.

Charges apply on pay and display car parks from 8am and 6pm, Monday to Saturday, including bank holidays and other public holidays.

Here is a list of car parks for each of the town shopping areas:

  • Car parks in Bearwood
  • Car parks in Blackheath
  • Car parks in Cradley Heath
  • Car parks in Oldbury
  • Car parks in Old Hill
  • Car parks in Smethwick
  • Car parks in Tipton
  • Car parks in Wednesbury
  • Car parks in West Bromwich

You can buy a monthly or annual parking permit which may save you money if you park regularly in certain pay and display car parks or streets.

You can also download the AppyParking app for iOS and Android for instant information about parking in Sandwell.

Street parking

Pay and display charges apply to parking in some streets in central West Bromwich.

Most bays are limited to two hours parking at a charge of Â£1.20 per hour.

Some streets outside West Bromwich also have pay and display parking. The charges and maximum length of parking vary and may be subject to change.  Always check the signs before parking in an on-street parking bay.

Parking for disabled people

We provide free parking spaces in all of our pay and display car parks for Blue Badge holders. A Blue Badge must be displayed.

Parking for Blue Badge holders in the multi-storey car park in West Bromwich is only available on level one.

Shopmobility

We have a Shopmobility scheme hiring out wheelchairs and motorised scooters from the ground floor of the Queen's Square multi-storey car park in West Bromwich. For details please ring 0121 553 1943.

Do motorbike users pay for parking?

Motorbikes can park in our car parks for free. Charges apply to motorbikes with sidecars attached.

Contact us

If you have any further questions about parking in Sandwell, please call the parking control office on 0121 569 4330. Our opening hours are 9am-4.30pm, Monday to Friday.
