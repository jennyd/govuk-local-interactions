To help you with your childcare needs we have useful information and links here to services relating to childcare. This includes types of childcare; childcare for children with SEND; choosing childcare, early education and working in childcare.

Information to help you with your childcare needs

  • Choosing Childcare

    Choosing childcare and tips on what to ask/look for, the different types of childcare available and information on how to find help for childcare costs e.g. childcare vouchers. Assistance with finding appropriate childcare via our brokerage service.

  • Childcare and Special Educational Needs Provision

    Support in finding childcare for children with Special Educational Needs and Disabilities and who to contact for help with finding childcare to meet your child's needs.

  • Free Early Education and Childcare

    Information on free childcare for 2 year olds (including how to check your eligibility), free early education sessions for 3 and 4 year olds and who to contact for help finding a place.

  • Working in Early Years and Childcare

    Advice on working in the Early Years and Childcare sector including becoming a childminder, setting up a childcare business, job vacancies and training.Â
