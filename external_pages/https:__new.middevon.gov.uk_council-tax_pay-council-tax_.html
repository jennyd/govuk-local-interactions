Pay Council Tax

We aim to make paying your Council Tax as painless as possible by providing a variety of different ways to pay – just choose the one that suits you best.

Direct Debit

Paying by Direct Debit saves you time and hassle. We offer a choice of payment dates (1st, 15th or 25th of the month) and the Direct Debit Guarantee means that you're always in control of your money.

Call us on 01884 255255 to set up a Direct Debit today - please have your account details ready. Alternatively, if you prefer to send us a paper copy please download a Direct Debit mandate, print it out and bring it in or send it to us in the post to Mid Devon District Council, Phoenix House, Phoenix Lane, Tiverton EX16 6PP.

Online

Our online payment service enables you to make payments for Council Tax and Business Rates securely over the internet.

Standing Order

If you prefer to pay through your bank by Standing Order, please contact your bank and provide them with your instalment amounts/dates and the following details in order for payments to be made to Mid Devon District Council, MDDC General Account, Account Number 72621192, Sort Code 60-21-27 and please remember to provide them with your reference number which you can be find on your Council Tax bill.

Telephone

Call our automated payment line 01884 233000 and follow the prompts to pay your Council Tax.

You can also contact Customer Services to make a payment directly by calling 01884 255255.

Cash, cheque, debit or credit card at one of our offices

Phoenix House, Phoenix Lane, Tiverton or Market Street, Crediton. See Contact details and opening hours. There is also a self-service cash or card payment kiosk at Phoenix House. 

By post

Send your payment to Mid Devon District Council, Phoenix House, Phoenix Lane, Tiverton EX16 6PP. Please make your cheque payable to Mid Devon District Council and write your reference number (found on your bill) on the reverse of your cheque.

At any Post Office with cash, cheque or debit card

Use your plastic payment card and make your cheques payable to Post Office Ltd. Please note that these payments can take up to seven days to reach us. For your nearest branch, see Post Office™ - Branch Finder.

Payzone

You can make a payment at a Payzone outlet using your payment card.
