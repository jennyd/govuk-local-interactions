community grant

The Community Grant is provided to help meet the needs of local groups and organisations through the development and provision of community based projects.

Projects seeking revenue funding and costing over £2,000 can apply for up to 25% of the total cost - maximum grant £20,000

Projects seeking capital funding and costing over £2,500 can apply for up to 20% of the total cost - maximum grant £10,000

Currently both revenue and capital funding is open to application and the deadline is 20 October 2011. Please submit your application before this date for consideration.

Please note: All grants are subject to funds being available.
