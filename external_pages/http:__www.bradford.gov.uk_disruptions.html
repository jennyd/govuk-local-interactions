Booking a library PC online

The online booking system for PCs at our libraries will be unavailable from Thursday 10 December 2015 until February 2016.

If you want to use a PC at one of our libraries you can still do so, but you will not be able to book a session online. Please check first to find out which libraries PCs are available.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause you.

Girlington Library

Girlington Library will close temporarily Thursday 5 November at 5pm. Find out how to access Library services while Girlington Library is closed.

Gritting

Gritting updates can be found on our @BradfordAlerts Twitter account. You don't need to register with Twitter to view the updates.

Information about gritting, including how to request gritting for a road or pavement, is available in our gritting FAQs.
