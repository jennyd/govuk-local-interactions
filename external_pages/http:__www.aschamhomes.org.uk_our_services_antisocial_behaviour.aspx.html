Anti-social behaviour (ASB)

The Anti-social Behaviour, Crime and Policing Act 2014 defines anti social behaviour as conduct that has caused, or is likely to cause, harassment, alarm or distress to any person; conduct capable of causing nuisance or annoyance to a person in relation to that person’s occupation of residential premises, or conduct capable of causing housing-related nuisance or annoyance to any person.

This can include hate crime, neighbour disputes, nuisance, criminal damage, graffiti, noise nuisance plus many others.
