'Dropped kerbs' and 'crossovers' enable vehicles to access property.

Apply for a dropped kerb

To construct a dropped kerb to access your property you must:

  • apply for 'dropped kerb vehicle crossing' to construct a new dropped kerb
  • apply for 'vehicle access crossing improvement' to improve an existing dropped kerb

Return your application to the Streetworks Engineer, along with a plan showing the preferred location of the proposed crossing.

Do not enclose payment with your application - when permission is granted we'll send you an invoice for £69. Please note that this fee is non refundable.

  • it takes 2 weeks to process an application
  • permission last for 12 months
  • you must use a contractor approved by us to carry out the works
