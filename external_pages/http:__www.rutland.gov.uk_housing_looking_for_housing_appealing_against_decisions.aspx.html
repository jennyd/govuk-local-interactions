Looking for Housing - Appealing against decisions

 

If you have applied to be included on Rutland County Council's housing register, you can ask us to review your case if you think we have made a mistake about:

  • your eligibility to join the register
  • the number of points you have been awarded
  • removing your name from the register

If you think the decision we have made is wrong you must write to us as soon as possible. Your case will be reviewed by a Senior Officer at Rutland County Council who was not involved in the original decision. You will then be notified in writing of the outcome of the review.
