Apply For a Home

 
Download as PDF
RSS Feed
Last Modified July 24, 2015
Share this page

 

Here at BCH we operate a choice based lettings system as part of the MyHomeChoiceFyldeCoast (MHCFC) scheme.  This means that you can 'bid' for properties that are advertised on the MHCFC website, as long as you meet the criteria for that property. When you 'bid' you are registering your interest in a particular property - and can have up to 5 bids each week.
