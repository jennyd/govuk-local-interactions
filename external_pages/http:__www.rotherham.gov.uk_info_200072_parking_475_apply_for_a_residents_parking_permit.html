Apply for a parking permit

  1. Apply for a parking permit (You are here)
  2. Which areas need a parking permit

Unless you are a blue badge holder, you need a parking permit to park in certain residential areas.

Permits for one vehicle cost:

  • £12.50 for 6 months
  • £17.50 for 12 months

Extra permits cost:

  • £25 for 6 months
  • £35 for 12 months

You can apply for up to three permits per household for the Wellgate area. Permits for the Hospital zone, Boston Castle and Eastwood South areas are limited to two per household.

You can also apply for up to 50 visitor day permits per year for any area. These cost £1.50 each. Extra day permits may be available in special cases.

You must provide proof of residence showing your name and address. This can be a copy of:

  • Your current Council Tax bill
  • Your current tenancy agreement
  • A recent utility bill

We will deliver your permits within five working days of receiving your application. If you prefer to collect your permit, please give a contact number when you apply.
