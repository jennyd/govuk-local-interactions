Homelessness

Homelessness can happen to anyone, that's why Fortis Living's Housing Options Team are there to support and advise you if you get into difficulties. You should contact us if you think your home is at risk as our aim is to prevent you from becoming homeless in the first place. Call 0330 123 0700 for more information.
