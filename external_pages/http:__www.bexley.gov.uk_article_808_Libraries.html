Libraries

Find out what the borough's libraries have to offer.

Visit Bexley Libraries to:

  • Check library opening hours and contact information
  • Renew library books and other items
  • Reserve books, DVDs and talking books
  • Borrow eBooks, eAudiobooks and eMagazines
  • See the computer facilities available at all libraries and book a computer online
  • Explore the range of top-quality, hand-picked online resources and databases
  • Find out what events and activities are going on for everyone
  • Plus much more

Joining the library is free and you can join online or simply by asking at any library in Bexley.

Remember, you don't need to be a library member to browse the Bexley Libraries website, but if you do have a valid library card you will be able to access your account, renew and request books, rate and review what you've borrowed, and make full use of the online resources 24/7.

Borrow eMagazines for free: Learn more on eMagazines.
