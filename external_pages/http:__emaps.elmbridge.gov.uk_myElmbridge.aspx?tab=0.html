My Neighbourhood

Search for a location 

Welcome to My Neighbourhood!

If you're new user, here's a few tips to get you started:

  1. Set the location you want by hitting the 'Change' Button and typing in an address or postcode. Local information - it's what My House is all about.

  2. Click on the Other things that may interest you button, you can add and remove items to any page on which it appears.

  3. Use My Nearest to find your closest services and facilities.

  4. See what's local with My Maps, decide what items you want to see and flag those nearest to you.

Okay, you're ready to go. Start by picking a location!
