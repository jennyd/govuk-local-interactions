Primary school admissions

The following information is for children starting Reception in 2016.

When can my child start school?

You can calculate the start year based on your child’s date of birth below:

Start years
Start or transfer Date of birth
Start Reception September 2018 1/9/2013 - 31/8/2014
Start Reception September 2017 1/9/2012 - 31/8/2013
Start Reception September 2016 1/9/2011 - 31/8/2012

Applications for September 2016 start

The closing date for applying for a Reception class place or year three junior school place was 15 January 2016.

Still need to apply?

If you live within the borough and still need to apply for a place (because you missed the deadline or moved into the area) you can still apply online using the eAdmissions website.

Please read our Personal Information Policy and validity of information given before applying online.

You need to apply online by Friday 12 February 2016. After 12 February you'll need to contact us for an application form.

If you do not live in the borough, you need to apply through your own council and you can include Richmond schools on your application form.

When making your application in eAdmissions you should:

  • Complete a separate application for each if you are applying for twins or triplets
  • Tell us in your application if your child will have a brother or sister (sibling) attending yourpreferred school(s) in September 2016

Certain schools need additional information to apply their admissions criteria and may require you to also complete a Supplementary Information Form. For example, schools that prioritise applications by faith. If you are applying for this type of school, please check the school's website for further information and to download a Supplementary Information Form.

If your child has a Statement of Special Education Needs (SEN) or an Education, Healthand Care Plan (EHCP), you don't need to complete the application.

Be fully informed before you apply

Read our admissions brochure before you apply. If you have any difficulty completing your application, please contact the School Admissions Team.

Applying for an in-year place

If you're not applying for a Reception or year three place in September 2016 you should read our in-year admissions information.

Expansion of East Sheen Primary School, Hampton Infant School and Hampton Junior School for September 2016

On 19 November, Richmond Council's Cabinet approved the expansions of East Sheen Primary from two to three-form entry, from September 2016 onwards; and of Hampton Infant and Hampton Junior from three to four form entry, from September 2016 and September 2019 onwards respectively.

East Sheen Primary and Hampton Infant will therefore admit 90 and 120 children respectively in September 2016, not 60 and 90 as stated in the Primary Schools Admissions Brochure(pdf, 886KB).

Application outcome

All Richmond residents, except those that applied online and were offered their first preference, will receive a letter notifying them of the outcome of their application regardless of application method.

Online

  • If you have applied online you will be sent an email with the outcome of your application during the evening of Monday 18 April 2016.
  • Please wait until you have received the email before logging on to the eAdmissions website.

The availability of results for viewing will be staggered to assist demand, so precise timings cannot be guaranteed.

Paper

  • If you submitted a paper application, a letter containing the outcome will be sent to parents/carers by first class post on Monday 18 April 2016.

Accepting an offer

You must accept or decline the place offered by Tuesday 3 May 2016.
