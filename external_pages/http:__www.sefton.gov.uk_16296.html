What is Be Active?

Every school holiday, our amazing Be Active coaches deliver a great range of activities to children aged 3-16 across Sefton. A great way for children to keep active, have fun and make friends in a safe environment, our activities include one to four day camps, shorter sessions and and reduced cost swim and splash sessions.
