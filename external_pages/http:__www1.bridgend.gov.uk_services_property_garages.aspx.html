Garages/Parking spaces to let

Bridgend County Borough Council owns garages and car parking spaces throughout the County Borough which are available to rent for the use of storing a vehicle. Garages cannot be rented to store furniture, garden equipment or to use as a workshop as they are susceptible to damage from condensation.

Do you have to be a council tenant to apply?

You do not have to be a council tenant to apply for a garage site/base.

How do I apply for a garage?

To apply to rent a garage/car parking space please put your request into writing and send to:

Bridgend County Borough Council, Property Services, Ravens Court, Brewery Lane, Bridgend, CF31 4AP

Tel: 01656 642700
Fax: 01656 642432
E-mail:property@bridgend.gov.uk

How do I terminate my garage tenancy?

To terminate the tenancy of a garage or a garage base a period of one month’s written notice must be given stating the date you would like the garage site/base to be terminated.

Tenancies of garages and garage bases cannot be transferred to third parties at the end of your tenancy.

Any rental arrears must be cleared before the termination can be accepted. If you fall behind with the rent at any time, the council may issue a “Notice to Quit” and you will be asked to clear the outstanding debt. The garage/base must be left in an acceptable condition.
