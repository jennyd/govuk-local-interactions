Day care

If you're disabled, getting older or need a little extra support, day centres can help you live as independently as possible.

How day centres support you

  • you'll feel closer to community life
  • develop new skills to keep your independence
  • if you have complex needs, specialist services are available  
  • some have transport and meals available
  • you can make new friends in a safe environment.

Choosing your day centre

You can search for day centres in Medway here.

Once you've found a centre or activity you like, you can simply make your own arrangements - like when you want to attend and what facilities you'll pay for.

Some day centres provide a more specialised care, and must be assessed by Medway Council to determine the type of support is best for you.

Phone 01634 334466

Email ss.accessandinfo@medway.gov.uk

How you pay for a day centre

If you're assessed for financial support by Medway Council, then some or all of your costs may be covered in a personal budget.
