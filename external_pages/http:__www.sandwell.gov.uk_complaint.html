Complaints procedure

  • We have a procedure for comments, complaints and compliments.
  • The children's social care complaints procedure is based on a principle of a 3 stage process.
  • The adults social care complaints procedure is based on a principle of a 1 stage process. 
  • The public health complaints procedure is based on a principle of a 1 stage process.

What if I am not happy with the way my complaint is dealt with?

  • You can request consideration for a stage 2 independent investigation after a stage 1 response has been provided (unless it is a complaint for adult social care or public health in this case you need to approach the Local Government Ombudsman).
  • You may approach the Local Government Ombudsman at any stage of your complaint. The office is unbiased and independent and investigations are carried out in private.

More information about the Local Government Ombudsman

If your are not happy with how your complaint about health and safety has been dealt with you can contact the Independent Regulatory Challenge Panel.
