Buying your council home

The Right to Buy scheme allows secure tenants to purchase their home at a discount. 

The amount of discount depends on how long you have been a tenant and if the property you want to purchase under the Right to Buy scheme is a house or a flat. 

Tenants thinking of exercising their right to buy can find more useful information about the discounts, details on how to apply and the application form on www.gov.uk or click on the links below:

·         Summary of Right to Buy

·         Right to Buy Application Form

If tenants are wondering whether they would be able to afford the Right to Buy option on their Council House, can use the government website to assist.

If you have any queries, please contact the Housing Management Team on 01530 454793, 660 or 786.
