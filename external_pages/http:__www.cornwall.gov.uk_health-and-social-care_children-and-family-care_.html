Children and family care

Last updated: 09/09/2015

The Cornwall Care and Support Directory is the main signposting and information service for the whole of Cornwall, for parents and carers of children and young people aged 0 to 20 or 25 if they have additional needs.

The directory has information on things including leisure activities, activities for under 5s, childcare, health and wellbeing, money matters, staying safe and more.

Child Protection and Safeguarding

If you have any concerns regarding a child / young person, please complete the on-line referral form to notifiy us of your concerns.  Alternatively you can telephone the Multi Agency Referral Unit (MARU) on: 0300 123 1116

Cornwall and Isles of Scilly Joint Agency Protocol on Children and Young People who run away or go missing from home or care
