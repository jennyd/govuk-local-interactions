Assistance with travel to home, school and college

The School Travel Service assesses eligibility for assistance under the Council’s Policy Covering Travel Between Home, School And College.

For those children and young people who qualify a suitable travel arrangement is made on public transport. Children who have special needs may be provided with more specialist arrangements.

A new policy for Home to School Travel and Transport came into effect on 1 September 2015.

The new policy, which includes a review and appeals process, is attached below.

For more information please visit Gov.uk - Subsidised College Transport

Or call the School Travel Team on 01274 385581
