Refuse and Recycling

Refuse and Recycling Service This is an overview of the service. We collect refuse and recycling waste fortnightly from all homes across the District. Find out more
Refuse, Recycling and Green Waste Collection Days You can check your Refuse, Recycling and Green Waste collection days online. All you need is your Post Code. Find out more
Green Waste We offer a paid for Green Waste Service running from April to March for 50 weeks of the year. You can subscribe at any point through the year. Find out more
Missed Collection If your bin or sack has been missed by our collection crew after being correctly presented it must be reported to us within 3 working days so that we can return to collect the waste. Find out more
Bulky Waste & Electrical Items We offer a chargeable kerbside collection service for bulky waste and electrical items. A charge of £15 is made to collect up to four items which must be paid for at the time you make the order. Find out more
Assisted Collection We provide Assisted Collections for householders who are unable to present their waste at the kerbside due to either disability or infirmity. Find out more
Recycling Sites/Bottle Banks In addition to our kerbside collection service, we also provide recycling facilities, for residents to recycle household waste such as textiles, paper and shoes. Each recycling site includes a Bottle Bank for glass. Find out more
Additional/Larger Wheeled Bins Larger bins are available if you produce healthcare waste, have two or more children in nappies under the age of four, or have five or more people in your family.

 Find out more
Charities and Schools We do not provide a waste collection service to charities or schools. This has to be carried out by a private waste collection company. Find out more
