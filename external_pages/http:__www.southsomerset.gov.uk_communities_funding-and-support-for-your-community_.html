Funding and support for your community

SSDC supports voluntary, community and charitable organisations in South Somerset which aim to make South Somerset a better place to live and work. Making a difference is not always about the money - these pages include other ways SSDC can help, contacts for other organisations here to help you and your community, and ways you can help yourself.

Funding and support for your community links

SSDC supports voluntary, community and charitable organisations in South Somerset.

SSDC Community Grants

We believe in supporting innovative communities who seek help to help themselves. Our successful community grants programme can support voluntary and charitable organisations, not-for-profit groups, parish or town councils and other organisations who benefit the wider community.

SSDC Community Loans

In some circumstances, we may be able to offer a loan to a community group to assist with a project.
