Street and Highway Defects form

You can use this form to report a problem with a road (pothole, flooding etc.) or a pavement, faulty street light, signs, bollards and other street furniture.  You can also use it to report cleanliness issues such as: graffiti, litter and litter bins, fly tipping, abandoned vehicles and dead animals
