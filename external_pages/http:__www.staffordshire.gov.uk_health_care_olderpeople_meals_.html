Community meals services

Meals on Wheels

What's on offer?

A hot meal served at lunchtime to your home, ready to eat.  Delivery is between 11am and 2pm.

Hot meals can be provided every day of the week if needed. The menu has a range of different meals for you to choose from.

View the menu through ICare's website.

How to request this service

The easiest way is to order your meals online through ICare's website.

There are 5 steps which you will need to follow when ordering.

Step 1: Enter your postcode.

Step 2: Choose between hot food and frozen food. For frozen foods a minimum order of 5 meals will need to be ordered.

Step 3: Choose dates you would like your meals to be delivered. Then select your main course and dessert.

Step 4: You will need to register before you can order your meals. It is a quick process which will need details including your name, address, email and a next of kin which will be needed if a driver has any problems delivering your meal.

Step 5: Payment, you will need to have a valid card to take payment.

Once your meal has been ordered you will receive an email to confirm your order.

If you have any problems registering using the online service or you wish to speak to someone about the meals you can contact I-Care on 01543 275015.

Paying for the meals

The current charges are:

  • Hot food - meal and pudding, or meal for only £5.30.
  • Frozen foods £3.75 per dinner and dessert. 5 meals minimum order for frozen foods.

There is an additional service for tea time which includes a sandwich, cake and juice for £3.25.

Meals can be paid either to the driver, setting up a direct debit or by invoice. We are in partnership with I-Care for the supply and delivery of meals.

To find out more please call 0300 111 8009 or email us at staffordshirecares@staffordshire.gov.uk. 

Further information 

ICare offer an additional service called the peace of mind meal service. This service can offer assistance with your meal, help with your daily chores and more support around the house. This includes help with menu planning, cutting up meal, taking out the rubbish and many more.

Further information about the service can be found through ICare's website.
