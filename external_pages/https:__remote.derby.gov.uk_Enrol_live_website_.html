Welcome to Derby City Council's School Admissions Application and Free 2 Year Old Childcare Check Site

Intake applications for starting at an Infant, Junior, Primary, Secondary School or a University Technical College (UTC)

  • Please use this site to apply for a school place for your child using the 'Create Account' option
  • If you have previously registered for an account please 'Login'
  • Applications for starting at a Secondary School or a UTC in September 2016 will be available from 9 September 2015. The closing date for applications is 31 October 2015
  • Applications for starting at an Infant, Junior or Primary school in September 2016 will be available from 6 November 2015
  • The Closing date for applications for applying for a Infant, Junior or Primary school is 15 January 2016.

Please note: Once the closing date and time has passed, you cannot make any changes. Please ensure that you have made applications for all the schools you wish to apply for.

Free Childcare for Two Year Olds

  • Please use this site to apply for free childcare for your two year old using the 'Create Account' option
  • Once you have completed the application we will be in touch by letter within 6 working days to let you know the outcome
  • You can apply for a place the term before your child is two, and start the term after your child turns two. It will finish at the end of the term that your child turns three. If you apply too early we will not be able to process your application until the term your child turns two.
