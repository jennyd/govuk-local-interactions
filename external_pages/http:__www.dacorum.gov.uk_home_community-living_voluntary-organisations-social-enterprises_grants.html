Grants

The Community Grant is designed to support community activities and wellbeing. It is aimed at community groups and voluntary organisations in Dacorum. If you are part of a community or voluntary group seeking funding, you can apply for a community grant by clicking on the apply button below.

There are three rounds of funding, which close at noon on the last Friday of May, September and January. The closing date for the remaining round in the current financial year (1 April 2015 - 31 March 2016) is as follows:

  • Friday 29 January 2016

The closing dates for funding rounds in the next financial year (1 April 2016 - 31 March 2017) are as follows:

  • Friday 27 May 2016
  • Friday 30 September 2016
  • Friday 27 January 2017

Support for applicants

Community Action Dacorum offers two types of support for local voluntary sector and community groups.

- support with completing the application questions
- support with completing the online form

Please use the contact details below for Community Action Dacorum to access this support.

If you have technical difficulties or specific queries about the Community Grant application, please contact us.
