Litter

We all have a duty to dispose of our rubbish in a responsible way. This includes the Council and residents of the district. Our street services work to help make the district a cleaner, safer and more environmentally friendly place to live and work.

If you wish to report an incident of street litter, please contact the Uconnect Customer Service Team:

  • Contact the Uconnect Customer Service Team: 01799 510510

To report a litter bin which needs emptying, please go to the Report street care and cleaning issues issues webpage.
