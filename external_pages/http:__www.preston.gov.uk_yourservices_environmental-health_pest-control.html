Pest control

We can help with the following three pest control issues:

  • rat problems
  • mice problems
  • cockroach problems

Please note that our service is free.

Reporting a pest control issue

To report a pest control issue, you will need to book an appointment through our report a pest control issue form.

Appointments can be made for a pest control officer to arrive between the following hours Monday to Friday:

Morning appointments

8.30am – 12.30pm 

Afternoon appointments

12.30pm – 4.00pm

Pests that we don't treat

If you have an issue with a different type of pest, please see the British Pest Control Association.

The British Pest Control Association has lots of information around the different sorts of pest issues you may experience. They also include a facility that lets you search for pest control specialists in your area.

Commercial pest control issues

We only treat pests on commercial or industrial premises for businesses and organisations that have a contract with us. All other businesses will need to contact a commercial pest control service. To help you find a commercial pest control service please visit the British Pest Control Association - find a pest controller.
