Voluntary and community groups

Services provided by voluntary and community groups. These may include registered charities, not for profit organisations, credit unions and social enterprises. Providers range in size from large national organisations to smaller community based services and projects.

You can find:

  • Providers of support groups and forums for children and young people
  • Services for leisure and recreational activities
  • National organisations who provide targeted support for parents and carers of children and young people with Special Educational Needs
  • Youth groups, playgroups and social groups
  • Organisations based locally who can help and support people to play an active role within their communities
  • Coventry based community activities
