Service Disruptions

Service Disruptions

Temporary changes to library and Local Link opening times on Tuesday 19 January

On Tuesday 19 January 2016 the opening times for some of our libraries and Local Link offices will be different from normal.

Ashby Library and Local Link, Brigg Library, Church Square House Local Link and North Lincolnshire Central Library will all be open from 12.30pm until their normal scheduled closing times. Riddings Library will be open from 2pm.

Haxey Library and Messingham Library will be closed all day.

Mobile library routes will run as normal.

You will not be liable for library fines if you are not able to return your books on this date due to the temporary closure. The library computer system will automatically renew books due on this date for a further three weeks.

We apologise for any inconvenience these changes in opening times may cause. Normal services will resume on Wednesday 20 January.
