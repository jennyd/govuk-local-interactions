There are a number of different ways for you to have your say and help us to design and deliver our services so that they meet your needs.

  • Petitions Scheme: Sets out how you can submit a petition, how it will be dealt with by the Council and what the Council can do to respond to the issues raised.

  • Contact your local councillor - find out who your local councillor is and how you can contact them

  • Vote - have your say in the ultimate way by using your vote.

  • Consultation- find out about how we consult, why we consult, how you can get involved. You can also find information about Current consultations and Recent consultations - results and outcomes

  • Join the Citizens' Panel  find out how you can help us to plan our services so we are providing the services that you want.

  • Tenant participation - find out how you as a tenant can become involved in the services we provide for you.

  • Comments, compliments and complaints - tell us what you think of the services we provide. Tell us what we do well, what we could do better and what we can do differently.

  • Give us your views and suggestions about crime and community safety

  • View and comment on recent planning applications
