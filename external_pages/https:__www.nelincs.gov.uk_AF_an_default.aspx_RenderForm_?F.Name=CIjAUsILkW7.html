Request for a dropped kerb

 

Please use this form to apply for permission for a dropped kerb (vehicular access, car crossing, vehicular crossing)

If you wish to have a vehicular access from an A or B road then you will need formal planning permission.

  • A roads are generally main roads which normally carry a 60-70mph speed limit.
  • B roads are generally less busy and connect to A roads and towns which normally carry a 30-40mph speed limit.

If planning permission is not required you will be issued with a quote within 4-6 weeks.
