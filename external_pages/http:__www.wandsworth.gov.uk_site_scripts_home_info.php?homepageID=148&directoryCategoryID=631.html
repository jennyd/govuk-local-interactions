Day services

Day services

Day services offer a place you can visit to meet other people during the day, as well as activities and events. There are many services that offer leisure, learning and health-based activities as well as day centres, which may offer additional support and care. Some services are for people with a particular disability, or just for older people, but most are open to everyone. If you're looking for something to do in the day you can find lunch clubs, social groups, drop-in services, and much more.
