Pay Your Bill

 

The easiest, quickest and safest way to pay your Council Tax is by direct debit. You just need to give us your details once and that's it.

If you would like to set up a direct debit now, please complete the form below. 

  1. Start the Direct Debit Form

Payments 2

If you don't want to set up a direct debit now, you can make your payment online. 

  1. Make a Payment

Payments 3

If, for any reason, you can't use either of these payment methods please email revenues.benefits@arun.gov.uk or call us on 01903 737752 to discuss your options.
