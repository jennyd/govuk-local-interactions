Libraries features

  • National Libraries Day

    Celebrating National Libraries Day - 6 February

    From 1-6 February, there will be a range of exciting free activities including Harry Potter events, local history talks and Ancestry online sessions. Plus there will be a local history quiz with prizes and a child membership promotion throughout February with prizes. For more information, contact your local library.

  • Renew a library book

    Renew a library book

    There are a number of ways you can renew a library book:

    • renew a book online
    • renew a book in person at your local library
    • renew a book by telephone on
      0844 414 22 70
