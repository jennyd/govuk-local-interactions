Council Tax: Payment Methods

Council Tax: How To Pay

How you can pay your Council Tax

Direct Debit; set up and pay automatically every month

You can choose to pay on the 1st, 11th, 20th or 28th of each month. 

If you do not currently pay by Direct Debit and wish to start doing so, you can use our online form HERE (or by using the link to the right).

If you wish to change any of the following :-

  • The date of an existing Direct Debit,
  • The name on the account your Direct Debit is set on, or
  • To change to use a different Bank/Building Society account,

Please Download a Direct Debit form here

The signed and completed form may be posted to us, or scanned and e-mailed to revenueservices@northampton.gov.uk.

Alternatively, you can arrange this by phone by ringing our Customer Services Team on 0300 330 7000.

Please have your bank details with you when you phone.

Pay Online; instant, secure and easy payment option

Payment may only be made using Visa, MasterCard, Delta, Maestro, Switch or Solo credit/debit cards.

Please note that a 1.34% commission charge is added where payment is made by credit card.

Our website provides a secure payment facility to pay the Council by credit or debit card.

Visit our Online Payments page to make payment.

BACS/Bank Transfer; transfer payments direct from your account to the Council

Our bank details are:-

HSBC Bank
Sort code 40-23-44
Account 40004197

(Please ensure you quote your Council Tax reference number with any payment).

24 Hour Telephone Payment Line

Please telephone 0845 372 1745 and have your account details ready.

Payment may only be made using Visa, MasterCard, Delta, Maestro, Switch or Solo credit/debit cards.

Please note that a 1.34% commission charge is added where payment is made by credit card.

Payzone and Post Office

Payments can be made at most outlets where you see the Payzone sign and at all Post Offices.

You can take your bill into the Payzone retailer or Post Office and your payment will be receipted using the unique barcode printed on it.

Find your nearest outlet

This service is free of charge.
By Standing Order

Payments should be made via the Council's bank, which is:

HSBC Bank
Sort code 40-23-44
Account 40004197

Please ensure you quote your Council Tax reference number with any payment)
By Post

Send to:  

Northampton Borough Council, The Guildhall, St Giles Square, Northampton, NN1 1DE.

All cheques should be made payable to "Northampton Borough Council".

Please write your council tax reference number clearly on the reverse of any cheques (please do not send cash by unregistered mail).

Please note that no receipt will be issued unless you request one.

Council Tax is normally paid over ten monthly instalments, April to January. However, from 2013/2014 if you wish to request monthly instalments until March, please contact us within seven days of receipt of your new bill.

Please note that in some circumstances where your account is in arrears we may not be able to offer this facility immediately.
