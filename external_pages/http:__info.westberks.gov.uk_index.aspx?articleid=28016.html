Membership of West Berkshire Library Service is free for everyone.

There is no minimum age for joining, but if you are under 16 your parent or guardian will need to visit a library to fill out a membership form for you. They will need to bring some proof of their address with them, such as a council tax form or driving licence. If you are 16 or over, you can apply online for membership now.

To use our full range of library services:

If you are not already a member of West Berkshire Libraries and you are 16 or over, you can join online by clicking on the acceptance button at the bottom of this page. Joining now will allow you to place up to three reservations online. You will be given an online borrower ID for this. You will need to take this, along with proof of your address, to your local library to collect your library membership card. You will need your library card to borrow items.

To use our eLibrary only and download eBooks and audiobooks online – www.westberks.gov.uk/elibrary

Current members of West Berkshire Libraries can start downloading now! You just need your library membership card number and PIN. If you are not a member and you just want to join the eLibrary, you can join online now. However, your membership will not be activated until you receive your West Berkshire library card.

Once you have accepted our conditions of membership and completed our registration form, your online borrower ID will be displayed on screen.

If you live within West Berkshire, please email this ID to librarysupport@westberks.gov.uk. We will then post your library card to you. As soon as you receive this you will be able to download eBooks and audiobooks from our eLibrary using your new library card number and PIN.

Please note that if you live outside West Berkshire you can still join our eLibrary. However, you will need to take details of your online borrower ID, along with proof of your address, to any West Berkshire library to collect your library membership card.

eLibrary titles automatically expire at the end of each loan period and so no fines are charged.

Conditions of Membership

  • To borrow items you must present your library membership card.
  • Items can be borrowed from and returned to any West Berkshire Library.
  • You can usually extend your current loans. Items may be renewed at any branch, by telephone or online. (You will need your library card number and PIN to do this.)
  • Renewals are not permitted if a reservation has been placed on an item you have on loan.
  • Overdue fines are charged for the late return of items.
  • Overdue notices are a courtesy reminder for your convenience.
  • All costs incurred by West Berkshire Libraries due to items lost or damaged whilst in your care must be reimbursed.
  • Please report lost cards to the library immediately. There will be a charge for replacement membership cards. You will be held responsible for any items borrowed before the loss is reported.
  • All reservation, inter-library loan and lost membership card charges are non-refundable.
  • If you change your address, name, phone number or email address, please notify us immediately.
