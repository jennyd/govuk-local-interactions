Deaths, Funerals and Cremations

Carlisle City Council provides a burials and cremations service. There are three cemeteries and a crematorium in the city. Please use the information on this page to find the service you require.
As well as a list of useful questions and answers to help you with deciding your next step we also providing online forms to make the whole process as easy as possible to use.
