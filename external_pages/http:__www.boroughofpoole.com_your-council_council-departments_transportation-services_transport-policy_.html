Transport Policy

 

Transport Policies for Poole are summarised in the Local Transport Plan (which covers Bournemouth and Christchurch too).  Reference copies are available in all Poole libraries.

Other related documents can be found in our Strategies For Poole pages.

There are a number of car parks in the area which are owned by private companies. The Council ensures that they are operated in accordance with parking policies on:

  • strategic matters
  • pricing
  • disabled parking
  • safety and maintenance

There are also a number of car parks maintained by the Council.
