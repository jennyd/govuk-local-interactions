About the Care Act

Care and support in England is changing. The Care Act 2014 will help make the care and support system more consistent across the country. On 1 April 2015 the changes to information, advice, carer support and support for people with care needs came into force.

The Care Act - Providers

The Care Act has major implications for adult care and support providers, people who use services, carers and advocates.
