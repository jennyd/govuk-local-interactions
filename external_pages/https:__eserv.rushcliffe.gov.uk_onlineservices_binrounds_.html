Refuse Collection Details

Find out on what weekday your refuse is collected, and when you need to put out your grey, green and blue bins. Or print out a refuse collection calendar for your area.

What you need to do

To find out when your bins will next be collected, simply enter your postcode in the box below then select 'Next'. Once you have completed a postcode search, you will be asked to select your address from a list of addresses for your postcode.

What we will do

We will tell you:

  • On what weekday your refuse is collected.
  • The next collection day for both the grey bin, and green and blue bins.

You will also be able to view an online refuse collection calender for your area, which you can download and print out. This calendar shows refuse collection dates for your area, and which colour bin you should put out each week.
