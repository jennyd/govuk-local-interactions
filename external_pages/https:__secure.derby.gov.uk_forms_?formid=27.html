Join Derby City Libraries

Do you live, work or study in Derby? If you do, you can join Derby City Libraries by completing the form below.

When library staff receive your form they will:
  • make you a member
  • e-mail you to let you know that your library card and PIN is ready to collect
  • keep your card and PIN at the library for three months
  • delete your membership after this time if you have not collected your card and PIN

Please note that:
  • when you come to collect your card and PIN you must sign your membership form and bring proof of your live, work, study in Derby status
  • without this proof we will be unable to give you your ticket and PIN
  • if you are eligible for any concessions please also bring proof of this
  • if you already have a library card for Derby City Libraries or Derbyshire County Libraries you do not need to join on-line

    • Once you have your ticket and PIN you will be able to:
    • borrow books, audiobooks and magazines from the library
    • use the computers in the library
    • access our quality on line databases
    • download e-books and e-magazines

    The fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required fields. All the information you enter will be submitted securely.

    * First name(s)
    * Last name
    * Address
    Postcode
    * Telephone number
    Mobile number
    * Email address
    * Confirm Email address

    Email addresses do not match.
    * Choose your home library -
    this is where you will collect your library card from. Remember to bring proof
