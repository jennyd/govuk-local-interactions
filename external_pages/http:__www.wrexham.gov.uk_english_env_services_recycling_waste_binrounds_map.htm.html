When is my Bin Collection Day?

Key: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday. Each week starts on a Sunday and ends on a Saturday. For further information please call Pride in your Streets 01978 298989.
To check which bin you need to put out, click on the dot on your house or property. If you cannot see a dot, click on the "+" icon to zoom in.

Did you know that you can now subscribe to a Bin Collection Day reminder via E-mail?

Wrexham operates an alternate weekly collection of garden waste (in green bins) and residual waste (in black, blue or maroon bins).

  • Refuse and recycling collection arrangements for Bank Holiday
  • Recycle with Michael Calendars

The calendars show which week is residual waste week (shown on the calendar in pink) and which week is garden waste collection week (shown in green).

Contact Information

If you require any further help please call Pride in your Streets 01978 298989, or email: contact-us@wrexham.gov.uk
