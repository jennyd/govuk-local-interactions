Bulky waste

Book online

Complete our online form and a member of our team will contact you to take your card details and process your application. Please ensure you provide a valid telephone number for us to call you back on.

Charges

  • White goods: Free of charge 
  • Up to three items: £20 (inc VAT)
  • Extra items: £6.67 each (inc VAT)
  • We can take up to five items per collection
  • If you receive housing benefit or council tax support you may be entitled to one free collection of three chargeable items per year.
  • If your collection is for chargeable goods you will need to pay by credit or debit card before we book your collection.
  • Cash payments are accepted at Contact Warrington.

The exact cost of your collection will be confirmed to you before a collection date is arranged.

Businesses

Please note we can only arrange bulky collections for household waste. To arrange a bulky collection of business waste contact Bulky Bobs direct.

Cancellations

If you want to cancel your collection and receive a refund you must call Contact Warrington before 12pm on the day before your collection is due. We no longer accept cancellations by email.

Cancellations received after 12pm will not be entitled to a refund as these will have already been passed to our contractor to arrange collection.

Where do I leave my items?

Please leave your items outside your property by 7am on the arranged day.

Make sure they are:

  • dry and covered – particularly furniture
  • accessible when we arrive to collect them
  • clearly identifiable
  • only those items booked for collection – any other items will be left.
  • on your driveway or your garden NOT behind locked gates or sheds.

In exceptional circumstances our contractor may take items from within the house. An adult must be present to sign a disclaimer for the contractor entering the property, in case of accidental damage during the removal process.

Please note:  

  • No refund will be given if the collection cannot be completed
  • Only items requested will be removed
  • Heavily soiled items will not be collected
