Things to do

  • Make a day of it

    With attractions from Tower Bridge to Museum of London, there's plenty of family fun to be had in the City

  • Hampstead Heath

    One of London’s most popular open spaces, situated just six kilometres from Trafalgar Square

  • City Information Centre

    Get advice on London and beyond from our friendly multi-lingual staff

  • City Gardens

    Relax in the Square Mile's beautiful gardens

  • Fantastic architecture

    Visit Roman remains and marvel at some of the world's most ambitious modern skyscrapers.

  • Guildhall Art Gallery

    See Pre-Raphaelite masterpieces and paintings of London for free as well as special exhibitions
