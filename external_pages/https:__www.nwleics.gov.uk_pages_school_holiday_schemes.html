Holiday Activities

Top tasks:

  • Book Online: Leisure

Our leisure centres always have a great range of exciting activities for your children over the school holidays.

There's plenty to get involved with for all ages - from activity camps to trips and arts and crafts to pool inflatables.

If you're a member of the Club Activ8 scheme you can redeem your loyalty points against identified activities.

Follow us on Twitter and Facebook to keep up to date with the holiday activity programmes and be sure not to miss out on any fantastic activities.

Find out what is happening this February, take a look in the February Half Term Leaflet 2016 (PDF Document, 4.13 Mb)

Crash Course Swimming Lessons

This February half term, Hood Park Leisure Centre is offering a 1 week long swimming crash course. This course is designed for children aged 4 plus who are complete beginners or confident beginners and will be held from 9-9.30am. The crash course costs just £27 and you must  book your place. For more information or to book a space, please contact Hood Park Leisure Centre on 01530 412181 or email nwlswimacademy@nwleicestershire.gov.uk
