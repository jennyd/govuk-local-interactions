Customer Services

Our Customer Service Centres provide easy access to information, assistance and advice on a wide range of Council services.

We have two Customer service Centres in the borough. One is based on Bell Street in Wigston and the other is based at Oadby Library on the Parade, Oadby.

For more information about their location and opening times please click here

We are committed to providing excellent customer service. We aim to assist residents and visitors with all council enquiries and try to resolve issues as quickly as possible. Our main council telephone line is 0116 2888961 and is available for customers who prefer to deal with their enquiry via the telephone.

For more information about when our telephone lines are available please click here
