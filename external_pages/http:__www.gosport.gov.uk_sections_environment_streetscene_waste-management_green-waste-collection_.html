Garden Waste Collection

To find out your collection day please view the map below. For more information please contact Streetscene.

 

About the service:

 

To meet the needs of local residents, the Council can collect and dispose of green sacks containing garden waste including:-



  • Grass cuttings
  • Shrub cuttings
  • Leaf fall
  • Exhausted bedding plants; and
  • Unwanted weed growth.

Garden waste collected is used for composting purposes and not disposed of along with your domestic waste. Composting tonnages contribute towards Gosport's overall recycling rate - for this reason green garden sacks which contain black sacks, are too heavy to lift or contain any other item not listed above, will not be collected. Please do not include food waste or soil. The weight limit for your green sack is 13kg - this equates to approximately half a sack of potatoes.


The Council introduced a chargeable kerbside garden waste collection service during 1992 in order to help those residents who are unable to transport green waste to the household waste recycling centre.  Under The Waste (England and Wales)(Amendment) Regulations 2012, garden waste is classified as a type of waste for which a charge may be made. This charge covers the cost of the sack, as well as the cost of its collection, and enables the Council to run the service. 

 

To view our garden waste service leaflet click here.


How to take part:

 

Special large, green, single-use plastic sacks identified with the Council logo must be purchased. These are charged at £1.30 per sack for OAP's and £1.70 per sack for other residents.

 

Once filled the sack must be made visible and available at the front of the property on the nominated collection day, by 7.00am. Simply locate your home on the map below then check the index for the appropriate collection day. 

  

Where to buy sacks:

Sacks can be purchased from the Town Hall, High Street, Gosport, between Monday and Friday, 9am until 5pm.  Please note that sacks must be purchased by cash only.

 

Should a resident be unable to visit the Town Hall and receives assistance with the normal domestic waste collection service a home delivery can be arranged by contacting the Streetscene Section.

A minimum order of 5 green sacks is required for home delivery, if possible, with payment prior to supply.

 

Sacks are also sold at Gosport Tourist Information Centre (TIC), South Street, Gosport which is open April to October, Monday to Saturday 9.30am - 5pm and closed on Sundays. November to March, Monday to Saturday 10am - 4pm and closed on Sundays.

Open Bank Holidays - 10am - 3pm. For more information on the TIC please click here.


Sacks are also sold at Homeflair 89 High Street Lee-on-the-Solent during normal store opening hours. Please click here.

 

Lee-on-the-solent Residents Association hold Lee on the Solent BOOST Events in the Methodist Church in Lee High Street, once a month (usually the third Wednesday), March to October, Garden Sacks will be available at any BOOST event throughout the year when a member of staff from Gosport Tourist Information Centre is in attendance. For more information on the BOOST events please visit the Lee-on-the-Solent Residents Association website here.   


Problem with collection?


If your sack has been placed out for collection by 7.00 am but not removed on the nominated day, please contact the Streetscene Section within the following 24 hours.  Providing the collection contractor has not listed any reason for non collection, a request will made for them to return and remove the green sack within 24 hours.
