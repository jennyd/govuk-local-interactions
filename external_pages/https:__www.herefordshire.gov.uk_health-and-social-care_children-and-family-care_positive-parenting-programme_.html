Positive parenting programme

Raising children isn't easy and we all need a little help sometimes. Herefordshire Council offers different parenting groups for parents and guardians of children and young people aged 0-16.

Our family support workers are trained to deliver the Triple P programme (Positive Parenting Programme), the Solihull parenting course and the Incredible Years parenting course (Webster-Stratton programme).

Please find below a list of the courses available.

You can find more information about the different courses via the following links:

Triple P: http://www.triplep-parenting.net/glo-en/home/

Solihull: http://www.solihullapproachparenting.com/

Incredible Years: http://incredibleyears.com/

International recognition - Triple P videos

Triple P International has been so impressed with our work and positive outcomes for families in Herefordshire that they have filmed some of the parents we have worked with. This footage is available to view on the International Triple P website:
