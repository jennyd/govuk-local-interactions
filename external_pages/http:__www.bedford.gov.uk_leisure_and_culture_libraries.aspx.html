Libraries

Visit your local library to discover a whole new world!

There are five libraries in the Borough, plus the mobile library and Library Link who deliver services directly to you. 

Bedford Borough Council wishes to develop a Library Strategy to set out the vision for the service over the next few years.  We want to understand how you are using libraries: which services are most popular, which services should be prioritised and how we could save money going forward.
Help to shape the future of Bedford Borough's libraries.
