Report a damaged road, path, pothole, road sign or a broken street light

We are responsible for the maintenance of all Council adopted roads, footpaths and street lights in the Borough.

As well as routine inspections, our team responds to calls about maintenance issues and determine if any repair work is needed.

We aim to carry out any repair work within 28 days or, where a fault is deemed an immediate risk to users, we will respond and make safe within 24 hours. 

 

How to report a damaged road, path, pothole, road sign or a broken light

Report a problem in one of the following ways:

  • Report a broken street light 
  • Report a pothole, road, path, pothole or road sign
  • Report the problem by telephone at 01642 391959

 

Government Pothole Fund

Local roads and footways are used by most people on a daily basis and are fundamental to the economic, social and environmental well-being of the communities they serve.

The Government have recently announced the creation of a Pothole Fund worth £200 million, with £168 million being made available (2014/15 financial year) to highway authorities in England to help repair damage to the local road network.
