Report a rodent or insect pest problem

If you are experiencing a problem with rodents, insects or other pests please report it here and we will contact you. All councils can give advice and some councils provide a treatment service for which there may be a charge.

You will be advised of which pests can be treated and any charge before a treatment is carried out.

Your contact details will be processed in accordance with current data protection legislation and will only be used for the purpose of processing your report.

Fields marked with a * are required.
