  • Sussex Student Card

    Apply for a card to get a 34% discount on train season tickets in Sussex if you are a student of sixth form age.

  • Concessionary seats

    Apply for a concessionary 'spare' seat sometimes available on school transport.

  • 3in1 Card

    Apply for a 3in1 Card to get benefits including cheaper bus fares for students aged 5-19 years.

  • Unizone rail ticket

    Apply for the season ticket offering unlimited train travel for students of sixth form age.

  • Disabled persons' railcard

    Apply for, renew or replace a railcard offering a third off the cost of rail travel for disabled people.

  • Free disabled persons' bus pass

    Apply for, renew or replace a free disabled persons' bus pass.
