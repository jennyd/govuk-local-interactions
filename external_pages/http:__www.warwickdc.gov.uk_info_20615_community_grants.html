Local advice and support for voluntary and community groups

South Warwickshire Funding Development Network (SWFDN) is an informal group of "experts" which includes local Funders and Development Workers across Warwick and Stratford Districts, including Warwick District Council. For advice and support around a specific funding or group development issue, visit the SWFDN web page.
