In case of emergency

Emergencies you may face

Examples include:

  • flooding
  • storms or snow
  • explosion
  • power failure
  • chemical fires.

Some initial steps to take


  • Keep all family and friends' phone numbers with you
  • Pack an emergency grab bag including radio, torch, first aid kit, medication, food and water.
  • Complete our Household emergency plan template

If you are evacuated


  • Follow instructions
  • Check your neighbours
  • Gather family members and pets
  • Take prescribed medicines with you
  • Do not return home unless told it is safe.

Kirklees Major Incident Plan

The Emergency Planning team are responsible for the development and maintenance of the Kirklees Major Incident Plan which details the generic arrangements for responding to emergency incidents as well as procedures for dealing with specific risks.

We are part of a multi-agency planning and response group that works in partnership to ensure there are adequate response mechanisms in place to protect the communities of Kirklees.

Contact us for more information on the plan.
