Resident's parking permits

 

In certain areas of the borough, residents are required to display a valid Residents Permit in order to park their vehicle without any time limit in the parking zone their property lies in.

From November 1 2014 the management and administration of residents' permits returns to Cumbria County Council. To apply or for enquiries contact 0845 6096609 or email westpp@cumbria.gov.uk .
