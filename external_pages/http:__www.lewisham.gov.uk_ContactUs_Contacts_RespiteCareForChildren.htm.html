Contact us

Page Content 1

Complete your enquiry using an online form or search for a service using our A to Z.

From 16 February 2015, the forms and evidence service in the Customer Service Centre in Laurence House will close in the afternoons. If you have a form to hand in or additional evidence to provide, you can visit us face to face Monday to Friday between 9am–12.30pm. Alternatively, you can use our online services at any time.

Please note that this is not applicable to housing benefit applications. If you need to submit a form or evidence in support of a housing benefit or council tax reduction claim, you must do this online using the forms below.

Most requested services

  • Pay your council tax by direct debit
  • Register for council tax (moving to the borough)
  • Close council tax account (leaving the borough)
  • Council tax address change (in the borough)
  • Housing benefit: new circumstances and evidence
  • Apply or renew a parking permit
  • Pay a parking fine
  • Find and comment on planning applications
