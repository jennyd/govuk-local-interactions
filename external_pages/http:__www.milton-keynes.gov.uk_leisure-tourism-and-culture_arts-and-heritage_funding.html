Funding

The list below shows a slelection of local and/or arts and heritage specific funding opportunities.  Please contact them directly to find out whether your project is appropriate to their funding criteria.

  • MK Community Foundation: for all your local funding needs plus annual arts bursaries
  • Arts Council England: national development agency for the arts
  • Big Lottery: funding opportunities
  • Heritage Lottery Fund: national lottery funding for heritage projects
  • WREN: grants for Buckinghamshire and Milton Keynes


If you are a voluntary or community group in MK you may also find support from Community Action MK. Contact: 01908 661623 or info@communityactionmk.org for further information.

A national website provided by NCVO and the Cabinet Office of the Third Sector also provides a very comprehensive database of arts & non-arts funding streams called Funding Central - website provides news, search tools and you can sign up for their regular newsletter.

If you are applying to Arts Council England or Heritage Lottery Fund please let us know, we may be able to help you develop your application or link in other relevant work to make your application more successful. Email us: arts@milton-keynes.gov.uk or heritage@milton-keynes.gov.uk. MK Council officers help to co-ordinate major bids, avoid clashes and ensure that bids stand the best possible chance of success.
