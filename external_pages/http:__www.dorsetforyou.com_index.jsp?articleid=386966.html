East Dorset District Councillors

East Dorset District Council has 29 councillors who are elected by the public every four years. They represent 16 different wards of East Dorset.

The political composition of the council is:

  Conservative 25 Members   
  Liberal Democrat 3 Members   
  Vacancy 1 Seat   

The Conservative Group have overall control of the Council.


The next district council elections will be held in May 2019. See latest and previous election results for East Dorset District Council.
