Pest control

Our pest control team offers a professional service to both domestic and commercial customers, either on a case-by-case basis or under contract.

We can deal with the following pests:

  • Wasps
  • Mice
  • Rats
  • Fleas
  • Cockroaches
  • Bed bugs
  • Squirrels
