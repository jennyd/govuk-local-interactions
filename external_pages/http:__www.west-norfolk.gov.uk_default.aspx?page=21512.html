The Council target is to respond within 3 days to all fly-tipping and litter reports.

 

Fly-tipping/litter

 

We will:

  • Endeavour to keep the Borough clean and litter free. 
  • Empty litter bins before they are full.
  • Help with your locally organised clean-ups.

You can help by:

  • Being tidy with your litter and chewing gum.
  • Using the free refuse tips and Home Waste Recycling centres.
  • Not leaving your dog's mess for others.
  • Not "Fly Posting" - it's untidy and illegal.
  • Not parking where you want us to clean.
  • You can use the online form Report Fly Tipping to report problems to us

 

Road Sweeping

 

The Council is responsible for the sweeping of the Streets.  A regular programme of sweeping takes place throughout the Borough.

 

For further information please visit our Road Sweeping page.

 

If you feel your road is not swept satisfactorily, or you have any other comments or questions, please complete the Online Street Sweeping Report Form.

 

Dead Animals/Syringes

 

To report a dead animal on the roadside please complete the Online Dead Animal Report Form.

 

If a syringe is found, do not touch it, leave it where it is and call us on 0800 253 2687.

 

Footpaths and Cycle Routes

 

For general information on Rights of Way and Cycle Routes use the contact details at the bottom of the page.

 

Street Lighting

 

Responsibility is split between the County, District and Parish Councils.

 

The County Council deal with all reports and will direct them to the appropriate body. Telephone: 0344 8008008. e- mail: streetlighting.pt@norfolk.gov.uk or visit the Norfolk County Council website to report the problem online. 

 

Road Signs

 

Road signage is the responsibility of the County Council.  For information or to report a problem telephone 0344 800 8020.

 

Street Furniture

 

Most street furniture, seats, benches and bins are the responsibility of the Borough Council of King's Lynn & West Norfolk.

 

As well as providing furniture directly, the Council operate a system allowing street furniture to be sponsored. 

 

For information or to report a problem, call us on 0800 253 2687.

 

Street Name Plates

 

The Borough Council of King's Lynn and West Norfolk is responsible for the replacement of street name plates.  Complete the online eForm to Report a Missing Name Plate.
