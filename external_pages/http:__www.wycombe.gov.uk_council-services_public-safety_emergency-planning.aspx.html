Emergency planning

We work with organisations such as the police, fire and ambulance service to regain normal levels of service following major emergencies.

Our emergency response is designed to support the emergency services as required during a major incident. We help those people involved in the event - survivors, friends and family.

We aim to keep you up to date with the latest information when a major emergency occurs. This information will be posted on our website, along with links to further sources of information where appropriate.

We provide advice and information on what to do in the event of a flood and extreme weather and how you can prepare.

Our Environmental Health team is responsible for investigating the spread of infectious diseases including food poisoning.

We provide advice about business continuity management to help your organisation handle an emergency, continue to function and recover effectively afterwards.
