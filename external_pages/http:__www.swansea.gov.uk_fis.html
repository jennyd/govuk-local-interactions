Family Information Service

The Swansea Family Information Service (FIS) is a one stop shop, providing free, quality, impartial information on a wide range of Childcare, Children's, Family Support and Family related issues and where relevant a signposting service.

Help is at hand for Childcare providers, those with an interest in opening a childcare provision, employers, organisations and Parents/Carers of children aged 0-19. 

Family support services directory

The Family Support Services Directory provides information on services available to children and young people.

Family and Children's Centres

Family Centres provide a range of quality community services to children, young people and families to build and strengthen relationships.

Childcare in Swansea

Information on how to choose childcare, what to consider and the different types of childcare identified, along with a list of childcare providers.

My child has a disability

Information for parents and carers of disabled children and young people in Swansea.
