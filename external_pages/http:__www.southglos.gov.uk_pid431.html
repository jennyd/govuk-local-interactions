A problem with pests at home

We provide pest control services to any resident in South Gloucestershire.

Our pest control officers advise and carry out treatments on a variety of pests including:

  • rodents (rats/mice)
  • squirrels
  • wasps
  • fleas
  • bedbugs
  • other insects such as flies, ants, cockroaches, moths and carpet beetles

We do not treat for pigeons, seagulls, birds, moles and bees. However we can offer professional advice.

Treatment timescales

If you call to book the service a pest control officer will normally call you back on the same or the following day to arrange an appointment at a time and date convenient for you.

If you complete our web form you will receive a call from one of our admin team to discuss timescales.

Request for pest control treatment

Cost of advice and treatment

We have a clear pricing structure (see Pest Control Charges 2015-16), with some concessions for those in receipt of certain benefits. Payment is normally taken by credit/debit card when you book a treatment.

The cost of any advisory visit will be deducted from the treatment cost should treatment be necessary and carried out at the time of the advisory visit. If treatment is not necessary, we can give you advice and peace of mind.

Contacting us

Please contact us using the online form, or using the contact details on this page. We are available Monday – Thursday 8.45 am to 5.00 pm and Friday 8.45 am to 4.30 pm.

The area we cover

We cover all of South Gloucestershire including: Bitton, Bradley Stoke, Charfield, Chipping Sodbury, Downend, Emersons Green, Filton, Hanham, Kingswood, Longwell Green, Mangotsfield, Patchway, Severn Beach, Staple Hill, Thornbury, Winterbourne, Yate and some surrounding areas. If you live outside South Gloucestershire let us know as we still may be able to help.
