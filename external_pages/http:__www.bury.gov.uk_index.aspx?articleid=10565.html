Primary school admissions

To be able to apply for a place in a Bury school

  • You must be resident in Bury. If you live outside Bury, you must apply through your home authority.
  • This is for children due to start school in September 2016 and born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012.

Important dates to note

  • November 2015 - Information on how to apply is distributed to parents.
  • 15 January 2016 - Deadline for applications. Applications received after 15 January 2016 will be treated as late applications.
  • 18 April 2016 - Emails will be sent after 2pm. for those that applied online. Letters will be posted for those that have applied by application on 18 April 2016.

Ready to apply online

The online system is available during the application period each year for children applying to start primary school in Reception class.

Primary school place application

What you need to know before applying

Read through the Admissions to Primary School Guide 2016 from the downloads area which guides you through the admissions process and should give you all the information you need.

Once you've decided which 3 schools you wish to show preferences for, you can apply online.

Have a read through our frequently asked questions which gives information on the following topics: 

  • When to apply
  • Criteria for offering school places and catchment areas
  • Offer of places

Important Information

These pages are intended as a guide to the policies and procedures of the Local Authority (LA). Please note that:

  • Bury Council does not fund places in independent schools.
  • LA Nursery and Primary Schools gives details and links to their websites.
  • Certain schools need additional information to apply their admissions criteria and may require you to also complete a Supplementary Information Form from the school's website.
  • Examination results are published in the school's prospectus. The Office for Standards in Education (Ofsted) publishes inspection reports Reports OFSTED - Inspection reports for each school.
  • School achievement and attainment tables for Bury Local Authority are available free from the Department for Education. The Local Authority number is 351. The telephone number for the Department for Education is: 0870-000-2288.
  • Details of school performance which can be searched on by school or area can be found on the Gov.uk website School Performance
  • Organising travel to and from school on public transport can be done via the Transport for Greater Manchester website. Transport for Greater Manchester
  • Information on tram services is available on the Metrolink website or at main Metrolink stations.
  • If your child(ren) need care before and/or after school, you can find information by using the Childcare Search - Bury Family Information Service
  • Links to neighbouring authorities can be found below:
    • Bolton Council admissions
    • Lancashire County Council - secondary school places
    • Salford City Council - admissions
    • Rochdale Council - admissions
