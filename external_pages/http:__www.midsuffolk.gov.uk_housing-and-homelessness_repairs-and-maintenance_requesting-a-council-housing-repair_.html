Requesting a Council Housing Repair

You can request a Council housing repair online  (opens new window) or by calling the  Repairs Helpdesk during office opening hours (Monday to Friday, 7.30am to 4:00pm) on 0845 600 1135 or 01449 724732. Please note if you report a repair online your repair request will only be processed Monday to Friday, 8.00am to 5:00pm.

When reporting a repair you must provide the following information:

  • Tenants name
  • Tenants address
  • Tenants telephone number
  • Tenants email address
  • Details of what needs repairing
  • Where the repair is needed
  • Other information that might help us i.e. access details
  • How the damage happened

You are encouraged to make the Repairs Helpdesk staff aware of any special circumstances that are likely to influence the response that the Council provides i.e. special circumstances concerning disability of occupants etc.

After you have reported your repair you will be sent a confirmation letter and repair satisfaction survey for you to tell us about the contractor’s performance and if necessary, any follow up work that may be needed. For work inside the home we will offer you a morning or afternoon appointment.

Please note that you will be charged if any damage to your home was caused by yourself, your family or any visitors.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION: Emergency or urgent repairs should be reported immediately by telephoning the Repairs Help Desk: 0845 600 1135 or 01449 724732. For more information on Emergency Repairs.
