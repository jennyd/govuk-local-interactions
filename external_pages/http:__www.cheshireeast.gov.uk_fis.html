Family Information Service (FIS)

FIS is a free, impartial one-stop-shop service for parents/carers and young people aged 0–19, providing accurate and detailed information on services in Cheshire East, regionally and nationally. From childcare to tax credits to guidance on bullying, the Family Information Service can assist for all manner of queries, big or small. For more info on what we can help you with download our Family Information Service Factsheet (PDF, 106KB).

FIS has now become part of the new 'front door' for children's Social Care and will take all of the initial calls for the Cheshire East Consultation Service. Further information with regards to the new service will be released over the next couple of weeks but in the meant time if you have any immediate concerns about the safety of a child then please call 0300 123 5012 (option2).

Cheshire East’s Family Information Service can be contacted by:

  • Calling at local rates 0300 123 5033 between 8.30am and 5.00pm, Monday to Thursday, 4.30pm on a Friday. An answer machine operates outside of these hours with messages collected the next working day.
  • Emailing us at fis.east@cheshireeast.gov.uk
