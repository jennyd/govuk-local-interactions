Parking fines

Parking throughout the Lancaster district is managed by Lancashire Parking Services. Lancaster City Council retains responsibility for car park enforcement.

Motorcycle parking

Motorcycles are exempt from pay and display charges in any city council pay and display car park, and on-street pay and display parking bays. However certain restrictions do apply - please see our motorcycle parking guide for full details.
