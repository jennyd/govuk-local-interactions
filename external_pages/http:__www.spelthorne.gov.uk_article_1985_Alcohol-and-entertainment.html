Alcohol, entertainment and late night refreshment

The Licensing Act 2003 deals with:

  • the sale of alcohol including off licences, pubs and restaurants
  • supply of alcohol in clubs
  • regulated entertainment such as live and recorded music
  • late night takeaways and eateries
