Older People

We may be able to offer you help and support if:

  • You are aged 65 and over
  • You cannot manage day-to-day tasks on your own
  • You do not have anyone who can help you with these tasks
  • You cannot carry on living at home without help

You can make a referral to adult social services by contacting the Integrated Access Team
