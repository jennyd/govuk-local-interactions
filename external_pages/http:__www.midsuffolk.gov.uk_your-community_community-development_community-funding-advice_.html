New fund for groups supporting older people  - Apply now!

 

 

Pargiters Trust Fund

The closing date for this new fund is 24th September 2015, looking to fund charities, voluntary and community groups providing services and support or advice to older people. Grants of up to £2,000 are available for helping in any of the following ways:

  • Improving health and well-being - Reducing isolation, providing hot meals and refreshments and helping older people to stay active through sports, arts, recreation, social clubs and volunteering.
  • Improving access to facilities, advice and training - Providing transport, supporting Good Neighbourhood schemes, befriending schemes and improving access to information and IT for older people.
  • Overcoming problems - Helping older people who are experiencing difficult circumstances due to an illness, injury, disability, bereavement or financial difficulty.

Dementia Friendly Communities Fund


This fund has been relaunched with new criteria and a closing date for applications of 24th September 2015. Grants of up to £2,000 are available to small, community based groups for activities within communities that can deliver a greater awareness and understanding of dementia or support to those affected by dementia including carers, so that they can remain independent and have choice and control over their lives.

Parish Councils, Charities, Community Groups, Voluntary Organisations - Find out how your project could be funded! Here at Mid Suffolk District Council we offer a grant finding service FREE OF CHARGE. What is Grant Finder?

A database with the latest information about grants and sources of funding. It covers over 3,000 potential sources of funding including information on:

  • European grants 
  • Millennium grants 
  • Heritage funding
  • Lottery grants 
  • Government sponsored funding 
  • Training initiatives 
  • Sports grants
  • Arts funding 
  • Help from Charities

Are you looking for:

  • Environmental improvements? 
  • Better transport facilities? 
  • New equipment? 
  • Village greens? 
  • New Premises?
  • Existing premises refurbished? 
  • Specialist needs equipment? 
  • Project feasibility work

Ask 'Grant Finder' to help identify what financial help may be available to you. Grant Finder will then be able to provide full details of all potential sources of funding, including relevant timetables, contact names, eligibility criteria etc.
