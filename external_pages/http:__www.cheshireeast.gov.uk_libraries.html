National Libraries Day 2016

Saturday 6th February is National Libraries Day, and we're inviting you to visit us and find out what we have to offer and to let us know why you love your library. There are lots of things happening in libraries tomorrow morning to celebrate - just visit your library's web page or Facebook page for details, or follow #librariesday on Twitter.

In addition, we're launching our learning offer on National Libraries Day. There are opportunities for all ages to explore - #learninlibraries.

Join the Library

Joining the library opens up a vast world of knowledge,entertainment, culture and information. Once you become a member you may borrow up to 20 items! It's free to join - just pop into your local library (all you need is one form of ID with your name and address) or join online. You don't need to live in Cheshire to join, but you must have a UK address. You'll also have access to a range of online services and resources in both Cheshire East and Cheshire West and Chester libraries.

When you use Your Library Online you can...

  • Manage your account
  • See what’s going on in your local library
  • Use our eResources to find out information on subjects including arts, music, legal information, public information, family and local history.
