Report a missed bin collection

Missed bin collection

If your bin has not been collected by 5pm on your scheduled collection day you can report it to us as missed.

If a missed bin is reported to us within three working days Biffa will return and collect your bin as soon as possible, providing it is not missed for any valid reason.

Report a missed bin collection

To ensure your bin is collected please follow the guidelines for putting out your bin.

If your bin is missed you can take your waste to a rubbish tip. 

Missed bulk bin collection

If you use a shared bin in a block of flats or building such as a church, council office or a school you can report a missed collection.
