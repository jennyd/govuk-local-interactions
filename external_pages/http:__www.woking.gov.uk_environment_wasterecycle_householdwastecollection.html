Your waste and recycling services

Please note wet cardboard cannot be collected.
Additional recycling bins are available for free of charge to containerise your extra recycling.
Click here for further information.

Select a service from the list below for more information, or download a copy of 'Your guide to recycling services in Woking'.

If you live in a flat or an apartment and use communal bin areas please visit our dedicated apartment recycling webpage.

Or, if you are our and about around the borough you can also recycle on the go, or visit one of our recycling bring sites.

If you have any questions regarding your recycling services or cannot find the information you are looking for, please contact us.
