Repairs and improvements

Printer-friendly page

How to report a repair

Before contacting us, please download and check the Repairs - Rights and Responsibilities section of the Housing Repairs Handbook. 

  • Housing Repairs Handbook - Rights and Responsibilities (PDF - 348 kb)

Send us an email to h.repairs@stalbans.gov.uk stating:

Address of property where the repair is needed: 
Your name: 
Contact telephone number: 
Email address: 
Brief details of the repair needed:

Please also include: 
Dates / times when you (or another adult) will NOT be available for this repair to take place 
(for example school runs, other appointments etc.)

We will contact you to arrange the repair.

Cancelling or changing a booked appointment

Please contact us as soon as you can if you need to cancel or change the time of a booked appointment, so the appointment can be given to another resident.

Chasing a repair

If you have already reported the repair please give details, including if you have reported the repair to a council staff member, Customer Service Centre or at a Citizen Service Point.

Other ways to report a repair

Repairs may also be reported between 8:45a m and 5:15 pm Monday –Thursday, and 8:45am and 4:45pm on Fridays

  • By telephoning us on 01727 819256

  • By visiting the Council offices
