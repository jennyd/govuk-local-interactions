Pavements

Pavements, particularly those constructed of paving stones, can suffer very badly from vehicle trespass. In the case of damage it is important, if possible, to report the vehicle details/findings so that the Council can recharge the full cost of repair. Illegally parked vehicles cost the council thousands of pounds a year in damaged paving, damaged grass verges and cause a serious problem for blind, disabled and elderly people.

To report any damage to paving stones please contact the Environmental Services Centre on 01642 726001.
