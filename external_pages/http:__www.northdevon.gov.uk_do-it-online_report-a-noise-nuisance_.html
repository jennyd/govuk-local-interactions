Report a noise nuisance

Remember - it costs less if you contact us online

Please see our general advice on noise nuisance before making a report.

To help us take action on your report, we will need as much information as you can provide, including the location. If you don’t complete all the questions, we may have to contact you for further details so please provide as much detail as possible. 

The personal information you provide will be processed in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
