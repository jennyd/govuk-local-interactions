Bulky Item Donations and Free Collections

 

British Heart Foundation offers free collections of furniture and electrical items (some exclusions apply). Book online or call 0808 250 0030.

 

You can donate unwanted electrical items to the Katherine House Hospice Re-use Shop located at the Cannock Household Recycling Centre (HRC).

 

Forums

 

Unwanted household items can be given away or exchanged via a series of websites and forums.

 

Furniture Re-use Network
Promotes the re-use of household furniture, repairing and re-using over 250,000 domestic appliances each year: http://www.frn.org.uk/.

Telephone: 0845 602 8003

 

Charity Retail Association

Re-use your unwanted items through charity shops, raising much needed funds: http://www.charityretail.org.uk/

 

Freecycle

International non-profit movement allowing members to pass on and collect unwanted items free of charge: www.freecycle.org/

 

Freegle

National grass roots organisation of people who are giving and receiving free unwanted items in their

immediate communities: www.ilovefreegle.org/

 

South Staffordshire Council is not responsible for the content of external websites listed below. Users of these forums do so at their own risk. The websites offer a hosting forum for exchangers to contact one another, and users consent to publishing personal details for this purpose. It is the responsibility of individual users of the websites to arrange collection or delivery of items. Please ensure that you protect your personal safety at all times during any exchanges. Individual disclaimers are available on each website. Use of the relevant sites and forums denotes acceptance of these terms and conditions.

 

Free Disposal at Household Recycling Centres

 

 
Bulky household waste including large electrical items can be recycled at the Household Recycling Centres located at Poplars Landfill (Cannock), Pendeford Mill Lane (Bilbrook) and Botterham Lane (Wombourne). These sites are operated by Staffordshire County Council. Please follow the link below for locations, opening times and other information regarding these sites.
 
External Link: Staffordshire County Council Household Recycling Centres
 

Chargeable Bulky Waste Collection

 
The Council will collect bulky household items such as fridges and furniture. Items are normally collected within 7 calendar days of the request.
 
The cost for a bulky household waste collection is a standard charge of £15.00 for every 3 items collected (maximum of 9 items):
 
1-3 items (inclusive) = £15.00
4-6 items (inclusive) = £30.00
7-9 items (inclusive) = £45.00
 
To arrange a bulky waste collection please call Customer Services on (01902) 696203.
