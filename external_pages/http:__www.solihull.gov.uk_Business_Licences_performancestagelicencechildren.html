Performance/stage licence for school age children

The rules governing children in entertainment covers children who take part in performances, including:

  • all broadcast performances
  • theatre
  • child photography
  • modelling

Apply

To apply please download an application form. 

Further information

For further information please refer to the following documents:

  • Licence application checklist
  • Licence holder and chaperone guidance
  • Restrictions in relation to children's performances
  • Child performance and activities licensing legislation in England
  • Statutory Regulations 2014
