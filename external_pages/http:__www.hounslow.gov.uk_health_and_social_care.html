Welcome to the health and social care webpages

We want to encourage and support everybody to be active and choose a healthier lifestyle as there is clear evidence to show that you could live longer and feel happier and more positive.

There are a range of services in Hounslow that can provide you with information and help to keep you well and get the most from your life.

Social care helps people who are vulnerable live safely and independently. We support children and families as well as adults and their carers.

To make sure that Hounslow residents receive responsive health and social care services that are timely and effective, we work in partnership with:

  • NHS Hounslow Clinical Commissioning Group

  • West Middlesex University Hospital

  • West London Mental Health Trust

  • Hounslow and Richmond Community Health Trust

  • Voluntary/community organisations

Our aim is to make sure that you receive the advice, information and services you need to make informed choices to improve and maintain your health, your independence and live safely within the London Borough of Hounslow, and that you are able to tell us what you think about our services in an honest and supported way.
