Pest control

Rats and mice in domestic properties

We can provide a pest control service to domestic dwellings for the treatment of rats and mice for £49.50 + VAT, with the following two visits free of charge. Please call us on 01884 255255 to arrange.

For all other pests please feel free to contact our appointed supplier Mitie Pest Control Services on 0844 335 0330 who can offer discounted rates. Or if you wish, you can make your own arrangements privately. For more advice concerning pest control please check the related links below: 

  • Ants 
  • Bats and Snakes 
  • Bees, wasps and hoverflies
  • Fleas 
  • Rats

Help if you are in receipt of qualifying benefits

If you are in receipt of Income Support, Housing Benefit, Council Tax Relief or Pension Credit Guarantee, we can provide a free pest control service to domestic dwellings for the treatment of:

  • rodents (rats and mice)
  • hornets
  • fleas
  • bedbugs
  • cockroaches

Please call us on 01884 255255 to verify your benefits entitlement if you wish to receive a free service for these pests.

If you require an out of hours service any weekday between 6.00pm and 8.00am and between 8.00am and 6.00pm on a Saturday, Sunday, public holiday or bank holiday, the charge is £49.50 + VAT with each subsequent visit free of charge. Please contact Mitie Pest Control Services directly on 0844 335 0330.
