Consultation and research methods

The council uses a number of research, consultation and engagement methods to involve our residents, including:

  1. Poole Opinion Panel - the Council's consultative panel. We are looking for new members to join the panel to have their say about the local area and council services. Find out more about the panel and how you can get involved.

  2. Shaping Poole Survey - residents survey conducted every three years.

  3. Focus groups and discussions on topical issues.

The research and consultation guide tells you more about the methods the Council uses.
