Libraries, museums and galleries

Discover something new today ... visit your local library

Books, talking books, reading groups, CDs, DVDs, computer and Internet access, email, learning ... whatever you're looking for there's a world of information and entertainment just waiting for you to explore and enjoy at Sunderland's public libraries.

Being a member of one of Sunderland's public libraries gives you access to over three quarters of a million books and other items; free access to the internet; music CDs and DVDs to borrow; homework support clubs; book clubs; large print books; talking books; children's story time sessions; study facilities; courses ... all with friendly staff at the heart of the community.

  • Museums and galleries

    Sunderland boasts a number of fantastic museums and galleries including Sunderland Museum & Winter Gardens, Monkwearmouth Station Museum, and the NGCA.

  • Digital library services

    Sunderland's libraries provide free access to a number of digital services, giving you access to a wide range of up-to-date sources of information, eBooks and eAudiobooks.

  • Local history and heritage

    The Local Studies Centre is situated on the first floor of the City Library and Arts Centre and is dedicated to the collection of local information, including local and family history.

  • Libraries - health information

    Sunderland Libraries provide a variety of health information for you. There are books to borrow, leaflets to take away and information on organisations that offer help and advice.
