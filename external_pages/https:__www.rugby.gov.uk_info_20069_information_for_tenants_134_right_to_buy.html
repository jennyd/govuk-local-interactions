Right to Buy

Print this page

Right to Buy gives council tenants the chance to buy their council home at a discount.

You can apply to buy your council home if:

  • it's your only or main home 
  • it's self-contained
  • you're a secure tenant
  • you've had a public sector landlord (eg council, housing association or NHS trust) for three years

Am I eligible?

Take the online eligibility test to find out if you qualify

How much discount could I get?

Use the online Right to Buy calculator to find out much your discount could be

Want to find out more?

Visit the Government's Right to Buy frequently asked questions (FAQ) webpage
