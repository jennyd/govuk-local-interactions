A-Z of licences

Animal boarding

B

Busking licence – Rushcliffe Borough Council does not issue busking licences.

C

Car boot sale – Rushcliffe Borough Council is not a markets rights authority. If your market or car boot is within six and two third linear miles from the nearest charter market, ie Nottingham City you must obtain permission from them. Nottingham City Markets can be contacted on 0115 915 6970.

M

Market rights – Rushcliffe Borough Council is not a markets rights authority. If your market or car boot is within six and two third linear miles from the nearest charter market, ie Nottingham City you must obtain permission from them. Nottingham City Markets can be contacted on 0115 915 6970.
