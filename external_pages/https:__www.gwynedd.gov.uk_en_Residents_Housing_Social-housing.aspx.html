Social housing

There is one contact point for all housing options enquiries and applications in Gwynedd – the Housing Options Team:

  • Phone: 01286 685100
  • Email: opsiynautai@gwynedd.gov.uk

To be considered for a social house by Cartrefi Cymunedol Gwynedd, Grŵp Cynefin or North Wales Housing you need to be on the Gwynedd Common Housing Register.

 

Who can get on the register?

  • homeless people
  • people legally owed a duty by a housing authority
  • people who live in insanitary / overcrowded or otherwise unsatisfactory housing
  • people who need to move for their health / welfare
  • people who need to move to a specific location in order to avoid hardship (for themselves or others).

Being on the register does not guarantee anyone a house.

 

How do I get onto the register?

Contact the Housing Options Team:

  • Phone: 01286 685100
  • Email: opsiynautai@gwynedd.gov.uk 


What will happen after this?

We’ll discuss your needs in order to decide on the best way forward.

If being put on the register doesn’t answer your needs, we will discuss the other options.

If you do qualify to be on the register, you will be prioritised for a house using a points system.

More details about the system: Common Housing Allocation Policy summary

More information about social housing in Gwynedd can be found on the Gwynedd Housing website
