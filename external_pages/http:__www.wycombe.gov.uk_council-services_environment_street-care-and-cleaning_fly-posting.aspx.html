Fly posting

To report fly posting:

  • please contact our Waste team on 01494 586 550 or email wasteteam@wycombe.gov.uk

Fly posting is the unauthorised placing of any sign, poster or sticker advertising or promoting an event or commercial activity on any street sign, public utility street cabinets or on private premises without the owner's permission, and it is illegal.

We are responsible for the removal of fly posting from council property and on the public highways in the district. Public utilities such as telecom companies or bus and railway companies are responsible for removing items that have been fly posted on their property.

For fly posted items on private property, it is the property owners responsibility for removal.

Temporary signs

If you would like to use a temporary sign on a road or pavement, you must check with Transport for Buckinghamshire.

  • See Bucks CC Signs (external website) for further information about temporary road signs
