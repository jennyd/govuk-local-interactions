Applying for a council home

Homechoice

If you would like to apply for a home in this district - this may be rented housing, shared ownership or low cost housing schemes - you will need to register on the Warwick District HomeChoice scheme.

Application form for Homechoice

Download and complete this form to register on the scheme and return it to HomeChoice, Warwick District Council, Housing Department, PO Box 2175, Riverside House, Milverton Hill, Leamington Spa, CV32 5QE. You can also get a form from Riverside House or by calling 01926 353366.

HomeChoice Application Form (369kb, PDF)

The housing register is used to estimate the current and future demand for housing and to assess the housing needs of the people. It is used by the council and all the housing associations with properties in this area and if you would like housing, your name must be on this housing register.

How the Homechoice scheme works

Full details of the Homechoice scheme, policy and the banding system.

Current properties and bidding

  • Anyone can browse the current property list
  • If you are already registered then you can bid online for a property
  • You can also bid by calling 01926 353366

Contact information

For advice about Homechoice contact the Housing Advice team on 01926 456446/456431 or email: hadvice@warwickdc.gov.uk.
