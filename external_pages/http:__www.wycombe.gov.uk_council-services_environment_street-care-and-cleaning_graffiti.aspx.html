Graffiti

To report graffiti on Wycombe District Council property:

contact our Waste team on 01494 586 550 or email wasteteam@wycombe.gov.uk

We are responsible for removing graffiti from council property.

We will remove racist or offensive graffiti within 24-hours of the report. We remove other graffiti as soon as possible.
Transport for Bucks is responsible for the removal of graffiti from bridges and subways.

  • See Bucks CC: bridges (external website) for further information.

Removal of graffiti on private property is the owner's responsibility. We can offer advice if your property has been vandalised.
