Garden waste service

Cut down your tip runs by signing up to our garden waste service.  

Our brown bin garden waste service runs for nine months of the year.  The 2016 service starts from 1st March 2016 and will be £40 per bin. 

Existing customers - if you have signed up to direct debit you don't need to do anything but you will still receive an invoice.  If you have not signed up to direct debit you will receive an invoice at the beginning of February.

To check the start date of your collection click here

Great for:

  1. Grass cuttings
  2. Branches (up to 4'' diameter)
  3. Clippings
  4. Weeds and plants

Can’t be used for:

  1. Kitchen waste, vegetables and fruit - have you considered composting?
  2. Any plastic, including bags and bin liners
  3. Turf, soil, rubble, concrete or stones - have you considered your local household recycling centre or our bulky waste service?
  4. Animal waste and bedding

Things to consider

The fee is annual and it is a flat fee. Part payments or concessions are not available.

Refunds are only available before your first collection is made.

You can order as many bins as you like for the current fee.  Brown bins are only available in the standard 240 litre size.

Bin liners cannot be used in your brown bin.  BDC uses a natural composting process which takes 14 weeks for the organic matter to rot down.  Many bin liners can take longer than this and therefore we ask that residents do not use liners in their brown bins.

Tell us you're moving

We collect from your address, so if you're moving house please let us know so we can update our records, here are our contact details. You can take your brown bin with you when you move.

Pay for your garden waste service

As a garden waste customer you will get an invoice from us week commencing the 8th February with all the payment details.  If you have signed up to Direct Debit you do not need to do anything, if you haven't you can pay by the following methods:

  1. Please contact us to set up a direct debit
  2. You can pay for the garden waste service online
  3. Over the automated telephone facility
  4. By bank transfer to account number 91131605 sort code 40-15-07. Make sure you quote your invoice number on the payment or we won't know you have paid
  5. By post with a cheque for the correct amount with your name, address, invoice number and telephone number clearly printed on the reverse - here is our address
  6. In person at our customer service centre, here is our address and opening times.
