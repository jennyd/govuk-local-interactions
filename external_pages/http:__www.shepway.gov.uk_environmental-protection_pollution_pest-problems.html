Pest problems

Information and guidance relating to pest problems in public and private premises

Rats, mice, insects and other vermin can carry diseases and damage property. These problems get worse if the infestation is not treated.

If you have pests in your home, you can arrange a pest control visit. This service is provided for us by Cannon Pest Control.

To arrange a visit from Cannon Pest Control:

  • call 0844 967 0722, or email enquiries@cannonpestcontrol.com
  • tell them that you are a resident of Shepway District Council

 

Fees

As a Shepway resident you get a discount on normal commercial pest control rates. Typical fees are:  .


Rats

£49 per treatment

A treatment usually consists of three visits.

 

Mice

£49 per treatment

A treatment usually consists of three visits.

 

Fleas/ cockroaches

£80 per visit

Usually 2 visits required. This rate applies to up to 6 rooms. If you have more than 6 rooms that need treating, you'll need to agree the cost with Cannon.

 

Bedbugs

£135 per visit

Usually 3 visits required. This rate applies to up to 6 rooms. If you have more than 6 rooms that need treating, you'll need to agree the cost with Cannon.

 

Wasps and hornets

£47.00 per nest

This service is only available for nests up to a height of 4 metres.

 

Out of office hours rate

£45 per visit

Add this to the standard rate.

 

What about other pests?

  • to deal with squirrels, herring gulls, feral pigeons, rabbits, moles, bats, foxes, bees and other pests - contact Cannon Pest Control for a free survey and a competitive rate
  • some wild animals such as badgers require a licence - call DEFRA on 08459 33 55 77, or email helpline@defra.gsi.gov.uk

 

Bothered by pests from your neighbour's land or property?

Call the council on 0844 967 0722
