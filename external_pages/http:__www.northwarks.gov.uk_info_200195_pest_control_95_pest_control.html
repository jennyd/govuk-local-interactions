Pest Control - our services and advice

Bedbugs, Fleas, Rats, Mice, Cockroaches, Wasps, Moles and Bees - What do we do?

This service carries out the treatment of rats and mice, plus public health insect pests (fleas, bedbugs, cockroaches and wasps).

To request and (if necessary) pay for a service call our contact centre on 01827 715341

A fee of £30.00 including VAT will be charged for the treatment of mice in domestic premises except for those on means tested benefits*. This fee has to be paid by credit/debit card in advance of the treatment.

In addition, fees and charges proposed for other public health insect pests

Prices
   Full/Standard  Discounted*
 Bedbugs  £50.00  £25.00
 Fleas  £50.00  £25.00
 Cockroaches  £50.00  £25.00
 Wasps  £50.00  £25.00
 Carpet Beetles  £50.00  £25.00
 Moles Initial assessment is free, our domestic price is £100 inclusive of VAT for up to six visits.  For non-domestic property, call 01827 715341 or email pestcontrol@northwarks.gov.uk to enquire or for a quote.

*Please be aware that on occasions during the peak summer months the service may not have the capacity to treat all wasp requests for all residents regardless of financial means. 

Qualifying benefits are -

  • Income Support
  • Housing Benefit
  • Council Tax Benefit (not single persons discount)
  • War Disablement Pension
  • Income Based Job Seekers Allowance
  • Pension Credit
  • Working Tax Credit (household income to be less than £15,050)
  • Child Tax Credit (household income to be less than £15,050)
  • Attendance Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Industrial Injuries Benefit

We also conduct sewer baiting for rodents on behalf of Severn Trent Water Limited, and where possible we undertake commercial rodent control contracts in certain non-domestic premises.

Request a quote for a commercial [non-residential] contract by emailing pestcontrol@northwarks.gov.uk

The service provides free pest identification and advice, and gives enforcement support to the Environmental Health Divisions Commercial & Licensing Team in respect of food premises inspection. Through this service, the Council also meets its responsibilities for enforcement of the Prevention of Damage by Pests Act 1949 and relevant parts of Public Health Act 1936.

The service does not currently treat the following:

Ants, cats, foxes, rabbits, bats, slugs, squirrels, pigeons, woodlice
