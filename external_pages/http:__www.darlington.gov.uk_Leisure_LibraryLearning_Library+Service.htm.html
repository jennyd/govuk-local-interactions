OneClickDigital

Welcome to OneClickdigital, the eBook and eAudioBook service from Darlington Libraries.

Access best selling e-Books & e-Audiobooks on your computer, MP3 player, tablet or mobile phone for free!

  • Simple 'One Click' download process
  • Compatible with iPhone, iPad, Android phones / tablets, Kindle Fire & PCs
  • Available 24 hours a day
  • FREE to Darlington Library members
  • Register and use the service from your home
  • Large number of titles to choose from

How to create a OneClick digital account

You will need to be a member of Darlington Libraries to access the service and then register and create an account with One Click Digital. 

Simply go to the OneClickdigital website [external link] You will need to enter your library card number and make sure you fill in all the boxes. You will be asked to create a username and password which you will use to login in the future. Once you have created your account, you will just need your username and password for each visit after that.

It is best to install Media Manager when you create your account, although it is still possible to download the software without it. You can change this option at any time by going to 'My Account' and then 'Preferences'.

Getting help and ​support

There is plenty of help available within the site, just click on the Help tab at the top. Use the FAQs first to see if your problem is covered there.

If you have technical questions, please contact the UK based support by email at: oneclickdigital@wfhowes.co.uk

OneClickdigital user guide

e-Book compatibility

e-Books may be downloaded onto e-Reader devices running Adobe Digital Editions.  Our e-Books are not currently compatible with the Amazon Kindle, however, they are compatible with the Amazon Kindle Fire and FireHD through the android app available in the Amazon App store.
