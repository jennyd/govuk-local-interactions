Starting School    Open / Close

A list of Portsmouth schools converting to academies can be found on the Department for Education website.

Check the school in your catchment area.

Please refer to our information booklet below.

By law, children must begin school at the start of the term following their fifth birthday. In Portsmouth, most children will start school when they are four years old. To start school in  September 2016, your child must be born on or between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012.

  • applications opened on Tuesday 3 November 2015
  • the closing date for applications was Friday 15 January 2016
  • offer date is Monday 18 April 2016
  • Applications cannot be made online after Friday 15 January 2016
  • Paper forms can be downloaded from the documents section below after 9 November 2015
    • Paper applications received after closing date will be processed after on time applications have been considered and allocated places
    • Paper forms can be downloaded from the documents section below or obtained from the Civic Offices or Portsmouth infant and primary schools

Transfer to Junior school      Open / Close

A list of Portsmouth schools converting to academies can be found on the Department for Education website.

Check the school in your catchment area.

Please refer to our information booklet below.

In late October/early November if your child is currently in Year 2  at an infant school, you will receive an application letter advising you how to apply for a place at a junior school. If you do not receive an application letter, please contact your child's school - or us.

  • applications opened on Tuesday 3 November 2015
  • the closing date for applications was Friday 15 January 2016
  • offer date is Monday 18 April 2016
  • Applications cannot be made online after Friday 15 January 2016
  • Paper forms can be downloaded from the documents section below after 9 November 2015
  • Paper applications received after closing date will be processed after on time applications have been considered and allocated places
  • Paper forms can be downloaded from the documents section below or obtained from the Civic Offices or Portsmouth infant schools

In-year transfers      Open / Close

A list of Portsmouth schools converting to academies can be found on the Department for Education website.

In-Year applications are for applying for a school place in Portsmouth for entry to any year group at other times of the school year (i.e. not starting school, junior transfer or secondary transfer in September 2016). Our in-year primary application form and in-year secondary application form - can be found below.  You cannot apply online.

Please note if you wish to apply for Lyndhurst Junior Academy, Charter Academy or St Edmund's Roman Catholic Secondary School you will need to apply directly to the schools using their own application forms.

The school admissions policies for the academic year 2015 - 2016 apply, which can also be found below.

Appeals     Open / Close

If we are unable to offer you a place at your preferred school, you have the right to appeal - follow the link for information on the appeals process.
