Garden Maintenance

Your responsibilities in maintaining your council garden

Keeping your Garden Tidy

Tenants should ensure that their garden(s) are tidy and free from excessive weeds and litter. If your garden is overgrown or untidy we will contact you to find out why you are not maintaining your garden.

You cannot remove, alter, replace or plant any boundary hedge or fence at your property without our permission in writing. All garden rubbish must be disposed of correctly.

If there is no particular reason why you cannot maintain your garden, we will encourage you to resolve the problem yourself or with help from family and friends. We will usually agree a timeframe for you to carry out the work and will check on progress until the problem is resolved. If you do not sort out the problem within a reasonable period of time we may consider taking legal action to resolve the problem. We check on the condition of gardens periodically.
