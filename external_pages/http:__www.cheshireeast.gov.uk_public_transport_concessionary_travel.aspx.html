Cheshire East Council will offer the concessions listed below for the financial year 2014 / 2016 to permanent residents of the borough:

  • English National Bus Pass - Free 
  • Senior Railcard - £24 if permanent resident of Cheshire East Council.
  • Disabled Person's Railcard - £13 if permanent resident of Cheshire East Council.
  • Cheshire East Blue Badge Scheme

Cheshire East bus pass renewals

If your bus pass is due to expire you will receive a letter to your home address approximately 4 to 6 weeks before your pass is due to expire, the letter will contain details on how you can renew your pass, options will vary depending on the pass type and the existing information we hold.

If you have received a letter with a unique renewal code and are in possession of your current bus pass then the quickest way to renew is online.

How to renew your pass online (letter received with renewal code):

Go to the Bus Pass Renewal Website

  1. Click on the Renew Bus Pass link
  2. Make sure you have your renewal letter and current bus pass to hand. Click Start Renewal.
  3. Follow the instructions on screen and enter your renewal code, the last four digits of your bus pass and your email address. Once you have entered this information click on the Enter Details button.
  4. If your renewal has been successful you will now see a confirmation screen.

You can also renew by phone. Call 0300 123 5029 (local rate) open 10am to 6pm Monday to Friday and 10am to 4pm Saturday.

Disabled bus pass renewals

If you have a disabled bus pass you will receive a letter 4 to 6 weeks before your pass expires. You will need to fill in the enclosed renewal form and send it back in the enclosed self addressed envelope with supporting evidence or a GP declaration as detailed by the evidence sheet.

Your new bus pass will be posted to your home address and should arrive within 21 working days from when we receive the above documentation from you.

Incorrect name or date of birth details

When using the online renewal service, if you notice an error with your name and/or date of birth you need to call the renewal number above. The agent will take your details and arrange for you to be called back to verify your details (this process is only for correcting typing errors). In some cases you may need to visit an office to show proof of the change.

If your name change is due to marriage / divorce you will need to go in person to one of the offices listed below with evidence of the change (marriage / divorce certificate etc).

Address changes

If you have moved home since your pass was issued you will need to update your details so we have the correct address to send the renewal letter and pass to.

Address changes can only be made in person at one of the offices listed below with a Council Tax, Utility bill etc as evidence of the change.

Lost or Damaged Cards

If you have lost your pass you will need to re-apply using the Replacement Bus Pass process.
