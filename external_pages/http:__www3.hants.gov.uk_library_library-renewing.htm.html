Renew a book or other loaned item

If you have a book or other item on loan it can be renewed online, by phoning 0300 555 1387, or at any library branch:

  • you can also reserve books online

  • you can renew a book for 4 weeks at a time

  • you can not renew any items already reserved by someone else, Fast Back items, or magazines

  • you must pay fines owed (if over £8) before you can renew, reserve, or borrow an item

  • you can renew music, games, and films up to 2 times - hire charges apply

  • other items can be renewed up to 5 times
