Nursery Schools

There are 17 primary schools with nursery units in the borough.

Read the Richmond Nursery Education Booklet 2016/17(pdf, 586KB) to find out all you need to know about the options available, and applying for your place.

  • Find your nearest nursery schools

Applications

Please contact your chosen nursery school(s) to enquire about their application process.

All nursery applications are handled directly by the schools so if you wish to apply for more than one nursery you will need to fill out a separate application for each nursery.

Applications for September 2016 close on Friday 11 March 2016. Late applications may be accepted if your chosen school still has some places, please contact them for more information or details about their waiting list.

Contact details can be found by following the links below or for more information please see the Richmond Nursery Education Booklet 2016/17(pdf, 586KB).

Windham Nursery School

Windham Nursery is part of the Windham Croft Centre for children, providing children's centre activities term-time and housing services dedicated to working with the families of children with disabilities.
