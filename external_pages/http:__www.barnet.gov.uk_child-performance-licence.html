Child Employment and Licensing

  • Schools and education

Here you will find links to information and relevant forms on Child Performance Licensing, Chaperone Licensing and Child Employment.  Please read the descriptions below and select the correct link for what you require.

Child Performance Licensing Form

This form is for applicants or parents who require a Child Performance Licence for a child or young person to be able to work in Film, Television, Theatre or Modelling

Chaperone Licence Application Form

This form is for any prospective applicants who want to apply to become a registered chaperone.

Work Permit Application Form

This form is for an employer who wants to employ a young person or a young person who wants to get a part time job.
