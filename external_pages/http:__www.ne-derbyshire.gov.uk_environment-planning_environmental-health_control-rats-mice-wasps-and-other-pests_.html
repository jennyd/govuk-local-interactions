Pest Control - getting rid of rats, mice, wasps and other pests in North East Derbyshire!

Our reliable, experienced team can help you with pest problems in your home or business.

We can treat:

 

  • Ants - £45.00

  • Bed Bugs - £65.00

  • Bees - £55.00

  • Cockroaches - £65.00

  • Fleas - £45.00

  • Mice - £41.00

  • Rats - £41.00

  • Wasps - £45.00

  • Other Insects - £45.00 (for example silverfish, carpet beetles, grain weevils, cluster flies - contact us for more details)

  • Fumigation of empty properties - £65.00

 

These charges relate to domestic properties.

 

Non-residential premises are charged at £55.00 per treatment.

    

We will treat bees, wasps, mice and rats without charge on public land when the infestation causes a threat to public health.

 

Prices include VAT and are per treatment for non-rodents, for rodents, the fee covers two treatment visits and a visit to remove bait boxes. We offer a discount for householders on a low income.

 

 

We don't treat:

 

Foxes, rabbits, squirrels, moles, slugs, spiders, bats or birds.

 

We can't treat every pest problem, but are happy to give free advice on any type of infestation and can identify most types of pest. Contact us for advice, our details are below, or take a look at the Natural England website for general information.

 

 

Are you a householder on a low income?

 

We will give you a 50% discount if you are on:

 

  • Housing Benefit,

  • Council Tax Benefit,

  • Pension Credit,

  • Income Support, or

  • Income-based Job Seekers Allowance.

 

Please Note: this discount is for residential treatments only, not for businesses.

 

 

How to arrange a treatment

 

To request a visit or for general advice please phone the Council's Contact Centre on 01246 231111

 

 

Emergencies

 

Unfortunately, we don't have an out of hours service, but will prioritise urgent cases such as rats inside the house or wasps nests indoors or preventing entry to your property. Please let the advisor know if your case is urgent when you call us.

 

Businesses with insect infestations

 

We will investigate reports of infestations at businesses across the district, see our Insect Infestations page for more details.
