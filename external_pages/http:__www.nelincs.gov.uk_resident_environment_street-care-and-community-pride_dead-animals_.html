How to report dead animals on the highway

We will remove any dead animal from the highway, beach, park or open space.

We also keep a register of all dead cats & dogs that are collected by our street cleansing operatives. If you are unfortunate enough to lose your pet, please contact us.

We do not remove dead wild animals from private residences.  These can be placed within your wheeled bin.

We do not dispose of pets. For further advice and guidance on how to dispose of your deceased pet, please contact your local veterinary practice.

For more information on what to do if you find a dead bird, please see the attached file.
