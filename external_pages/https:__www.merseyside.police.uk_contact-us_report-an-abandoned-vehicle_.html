Report an abandoned vehicle

This form is specifically designed for the reporting of abandoned vehicles in the Merseyside Police area, which do not require an urgent police response.

If you are reporting a vehicle outside the Merseyside area, please contact your local force.

Reporting false information is a crime. If you are unsure please call 101 for advice. Please don't use this form to report an emergency. However, if the vehicle is in a dangerous position, on fire, has been involved in an accident, has been used in a crime or abandoned in the last two hours, please call 999.

Your completed form will be transmitted to our Contact Centre. You will receive acknowledgement of your submission from our Contact Centre staff within 2 hours of receipt. If you do not receive an acknowledgement or contact from us within 2 hours please call 101.
