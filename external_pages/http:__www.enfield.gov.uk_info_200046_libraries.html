New 24 Hour Renewals Line

From 30 November, our 24/7 renewals phone number will change to 0333 370 4700 (local call charge).

Join your Local Library today!

Joining your local library has never been easier. 17 Enfield Libraries plus a Mobile Service to join and visit across the borough. Books, DVDs, CDs, E-Audio, E-Books, Online Resources and much more for all the family.

We are also a London Libraries Consortium (LLC) member that allows you to borrow hundreds of thousands of items from over 165 libraries across London, Find out how to join here

Library opening hours

Now that Palmers Green Library has re-opened, Ridge Avenue, Winchmore Hill and Southgate Circus libraries will be reverting to their original opening hours.

Library Development Consultation

The consultation on the Library Development Plan is now closed. Further information including the final report can be found here


Online Catalogue

If you need help using our online catalogue , to browse, borrow or renew, here is a useful guide with tips for searching. Please ask a member of staff at your local library for more help.
