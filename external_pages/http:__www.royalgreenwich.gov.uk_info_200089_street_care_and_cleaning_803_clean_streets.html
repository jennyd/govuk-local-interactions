Find out when we clean a street

We sweep all residential streets at least once a week and clean town centres daily.

We also carry out a weekly sweep of streets in the borough for dog fouling.

Find out when we clean a street by using the tool below:

We also take specific steps to tackle environmental crime (crime that negatively affects the environment). Read more about how we tackle environmental crime.

What you can do

You can help to make Royal Greenwich a more pleasant place to live by:

  • taking your litter home
  • reporting build-up of street litter
  • reporting overflowing litter bins
