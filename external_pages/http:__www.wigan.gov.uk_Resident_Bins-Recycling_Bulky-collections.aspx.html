Bulky collections

Furniture donations to charities

We work proactively with a number of local charities, who collect furniture and white goods (where possible) to support local people in need. They do this free of charge. Items should be in good condition and work correctly.

Further information and contact details for these charities:

  • The Brick Homeless Project (external link)
  • Compassion in Action (external link) - collect, refurbish and re-deliver to families in crisis. A skills training centre for unemployed people is used to restore secondhand furniture.

Please note that there are also other local and national charities who provide this service.

Council bulky waste collection service

Alternatively, we offer a chargeable service for collection of bulky waste items. We would however ask you to consider if your items may be suitable for reuse, before contacting us to book a collection.

Charges

  • £10 for one collection of up to three bulky waste items per property in a 12 month period
  • One or two extra items can be added to the collection at a cost of £5 per item
  • Extra collections in a 12 month period will cost £79 each.

Where do I leave my items?

Please leave your items outside your property by 7am on the arranged collection day.

Please make sure they are:

  • dry and covered - especially any absorbent items such as carpets, mattresses and settees.
  • accessible when we arrive to collect them
  • clearly identifiable
  • only those items booked for collection - other items not described will be left.
