Waste & recycling services

You can now recycle even more in your blue bin - you can now put waxed food and drink cartons, aerosol cans, books and metal pots, pans and trays into your blue lidded recycling bin. Find out how you can now recycle even more (PDF 2.12MB) or watch the video.

Suffolk Coastal's waste and recycling services are operated by our partners Suffolk Coastal Norse (formerly Suffolk Coastal Services), a subsidiary of Norse Commercial Services.

If you have any questions about your waste and recycling services or if you would like any more information or advice, please contact Suffolk Coastal Norse on 01394 444000 or email scs@ncsgrp.co.uk.
