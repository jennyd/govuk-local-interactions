Care Act 2014

The Care Act is a significant change to care and support for adults and their carers. The Act consolidates existing adult social care law and  modernises and simplifies a complex legal system. Key areas of change from April 2015 are:

  • General responsibilities on local authorities including promoting people’s wellbeing, focusing on prevention and providing information and advice,
  • The introduction of a consistent, national eligibility criteria,
  •  New rights to support for carers, on an equivalent basis to the people they care for,
  • Legal right to a personal budget and direct payment,
  • The extension of local authority adult social care responsibility to include prisons, and
  • New responsibilities around transition, provider failure, supporting people who move between local authority areas and safeguarding

Reforms to the way that social care is funded were initially due to be effective from April 2016 the Government has decided to delay implementation of the cap on care costs system, including changes to the means test, until 2020. The Government set out its decision in a Written Ministerial Statement on 20 July 2015.

The delay will allow time to be taken to ensure that everyone is ready to introduce the new system and to look at what more can be done to support people with the costs of care.The national Care and Support Reform Team has written a briefing for contact centres and a briefing for all local authorities to explain the delays and their effects.
 

The Care Act in Milton Keynes 

The Council have, for some time, offered Deferred Payments, assessments and services for carers and access to personal budgets and direct payments.  Therefore, if you are an existing service user or carer, the Council do not expect your services to change.

The Council will continue to work with individulals to enable them to remain independent for as long as possible and to prevent and reduce the need for care and support.  The Council will be developing ways to give information and advice that you need more quickly and easily by an improved information and advice service.  

The Council has introduced a Programme Board to oversee the implementation of the Care Act in Milton Keynes.For further information please contact: CareAct@milton-keynes.gov.uk Tel: 01908 253410
