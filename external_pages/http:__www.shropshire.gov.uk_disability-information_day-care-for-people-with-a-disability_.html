Day care for people with a disability

Who is eligible for disability day care?

Day care may be offered following an assessment of your care needs.

For further information please contact us.

Bridge House

Bridge House is based in Telford and caters for adults under 65 with a range of disabilities. This is a respite and rehabilitation unit offering some alternative therapies and personal care, including bathing, physiotherapy and speech therapy. Visit the Bridge House website to find out more.

Headway

Headway is based in Shrewsbury and is specifically for adults under 65 with an acquired brain injury. The day centre has a relaxed atmosphere but offers users a structured day. Activities include cooking, computing and group games. Visit the Headway websiteÂ to find out more information.

Respite Solutions may also be able to help you.
