Advice on Housing and Homelessness

Helping people who are homeless, or at risk of becoming homeless.

 

We’re working closely with partner organisations to help people stay in their homes or find housing if they need it.

If you are homeless now, or are threatened with being made homeless within the next 28 days please contact the Housing Options team for advice as soon as possible.

Housing Advice

If you need advice on staying in your home (for example if you are having financial or relationship problems) or are 16/17 and trying to find accommodation, our Housing Options team may be able to help you.

They may also be able to help with problems such as harassment and illegal eviction or domestic violence and can put you in touch with other agencies that offer support and advice.

 

Council Properties 

If you rent a council property and are having difficulties paying your rent, please contact Rykneld Homes and ask to speak to the Financial Inclusion Team who will try to help you.  

If you are wanting to apply for a council property, why not take a look at the Choice Move website for more information?

Out Of Hours Service / Call Derbyshire

The "Out of Hours" service for homeless households in Derbyshire, formerly the Emergency Duty Team is now "Call Derbyshire". You can contact this team on:
