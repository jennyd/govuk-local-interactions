Reigate & Banstead Borough Council has offered a tried and tested Pest Control Service for many years to both residential and commercial clients.  We pride ourselves on offering a friendly, trustworthy and hassle-free service.

Why do I need a pest control service?

Millions of pounds per year across the UK are spent repairing the damage caused by pests such as rats, mice and cockroaches so acting quickly to nip potential issues in the bud could save you both money and peice of mind in the long run!  Some pests are carriers of disease harmful to livestock and humans. Many destroy and contaminate foods stuffs, gnaw at electrical cables, damage pipes and deface the fabric of buildings. They are often the cause of fire, floods and prosecution due to breaches of the legislation. Above all they can potentially threaten your health, reputation and livelihood as well.

Booking a pest treatment

Pest control online booking

By clicking on the green button above or at the bottom of the page you will be able to select the appropriate treatment option, book a convenient time for your appointment and pay for it all online. By booking with your Local Council you can be rest assured your in safe hands with our tried and tested service. Pests treated & Prices

The Council offers a treatment service for the following pests:

  • Rats
  • Mice (inside property only)
  • Squirrels (in domestic roof voids only)
  • Moles (in domestic gardens only)
  • Wasps and hornets
  • Common black ants
  • Cluster flies
  • Fleas
  • Bedbugs
  • Cockroaches

With the exception of squirrels and moles, we can treat most commercial properties too, please see the online booking site for details of how to book. Please note we can not offer treatments at this time to commercial food premises that we inspect ourselves for food safety purposes.

For information on fees please follow the link at the bottom of the page.

Wasps – important additional information

  • You need to know where the wasps are flying in and out of their nest, for insecticide application reasons.
  • Lofts will be treated only if they are fully boarded to provide safe access for the technician.
  • Providing the entrance to the nest can be seen, it is usually possible to treat up to first floor eaves level.
  • Treatment does not include removal of the nest or of old nests.
  • We do not treat bees, so please ensure that it is a wasp’s nest before booking, as refunds will not provided if a visit is made but no treatment can be carried out.

Information on pest species

For further information on pest species treated, please see our pest controllers fact sheets (Dial a Pest website).
