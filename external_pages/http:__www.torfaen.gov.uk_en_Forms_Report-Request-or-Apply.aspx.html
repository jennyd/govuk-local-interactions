Report, Request or Apply

We want to make it as easy as possible for you to access council services at a place and time that is convenient for you. The council has expanded the range of services that you can access online, helping to ensure that your request goes directly to the right people who can answer your query as quickly as possible.

So whether you want to request a new recycling box or report a pothole, you can join the growing number of Torfaen residents who are contacting us online.
