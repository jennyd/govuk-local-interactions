Secondary schools

Applying for secondary schools in 2016

If your child will turn 11 between 1 September 2015 and 31 August 2016, he or she will transfer to secondary school at the start of the new school year in September 2016. 

The closing date was 31 October 2015 and the online application service is now closed.

Applicants can still access the service to change their personal details if necessary. If you have applied online, you will receive an email on 1st March 2016, if you have applied using an application form, you will be notified of the result by letter.

Late applications

If you have not yet applied for a secondary school place, or you wish to change your mind about the preferences or the order of preferences after the closing date, you must complete a late application form.

  • Secondary school application form (late applications)

We will only look at late applications and late changes of preference after we have dealt with the applications we received on time. This means that you are less likely to get a place at the school you want. 

Please DO NOT use this form to apply for a school transfer. You must apply via the moving schools page to apply for a school transfer. 

It is important that you read the ‘Applying for a place at secondary school 2016/17’ booklet before making your application. 

If any of the schools you want your child to go to is a Wigan voluntary aided high school the governors will need extra information so that they can consider your application against their faith criteria. If you do not give this information your child may be refused a place. 

You are strongly advised to complete a supplementary information form for each voluntary aided school you name as a preference.
