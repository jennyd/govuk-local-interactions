Resident parking permits

Resident parking zones are only for the use of the residents and a valid parking permit must be displayed at all times (visitors must display an appropriate visitors permit). 

Blue badge holders may park in a resident parking zone but must display their blue badge. Motorists who park in a residents parking area without a valid pass will receive a parking ticket.

If you notice anyone ignoring any of our parking restrictions, please report it to us using the parking problems online form.

Resident only parking zones

Warrington has the following areas designated as parking zones:

  • Bank Quay area
  • Heathfield area
  • Tesco/Stadium area
  • Whitecross area

View the full list of streets within the resident parking schemes [pdf]. 

The resident parking permit terms and conditions must be followed.

Apply for a resident parking permit 

To complete the online form for a resident parking permit, visit or parking permit or a permit renewal you will be required to upload the following documents.

Proof of address

You must provide one document from the list below, dated within the last three months:

  • A utility bill (Electricity, Gas, Water, Landline Telephone, Council Tax).
  • A formal Tenancy Agreement, or, a Housing Association Rent document, or, a Property Purchase document, or, a Lease Agreement document.
  • A personal Bank or Building Society Statement, or, a Credit Card Statement.

Proof of vehicle ownership

You must provide one document from the list below:

  • A Vehicle Registration Document (V5C) showing your full name and address.
  • A valid Motor Insurance Certificate and Schedule, showing your full name and address and your Vehicle Registration Mark.
  • For a Company vehicle we will accept a typed letter (on official company letter-headed paper) confirming that you are the sole driver, and showing your full name and address and Vehicle Registration Mark. 

Please note If you live within the Bank Quay permit zone there is no need to apply for a visitors permit as the area is provided with 90 minute waiting bays.

All visitor permits (excluding the Bank Quay area) must remain with the property if you move.

Renewals

Resident parking permits are valid for 12 months, and so must be renewed each year.  You can do this online using the parking permits online form. 

Replacements

If your pass has been lost or stolen, you can use the parking permits online form to request a replacement. 

If you change your vehicle

If you change your vehicle you must apply for a new permit.  You can do this using the parking permit online form.
