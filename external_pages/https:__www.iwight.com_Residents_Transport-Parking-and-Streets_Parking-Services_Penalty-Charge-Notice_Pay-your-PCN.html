Pay your PCN

A Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) is issued at the cost of £70 or £50. Payment of £35 or £25 will be accepted if payment is received within 14 days from the date of issue (depending on the level of contravention).

Payment Options:

Automated telephone service: This system is not working at present.  Our supplier is aware and we are hoping that it will be up and running again soon. 

Internet: To pay your penalty Charge Notice online, please click here.  Please note, the balance will not be available until at least one working day after the PCN was issued.  When entering your details, you need to quote the 8 digit reference including the prefix IW and also the registration number without any gaps (eg Penalty Charge Notice IW12345678, vehicle registration ABC1234) .

Online Banking: To pay your Penalty Charge Notice via online banking, you will need the Councils details:  Sort Code: 54-10-34, Account Number 47672552.  Please ensure that you quote the 8 digit number with the prefix PCNIW as without we will not be able to allocate the payment correctly.

Post: Cheques/postal orders should be made payable to Isle of Wight Council and sent by recorded delivery to Civic Centre, Broadway, Sandown, Isle of Wight PO36 9EA.

Please quote the PCN number and your vehicle registration number for all payments.

Receipts will only be sent if a stamped address envelope is provided.

It is the responsibility of the payer to ensure that payment is received within the appropriate time. The council will not accept responsibility for undelivered or delayed payment.
