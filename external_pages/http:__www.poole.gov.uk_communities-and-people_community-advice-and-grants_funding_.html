Funding

For community groups

For funding searches use the Poole Open4 Community database.
Poole CVS can provide voluntary, community and faith groups with more specific advice and help to write bids.  They may also be able to help identify specific funds that may be open to your group and its activities.

Dorset Community Foundation give out small grants to local organisations. You may want to sign up to their grant alerts.

For businesses

For funding searches use the Poole Open4 Business database.
For further information about funding for businesses our Economic Development Team pages.

For individuals

For individuals that are facing hardship the Turn2us website may help.
For local debt advice contact Poole Citizens Advice Bureau, or you may find the information you need at www.adviceguide.org.uk.
 

Local giving

If you would like to give money to support local organisations in Poole and the surrounding area please talk to our partners Dorset Community Foundation.
