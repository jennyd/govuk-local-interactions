Library News

Dinosaur Fun at Tameside Libraries
  • Come to the library this half-term and join us for a fun free holiday activity with a Dinosaur theme. We’ll have crafts, a game and a treasure hunt. For children aged 4+ and their parents and carers. No need to book, just drop-in on the day.
Where there’s a will, there’s a way!
  • On Wednesday 10 February from 10 – 11.30am. Dukinfield Lively Library Club for over 55s and the visually impaired will be having a fascinating talk covering the history of wills and how to write a modern will for modern families. There is no need to book, just turn up on the day.

    The Club meets on the second Wednesday of every month at Dukinfield Library on Concord Way. We offer a varied programme of events and the chance to meet new people and try different things.
Starting Up: Business Finance event 
  • Business Finance - Securing funding for your business, talk by Graham Niven 

    Ever thought of running your own business? Tameside Central Library, Old Street, Ashton is hosting a free event “Business Finance” on Wednesday, February 24, 6pm to 8pm.

    For more information and to book your free place view: Starting Up: Business Finance page.
