Borrowing and Charges

What you may borrow

Library members can borrow books, audio books, language courses and toys free of charge. There are small charges for borrowing music CDs, DVDs and Blu-Rays.

Members may borrow up to 12 books/audio books, eight music CDs and four Blu-Rays/DVDs/videos to a maximum of 24 items.

The loan periods for each item are:

  • books and audio books for three weeks
  • language courses for six weeks
  • music CDs for one week
  • Blu-Rays, DVDs and videos may only be borrowed for two days or for one week.

Membership Information

  • You will need to present your Library card for:
    • Issuing
    • PC Access
    • Printing
  • Maximum number of times you may renew an item is six (unless of course the item has been reserved)
  • Membership must be renewed every three years

In addition, you can borrow from our Digital Library for downloadable audiobooks and eBooks. Please visit our Digital Library page for more information.

Library charges

General Charges

  • Reservations: the standard charge is £1.10; the charge for the over 60s is 55p
  • Overdue books per item per day (except Blu-Rays and DVDs): the standard charge is 20p; for the over 60s it is10p
  • Maximum overdue charge per item (except Blu-Rays and DVDs): the standard charge is £6; for the over 60s it is £3. There are no overdue charges for those under 16 years
  • Lost ticket replacement: the standard charge is £2. The charge for the over 60s and the under 16s is £1
  • Small Ads: A5: £2.50 per three weeks

If you lose or damage an item we must charge you for the cost of replacing it. For Blu-Rays/DVDs/videos this would be the cost of the rental edition. If an item is out-of-print or out of production, then standard charges apply to a maximum of £18.

Audio Charges (per one week loan)

  • CDs: the standard charge is 55p; for the over 60s it is 25p
  • Renewal fees are at the rates stated above
  • Overdue charges apply at the standard 20p per day per item; for the over-60s it is 10p per day per item

Spoken Word/Audio Books (per 3 week loan)

  • The standard charge is £1 per item
  • Renewal fees are at the rates stated above
  • Overdue charges apply at the standard 20p per day per item

Audio items are free-of-charge for people under 16 years, over 60 and for people registered blind or visually impaired

Blu-Rays/DVDs (maximum of 4)

  • Blockbuster Films are £3 per loan for two days
  • Feature Films and Documentaries are £1.10 per loan for one week
  • Multi-DVDs (sets of 4 or more) are £3.30 per loan for one week

If DVDs and Blu-Ray are overdue, full hire charges will apply for every additional hire period stated.

Videos (per 1-week loan)

Videos are free; overdue charges of 20p per day apply to videos only.

Photocopying/Printing/Computing Services

  • Black and white photocopies, A4 10p per page
  • Black and white photocopies, A3 20p per page
  • Colour photocopies, A4 50p per page
  • Colour photocopies, A3 £1 per page
  • Black and white printing, A4 10p per page
  • Colour printing, A4 50p per page
  • Sale of USB (2GB) £7.50

Facsimile Services

  • Outgoing UK (per sheet) £1
  • International (per sheet) £1.50
  • Incoming faxes (per sheet) £1
