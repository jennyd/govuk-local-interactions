Planning applications and decisions

Search, track, object or comment on planning applications.

Commenting on Planning Applications

Planning Online will provide you with the formal start and end dates for public consultation. However the "Comment on an application" button will be enabled until a decision is made.

Comments must be made in relation to Planning Issues only, any personal references and or vexatious comments will not be taken into account or kept on record.

In the case of a householder application, or minor commercial application, in the event of an appeal that proceeds by way of the expedited procedure, any representations made about the application will be passed to the Secretary of State and there will be no opportunity to make further representations.

We would prefer comments to be submitted during the formal consultation period. Comments can be submitted after the end date but we cannot guarantee they will be taken into account.

Building Regulation applications are for information only and comments can not be made on these applications.

Updates

Planning & Building Control Online is continuously updated throughout the day. All applications and appeal details will only be made available online once we have checked that all information needed to accept the application is complete.

Registration

If you choose to register with the service you will find additional functionality available to you. This includes tracking applications, saving searches and receiving email notification about tracked searches and new search results. You do not have to register to use this service. If you do the minimal amount of information is obtained and stored with the system, it shall not be accessed or used for any other purposes.

You can see the details of the application online at www.wolverhampton.gov.uk/planningapplications or at reception desk 24 on the second floor in the Civic Centre. You do not need an appointment.

Planning Service Disclaimer - Important Notice

Please do not rely on information from this site as an alternative to traditional searches, where completeness of information is essential.

While Wolverhampton City Council takes great care to ensure that the information provided on this site is up to date and accurate, we cannot accept any liability or responsibility should you rely on the information provided and ultimately find it to have been incorrect.

We offer a comprehensive land and property search service, details of which can be found on our Local Land Charges Page.

Where the date is shown as 01/01/1888, this is a dummy date used to complete mandatory fields within Planning Online.

If you have any reason to suspect that the information is incorrect, please contact us immediately by e-mailing planning@wolverhampton.gov.uk to enable us to investigate the matter and make any corrections as soon as reasonably practical.

Copyright Notice

Plans, drawings and material submitted to the council are protected by the copyright acts (section 47, 1988 Act). You may only use material which is downloaded and/or printed for consultation purposes, to compare current applications with previous schemes and to check whether developments have been completed in accordance with approved plans.

Further copies must not be made without the prior permission of the copyright owner, (the applicant/agent/architect and Ordnance Survey in the case of maps and site plans).

You must click the 'I Agree' link to acknowledge that you have read, understood and agree to the Planning Service Disclaimer and copyright limitations before you will be allowed to view Planning & Building Control Online.

If Planning Online does not load after clicking 'I agree', it may be because port 2707 is being blocked. Please contact your IT administrator for further assistance.
