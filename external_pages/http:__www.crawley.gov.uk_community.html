Community and Neighbourhoods

Your Town

Crawley is home to more than 100,000 people. It's a town with a growing pride that offers something for everyone.

Your Community

Get involved in your local community , through volunteering, the arts, clubs and groups and find out how the Council is working to improve your area. Our Community Safety initiatives help to make Crawley a safer place to be.

If you want to find a club, group, charity or other organisation in Crawley, take a look at our Community Directory . There are listings for all different types of groups, all based in or serving Crawley.

If you work for a local voluntary or community group, you may be able to apply for grant funding from Crawley Borough Council.

click the map

Your Neighbourhood

Crawley has 13 neighbourhoods, all with their own facilities and unique character. Find out what services are available in your neighbourhood and how you can get involved to make it a better place to live.

Each neighbourhood has a community noticeboard to advertise what’s happening locally.
