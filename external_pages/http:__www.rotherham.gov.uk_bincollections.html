Collection schedules

We provide bin collections on a two weekly cycle to most of the borough. Generally, bins will be collected in one of the weeks and recyclables in the other week. On a few streets, we may not make collections of recyclables due to local circumstances.

Special collections

Some streets have special collection arrangements, where your recycling or garden waste collections are not on the same weekday as your household waste collection.
