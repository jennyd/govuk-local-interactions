Current consultations and advice sessions

Your views are important to us. Would you like to take part in current consultations in Sedgemoor? Ongoing consultations will be listed on this page.

  • Bridgwater Town Centre and Blake Gardens Consultation

    Please can you spare a few minutes to complete a consultation on Bridgwater Town Centre and Blake Gardens? Sedgemoor District Council and Bridgwater Town Council, alongside partners, are working to complete improvements to the facilities in Bridgwater Town Centre and Blake Gardens, Bridgwater. This consultation is open until 19th February 2016.

  • Consultation Taxi Fees Increase

    Proposed Increases for Fee for the Taxi trade. Any responses must be received by 3rd March 2016

  • Sustainability Appraisal

    Sustainability Appraisal (SA) is a systematic process, aimed at appraising the social, environmental and economic effects of plans, strategies and policies and ensuring that they accord with the objectives of sustainable development. SA is required by the Planning and Compulsory Purchase Act 2004 and incorporates Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA) as required by the SEA Directive.

  • Emerging Local Plan Consultation

    Between 26th October and 18th December 2015 the Council is undertaking public consultation on the first stage of the Local Plan review.

  • Assessing Rural Housing Need in villages

    A Housing Needs Survey will highlight local demand for both affordable housing and other forms of housing. Affordable housing is for local people who are unable to rent or buy accommodation on the open market.

  • Bridgwater Northern Gateway: Development Brief and Design Principles

    Sedgemoor District Council is currently in the early stages of preparing a Development Brief and Design Principles document for land to the north of Bridgwater.

  • Public Meeting Questionnaire

    If you have attended a public meeting at Sedgemoor District Council, we are interested in hearing your feedback.
