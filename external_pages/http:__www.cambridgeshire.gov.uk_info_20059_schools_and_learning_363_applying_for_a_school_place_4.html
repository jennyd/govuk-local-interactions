Primary: Reception, junior or middle school

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012, they are due to transfer to primary school in September 2016 and you must have applied for a school place by 15 January 2016.

Reception 2016

Please note that 2 new schools are opening for September 2016:

Ermine Street Primary Academy, serving the new community of Alconbury Weald. School code 873 2037.

Bearscroft Farm Primary School, serving the Bearscroft housing development in Godmanchester. School code 873 2036.

Please note there is no automatic transfer from nursery to Reception - applications must be made in all cases.

Junior/Middle 2016

Please note that you will only be able to apply for Junior schools in the Junior transfer group, and for Middle schools in the Middle school transfer group.

If you wish to apply for a place in Year 3/5 in a Primary School for September 2016, you will need to apply on an In Year form in May 2016.

Timetable

16 November 2015 to 15 January 2016

  • Read First Steps: How to apply for a primary school place (pdf, 4.4mb) and the Addendum (pdf, 11kb). This booklet guides you through the process and explain how places are allocated if there are more applications received than places available.
  • Apply for a place online (Opens Monday 16 November 2015).

View allocation information for previous years and Cambridge City primary schools admissions information

15 January 2016 - deadline for applications

  • All paper applications must be received by 5pm on Friday 15 January 2016.  Online applications must be completed by 11:59pm.
  • If you apply after this time your application will not be considered until the second round of allocations on Thursday 19 May 2016.

18 April 2016 - Offer date

  • Email confirmation of offered places will be sent to those parents who applied online and requested it on Monday 18 April 2016.
  • Written confirmation of offered places will be posted to all applicants, via second class post, on Monday 18 April 2016.

April 2016

  • Places declined by parents are re-allocated to children currently waiting on the school reserve lists.

19 May 2016 - Second offer date

  • Offers will be made on Thursday 19 May 2016 for applications received between Saturday 16 January and Saturday 30 April 2016.

17 May 2016 - Appeal deadline

  • Parents wishing to submit an appeal must do so by Tuesday 17 May 2016.  Appeals will be heard by Friday 22 July 2016.*
    (*Please note this is a correction to the date published in the Determined Co-ordinated Admissions)

June 2016

  • Primary admission appeals take place.

September 2016

  • Your child starts primary school.

Supplementary Information Forms (SIFs)

If you are applying to certain schools for religious reasons you will need to submit an appropriate Supplementary Information Form (SIF) to the school by the deadline.

Special educational needs

If your child has had extra support in their nursery, pre-school or early years setting but does not have a Statement of Special Educational Needs / Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan (from September 2014), you will need to follow the primary school admissions process. This is also the case if they are undergoing assessment.

If your child has a Statement of Special Educational Needs / Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan, your SEN Casework Officer from the Statutory Assessment and Resources team (START) will contact you to explain the application process for your child.

For more information view our pages on special educational needs.

Additional information

For all other applications outside of the usual admission rounds, including admissions from abroad, see our information on in-year applications.
