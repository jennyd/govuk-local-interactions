Allotments

How do I apply for an allotment?

 

You can use the online form

Email allotments@gosport.gov.uk

Telephone 023 9254 5605

Write to Community and Customer Services, Gosport Borough Council, Town Hall, Gosport. PO12 1EB.

 

  • PLEASE NOTE: YOU CAN ONLY APPLY FOR THE ALLOTMENT SITES LISTED BELOW ALONGSIDE THE WARD IN WHICH YOU LIVE.

  • PLEASE NOTE: THE WAIT LISTS FOR LEESLAND AND LEE ON THE SOLENT ARE CURRENTLY CLOSED, ALTERNATIVE SITES ON OFFER ARE BROCKHURST, CAMDEN, MIDDLECROFT, ROWNER.
