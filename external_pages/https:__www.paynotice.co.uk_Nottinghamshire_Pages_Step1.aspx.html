Pay a Penalty Charge (parking fine)

Online Payments for Notts Parking Partnership - Web Payment

You may use this system to pay an outstanding Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) using your Credit or Debit card. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Switch, Maestro, Solo or Delta.

Reference Details

Please enter your Penalty Charge Notice number and your vehicle registration in the boxes below. These are displayed on the front of your PCN and/or any correspondence you may have received.
