Report It

Report it provides information and links to report problems online.

Please select from the options below.

  • Top Items in Report It

    Change of Address Form

    Use this online form to notify services within Stockport Council of your change of address.

    Missed Collection Report

    Report a Missed Bin Collection

    Pot Hole & Tripping Hazard Report

    Use This online form to report a Pot Hole or Tripping Hazard.

    Blocked Gullies/Drains Report

    If you are concerned that a public highway gully or drain is blocked then you can report it using this online form.

    Street Light Fault

    Use this online map and form to report an issue with a street light.

    Street Cleansing Report

    Report litter, problems with litter bins, dog fouling, broken glass, public toilets and leaves or deceased animals on the footway using our online form.

    Change of Circumstance - Council Tax

    If you receive benefits, or a discount, exemption or reduction on your Council Tax, you must us tell within 21 days of any change in your circumstances which may affect your entitlement.

    Change of Circumstance - Housing Benefit

    Complete our online form if you receive benefits, or a discount, exemption or reduction on your Council Tax and there has been a change in your circumstances.

    Grit Bin Refill

    You can use this form to request a Grit bin refill.

    Gritting Request

    You can use this form to request Gritting for special circumstances e.g. funerals, planned hospital visits or a Grit bin refill.

    Traffic Light Faults

    You can report a problem online about a set of permanent traffic signals within Stockport at Transport for Greater Manchester.

    Pest Control Treatment Request

    Complete our online form to request a pest control treatment.
