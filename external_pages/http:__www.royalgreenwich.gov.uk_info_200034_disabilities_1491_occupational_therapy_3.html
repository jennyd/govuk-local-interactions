Occupational therapy

Equipment and home adaptations

View pages in this section

The Royal Borough of Greenwich has a specially trained occupational therapy team who can help you to choose, obtain and use equipment and adaptations to your home.

Getting the right equipment

We will assess your needs to determine what equipment you need. Once we have determined which equipment you need, we will loan it to you for as long as you need it. We aim to deliver your equipment within seven working days. We can advise you on fitting and using your equipment.

Home adaptations

We may recommend adaptations to your home - such as fitting support rails in the bathroom - and where appropriate make arrangements for the adaptation to be carried out.
