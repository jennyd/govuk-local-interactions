Paying my business rates

Direct debit

This is the quickest, safest and easiest way to pay your council tax to us. All you need is a bank or building society account (that allows you to pay by direct debit) and payments will be taken direct from your account each time. You also get a choice of payment dates when you choose to pay monthly by direct debit: 1st, 16th or 28th.

You can also pay weekly,fortnightly or 4-weekly if that's more convenient, where payments are taken on each Monday.

To set up a direct debit, download a direct debit mandate complete it and return it to us as soon as possible. Alternatively, you can ring us on(024) 7637 6530 or (024) 7637 6534 and set it up over the telephone.
