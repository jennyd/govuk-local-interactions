Transport and travel help

Dial-a-ride

Stratford

U BUS

U Bus is a door-to-door community transport service for all residents, whatever age, wherever you live in Stratford District, who are stuck for transport to get to local destinations on Mondays to Fridays between 0930 and 1430. U Bus’s four vehicles are fully accessible for people with mobility problems, and our drivers will be happy to help, but please do let us know if you need assistance and/or are a wheelchair user when you make your booking. The scheme is aimed at those who:

  • Have a genuine need for local transport;
  • Cannot access public transport because of mobility problems or other issues;
  • Live in an isolated location with no or infrequent levels of public transport available.

Information and a registration forms are available by phoning 01789 264491.

Once you’ve joined, book your U Bus journey by phoning the booking team on 01789 264491 on Mondays to Fridays between 9.30am and 2.30pm. Subject to availability, you can book your journey up to 24 hours before you wish to travel. You can book a journey up to seven days in advance. The earlier you book, the better chance of being given a journey to exactly match your requirements.

The service can be used for a wide variety of journey purposes – visiting friends, attending social groups or leisure activities, lifelong learning and of course, shopping.

But please note it’s not for Hospital visiting or appointments. The Volunteer Centre Medical Journey Scheme operates for such journeys. Contact VASA on 01789 296334.

Group hire for elderly passengers

Age UK Warwickshire

Available in Rugby Borough, Stratford District and Warwick District

Age UK are acutely aware of the needs of older and disabled people and transport is an important aspect in helping to alleviate social and rural isolation. In order to cater for such needs Age UK have based disabled-access minibuses across Warwickshire, including Stratford and Bidford.

The minibus fleet is driven by volunteer drivers who have been certificated to the Minibus Driver Awareness Scheme (MiDAS). The vehicles are for hire to transport older or disabled people to Day Clubs, Day/Shopping Trips, Garden Centres and Pub Lunches.

For further information contact David Glasock on 07969 393 862

Back and 4th Transport

South Warwickshire

Back and 4th’s Group Hire Service is available to community groups and voluntary organisations based within South Warwickshire. Groups are able to hire 15 or 16 seater minibuses with drivers at subsidised rates for travel to places of interest, meetings or social events.

All our vehicles are fully wheelchair accessible which allows wheelchair users to travel alongside their companions. To be eligible for our Group Hire Service your organisation must be ‘not for profit’ and fall into the following categories:

  • Disability groups,
  • Rurally Isolated groups,
  • Elderly groups.

All of our volunteer drivers have been checked by the Disclosure and Barring Service and have undergone MIDAS training.

We do not have any restrictions on how far organisations can travel. In summer months we regularly transport groups on day trips to the seaside.

If you would like a quote for a journey or would be interested in volunteering as a minibus driver please call the office on: 01789 471595

backand4th@wrccrural.org.uk

Medical transport

North Warwickshire

Beeline Transport

Beeline Community Transport are a registered charity with a team of volunteer drivers who use their own cars to provide a door-to-door transport service to hospitals, clinics and other essential health services such as doctors’ surgeries, chiropodists, dentists and opticians. This service is for district residents, or those registered with a North Warwickshire GP, who are unable to use public or private transport due to sickness or disability.

Partnership Centre
Coleshill Road
Atherstone
Warwickshire
CV9 1BN.

Telephone: 01827 717074 (9am-1pm. Answerphone at all other times.)

info@volunteercentre.org.uk

Nuneaton and Bedworth

Medi-Car Transport Service

A community transport service operated by the Volunteer Centre – Nuneaton & Bedworth, providing support to people who are unable to use volunteer transport.

Our drivers are all volunteers, using their own vehicles and giving their time for free. We provide transport to hospitals, doctors’ surgeries, clinics, dentists, opticians or any medically related journeys.

For further information contact the Volunteer Centre at:

Telephone: 024 7631 5151

admin@volcentre.org.uk

Rugby

Volunteer centre – Hospital car scheme

The Hospital Car Scheme provides journeys by volunteer drivers to:

  • Hospital Appointments,
  • Pre-op assessments,
  • Clinics,
  • Visiting – but not usually doctors appointments.

Requests can be made directly to the Volunteer Centre Rugby. 5 Days notice is usually required and the cost is estimated prior to the journey.

For full details contact the Volunteer Centre Rugby:

Stratford

Stratford-on-Avon District Volunteer Centre Community Car Scheme

The scheme is for Health-related journeys for people of all age groups that have a genuine need for transport within our District to essential appointments at Hospitals, Doctors, Dentists and Opticians plus visiting close relatives at Hospitals.

We require at least 3 days notice and have local offices as follows:

Alcester 01789 763117

Shipston 01608 663122

Southam 01926 817525 (open 10am to 12.30pm)

Stratford 01789 262886

Please ring the Stratford Office should you require further information.

Warwick

Warwick Health Transport Service

The service will help Older people, living in the Warwick District (Leamington, Warwick, Kenilworth and surrounding areas ) who can walk unaided and who need transport to medically-related appointments. This can include appointments at hospitals, GP surgeries, chiropodist, dentist etc.

For further information or to request a driver (giving one week’s notice if possible ) call 01789 472615 during the office opening hours (Monday to Thursday, 10.00am to 1.00pm).
