Brown Bins

Brown Bins

Take advantage of our Garden Waste Collection Service. For an annual charge of £42 we will supply a standard size brown wheeled bin which will be collected 26 times a year on a fortnightly basis.

Applying for a Brown Bin

Since the garden waste collection service was introduced in 2002 over 18000 householders have joined the scheme and last year we composted over 7500 tonnes of garden waste at local composting sites.

The brown bin service costs £42.00 per year and the bin is emptied every two weeks.

To join the scheme please call us on 01362 656870 to pay for your bin over the phone or alternatively you can download our application form and send this to SERCO along with your cheque payment. Your brown bin will then be delivered to you within 5 working days and you will receive a collection calendar confirming when your bin will be emptied.

Renewals

We will contact you in writing about a month before your bin is due for renewal. You can renew by debit or credit card or cheque. If you no longer require the service please advise us and we will arrange to retrieve the brown bin from your property.

Moving Home

If you move home within the Breckland area, please inform us of your new address details and take the brown bin with you when you move. If you are moving out of the Breckland area you can leave the bin at the property for the new residents or contact us to arrange retrieval.
Please note: We are unable to offer refunds or part-refunds for the garden waste collection service.

What Can and Can't go in your brown bin?

What CAN go in? What CAN'T go in?
Hedge clippings Tree trunks and fencing
Grass cuttings Soil and stones
Leaves and shrub prunings Rubble and Turf
Weeds and plants Kitchen waste (including fruit and veg peelings)
Cut flowers Flower pots
Twigs and small bracken Straw, sawdust or animal bedding

Collection Days

To ensure a problem free collection service please ensure your bin is presented at your property boundary by 6.30am on your collection day. Do not overfill your bin or leave additional garden waste next to the bin as this will not be collected.

To determine your collection day we need to know where you live.

Other: 
  • Composting

If you live in a town, please enter your town and / or street name. If you live in a village, please only enter your village name.


Our handy Garden Waste Calendar contains details of adjustments to collection dates over the Bank Holiday periods and advice on what to put in the bin.
