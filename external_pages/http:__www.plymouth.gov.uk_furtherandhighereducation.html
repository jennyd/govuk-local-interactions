Further and higher education

There are a range of sources that will provide you with information and advice on further and higher education opportunities. The following websites provide useful information from local and national networks and providers:

  • Careers South West is for you if you are a teenager and need help to sort through all the decisions you need to make. They support you by providing independent and impartial information, advice, guidance and practical help. They can also help you up to the age of 25 if you have special educational needs. Your adviser will help you find up-to-date information about careers, jobs, education courses, volunteering, training opportunities and other personal support so that you see all the options open to you and make informed choices.
  • Nextstep is for you if you are an adult looking for information and advice about learning and work.
