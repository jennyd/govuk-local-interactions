Coventry Family Information Service

Our Family Information Service is totally free, impartial and confidential. The friendly, expert team is available to talk to you about all areas of childcare: activities, education and any other care and support service that is available in Coventry.

You can:

  • search our website for information
  • use our online childcare request form
  • call our enquiry line for advice

How can the FIS help you?

We can help you with:

  • providing lists of childcare for your individual needs.
  • information about the average cost of childcare in the city and the financial help that's out there and who to contact to claim it.
  • information on quality standards and how to check if your chosen childcare provider is registered.
  • information on national and local support agencies and organisations.
  • advice and information on where to go for help and support if your child has an additional need.
  • information on leisure activities and groups.  
  • and much much more...
