allotments

Listen

The Council does not operate statutory allotments.

Enquiries for available allotments in your area should be directed to your parish council.

further information

Waiting lists are lengthening as allotments are becoming more and more over-subscribed. If you have spare land of your own or are looking for land to grow your own vegetables on then 'Landshare' may be able to help. They put people who want a small area of land in touch with those who have land available. For more information you can visit the Landshare website.
