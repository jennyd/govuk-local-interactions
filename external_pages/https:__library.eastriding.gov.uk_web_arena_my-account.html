Log In to your account to manage your interaction with the library.

To view these pages either Log In by entering your Library Card Number and PIN in the boxes to the right of the page, or if you have already completed the Log In procedure, use the links below or the navigation pane to the left to go to the relevant page.

How do I get my PIN?

PIN numbers can be issued in person at any East Riding of Yorkshire library or via email to the email address registered on your account. Alternatively, you can use the join the library form (opens in a new window) and complete your details, including your library card number.  We will then post your PIN to you and with this you can check your personal account online. For security reasons, we are unable to tell you your PIN over the telephone.
