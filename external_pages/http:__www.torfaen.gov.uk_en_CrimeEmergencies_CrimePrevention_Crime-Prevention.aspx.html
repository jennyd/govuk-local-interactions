Crime Prevention

Community Trigger

The community trigger provides an opportunity for victims of persistent anti-social behaviour to request a review of actions taken by agencies when they feel these actions have not been adequate to resolve the problem

CCTV

CCTV plays a major role in making Torfaen safer. View some frequently asked questions about CCTV

Gating Orders

Gating Orders are used as a measure for combating anti-social behaviour

Cold Calling Control Zones

Cold Calling Control Zones aim to reduce the number of victims of doorstep crime such as distraction burglary, rogue traders and bogus callers

Hate Crimes and Hate Related Incidents

Reporting issues of hate crime helps us track the extent of the problem in the local area and do the right things to make the community safer

Off-road Biking

If an off-road motor vehicle is driven around Torfaen the chances are that the law is being broken. Report incidents of off-road biking here
