Consultations

Forest of Dean Council Consultation

Consultation can be defined as a process where advice, information and opinions are sought.  The consultation we carry out will help shape our policies and services to meet the varying needs of our residents.  Everyone should have the opportunity to have their say on issues that affect them.


We are currently carrying out a six week external consultation on the Contaminated Land Inspection Strategy 2015 to 2020 for the Forest of Dean.  The document can be viewed by clicking on this Contaminated Land Strategy link.  Comments can be made in writing to Karen Toomer, Scientific Officer, Environmental Protection, Forest of Dean District Council, High Street, Coleford, Gloucestershire GL16 8HG or email karen.toomer@fdean.gov.uk  The consultation will close on 24 February 2016.
