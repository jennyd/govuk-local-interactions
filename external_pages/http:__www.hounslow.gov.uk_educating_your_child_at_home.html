Educating your child at home

Help for nursery, primary and secondary school pupils

We recognise the right for parents who wish to carry out education at home by giving full legal responsibility to the parents or carers. We also evaluate the suitability of the home provisions provided by the parents and carer.

The right to home education is conditional upon the parents providing an efficient education suitable to the age and ability of the child.

If you want to educate your child at home, please visit the websites listed under external links for more advice.
