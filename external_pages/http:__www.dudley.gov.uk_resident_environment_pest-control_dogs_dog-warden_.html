Dog Warden

The Council operates a dog warden service; providing advice regarding dog control; dog fouling and lost or stray dogs. 

The Council has a duty where possible to pick up stray dogs. If you lose or find a dog within the Borough; in the first instance you need to contact the Council.  

For further information, please contact us as per the details below.

Contact Details

  • Name Pest Control
  • Address Directorate of the Urban Environment, Lister Road Depot, Lister Road, Dudley, DY2 8JW
