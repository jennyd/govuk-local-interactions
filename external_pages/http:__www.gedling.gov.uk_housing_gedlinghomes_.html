Gedling Homes

‌Gedling Homes owns and manages all the properties that used to belong to Gedling Borough Council.‌

Please contact Gedling Homes on 0115 905 1515 for:

  • Repairs and maintenance
  • Estate management issues
  • Rent payments
  • Allocation of homes
  • Tenant involvement

‌‌‌Please contact Gedling Borough Council if you need to discuss:

            • housing benefits
            • accommodation for homeless people
            • new affordable housing development
            • regeneration
            • issues relating to private rented housing.

 

Most Gedling Homes properties are let through Homesearch. If you want to rent a social housing property ‌in Gedling, you need to register with Homesearch. You must normally have a housing need and a local connection to Gedling in order to qualify. Gedling Homes make the final decision on who to let their property to.

There are some sheltered housing flats in Mapperley, Carlton, Netherfield, Calverton that you can rent directly from Gedling Homes - please contact them to see what is available.‌

Our achievements together

In 2008, Gedling Borough Council's tenants voted overwhelmingly to transfer their homes to Gedling Homes. Gedling Homes now owns and manages all of our former council housing stock, and we work closely with them to ensure a great quality of life for people in our neighbourhoods.‌

The main reason for the stock transfer was to unlock millions of pounds of funding to deliver the Gedling Tenants Standard. This was the standard developed by tenants for their housing to meet. Gedling Homes has now completed a major programme of works and refurbishments, including double glazing, security improvements, kitchen replacements, bathroom replacements and modern, efficient heating systems.

‌ We have also completed the Public Realm Works programme together - a series of improvements to our estates and neighbourhoods. These include new landscaping, fencing and lighting, resurfacing paths and car parks, removing derelict garages and installing new play equipment.
