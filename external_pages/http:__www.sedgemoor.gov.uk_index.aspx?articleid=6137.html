Benefits

Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support are paid by your local Council to help towards the cost of your rent and Council Tax. This section will tell you how to claim help, report changes and also includes information on other benefits / financial support. Please remember that if you are applying for or are in receipt of Universal Credit you will normally receive help towards your rent payments with your Universal Credit award.
