Request a new bin

How to order

To request a new or replacement bin, bag or box:

  • Email: wasteteam@wycombe.gov.uk
  • Telephone: 01494 586 550

Residents must pay a delivery charge of £7 for each container in advance. We will arrange for the delivery when we get your payment.

The delivery charge applies to the exchange of large and small rubbish and recycling wheeled bins.

We will repair or replace any damaged bins and boxes provided for domestic use, if you are able to present the damaged bin for exchange. Failure to present the damaged bins will mean that we are unable to repair or supply a replacement. Please be aware we may charge for replacements. Contact us on 01494 586 550 or email wasteteam@wycombe.gov.uk for advice.

Your responsibilities

Please note:

  • Residents are responsible for their wheeled bins and recycling boxes
  • Where a wheeled bin is lost or stolen, residents are responsible for purchasing a replacement
  • When moving house, residents must leave any refuse and recycling containers behind
  • Developers are responsible for purchasing wheeled bins and recycling boxes for new builds

Bins for new builds

If developers choose not to purchase containers from us, they must ensure that the containers they purchase conform with the our specification.

If developers purchase their own containers, any damage, loss or wear and tear is at their own risk and we will not take responsibility for these costs.
