Grants advice service

Last updated: 24/08/2015

Is your community group or business looking for funding?

If you are a business or a community group / voluntary organisation and looking for funds, you can follow the links below to run grants searches.

To make contact with the Communities and Devolution team or for enquiries about the grant search programmes or advice about looking for grant funding please contact grants@cornwall.gov.uk

Online funding search

Please follow the link below to access the Funding Central funding search programme to undertake a grant search for your community group or community project. This service is free. If you create a registration using your email and a password you will be able to carry out as many searches as you need. 

  • Funding Central  

If you have previously used the GrantNet search engine you will no longer be able to access this. Please register with Funding Central.

If you are a business based in Cornwall please follow the link below to access the GrowthHub Business Support. This service is free and can be used as often as you need. 

  • GrowthHub

Information about the funding available to help you due to the recent severe weather conditions is available on the Emergency funding information page.

For community groups we can also refer you to other specialist advisors such as

  • Cornwall Community Rural Charity (CRCC)
  • Penwith Community Development Trust (PCDT)
  • Volunteer Cornwall
  • Community Energy Plus
  • Cornwall Voluntary Sector Forum

For local businesses we can refer you to other specialist advisors such as

  • GrowthHub
  • Cornwall Development Company
  • Cornwall Council Economic Development
  • Cornwall and Isles of Scilly Local Enterprise Partnership
  • Outset
  • Get Set For Growth

Training events and other information

Information is produced monthly when available and can be viewed as pdf documents that open in a new window. They are in date order, with the most recent at the top. (The latest information will be listed). 

  • New Community Assist including ProHelp and Free Accredited Training for community organisations.
