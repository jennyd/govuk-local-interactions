Pest Control

We no longer provides a Pest Control Service; however we have worked to provide a preferred Pest Control company.  Command Pest Control offers competitive rates and is experienced in dealing with a wide range of domestic pest problems.  

 

To book a treatment and ensure that you get the special rate we have negotiated with Command Pest Control you need to call 01553 650064. 

 

If you require information or advice on the services which they provide visit their website at http://www.commandpestcontrol.co.uk/

 

Alternatively you can contact your own Pest Controller by looking through the phone directory under Pest and Vermin Control or by searching the internet.

What is the charge for pest controllers to visit for rats or mice?

 

The preferred Pest Control company, (Command Pest Control); now charge £11.40 per visit in respect of rats and mice.

 

I’m on Council Tax Benefit - Will I have to pay?         

 

Free treatments for those on Council Tax Benefit have stopped and as a result charges will apply.

 

It’s not fair that I have to pay now and didn’t have to before.

 

Under the Government spending review, it is not possible for us to continue to subsidise treatments for those on benefits

 

Why has the Council stopped providing this treatment?

 

Under the Governments spending review, every local authority has had to look at the services it provides and reassess its priorities.

 

Although a Pest Control service was valuable to users, it can also be provided by private contractors. Unlike services such as refuse collection and street cleaning pest control is not a statutory requirement.

 

We have worked to provide a preferred pest control company, which is experienced in dealing with domestic calls and who has competitive rates to replace our Pest Control Service.

 

This is a public health issue, is the Council responsible?

 

The Council is still responsible for public health and if an issue is believed to be a danger to public health, it will still be dealt with the same as we have always done.

 

I need a re-treatment - What do I do?

 

We have stopped providing this service. You will now need to refer to Command Pest Control (preferred pest Control Company) or find another provider in the Yellow Pages.

 

I have a bait box left from my previous treatment, what should I do with it?

 

We are unable to collect the boxes. It is your choice to either dispose of it or purchase the poison you need to refill it. The preferred pest control company (Command) do offer a service to collect the bait for a one off fee.

 

I’m not paying for a rat which came from my neighbour’s property, what can I do?

 

If the rat is causing a nuisance to you, you should discuss this matter with your neighbour and try to come to an agreement regarding the payment of the treatment fee. 

 

If the problem continues, please come back to us (by contacting the Customer Information Centre 01553 616200) and your complaint will be referred to the Community Safety and Neighbourhood Nuisance team who will determine whether an investigation is required.
