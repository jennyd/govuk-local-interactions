Street cleaning services

  • Parking, roads and pavements

How we keep Barnet's streets clean

  • we mechanically sweep approximately 833 kilometres of roads
  • we aim to empty the borough's 3,000+ litter bins before they become full
  • all residential roads are swept every 6 weeks

For more information on our street cleaning service please see our  Street Cleaning Service Standards.

Town Centres

Barnet has 69 neighbourhood shopping centres, which are cleaned every day.

In all town centres we have town keepers who improve the quality of life by looking after the town centres and tackling problems such as flytipping, litter and graffiti. Town keepers talk to traders and residents to help familiarise themselves with community issues, such as vandalism, abandoned vehicles or trade waste problems. Each town keeper is responsible for their own town centre. They carry out an early morning patrol, report all damage and make arrangements for repairs to be carried out such as:

  • repairs to phone boxes
  • lamp columns
  • bus stops
  • bollards
  • other street furniture

Leaf clearance

During November and December we clear fallen leaves from the borough's roads and pavements.

Trunk roads

Transport for London is responsible for cleaning the trunk road network within the borough. We are responsible for cleaning nearside footpaths, nearside and offside channels along with central reservations, which are all swept four times per year.
