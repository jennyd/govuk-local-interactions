What is Council Tax?

Council Tax is a locally set tax that is payable on all domestic properties. It is your contribution to the many services the Council provides such as schools, roads, and rubbish collection, as well as the Police, Fire Service and many other local services. Some properties will be exempt from Council Tax.

  • For more information view the Council Tax leaflet 2015-16 (PDF, 199KB) and Cheshire East Council's Corporate Vision Booklet 2015-16 (PDF, 6MB).

Authority to Disclose Information
