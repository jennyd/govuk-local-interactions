Allotments in Elmbridge

Find an allotment

Elmbridge Allotments are spread throughout the Borough. Currently none have vacant plots. However as there are nearly 20 allotment sites in the Borough so there might be future opportunities to take up plots when they are relinquished. Please contact the Allotment Association in the area which suits you to join their waiting list.

|How do I rent an allotment?

  1. Check "|My Neighbourhood" for details of your nearest Allotment (Leisure option)
  2. Look for the contact on the Site Contact Details list below.

Cost of Hiring an allotment

Costs vary from one location to another. Please do not hesitate to contact the Allotment Associations below to find out what their rates are.
