Find your Councillor

You can find out who your local councillors are by using   My East Herts or our online map system.

You can type in your address in the top bar, and use the "Query Map" tool from the left hand tool bar.

Councillor details will show up under the "Ward Profile" table.

You can also view the alphabetical list of Your Councillors, which includes contact details.

You can also search for your local councillor details on the "Write to Them" website. (Write to Them is provided by mysociety.org)

If you know your Ward, or know which councillor you are looking for more details on, you can find a list of all councillors in our Committee and Democratic Information system.
