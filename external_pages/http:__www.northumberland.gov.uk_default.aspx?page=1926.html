Families information service Northumberland

The families’ information service is a free, impartial service providing detailed general information on a wide range of services for children and young people in Northumberland and their families. We also provide detailed advice on childcare and early year’s education provision.

Families information service

We offer information to children and young people in Northumberland who are aged 0 to 20 years (or 0 to 25 years where they have additional needs) and their families.

  • To find out more, click on the links below, contact us, or click here to view our families information service booklet to find out who we work with and what we do.
  • To find out more about childcare or early year’s education, click here.
  • To view the Northumberland Early Years Professional Development 2015/2016, please click here.

For queries about any of the services we mentioned on this page, further help or general information and advice about childcare in Northumberland, please contact us on:

  • Freephone: 0800 023 4440
  • Tel: 01670 624889
  • Email: fis@northumberland.gov.uk
We’re open from 9am to 5pm, Monday to Thursday, and 9am to 4.30pm on Fridays (closed on bank holidays).
