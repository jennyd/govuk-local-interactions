Allotments

Allotment locations

Use of allotments for home grown fruit and vegetables are now very popular and very much in demand. In this area public allotments are managed by Hinckley and Bosworth Borough Council, individual parish/town councils or by private companies.

Borough Council allotment locations:

  • Earl Shilton: Heath Court Allotments (five plots)
  • Hinckley: Hollycroft Allotments (14 plots)
  • Hinckley: Langdale Road Allotments (37 plots)
  • Hinckley: Middlefield Lane Allotments (12 plots)
  • Hinckley: Wykin Road Allotments (19 plots)
  • Markfield: Hill Hole Quarry Allotments (36 plots)

Waiting times

We currently have 65 people overall on our waiting list for all our allotment sites. Waiting time varies and often depends on the popularity of the allotment site.

Contact details

For more information on the council allotment sites or to report damage to the boundary fencing please email GreenSpaces@hinckley-bosworth.gov.uk or telephone 01455 238141.

Allotments managed by parish and town councils

For information including fees and waiting times, please contact the relevant parish and town councils:

  • Bagworth: Park Lane
  • Barlestone: Newbold Road
  • Burbage: Woodland Avenue and Blackway Meadow
  • Desford: Hunts Lane
  • Groby: Ratby Road  and Orchard Close.
  • Ratby: Taverner Drive
  • Stanton under Bardon: Main Street top and Main Street bottom
  • Thornton: two sites
