Finance funding and grants

We have a range of services available to support Gloucestershire organisations with funding, identifying funding opportunities and bid development.

External Funding

Since 1st December 2014, The Gloucestershire Funding Opportunities bulletin is produced and distributed by the Gloucestershire VCS Alliance. This bulletin is regularly updated and lists some of the key funding opportunities from Lottery distributors, Central Government and Charitable Trusts, open to Gloucestershire organisations. You can get the latest issue of the bulletin at http://www.glosvcsalliance.org.uk/external-funding-opportunities-bulletin/

Funding information for business

Gloucestershire County Council in partnership with Funding Circle launched a new loan funding scheme in 2013, to help stimulate local economic growth . For further information please see

http://www.fundingcircle.com/

County council-backed scheme injects £1.1m into local economy

Information and advice for businesses is available on the Business Zone page of the Gloucestershire Local Enterprise Partnership website.

http://www.gfirstlep.com/gfirst-LEP/Business-Zone/
