Dead animals - removal

Report a dead animal

To report a dead animal on public land:

  • contact our Waste team during office hours pn 01494 586 5500 or email wasteteam@wycombe.gov.uk
  • out of hours service (if the animal is causing a hazard) call Transport for Buckinghamshire on 01296 486 630

We are responsible for the removal of dead animals found only on public land. This includes wild animals such as badgers and foxes as well as domestic pets such as cats and dogs.

We are unable to remove dead animals from private property. This is the responsibility of the property owner.

The responsibility for removing dead animals from motorways or the Marlow bypass rests with the Highways Agency.

  • For dead animals found on motorways please call the Highways Agency on 0300 123 5000 (available 24-hours a day) or email ha_info@highways.gsi.gov.uk

What happens if it is a domestic pet?

When we remove a domestic animal such as a cat or dog, we will try to identify and inform the owner from a collar or microchip. We will store domestic animals in a freezer for a period of time while this process takes place in order to give the owner the opportunity to reclaim their pet.
