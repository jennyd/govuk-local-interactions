Forms for council tax and benefits administration

This page lists the various council tax and benefits forms available to be completed online or downloaded.

Online council tax forms

  • Apprentice or youth training trainee discount application form
  • Care home or in hospital exemption application form
  • Carers, home carers or unpaid carers discount application form
  • Direct Debit instructions
  • Disability reduction application form
  • Empty dwelling last used for charitable purposes
  • Former occupant now deceased
  • Former occupant receiving care elsewhere discount application form
  • Landlord change of occupier form
  • Moving home notification
  • Person in detention discount application form
  • Severe mental impairment discount application form
  • Single person discount application form
  • Structural or major repairs discount application form
  • Student discount application form
  • Unoccupied and unfurnished discount application form

If you need to submit a more general council tax enquiry, please use our council tax enquiry form. The form will also let you upload any supporting documents.

If you need to submit a more general housing benefit enquiry, please use our benefits enquiry form. The form will also let you upload any supporting documents.
