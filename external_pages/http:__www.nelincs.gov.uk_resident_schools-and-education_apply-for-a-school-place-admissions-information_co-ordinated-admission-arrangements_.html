There are three points in your child's life, when you have to apply for a school place. These are starting school for the first time, transferring from infant to junior (if applicable) and transferring from primary to secondary. These are known as "normal" points of admission.

There may also be a time outside of these "normal" points of admission when you may need to apply for a school place, for example due to a house move into, or within North East Lincolnshire. This is called an "in year" application.

Starting school for the first time

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012, your child will be "starting school for the first time" in the next academic year. Please read the following information:

  • Starting School for the First Time - Admissions Information

Transfer of pupils from infant to junior school

If your child is presently in Year 2 and in a North East Lincolnshire Infants School, your child will be transferring to junior school in the next academic year. Please read the following information:

  • Transferring to Junior Schools - Admissions Information

Transfer of pupils from primary to secondary school

If your child is presently in Year 6, your child will be transferring to secondary school in the next academic year. Please read the following information:

  • Transferring to Secondary School - Admissions Information

In Year Admissions

If you are moving into North East Lincolnshire or your child attends a school in North East Lincolnshire and you are requesting your child be admitted to another school. Please read the following information:

  • In Year Admissions - Admissions Information
