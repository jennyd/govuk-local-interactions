About this service

All pest treatments are carried out by our fully trained Pest Control Officer who will survey the infested area and treat as needed.

We also offer contracts for some pest control treatments which are available to give you peace of mind if you have a regular problem with pest activity.

Our pest control officer is able to identify most pests and will be happy to give you details of any strange insects you find in your home, free of charge.

For more information on any of these subjects please contact us on 01823 356337 or at pest.control@tauntondeane.gov.uk

How do I use it?


To arrange a pest control treatment, or for information on pests, contact the Pest Control Service on 01823 356337 or you can email us pest.control@tauntondeane.gov.uk. We will ask a few questions and, if needed, make an appointment for the Pest Control Officer to visit you.

What is covered?


We do treat:

Rats
Mice
Wasps

We do not treat:

Ants
Bedbugs
Bees
Carpet beetles
Case bearing clothes moths
Cluster flies
Common housefly
Fleas
House dust mites
Moles
Pigeons
Grey squirrels
Woodlice
Woodworm



If you need advice about dealing with badgers, birds, deer, foxes, mink, moles or rabbits please contact Defra Wildlife Advisory Unit on 0845 6014523 or visit the Defra website (external link).

How much does it cost?


Domestic properties:

The cost of treatments are:

Rodents - full charge £46.25 per treatment (subsidised charge £23.12)
Wasps - full charge £46.25 per treatment (subsidised charge £23.12)


We can treat one or two nests at the same visit.  Where three or more nests are found, an additional nest charge of £9.00 (full charge) or £4.50 (subsidised charge) is payable for each additional nest before treatment can commence.


A call out fee of £23.12 will be charged if a pre-arranged appointment is missed.

The subsidised charge will only apply if you or your partner are in receipt of, and can provide proof of:

 

Income Based Job Seekers Allowance

Income Support

Employment and Support Allowance Income Based (ESA)

Working tax credit

Child tax credit

Housing Benefit

Council tax reduction scheme

Pension Credit Guarantee Credit

Pension Credit Savings Credit

 


Domestic contracts are available for the treatment of rats and mice for £95.50 per year.

Commercial properties:

The cost of treatments are:

Rodents - £69 per hour plus materials plus VAT. If treatment is required a minumum of two visits will be
made.
Wasps - £53.00 plus VAT. Additional nests are charged at 20%

A call out fee will be charged if a pre-arranged appointment is missed.

Commercial contracts are available for the treatment of rats, mice and insects. Contracts are competitively priced on an individual basis using an hourly rate and material costs.

Laws and regulation


We have a duty under the Prevention of Damage by Pests Act 1949 to ensure that the district is kept free of rats and mice. We can serve notice upon the owners or occupiers of infested land requiring that they get rid of any infestation.
