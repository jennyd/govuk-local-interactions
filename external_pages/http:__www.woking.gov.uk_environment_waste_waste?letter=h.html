Hair dryers

Hair appliances such as hair dryers, hair straighteners, hair clippers and curlers can be collected as part of your kerbside WEEE collection for recycling. Please place your items out for collection alongside your refuse bin on your refuse collection week. If you live in a flat,these can be placed into your pink lidded electricals bin, or dedicated electricals collection point.

Hazardous waste

Hazardous waste such as pesticides, paint, cleaning fluids, white spirit and bleach should not be put in with your household waste, poured into drains or onto open ground or burnt on a bonfire. Instead it should be disposed of carefully at a facility equipped for processing hazardous waste.

Some types of hazardous waste can be recycled such as some batteries and oil.

Other products such as cooking oil, bleach, oven cleaners, disinfectants, pesticides and paint can be taken to the Community recycling centres at Martyr's Lane Tel. 01483 721119, Woking and Slyfield, Moorfield Road, Guildford Tel. 01483 532187. Up to six sheets of asbestos from corrugated roofs can be taken to Slyfield.

Consult your retailer on the disposal of photographic chemicals and take out-of-date or unused medicines back to your pharmacy.

If you are not sure about the safest way of disposing of an item, please contact the Council's Customer Services, on 01483 755855 or Surrey Waste Management, on 01306 748300.
