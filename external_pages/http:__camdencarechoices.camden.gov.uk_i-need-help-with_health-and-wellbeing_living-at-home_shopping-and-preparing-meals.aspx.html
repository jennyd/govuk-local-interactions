Shopping and Preparing meals

Good nutrition is important at any age but many people don’t eat properly because they experience problems preparing food or feeding themselves. There are a wide range of helpful services available.


shopping and home delivery

Some people find it difficult to get out and about to do their shopping due to disability, frailty or illness but there are alternatives.

someone to help you prepare meals

If you are unable to prepare, cook or reheat meals for yourself it is probably time you thought about getting some help.

getting to the shops (shopmobility)

If you need help getting to and from the shops, the wheelchairs and scooters offered by Camden Shopmobility could solve your mobility problems.
