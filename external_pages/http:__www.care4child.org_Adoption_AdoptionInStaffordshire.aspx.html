At Staffordshire County Council, we believe that all children have the right to a secure, stable and loving home. We are looking for adopters who can offer love and understanding and be there to support our children through childhood and beyond. 

Adoption is a way of providing a new family for children who cannot be brought up by their own parents. It's a legal procedure in which all the parental responsibility is transferred to the adopters. Our priority is to find those people who can offer children a sense of security, continuity and commitment.

Could you be who we are looking for?

Due the success of recent recruitment campaigns we are currently only looking for adopters for children aged 3+, sibling groups of 2 or 3 children, children with additional needs or from ethnic minority backgrounds.

Staffordshire has temporarily suspended recruitment for adopters only wanting to be considered for children in the 0-3 age range with no additional needs. We will continue to review the situation on a month to month basis and advise anyone who is interested in adopting this age range to come back to us in 6 months. We have also temporarily suspended recruitment for Fostering for Adoption.

If you would like to find out more about adopting with Staffordshire County Council give our enquiry line a call, visit our recruitment bus at one of our roadshows or  complete our online enquiry form and we will contact you to discuss your interest.
