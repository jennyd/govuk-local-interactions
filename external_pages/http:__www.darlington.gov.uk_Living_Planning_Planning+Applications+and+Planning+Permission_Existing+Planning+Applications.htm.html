View planning applications online

View and comment on planning applications online [external link]

With our online system you can:

  • View and comment on planning applications
  • View planning decisions
  • View relevant plans and documents
    • Documents from applications before November 2010 are not available online. These documents can be viewed at the Customer Service Centre in the Town Hall (please make an appointment if you want to meet with the case officer for a specific application)

It is available 24 hours a day and you won’t need to travel to the planning office to find out what’s happening near you!

For more information please view How to Use MyServices Planning [pdf document]. You can also contact our planning team for help on 01325 405222 or planning.enquiries@darlington.gov.uk

What if I don't have internet access or don't want to comment online?

There is a facility to use MyServices Planning on a dedicated computer that is located in the Reception Area in Customer Services in the Town Hall. You can also view planning application records in the Town Hall.

If you don't want to comment online, there are other ways you can comment on a planning application.
