Our directory includes contact details for over 850 community and voluntary groups in Brent.  From carers and faith to sports and youth clubs.

To browse a range of groups you can choose from the drop down list of subjects and the type of people you are looking for.  Alternatively for a more specific search, you can use the text search box.

It is free to register your community group or voluntary organisation if your group is either based in or serves the residents of Brent. View our full terms and conditions for joining our directory.
