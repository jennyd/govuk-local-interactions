Bulky Household Item Removal

Bulky Household Item Removal (Domestic Properties).

Bulky waste refers to items that are too large to be taken away with the normal refuse collection. This can mean items such as furniture, white goods, fridges and freezers. The department will remove large household items that the householder cannot be reasonably expected to dispose of without assistance for a charge.

We will lift

  • Furniture (Tables, suites, beds, cabinets, desks, bookcases , chairs, wardrobes )
  • White goods (Fridges, freezers, cookers, tumble dryers, microwaves etc)
  • Garden Equipment (Small Lawnmowers (not ride on), garden implements, summer seats, garden table/chairs, barbeques, bikes )
  • Miscellaneous (Small amounts of carpet and underlay (off cuts or no more than 1 rom size) )
  • WEEE^ (small electrical equipment, computers, televisions,)

^Please state when booking if your collection includes electrical items

We won't lift

  • Wood (Including sheds, fences, skirting board, architraves, gripper rods, broken up furniture, dog kennels)
  • Metal (Including iron bars, corrugated iron, garage doors, gates)
  • DIY Materials (Bricks, blocks, concrete, pipes, guttering, plasterboard, paint )
  • Fixtures & Fittings (Fireplaces, kitchens, bathroom suites, doors, radiators, storage heaters, water cylinders/tanks, heating systems, stair banisters, windows, slide robes, glass front fires)
  • Garden Waste (Grass/Hedge Cuttings, Trees)
  • Miscellaneous (Asbestos, house clearances, Sunbeds, Oil Tanks, Gas Cylinders, Car Parts, Concrete Coal Bunkers, Pianos)
  • Bage (Household refuse, gardens waste etc)

*Under the bulky scheme we do not remove garden waste.

*The Council only offer a kerbside collection of garden/organic waste in the green bin, we do not offer any further collection services for garden waste, soil or rubble.

Please select Collection Issue:
Bulky Household Waste Select Bulky Household Waste (1 to 8 items has minimum charge of £33.50.
Each extra item is £6.70)
Other type of Waste Select Other type of Waste ()

Further Information:

Payment must be received prior to the removal of the items.

* Items not covered by the above service:
These may be removed under a separate scheme at a cost negotiated on each individual case. Please contact the Environment Department of the Council for further information.

Where do I leave my items?

Items can be collected from:

  • If possible it is best to leave the items arranged for collection outside your property (e.g. front or back garden)
  • If agreed with us beforehand we can collect from inside your property (you may have to sign a disclaimer once items have been removed in case of accidental damage during the removal process) or your garage or shed, but only if you are present
