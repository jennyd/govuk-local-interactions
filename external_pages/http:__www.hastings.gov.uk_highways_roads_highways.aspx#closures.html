Highways

East Sussex County Council is responsible for all highway management across the town the exception of the following functions which will continue to be provided by Hastings Borough Council:

  • Adopted and unadopted roads and streets
  • Spillages and gulley cleaning
  • Temporary road closures
  • Road markings and signage
  • Alleys and alley gating

The Highways Call Centre number for East Sussex County Council is 0345 60 80 193.
