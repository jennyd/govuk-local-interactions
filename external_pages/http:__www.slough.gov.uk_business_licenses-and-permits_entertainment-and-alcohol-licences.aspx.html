Entertainment & alcohol licences

The Licensing Act introduced a single licence scheme for licensing premises which supply alcohol, provide regulated entertainment and late night refreshment.

Straight to...

Personal licences Authorises a specified person to supply alcohol or allow the supply of alcohol at a premise with a valid premise licence.
Premise licence Can be granted for a business supplying alcohol, entertainment or late night refreshment (on or off the premises).
Club premise certificates Authorise qualifying clubs to use club premises for qualifying club activities.
Temporary event notice You will need to apply for a permit to hold temporary events or functions.
Responsible authorities As well as our licensing unit, find other responsible authorities here.
Representations View list of recent applications received for premise licences/club premise certificates and how to comment.
