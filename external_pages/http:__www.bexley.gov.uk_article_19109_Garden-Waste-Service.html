Garden Waste Service

The garden waste service is available for the discounted price of £30* for the first year if you sign up by 31 December 2015.

The normal price for the service is £33 for the first year. The cost of the service works out as £1.32 per collection, £1.20 if you sign before 31 December 2015.

Second and subsequent bins are charged at Â£30 per bin for the first year.

Members of the garden waste service will receive:

  • A 240 litre brown bin
  • 25 fortnightly collections per year (collections are suspended over Christmas / New Year)

Garden waste memberships last for 12 months. Your memberships will start from the first Monday of the month following the bin being delivered.

How to sign up

  • Online via a debit / credit card

Delivery of garden waste bins

Demand for the new garden waste bins has been significantly above expectations with over 27,000 memberships signed up for. The garden waste bins are being delivered on a first come first served basis. The table below shows when the garden waste bins will be delivered based on when the bin was ordered. Collections

Your garden waste bin will be emptied fortnightly on the opposite week to your green refuse bin.

Please make sure your garden waste bin does not contain any food recycling. Food recycling is collected separately in a food recycling box.

Frequently asked questions

If you have any questions about the garden waste service please visit the Garden waste FAQs. If you cannot find the answer to your question please contact us.

What can I recycle?

Yes please

  • Grass cutting
  • Hedge cuttings
  • Weeds
  • Garden plants
  • Flowers
  • Dead leaves
  • Twigs
  • Fallen Fruit

No thanks

  • Food
  • Manure
  • Soil, stones, gravel and rubble
  • Fences, MDF or treated wood
  • Plastic bags
  • Dog Faeces
  • Plastic plant pots
  • Larger branches longer than 50cm or wider than 6cm in diameter

Cancellation of membership

If you are purchasing the service for the first time, you have 14 calendar days from the day after you sign up for a membership to cancel the service. Notice of which can be made online or by contacting the Garden Waste team.

You may leave the service at any time by notifying us and we will arrange to collect your bin. However if this is after 14 calendar days then unless we are in breach of any of its terms we have the right to retain all of the annual fee as a contribution towards any losses or costs that we incur as a result of your cancellation. Notice can be made online or by contacting the Garden Waste team.
