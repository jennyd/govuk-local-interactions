Conservation Areas Character Appraisals

South Lakeland District (outside of the national parks) contains 10 Conservation Areas. The following were designated between 1969 and 1993 on the basis of their "special architectural or historic interest": Kendal, Burton, Heversham, Kirkby Lonsdale, Milnthorpe, Beetham, Ulverston, Grange over Sands, Cartmel, Newlands.  Local plan policy requires development proposals to preserve or enhance the character of Conservation Areas. 

Conservation Area Character Appraisals

Character Appraisals have been prepared and adopted by us for all 10 Conservation Areas and these are available for viewing below.  Each appraisal has a main body of text and various map appendices.

Trees within Conservation Areas

If you intend to carry out work on trees in a Conservation Area you should complete a 'Notification of Intent' or 'Application to carry out work on a tree protected by a Tree Preservation Order as appropriate. Find out more about trees.
