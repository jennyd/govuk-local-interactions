Report a problem not listed on Report It form

Please use this form to report a problem that is not listed in the Report It! section. We will endeavour to get your request directed to the right department.

If you're not sure Lichfield District Council is the right organisation to help you with your problem please check our 'which council?' page before completing this form. The page includes lists of services that are provided by the different councils, including Staffordshire County Council and Parish Councils.
