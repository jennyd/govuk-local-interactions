What to do if you receive a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN)

Guidance on the procedures to follow if you receive a civil PCN in Bexley.

Challenge your PCN

If you're thinking of challenging your PCN, our interactive service will give you:

  • An on-the-spot answer to your query
  • Advice on what evidence to upload if we need more information; and
  • The likely outcome of an appeal if you decide to challenge your PCN

You can challenge your PCN, pay or find out more.

The London Borough of Bexley's civil parking enforcement contractor NSL, are authorised to issue PCNs to vehicles parked within the borough under the Traffic Management Act 2004 (TMA 04). Within the regulations set down in the TMA 04 CEOs are authorised to serve PCNs through the following methods of service.

The method of service used dictates the way in which the PCN will progress through the statutory recovery process. Please select the relevant method of service from the options on the left side of the page, or if you wish to pay the PCN please go to the bottom of the page for a list of options available to you.

Handed to motorist / fixed to vehicle

PCN was either served by being handed directly to the person considered by the Civil Enforcement Officer (CEO - formerly Parking Attendants) to be in charge of the vehicle at the time of the alleged contravention or the PCN was affixed to the vehicle (usually the windscreen, but it does not have to fixed to the windscreen to be served correctly). A PCN does not need to be placed within a bag in this borough for it to be affixed to a vehicle and served correctly (served directly).

Approved device

Evidence of alleged contraventions has been recorded through mobile CCTV camera units and if evidence is sufficient, a Penalty Charge Notice / Notice to Owner (PCN/NtO) will be served through the post on the person/s supplied by the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) as the owner of the vehicle on the date of the alleged contravention, (served through the post).

Vehicle driven away / prevented from issue / serving

A CEO started the issuing process for a PCN prior to a motorist driving the vehicle from the location, (the PCN does not need to have printed or to be printing for a PCN to be served in this way), or, the actions of the motorist prevented the CEO from either affixing the PCN to the vehicle or handing it to the motorist. A PCN/NtO will be served through the post on the person/s supplied by the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) as the owner of the vehicle on the date of the alleged contravention, (served through the post).

Pay the PCN

If you wish to make payment against a PCN please select one of the following options:

  • Online
  • By telephone - (24 hour automated line - not manned) 020 3045 5200 
  • By post - crossed cheques or postal orders only to: The London Borough of Bexley, PO Box 376, Bromley, Kent, BR1 3XJ. Please write on the reverse of the cheque the PCN number and contact details or provide a covering letter
  • In person - Only at North Heath library, Mill Road, Erith, Kent, DA8 1HW (details for the library can be found at North Heath library)
