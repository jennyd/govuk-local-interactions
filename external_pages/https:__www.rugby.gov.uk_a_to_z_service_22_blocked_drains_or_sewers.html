Blocked drains or sewers

Outline

Drains are pipes that carry waste and surface water from one or more houses. When one drain meets another it becomes a sewer. Severn Trent Water are responsible for Sewers in the borough of Rugby.

Surface water from roads and footpaths is the responsibility of Warwickshire County Council.
