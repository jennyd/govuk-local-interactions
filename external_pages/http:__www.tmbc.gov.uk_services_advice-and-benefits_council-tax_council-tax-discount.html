Council tax - discount

Last Updated: 06th November 2015

In some cases we can reduce the amount of council tax you have to pay.

  • Discounts for those living alone
  • Discounts for people who are disregarded
  • Second homes
  • Reductions for disabilities
  • Reductions for people with low incomes
  • Discount for unoccupied and unfurnished properties
  • Discount for major repairs
  • Discount for annexes

Further Information

  • Email: revenues@tmbc.gov.uk
  • Tel: 01732 876388
