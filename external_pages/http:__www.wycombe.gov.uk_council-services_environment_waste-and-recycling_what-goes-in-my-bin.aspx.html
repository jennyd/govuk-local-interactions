What goes in my bin?

Black bin→

All non-recyclable waste.

Blue bin→

Mixed recyclables: aerosols, foil, rigid plastic, cartons, glass bottles and jars, tins and drink cans.

Green box→

Paper and cardboard: books, catalogues, telephone directories, newspapers, magazines, junk mail.

Green bin→

Garden waste: leaves, grass, weeds, small branches, dead plants and flowers, twigs and prunings.

Food waste collection→

Any food waste, scraps or leftovers. Use the smaller silver caddy in your kitchen. Place brown caddy out for collection.

Pink sacks and clear bags→

Textiles: linens, shoes and clothing. Batteries: household batteries, for mobiles, laptops and power tools.
