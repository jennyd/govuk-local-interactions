Swap home with another tenant anywhere in the United Kingdom

You can swap your home with another council or housing association tenant anywhere in the United Kingdom. This is called 'mutual exchange'.

Advertise and find a home

You will need to find another tenant who wants to swap homes with you. You can advertise your home and get information on potential homes:

  • East London Lettings Company is a website that can help you find a tenant to swap with in east London. You will need to register on the East London Lettings Company website.
  • Home Swapper is a website that can help you find a tenant to swap with anywhere in the United Kingdom. It is free for secure Hackney council tenants.  You will need to register on the Home Swapper website.
  • Noticeboards/ books in our Neighbourhood Housing Offices. You will need to complete a Mutual Exchange application form (Word, 52KB) and return to us.
  • Word of mouth.
  • Notices in shop windows.

Tips for successful swaps:

  • Add photographs of the internal and external areas of your home. 
  • Include details of local amenities such as buses, trains, local shops.
  • Check the East London Lettings Company and the Home Swapper websites regularly and answer any enquiries you have received.

Apply for a swap

When you find someone to swap your home with, you will both need to ask your landlords to approve the swap.

You do not need to be registered on Hackney Council's housing list to swap homes. If you have any rent arrears, you will not be allowed to swap homes until it is paid.

You will usually keep your right to buy if you swap with another council tenant. However, you may lose this right if your swap is with a housing association tenant.
