This scheme helps people on low income with their Council Tax. You can claim Council Tax Reduction if you are working but your earnings are low or if you are receiving state benefits.

Council Tax Reduction (CTR) is subject to a means test â your income is measured against your needs and the amount of Council Tax you pay. CTR can only help towards your Council Tax.

If you are paying rent you may be able to claim Housing Benefit.

Sometimes the Department for Work and Pensions can help with mortgage interest if you are claiming a means tested benefit.
