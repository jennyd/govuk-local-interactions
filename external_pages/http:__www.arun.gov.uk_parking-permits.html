Parking Permits

 

The full terms and conditions for these permits can be found in the Conditions of Sale.pdf [pdf] 51KB. Replacements for lost permits are charged at £5.00. Purchasing a permit does not guarantee a space.

 

Town centre permit

Town centre permits are valid in St Martins, Surrey Street, Anchor Springs and Manor House car parks (Littlehampton) and London Road, Regis Centre, Hothamton and Lyon Street car parks (Bognor Regis). They are available to cover:

  • 5 days (Monday to Friday) on an annual basis for £357.44
  • 7 days on an annual basis for £408.52
  • 7 days on a monthly basis for £91.92

 

Seasonal permit 

Seasonal permits are valid in West Beach, West Green, East Green, The Wall, Sea Road, Banjo Road and Mewsbrook car parks (Littlehampton), Hotham Park, West Park, Gloucester Road, Rock Gardens and Culver Road car parks (Bognor Regis) and River Road car park (Arundel). They are available to cover:

  • Summer season (1st April – 30th September) for £71.48
  • Winter season (1st October – 31st March) for £51.06
  • On an annual basis for £122.54

 

Fitzleet permit 

The Fitzleet permit is valid on floors 3 and above of Fitzleet multi-storey car park only. It covers 7 days a week at an annual charge of £102.13. Make an application for a Fitzleet permit.

 

Fitzalan permit

The Fitzalan permit is valid in Fitzalan (pool) car park, Arundel only. It is available:

  • Annually for £250.00
  • For the winter season (1st October – 31st March) for £100.00. 

Please note that Members of the Chamber of Commerce will be eligible for a 20% discount. Please contact The Arundel & Downland Community Leisure Trust for more information.

 

Bognor Regis resident permits

Resident permits are available to those living within the controlled parking zone (CPZ) CPZ on-street parking map.pdf [pdf] 920KB in Bognor Regis who do not have adequate off-street parking. They can be used in any ‘permit holders only’ parking space within the CPZ, but not in pay and display or off-street car parks. It does not guarantee a particular space, and does not mean that a space will always be available. The permit must be displayed in the vehicle, failure to do so may result in a parking ticket (penalty charge notice) being issued. Trailers are not allowed in ‘permit holder only’ spaces. The permits are available at the following costs:

  • First permit - £20.00 for 6 months or £40.00 for 12 months
  • Additional permits - £40.00 for 6 months or £80.00 for 12 months
  • Disabled drivers may receive their permit free of charge

Please download and complete the Zone Parking Permit Application.pdf [pdf] 297KB and either send it to us at the address below, or take it to Bognor Regis Town Hall where a temporary permit can be issued for immediate use. You will need to provide the fee, proof of full time residency, proof of ownership of the vehicle and a copy of a disabled drivers (blue) badge, if applicable.

We will send you a reminder when the permit is due to expire. If none of your details have changed, you will just need to return the form Zone Parking Permit Renewal [pdf] 289KB and fee. If your information has changed, you will need to supply supporting evidence as above.

If your details change during the permit duration, do not change it yourself as this will invalidate it. Please return the permit to us, along with proof of the new information and we will reissue it to reflect the changes. If you move outside of the CPZ, you must return the permit to us and we will issue a refund for every full month remaining. Replacements for lost or stolen permits will be charged at £5.00.

 

Bognor Regis non-resident permits 

Non-resident permits are available to anyone who owns or works at a business within the CPZ CPZ on-street parking map.pdf [pdf] 920KB Please contact us on 01903 737655 To check availability as spaces are limited. Permit holders will be allocated 2-3 roads in which they will be entitled to park within a ‘permit holders only’ space, but not in pay and display or off-street car parks. It does not guarantee a particular space, and does not mean that a space will always be available. It is possible for more than one vehicle to be included on the permit on an either/or basis, but it must be displayed in the parked vehicle. Failure to do so may result in a parking ticket (penalty charge notice) being issued. Trailers are not allowed in ‘permit holder only’ spaces. The permits are available at the following costs:

  • 6 months for £125.00
  • 12 months for £250.00

The application forms are available at Bognor Regis Town Hall, where you should also take proof of business ownership or employment and the fee.

If your details change during the permit duration, do not change it yourself as this will invalidate it. Please return the permit to us, along with proof of the new information and we will reissue it to reflect the changes. If you wish to surrender we will issue a refund for every full month remaining. Replacements for lost or stolen permits will be charged at £5.00.

 

Bognor Regis visitor permits

Visitor permits are available to residents of the CPZ CPZ on-street parking map.pdf [pdf] 920KB to allow their visitors to park in a 'permit holders only' bay. They are scratch off permits which are available to purchase at:

  • £3.50 for a book of 10 x 2 hourly permits
  • £7.00 for 5 x daily permits
  • £7.00 for one weekly permit

These are available to purchase at Bognor Regis Town Hall, where you must provide proof of residency within the CPZ. 

 

Bognor Regis healthcare permits 

Healthcare permits are available to those who need to park within the CPZ CPZ on-street parking map.pdf [pdf] 920KB for the purpose of home visits to patients as part of their employment. Permits are valid in on-street ‘permit holder only’ bays and in pay and display car parks for a maximum of 2 hours. They are not valid for use in taxi, loading and unloading bays, on yellow lines or outside healthcare premises. It does not guarantee a particular space, and does not mean that a space will always be available. The permit must be displayed in the vehicle, failure to do so may result in a parking ticket (penalty charge notice) being issued. It is possible for more than one vehicle to be included on the permit on an either/or basis, but it must be displayed in the parked vehicle. Trailers are not allowed in ‘permit holder only’ spaces.

The permit is available for 12 months at the cost of £10.00.

The application forms are available at Bognor Regis Town Hall. You should provide an officially headed document from management, confirming employment with a healthcare agency and outlining the reasons home visits are necessary, and the fee.

If your details change during the permit duration, do not change it yourself as this will invalidate it. Please return the permit to us, along with proof of the new information and we will reissue it to reflect the changes. Replacements for lost or stolen permits will be charged at £5.00.
