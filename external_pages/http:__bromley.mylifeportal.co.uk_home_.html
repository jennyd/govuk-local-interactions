    Welcome to the Bromley MyLife website...

    ...here to help you make informed choices about your life

     

    The Bromley MyLife website can help you learn how to find extra support to help with your care needs by helping you - or the person you care for - see more clearly what services are available in the local area and how to access them. It pulls together support and services from birth up to old age, across social care, health, education and wider support within the community.

    The options from the page are grouped under five main headings:

    1. information for adults aged 18 and over with care and support needs (including Bromley Council's Adult Social Care Services)
    2. information for children and young people with special educational needs and disabilities (the Bromley 'Local Offer')
    3. information about Fostering and Adopting for Bromley Council
    4. information to help keep healthy and well
    5. information for people who care for someone


    We are still building, developing and enhancing many parts of the Bromley MyLife website so we are very keen to hear your views and comments on the website. You can let us know what you think, let us know if we are missing anything, or let us know if you spot an issue within the site, by completing the Site Feedback box or e-mailing mylife@bromley.gov.uk.
