Parks and Green Spaces

    Select from the list of Parks and Green Spaces

    Parks Services aim is to provide parks, sports pitches, play facilities, open spaces and allotments for community use to enhance the local environment and residents’ quality of life. 

     

    Over 90 per cent of Chelmsford’s residents visit and enjoy our parks and green spaces each year. A variety of events are held throughout the year in our local parks ranging from Heart and Sole Healthy Walks, fairs, family fun days and Play in the Park children’s activities. 

     

    We offer a wide variety of parks and gardens with beautiful planting schemes for you to enjoy. Many parks are situated within walking distance of the City and bus routes.  We offer a broad programme of activities for volunteers and there are always opportunities to get involved and support us.
