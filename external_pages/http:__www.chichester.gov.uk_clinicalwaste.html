Household clinical waste

We operate a free clinical waste collection service for domestic properties and the service is available to businesses for a fee. Collections are made weekly, fortnightly and monthly  or on an ad hoc basis. The service includes the provision of clinical waste sacks (either yellow with black stripe or orange depending on the type of waste) and yellow lidded sharps boxes (for sharps such as epi-pens and diabetic syringes).

If you require a clinical waste collection please download, print and complete the Clinical Waste Application Form. If you are not able to download the form please contact us and we will send you one.

  • Clinical Collection Application Form [pdf 89kb]

To ensure we understand the type of waste we are collecting we require your Community Nurse, GP or Consultant to indicate the type of waste being produced and to sign the form. Once we have processed your completed form we will contact you to confirm the provision of the service for you and to arrange the details of the collection. If you require an urgent collection and are not able to get your medical practitioner to complete the form beforehand please contact us and we may be able to help.
