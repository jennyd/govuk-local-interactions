Sheltered housing

Changes to the application process

New applications now go through Property Pool Plus, Merseyside's choice based lettings system, and not ACCESS Liverpool.

This means that you will be able to view and apply for sheltered housing accommodation across the five Merseyside local authority areas: Liverpool, Halton, Knowsley, Sefton and Wirral.  It also means that the process of allocating properties is more cost efficient, open and transparent.

Please note:  If you are an existing customer currently on a waiting list for a sheltered housing scheme, the list will be honoured in application date order.

How to apply

Join the Property Pool Plus housing register

You must join the Property Pool Plus housing register before you can apply for accommodation. 

Apply online via the Property Pool Plus website

  • Or download, complete and return the paper application form.

You can also ask for a paper copy from a housing association office or a One Stop Shop.

If you need help completing the application you can contact your housing association or visit your local One Stop Shop.

Find a property

Once you have registered, you can search the Property Pool Plus website for sheltered housing that meets your needs. The system will show vacant properties and allow you to express an interest or 'place a bid'.

At the moment, only sheltered housing schemes with no waiting lists are shown on Property Pool Plus. From 1st October 2012 all sheltered housing schemes will be listed along with an auto bid facility for vulnerable customers where bids can be placed on their behalf. To set up auto bid contact your housing association.

If you don't have access to the internet, or prefer not to use the online service, don't worry.  Properties will continue to be advertised each week on display boards in housing association offices and newsletters.

Applying for a property

When you have found a property you can apply or 'place a bid':

  • Online via the Property Pool Plus website.
  • By telephoning the 24 hour automated bidding line on 0300 777 3022.
  • By texting 07537 402 602.
  • Through your TV if you are a Virgin Media or Sky subscriber.

Remember, only those schemes without waiting lists are currently shown on Property Pool Plus.  The full list of sheltered housing schemes will appear on Property Pool Plus from 1st October 2012.

Any concerns or questions?

If you have any questions or would like further advice, please contact the ACCESS Liverpool team or read the Property Pool Plus FAQs.

ACCESS Liverpool is a partnership between the council and registered social landlords and deal with accessible housing in Liverpool.
