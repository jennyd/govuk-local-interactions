Things to remember about your containers

  • Put your recycling items straight in to the bin, please don’t bag them up.
  • Flatten and cut down all cardboard so that it fits inside the box/bag.
  • To prevent any garden waste sticking to the bottom of your bin you can line the base with a sheet of newspaper.
  • Don't put any carrier bags in the recycling bin.
  • Do not put light bulbs, batteries and textiles in the same bag.
  • Place your clear polythene bag on your purple top bin for collection.
  • The lid of your bin must be closed with no additional bags/waste left next to the bin.

If you are missing any bins or containers or you would like additional recycling containers you can order these by contacting us. All households are supplied with one red top bin as standard. If you are a family of six or more you can apply for an additional bin, please note that there is a charge of £25 if your request is approved.

If you have extra rubbish or recycling you can take it to one of our rubbish tips (Community Recycling Centres), use our A to Z of recycling and rubbish to what items can go to which rubbish tip.
