Free childcare for two-year-olds

If your child is two years old and you’re in receipt of certain benefits or are a working family on a low income you may be entitled to some free childcare.

  • Follow the link for information on free childcare for three and four-year-olds

Who can apply?   Open / Close

All two year-olds who meet any one of the following criteria will be eligible.

If their families receive:

  • income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • support through part 6 of the Immigration and Asylum Act
  • the guaranteed element of State Pension Credit
  • working Tax Credits and earn no more than £16,190 a year
  • child Tax Credit (but not Working Tax Credit) and have an annual income not over £16,190
  • the Working Tax Credit 4-week run on (the payment you get when you stop qualifying for Working Tax Credit)

A child will also be eligible if any of the following apply:

  • they have a current statement of special educational needs (SEN) or an education, health and care plan
  • they get Disability Living Allowance
  • they are looked after by their local authority
  • they’ve been adopted from local authority care
  • they’ve left care under a special guardianship order, child arrangements order or residence order

How much childcare is available     Open / Close

If eligible, your child will receive up to 15 hours of childcare per week, for up to 38 weeks of the year. Free childcare can be used flexibly at a variety of early education providers including nurseries or with a childminder. There are guidelines on how you can use your hours but we can discuss this if/when your child is accepted.

When could my child start?     Open / Close

If you receive a place, your child can start their free childcare the term after their 2nd birthday. Funding will continue until your child becomes eligible for free early education the term after their third birthday.

How do I apply?   Open / Close

We want to keep the application process as simple as possible and so you can self refer or ask someone working with your family to give you a hand.

Once you’ve completed the form return it to:

Two year old funding, Education Early Support,Floor 2 Core 1, Civic Offices, PO1 2EA

If you’re eligible, we’ll send you a letter to take to your chosen childcare provider – it’s as simple as that!

If you have any questions- give Portsmouth Family Information Service a call on 023 9268 8830. They can answer your questions, help you to complete the form and if you’re successful - look at childcare options with you and help you find the setting that’s perfect for your child. Alternatively, email fis@portsmouthcc.gov.uk.

If you have already been accepted   Open / Close

We can run a detailed search, for example - if you’d like a childminder who has experience working with children with special educational needs or a nursery that’s close to an older child’s school.

Follow the link to find out more or to contact Portsmouth Family Information Service.
