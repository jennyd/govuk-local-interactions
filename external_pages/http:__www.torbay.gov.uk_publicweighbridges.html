Public Weighbridges

To operate a public weighbridge in Torbay, you require a certificate of competence issued by the Chief Inspector for Weights and Measures from Torbay Council.

Part III of the Weights and Measures Act 1985

The Weights & Measures Act 1985 requires that operators of Public Weighing and Measuring Equipment made available for use by the public and for which a charge is made are certificated. The certificate is issued by the Chief Inspector of Weights & Measures for the authority. It is only issued when the Chief Inspector is satisfied that the operator has sufficient knowledge for the proper performance of his duties. Visit the OPSI - Weights and Measures Act 1985  website for a summary of eligibility for licence
It is in the public interest that Torbay Council must process your application before it can be granted. If you have not heard from the local authority within one week please contact Trading Standards.

Failed Application

If your application failed please contact Torbay Council in the first instance. You may appeal to the Secretary of State if your application is refused.
