Dorset County Council's planning application search:

Dorset County Council is responsible for determining applications for minerals and waste development, as well as county council developments, such as schools. All other applications are dealt with by the relevant district or borough council; Christchurch, East Dorset, North Dorset, Purbeck, West Dorset and Weymouth & Portland.

You can also search Dorset County Council's planning applications.
