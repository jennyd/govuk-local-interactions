Day centres for older adults

Day centres offer a range of activities for older adults living alone, with their families or carers. Going to a day centre can:

  • help prevent social isolation
  • help prevent you feeling lonely
  • help you meet new people
  • help you remain as independent and socially active as possible
  • provides a break for your carer

Day centres in Thurrock

We have 5 day centres in Thurrock to work with:

  • people with dementia
  • people with mild memory loss

Thurrock Lifestyle Solutions

Thurrock Lifestyle Solutions provides day centre activities for disabled adults in Thurrock.
