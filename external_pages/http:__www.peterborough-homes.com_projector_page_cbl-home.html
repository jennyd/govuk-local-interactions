Home

Welcome to the Peterborough Homes Choice Based Lettings website.

This displays all the properties that are available for the current week. You can bid for 3 properties per week from Tuesday to Sunday at midnight by clicking on the Log On button on the left hand side of the screen.

You can search for properties by the area in which you would like to live, by the type of property you require or look at all the properties listed by using one of the 3 buttons below.

!!Important Information!!

If you are experiencing difficulty in bidding, it may be that you are not eligible to bid on a property of that size. You can find more information on your bedroom entitlement at the link below.

Bedroom standards policy

Please ensure that we have the correct telephone number on your application or we may not be able to contact you and therefore will be unable to offer you a viewing. If your number has changed please advise us as soon as possible by emailing housing.needs@peterborough.gov.uk

Please be respectful that some properties advertised are still occupied, please therefore wait until your allocated viewing date in order to be shown around.

Please note if you use a Firefox web browser this is not compatible with this website
