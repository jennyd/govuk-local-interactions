Housing Options Wizard

Introduction

Welcome to the Housing Options wizard.

This tool will ask a series of questions and then guide you through the different options you can explore to enhance your housing situation.

Why not just wait for a Social Housing tenancy

Social Housing is in great demand and often people can wait a long time before being offered a tenancy. Housing options are the alternatives to Social Housing that may make it easy and quicker for you to move house.

What housing options are available?

There are lots of different options available but they are not suitable for everyone. Using this wizard you will be able to see which options are suitable for you. Some of the available options include:
  • Shared Ownership
  • Mutual Exchange
  • Social Housing
  • Private Rented
  • Mortgage rescue
  • Sheltered housing
  • Supported housing
  • Overcrowded or under occupied assistance
  • Support services
  • Mortgage advice
  • Foyer
  • Floating support
  • Improvement agency
  • Housing advice
  • Money or Benefits advice
  • Refuge
  • Night Shelters
