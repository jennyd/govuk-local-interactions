Reserve or Request an item

Reserving an item found on the catalogue

You can reserve items found on the catalogue using your library card number and PIN.

Key Points
  • There are no charges for reserving items
  • You can reserve items on loan to other borrowers and those showing as 'available'
  • We will inform you when items are available for collection
  • You would normally have 10 days in which to pick up your item


  • Click Library Catalogue
  • Search for the item you want
  • Click on the title and select Place Reservation (the Place Reservation button will only appear if the item can be reserved)
  • Type in your library card number and PIN (if you have not already logged into your account)
  • Choose the library from which to pick up the item
  • Add any notes and the date by which you require the item
  • Details of your reservation will be displayed and include your ranking on the waiting list (if there is one)

    • Requesting an item not found on the library catalogue

    If you cannot find the item you are looking for on our library catalogue, we can usually obtain it for you from another library via our inter-library loans service.

  • DVDs and music CDs cannot be requested using this service. Email allison.kirby@southampton.gov.uk with suggestions for titles not in stock
