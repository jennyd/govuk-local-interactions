Latest News

Housing Benefit and Local Council Tax Support Surgery - Every Tuesday between 1pm and 5pm there is an experienced advisor at Great Dunmow library to assist with any queries regarding help with your rent and/or Council Tax. You do not need an appointment to use this service, just turn up and you will be able to speak to a specialist in private.

Local Council Tax Support (LCTS) - Council Tax benefit ceased on 31 March 2013. The new scheme is called LCTS and it is designed and run locally by individual councils. For more information please visit our page on Local Council Tax Support. You can also find out about how the scheme was agreed and how the results of the consultation were presented to the council on the LCTS Scheme page.
