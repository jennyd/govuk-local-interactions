How to report a repair

Your responsive repairs are delivered by our transformed repairs service which has been designed with the help of our tenants using your feedback.

Report a repair

  • Online using our Housing Repair Request Form
  • By telephone on 0300 123 6633 
  • By SMS text to 0778 620 0802. Text FIXIT followed by your name, address and a description of your repair
  • In person at your local housing office
  • In writing to your local housing office
  • By Typetalk - We welcome calls through Typetalk. Textphone users please prefix our telephone number with 18001

Our reporting team will talk through the repair requirements with you, and will then use our repairs diagnostic system to make sure an appointment is made for the correct service with the right time to carry out the work.

Emergency repairs

If a repair is an immediate risk to people or a property it can be reported using the 24 hour contact number 0300 123 6633.

This service is for emergencies only so please only use it for genuine emergency repairs. If we find that the work was not actually an emergency you may be charged.

Repair reference number and text alerts 

For each repair reported, a reference number is given.

You will then receive up to three SMS text messages, the first as a receipt within 24hours of raising the repair, the second as a reminder on the day before the repairs appointment (for 3 and 20 day appointments only) and the third as the operative is on the way.

Customer communication

A 'Whats Next' card will be given to you if further repairs or assessments are needed. The card will explain what is needed to complete the repair and also offer contact information for the relevant officers or services.

We will review our reporting methods to make sure that they reliable and easy to access for tenants.

Repair appointments

South Tyneside Homes will offer tenants an appointment for all works apart from emergency repairs. Appointments are provided during:

  • 8am - 8pm Monday to Friday (excluding bank holidays)
  • 9am - 1pm on Saturdays (excluding bank holiday weekends)

If we need to visit a number of times we will notify you on the first visit and discuss future access arrangements.

Also if further repair work is reported whilst we are attending to a repair on site, we will try and resolve the issue on the same visit.

Missed appointments cost South Tyneside Homes in terms of time and money. If tenants miss a pre-agreed appointment the job will be cancelled, a card will be left informing the cancellation and that the repair will need to be reported again to South Tyneside Homes.

A charge may be issued.

If it's an emergency appointment and you aren't available it may be necessary to force access. Again, additional costs may be charged.
