PEST CONTROL

For some District Council areas support is given for treatment of specific pests.  Please see below for the qualifying criteria for the support and which pests the service applies to.

If you are unsure which pest you are dealing with please take the advice of the pest control specialist before they visit.  Any resident calling out a contractor for a pest qualifying for free treatment that turn out to be something different which is chargeable will be expected to pay the contractors charges.  

Bromsgrove District Council Pest Control

Free treatment for those in receipt of

• Employment & Support Allowance (ESA)
• Full Housing Benefit
• 100% Council Tax Support

Person requesting treatment will need to show proof of entitlement before treatment is undertaken.

Treatment available for the following pests only - Rats, Mice, Fleas, Bed Bugs, Cockroaches & Wasps Nests.

 

Redditch Borough Council Pest Control 

Free treatment for those in receipt of

• Employment & Support Allowance (ESA)
• Full Housing Benefit
• 80% Council Tax Support

Person requesting treatment will need to show proof of entitlement before treatment is undertaken.

Treatment available for the following pests only - Rats, Mice, Fleas, Bed Bugs, Cockroaches & Wasps Nests.

NOTE: RAT TREATMENT IS FREE TO ALL RESIDENTS WHETHER ON BENEFITS OR NOT.

 

Wychavon District Council Pest Control

Free treatment for the following pests only is available to Wychavon residents on qualifying benefits - rats, mice, fleas, cockroaches, bedbugs and wasps. The qualifying benefits are: 

  • Income Support
    • Income Related Employment & Support Allowance
    • Job Seekers Allowance
    • Disability Allowance
    • Attendance Allowance
    • Housing Benefit (must be paid 100%)
    • Council Tax Benefit
    • Pension Guarantee Credits

The person requesting treatment will need to show proof of entitlement before treatment is undertaken.

 

Wyre Forest District Council Pest Control 

Free treatment for rats, mice, fleas, cockroaches, bedbugs and wasps is provided for the following residential benefit holders:

• Income Support
• Job Seekers Allowance
• Employment & Support Allowance
• Disability Allowance
• Attendance Allowance
• Housing Benefit, (must be paid 100%).
• Council Tax Benefit.

Person requesting treatment will need to show proof of entitlement before treatment is undertaken.

 

Malvern Hills & Worcester City Council areas

There is no service for pest control provided by Malvern Hills District Council or Worcester City Council. The contractors listed below will offer services to treat your pests but you should request detail of the applicable charges.
