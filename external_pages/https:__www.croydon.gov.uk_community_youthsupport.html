Youth support

Youth support services & information

  • Borough-wide youth projects

    Croydon Integrated Youth Support Service provides young people aged 13-19 with something to do, somewhere to go and someone to talk to.

  • Youth support service

    Providing young people, with opportunities, to have a bright future.

  • Croydon WorkCard

    If you're a young person who lives in the borough, tell us your views on Croydon WorkCard aimed to make work experience more flexible and rewarding.

  • Turnaround Centre

    Croydon's Turnaround Centre offers young people a one-stop-shop to a wide range of services where they can drop-in for advice and support in a safe and secure environment.

  • Voluntary and community sector youth organisations

    A list of some of the voluntary organisations that work in partnership with the youth service.

  • Youth hubs

    Youth hubs in Croydon providing positive out of school activities

  • Youth offending service

    Supervision, advice and support to those who have been identified by the police or courts as young offenders and their families.
