Wheeled Bin Garden Waste Collection

Page last updated at 30 December 2015 at 10:14

Join The Council’s Garden Waste Collection Service 

Join with thousands of borough residents and benefit from a fortnightly collection of your garden waste from your kerbside. 

This recycling service provides a great value for money collection of your garden waste every fortnight direct from your kerbside.  There is also a money back guarantee as part of the Councils ‘buy it and try it’ offer if, after 6 weeks, you aren’t completely satisfied with the new service.

Some of the key benefits

  • Save petrol and time with fewer journeys to your local recycling centre
  • It is easy to recycle your garden waste, simply fill up your brown lidded green bin and place it out for collection on your collection day and we will do the rest
  • Avoid unwanted 'creepy crawlies' in your vehicle

 

Want to join the new service?

Follow one of the links below and renew your subscription or sign up as a new customer today.

  • Join, renew or buy an additional bin 
  • Sign up by Direct Debit
  • Confirm your benefit reduction online 

How does the service work?

When you subscribe to the service you will receive a 240 litre wheeled bin and a welcome pack telling you how the service works and your collection dates for the year.

Your garden waste will be collected, on a fortnightly basis, with the exception of the Christmas/New Year period.

Simply put your garden waste in your bin and leave it by the kerbside by 6.30am, every fortnight, on your specified collection day.

What can go in your garden waste bin?

   

Your collected garden waste is taken for composting and turned in to a soil-conditioner called Pro-Grow which you can buy at your local Household Waste Recycling Centre.

‘Buy it’ and ‘try it’

Not sure if the service will work for you? Why not join and try it out!

If you have used the collection for six weeks (3 collections from the start of your subscription) and are not fully satisfied with the service we will refund the full amount.

What will it cost?

The costs shown below are an annual fee from 1st April 2016 to March 31st 2017.

As a special offer, if you subscribe now we will aim to deliver your bin within 10 working days and then give you free collections up until 1st April 2016.

 

 Standard Price Price for customers in receipt of Council Tax Support or Housing Benefit
First Bin £34.00 £24.50
Each Additional Bin  £29.00 £21.50

 

Reductions are available for people who require more than one bin or are in receipt of Housing or Council Tax benefit (proof of your benefit will need to be provided when paying for the service).

Direct Debit

If you are a new or existing customer and would like to pay by Direct Debit, download the Direct Debit form (pdf). then complete the form and return it to us at the following address:
Garden Waste Service, Direct Services Hedge End Depot Botley Road Hedge End Southampton SO30 2RA. Please ensure you return your direct debit form to us by post as we are unable to receive faxed or emailed copies.

If you choose to pay by Direct Debit you will receive a £2.00 discount for your first year of subscription (this will be £1.00 if paying the October to March Price). This discount applies only to customers who have not previously paid for the service by Direct Debit. The Direct Debit discount only applies to those paying the full standard price in the first year of subscription by Direct Debit method of payment.
