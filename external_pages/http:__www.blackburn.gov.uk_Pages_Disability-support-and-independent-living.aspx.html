Disability support and independent living

Page Image
Page Content

The Council provide help and support for people who have a disability and their carers.

We can provide help, advice, information and training to promote independence, social inclusion and access to life opportunities in the following areas:

  • occupational therapy
  • sensory impairment
  • aids and adaptations
  • surveying
  • telecare
  • moving and handling
