Changes for public holidays

In 2016/17, there will be waste collections on Good Friday, the May bank holidays and the August bank holiday. There are no collections on Easter Monday, Boxing Day or New Year's Day. The tables below show whether or not there will be changes to waste collections for each public holiday this year.

Easter 2016

Usual day Collection changes
Monday
  • Monday 21 March usual collection
  • Monday 28 March moves to Tuesday 29 March
Tuesday
  • Tuesday 22 March usual collection
  • Tuesday 29 March moves to Wednesday 30 March
Wednesday
  • Wednesday 23 March usual collection
  • Wednesday 30 March moves to Thursday 31 March
Thursday
  • Thursday 24 March usual collection
  • Thursday 31 March moves to Friday 1 April
Friday
  • Friday 25 March usual collection - no change for Good Friday
  • Friday 1 April moves to Saturday 2 April

May 2016

Usual day Collection changes
Monday
  • Monday 2 May usual collection - no change for early May bank holiday
  • Monday 30 May usual collection - no change for spring bank holiday
Tuesday
  • Tuesday 3 May usual collection
  • Tuesday 31 May usual collection
Wednesday
  • Wednesday 4 May usual collection
  • Wednesday 1 June usual collection
Thursday
  • Thursday 5 May usual collection
  • Thursday 2 June usual collection
Friday
  • Friday 6 May usual collection
  • Friday 3 June usual collection

August 2016

Usual day Collection changes
Monday
  • Monday 24 August usual collection - no change for summer bank holiday
Tuesday
  • Tuesday 25 August usual collection
Wednesday
  • Wednesday 26 August usual collection

  • Thursday 27 August usual collection

  • Friday 28 August usual collection
