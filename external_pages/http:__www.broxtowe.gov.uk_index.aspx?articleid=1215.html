Allotments

The Borough Council manage a number of allotment sites in the southern half of the Borough., With the current popularity of allotment gardening we do have waiting lists at some sites.

If you are interested in going on the waiting list for a new plot please contact us.

Allotments
SitePlots
Wollaton Road, Beeston116
Trent Vale Road, Beeston37
Grove Avenue, Chilwell147
Dennis Avenue, Beeston16
Borehole, off George Avenue, Chilwell131
Attenborough Lane, Chilwell11

In addition there are 2 sites at Inham Nook, Chilwell and Leyton Crescent, Beeston managed by allotment associations.

Charges

Effective 1st January 2016 per 250m2 Plot £30.00, water charge £15.00 per plot.

Effective 1st January 2017 per 250m2 Plot £31.00, water charge £15.50 per plot.

Half Price Concessionary Rates are available for those on State Pension or Disabled and Unemployed (proof is required).

A refundable deposit of £35 is required, which covers the site key (£10). This is refunded if the plot is returned in good condition together with the key.

Other Allotments in the Borough

The Parish and Town Councils of Brinsley, Eastwood, Greasley, Kimberley and Stapleford manage their own allotment sites. Please see the Offsite links on the right hand side.
