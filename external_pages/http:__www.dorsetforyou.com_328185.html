Dorset's SEN Transport Guide

Print

Dorset County Council's SEN Transport guide for parents and carers aims to offer parents and carers a greater choice and control over the way in which they make arrangements for their child/children to travel to and from school.

Some families may be eligible for support from Dorset County Council which could be in the form of a pass for use on public transport or the option of a personal travel budget.

  • Details and key services of school transport for children with special needs including a transport guide for parents and carers.
  • To find out more - email sentravelteam@dorsetcc.gov.uk or call them on 01305 225023

From September 2015, the SEN and disabilities transport policy is being reviewed in Dorset County Council for home to school travel arrangements for families with children and young people with special educational needs and disabilities
