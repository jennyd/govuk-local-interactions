We value the views of everyone who lives in Birmingham.

Have your say - take part to make a difference.

Consultations

Visit www.birminghambeheard.org.uk for all of our current consultations, and to see the results of past consultations.

Be Heard allows you to give feedback on the issues you find important, anything from the budget to recycling, new homes to adult social care.

Be Heard lets you see and take part in consultations from other public sector bodies, such as the NHS and charities.

Birmingham Speaks is an additional initiative specifically supporting the budget process; it's a less formal space allowing you to share and rate ideas to help us meet our budget requirements.

You can find out more about all of the consultations we have run recently in Be Heard's 'We Asked, You Said, We Did' section.


Watch council meetings

Other ways you can have your say

You can:

  • go to Ward Committee Meetings or District Committee Meetings
  • join us on social media
  • contact your local neighbourhood forum
  • submit a question to a meeting of the full council
  • organise and present a petition
  • contact your local councillor

For more information contact the Be Heard Team on 0121 675 4476 or email beheard@birmingham.gov.uk
