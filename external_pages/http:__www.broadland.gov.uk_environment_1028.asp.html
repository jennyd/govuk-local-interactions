stray dogs

Listen

Broadland District Council provides a service to collect stray dogs from Monday to Friday 9.00am - 5.00pm.

It is a legal requirement that all dogs wear a collar and tag to indicate to the finder who owns the animal. In addition you can have your dog micro chipped. Ask for details at your local veterinary clinic.

Owners must keep their animals under control at all times. If they do not and their animal goes missing then they can be liable for any damage the animal causes.

what to do next...

  • View the stray dogs FAQs page for further information
  • Contact the Contracts Team if you have lost or found a dog
  • Contact the out of hours service on 01603 705121 if you lose or find a dog out of normal hours
