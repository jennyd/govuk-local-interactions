Admissions to Schools

Blaenau Gwent School Admissions Round: School Place Allocation for the 2016/17 Academic Year

The admissions round for 2016/17 commences as of Tuesday 1st September 2015.

If you are still unsure about which school to choose for your child, please see our Starting Schools Booklet for 2016/17. To request a copy please contact the Admissions Officer as follows: 01495 355493 or email: education.department@blaenau-gwent.gov.uk

Important dates for you as a parent to note can be viewed here: (Click on the poster below)

 

Please see below information on how and when to apply for a Nursery, Reception and/ or Secondary place for your child(ren):

Nursery

All children living in Blaenau Gwent are entitled to a free part-time education place at the start of the term following their third birthday (e.g. if your child in 3 years of age on the 16th December 2015, they would be entitled to a nursery place from January 2016). This is commonly referred to as a ‘Rising 3’ place. Admission to nursery settings is usually on a part-time/ half-day basis for 5 days per week.

  • If your child is 3 years old between 1 September 2015 and 31 December 2015 they will be eligible for a Rising 3 place in the spring term, January 2016.
  • If your child is 3 years old between 1 January 2016 and 10 April 2016 they will be eligible for a Rising 3 place in the summer term, April 2016.
  • If your child is 3 years old between 11 April 2016 and 31 August 2016 they will be eligible for a Rising 3 place in the autumn term, September 2016.

If you wish for your child to attend a nursery setting at any of the schools outlined within the ‘Starting Schools Booklet’  / , you will need to apply for a nursery place for 2016.

The closing date for all nursery applications for the 2016/17 academic year is 5.00pm on 20th October 2015.

Please complete the following form for nursery admissions.


Only applications received by the published closing date, with relevant supporting evidence for the receipt of application forms, will be considered in the initial round of allocations of pupils. Forms received after this date, or incomplete forms, may result in a child being refused a place at their chosen nursery school.

Reception  - the reception round commences on the 5th January 2016. *Please note reception application forms will not be available until this date

Children are eligible for admission into reception at the start of the school year in which their fifth birthday falls (i.e. if your child is 5 between the 1st September 2015 and 31st August 2016 they are entitled to a reception place in September 2016). Children are not legally required to attend school until the start of the school term following their fifth birthday.

When applying for places in reception class, parents/ carers must be aware of the following:-

  • The closing date for reception application forms for the 2016/17 academic year is 5.00pm on the 2nd March 2016.
  • Only applications received by the published closing date, with relevant supporting evidence, will be considered in the initial round of allocations of pupils to schools. Forms received after this date, or incomplete forms, may result in a child being refused a place at their chosen primary school.
  • If a child is currently attending the nursery setting of their parent’s chosen school, an application form will need to be submitted as attendance at the nursery setting will not guarantee admission into reception at that school.

Secondary - the secondary round commences on the 29th September 2015. *Please note secondary application forms will not be available until this date

Children normally transfer from primary school to secondary school at the beginning of the school year following their eleventh birthday (i.e. if your child is 11 between the 1st September 2015 and 31st August 2016 they would be entitled to a secondary school place in September 2016).

Whilst every effort will be made to accommodate pupils, an application must be made to attend Secondary School at the appropriate time.

The closing date for secondary admission forms for the 2016/17 academic year is 5.00pm on the 24th November 2015.

Only applications received by the published closing date, with relevant supporting evidence, will be considered in the initial round of allocation of pupils to schools. Forms received after this date, or incomplete forms, may result in the child being refused a place at their chosen secondary school.

*For further information on the admissions process please contact the Admissions Officer on: 01495 355493 or email: education.department@blaenau-gwent.gov.uk

You have responsibility, by law, to ensure your child receives appropriate full-time education.

For more information on the School Admissions Process please refer to the Blaenau Gwent School Admissions Policy for Nursery and Statutory Education 2016/17
