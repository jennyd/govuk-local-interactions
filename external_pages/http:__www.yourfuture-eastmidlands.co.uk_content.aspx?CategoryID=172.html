Leicester City Connexions
The careers service for young people in and around Leicester city.
 


Leicester and Leicestershire Passportfolio

Passportfolio helps you to find out more about yourself, show what you have achieved, and take control of your future. You can record skills, achievements, build a CV and explore options.


NCS - National Citizen Service

 NCS gives 16 and 17 year olds the best Summer ever! Instead of hanging around at home NCS will occupy you for 4 weeks, giving you the opportunity to develop your personal skills and achieve amazing things with existing and new friends! The experience will look good on your college, university or job application. From time to time we list some of the events in our events area but do check the NCS website too.
