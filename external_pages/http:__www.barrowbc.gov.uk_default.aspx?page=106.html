Recycling and refuse collection.

We are committed to helping you recycle your waste, collecting your refuse and keeping the streets clean.

All 33,000 households in the Borough receive:

  • A weekly refuse collection.
  • A fortnightly recycling collection.

 

In addition 19,000 households receive a fortnightly garden collection.
