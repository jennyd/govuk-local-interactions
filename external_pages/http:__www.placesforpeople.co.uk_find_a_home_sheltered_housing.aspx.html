We have a long track record of providing homes for older people. We focus on creating living environments that give you greater independence with support when you need it.
If you're over 55 and looking for a new home with a little bit of support then one of our sheltered housing developments might be just what you need. You'll still have your own place, with the high-quality accommodation and privacy you expect but in a secure environment.
You'll get a self-contained apartment with a lounge, kitchen, bedroom and private bathroom. And you don't have to worry about maintenance. If building or repair work needs to be done, we take care of it, giving you the freedom to simply enjoy your living space.
All our sheltered developments have a dedicated support officer who you can call on for help. This, coupled with a 24-hour back up service provided by our contact centre, means that support is available at any time, day or night.
And, because our sheltered developments are situated in the heart of communities, you can still be close to friends and family. We'll even help you get the best of the local services available such as healthcare, leisure and social activities.
Applying for a home
You can apply for sheltered housing if you are over 55, or if you are under 55 and have a disability. If you are part of a couple and one of you meets our criteria, you are also welcome to apply.
Check to see if there is a sheltered housing development in your area. To find out more, simply contact the development you are interested in or call our national number on 0800 432 0003.Sheltered housing is purpose-built rented accommodation for people over 55. Sheltered housing is purpose-built rented accommodation intended for people over 55. You enjoy the privacy of your own place, a secure environment and 24-hour support if you need it.

  1. Stay independent: self-contained apartments with lounge, kitchen, bedroom and private bathroom.
  2. Purpose-built apartments: designed to meet your needs now and in the future.
  3. Stay local: our developments are in the heart of communities - so you can stay close to friends and family.
  4. 24-hour support: access support anytime by calling our contact centre or by speaking to onsite staff.
  5. Assistance: we'll help you access the best local services including healthcare, leisure and social activities.

Why choose us?

We've been providing attractive, comfortable homes for older people since 1965. Our service is based on listening to customers to make sure that we provide the best possible living experience. And as a charitable, non-for-profit company, you can be sure you get the best value for money.

View our sheltered schemes and contact your chosen scheme directly to discuss vacancies. 

Our accommodation is available to people who are:

  1. Over 55
  2. Under 55 with a disability
  3. In a couple and one partner meets the above criteria.

Take a look at our sheltered housing schemes and contact your chosen scheme to check whether you are eligible.
