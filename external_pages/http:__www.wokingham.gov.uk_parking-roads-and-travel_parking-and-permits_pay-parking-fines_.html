Pay parking fines

Standard charges

A standard parking fine costs £80.00. This is reduced to £40.00 if paid within 7 days.

An excess charge is issued if the ticket expired in the last hour. It costs £40.00. This is reduced to £20.00 if paid within 7 days. 

Parking fines must be paid within 14 days of the issue date. It is an offence not to pay.

We only accept parking fine payments for car parks we own.

  • Pay parking fines online

  • Expand / Collapse

 

We only accept parking fine payments for car parks we own. After selecting the button above:

  • Select Other Payments from the left hand side
  • Then select Exc/Std Charge Notice from the dropdown box
  • Under reference enter the 8 digit reference number on your ticket starting with WK
  • Input the amount of your fine
  • Under optional description input registration number and the name of the car park

  • Other ways to pay parking fines

  • Expand / Collapse

You can also pay by:  

  • Cheque: crossed and made payable to 'Wokingham Borough Council' with the notice number on the back of your cheque
  • Debit or credit card  
  • Bankers draft
  • Money or postal order
