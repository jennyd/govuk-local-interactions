Missed bin collection

If all the bins in your road have been missed you do not need to contact us as these are recorded. We will return to collect them within 2 working days - Monday to Friday - usually the following day.

If just your bin has been missed you must report this to us within 48 hours of it being missed. We will return within 2 working days to collect it. You can report your missed collection below.

You don't need to report a missed collection if your blue bin has a yellow, amber or red tag on it - we will visit you - please remove any non-recycleables from your bin.

Report a missed bin collection

It's worth bearing in mind:

  • around 30,000 bins are emptied each day by 60 staff working from more than 20 vehicles
  • we collect bins throughout the day, which means your recycling, refuse and kitchen and garden waste may be collected at different times
  • drivers will only take their vehicles down a road if they decide it is safe to do so without putting other road users, property or staff at risk
  • disruptions can be caused by parked vehicles blocking a road, mechanical faults and breakdowns, and adverse weather conditions
  • 'operational difficulties', can include road traffic delays, delays at tipping sites, staff sickness and events beyond our control, which may mean your bin is not collected on its scheduled day
