Adapting your home

Adaptations to your home can help you live safely and independently making everyday tasks easier. 

Nottinghamshire Help Yourself lists a number of organisations who provide home and housing support services. 

  • Small adaptations
  • Handy person adaptation service
  • Major adaptations
  • When adaptations can't help

Small adaptations

Small adaptations can help with everyday tasks and activities such as:

  • struggling up and down stairs (for example grab rails and hand rails)
  • getting in and out of your home (for example half steps)
  • turning taps, door handles and locks.

You can get small adaptations done in several ways:

  • a private company - use our approved trader scheme
  • handy person adaptation service
  • through our occupational therapy service - you need to have a care and support assessment and be eligible for care.

Handy person adaptation service

The handy person adaptation service provides help with:

  • essential minor adaptations
  • small practical jobs.

It is available to people living in Nottinghamshire (except the City of Nottingham) who are either aged 60 or over or disabled. 

The basic charge for adaptation work is £15 and you can have up to £250 worth of adaptations supplied and fitted in a single job. The basic charge for handy person jobs is £15, but you need to supply all materials for the job. All charges and limits are subject to change. 

The work is carried out by professional traders from our approved trader register regulated by Trading Standards.

Find out more

You can contact us and tell us about your needs by:

Major adaptations

A major adaptation to you home is usually one costing more than £1,000. It can include:

  • widening doors
  • installing stair lifts or ceiling hoists

If you are a disabled person who needs help to live independently, you might be eligible for a Disabled Facilities Grant to help fund the adaptations to your home. For more information see our Disabled Facilities Grant Factsheet [PDF].

If you are planning to pay for the work yourself, and you are using a private company, make sure you agree a price beforehand. Our trading standards page can offer advice if you have any disputes. 

Repairs

For any adaptations arranged by us the responsibility for repairs on these depends on what the adaptation was and when it was done. 

Stair lift

For repairs on stair lifts that were installed:

  • pre 1996 contact Prism Medical on 0844 980 2260 and select option 4
  • after 1996 contact the home owner or landlord.

If you are unsure when a stair lift was fitted contact us on 0300 500 80 80.

Ceiling track hoist

For repairs on a ceiling tack hoist contact Prism Medical on 0844 980 2260 and select option 4. 

Wet room

For repairs on a wet room installed after 1996 contact the home owner or landlord.

When adaptations can't help

Some homes aren't suitable for adaptations and some people still won't be able to manage in their home even if adaptations have been done. We can advise you about what is involved in moving to a more suitable property.

Our somewhere to live page gives more information about housing options.
