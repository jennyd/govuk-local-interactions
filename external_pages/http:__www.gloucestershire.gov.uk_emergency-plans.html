Emergency Plans

Information about the plans we make to deal with emergencies.

Planning is an important part of our work. We make plans so that if there is a major emergency in the County, the people of Gloucestershire are supported and essential services are restored as quickly as possible. Our plans detail how local services will work together to respond to any type of emergency. Every plan is regularly updated and also thoroughly revised at regular intervals. 

Please see the Gloucestershire Local Resilience Forum site to see many of the multi agency plans we contribute to as well as a community risk register which has been developed to identify the main hazards in the Gloucestershire area.
