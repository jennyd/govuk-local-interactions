Rate relief schemes


There are a number of rate relief schemes available that you may be able to get help with reducing the amount of business rates you pay:

a) Reoccupation relief
b) Partially Occupied Property Relief *
c) Charity and Community Amateur Sports Club relief
d) Small Business Rate Relief
e) Discretionary Rate Relief *
f) Retail Relief
g) Local Discount Scheme
h) Hardship Relief *
i) Unoccupied Property Relief
 
* These are reliefs that are awarded at the discretion of the Council. For further details, please view the Discretionary Rate Relief Policy
