School term dates 2015/2016

Academies and some schools are able to set different dates.

Autumn term 2015

3 September to 18 December 2015
Half term: 26 October to 30 October 2015

Spring term 2016

5 January to 24 March 2016
Half term: 15 February to 19 February 2016

Summer term 2016

11 April to 21 July 2016
May Day: 2 May 2016
Half term: 30 May to 3 June 2016

Training days

Responsibility for setting training days passed to schools on 23 March 2015, after the Local Authority had set 2 September 2015 and 4 January 2016.  Please check with individual schools whether they are using these dates.

Schools have three further training days available to take at their discretion. Please contact your school for details.
