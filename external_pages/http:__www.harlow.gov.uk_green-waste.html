Green waste

Harlow Council offers two hassle-free services for you to dispose of your garden waste. With both the Premium and bagged services we will collect grass cuttings, hedge cuttings, prunings, leaves, bark, flowers, weeds and branches, which will be sent for composting.

Green Waste Premium service (fortnightly wheelie bin)

Sign up for a Green Waste Premium service and we will provide the wheelie bin to recycle your garden waste all year round, including your real Christmas tree in January. If your property cannot accommodate a wheelie bin we will provide you with bags instead.

Sign up for your Green Waste Premium service for £94.92 for collections starting April 2015 to 31 March 2016, which equates to £3.95 per collection. Please read the refund procedure for bookings.

If you are signed up for Green Waste Premium service you can put your real Christmas tree out next to your brown wheelie bin on your January 2016 collection day.

Sign up for your Green Waste Premium service online

Alternatively you can sign up through Contact Harlow

Green Waste bagged collection

Book a green waste bagged collection, using either your own bags and pay 80p per bag of green waste or book for free when using our pre paid bags. Buy a roll of 20 pre paid bags for £16.00 from Contact Harlow. Please read the refund procedure for bookings.

You can book a real Christmas tree collection using the Green waste bagged collection service for just £2 a tree. For an artificial Christmas tree you can book collection using the Bulky collection service.

Book your Green Waste bagged collection online

Alternatively you can book through Contact Harlow.

Remember that the person collecting the waste must be able to lift bags / bundles into the back of the vehicle, so please do not overfill them. If the green waste is wet, please put less into each bag to reduce the weight.
