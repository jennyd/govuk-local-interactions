Removal of needles and syringes

We will remove needles and syringes within one working day.

If there is an immediate and serious threat to safety? Or is it a weekend or bank holiday, get advice about emergency clearance

Otherwise, report the problem now.

However, it is not our responsibility to remove needles and syringes from private property or land, your should contact the land owner direct including:

  • Northwards Housing,
  • Eastlands Homes,
  • Adactus Housing Association
