Online payments

Important information regarding Internet Explorer 6, 7 and 8 on Windows XP

Welcome to the Gosport Borough Council 24 hour secure Internet payments service. You may make a variety of payments using this secure site with most major credit or debit cards (not Diners Card or American Express).

Please select the required payment type from the following list:

  • Council Tax (please use the payment form below)
  • Overpaid Housing Benefit (please use the payment form below)
  • Housing Rents (please use the payment form below)
  • Invoices (please use the payment form below)
  • Business Rates (please use the payment form below)
  • Allotments (please use the payment form below)
  • New bin size
  • Parking Charge Notice (PCN)

If you have any queries please contact us by phone (023) 9258 4242, 9am - 5pm, Monday - Friday or email:  enquiries@gosport.gov.uk. Alternatively, you can pay using our 24hr payment line on 08000 213995 - you can only pay for Council Tax, Debtors Invoices and Overpaid Housing Benefits using this system.
