Council home exchange

Mutual exchange

If you wish to move from your present accommodation by means of a mutual exchange, that is when both parties wish to apply to swap/exchange properties they presently occupy, you are required to apply to the council by completing an application for consent to mutual exchange (27kb, PDF). This form should be completed once you have found a tenant to exchange properties with.

There are several ways in which you may find someone wishing to mutually exchange. A list of properties available to exchange are listed on Homeswapper and it is free to register. You may advertise your accommodation in local shops/newspapers, or you may know someone with whom you would like to swap. 

Permission must be given by us before any exchange takes place.

For further information or help contact the Housing Officers on (01926) 353366 or email: hsgem@warwickdc.gov.uk
