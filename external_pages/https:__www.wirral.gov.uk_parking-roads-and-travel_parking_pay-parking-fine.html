Pay a parking fine

Pay online by credit or debit card

It is quick and easy to pay online. All online card payments are secure.

Pay online

24 hour telephone payments

Call 0151 606 2345 to pay by credit or debit card.

Cheque or postal order

Please make your cheque or postal order payable to Wirral Council and send to:

Finance Department
PO Box 2
Birkenhead
Wirral
CH41 6BH

Clearly write the Penalty Charge Number and Vehicle Registration number on the reverse of the cheque.

Timescales for paying your parking fine

For both higher and lower rate penalties a discount of 50% still applies if the penalty is paid within 14 days of issue, or 21 days for postal Penalty Charge Notices.

Difficulty paying your parking fine

If you are having difficulty paying your parking fine you can ask to pay your fine in instalments.

Ask to pay a parking fine in instalments

Lost Penalty Charge Notice

If you have lost or don’t know your PCN number, please email parking.engineer@wirral.gov.uk with the following information:

  • the registration number that the PCN was issued to
  • the location where the PCN was issued
  • the date and time the PCN was issued

We will email you back with your PCN number.

Enforcement agency letters

If you have received a letter from an enforcement agent regarding your Penalty Charge Notice, please comply with the details of the letter and contact them directly. We are unable to take payments after the debt has been transferred.
