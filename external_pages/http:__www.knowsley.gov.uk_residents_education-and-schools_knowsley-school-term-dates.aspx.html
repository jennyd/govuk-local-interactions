Knowsley school term dates

Term dates for Knowsley primary, secondary and special schools from 2015 to 2017.

Deregulation Bill – 2015

With effect from September 2015, the Deregulation Bill 2015 (now an Act) grants maintained schools the responsibility for setting their own school term dates.  Knowsley Local Authority had previously agreed the adoption of a standard school term and holiday pattern with the Greater Merseyside Authorities.  It is hoped the following principles and recommended calendars continue to be followed by all schools in Knowsley, in order to ensure consistency for our families.

The principles on which the school term dates are set are:

  • All terms will start on a Monday (except where a bank holiday prevents this, in which case the term will start on a Tuesday).
  • October half term will always be the last full week in October
  • February half term will always be the third week in February
  • Easter Break, known as Spring break, will always be the first 2 full weeks in April and will not move with the Easter holiday
  • Easter may still fall in the Spring Break, but where it doesn’t, it will still be observed by the two bank holidays for Good Friday and Easter Monday
  • There will always be a break for 1 full week in May beginning with the Spring Bank Holiday Monday
  • Where a school is open for part of a week, the school will be open for 3 or more days to encourage good attendance
  • The five INSET teacher training days are to be agreed by each school governing body

The 2015-2016 academic calendar has previously been published on the website and all schools will have determined their dates accordingly.  The 2016 – 2017 calendar is a recommendation based on the agreed principals above.  Each governing body will be required to determine their 2016 dates onwards and publish them on the school’s website. 

Please check your child’s school term dates with your child’s school
