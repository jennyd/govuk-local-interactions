Care services and help in your home

If you need help in your home there are personal care and support services that can help you. 

There are different types of homecare and support services that can help with various aspects of your home life:

  • Health and nursing care
  • Personal care, such as help with washing or dressing
  • Companionship and befriending
  • Preparing and cooking meals
  • Household chores, such as cleaning and vacuuming
  • Help with your garden
  • Household repairs and maintenance

    Further information

  • Search our directory to find services to help you
  • Support with confidence is a scheme which checks and approves providers for personal care and other help in your home
  • The NHS website has advice about choosing care services and also contains lots of information to help you in your home
  • The Money Advice Service has more information on choosing the right sort of care services
