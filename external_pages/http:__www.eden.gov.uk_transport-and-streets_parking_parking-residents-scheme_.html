Parking Permit Scheme for Residents

We no longer issue residents parking permits please contact Cumbria County Council.

What are resident's parking schemes?

Resident's parking permit schemes enable residents who live in a restricted parking zone in Penrith and Appleby-in-Westmorland to apply for parking permits. Permits allow vehicles to be parked for unlimited length of time within the parking zone that the permit applies to. However, permits do not guarantee that a parking space will be available and spaces cannot be reserved for individuals outside their home.

To apply for a new or visitor parking permit

See Cumbria County Council parking permit web page.
