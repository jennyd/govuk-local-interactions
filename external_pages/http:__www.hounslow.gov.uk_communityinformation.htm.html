Community information

We offer three directories giving details of groups across Hounslow.

An e-form can be completed by new organisations wishing to include their information in the directories. Any existing organisations who wish to amend existing information can do so by sending their revised details to: community.development@hounslow.gov.uk – please make sure your details are up to date.

Hounslow directory of voluntary organisations

Provides information about the voluntary and community sector within Hounslow; outlining the categories, contact details and operating hours of more than 700 organisations.

These groups could offer you volunteering opportunities, let you know what’s happening in your area or be local partners for funding bids. For example you can search by interest or by area, for example ‘Environment’ and ‘Feltham’.

Community buildings directory

An online directory of community buildings working in Hounslow, listing a wide range of activities and services to contribute to as well as to use.

Solicitors directory

An online directory of solicitors (Community Legal Service) working in Hounslow and lists a wide range of services that you can use.
