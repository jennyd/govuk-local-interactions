Direct Payments

What are Direct Payments?

Direct Payments are another way to get support to meet your personal and social needs. Instead of this being provided by Social Services, you are given the funding to set up services yourself.

This can give you greater control over who will help you and in what way they will do it. This may also fit in better with your lifestyle and will give you greater choice and flexibility.
