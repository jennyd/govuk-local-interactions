Pest Control

The aim of the pest control service is to prevent harm to human health and property by pests. This is achieved by regularly surveying the district for evidence of public health pests, responding quickly to complaints and taking action where required. The council’s experienced pest control officers offer advice and provide a professional service to both domestic and commercial premises.

For more information about our services call 01594 810000 or email: council@fdean.gov.uk.
