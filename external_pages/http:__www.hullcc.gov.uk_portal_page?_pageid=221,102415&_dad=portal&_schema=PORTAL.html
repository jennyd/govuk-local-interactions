Bereavement services     

Important notice

Please note - due to the increase in day light hours - the cemetries and crematorium grounds closing times will be -

From Wednesday 1 April 2015 onwards the gates at the following locations will be locked at 7.30pm -

  • Northern Cemetery
  • Eastern Cemetery
  • Priory Woods Cemetery
  • Western Cemetery
  • Hedon Road Cemetery
  • The Chanterlands Crematorium

Please be aware that the above times are for the main gates. Other access gates will be locked earlier.

From Wednesday 1 April 2015 Bereavement Services will only be able to take debit or credit card payments only. We no longer accept cash or cheques. Please accept our apologies for any inconvenience caused. 

There are five cemeteries and a crematorium located within the city boundary, these are - 

  • Eastern Cemetery
  • Hedon Road Cemetery
  • Northern Cemetery
  • Priory Woods Cemetery  
  • Western Cemetery
  • Chanterlands Avenue Crematorium

We provide support and advice on all issues relating to burials, cremations and cemetery grounds within the city. 

  • cemeteries and burials
  • cremations
  • funerals – arranging a funeral and expenses
  • memorials and after cremation commemoratives
  • family history and historical information

Fees and charges
There are fees and charges for our bereavement services, however, this is operated on a not-for-profit basis and all the money received contributes towards the cost of providing the best standards.

  • Burials
  • Cremation
  • Vaults, niches and plaques
  • Memorials

How to contact us 

It is important that we provide clear, quality advice and support to all our families. It is crucial that we are able to take the appropriate amount of time with you and we do not want you to feel rushed.

If you wish to meet with an officer you must contact the administration team so an appointment can be made for you to make sure that the right officer is available for you. This will avoid you having to wait to speak to an officer or worse there being no one available to support you.

At the crematorium - you must make an appointment before attending at the administration office.
