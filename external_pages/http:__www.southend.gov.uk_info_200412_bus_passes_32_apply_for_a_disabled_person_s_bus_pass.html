Apply for a Disabled Person’s Bus Pass

Please see our application form if you are disabled and require a companion to travel with you due to severe disability and handicap.

Download an application form here

You would qualify for a Disabled Personâ€™s Bus Pass at any age if you:
Please provide one of the following with your application:
A
 Are blind or partially sighted.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme award letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or eye specialist.
B
 Are severely or profoundly deaf.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or hearing specialist
C
 Cannot speak.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or specialist
D
 Have a disability or have suffered an injury that has a long-term effect on their ability to walk.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or specialist
  • A walking aid or artificial leg does not give automatic right to eligibility
E
Are without the use of both arms.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or specialist
F
Have a learning disability.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Higher Rate Mobility allowance of the DLA
  • War Pensioners Mobility Supplement
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from GP or hearing specialist
  • Confirmation of registration with Social Care
  • Have or had special education provision
G
Are prohibited from driving a motor on medical grounds.
  • Proof of PIP Payments with a score of at least 8 points
  • Confirmation of refusal of a driving licence
  • Armed Forces Compensation Scheme letter showing reason for the decision
  • Letter of confirmation from a GP or specialist to show the disability would prevent you having a driving licence.
