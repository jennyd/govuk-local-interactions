Child Performance Licences

New Legislation February 2015

New legislation came into place on the 6th February 2015: The Children (Performances and Activities) (England) Regulations 2014.

  • Child performance and activities licensing legislation in England departmental advice - GOV.UK
  •  Examples of Best Practice - Child Performance and Activities Licensing by LAs in England (PDF format 424KB)

  •  Restrictions in relation to all performances table Feb 2015(PDF format 29KB)

Please check this page regularly for further updates.

Apply for a Performance Licence

Required when the child will be paid for their performance, requires time off school, the performance lasts for more than three days or the child has performed for more than three days in the six months leading up to the first date of performance applied for.

  •  Licence Application Template May 2015 (Word format 139KB)

Apply for an Exemption Certificate

Required when no payment is made to the child except for expenses, no time off school is required; the performance run is four days or less and the child hasn't already performed on four occasions in previous six months.

  •  Exemption Application Part1 (Word format 108KB)
  •  Exemption Application Part 2 (Excel format 89KB)

Licences can only be applied for by the producer, organiser or agent of a performance.

Applications and supporting information should be emailed to cee-admin@northamptonshire.gov.uk. Please allow up to 21 days for processing.

Further details can also be found on the NNCEE webpage or contact CEE-Admin@northamptonshire.gov.uk  for further information.
