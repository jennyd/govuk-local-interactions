Roads street cleaning programme

How do you rate this information / service?

What we do with your feedback

We are responsible for the sweeping of streets and removal of litter.

What the service is for:

The service covers all public sites and roads managed by the council.

We will:

  • clean town centres and major shopping areas daily
  • regularly clean main roads
  • ensure residential and minor roads are kept clean
  • remove fly-tips
  • empty litter bins on behalf of town and parish councils
  • help your locally organised litter picking event.

Mechanical road and manual sweeping is scheduled to take place on a regular cycle in heavy-use areas, with reactive sweeping otherwise.

High amenity areas such as town centres and major shopping areas are cleansed daily.

Other activities within the street cleaning operations include:

  • cleaning up spillages
  • cleansing of public convenience
  • removing dead animals

When identified that an area or incident requires attention this will be dealt with promptly.

Please note that parks and open spaces are being managed and cleaned by the relevant town or parish council.

How you can access the service:

To report any street cleansing issue please contact Environmental Services.

You can help us by:

  • not dropping litter. This includes chewing gum and cigarette butts as well as food wrappers and drink bottles and cans
  • cleaning up after your dog
  • not parking where you want us to clean
  • using a reputable company to dispose of your excess household or business waste and checking that they hold a waste carrier license
  • having waste collection contract for your business
  • reporting your concerns to customer services.

 How long will it take?:

48 hours after receiving complaint.
