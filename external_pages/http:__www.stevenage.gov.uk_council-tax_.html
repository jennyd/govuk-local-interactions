Council Tax

  • Single Person Discount Review
    We are undertaking a review of all of the Council Tax discounts currently in place across the Borough.
  • Important changes to bailiffs action
    The Taking Control of Goods (Fees) Regulations 2014 come into effect from 6 April 2014.
  • Council tax
    Council tax is payable on domestic dwellings and is charged by the local authority to pay for the services it provides. Council tax charges are published each year together with information on how the charges are assessed, eligibility, how to pay etc.
  • Council tax - band enquiries and reductions
    Band enquiries and information about band reductions.
  • Council tax reductions for people with disabilities
  • Council tax discount
    Processing of requests and assessment of eligibility for a single person or other discount on their council tax.
  • Council tax exemptions
    The council tax exemption scheme may exempt unoccupied and occupied properties from council tax.

    Information about the Exception Hardship Relief (EHR) policy.
  • Empty Homes Review 2015
    In support of the government's initiative to tackle the shortfall in national housing we are currently undertaking a review of some homes registered as empty with a company called Capacity Grid.

    If you are having financial difficulties there are agencies that can help you.
