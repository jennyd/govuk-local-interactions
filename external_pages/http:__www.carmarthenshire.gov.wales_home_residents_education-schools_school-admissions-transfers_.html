School admissions / transfers

Admission to school is not an automatic process and you must submit an application by the specified date.

Not getting a place at a school of your choice can be very upsetting and distressing for you and your child.

You can apply for your child's place online, with the exception of places at *Voluntary Aided Schools where you will need to contact the school directly.

You can apply for admission to a nursery or primary school up to five years in advance which will assist both schools and us with forward planning. Whilst early application will not guarantee or give priority to an application it will ensure you meet the deadline date.

We look forward to welcoming your son or daughter to one of our schools and wish them every success in their studies.

* Voluntary Aided Schools - There are a number of schools in Carmarthenshire which have Voluntary Aided status.

The schools are in the main, run and funded in the same way as state schools and often , but not always, have a religious character. They follow the national curriculum and where they are a faith school they may teach religious education according to their own faith.

A trust or foundation will have a substantial influence over the school and appoint the majority of the Governing Body who can set their own policy and criteria for the admission of pupils and the appointment of staff. They will also have greater control of the school building and site.

To apply for a place at a Voluntary Aided schools you will need to contact the school directly.
