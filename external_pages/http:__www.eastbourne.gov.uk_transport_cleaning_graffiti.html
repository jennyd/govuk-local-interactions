Report Graffiti on the Streets

Use our online form to report graffiti on Eastbourne streets.

We only have the authority to clean up graffiti (illicit writing or drawings) in public or council owned spaces. This means we cannot deal with graffiti on:

  • Commercial or industrial property

  • Private residential and Housing Association property

  • Railways

  • Watercourses
