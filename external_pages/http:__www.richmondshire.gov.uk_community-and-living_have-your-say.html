What's your view?

We are always interested to hear your suggestions and feedback about the Richmondshire community that you live and work in. We can only improve things if you let us know what the problems are, so make sure you have your say!

Every year the Council undertakes consultation on all sorts of issues. These are time limited consultation activities so this area allows you to voice your opinions and suggestions at any time.

Contact the Council in various ways - please view them here.

 

Consultation Officer at Richmondshire

Richmondshire District Council's Consultation Officer, Frances Bainbridge, is available to help communities with their consultation and survey needs offering advice, support and assistance with survey design, question formation, survey layout, printing and analysis of returning forms. Equalities Monitoring

On some of our questionnaires and surveys we undertake equalities monitoring to ensure that we are providing fair and equitable services to all our customers. All the information you provide is treated confidentially and used for statistical monitoring only. This leaflet provides more information about why we need to do this.

Scrutiny Committees

Councillors on Scrutiny Committees are able to look into any topics and issues that affect the wellbeing of the residents of the district and by looking closely at a subject can make recommendations for improvements. They look internally at the workings of the council together with its policies, strategies and performance around council services and also at external authorities and agencies providing 'critical friend' challenge.

If you think there are issues or services that would benefit from further scrutiny, let us know using the online form.
