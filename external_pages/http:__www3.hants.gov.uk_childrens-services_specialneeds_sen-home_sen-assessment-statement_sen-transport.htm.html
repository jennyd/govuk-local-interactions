Transport to school or a resourced provision for children and young people with an Education, Health and Care (EHC) plan

Children and young people with an EHC plan may have to travel several miles in order to get to the school that they have been offered a place at. This can be either a special school, a mainstream school or a mainstream school with a resourced provision.
