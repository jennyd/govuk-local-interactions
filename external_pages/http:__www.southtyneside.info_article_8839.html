The Word

The three-storey building, being built on the town's Market Place, will celebrate the borough's literary heritage, and will be a home for North East writing talent. South Tyneside has a tradition of writing that stretches right back to Bede, who was one of this country's truly great authors and scholars.

To find out more about this major new cultural attraction in the borough, please see The Word.
