Bulky Household Waste Collections

Mid Suffolk  and Babergh District Council provide a chargeable collection service (£28.50 for up to five items) for disposing of items that householders are unable to take to the Household Waste and Recycling Centres. Unfortunately, the majority of items collected via the bulky waste collection service are sent to Energy from Waste (EfW) plant, we do recycle items where possible.

Before requesting a collection

Please read the following and use the links below to confirm that the items you wish to have collected are acceptable;

  • The service is not intended as a house clearence service. All items must be easily accessible at one point outside the property, at the point your bins are collected or the edge of a driveway.  The crews will not enter a property, garage or shed to collect any items.
  • The service is only available to domestic properties.
  • Ensure that we are able to collect the items you are requesting by checking our Accepted Items for a Bulky Household Waste Collection (234 kB, PDF)
  • Items should not be filled with other waste (i.e. a fridge should not contain food)
  • If you require bags of refuse to be collected, no garden waste (up to 10 bags) - this counts as five items. If you have further bags or other bulky waste items you will need to arrange and pay for another collection. Two bags of rubbish count as one item.
  • Carpet does not include underlay; this is a separate item; one room carpet is one item, but must be manageable by two persons, rolled and tied.
  • All the items requested for collection must be out ready on the day of the collection for 06:30am.
  • Bookings for a Monday must be received before midday on Friday, the last working day prior to the collection day.

How to request a collection

**Please check availability before making your booking - Bulky Availability

If you wish to request a collection of bulky household items, please visit our online Request a Bulky Household Waste Collection page or call the Waste Services team on 01449 778678.

You will be asked to provide the following details;

  • Name, address and a contact telephone number
  • The address the items are to be collected from (must be within Mid Suffolk and Babergh areas)
  • Any special arrangements e.g. items will away from the kerbside due to it being on a busy road
  • Date/week the collection is required, please refer to the to the collection schedule to identify your collection day

Collections can not take place until payment has been received. Requests and payments must be received before midday on the last working day prior to the collection day.  

Bookings received after midday on the last working day prior to a collection or once we have listed a date as 'Full', we will automatically be allocated to the next available date.

Bank holiday collections will usually be made a day later than stated.

Recycle, Re-Use

Remember, good quality furniture could be re-used or recycled at the following:

  • Ipswich Furniture Project Telephone: 01473 404004 or email: service@ipswichfp.org
  • Gate House Furniture Project Telephone: 01284 754967 or email: info@gatehouse.org.uk
  • Addullam’s Cave Stowmarket charity will take away furniture. Telephone: 01449 770082
  • British Heart Foundation will take good quality furniture and working electrical goods. Telephone: 0844 499 4145
  • FIND (Families in Need) – will take furniture. Telephone: 01473 833351
  • St Elizabeth Hospice will collect good quality furniture but no white goods, free of charge, from both Babergh and Mid Suffolk areas.  Telephone: 01473 744080
  • Place items free on www.freecycle.org where there are a network of people recycling items locally
  • www.tradeleftovers.com: This site provides free listings for your leftover building materials, helps you to find surplus stuff you could use - locally and cheap!. Helps to reduce unnecessary waste in the building industry.
  • www.recycle.co.uk:  This site provides a free service for both Charities and members of the public.
