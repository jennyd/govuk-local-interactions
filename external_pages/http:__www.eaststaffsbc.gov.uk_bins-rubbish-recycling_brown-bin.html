Brown bin

What you can put into your brown bin?

From the garden:

  • Leaves and bark
  • Prunings
  • Dead flowers and plants
  • Weeds
  • Hedge clippings
  • Grass mowings
  • Branches (maximum 4” diameter)
  • Real Christmas trees

From the kitchen:

  • Meat, fish and bones
  • Vegetable and fruit peelings
  • Bread
  • Tea bags
  • Egg shells
  • Coffee grounds

What can't go into the brown bin?

  • Plastic of any type
  • Pet litter or animal waste
  • Rubble or large quantities of soil or turf
  • Cardboard or paper
  • Polystyrene
  • Textiles

If your brown bin contains any of the above non-accepted items the collection crew will not be able to empty it.
