Online forms: Requesting Replacement and/or additional Refuse and Recycling Containers

This form has a maximum of 8 pages

Please fill in all the required fields on this form to submit your request for new or replacement refuse bins and recycling containers. You can also use it to request additional recycling containers or a new sticker for the garden waste scheme if your old one has been lost/damaged.

This form will take approximately 5 minutes to complete. Question marked 'Required' must be answered.

NOTE:

Green Bins

If you are requesting a green bin for garden waste, the subscription charge for the green bin must be paid in advance. We will contact you to arrange this if you have not already paid. Further information on the garden waste subscription charge

If your green bin has been lost or stolen, and already had a paid garden waste sticker on it, the sticker will be cancelled and a new one issued as a replacement. We may need time to investigate the matter before issuing a replacement sticker/bin.

Grey Bins

This form is not for requesting an additional grey wheelie bin. Please use the online form for an Additional Grey Bin
