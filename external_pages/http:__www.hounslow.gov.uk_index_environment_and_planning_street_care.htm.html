Street care and enforcement

If you would like to know when your road is due to be litter picked, please contact us.

Road cleaning schedule

Your road will be inspected on a fortnightly basis and cleansing if required, between Monday and Friday. This will involve litter picking and sweeping.

Please note that any spillages arising from the domestic waste and recycling service should be reported to 020 8583 2000 so that the spillages can be cleared promptly.

Removal of dumped rubbish

We will remove most dumped rubbish and flytipping from your road within 24 hours of the waste being reported to us, as long as the rubbish is on the public highway; which includes the footpath and carriageway. Please contact 020 8583 2000 and we will arrange for the rubbish to be cleared as soon as possible.

We would request residents to be alert and report any incidences of fly tipping and where possible, details of who has dumped the rubbish including vehicle registration, colour, make, model, time and date of the incident.

Waste on private land

If you wish to report issues of waste on privately owned land that is causing a nuisance or attracting and harbouring rats, please call 020 8583 2000 to report the matter. Our pollution team can investigate and may take action where appropriate.
