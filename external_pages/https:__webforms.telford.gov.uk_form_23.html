Important Notice

If you are reporting damage to street furniture that requires Urgent attention - please inform Telford & Wrekin Council on 08451 559955.

For damage caused by a Motor Vehicle or a Traffic Incident that has caused damage which may place the public at risk please call 999 and Telford & Wrekin Council on 08451 559955

If the damage has NOT been caused by a motor vehicle or is not causing any dangerous issues please proceed with logging the incident and Telford & Wrekin will investigate.
