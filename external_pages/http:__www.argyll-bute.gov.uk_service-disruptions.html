Roads
Cross Street, Campbeltown closed to vehicles from 5th January Last updated: 25 Aug 2015 - 14:57 (originally posted: 24 Dec 2014 - 10:25)

Cross Street, Campbeltown remains closed to vehicles due to the construction works at the Town Hall and 50-52 Main Street / 2 Cross Street.  It is hoped that the road will re-open in January 2016.

Pedestrian access to Burnside Square and Union Street will be maintained as far as possible by way of a covered walkway.

There may be an odd occasion when the street will be completely closed, during periods of unavoidable risk to public safety.  If the street does have to be closed at any point the contractor will inform those affected at the earliest opportunity.

We would like to thank you for your continued cooperation with the THI project and assure you that the contractors will continue to keep disruption to an absolute minimum. Please watch out for updates on the website and in the local paper.
