Car parks

How do I apply for a car park permit ?

Permits are now virtual, meaning that you no longer need to display a paper permit in your vehicle. To apply for or renew a permit go to the RingGo website and select the type of permit you want to apply for against the Central Bedfordshire logo. Then follow the on screen instructions to fill out the application form and upload your documents. We will then review your application. Once we have accepted your application you then need to log back onto the RingGo website to purchase and start your permit session.

Market traders - Leighton Buzzard

Parking for market traders is available in the Duncombe Drive car park in Leighton Buzzard (on Tuesdays and Saturdays) in marked red bays, which are reserved until 9.30am. After this time the public can park in the red bays providing there is a free bay. 

Parking sessions are now virtual, meaning that you no longer need to display a pay and display ticket in your vehicle. To apply for or renew a parking session go to the RingGo website and select the market trader parking session against the Central Bedfordshire logo. Then follow the on screen instructions to fill out the application form and upload your documents. We will then review your application. Once we have accepted your application, you then need to log back onto the RingGo website to purchase and start your parking session.

Casual market traders can purchase a reduced daily parking session at the cost of £3.50 per day from the pay and display machines located in the car parks.

Market traders must display a traders permit to qualify for the reduced parking session.

Parking session charges

1 day = £3.50

12 months = £64.00 per market day you are attending each week

Market traders - Dunstable

Parking for market traders is available in the Ashton Square car park in Dunstable (on Wednesday, Friday and Saturday) in marked red bays, which are reserved until 9.30am. After this time the public can park in the red bays, providing there is a free bay. 

Parking sessions are now virtual, meaning that you no longer need to display a pay and display ticket in your vehicle. To apply for or renew a parking session, go to the RingGo website and select market trader parking session against the Central Bedfordshire logo. Then follow the on screen instructions to fill out the application form and upload your documents. We will then review your application. Once we have accept your application, you then need to log back onto the RingGo website to purchase and start your parking session.

Casual market traders can purchase a reduced daily parking session at the cost of £3.50 per day from the pay and display machines located in the car parks.

Market traders must display a traders permit to qualify for the reduced parking session.

Parking session charges

1 day = £3.50

12 months = £64.00 per market day you are attending each week
