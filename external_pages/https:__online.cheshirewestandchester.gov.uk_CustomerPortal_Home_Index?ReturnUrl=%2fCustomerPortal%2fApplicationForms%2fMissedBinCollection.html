My West Cheshire

Welcome

A West Cheshire account gives you access to council services and payments - from applying for a household waste permit paying your council tax. It is easy to create and free. Sign up now, and we'll make Cheshire West and Chester do the work for you.

Benefits for a West Cheshire Account:

  • We'll remember you, so you won't need to tell us your details more than once
  • View your council tax details
  • Track your service requests
  • Find out about planning applications near you
  • Get alerts about refuse collection changes
