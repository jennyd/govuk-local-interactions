Play areas

Woking Borough Council prides itself on providing innovative and challenging play equipment throughout the Borough in its 40 Play Areas.

In 2004 Woking Borough Council began a play area improvement programme which has seen the refurbishment and redesign of seventeen play areas across the Borough. This work was carried out in co-ordination with the Council's appointed play area design partner, Russell Play Ltd.

If you would like any further information regarding the redevelopment of the play areas please contact the Cultural and Community Development Team on:

Tel: 01483 743669.

email: greenspaces@woking.gov.uk

All equipment is maintained to the highest standard, to ensure compliance with both British and European safety laws with regular maintenance checks carried out.

Should you notice any damages to any of the play area equipment please contact the Council's ground maintenance partner Serco on

Tel: 01483 775422

email: helpdesk.Woking@serco.com
