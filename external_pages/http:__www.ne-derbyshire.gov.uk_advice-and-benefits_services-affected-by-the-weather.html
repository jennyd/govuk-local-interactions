Services Disrupted by Severe Weather

 

Snow is affecting much of the district today (30th January).


Find up to date information about disruption to bin collections here.

Check our local leisure centre pages for details of changes to opening hours: Dronfield Sports Centre, Eckington Swimming Pool and Sharley Park Leisure Centre.

Find out about school closures in the area or get the latest weather reports and travel updates.

 

Find out what you can do to prepare for or deal with flooding.  

Find out about gritting and snow clearance on the Derbyshire County Council website.

 

Why not bookmark this page, or maybe follow us on Twitter to keep up to date.

 

Get emergency advice during severe weather from the Derbyshire Prepared website.

 

 

 

School Closures:

Derbyshire County Council has a dedicated web page with up to date information about closures of schools across the county. Take a look at their School Closures page for more details, or why not sign up to get alerts about school closures in your area?

You can also keep up to speed with which schools are open and closed during particularly bad weather by tuning in to Peak FM on 107.4 and 102 FM or check the PeakFM website or the PeakFM page on Facebook for regular updates. You can get updates for the south of our district from Radio Derby. Please do not call the radio stations as all information will be given on air.
