Reserve a book

Log in to our catalogue to reserve an item. You will need your 12 digit library card number and PIN.

Reserve a book »

How much will it cost to reserve a book?

This service will cost 50p per request and is free for children, students under 19 and concessions.

How will I know when my book is ready for collection?

We'll send you an email to let you know.
