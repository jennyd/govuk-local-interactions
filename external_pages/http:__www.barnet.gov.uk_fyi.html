FYi (families and young people's information service)

  • Children, young people and families
Zoom

FYi is Barnet’s families and young people’s information service, that provides information, advice and guidance for families who have children or young people up to the age of 20. We also provide this service to professionals who work with children, young people and families.

If you are looking for specific information and can not find this on our web pages, please email or call the FYi team and we will endeavour to find out the information you require on a range of childcare or family issues.
