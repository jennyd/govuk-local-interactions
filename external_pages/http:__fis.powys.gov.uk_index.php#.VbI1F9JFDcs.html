Welcome to Powys Family Information Service

The Family Information Service (FIS) provides free advice and information

 on a wide range of childcare options and activities for children aged 0-25,

 their families and carers. This includes information on Day Nurseries,

Registered childminders, out of school clubs, Pre-school playgroups and

parent and toddler groups. We also provide help and advice on paying for

childcare and working in childcare.

For advice on all money matters from the small to the big things in life

check out our Money Advice Page.
