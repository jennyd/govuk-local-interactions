Public Register

Under the Licensing Act 2003 we have to keep a Public Register that holds information on Licences and Certificates we issue under the act.

Our register holds information on all applications and licences that we administer for the Northants Licensing Partnership.

Search our Licensing Public Register.

For example, you may view the register for approved applications for House to House and Street Collections.

We are currently transferring our licences to a new software system, so if you cannot find the licence you are looking for please contact us at the Licensing Unit and we will be able to give you the relevant information.
