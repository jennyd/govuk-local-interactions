Child Employment

Many children enjoy getting a part-time job, such as waitering, shop work, paper round or working in the family business, as well as many other types of light jobs. 

The sections below provide information regarding the law, how to obtain a work permit and advice on the types of work a child may undertake.

Without a work permit the child is working illegally and without insurance.

  • Advice for Employers
  • Advice for Parents

Find out more information on Child Employment

To find out more information or gain some general advice you can get in touch with the Child Employment Officer.
