Dog Warden Service in Weymouth and Portland

Listen

The Weymouth and Portland Dog Warden gives advice, educates and may take formal action, including the service of fixed penalty notices and prosecution in appropriate cases. Dog fouling, stray and lost dogs can be reported in Weymouth and Portland online.

How to contact the Dog Warden

Please call us on 01305 838000 for the most immediate response, or complete the short online form to report:

Lost or stray dogs 

Dog fouling

Unreasonable and excessive levels of dog noise

Attacks from dogs on other animals

Breach of Dog Control Orders

Please see the borough council's Stray Dog Charges (pdf, 140kb) (opens in a new window) for this service.

Where and how to walk your dog

There are a number of Dog Control Orders you should be aware of when walking your dog in Weymouth & Portland. 

For further information and details of how you can prevent complaints about you and your dog please see 'What a responsible dog owner can do'.

Mistreatment or neglect of dogs

The Dog Warden is not responsible for the investigation of mistreatment or neglect of dogs. If you are concerned about the treatment of a dog you should contact the Royal Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (opens in a new window) on 0300 1234 999.

Further advice

The Dog Warden is happy to speak to members of the public on all dog related issues. If you have queries please contact the Dog Warden on 01305 838000. More information can be found in the  Weymouth and Portland dog owners guide (pdf, 441kb) (opens in a new window).

  • Weymouth and Portland Dog related Public Space Protection Orders

    Weymouth & Portland Borough Council is required to consult on current dog related orders and proposed changes under the new Anti-Social Behaviour, Crime and Policing Act 2014. Following consultation, Dog Control Orders will become part of Public Space Protection Orders (PSPOs). The borough council is required to consult on these orders every three years to ensure public engagement and consideration of public views.
