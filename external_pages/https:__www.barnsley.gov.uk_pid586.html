    Sorry, we don't provide that service

    The service you're looking for is not one of the 700 (approximately) that we provide to the borough.

    There are more than 1000 public services available nationally that are delivered by local authorities and in some cases private businesses. There are around 300 of those that don't apply to Barnsley, for example lighthouse maintenance services and it appears the service you're looking for is one of them.

    So, it may be that the service is provided in Barnsley, but by another organisation or simply that it does not apply in the borough.

    Please consider visiting GovUK and locating the service there. GovUK offer a post code search facility that will identify the nearest council providing it for your convenience.

    Alternatively, some of our housing services are provided by Berneslai Homes and you may consider searching their website:
