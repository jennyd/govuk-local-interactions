Citizenship ceremonies

 
Download as PDF
Last Modified July 25, 2015
Share this page


  • To arrange your Citizenship ceremony phone 01253 477177 and quote your Home Office number
  • For general enquiries about applying to become a British Citizen, contact the Home Office on 0151 237 5478 or visit the Home Office Website. 

Citizenship ceremonies are held monthly at the Town Hall to confer British Citizenship.

You can provide the names of guests who you would like to attend to witness your ceremony. These must be given in advance, preferably when you phone to book your ceremony.

The citizenship ceremonies normally take place in groups of 10-20 people in order to reinforce the community nature of citizenship. If you would prefer to have an individual ceremony, you can choose to hold your ceremony either at The Wedding Chapel or at any of our Approved Venues in Blackpool. This does mean paying an additional fee per participant.

Certificate of Naturalisation

Your certificate is sent to us by the Home Office. If the personal details you gave us do not appear correctly on your certificate, you should return it after the ceremony has taken place to:

Home Office Nationality Group
PO Box 306
Liverpool
L2 7XS

You should explain the mistake and enclose evidence to support what you say. The Home Office usually wants to see official documents as evidence. If the mistake is yours, they may not be able to change it.

Our address is:

Blackpool Registration Service
Municipal Buildings
Corporation Street
Blackpool
FY1 1GB
