Social housing tenants

Council housing

The housing management provider for all Cheshire West and Chester Council homes in Ellesmere Port and Neston is the Plus Dane Group.

If you are interested in renting council accommodation in these areas, you must first register with Trust Homechoice.You can read more about this on the Choice Based Lettings page on this website.

Where to find help

Information is available on this website for social housing tenants about:

  • Housing Associations
  • Pay your rent or leaseholder charges

Information is also available from:

  • Choice Based Lettings
  • Plus Dane Group
  • Trust HomeChoice 

Downsizing scheme

If you are a social housing tenant and your home is too large for you to manage or you have spare bedrooms you could take advantage of the downsizing scheme.

The scheme is supported by the Cheshire and Warrington Housing Alliance to help people move to smaller, more manageable accommodation. The scheme is run by registered providers (housing associations) in the area and can support tenants in social housing with practical assistance who would like to move to a smaller home.

If your landlord is listed here you may qualify for the scheme:

  • Golden Gates Housing Trust: 0800 25 26 27  
  • Peaks and Plains: 0800 012 1311
  • Plus Dane: 0300 123 4560 or 0800 169 2988
  • Wulvern: 01270 506200

The Scheme can also offer the following help and assistance with moving:

  • Removals
  • Disconnection and reconnection of domestic appliances
  • Telephone reconnection

To find out if you are eligible for the scheme and to register contact your landlord on the above numbers. Households wishing to take advantage of this scheme must have a clear rent account and not be the subject of any current antisocial behaviour case or any tenancy breaches.
