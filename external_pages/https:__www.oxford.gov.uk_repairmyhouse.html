We provide a housing repairs service for Council tenants. We operate 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

You can;

  • Report a repair to us 
  • Choose a date and time for us to visit and carry out the repair
  • Track progress with your repair (if you register for an account)

Already registered?

If you have an online account with us, registering or signing in will reduce the time it take to complete your housing repair request.

You will also be able to track the progress of your repair online.

Before you start

Right to buy

Once you have claimed the Right to Buy we will not normally repair your home except for our statutory obligation. 

Your improvements

It is your responsibility to maintain any improvements you have carried out yourself. 

Disabilities

If your home has been adapted or altered to better meet your needs due to a disability, please call us
