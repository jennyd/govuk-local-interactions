Community and Public Transport

Discover more information for planning your journey to travel passes available across South Holland.

Across South Holland there are numerous transport initiatives that can help individuals and groups to access local services.

Call Connect

A demand responsive transport service for all ages. It uses minibuses to link the passengers with bus routes across Lincolnshire. Services pick up and drop off at designated locations in towns and villages in South Holland in response to customers' advance bookings.

Call Connect Buses

Wheels 2 Work

No transport to access work, apprenticeship or training? Wheels 2 Work Lincolnshire may be able to help by providing affordable scooter training and hire.

Wheels to Work

Public Transport - Buses and Trains

Find out details of buses and trains running in South Holland to help plan your travel journeys.

Journey Planner

Holbeach Community Minibus

A charity trust, funded locally, owning a 16 seater minibus that is available to local groups for hire. Contact 01406 422825 to check availability and charges.

South Holland Voluntary Car Scheme

A low cost voluntary car sharing scheme across some parishes in South Holland. The service provides door to door transport for hospital, medical appointments and social trips. Contact the co-ordinator on 01775 630144.

Minibus Brokerage

Lincolnshire County Council have created a list of local community minibus providers. Minibuses are available to community groups to hire for trips and outings.

Minibus search

Car Sharing

Many people in Lincolnshire car share which is a fantastic way of saving money and reduce your carbon footprint. Lincshare is a free travel matching service for all those who live, work and travel in and around Lincolnshire.

Car Sharing

Community Transport Search

If you are not sure what community transport might be available to you, an opportunity is available to search voluntary or community transport schemes for your location.

Community Transport Search

Travel Passes - Lincolnshire Concessionary Fares

Travel concessions for those who are of pensionable age or anyone who qualifies for a pass on grounds of disability are now available through Lincolnshire County Council.

Lincs County Council

Funding for transport

Groups can request a donation for projects related to wheels, transport and the motor industry (including minibuses).

Minibus funding
