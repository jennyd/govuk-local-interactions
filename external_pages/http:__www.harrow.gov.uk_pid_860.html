Food business registration

All business selling or supplying food or drink must send a completed Food Premises registration form

Skip permits

If you wish to put a skip on the public highway, it needs to be licenced. If the skip is to be situated on private land it does not need a licence

Scrap metal dealers

Apply for a licence to be a scrap metal dealer
