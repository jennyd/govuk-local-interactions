Council Tax Support

Listen

Council Tax Support is available to help people who have to pay Council Tax. Check your eligibility and find information about how to make a claim.

Alternatively you can claim Second adult rebate Please note that you cannot get both Council Tax Support and second adult rebate at the same time. If you qualify for both, you will receive the one that pays the most.

What is Council Tax Support?

Council Tax Support is available to help people who have to pay Council Tax.

Can I claim Council Tax Support and how

To be considered for Council Tax Support you will need to complete an application form.

Backdating your Council Tax Support claim

We can backdate your claim if you have good reason for not claiming earlier.

Second adult rebate

Second adult rebate is a rebate you can get on your Council Tax if you can't get help through the Council Tax Support scheme.

Non-dependants

If you are claiming Housing Benefit and/or Council Tax Support, an adult person who lives with you will be treated as a 'non-dependant'.

Disputes and appeals

Information on how to dispute and appeal against your local authority's decision on claims for Council Tax Support and Housing Benefit.
