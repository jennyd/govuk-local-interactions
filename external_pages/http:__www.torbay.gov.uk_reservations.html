Reserve a library book/item

  • Reserve - Library Item 


Library members can reserve a library book or other item if it is on loan or at another branch. We will tell you when it is ready for collection.

What you can reserve

  • You can reserve library books, language course DVDs and music CDs.

How to make a reservation

  • Log in to the Library catalogue using your library PIN number
  • Choose the item you want
  • Click the the Reserve Now button
  • Click the tick box to select the item
  • Select the branch you wish to collect from
  • Click the Reserve Selected button to confirm
  • You will receive a postcard when your item is ready to collect
You can also reserve in person at the library by asking a member of staff.

Inter Library Loans Service

If a book you are looking for is not in stock in Torbay Library, ask a member of staff to give you a reservation card to complete and we will do our best to obtain the book for you from another Library within the UK. For details see our Inter-Library Loans page.
