Asbestos

Notification of work with asbestos

All notifications of work with asbestos (including removal) are to be made online. Notifications for both the Health and Safety Executive (HSE) and all Local Authorities (LA) are submitted using the same on-line notifications systems. No other notification method can be used.


Licensable work with asbestos

The Licensed Asbestos Notification Form (ASB5) has been replaced by an on-line only notification form.

Before undertaking licensable work, the asbestos licensee must give at least 14 days notice in writing to the appropriate enforcing authority of the details of the proposed work. The online notification form is suitable for all UK licensed asbestos work and will be sent electronically to the relevant appropriate enforcing authorities.

The notification period starts once you submit this form. You will receive a copy of the submitted form as part of the process. If the information changes you need to update the information submitted.

Follow this link for more information go to the notification form for licensed work with asbestos.


Notifiable Non-licensed Work with asbestos

Notifications to the HSE or a LA about non-licensed work with asbestos containing materials are submitted using the same on-line notification system.

The requirement is to notify the appropriate enforcing authority before work starts so far as is reasonably practicable. You will receive a copy of the submitted form as part of the process.

Follow this link for more information go to the notification form for non-licensed work with asbestos.


Further information on asbestos

Further information on managing and working with asbestos is available on the Health and Safety Executive's website.
