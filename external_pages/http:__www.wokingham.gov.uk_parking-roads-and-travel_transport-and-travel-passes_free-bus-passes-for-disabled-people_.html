Free bus passes for disabled people

Who can apply

You can get a disabled person's bus pass if you live in Wokingham borough and are:

  • Blind or partially sighted
  • Profoundly or severely deaf
  • Without speech
  • Have a disability or injury that has a long-term affect on your ability to walk
  • Have no arms or can't use both your arms
  • Have a learning disability including a big impairment to intelligence or social function

There is no age restriction for a disabled person's bus pass. 

  • How to apply

  • Expand / Collapse

 You can apply by post or email (details on the application form) or in person by visiting our offices at Shute End:

  • Complete a concessionary fares application form (PDF document)  
  • Provide a passport sized photo
  • Send proof of your name, date of birth, address and any disability  - e.g. driving licence, passport, utility bill, letter from doctor, letter from Department for Work and Pensions

If you come to our offices to apply you won't receive your bus pass straightaway.

  • When and where bus pass can be used

  • Expand / Collapse

Free national off-peak bus pass

You can use your free bus pass on:

  • Any registered local bus service in England
  • From 9.30am to 11pm Monday to Friday
  • All day at weekends and bank holidays

Our bus pass

In addition you can use our bus pass on:

  • Any local bus service in Wokingham borough, Reading borough and West Berkshire Council areas between 9am and 9.30am Monday to Friday

  • Companion travel passes

  • Expand / Collapse

You can apply for a companion travel pass if you care for a disabled person who can't travel alone. 

The companion travel pass allows 1 carer to travel for free at the same time as the disabled person.

For more details read the bus pass page.

  • Other disabled transport services

  • Expand / Collapse

Other transport services for disabled people include:

  • Dial-a-Ride bus service door-to-door transport - visit Dial-a Ride website 
  • Keep Mobile transport to anywhere in the UK for advance bookings - visit Keep Mobile website
  • Readibus timetabled and self-booked buses running from 7.15am to 11pm daily - visit  Readibus website
