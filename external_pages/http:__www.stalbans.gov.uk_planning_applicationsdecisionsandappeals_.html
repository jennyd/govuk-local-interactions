Applications, decisions and appeals (weekly lists)

Printer-friendly page

Members of the public are able to view planning and tree works applications at the customer services area located on the ground floor of the Civic Centre or online using our planning applications search facility.

Opening Times are: 
Monday - Thursday 8.45 am - 5.00 pm 
Fridays 8.45 am - 4.30 pm. 

Details of the previous week's planning and tree works applications will be updated on this page each Monday.

Planning applications, tree work applications and decisions

Details of planning and treework applications registered and planning applications determined by week. Published at the top of each weekly list is the deadline date for the call-in period for District Councillors to call an application in to Committee. Only the following applications can be call-in to Committee: Planning Applications; Householder Applications; Certificates of Lawfulness (Existing); Listed Building Consents; Conservation Area Consents; Advertisement Consents and Telecommunications Applications. Other applications, for example Discharge of Conditions or Non-Material Amendments, are determined under delegated powers.

If you have Acrobat Reader version 8 and above, clicking on the hyperlink under each application on these lists will take you directly to the application on our website.

Tree works applications made prior to 19 June 2015 can be viewed in the Trees and Woodlands section of our website.

Planning appeals

For information on the appeals process, please see the Planning Portal's guidance on appeals.  Please note that decisions on Householder Planning Applications  will be issued within eight weeks. (Planning Inspectorate's Guidance on Householder Appeals.) As soon as we receive the Appeal Decision from the Inspectorate, it will be published on our website and can be viewed using our Planning Applications Search facility by entering the original planning application reference number.
