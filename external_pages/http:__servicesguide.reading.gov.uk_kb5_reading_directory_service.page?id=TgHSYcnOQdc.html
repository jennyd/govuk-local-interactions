Cressingham Resource Centre

Cressingham Resource Centre is a Reading Borough Council resource that offers short break (respite) services to families with a child or young person with a disability. The Centre provides services to children and young people aged 5 – 18 years, who live in the Reading Borough area and whose families are significantly struggling to manage their child’s needs. The services offered at Cressingham are daycare sessions and overnight stays.
For further details: Contact the Access & Assessment Team, Tel: 0118 937 3641 to find out if your child is eligible for this service.
