Secondary school

If you are a Cambridgeshire resident, or will be moving into Cambridgeshire, and your child was born between 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005, they are due to transfer to secondary school in September 2016. There is no automatic transfer to secondary school, an application must be made.

Parents of children applying for Year 9 at an upper school, or wishing to apply for a Year 10 place at the University Technical College (UTC), must have applied for a school place by 31 October 2015. Late applications may only be made on paper.

Read our guide: Next steps: Admission to secondary school 2016-17 (pdf, 497kb)

You can apply using a paper application form (pdf, 228kb)

Timetable

31 October 2015 - application deadline

  • All paper applications must be received at our office by noon on Monday 2 November 2015
  • All online applications must be completed by 11.59pm on Saturday 31 October 2015.
  • Applications received after these dates must be made by paper application, and will not be processed until the second round of allocations on 20 April 2016

November 2015 to February 2016

  • The Admissions Team will liaise with schools and other authorities to make sure that, where possible, parents receive a single offer for the highest preference school which is able to offer a place.

1 March 2016 - offer date

  • Letters are posted second class to all parents who applied on time by Saturday 31 October 2015. Email notifications are also sent out to those who applied online and requested confirmation by email.
  • Check your secondary school application offer online
  • View allocation information for oversubscribed schools (pdf, 180kb)

2 March to 31 March 2016

  • Places declined by parents are re-allocated to children currently waiting on the school reserve lists.

13 April 2016 - appeal deadline

  • Deadline for parents to submit an appeal if they wish their appeal to be heard by 16 June 2015.

20 April 2016 - second offer date

  • For applications received between 1 November 2015 and 31 March 2016.
  • Last years Allocation Information September 2015 - round 2 (pdf 167kb)

April 2016 onwards – secondary appeals

  • Secondary admission appeals take place.

September 2016

  • Your child starts secondary school.

Applying for other admission rounds

Year 9 - transfer to upper school

You can make an application via the Council for children transferring to Year 9 at upper school. For further information please refer to Transferring to upper school guide (pdf, 33kb). Please note if you to apply for a Year 9 place at a Cambridgeshire secondary school, this must be made through the in-year application process.

Year 10 - University Technical College

You may apply for a Year 10 place at Cambridgeshire's University Technical College (UTC). For further information on how to apply, please refer to UTC admissions guide (pdf, 520kb) and UTC application form (pdf, 228kb).

Year 10 - Cambridge Regional College

Cambridge Regional College will also admit into Year 10 for September 2016 entry. They can be contacted on 01223 418 200 or enquiry@camre.ac.uk

National Offer Date – 1 March 2016

If you applied online for your child’s secondary school place for September 2016, you can view the results of the allocation process online from 1 March. Please log in to the online facility using your previously registered details.

Confirmation of offers will be emailed to parents who applied online and requested it, from 9am onwards on 1 March 2016.

Written confirmation of offered places will be dispatched to all applicants, via second class post, on the 1 March 2016.

  • To accept your school offer please contact the school directly.
  • To decline your school offer, please complete the refusal slip on your letter and return it to the Admissions Team.

If your child has been refused a place at any of your preferred schools you are entitled to an independent appeal against this decision.
