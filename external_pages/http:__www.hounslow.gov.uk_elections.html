Elections and Voting

Notice of by-election for Cranford Ward

Following the death of Cllr Sangha, a by-election has been called for the Cranford Ward. The election will take place on Thursday 11 February 2016. Poll cards will be issued to all registered electors who live in the Cranford Ward by Monday 11 January and any residents in the ward who are not currently registered will have until Tuesday 26 January to apply to register.

The list of candidates at the by-election can be found in the Statement of Persons Nominated which you can downloaded from the right hand side of this page.
