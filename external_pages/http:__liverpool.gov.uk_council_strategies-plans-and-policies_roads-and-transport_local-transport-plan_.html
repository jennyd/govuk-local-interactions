Local transport plan

The Local Transport Plan aims to have a joined up transport network for Merseyside and ensure that the people of Merseyside can easily get to public transport.

Produced jointly by the five Merseyside local authorities (Liverpool, Knowsley, Sefton, Wirral, St Helens), it sets out a 10 year strategy and a five year plan with the following objectives:

  • To support development.
  • To slow the growth in car use.
  • To make the most efficient use of the existing transport network.
