To arrange a bulky item collection, please call us 0208 770 5000 and then select option 6.

Sutton residents can dispose of their unwanted household waste and recyclables for free at Kimpton Park Way reuse & recycling centre. If you are unable to transport your bulky household waste to Kimpton Park Way, a bulky household waste service is available for residential properties.

Standard Household Furniture

Standard bedroom, living room and dining room furniture items can be collected (see below for the list of items we collect). Please also note:

  • pre-booking and payment must be completed before the collection can take place

  • bulky items are charged at £25 for up to 3 items and £5 for each additional item 

  • payments are non-refundable

  • items must be placed outside of the property, on the ground floor, and in a place that is visible and accessible from the highway from 06:00 on the collection day

  • there may be a charge to return to the property if items are not out from 06:00 on the day of collection.

To arrange a bulky item collection, please call us 0208 770 5000 and then select option 6.

Items we collect:

  • bed base

  • cabinets

  • chairs (including arm chairs)

  • desks

  • headboards

  • mattresses

  • sofas

  • tables

  • other standard furniture

Electrical Items and White Goods

Electrical items and white goods are priced individually. Please note:

  • Pre-booking and payment must be completed before the collection can take place

  • payments are non-refundable

  • items must be placed outside of the property, on the ground floor, and in a place that is visible and accessible from the highway from 06:00 on the collection day

  • there may be a charge to return to the property if items are not out from 06:00 on the day of collection

To arrange an electrical item collection, please call us 0208 770 5000 and then select option 6.

Items we collect:

  • cookers/ovens/hobs £8.40

  • fridges/freezers £20.00

  • tumble dryers £8.40

  • dishwashers/washing machines £9.80

  • other small electrical items (e.g. DVD players, computer units, etc) £5.00

Items we are unable to collect:

Large items like rubble and cast iron baths are excluded from the service, but these can be disposed of at Kimpton Park Way reuse & recycling centre.  Examples of items that can not be collected are:

  • household fixtures and fittings - doors, window frames, double glazing units, skirting boards, kitchen units, fitted cupboards, worktops, sinks, baths, toilets, fitted carpets, pianos

  • outdoor items - large garden play equipment, rubble, bricks, fence panels

  • organic waste - garden cuttings, food waste

  • hazardous waste - paint, chemicals, asbestos, fluorescent lights
