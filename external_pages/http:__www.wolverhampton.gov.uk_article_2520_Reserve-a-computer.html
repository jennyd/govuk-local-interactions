Reserve a computer

You can reserve a computer at any Wolverhampton library online.

Library members can use a computer in Wolverhampton's libraries for up to 3 hours per day.

All computers have:

  • Internet access
  • Microsoft Office applications including Word, Access, Excel and Powerpoint

Free Internet access

Under 18s will need their parents or guardians' permission to use the computer and Internet facilities.

Before using the free Internet service, you must agree to the conditions of use and Internet policy at the library.

You'll need to book in advance as the computer facilities are very popular.

If you want to book a computer online, please try to give us at least 48 hours notice.
