Apply for a school place

Apply for a school place

Online applications for starting school in the reception class, transferring from infant to junior school, from junior or primary school to secondary school, or from secondary school to year 10 of a UTC (University Technical College), in September 2016 are now closed. If you need to apply please contact us for a paper form to be sent to you.

In year transfers

If you want your child to move school at any other time (for example if you have moved house) this is known as an in year transfer.

Apply for an in year transfer

You can contact the service if you would rather a paper form be sent to you.

Supplementary information for all Catholic and some Church of England Schools

If you are applying for a place at one of the Catholic schools in the area, or at the Church of England primary schools in Eastoft, Owston Ferry, or Wootton, in addition to completing the main application form either online or on paper you will also need to complete a supplementary form.
