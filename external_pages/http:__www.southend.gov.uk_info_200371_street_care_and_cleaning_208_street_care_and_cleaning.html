Street Care and Cleaning

The Environmental Care Team aims to help all members of the community to live, work and visit a clean and tidy town by tackling:

  • untidy sites - private land, over growth and waste
  • dumped rubbish
  • overgrown gardens
  • fly tipping
  • fly posting
  • vandalism and graffiti
  • dog fouling
  • damaged dog waste bins
  • abandoned vehicles.

Please use our online forms to report any of these issues.

We aim to respond to all reports within 24 hours and keep you updated on the course of action to be taken.

Leaf Fall

In the autumn roads with a large number of street trees receive additional visits in order to remove accumulated fallen leaves. A list of streets and their scheduled visits can be found here.
