Welcome to the official website of Cumbria Youth Alliance!

We support the young people of Cumbria and support other organisations that work with young people.

If you are a young person and want to find out how CYA can help you, just click the ‘For Young People’ tab to see the different projects you can get involved in and a contact form to get in touch with us!

If you are a professional working with young people, check out the ‘For Professionals’ tab to see what our projects could offer you. It is also worth checking our Training and Events and News (including our latest newsletters and Funding gazettes!)

If you would prefer to chat to one of the team, just call us on 01900 603131.

ABOUT US

About

Established in 1997, Cumbria Youth Alliance has provided infrastructure support for voluntary sector organisations.

Mission

“Working with and for young people to enable them to reach their full potential”

Description

Cumbria Youth Alliance works with young people through the number of projects we run and also with organisations that work with young people. For more info see our member director
