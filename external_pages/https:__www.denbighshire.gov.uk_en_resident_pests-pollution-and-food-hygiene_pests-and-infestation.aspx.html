As of 1st March 2015, we no longer take any new requests for pest control treatment in private homes.

Pests in a private home

If you have problems with pests you can arrange for a private pest control company to visit you, or you can treat the problem yourself.

Get pest control advice from the British Pest Control Association


Pests in a council housing property

In some cases, we may be able to treat the problem if you are a council housing tenant.

Report a pest control problem in a council house 


Pests in public places

If you are concerned about pests in a public place, please let us know.

Report a pest control problem in a public place


Seagulls

It is illegal to intentionally injure or kill a seagull, or damage or destroy an active nest.  However, there are some things you can do to discourage seagulls from nesting.

Get advice on discouraging seagulls from the RSPB
