Book a pest control service

We offer pest control services to local residents through a partnership agreement with pest control specialists - MITIE Pest Control.

Find out more using the links below:

How quickly can we help?

There is a one-working-day response time for health hazards such as rats, mice, bees and wasps (subject to costumers availability and access).

All other requests for service have a three working day response time (after notification and subject to customer's/your availability and access).

An out of hours service is available for cases posing a serious and urgent threat to health.

Back To Top

How to book

Call MITIE Pest Control for details on 01242 696969. When calling, let the member of staff know you are a Lichfield District Council resident.

Back To Top

How much will it cost?

£36 - Rats
This service is free of charge if you can provide documentation to the contractor that you are in receipt of one of the following benefits:

  • Income Support
  • Job Seekers Allowance
  • Employment and Support Allowance
  • Pension Credit

£60 - All other pest control treatments including mice, wasps, fleas, etc.

All prices include VAT, charged at 20%

Back To Top

How many times will you visit?

Rats and mice have a minimum of two visits and a maximum of four. All other treatments have a minimum of one visit.
