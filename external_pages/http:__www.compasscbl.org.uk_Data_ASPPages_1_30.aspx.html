Welcome to Compass

Looking for a property to rent in the Tees Valley area? Our aim is to help you find an affordable home and to give you more choice in deciding where you would like to live.

We offer a wide range of affordable housing options, including properties for social rent, low cost home ownership, mutual exchange and accredited private rented accommodation throughout Middlesbrough, Stockton, Hartlepool, Redcar and Darlington.
