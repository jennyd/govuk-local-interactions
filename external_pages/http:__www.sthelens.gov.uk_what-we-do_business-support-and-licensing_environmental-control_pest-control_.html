Pest Control

Introduction

Pests can be a real nuisance, damage your home, affect your business or endanger your health… The good news is that our Pest Control service can help get rid of them, often free of charge!

We provide a rapid response and offer advice and treatments for a wide range of problems in both domestic and commercial premises, from public health pests such as rats, mice, fleas and cockroaches, to nuisance pests such as wasps and ants.

Domestic Services

  • FREE consultation & advice for all local residents
  • FREE home treatments for rats, mice, fleas, bedbugs or cockroaches
  • No need for you to call a commercial pest control company and pay to have the problem removed!
  • Low, one-off cost (including VAT) for treating other pests: £41 for ants, wasps, or bees (prices for 2015/16);
  • £78 for squirrels; £98 for moles (prices for 2015/16)
  • FREE information on future pest prevention
  • Efficient, reliable customer service

Commercial Pest Control

  • FREE consultation & advice to local businesses
  • Lowest cost pest control services in the Northwest
  • Discreet, reliable customer service
  • 100% business customer satisfaction rate (2010)
  • FREE information on future pest prevention
  • Different service options

(a)    Individual treatment service – these are chargeable at £50 +VAT for 2015/16 per hour (minimum 1 hour), regardless of what pest is treated.
(b)    Annual service contract -  to suit individual needs; an Officer will visit to discuss your requirements & provide a quote

Customer Service Charter

Our staff will:

  • Be qualified, experienced and courteous
  • Identify themselves and show their official identification if you ask for it.
  • Inspect your home or business to confirm which pest is causing the problem.
  • Tell you how they intend to treat the problem, and what precautions you should take
  • Advise you if there will be a charge for the treatment before proceeding
  • Use safe, approved materials and methods
  • Lay bait in a manner which avoids harm to other creatures
  • Leave you a treatment record sheet telling you which pesticide has been used
  • Make a further visit to check how effective the treatment has been and lay further baits if necessary
  • Advise you how to try and prevent the problem from occurring again.

Contact Us

If you would like us to help, please contact us directly via 01744 676338 or environmentalhealth@sthelens.gov.uk
A Pest Control Officer will then respond to you as soon as possible in order to discuss your problem and make an appointment if necessary. 
We normally operate from Mondays to Fridays during office hours, i.e. 9am to 5pm. 
In case of emergency at weekends, early mornings, or late at night, please contact 01744 676728.

Sewer-Baiting

The Council undertakes an annual sewer-baiting programme in order to proactively control rat populations throughout the Borough.  This is part funded by United Utilities, and is conducted in two 6-week programmes beginning in November and in February. 

Data collected from both rodent complaints and previous sewer baiting programmes is used to ensure that treatments are targeted at the areas of highest rat activity.
