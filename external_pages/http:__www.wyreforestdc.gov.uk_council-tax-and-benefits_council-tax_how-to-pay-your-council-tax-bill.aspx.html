How to Pay your Council Tax

You will need your council tax account number (can be found in the top right hand corner of your bill) or barcode to make payments.

Manage your account online, sign up to My Tax and Benefits, where you can view installments, due dates, report any changes to your household and apply for a discount or exemptions online.

Check your Council Tax balance 

Don't forget that you can also ask to spread your Council Tax bill payments over 12 months instead of 10.

Please note, we do not accept cash payments at Wyre Forest House

Direct debit

Direct Debit is one of the safest and most reassuring ways of paying your bill as payments are made automatically.
Sign up to Direct Debit online or download Direct debit form, you can also sign up and manage your account at My Tax and Benefits 
Online

You can make safe, secure payments at any time of the day or night from your PC, tablet or mobile. 

Make an online payment

*Please note- a charge of 2.25% applies to credit card payments

Check your Council Tax balance
24 hour automated telephone service

Call us 0300 456 2724

At Paypoint/Post Office branches

Your new bill will have a bar code on it, this will enable you to pay at any Paypoint outlet or Post Office. You must have the bill with you to pay.

Please note - you can pay by card or cash at Post Offices but not all PayPoint outlets will accept card payments. Those that do may charge a small fee.
