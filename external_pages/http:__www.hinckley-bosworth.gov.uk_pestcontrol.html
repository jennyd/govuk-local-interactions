Pest control

Overview of pest control

Pests we treat

We treat pests in both private and commercial properties.

We treat the following pests. Find out about a service and how much it costs:

  • Crawling insects: cockroaches, bed bugs, carpet beetles and ants (commercial properties only)
  • Flying insects: wasps, flies, fleas, moths
  • Rodents: rats and mice

Waiting time

There is a waiting time for this service as it is a popular service with very competitive fees.

Pests we don't treat 

The council does not provide a service to deal with the following pests. If you have a problem with these pests you will have to engage the services of an independent pest control company:

  • Bees
  • Birds
  • Weevils
  • Squirrels
  • House dust mites
  • Larder beetles
  • Moles
  • Silverfish
  • Slugs
  • Foxes
  • Woodlice
  • Foxes
