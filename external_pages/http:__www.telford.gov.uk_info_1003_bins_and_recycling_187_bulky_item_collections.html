Bulky item collections

Bulky household items such as furniture and electrical appliances cannot be collected as part of your normal bin collection.

Items we will collect

Items we will NOT collect

How much will it cost

We charge £18 per collection, which is for up to six items.

Please note your collection booking must be paid for in advance of the collection date.

Discounts

A discount is available to residents who meet one of the following conditions:

  • Telford Loyalty Card (TLC) holders receive a 10% reduction, paying £16.20 – your TLC number must be provided when booking.
  • households where a resident is 65 years of age or over receive one free bulk collection during 2015/2016 – your date of birth must be provided when booking.
  • residents in receipt of Council Tax Support or Housing Benefit receive a 25% reduction, paying £13.50 – your reference number must be provided when booking.
  • residents who have a long standing illness or disability that limits your daily activities receive one free collection during 2015/2016.
  • As part of the pride in your community programme, additional pilot campaigns are taking place in targeted areas throughout the Borough.

Preparing for collection

Do:

  • leave all items out from 7am on the day of collection
  • make sure items are outside your property, all in one place and can be easily reached
  • remove any nails from wood or furniture
  • tie, tape or bundle large amounts of wood like flooring, old furniture units, branches or hedging
  • tape up any glass - we can only lift panes of glass if they are still in their frame and have been taped over

Don't:

  • leave items inside your property, we can't enter parts of your home, like garages and sheds, to collect items
  • leave out any items other than those you originally booked with us - we can’t collect them
  • leave out waterlogged carpets or mattresses
