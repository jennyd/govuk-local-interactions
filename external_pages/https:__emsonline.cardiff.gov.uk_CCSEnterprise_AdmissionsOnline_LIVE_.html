Welcome to Cardiff Council's Online Admissions System

Cardiff School Admissions 2016/2017

Admission to Schools Booklet 2016/2017

The Online Admissions System accepts applications for places in Community Primary Schools and Community Secondary Schools for September 2016. The closing date for applications is

Monday 30 November 2015 - Transfer to Secondary Education 2016

Monday 11 January 2016 - Admission to Primary Education 2016

For a list of schools in Cardiff please follow this link

If you would like to make an application in Welsh, please use Newid i Gymraeg (see link above).

Please note that Roman Catholic schools, Church in Wales schools and Whitchurch High School (Foundation School) have separate admission arrangements and application forms to other LA schools. Please contact the school directly for more information on how to apply for a place.

Applications for a place in schools outside of Cardiff County should be made directly to the Local Authority concerned.

How to apply?

Before you begin parents/guardians are required to register. Once your registration is complete, you will be able to proceed with your online application, detailing up to 3 Community School preferences. Your application can be viewed and amended at any time up until the closing date of:

Monday 30 November 2015 - Transfer to Secondary Education 2016

Monday 11 January 2016 - Admission to Primary Education 2016

For guidance on how to register on this site or if you experience any difficulties whilst making your application on line, please Contact Us (see link below).

Why Apply Online?

Please read the information below which explains the benefits of applying online for school places.

  • It is quick and easy

  • You can apply from home 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

  • There is no risk your application will be lost in the post

  • You will receive an email confirming receipt that your application has been submitted and received by the Authority

  • The system is secure and has a series of security features that will prevent others from seeing your information

  • You will be able to view your offer on your online account on the published offer day
