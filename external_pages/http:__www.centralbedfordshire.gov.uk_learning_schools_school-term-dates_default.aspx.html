School holidays and school term dates

2016 - Spring school term and school holidays
  • term starts on 5 January
  • half term from 15 to 19 February
  • term ends for the Easter school holidays on 24 March
2016 - Summer school term and school holidays
  • term starts on 12 April
  • half term from 31 May to 3 June (30 May is a bank holiday)
  • summer term ends on 22 July
2016 - Autumn school term and school holidays
  • term starts on 5 September
  • half term from 24 October to 31 October
  • term ends on 16 December
2017 - Spring school term and school holidays
  • term starts on 4 January
  • half term from 13 to 17 February
  • term ends for the Easter school holidays on 4 April
2017 - Summer school term and school holidays
  • term starts on 19 April
  • half term from 30 May to 6 June (29 May - bank holiday)
  • summer term ends on 21 July
Do school term dates vary?

The school term dates and school holidays will be correct for the majority of schools. However, school term dates can vary slightly between schools eg for teacher training days at the start or end of a school term. The school should advise you in advance of any changes to their school term dates. If you are in any doubt, please contact the school directly.

BCUS is the Biggleswade Community Union of Schools

BCUS is the Biggleswade Community Union of Schools. It comprises of:

  • Stratton Academy
  • Biggleswade Academy and Edward Peake Middle School
  • Caldecote V C Lower Dunton Lower
  • St Andrews Lower
  • Lawnside Lower
  • Northill Lower and The Lawns
