Who can apply?

Concessionary passes enable eligible people to travel on reduced fares or for free across Greater Manchester and in some cases nationally.

Please Note: Concessionary passes are issued by Transport for Greater Manchester (TfGM).  Manchester City Council does not issue concessionary travel passes.

Please use the concessionary fares page of the Transport for Greater Manchester (TfGM) website for full details of who is eligible and how to apply for a pass.

Information about concessionary fares and passes can be found using the links below;

  • Low fares and free travel - over 60
  • Low fares and free travel - disabled people
  • Low fares and free travel - blind and partially sighted people
  • Low fares and free travel - deaf people
  • Low fares and free travel - under 16
  • Low fares - young people over 16
