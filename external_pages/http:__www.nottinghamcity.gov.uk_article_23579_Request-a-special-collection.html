Request a special collection

Contact Waste Management

The council offers a free collection service to city residents for bulky household items such as sofas and carpets.

If you are aged 16 or over (and eligible to vote) you will need to be on the Electoral Register to prove that you live in the city at the address stated below and are therefore eligible to receive this service.

We are unable to collect certain restricted items such as tyres and car batteries.
