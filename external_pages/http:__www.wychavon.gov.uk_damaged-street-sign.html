Report Damaged Street Sign

For Highways Signs e.g. Speed Limit, Village signs, etc. Please use the following online reporting system.

*

To help us find the exact location, please tell us the nearest landmark, ie if it is close to a particular house, shop, or road junction.

*

In order to help us with your request quickly, please fill in as many details as possible. It would help us to deal with the problem quickly if you could give a brief description. For instance, is the sign on a wall, a post or a railing?

*

Please give the time and date that you first noticed the problem.

You do not need to give us your name but it would be helpful to us to have a contact telephone or email in case we need further information to follow up this report.
