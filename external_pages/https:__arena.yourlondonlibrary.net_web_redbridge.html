Welcome to Redbridge Libraries!

On this website you will find everything that is available in our libraries and much more

Join online to take full advantage of what the libraries have on offer for you.

Use the top search box and advanced search page for more options. Search the catalogue and filter the hits to find precisely what you are looking for.. On the way you will find lots of exciting suggestions that will give you the inspiration to read, listen to and watch things you may not have thought of yourself.
 
The website will also enable you to join in discussions on the forums, provide readings tips, make recommendations and write reviews, amongst other things.
