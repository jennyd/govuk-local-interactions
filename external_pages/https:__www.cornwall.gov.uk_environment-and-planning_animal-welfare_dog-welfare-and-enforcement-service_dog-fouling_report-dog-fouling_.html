Report dog Fouling

Last updated: 08/06/2015

Please use the form below to report dog fouling. We will investigate all complaints that are received - although the level of service that we can provide will obviously vary depending upon the quality of information that is provided.  For example we would be in a better position to take action should you be able to provide a full description of what happened, including date, time, location, what you witnessed, description of the dog/owner and as much information as possible about the incident - also any information regarding the offender - which could include name/address (if known), vehicle registration number or any other identifying feature.
