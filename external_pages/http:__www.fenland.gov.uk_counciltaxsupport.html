Council Tax Support and Housing Benefit

Information about how to claim and our online benefit calculator, which tells you how much support you could get. This section also gives information about: reporting a change of circumstances, overpayments, fraud, appealing against a decision, discretionary housing payment and local housing allowance...

If you're unemployed, in work with a low income, or a pensioner you may be able to claim Housing Benefit or Council Tax Support.

Housing Benefit is there to help you meet the cost of your rent. Council Tax Support is similar to Housing Benefit, and can help you meet the cost of your Council Tax if your name is on the bill. You can complete an online Benefits Calculator, which will tell you if you may be entitled for Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support based on the information you give. More information about this, and how to claim, can be found in the sections below.

We just look after Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support. You can also check if you're entitled to other benefits from the Government here.
