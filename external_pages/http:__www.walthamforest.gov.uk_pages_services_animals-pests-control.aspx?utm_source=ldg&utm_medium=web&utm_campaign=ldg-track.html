Report a problem with vermin

Report a problem with rats in an open space online via our online form.

If you are unable to complete our online form please email wfdirect@walthamforest.gov.uk, call 020 8496 3000,& or visit a Library Plus branch for advice on how to complete the form.

Please do not use this form for reporting ants in public places, or pest issues in domestic or commercial premises such as maggots in bins.

Pest control services

Pest control services are provided for residents of the borough, based on risk to health. This is reflected in our cost of treatment for the particular type of infestation.

To arrange treatment please contact Waltham Forest Direct on 020 8496 3000.

For information on the services we provide contact us on wfdirect@walthamforest.gov.uk or phone 020 8496 3000.

Paying for pest control services

Payments must be settled in advance of the pest control officer arriving for the appointment.

Payments are made at the time of booking the appointment, we take credit/debit card payment on the phone when you are arranging your treatment appointment.

In order to benefit from the reduced charge or licensed landlord charge you will need to provide evidence of your eligibility at the time of payment, eg license number for landlords.

Treatment of commercial premises by arranged price

To arrange treatment please contact Waltham Forest Direct on 020 8496 3000. For further information on the services we provide please contact us on wfdirect@walthamforest.gov.uk or phone 020 8496 3000.

No treatment is guaranteed, Further visits will be charged.

Call-out / cancellation fee

A cancellation fee of £36 is applicable where an appointment is cancelled within less than one working day (less than 24 hours' notice) or an appointment is not kept. A call out fee of £36 is applicable if a Pest Control officer is dispatched.

Does someone need to be in when the pest control officer arrives

Yes. We will not enter premises unaccompanied for our own security and health and safety reasons. We need to leave advice and information with a responsible person after our treatment. Our morning appointments are from 8am-12pm and afternoon from 12pm-3pm.

Not sure what pest you've got...

If you don’t know what type of pest is causing the problem, we can help.

Drop off a sample (in a sealed see-through sealed container or jar) together with your name and contact details at:

Sycamore House Reception
Waltham Forest Town Hall Site
Forest Road
Walthamstow E17 4JF

Between 9am-5pm Monday-Friday.

And a pest control officer will contact you within 48 hours about the type of pest.
