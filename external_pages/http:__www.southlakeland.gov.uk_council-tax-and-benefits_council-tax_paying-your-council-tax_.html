Pay for it

Make a payment online

We usually process payments within three working days.  When making a payment have a pen and paper handy to write down the payment reference.

Payment Cards you can use
Council Tax Debit  
Parking Penalty Charge Notices Debit and Credit
South Lakes Housing Rents Debit and Credit
Business Rates Debit
Sundry invoices Debit and Credit
Housing Benefit overpayments Debit and Credit
Business Improvement District Kendal Debit

We accept

  • Visa or Maestro debit cards
  • Visa or Mastercard credit cards  

Other methods of payment

You can also pay
  • by direct debit
  • bytelephone
  • by post with a cheque
  • at local post offices and shops in the district
  • directly to our bank account
You cannot pay council tax or national non domestic rates by credit card.
