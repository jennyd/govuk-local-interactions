Request bulky waste pick-up

What we can and can’t collect

We can help dispose of bulky household items that are too large for your regular waste collection. We can collect most freestanding items or furniture that can be safely carried between two people, that you would normally take with you if you were to move house.

We do make a charge for providing this service, please see What it will cost below for prices.

We can also arrange for the collection of electrical goods. This includes petrol driven lawnmowers and any items fitted with an electrical plug or using batteries for power.

Items that we can't take should be taken to your local recycling centre. Please refer to Devon County Council's Recycling Centres webpage as there may be a charge for the disposal of certain items. We do not collect commercial fridges and freezers from business premises. Please see our Commercial waste & recycling webpages if you need to dispose of trade waste.

When we collect

Bulky waste can be collected at any time between 6.00am and 4.00pm on the day of collection.

Non-electrical items

Non-electrical items are collected every Thursday, except after bank holidays (see Check collection dates) and we can accommodate up to 15 bookings per Thursday, which is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Providing there are slots available, you will need to book the pick-up by 5.00pm on Tuesday for collection the same week.

We will ask you to leave non-electrical items outside your property the night before or by 6.00am on the day of collection.

Electrical items

Electrical items will need to be kept dry, which may mean that you will need to be at home when they are collected. Electrical items are collected separately, fortnightly on Thursdays. Please contact us to enquire when the next electrical collection is due.

What it will cost

One item (electrical or non-electrical)* £18
Each additional item £7
Fixed price items** £25

 

*A bed, headboard, base and mattress counts as one item.

**Fixed price items include:

  • Table including up to 4 chairs
  • Three-piece suite (settee and 2 armchairs)

 

Ten or more items will require a visit from an officer to provide a quote. Please contact us if you wish to arrange this.

If you wish to book a bulky waste collection, please telephone our Customer First team on 01884 255255. We can accept payment over the telephone using a debit or credit card, or by cash or cheque at one of our offices, see Contact details and opening hours.

Have you thought about reuse and recycling?

If you have any items which are still in a decent working condition, please think about ways of re-using or donating your items to local charities and reuse organisations such as:

  • Turntable Furniture
  • British Heart Foundation
  • ReFURNISH
  • Freecycle Network
  • Freegle
  • Freely Wheely
