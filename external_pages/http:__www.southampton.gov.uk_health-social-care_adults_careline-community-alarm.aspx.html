Careline - community alarm

The CAREline Community Alarm is a personal alarm button, which can be worn as a necklace or a wrist strap at home. In an emergency, when you press the button, it automatically dials the CAREline service.

CAREline is available 24 hours a day, every day of the year. The alarm unit is easily connected to your telephone and electric socket.

You can use CAREline:

  • at any age
  • if there are long term health needs
  • for older people and the elderly frail
  • if you are threatened with harassment and repeated burglary.

Apply for CAREline

What happens when I press the alarm button?

You will hear a customer advisor ask you if there is a problem and how they can help. They will already know who you are, where you live and any important medical information. If you need help they will either contact a key holder, your doctor, the emergency services or a CAREline responder. Who they call will depend on which CAREline service you are using.

Even if you are unable to speak we will know who you are and where you live. If you are unable to respond, we will send help to your home.

Costs and Services

The set up fee includes rental of the alarm unit, installation and advice from CAREline staff.

  • £10 plus VAT if you live within Southampton
  • £20 plus VAT If you live outside the city

After set up two levels of service are offered:

Silver service – monitoring

This service is available to anyone within a 20 mile radius of Southampton. A key-holder will be called as soon as the customer service advisor is aware of your problem.

You will need to provide details of two people, who live at different addresses in the Southampton area. They need be prepared to hold a key to your home and be physically able and willing to help you when required.

Cost: £2.50 a week, plus VAT

Gold service - monitoring and response

Gold service is only available to residents living in Southampton.

A professional team of CAREline responders, employed by Southampton City Council, will be sent to visit you in an emergency. They will quickly retrieve your door key from a key-safe, which we install on the exterior of your property. The key-safe is fitted with a code known only to CAREline staff.

Cost: Key-safe installation: £40 plus VAT (This payment can be spread over instalments by arrangement). Then £3.50 a week plus VAT.

VAT – you may not have to pay VAT if you have a disability, injury or illness – please contact us for more information:

community.alarm.service@southampton.gov.uk
