Schools and catchment areas

Designated or "catchment" areas

Most schools have a designated area which has highest priority for admissions. However parents can apply for places at any school suitable for their child even if they do not live in this designated area.

You can find out your nearest school and which designated area you live in on our find your nearest page - note the information on this page is under review, contact our admission team for full details of nearest local schools and for a copy of our admission guide

This search will include information about designated areas we set for community and voluntary controlled schools.
