In order to report a missed bin collection, you must first login (below) or register for Wealden 24/7.

You may find the information below useful:

Check your bin day

If you are unsure when your collection days are or what to put in each bin, you can find out using our bin collection day search facility.

Deadline for Reporting a Missed Bin

A missed bin must be reported to the Council at the latest by 5pm the following working day after the scheduled collection day. If the missed bin was not reported before this deadline, we are unable to arrange collection. You will need to wait until your next scheduled collection, or take the waste and/or recycling to a Household Waste Recycling Site (external link).

Qualifying for a Missed Bin Collection

In order to receive a missed bin collection, you must have met the following criteria:

  • Placed the correct bin/container out for collection
  • Placed the bin/container out for collection before 7 am
  • The bin/container must not have been contaminated (for example; landfill waste, or cardboard in a green waste bin, glass in the recycling bin, or hard-core in a landfill bin).

If you cannot confirm that the above criteria was met, we are unable to arrange a missed bin collection.  You will need to wait until your next scheduled collection, or take the waste and/or recycling to a Household Waste Recycling Site (external link).

New users, register for Wealden 24/7

To register for a Wealden 24/7 account you need to:

  • Have an email address
  • Live in the Wealden District

Enter your email address below and choose a password. We will then send you an email containing an activation link - just click on the link to activate your account (please check your 'junk' or 'spam' folder if you can't see the email in your inbox).

By registering for a Wealden 24/7 account, you are agreeing to our terms and conditions.

Report a Missed Waste Collection

To report a missed waste collection, please send an e-mail to info@wealden.gov.uk stating the following details:-

  • Your full name, address including post code and telephone contact number
  • The date the collection should have taken place
  • The type of collection that has been missed, i.e. domestic waste bin, recycling bin, recycling box or garden waste bin

Please remember we can only arrange for a missed bin to be collected if it is reported by 5 p.m. the day after the collection day. Bins contaminated with other waste, e.g. domestic refuse in a recycling bin, will not be collected

About Wealden 24/7

Wealden 24/7 allows you to request services from the Council 24 hours a day, 7 day a week. You do not need to wait on the phone, or come into the office.

Along with requesting services, you will be able to view information related to your property.

Registration takes just a few minutes, then you can login to your account.
