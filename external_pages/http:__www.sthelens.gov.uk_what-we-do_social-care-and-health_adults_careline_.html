Careline

About the Careline service

St Helens Councils Careline Service was established  to provide a monitoring and response service to elderly and vulnerable people so they can  maintain independence and security within their own homes. 

The Careline Control Room is operated by specially trained staff who will respond quickly and sensitively,  via a Lifeline Emergency unit, pendant or special installed detector.
