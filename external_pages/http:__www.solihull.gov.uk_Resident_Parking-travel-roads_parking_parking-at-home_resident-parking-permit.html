Resident parking permit

Resident parking permit schemes operate in the following roads:

  • Austcliff Drive
  • Edwardian Close
  • Kilsby Grove
  • Sevington Close
  • Thorngrove Avenue

If you would like to apply for a scheme to be introduced in your road, please complete the residents parking permit scheme application form.

How do I get a permit?

You can apply for either a residents or visitors permit by telephoning 0121 704 6111.

The permit costs £95 and allows you to park on the road during normally restricted hours.

Fees

Each household wishing to join the scheme must pay a joining fee of £180.

In the joining year and annually thereafter, an annual permit charge of £95 is also applicable.
