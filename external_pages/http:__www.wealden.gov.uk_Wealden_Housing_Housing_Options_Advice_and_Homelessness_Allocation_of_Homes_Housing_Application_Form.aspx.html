Housing Application Form

Reveal all
Close all

Thank you for completing the eligibility toolkit, based upon the answers that you gave, you may be eligible to join our housing register. If you would like to apply you need to complete a housing application form. Please complete the electronic version, following the steps below.

Complete the electronic Housing Application form

  1. Electronic Housing Application Form (pdf)
  2. Save the pdf file to your computer
  3. Open the saved form and complete it
  4. Once completed, go to 'Save As', select 'pdf' and save it again on your computer
  5. Attach the completed form to an email and send to: housingapplications@wealden.gov.uk

This form will be processed electronically,  however, a signed copy is also required. Please therefore print your completed form and post it to us, along with any other relevant documents, using the address at the bottom of this page.  If you do not have access to a printer, you may be required to attend the Council's offices in order to sign your application form.

 

If you are unable to complete the application form electronically please download a copy of the Housing Application Form (pdf) and post it to us using the address below.

Please be aware of the following when completing the form:

  • Please ensure you complete every question that applies to you, incomplete forms will be returned to you and will delay the time it takes to process your application.
  • The Housing Register is open to anyone over the age of 16 years who is eligible.
  • This Council maintains a Housing Register on the Orchard Integrated Housing Management Computer System in order to record the personal details, circumstances and preferences, and to assess the relative needs of all eligible applicants.

    All personal information will only be used for the purpose of dealing with your application for housing and for keeping you updated about your application. All data will be held in a secure manner. It will not be used for any other purpose without your permission and will not be kept for longer than necessary.

    We will treat all personal information by which you can be identified (i.e. name, address, email) in accordance with the provisions of the Data Protection Act 1998.
  • The Council will follow our Allocations Policy 2014 (pdf)  when allocating homes.
