Private sector housing

Private sector housing includes owner-occupied housing and housing rented from a private landlord or letting agent.

Our private sector housing teams cover two main areas:

  • owner-occupied housing
  • private rented sector housing

Owner-occupied housing includes advice and assistance for people who own and live in their homes with issues such as energy efficiency, minor adaptations and equipment to help people remain independent and disabled facilities grants.

Private rented sector housing includes advice and assistance for people who live in privately rented accommodation with issues such as housing conditions, tenancy issues and houses in multiple occupation.
