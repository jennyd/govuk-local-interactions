Free learning and childcare for 2 year olds

Family Information Service (fis@kirklees.gov.uk) Last updated - December 2015
On this page
  • Entitlement and application,
  • About free learning and childcare for 2 year olds,
  • When eligible children start their free place,
  • After you have applied,
  • Contact the Family Information Service

Entitlement and application

    To apply for a place now your child must be born between 01/01/2013 and 31/03/2014

  • Apply online (Kirklees Parent Portal)

    • Apply using the Kirklees Parent Portal if you claim one or more of the following benefits:

    • Child Tax Credit and/or Working Tax Credit and your annual household income is less than Â£16,191 (as assessed by Her Majesty's Revenue & Customs).
    • Income Support.
    • Income based Job Seekers Allowance (JSA).
    • Income related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA).
    • Support under part VI of the Immigration and Asylum Act 1999.
    • The guarantee element of State Pension Credit.
    • Universal Credit.

  • If you do not claim any of the above benefits

    • Please contact the Family Information Service (FIS) to apply for a place, as your child will be eligible for a free place if they:

    • Receive Disability Living Allowance (DLA).
    • Have an Education, Health and Care plan.
    • Are looked after by the Council, or have left care through a Special Guardianship order, Child Arrangements order or adoption.
    • Have a Child Protection or Child in Need Plan.

About free learning and childcare for 2 year olds

  • Children can have up to 15 hours a week for 38 weeks of the year (school term time)
  • Some early learning providers offer free hours in the school holidays, for example 11 hours a week for 51 weeks of the year.

When eligible children can start their free place

If your childs 2nd birthday is betweenTheir free place can start from
1 January and 31 Marchsummer term (after Easter)
1 April and 31 Augustautumn term (early September)
1 September and 31 Decemberspring term (early January)

After you have applied

  • If you are eligible, we will send you a voucher to give to your chosen provider.
  • Choose an approved provider by using the childcare search, or contact the Kirklees Family Information Service (FIS).
  • If we cannot confirm that you are eligible we will write to you requesting additional information.
