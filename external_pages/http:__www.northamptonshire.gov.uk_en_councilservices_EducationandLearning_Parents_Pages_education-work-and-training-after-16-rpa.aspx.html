Education, Work and Training post-16 (RPA) 

Raising the Participation Age

All young people are now required to stay in post-16 learning until the end of the academic year they turn 17 - and from September 2015 until they turn 18.

This means making a choice from the following options:

  • Staying on at school in sixth form
  • Studying at a Further Education College
  • Apprentice schemes
  • Finding a job with part-time training
  • Volunteering with training
  • Starting your own business

The resources below will help you to understand what this means for you. If you have any questions or for further information, please contact your school.
