Dog fouling

In Wirral, it is an offence for an owner or keeper of a dog to fail to clear up after their dog has fouled. Dog faeces carry harmful infections such as toxocariasis, which can cause blindness.

  • always carry bags when you take your dog out so you can clean up after it
  • put dog waste in a nearby litter bin or your own wheelie bin

If somebody doesn’t clean up after their dog, report it online.

Report dog fouling

We can issue a £50 fixed penalty notice to the dog walker or owner. We can also prosecute and a court can issue a fine of up to £1000.

The council's enforcement officers patrol areas of Wirral where dog fouling is of particular concern. You can tell us about a place where dog fouling is a real problem, and we will use this information when deciding where to focus our patrols in the future.
