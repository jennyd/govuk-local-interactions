Alcohol, Entertainment & Late Night Refreshment

Alcohol, Entertainment & Late Night Refreshment Licences required for working in licensed trade or entertainment
Personal Licence Authorisation for individuals to supply alcohol at an alcohol-licensed premises.
Premises Licence Authorisation for indoor and outdoor premises to supply alcohol, provide entertainment and late night refreshment
Temporary Event Notice (TEN) Authorisation for indoor and outdoor licensable activities at both licensed and unlicensed premises
Club Premises Certificate Authorisation for club premises to supply alcohol and provide entertainment
