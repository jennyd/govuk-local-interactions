Appeal against a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN)



Due to technical issues which affected our IT systems and the Christmas period we are experiencing a backlog of appeals. All cases that have received an appeal are on hold until a response can be sent. Discount periods will still be honoured if applicable.

In order for us to respond to these appeals as quickly as possible we would be grateful if you did not contact Parking Services to chase up a response, as this will further delay the Parking team from replying to letters.

We apologise for the delay in replying to appeals and for the inconvenience that this is causing and will reply to your correspondence as soon as possible.

Thank you for your patience.

City and County of Swansea Parking Portal

Welcome to the City and County of Swansea parking portal which can be used to pay penalty charge notices and challenge penalty charge notices.

Please select from one of the following options:

  • Challenge a PCN If you have received a Penalty Charge Notice, you can appeal against this online.

    Click here to appeal now.
  • Pay a PCN If you have received a Penalty Charge Notice, you can pay via our online payment system.
