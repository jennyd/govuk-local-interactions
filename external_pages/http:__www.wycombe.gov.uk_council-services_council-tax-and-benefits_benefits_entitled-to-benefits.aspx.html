Am I entitled to benefits?

If you are on a low income, you may be entitled to some help. Depending on your income and circumstances, council tax support and housing benefits can make a significant contribution towards your council tax and rent.

Housing benefit

You can claim housing benefit if you pay rent to the council or a private landlord. You cannot claim housing benefit if you live with, or have to pay rent to a close relative such as a parent, step-parent, son, daughter, stepson, stepdaughter or their partners.

  • local housing allowance

Council tax reduction (from 1 April 2013)

Council tax reduction applies from 1 April 2013. See our council tax reduction page for more information.

Child care benefits

Free childcare for 2 year olds (external website): Buckinghamshire County Council introduced the offer of free childcare places from September 2013 to all eligible families with a 2 year old who live in Buckinghamshire.
