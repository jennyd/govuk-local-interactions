Day Services

Find a day service in your local area

Day services provide opportunities to meet others in a supportive environment, enabling you to enjoy your independence and get involved in a range of group or person centred activities.

The service caters for different needs and is able to adapt activities to suit both your interest and ability. Many people attend every day, taking advantage of the wide variety of activities across multiple sites, whilst others attend once or twice a week for specific interests, or to meet with friends.

There are day services to support a range of needs including older people, those with physical and sensory impairments, and people with learning disabilities.

Contacts

Visit our dedicated website for more information www.nottinghamcity.gov.uk/supportingadults.

If you would like an assessment to see if you may be eligible to receive Council services at a reduced rate, please call Nottingham Health and Care Point on: 0300 300 3333, option 2.

 Black and Minority Ethnic Day Services

The services listed below cater for adults over 65 year, primarily from Black and Minority Ethnic backgrounds

  • The Indian Community Centre in Carrington
  • Marcus Garvey Centre in Lenton , catering primarily for individuals from African and Caribbean backgrounds
  • The Pakistan Centre

Learning Disabilities Day Services

The services below cater for adults over 18 year, with learning disabilities. 

  • Evergreen Day Service in Mapperley
  • Martin Jackaman Centre in Aspley
  • Summerwood Day Service in Clifton
  • Shared Lives is an adult placement scheme, which is able to offer day care for individuals with learning disabilities either out in the community, or in the home of a Shared Lives carer

 Physical and Sensory Impairments Day Service

  • Acorn Resource Centre in Hyson Green, provides a day service for adults over 18 years with physical or sensory impairments

 Older Persons Day Services

Our Old Persons day services, listed below, cater for adults over 65 year, with varying care needs and from varying ethnic backgrounds.

  • Albany House in St Ann's
  • Long Meadow Day Service in The Meadows
  • The Willows Day Service in Beechdale

Mental Health Day Service

  • The Open Door Project in Beechdale, provides a day services for individuals with varying mental health issues
