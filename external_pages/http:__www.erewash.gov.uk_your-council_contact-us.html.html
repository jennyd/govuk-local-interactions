Contact Us

Contacting Erewash Borough Council

You can contact us in a number of different ways, by email, by telephone, via this website, social media or by visiting our service centres at Ilkeston or Long Eaton. You can also contact us by completing a general enquiries online form.

  • Online: General enquiries form
  • Email: This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it.
  • Telephone: 0115 907 2244

Normal Opening Times

Contact centre opening hours:

  • Monday to Thursday 8:30am to 5:00pm
  • Friday 8:30am to 4:30pm

If you would like to telephone our contact centre you may do so on 0115 907 2244. Please note that your calls may be recorded for training and monitoring purposes.

Out of hours Emergencies

For out of hours emergencies please telephone 01629 533078.

Town Hall Reception, Cashiers and General Enquiries opening hours:

  • 8.30am to 5pm Monday to Thursday
  • 8.30am to 4.30pm Fridays

Face to Face Revenues/Benefits Enquiries opening hours:

  • 8.30am to 4.30pm Monday to Thursday*
  • 8.30am to 4.00pm on Friday*

*Please note that between 8:30am and 10am an appointments system is in operation and the drop in service starts at 10am. Appointments can be made by telephoning us on 0115 907 2244 or by calling in at either Ilkeston or Long Eaton Town Hall.

Other Services

For enquiries relating to street lighting, education, road maintenance, and libraries, please contact Derbyshire County Council. To request a road to be gritted, visit their gritting request form.
