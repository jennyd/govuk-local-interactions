Recycling collections

Recycling plastic and glass

What can go in my brown bin?

  • Glass bottles and jars - please remove lids
  • Food and drinks cans - please remove lids
  • Empty aerosols - please remove lids
  • Clean foil only
  • Biscuit/sweet tins
  • Plastic bottles - please remove lids

Below is a list of Codes most Material Recycling Facilities accept:-

  • HDPE Natural - e.g. milk bottles
  • HDPE Coloured - e.g. detergent bottles/shampoo bottles
  • PET Clear - e.g. coke bottles
  • PET Coloured - e.g. pop bottles such as 7up

Please ensure all bottles, jars and cans are empty of any remaining contents and liquid.

Paper and cardboard recycling can be presented for collection co-mingled inside a cardboard box or a sack and will be collected by the cardboard recycling teams.

What can't go in my brown bin?

  • Plastic bags and plastic film
  • Juice, soup and milk cartons 
  • Margarine tubs, ice cream tubs
  • Yoghurt pots
  • Black food trays
  • Cat food pouches
  • Rubber
  • Any other metals
