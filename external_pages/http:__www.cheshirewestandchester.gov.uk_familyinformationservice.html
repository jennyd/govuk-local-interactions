Childcare

Find childcare

You can search for childcare in the Cheshire West and Chester area by using the online search facility.

  • Start a childcare search

Where to find help

Not all childcare services have agreed to their details being shown on the internet, therefore some childcare services will not be listed on this website. If you require additional search options contact us. 

  • Email: fis.west@cheshirewestandchester.gov.uk 
  • Telephone: 0800 0852 863 (Monday - Friday between 8.30am and 4.30pm) 

If you are looking for information on another issue just select the page for you from one of the user zones.

  • Children's Centres
  • Childcare Providers Zone

If you need information, advice or guidance about childcare, free nursery places and benefits available to you contact the Family Information Service:

  • Email: fis.west@cheshirewestandchester.gov.uk 
  • Telephone: 0800 0852 863 (Monday - Friday between 8.30am and 4.30pm)  

We provide a free, impartial and confidential (unless there are reasons for concerns about a child's safety and/or welfare) service to parents and carers, young people and professionals.

We provide information on:

  • Childcare and options available
  • Benefits and grants
  • Parenting support
  • Family matters

Interactive guides for parents and carers of children aged 0 - 18

Every parent or carer wants to know what to do when a child is ill - use these interactive handbooks ‘A Parent’s Guide to Health and Wellbeing’ to learn how to care for your child at home, when to call your GP and when to contact the Emergency Services.

It will point you in the right direction and explain what you can do at home, or where you need to go to get assistance and advice. It also contains general welfare information which will help you keep you and your child safe and healthy and support them to lead a healthy lifestyle.

  • Interactive guides

These handbooks cannot replace specialist care. If you are worried, get further advice. This handbook has been put together with help from health care professionals. All factual content has been sourced from Department of Health, Birth to Five, 2009 edition, NHS Choices, British Association of Dermatologists, The Meningitis Trust and NICE.
