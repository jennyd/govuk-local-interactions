Finance

This section of the site provides information about the financial support available to parents. 

Please note that financial information is now provided by Wiltshire Council's Revenue and Benefits Team. They will operate a helpline Monday - Friday from 9:30am- 4:30pm. Customers who require information should telephone 0300 003 4560.

As the information in this section may still be useful to parents we will continue to publish it and keep it up to date. Our website also includes links to other websites where more detailed information can be found. If you feel a conversation with us would be useful please feel free to call our helpline on 08457 585072.
