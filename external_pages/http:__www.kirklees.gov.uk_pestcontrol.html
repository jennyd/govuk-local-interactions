How we deal with pests

Our aim is to deal with the immediate problem and attempt to discover the root cause to prevent it from happening again. This may include advice and recommendations on minor proofing repairs and, in the case of rats inside properties, a drainage investigation to identify any defects. A drain fault is the most common cause of rats invading properties.

Domestic properties: owner occupiers

  • If you are the owner and occupier of a private property charges apply.

Domestic properties: privately rented property

  • Check your tenancy agreement to see who is responsible for pest control on your property; it may be the landlord's responsibility.
  • Pest control treatment is charged at domestic rates if you, as the tenants, pay for the treatment yourself.
  • If the landlord is responsible for the control of pests at your property (either directly or via a third party such as a letting agent), the landlord or agent must contact us directly to agree the treatment. If they agree, then the work will be charged to the landlord at the commercial rate.

Commercial pest control contracts

  • Commercial pest control contracts are tailored to meet the individual needs of the business by our experienced surveyors.
  • Prices are by quotation, following an on-site assessment.
  • We currently maintain over 500 commercial pest control contracts with a variety of businesses.
  • These include not only food outlets and retailers but also businesses involved in textiles, electrical engineering, and pharmaceuticals.
  • Please contact us to arrange a quotation: 01484 414901

Out of hours service

Given notice, we are able to schedule some appointments for early morning or late afternoon and early evening.

During the busy summer season our staff often work evenings and at weekends, so if you are not available during the day, we may be able to arrange a visit time more convenient for you.

Privately owned properties

  • Must pay the relevant treatment charges plus an additional call-out fee.

Council tenants

We provide an emergency out-of-hours service in the evenings and at weekends for council tenants.

  • Contact Housing Emergency Repairs: 01484 414850 and ask for Pest Control.

Other services

During the busy summer season our staff often work evenings and at weekends, so if you are not available during the day, we may be able to arrange a visit time more convenient for you.

Insect identification service (free of charge)

  • Send a sample of the insect, along with your name and address and telephone number to the Pest Control Service at the address given below.
  • Please give as much information as possible about where the insect was found.
  • You will be contacted if the insect is considered a potential hazard to health.

Pigeons

  • We do not trap or poison pigeons.
  • We do provide a service for pigeon proofing buildings. It is a chargeable service and is priced by quotation.
  • Under certain circumstances, we will carry out a pigeon cull by shooting, but under no circumstances will shooting take place outside or in public places
