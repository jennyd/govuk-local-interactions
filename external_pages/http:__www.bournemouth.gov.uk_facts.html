Bournemouth Children's Information Service Directory

We provide free information and guidance on early years education and child care in Bournemouth.

Our friendly team aims to provide you with enough information to enable you to make choices about childcare that are best suited for you and your child.  If you are unable to find what you are looking for or need more information? Call us on 01202 456222 or email cis@bournemouth.gov.uk.
