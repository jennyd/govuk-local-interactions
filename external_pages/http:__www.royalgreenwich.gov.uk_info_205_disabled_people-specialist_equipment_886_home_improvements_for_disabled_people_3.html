Home improvements for disabled people

How to apply for help

View pages in this section

If you need help adapting your home because of disability, your first port of call is the Initial Contact Officers within Adult Social Care.

Use the numbers on the right of this page to contact the appropriate team.

  • The initial contact officer will ask you some questions to see if you qualify for help. If you do, you will be placed on a waiting list for an occupational therapy assessment.
  • If the occupational therapist then thinks that you need a major adaptation to your home, they will pass your details onto the Disability and Home Improvement Team.
  • The initial contact officer will also give you advice on any other help you may be eligible for, including applying for a Disabled Facilities Grant.
