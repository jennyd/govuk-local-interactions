Information Point

A directory of care and community organisations in Sandwell.

You can browse the categories below, view the A to Z of records or search for words. Click on `Advanced search` to search the directory by postcode, town or your particular needs.

Inclusion in this directory does not imply that Sandwell Council endorses the organisations and no responsibility is assumed for the information in their entries or their services. Please also see the terms and disclaimer page.

Search the directory:

Enter a word or phrase to search for. Use a comma to separate a list of words or phrases when you want to search for all of them e.g. short breaks, holidays, days out. Records will be shown that contain any of the words and/or the exact phrases you have listed.
