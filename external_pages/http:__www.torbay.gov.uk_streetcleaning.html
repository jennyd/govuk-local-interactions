Street Cleaning and Litter Bins

  • Report - Dog Fouling

  • Report - Overflowing Litter Bin

  • Report - Fly-posting

  • Report - Graffiti

  • Report - Litter


Torbay Council is responsible for the cleaning of all public roads, the provision and emptying of bins for litter, the removal of graffiti from public buildings and also for the removal of fly tipping on public land. TOR2 carry out these services of behalf of the Council.
We will:
  • Clean town centres daily
  • Remove accumulations of rubbish from Council land
  • Empty litter bins
  • Help with your locally organised clean-ups
  • Collecting litter from public streets
  • Beach Cleaning
Litter in a public place is unsightly, can be a danger to animals and above all makes an area unattractive to visitors.  Littering is also an offence. The average fine for littering is around £80, but can be up to £2,500. Litter ranges in size from a single sweet wrapper, to a bag full of rubbish. The Environmental Protection act 1990 states if a person drops, throws, deposits, or leaves anything in a public place, they are committing a littering offence. That’s why you should always use a litter bin or take your rubbish home,
In order to improve efficiency and to reduce costs, the Council is rationalising the number of litter and dog bins. In the majority of cases dog bins have been removed where they are in close proximity to a litter bin which can be used for dog waste. We will be adding stickers to these bins to inform the public.
People can also use their own waste bins at home so if there is not a litter bin available, please take your rubbish home for normal residential collection.
You can help us by:
  • Being tidy with your litter and chewing gum in an appropriate way
  • Using the Household Waste and Recycling Centre (HWRC) in Paignton to dispose of unwanted items.
  • Removing your dog mess and not leaving your dog's mess for others
  • Not 'Fly Posting' - it's untidy and illegal
  • Having a proper commercial waste collection contract for your business

Dead Animals

Our street cleansing services include the removal and disposal of dead animals from the highway.

Report your concerns to us

If you would like to report an environmental crime such as dog fouling, fly-tipping, graffiti, fly-posting or litter in your neighbourhood, complete our online forms, providing as much detailed information as you can. Remember that the more information you can provide, the more likely it is that offenders will be caught!
If you have any objection to the reduction of bins, please email highways@torbay.gov.uk identifying the location.
