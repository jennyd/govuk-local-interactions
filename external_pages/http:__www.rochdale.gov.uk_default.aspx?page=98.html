​​​​​​​​Celebrating our communities

We believe people’s different backgrounds, cultures, religions and viewpoints should be mutually respected, and we’re working hard to make everyone across Rochdale borough feel the same. We want our communities to feel confident in working together to create a brighter future for Rochdale borough.

Rochdale borough has a great reputation for welcoming people from other places.

Newcomers from across the world have made an important contribution to our great borough and its history and it would not be the place it is today without its rich mix of backgrounds and cultures.
