Pay for

Welcome to the Peterborough City Council  24 hour secure Internet payments service. You may make a variety of payments using this secure site with most major credit or debit cards (not Diners Card or American Express).

What may I pay?
You may pay for any of the accounts shown below by clicking on the selected item.

'Other payments' currently includes building control, development control, household collections (including brown bins), ordnance survey and planning obligations payments. Further payment options will be added as we develop this function.

To pay for Attendance Service Fixed Penalty Notices select 'Other Payments' and then 'Attendance Service'.


Please select from the following list:

  • Council Tax
  • Penalty Charge Notices (Parking)
  • Invoices (including social care charges)
  • Non Domestic Rates
  • Housing Benefit Overpayments
  • Other Payments

Security Information

All online card payments are protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL) with an encryption key length of 128 bits, which is the highest level commercially available. Your payment card details are directly processed by the Payment Management Division of Capita Software Services, a leading provider of secure online payments services to local government, and are not collected nor accessible by the Council.

Capita Software Services' online payment management solutions are independently and rigorously security assessed, and are certified by Visa and MasterCard as a Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) Level 1 payment processor. Further information about this payment card security standard can be found at https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/security_standards/pci_dss.shtml
