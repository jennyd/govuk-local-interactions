Trees

Print this page

We are responsible for looking after trees in the following areas:

  • Rugby Borough Council's parks, open spaces, recreation grounds, nature areas, allotments and cemeteries
  • Rugby Borough Council's sheltered housing complexes
  • Street trees within the urban area of Rugby
  • Tree planting within the urban area of Rugby

If you have a query regarding a street tree in the remainder of the borough please contact Warwickshire County Council.

We have extra responsibilities for trees that are within a conservation area, or are covered by a Tree Preservation Order (TPO). If you need to maintain or fell a trees in a conservation area or covered by a TPO you must contact us first.
