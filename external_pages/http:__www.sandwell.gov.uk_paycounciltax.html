Paying your Council Tax

Pay by Direct Debit

Direct Debit is the safest and easiest way to pay your Council Tax. You can set one up now easily online, you can also use the online form to change your existing Direct Debit.

You just tell your bank or building society that you want us to take monthly payments direct from your account.

If you donâ€™t have a Council Tax account number yet then you will need to complete an Online Move Form before you try to set up a Direct Debit.

Please note that we expect all of your instalments to be paid up to date at the time you complete this form. If any payments are outstanding you may receive a revised bill or further recovery action may be taken.

Set up a Direct Debit online now

You can choose to pay on the 5th, 15th or 28th of the month and you can choose to pay in either 10 or 12 monthly instalments.

If you have received a court summons or liability order and wish to make an arrangement to pay please fill in our special arrangement form.

If you already pay your Council Tax by Direct Debit and you have not cancelled your instruction your payments will continue to be collected each year. There is no requirement for the direct debit instruction to be set up each year. 

Find out more about paying by Direct Debit and our Direct Debit Guarantee.

Contact us if you need any further advice or assistance.

Pay online

You can also pay your Council Tax on the web with your Visa, MasterCard, Solo, Visa Delta, Visa Electron or Maestro cards.

Other ways to pay

You can also pay over the telephone, in person or by standing order.

How much Council Tax do I owe?

You can check your Council Tax balance by going to our online payment page, entering your Council Tax reference number (the 8 digit number beginning with a 7 from your bill) then clicking on 'How much do I owe?'.

Your instalments

Your Council Tax is normally payable in ten monthly instalments, starting on 1 April each year.

We are now offering the option to pay your Council Tax over 12 instalments. If you would like to take up this option for the next financial year (2014/2015) please let us know by emailing contact@sandwell.gov.uk or writing to us at Sandwell Council (Benefits and Council Tax), PO Box 239, Sale M33 6GU.

Please note that the number of instalments you pay will depend on when you register to extend your payments.

Need a copy of your bill?

If you need a copy of your bill complete the online form we will send it to you in the post.

Go paperless

Did you know that you can register to receive future any Council Tax bills by email?
