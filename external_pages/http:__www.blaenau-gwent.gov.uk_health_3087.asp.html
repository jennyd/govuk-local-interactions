Older Persons Bus Pass

Over 60's Bus Pass

If you are over the age of 60 and a permanent resident in Blaenau Gwent then you are entitled to a free bus pass.  This pass allows you to travel on practically all local bus services anywhere in Wales, free of charge.  For a bus pass application form please contact the Joint Passenger Transport Unit (JPTU)  on (01495) 355444 or e-mail us at the address listed below.

Where and when can I use my pass?
 
Your pass will be valid for cross border journeys into England provided that your journey is unbroken and starts or finishes in Wales.

How do I use my pass?
 
Travel using your pass, is subject to the rules and regulations of the operators providing the service. You will need to show your pass to the driver when you board the bus to receive your concession.
 
It must be shown to an inspector if requested. Failure to show your pass will result in the concession being refused and the fare for the journey being charged. The pass does not give you any rights other than those enjoyed by any fare-paying passenger.
 
Your pass remains the property of the Gwent Joint Passenger Transport unit and may be withdrawn by the Council or the operator if misused.

Replacement Bus Pass
 
There is a charge of £5.00 for replacement passes. If you require a replacement bus pass please contact the JPTU on (01495) 355444.

Found Bus Pass
 
Please return any found bus pass to the JPTU at the address detailed below, or any local Council office, and the pass will be returned to the owner.
