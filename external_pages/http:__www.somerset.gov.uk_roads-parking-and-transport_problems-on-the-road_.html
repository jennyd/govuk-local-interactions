Problems on the road

 
  • Report a pothole or road defectWe repair potholes and other road defects to ensure safe access for all road users, and to maintain the structure of the road or footway in accordance with our Highway Safety Inspection manual
  • Report a dead animalContact us to report casualties on the road, footway or verge that may be a hazard to the public
  • Report a blocked drain on the roadWe provide a drain cleaning service which includes road drains on all highways maintained by us
  • Report a damaged road gully, manhole or drain coverYou can report damaged/defective gully gratings and/or drain/manhole covers by using the button on this page
  • Report problem with a street lightWe maintain and repair street lights in accordance with national standards to provide safe access to all road users
  • Report a damaged traffic sign or bollardRoad traffic signs and bollards are maintained and repaired in accordance with national standards to provide safe access to all road users
  • Report a problem with a public right of wayReport problems on public rights of way (footpaths, bridleways, restricted byways and byways open to all traffic) using our interactive map
  • Report an overgrown verge or hedge on the roadReport an overgrown verge or overhanging vegetation on the highway using the online service on this page
  • Report weeds on the roadwe are responsible for controlling certain noxious weeds, including ragwort
  • Report a problem on a pavement or cycle pathWe aim to provide safe footways and cycleways of satisfactory appearance to encourage increased usage by the public
  • Report flooding or mud on the roadWe maintain the highway network in a safe and accessible condition for highway users
  • Report hazard on the roadExamples of hazards can include hazardous waste, traffic cones. blown tyre, rubbish
  • Report snow or ice on the roadWe are responsible for highway winter service in Somerset except for the M5, A303 and A36
  • Report a problem with street and roadworksWe manage the maintenance of the public highway in Somerset. Public highways are the roads and pavements that are managed at public expense
  • Report problem with a tree by the roadWe are responsible for the maintenance of the majority of trees on roadside verges, including street trees in urban areas
  • Report flyposting or graffiti on the roadDepending on the nature and the location of the signs and graffiti we will either remove or if known request that the person responsible removes them
  • Report damage to a bridge or retaining wallWhen you report an incident to us we will assess the severity and assign an inspector
  • Report soil run-off on the roadWe are responsible for keeping roads and pavements free of flooding that might cause a hazard or an obstruction to highway users
  • Report an advertising sign on a pavementWhen you report an obstruction we will inspect within 24 hours where resources permit
  • Report unnecessary road signsYou can report unnecessary road signs using the report now button on this page
  • Unauthorised use of a roadside vergeAction is determined by the nature of the obstruction and where appropriate will be in accordance with the Highway Safety Inspection Manual
  • Heavy Goods Vehicles (HGVs) and abnormal loadsContact us to report concerns about the number of Heavy Goods Vehicles (HGVs) driving through populated areas.
