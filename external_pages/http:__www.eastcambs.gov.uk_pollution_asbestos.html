Asbestos

Since 16th July 2004 all types of asbestos has been classed as a hazardous and controlled waste.

Only Environment Agency registered companies and carriers are allowed by law to handle, transport and dispose of any types of asbestos.

A consignment note, issued by the Environment Agency, must accompany every shipment of asbestos, no matter how small.

How and Where can I get rid of asbestos?

Householders wishing to dispose of asbestos should contact Customer Services on 01353 665555.

You should visit the Council office with proof of residency in East Cambs, for example a household utility bill or council tax bill, customer services will ask you some questions about the waste to confirm it is asbestos from a household source.

You will be asked for the following information, which will be forwarded to the AmeyCespa Waste Management Park at Waterbeach, where you will be able to take the waste for disposal.

  • The quantity of asbestos for disposal
  • The nature of the asbestos (sheet etc)
  • Your full name and address
  • Type, and registration number of vehicle that you plan to take the waste to the site in
  • Date you plan to take it to the site

Customer Services will issue enough bags to contain the asbestos, and instructions on where to take the waste. They will also issue an authorisation code.

You are responsible for bagging up the asbestos in the bags provided, without breaking it up, and sealing each bag.

You can then take the waste to the AmeyCespa Waste Management Park off the A10 at Waterbeach (please call prior to delivery on 01223 861856) reporting to the weighbridge before depositing your asbestos, taking any spare bags with you.

The weighbridge operator will record your details and the authorisation code issued by the Council, they will then supervise the unloading of the asbestos waste and issue you with a receipt.

Any bags without an authorisation code will be rejected.

For more information please contact the Waste Team, or the Environment Agency, general enquiries line 0845 933 3111.
