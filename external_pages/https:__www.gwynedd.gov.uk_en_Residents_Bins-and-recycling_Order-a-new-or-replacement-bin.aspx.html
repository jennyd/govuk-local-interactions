Order a new or replacement bin

Please use this form to order a new bin or new waste and recycling items:

  • enquiry / complaint about the waste and recycling service  

or call 01766 771000.

You will be asked to agree to the terms and conditions below when you submit your request.

You can order:


Blue recycling box

  • up to 4 blue recycling boxes (includes lid)
  • up to 4 sets of separators to place in the boxes to separate the items within the box 
  • up to 4 new lids for the blue boxes (to replace lost/broken lids) 
  • up to 4 nets to place on top of the boxes (to use instead of a lid if you are finding that your lids are frequently lost or broken)


Cartgylchu (Dwyfor and Meirionnydd residents only)

  • 3 blue boxes on wheels
  • Top box – paper/card

  • Middle box - plastic bottles, pots, tubs, trays and cartons 

  • Bottom box – glass/cans


Brown food bin (22 litre)

  • up to 2 brown food bins
  • You can't order food waste bags if you don’t own a brown food bin.


Kitchen caddy (5 litre) 

  • kitchen caddy (5 litre) to store food waste in the kitchen. Annual supply of food waste bags to keep the bin clean. You will need to empty the content of the kitchen caddy into the brown food bin for collection. Do not put the kitchen caddy out for collection.


Brown garden waste bin (or garden waste bags)

  • Every home can receive ONE of the following:
    • 1 large brown bin – garden waste (240 litre)
    • 1 small brown bin – garden waste (140 litre) 
    • annual supply of garden waste bags

Green waste bin (or black bin bags)

  • Every home can receive ONE of the following:
    • 1 large green bin (240 litre)
    • 1 small green bin (140 litre)
    • annual supply of black bin bags
  • you can only order one annual supply of black bin bags per year 
  • it is possible to order a hessian sack to protect the bin bags from being torn by animals (you can only order a hessian sack if you use black bin bags)
  • If more than 7 people live in your property, you can apply for an extra large bin (360 litre). To make an application for a larger bin complete the application form and send to: Waste and Recycling Helpdesk, Council Offices, Cibyn, Caernarfon, Gwynedd, LL55 2BF


Moving house?

If you move home, please leave all bins and boxes at the old house.

If there aren’t any bins/boxes at the new house, please use the enquiry / complaint about the waste and recycling service form or call 01766 771000 to apply for any new equipment needed.

We will distribute any equipment ordered within 15 working days.


For information about which items you can place in which bin and find out your collection date, go to the bins and recycling information pages.
