Gritting

Our full Winter Service is currently in operation. We are monitoring weather forecasts and treating all roads and streets on our gritting routes when temperatures fall below freezing. Find out more about our Winter Service.

We are doing everything we can to help our communities in the flooded areas. Many thanks to all the volunteers and community groups that have also joined the effort. Follow this link to get the latest news, advice and information about financial support.
