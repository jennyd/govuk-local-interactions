Electrical & Bulky Collections

‌If you have got any large furniture or electrical items to dispose of we can collect from your house. Items which are still in good working order may be acceptable to a charity.

We have two different waste types that we collect:

  • Type 1 - domestic fridges/freezers and electrical items e.g. television, washing machine, microwave, cooker
  • Type 2 - bulky non-electrical items e.g. mattress, sofa, carpet, wardrobe

How we deal with each of these waste types varies and so we have different collection rounds for each type.

Collection and disposal charges

We charge for the number of items to be collected from each type of waste.

  • One item of either waste type - £14.50
  • Up to 3 items per waste type - £17.50
  • Up to 5 items per waste type - £23
  • Up to 10 items per waste type - £34

Example collection charges

If you ask for one mattress, one television and one microwave to be collected you will be charged £14.50 (one Type 2) + £17.50 (up to three Type 1) = £32

If you ask for two mattresses, two carpets and a washing machine to be collected you will be charged £23 (up to five Type 2) + £14.50 (one Type 1) = £37.50

We will always confirm the total charge before agreeing to the collection.

Booking your collection

To book and pay for your collection telephone our Customer Services team on (0115) 901 3621.

Collection information

Items need to be easily accessible by our crews. If you are unable to remove the items from inside your property or wish to cancel or amend a collection request, please contact us beforehand.

Getting rid of large items yourself

Should you get rid of waste yourself remember you have a legal responsibility to transfer it to an authorised waste carrier. Many vans drive around the area looking for scrap to take. These are often unlicensed waste carriers who may dump your waste illegally after removing valuable scrap metal. If this happens with your waste you will be held responsible and liable to a £5,000 fine or could even face prosecution.

You can check if a scrap metal merchant is registered on the Environment Agency website or call 08708 506 506 for more information. You can search the public register of waste carriers. If in doubt please call the police.

Small electrical items

Recycle your old, broken or unwanted small electrical items such as:

  • Personal care items (shaver, hairdryer, straighteners etc)
  • Gadgets and toys
  • Computer equipment
  • Gardening equipment
  • Kitchen and homeware (kettle, DVD, food processor etc)

 at the following recycling centres around the Borough:

  • Arnold - Leisure Centre car park
  • Mapperley - Haywood Road car park
  • Netherfield – Morrisons
  • Ravenshead -  Milton Court
