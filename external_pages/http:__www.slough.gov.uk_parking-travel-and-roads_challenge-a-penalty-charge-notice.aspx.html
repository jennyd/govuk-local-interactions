Challenge a penalty charge notice

You are entitled to make representations to Slough Borough Council against a PCN using this form. Representations received before a Notice to Owner is served on you will be considered. You may appeal to an adjudicator if your representations are rejected.
If a challenge is received within the 14 day discounted period, but the challenge is not accepted, we will allow a further 14 days from the date the rejection letter is served so the reduced penalty charge can be paid.
The case will be put on hold whilst we investigate your challenge and a reply will be sent within 10 working days by post.
 
Challenges can be made through completing this form. Please note verbal or telephone challenges cannot be accepted.
 
Please use this form to challenge a Penalty Charge Notice.
Please note: Slough Borough Council will only respond to the postal address stated or address details supplied by the DVLA.
 

To submit files to support your challenge

You can include up to 5 files or images to support your claim. Each file must be no larger than 2MB.
