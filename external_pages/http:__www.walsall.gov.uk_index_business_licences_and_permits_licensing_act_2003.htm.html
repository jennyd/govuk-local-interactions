Licensing Act 2003


The Licensing Act 2003 covers:

  • the sale or supply of alcohol
  • the provision of regulated entertainment
  • the supply of late night refreshment

The Licensing Authority may issue:

  • premises licences
  • club premises certificates
  • personal licences (associated with the sale of alcohol)
  • temporary event notices (for occasional events involving less than 500 people)
