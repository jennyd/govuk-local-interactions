Alcohol, entertainment and food licenses

 

  •  Liquor licensing policy

Rushcliffe Borough Council's liquor licensing policy.

  •  Personal licenses

A personal licence allows the holder to sell or authorise the sale of alcohol from premises with a premises licence or temporary events notice.

  •  Premises licenses

A premises licence allows licensable activities to take place on a set of premises.

  •  Sex establishment

Application to apply for a sex shop or cinema licence.

  •  Temporary events notices

If you wish to hold an ad-hoc event in the authorities area, you must give a temporary event notice (TEN) to your local licensing authority.

  •  View licence applications

View applications for liquor licences, including premises licenses.

  •  New premise applications

View a list of new applications that are open for representation.
