Licensing

Safeguarding awareness training The Hackney Carriage and Private Hire Licensing Policy 2015 to 2019 makes it a requirement for drivers to undertake safeguarding awareness training. Find out how to book yourself onto a course.

Our licencing teams are responsible for issuing licences, and handling licence applications, relating to alcohol and entertainment; taxis and private hire vehicles; animal welfare; gambling; and other types of licences.

To find out how much a licence will cost, take a look at our fees listing for 2015/16.

For any additional information since this date please contact Licensing@shropshire.gov.uk
