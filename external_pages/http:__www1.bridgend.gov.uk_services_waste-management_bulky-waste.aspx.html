How to find us

Bulky Waste

To book your collection or for advice on the Bulky Waste service, call the helpline on Bridgend (01656) 643643. After booking, the collection will take place within 10 working days.

The council will collect most types of bulky household refuse (such as beds, sofas, chairs, furniture etc).

However, the council is not able to collect garden waste, builders waste, rubble, fireplaces or fluorescent tubes. Householders wishing to dispose of these items are invited to visit any of the civic amenity sites. For details of locations please see the Civic Amenity Site web page.

How can I book in large household items for collection?

The council offers a bulky waste collection for large household items for which there is a charge of £15 for 3 items. You are able to have additional items booked in at a charge of £5 each, maximum of 15 items.

Where items are classed as construction waste we are unable to offer a collection, please see list below.
