To find out more about your councillor, including how to contact them, click on their name to visit their dedicated page.

If you are unsure of the name of your councillor simply type your postcode in the postcode search box or visit the My Mendip section of the website.

You can find your councillor's name in the Council and Democracy section of My Mendip, which contains a range of information about services local to you.
