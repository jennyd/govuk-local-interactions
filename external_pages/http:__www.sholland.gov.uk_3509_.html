Community and Voluntary Sector Grants

Please find below details of some of the most popular and accessible grants and funding available to voluntary and community organisations.

Awards for all

Awards for All gives groups an easy way to get small Lottery grants of between £300 and £10,000.They fund projects which address the issues, needs and aspirations of local communities and people. It will fund a wide range of community projects aimed at developing skills, improving health, revitalising the local environment and enabling people to become more active citizens.

Awards for All

Lottery Funding

The BIG lottery fund BIG lottery fund has several funding opportunities. There are other separate national lottery funding opportunities for projects looking at Sport - Sport England Sport England heritage - Heritage Lottery Fund Heritage Lottery Fund and the Arts - Arts Council Arts Council

Lincolnshire County Council (LCC) Grants

LCC administer several funding pots open to voluntary groups and parish councils Lincs County Council Grants Pages

Lincolnshire Community Foundation

Manage several grant funding pots that can support a wide range of community and voluntary projects Lincolnshire Community Foundation

Covenant Fund

Find out about the £10 million per annum Covenant Fund to support the armed force's community and veterans integrate into their community and projects which address issues facing veterans in the criminal justice system Covenant funds

Grants for minibuses and transport

Groups can request a donation for projects related to wheels, transport and the motor industry Grants for transport

Disability Grants

Search grants that are available for people with disabilities, their families and carers. There are many charities and Trusts that provide funding towards the high cost of disability equipment, holidays, housing, days out; anything above and beyond the normal costs of everyday living Disability Grants

Receiving more information

If you would like to sign up to receive regular e-mail bulletins containing funding training and volunteering opportunities then please email the SHDC community development team at community@sholland.gov.uk with your name, organisations name and correct email address or call 01775 761161.
