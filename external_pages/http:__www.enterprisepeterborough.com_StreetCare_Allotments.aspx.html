Have you ever fancied trying your hand at gardening while enjoying fresh air and even fresher food, all while meeting friendly people? Then why not choose to rent an allotment plot in Peterborough?

The city has 25 allotment sites with around 1450 allotment plots.

An allotment plot is around 250 square metres, but if you feel that this would be too big, we can arrange half plots to enable you to grow produce on a smaller, easily managed plot.

We can even cultivate your new plot just before you take possession, so you can start planting and growing your own food straight away.

All of our allotment gardens have a site representative who can offer advice on what to grow, when and how to grow it, assist you with any general allotment concerns you may have and help ensure that you enjoy your plot..

Plots become available on an ongoing basis and renting one could not be easier. Just e-mail  allotmentspeterborough@amey.co.uk to express your interest and we will reply regarding the current availability.
