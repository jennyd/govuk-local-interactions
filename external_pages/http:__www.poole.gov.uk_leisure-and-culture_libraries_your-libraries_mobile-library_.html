Mobile Library

Mobile Library

Following public consultation and careful consideration by Councillors, a decision has been made to withdraw the mobile library vehicle at the end of December 2015 to support necessary savings to the budget for Poole Libraries. The Council recognises that library services are highly valued by users in the areas affected and there are a number of alternative library services that are available to you.
