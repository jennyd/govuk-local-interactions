Pay a Parking Fine

|Pay for it

Our commitment

If you have any problems with the payment service, please |look at our FAQs or contact Customer Services on 01372 474474

To pay a Parking Fine or Penalty Charge Notice (PCN):

  • Make sure you have your PCN number and Vehicle Registration Number
  • Check the PCN or the most recent correspondence for the penalty amount
  • Pay by credit or debit card, except American Express, JSB and Diners Club cards

Payment of a penalty is considered as an admission of liability to the charge therefore if you decide to make an appeal do not send payment at the same time. If both are sent payment will be taken and the appeal will not be entered into.

Alternative ways to pay

Phone 01372 474020 to make an Automated Telephone Payment (ATP) using a credit or debit card. When payment has been made you will get a unique reference number.

Please check your bank records to verify a payment has been made or contact Customer Service on 01372 474474.
