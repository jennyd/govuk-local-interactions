Welcome to Newark and Sherwood Homes

Our Vision is: "to deliver excellent Housing Services"

Newark and Sherwood Homes is a Council owned housing management company which was set up to manage and maintain Council housing within Newark and Sherwood.  Read more about Newark and Sherwood Homes...

Help to purchase a home (Social Mobility Scheme)

The Right to Buy Social Mobility scheme provides eligible Newark and Sherwood District Council tenants with cash grants to help them buy a home on the open market.
 

Our NSH Calendar 2016 & Annual Report shows how we are performing against the local service standards which were set with tenants and exceed the national standards set for housing service providers. 

By working with tenants and other partners we improved the already high performing and low cost service we provide to you and this report provides detail on how we did this and how we will work with tenants to further improve this in the future.
