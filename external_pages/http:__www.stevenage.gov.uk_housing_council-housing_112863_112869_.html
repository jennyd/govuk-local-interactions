Report a Repair

When reporting a repair please have the following information ready:

  • Your name, address and contact number
  • The best times to contact you at home
  • As much detail as possible about the repair

To report your repair, please use the link below.

  • Report a repair

For emergency repairs outside of normal office hours please telephone 01438 314963. Use the link below for more information.

  • Emergency Repairs

To report a repair to a communal (shared) area use the link below

  • Report a repair to a communal area

If we have enough information we will order the repair straight away. Sometimes we will need to inspect the work first. We will let you know if this is the case.

If you are reporting damage caused by others you should report this to the Police first and record the Police crime incident log number.

You can now track the progress of your repairs by signing up for our Online Customer Account. This will allow you to check that we have received you repair and to see when we will complete it by. To sign up, or to log in if you have already signed up, please click below:

  • Sign up to online account
  • Log in to your account
