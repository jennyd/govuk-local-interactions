Report flooding or mud on the road

 

You can report flooding, surface water and soil run-off on the highway by using the button below.

Please phone reports of mud on 'A' class roads (for example A38) straight to the police on 101. All other roads can be reported online.

Report now

When contacting us, please provide the following information:

  • Your name and telephone number 
  • The exact location of the flooding or mud 
  • Is it outside a specific property? 

  • Do you know who deposited the mud, for example,  the landowner or the company name? 

We maintain the highway network in a safe and accessible condition for highway users by: 

  • Preventing, wherever possible, mud being brought on to the highway 
  • Minimising the problems with related flooding and surface water drainage systems serving the highway 

In a rural county such as Somerset, it is reasonable to expect some mud on highways, particularly in areas where farming is a major activity. It is, however, an offence to deposit material on the highway and persons doing so may be liable to prosecution and for the costs incurred in cleaning up. If mud constitutes a danger or obstruction then action may be required to have this nuisance removed. Whether any action is taken will depend on the severity and location of the mud.
