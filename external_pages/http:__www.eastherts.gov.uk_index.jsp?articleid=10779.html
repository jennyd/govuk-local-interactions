Dangerous Buildings or Structures

In East Herts we are on 24 hour standby to deal with emergency dangerous structures - simply phone our switchboard on 01279-655261.

If a building or structure become dangerous, the Council can apply to the Magistrate's Court for a court order to be served on the owner to remove the danger within a specified period. This can mean repair, removal of the dangerous bits, or complete demolition.

If the owner fails to respond within the given time period, the Council can carry out the work themselves and recover the cost from the owner. The owner is then also guilty of an offence for not complying with the order and could be fined by the court.

In an emergency, the Council have the power to carry out whatever work is immediately necessary to remove the danger, but they must try to contact the owner first. Costs can also be recovered in such cases, but the Council must be sure that emergency measures were absolutely necessary and that it could not wait.
