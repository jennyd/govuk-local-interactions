Digital library services

Sunderland's libraries provide free access to a number of digital services, giving you access to a wide range of up-to-date sources of information, eBooks and eAudiobooks.

Members of Sunderland's libraries can now borrow a range of eBooks and eAudiobooks.

To download titles from the digital library, all you need is your Library Card Number and PIN.

  • BorrowBox Library

    Library members can download up to four eBooks and four eAudiobooks to a computer, tablet or other mobile device.

  • eBook Service

    Library members can download up to three titles to a computer, tablet or other mobile device.

  • Digital subscription services

    If you hold a Sunderland library card, you can access KnowUK, Newsstand, Oxford Dictionary of National Biography, Oxford English Dictionary and Oxford Reference Online from any computer with internet access.

  • Computers and the internet in libraries

    You can access the internet for free in libraries across the city.

  • Download our library app

    Use the app to link back to our mobile-friendly webpages or scan the barcode of a book to see if it's in stock.
