Contact Royal Borough of Greenwich

How to contact the Royal Borough

View pages in this section

If you are looking for information or how to contact council services, the quickest way is to first try to find it on this website. You can: 

  • use the search bar at the top of each page
  • browse the website for more information
  • use the A to Z of services.

Your area of enquiry

Select a topic from the links list below to find out how you can get in touch with the service you want, including postal addresses.

Each service has a different postal address, but general enquiries can be sent to:

Customer Services
The Woolwich Centre
Wellington Street
Woolwich
SE18 6HQ

General telephone enquiries: 020 8854 8888
