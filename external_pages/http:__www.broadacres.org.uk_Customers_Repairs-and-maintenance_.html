Repairs and maintenance

How to report a repair

You can report a repair to us in the following ways:

  • Online via our reporting a repair form (except emergencies).

  • By logging into the Customer Login. If you have not already registered you can do so quickly and easily here.

  • By telephone on 01609 767900.

  • E-mail - info@broadacres.org.uk

The priority of your repair

All repairs are important to us but we need to deal with them in order of priority.

Emergency:  Your repair may need to be attended to quickly because there is a risk of danger to your health and safety, or serious damage to your home.

If you report an emergency repair, we aim to be there and solve the problem within 24 hours.  We will try and let you know when we expect to arrive and keep you informed when we are on the way. We may make safe at first, and then arrange a follow up appointment to complete the works needed.

In an emergency, report your repair immediately on 01609 767900.

Other repairs: All other repairs will be appointed and we will arrange a time and date that is convenient for you.
