Pay a Penalty Charge Notice online

You can use this website to pay parking tickets (PCNs) issued by East Sussex County Council

Please wait 24 hours after the ticket was issued, before paying.

You will need:
  • the PCN number
  • the vehicle registration number
  • a valid credit or debit card.
Please keep a copy of your receipt number as proof of payment.

This system will accept Visa, Mastercard, Switch/Maestro and Solo.

Security Information: A padlock displayed on your browser indicates you are using a secure, encrypted connection.
