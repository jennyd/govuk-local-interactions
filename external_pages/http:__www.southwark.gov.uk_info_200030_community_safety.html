Community safety and enforcement


Crime and the fear of crime is one of our residents' main concerns.

We work in partnership with the police, fire service, victim support, and other agencies to tackle crime, antisocial behaviour and substance misuse.

Making the borough safer for all is just one of our fairer future promises to you.
