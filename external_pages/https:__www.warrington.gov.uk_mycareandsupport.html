My Care and Support Directory

A directory of social care and support services, clubs, groups, community and voluntary organisations, for people in the Warrington area. You can browse the categories below, search for words or use the A-Z search.

Inclusion in this directory does not mean that Warrington Borough Council endorses the organisations and the Council has no responsibility for the information and services outlined in the entries.

  • Disclaimer and important information to read before using the directory
  • Further information about My Care and Support Directory

Search the directory

Enter a word or phrase to search for. Use a comma to separate a list of words or phrases when you want to search for all of them e.g. short breaks, holidays, days out. Records will be shown that contain any of the words and/or the exact phrases you have listed. Further search hints. 

View by category

Help to live at home

Includes providers of home care, maintenance, personal care, meals, gardening and more.

Advice, information and advocacy

Including providers of advocacy services, translation services, benefits and money advice.

Safety and crime

Includes home and community safety, safeguarding and domestic abuse issues.

Housing options and residential care

Includes care homes, sheltered accommodation, homelessness support and more

Different people who need support

Includes learning disability, sensory impairment, physical disability and minority ethnic communities

Health and wellbeing

Includes mental health, healthy lifestyles, drug and alcohol services, support groups and bereavement

Money issues

Includes help to manage and fund your own care, personal budgets, benefits and entitlements.

Activities and getting out and about

Including sports, arts, museums and libraries, transport, travel and more.

Education, employment and training

Includes employment support, options for training, learning and returning to work.
