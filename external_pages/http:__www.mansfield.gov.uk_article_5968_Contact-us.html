How to find us

You can contact us by phone, email or post, or visit us in person. But please only do this if you can't find what you want online.

Doing things online is much cheaper than other contact methods, which means we can spend more on essential services.

Our contact details

Public holidays

We are closed for business on the following public holidays in 2016 (excluding Christmas holidays):

  • Good Friday March 25 and Easter Monday March 28

  • Early May bank holiday Monday May 2

  • Spring bank holiday Monday May 30

  • Summer bank and concessionary holiday Monday 29 and Tuesday 30 August

How to find us

Our main offices are at Civic Centre, Chesterfield Road South, Mansfield, Nottinghamshire, NG19 7BH.

By road: Mansfield is easily accessible by a good road network and is situated close to Junctions 28 and 29 of the M1.

By rail: Mansfield train station is a 10 minute walk from the Civic Centre. For timetable and fare information contact network rail.

By bus: Mansfield Bus Station is a 10 minute walk from the Civic Centre. Information is available from your local bus provider or visit Traveline.

Parking: There is a pay and display car park to the rear of the Civic Centre. Details of parking charges can be found on our website.
