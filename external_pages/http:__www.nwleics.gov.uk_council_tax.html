Council Tax pays for vital local services we all depend on. Our dedicated team is here to help you pay your bills - and help you if you're finding it hard to pay. 

We send out annual Council Tax bills in March every year.

The charge is legally payable over ten instalments (April to January) and this is what most people do.

There is also the option to pay over 12 months - you just have to ask. Depending on when we receive your request we will spread your charge over the remaining months in the financial year. You can also make block payments (for half a year, annual or weekly) - but they must be paid in advance.

You may be entitled to a reduction or an exemption. We're here to give you clear and consistent information, promptly, accurately - and in the strictest confidence.

Changes to the local council tax support scheme

Since April 2014 working-age customers who receive Council Tax Support have had to pay at least 15% of their Council Tax bill. Read more: Changes to the Council Tax Support scheme
