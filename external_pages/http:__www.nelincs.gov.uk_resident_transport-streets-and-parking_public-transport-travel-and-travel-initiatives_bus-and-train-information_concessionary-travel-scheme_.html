Information about your local Bus Pass (English National Concessionary Travel Pass).

From 6 April 2010 the entitlement age for a Concessionary Travel Pass changed. If you live in North East Lincolnshire and are of eligible age, you can apply for the English National Concessionary Travel Pass;

  • Your precise age of eligibility will depend on your individual date of birth. For further information download the spreadsheet on the right hand side of the page to see when eligible.

Using your bus pass before 09:30am from 1 June 2015

From 1 June 2015, concessionary pass holders will no longer be eligible for the 50p flat fare when travelling before 9.30am on weekdays.

Bus pass holders will need to pay the full fare for each trip if they want to travel before 9.30am, but will still be able to travel for free as usual after 9.30am.

The change will affect the following types of bus pass holders:

  • Senior citizens
  • Disabled persons pass holders
  • Companion pass holders

Phone n Ride and Dial a Ride service

Phone 'n' Ride and Dial-a-Ride services are exempt from full fare when travelling before 09:30am. Local residents can continue to use their bus pass for free on these services without time restrictions.

Rail

The Travel Pass also allows discounted rail travel after 9:30am between any two stations within North East Lincolnshire (any two train stations between Cleethorpes and Habrough).

Visitors to North East Lincolnshire

Visitors to North East Lincolnshire with another local authorities English National Concessionary Travel Pass are eligible to use their pass for free from 9:30am until 11pm Monday to Friday, free all day weekends and bank holidays on locally registered bus services within North East Lincolnshire. Visitors to North East Lincolnshire can not obtain discounted rail travel within North East Lincolnshire.

Visitors to North East Lincolnshire that hold an English National Concessionary Travel Pass are not eligible to travel for 50p before 9:30am and will be liable to pay the full fare if travelling before 9:30am.

Renewing your Concessionary Pass

All renewals have to be made in person, by the pass holder with no exceptions. A pass can be renewed a week before it is due to expire, but if there are exceptional circumstances such as going away on holiday, the pass can be renewed earlier. Please remember to bring proof of eligibility.

Further information

We have produced a leaflet explaining the North East Lincolnshire Council Concessionary Travel scheme in further details. You can download your copy from this page.

Alternatively telephone the Contact Centre or email on the details given above right on the page.
