Pest control

Gosport Borough Council offers a pest control service for the eradication of rodent and certain insect infestations.

The treatment for rats and mice in domestic premises is free. The treatment for fleas, wasps and certain other insects in any premises and for rodents in commercial premises, carries a charges. Individuals in receipt of income support pay a discounted charge. Charges are detailed below.

 

Wasps, fleas, carpet beetles, rats and mice

Our Service

Homes

  • We provide free treatment to eradicate of rats and mice in homes but a charge is payable for other pest treatments. The charge will be made before treatment unless you receive job seekers allowance or income/pension support, in which case there is a reduced fee. If this is the case, please tell our agent when phoning to book an appointment or to get further advice on 02392 545609. We will need proof of benefits.
  • Insect infestations can be treated but this is a chargeable service.
  • Our pest control officer will visit between 08.30 and 16:30 although appointments will be made for am (08.30 - 12:30) or pm (12:30 - 16:30) only. We are unable to provide timed or weekend appointments
  • We are unable to provide a service for pigeons, squirrels, foxes and bees. If you are being effected by a swarm of honey bees, you can contact the British Bee Keepers Association  via their website www.bbka.org.uk  or telephone on 0871 811 2282, where you can obtain details of a bee collector in your area. Opening hours are Mon - Fri 09:00 to 17:00. Alternatively, you can visit Fareham and District Beekeepers Association   at www.fdbka.co.uk . If you are unsure of whether you have honey bees, please see the below identification chart which can help you to find out which bees they might be.
  • Under the Prevention of Damage by Pests 1949, we have powers to ensure landowners keep their properties free from rats and mice or items that may provide harbour for them. If you have a complaint about this please contact us.

Commercial properties

We provide a chargeable pest control service for commercial premises. If you are interested in a pest control contract please contact us on 02392 545609 and ask for pest control.

Helping yourself

  • If you feed wild animals and birds in your garden, remember the food may also attract rodents to the area. We suggest that you put the food in sturdy, pest proof containers above the ground and clear fallen debris from the ground regularly
  • Rats are well known inhabitants of the foul water sewerage system, so please ensure you maintain any pipe work for which you are responsible.

  

 

If you would like to use this service please telephone us on 02392 545609 or e-mail ehs@gosport.gov.uk.
