Fostering

Fostering

If you would like to find out more about fostering we would love to hear from you.

Phone: 01384 815833
Email: fostering.cs@dudley.gov.uk     
or complete an online form         

You can also attend one of our Fostering Friday drop in sessions and have an informal chat with a member of the fostering team who can answer any questions you may have.

Dudley fostering team provide a range of foster care including temporary and more long term foster care for children and young people.

The service also offers day care and short breaks which includes arrangements for children with disabilities.

Foster care service standards

Our service puts children at the heart of everything we do. Below you can find our service standards which help us to ensure we always strive to put our customers first and provide the very best levels of customer service.

Education for Children in Care

The Virtual School for Looked After Children monitors, supports and advises on the education of children in care.  Visit the Virtual School web pages to find out more and the vital role that carers play in encouraging and supporting children and young people with their education .
