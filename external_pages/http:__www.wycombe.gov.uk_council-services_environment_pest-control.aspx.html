Pest control

Our contractor, DialAPest (external website), operates a pest control service on our behalf.

  • Request a pest control appointment online (external website)
  • Request a call back (external website)
  • Call DialAPest directly (seven days a week, 9am to 5pm)

Alternatively you may wish to use the services of an independent pest control company: see the British Pest Control Association (external website) for a list of contractors.

Pest control prices

Pest control prices
PestPrice (including VAT)
Fleas£68.00
Mice (domestic)£39.50
Rats (domestic)Free
Ants£68.00
Squirrels/glis glis (edible dormouse)£84.00 (note: payment for six weeks rental or up to five collections, whichever comes first, and there is a returnable deposit for the cage)
Wasps£39.00 (includes all nests at one premises treated on one visit)
Miscellaneous (bedbugs,cockroaches)£68.00

Further advice on payment can be given at the time of booking.

Commercial premises are not eligible for the prices above but can request a free pest control survey and quotation for their specific needs. Commercial premises include houses in multiple occupation and any communal parts of any building or land.

Types of pest

DialAPest treat the following types of pest in domestic properties:

  • Fleas
  • Mice
  • Rats
  • Ants
  • Squirrels
  • Glis glis (edible dormouse)
  • Wasps
  • Other types of pest such as bedbugs and cockroaches

Pest fact sheets (external website)

Bees

Our pest control contractor does not deal with bees. There are amateur beekeepers within the Wycombe district who can remove swarms of honey bees (other species of bees cannot be dealt with). The service is free, but as the association is a registered charity, they take donations for providing this service.

For a local beekeeper and advice, please see High Wycombe Beekeepers Association (external website).
