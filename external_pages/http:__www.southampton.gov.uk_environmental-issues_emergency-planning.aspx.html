Emergency planning

Southampton City Council emergency plans

To enable an efficient response to incidents in the city in order and ensure the welfare of the people and the environment following a major incident the following are some of the plans in place for Southampton:

  • Southampton City Council Major Incident Plan
  • SotonSafe Off-site Reactor Emergency Plan (and the Radiation Emergency in the Port of Southampton leaflet) 
  • HIOW LRF Multi Agency Flood Plan
  • Oil and Chemical Pollution Plan
  • Pandemic Influenza Plan
  • Heatwave Plan
  • Cold Weather Plan

The Emergency Planning team also works with other agencies and the emergency services across Hampshire when the incidents require wider support.

If you would like further information please contact us at emergency.planning@southampton.gov.uk
