Local Transport Plan 2011-2026

Cheshire West and Chester Council's Local Transport Plan sets out our plans and priorities for transport from 2011 to 2026.

Our overall goal is to work towards providing and managing a well maintained, safe, integrated and sustainable transport network. This is essential to help support our long term social, economic and environmental goals for the local area in the years to come.
