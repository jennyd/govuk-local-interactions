Join the Library

Joining the library is quick and easy. You can join by going into any Luton Library with one proof of your name and current address.

Alternatively, click on the link below to join online, and pick up your library card at a chosen library:

Join online now

 

Renewals

To renew your items, you need your library card and Personal Identification Number (PIN) with you. You can renew by:

  • clicking on this link  
  • Visiting any Luton library
  • Phoning any Luton library during opening hours

If you have forgotten or lost your PIN please visit any Luton Library with your library card and ask a member of staff.
