Statement of Purpose

The Copyright Acts protect plans, drawings, etc. submitted to the Council. Material may be used for consultation, comparison with previous schemes and to check that developments are in accordance with approved plans. Further use may only be made with the prior permission of the copyright owner.

Comments on applications can be submitted using this web-site or by writing to: Planning Service, South Somerset District Council, Brympton Way, Yeovil, Somerset, BA20 2HT. Comments and letters (including names and addresses) are available to the public and may be published on the website.
