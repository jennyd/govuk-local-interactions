Barnet Libraries Festival 2016 Join us for a fortnight of author talks, workshops and lots of fun. Starting on Saturday 6 February until Sunday 21 February, there are events for children, young people and adults. Featuring award winning Rob Biddulph, bestselling author Mark Billingham, crime writer Erin Kelly, women's authors Julie Cohen, Rowan Coleman, Katy Regan, Lucy Robinson and meet Great British Bake Off finalist Richard Burr.   

 

There is currently no wifi available at Edgware Library. Engineers are working to resolve the problem. We apologise for any inconvenience

 

Our Zinio service will be unavailable on Tuesday 9th February between 11am and 12 noon for maintenance.
