School holiday dates 2016 - 2017 [pdf document]

The number of term days shown on this calendar is 193 with 2 days (1st & 2nd September 2016) allocated for teacher training activities. Within the period 5th September 2016 to 31st August 2017, 3 further days within the above school terms are to be utilised for teacher training activities. These dates are to be decided at individual school/academy level. Pupils will not attend on these 5 teacher training days. Please contact the relevant school/academy for further details of the dates for the training days.

Please note that dates may vary on an individual school basis, so it is always best to check with your school.

For information about holiday activities in Darlington visit our events for children and young people page.
