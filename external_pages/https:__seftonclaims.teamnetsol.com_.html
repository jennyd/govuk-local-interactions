Calculate and claim Housing Benefit and Council Tax Reduction

If you live in the area covered by Sefton MBC, you can use this application to:

  • see how much Housing Benefit and Council Tax Reduction you could get if you rent and then make a claim.
  • check and claim Council Tax Reduction if you own your home
  • find out if you're entitled to Income Support, Pension Credit or Tax Credits

The form is in two parts. The first is a calculator which can give you an indication of what you may be entitled to. The second part allows you to make a claim.
