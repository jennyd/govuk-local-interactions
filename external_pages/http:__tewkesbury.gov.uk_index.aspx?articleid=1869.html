Home ownership options

There are a number of different affordable home ownership options available in the borough.

What options are available?

Home ownership schemes enable first-time buyers to buy a share of a home and get onto the housing ladder and include Help to Buy and Shared Ownership.  For many people, such affordable home ownership options may provide the best opportunity to own a home in the area in which you want to live and work. 

Who runs these schemes?

Help to Buy South is our area's Government appointed HomeBuy Agent and Help to Buy Agent for Gloucestershire.

Am I eligible?

The Help to Buy South website details eligibilty criteria for each scheme on their website, click here to go directly to this information.

How do I apply?

Information about apply for each scheme is available on the The Help to Buy South website, click here to go directly to this information

Who do I contact?

Contact Help to Buy South by calling customer services on 0800 456 11 88 , by emailing info@helptobuysouth.co.uk or visiting the Help to Buy South website www.helptobuysouth.co.uk.
