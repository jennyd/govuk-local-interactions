Information alert

Black bag collections - Friday 5 February

Unfortunately, we were unable to complete black bag collections in Worlington, Chulmleigh, Burrington, Bishops Nympton and Meshaw today (Friday 5 February). Instead, please ensure your black bags are at the kerbside by 7am tomorrow (Saturday 6 February) when we will return. We apologise for any inconvenience.
