Right to Buy

You must be a public-sector tenant for at least five years to qualify for Right to Buy. We may take account of the time you spent as a tenant of another council or some housing associations.

Only registered tenants and family members living in the property as their only or main home can be included in the Right to Buy scheme. Your rent account should be fully up to date.
