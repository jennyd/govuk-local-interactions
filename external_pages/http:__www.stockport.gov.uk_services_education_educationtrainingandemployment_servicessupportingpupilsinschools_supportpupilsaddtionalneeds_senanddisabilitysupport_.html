Special Educational Needs (SEN) and Disability Support

Children with special educational needs (SEN) all have disabilities that make it harder for them to learn than most children of the same age. These children may need specialist support at home and in school.

To view all services that support all children and young people with special educational needs and /or disabilities aged 0-25 please view the Stockport Family Information Hub website.

The Stockport Children & Young People's Disability Partnership (CYPDisP) offers a range of specialist social, health and educational services, aimed at meeting the needs of children with a disability.

The Stockport Children & Young People's Disability Partnership (CYPDisP) website introduces parents to all the services within the partnership.  The site outlines how to make a referral to these services if your child is experiencing issues relating to health, social or education difficulties.
