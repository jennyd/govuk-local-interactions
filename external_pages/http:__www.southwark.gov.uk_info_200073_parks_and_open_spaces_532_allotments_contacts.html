Allotments contacts

Allotments

All allotment sites in Southwark are managed independently by voluntary organisations, and only some of the sites listed are owned by the council. The sites vary in size, most have a waiting list and if details are not listed please apply on site in person for more information.

Bermondsey 

  • Alscot Road Allotments
  • Lynton Road / St James Road East Allotments
  • Lynton Road / St James Road West Allotments
  • Paterson Park Allotments - managed by Bermondsey and Rotherhithe Allotment Society. No allotments available and there is a five to six year wait. Allotments are allocated according to the waiting list. 
Contact: Bermondsey & Rotherhithe Horticultural Society, PO Box 66976, London, SE16 9BQ

Borough and Bankside

  • Lamlash Street Allotments - managed by Lamlash Allotment Society. No allotments available, waiting list only

Contact: 21 Hayles Street, London SE1 4SU

Walworth

  • Aylesbury Road Allotments - for local residents only.
  • Fielding Street Allotments - managed by Walworth Allotment Society

    Contact: Christopher Collins.  

    Camberwell

    • Caspian Street Allotments - managed by Caspian Street Allotment Society. All membership enquiries should be sent to caspianmembership@gmail.com 
    • Grove Hill Park Allotments - managed by Camberwell Gardeners Guild. Contact: 
    • Lettsom Gardens - managed by Lettsom Gardens Association.

    Peckham

    • Bonar Road and Surrey Canal Allotments

    North side managed by Bonar Road Allotment Society. South side managed by Surrey Canal Allotment Society 

    • Contact for Bonar Road Allotment Society: to be announced shortly
    • Contact for Surrey Canal Allotment Society: Alison Cookson-Hall  

    Nunhead and Peckham Rye

    • Stuart Road Allotments (Nunhead Reservoir).
    • One Tree Hill / Honor Oak Allotments - managed by One Tree Hill Allotment Society

      Dulwich

      • Friern Road Allotments - managed by Friern Road Allotment Society. Apply on site Saturday or Sunday morning

      Contact: Tony and Heather Draper, 101 Friern Road, London SE22 0AZ

      • Dunstan Road Allotments - for local residents only. Please apply on site
      • Grove / Dulwich Common Allotments - managed by a local committee of the Camberwell and District Allotment Society
      • Grange Lane Allotments - managed by a local committee of the Camberwll and District Allotment Society
      • Gunsite Allotments - managed by a local committee of the Camberwell and District Allotment Society
      • Cox's Walk / Lordship Lane Allotments - managed by the Dulwich Horticultural and Chrysanthemum Society
