Stockport Fund

Forever Manchester is a community of donors committed to improving the quality of life and helping to build stronger communities across Greater Manchester.

Since the organisation began in 1989, over £20 million has been distributed to over 8,000 community groups and projects, changing the lives of many people in Greater Manchester.

Groups based in Stockport can currently apply for the Stockport Fund through Forever Manchester. Other sources of funding may become available to Stockport groups through the Forever Manchester from time to time.

The Stockport Fund supports the activities of local community or voluntary groups, projects or individuals that contribute positively to the quality of life and wellbeing of people and communities in Stockport.

They offer grants of up to £3,000 to pay for:

  • One off projects or events
  • Local or borough wide projects
  • Minor start-up costs (get new idea/project 'off the ground')
  • The purchase of equipment
  • Will consider supporting annual running costs for an organisation or service. (Please note that this does not include staff costs).

The Stockport Fund supports voluntary and community organisations to run projects/activities which address any or all of the priorities listed below:

  • Older People
  • Children and Young People
  • Safer, Stronger Communities
  • Sustainable Environment
  • Physical and Economic Regeneration
  • Health and Wellbeing.

For information on how to apply please visit the Forever Manchester website.
