Hazardous household waste collections

We can collect small amounts of hazardous household chemicals from residents in Solihull.

Hazardous waste we collect

  • household chemicals, e.g. battery acid, phosphoric acid, anti freeze
  • garden chemicals, e.g. pesticides, herbicides, feeds
  • powdered or solid chemicals e.g. lawn feeds, weed killers etc

Please note we retain the right to refuse a collection if the waste is found to be unacceptable under our licence conditions.

We do not collect asbestos.

The service is limited up to five items per household. Items must be no more than 5 litres and 5 kilograms in size.

When are collections made?

We provide the service in January, April, July and October. Collections are made Monday to Friday, during office hours.

We will telephone you to advise the date of collection.

You don't need to be in but the items for collection must be presented at the front of the property clearly marked so they can be easily identified.

Only listed items will be collected, items not described or listed will not be taken

Book a collection

Book online

Safe storage of hazardous chemicals

Chemicals and hazardous materials should be kept in their original containers. Store in a cool, dry place out of reach of children.

Do not pour hazardous household waste down the sink, drain or toilet and never mix chemicals.

For further information on banned pesticides and chemicals please visit the Health and Safety Executive.
