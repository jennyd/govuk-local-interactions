Arts, Events and Play

We provide a vast array of community events in the borough from Carnivals to Play Days, as well as promoting and encouraging people to take part in arts opportunities.

Bonfires & Fun Fairs

Find out more about organised firework events and when the fun fairs are in the borough

Arts

Find out about arts activities in the borough and what help and support is available for arts groups running their own projects and activities

What's On

Our What's On guide will let you view all events that are currently taking place within Gedling. Have a look, see what you like and come and join in the fun!

Gedling Show

The annual Gedling Show is a chance to get involved in the horticultural opportunities as well as having a great day out.

Arnold Carnival

Taking place in June every year the Arnold Carnival is a weekend full of fun, entertainment and activities for the whole family.

Play Days

There are a number of play days that take place across the borough each year. The events are aimed at encouraging children to play in a safe and friendly environment
