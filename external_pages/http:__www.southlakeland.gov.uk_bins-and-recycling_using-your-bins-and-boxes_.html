Recycling boxes and waste bins

Leave your bins, bags or recycling boxes at the kerbside by 7am. 

Collections are made between 7am and 3pm.

Collections are usually made at the boundary of your property in view of the road, although it may be at another agreed location. 

Please make sure they are not causing an obstruction.

If you have a question contact us.

Using bins and boxes


To make sure you use your boxes and bins safely: 

  • follow the user guide on the side of the box
  • use both hands to move them 

if you are elderly or disabled, and feel you may have a problem moving your bins or boxes, please telephone 01539 733 333.

Request assistance with a query about your collection point.

If you wish to report a missed waste collection please use our form report a missed collection or telephone us.

We provide an assisted collection service for people who are unable to move their bins or boxes because of disability or ill health.

Apply for an assisted collection

We will sort your glass, cans and paper at the kerbside. Please: 

  • rinse bottles and cans before putting them in the blue box
  • do not put bottles inside cans
  • do not use the green bin or recycling boxes for materials not included in our guidelines or we might not empty it

After collection the lid will be left in the box to show it has been emptied. 

Please remember to pick up your bins and boxes after emptying. They can be cleaned using warm, soapy water. 

Wrapping your waste can help keep your grey bin clean.

We will not return for grey bins or waste bags that are not left for collection.

Please remember additional space will be required around the bin or box for opening and to allow them to be removed.

There are also facilities at your your local recycling centre  for materials such as cardboard and plastic that are not collected. 

If you are unable to use these you should squash plastic bottles and flatten cardboard to minimise the amount of space taken in the bin.

We will also collect one standard bin bag of waste with a wheelie bin.
