    Demolition enforcement

    Find out about the enforcement powers we have for demolition.

    We have powers to control and enforce demolition works.

    If you want more information or want to give notice of demolition works see our Control and advice for demolitions webpage.

    It is an offence under the Building Act 1984 to:

    • not give notice of demolition
    • not comply with conditions or time limit set by us for your demolition work

    This would result in a maximum fine of £2,500 - level 4 of the standard scale as shown in Part 1, Section 17 of the Criminal Justice Act 1991.

    Need help? Contact us

    You can find more information and legislation on demolition in the following:

    • Building Act 1984, Section 80 Notice of Demolition
    • Building Act 1984, Section 81 Local Authority Demolition Notice
    • Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015
    • Health and Safety Executive
    • Dangerous Structures
