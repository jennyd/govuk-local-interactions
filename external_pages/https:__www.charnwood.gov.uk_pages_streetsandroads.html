The trunk road network in Charnwood comprises the M1, A6 and A46. Highways England (previously the Highways Agency) provides day to day management of these routes. A similar service for other local roads and pathways is provided by Leicestershire County Council who deliver front-line highways services under the name of Leicestershire Highways.

Road Safety

Charnwood Road Safety Committee (incorporating Home Safety) consists of a group made up of representatives of Charnwood Parish Councils, Borough Councillors and the police. Its remit is to investigate road safety concerns in the Borough and, if necessary, liaise with the Highway Authority to bring about improvements.

The committee meets four times a year at Southfields, Loughborough. If members of the public have particular highway safety concerns, they are welcome to write to Democratic Services, Charnwood Borough Council, Southfields, Southfield Road, Loughborough, Leics. LE11 2TX
