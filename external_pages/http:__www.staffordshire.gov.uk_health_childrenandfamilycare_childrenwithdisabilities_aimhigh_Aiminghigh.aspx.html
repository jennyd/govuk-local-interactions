Short Breaks (Aiming High)

Aiming High in Staffordshire commissions short break activities for children and young people with a disability or special educational need/additional need and their families.

Short breaks provide fun activities outside of the school day where children and young people can try new things and develop confidence independently from their families or as part of a family experience.

For more information you may like to watch some short YouTube clips of Aiming High activities.
