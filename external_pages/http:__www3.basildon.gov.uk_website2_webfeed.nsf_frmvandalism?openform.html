Report Vandalism and Graffiti

Use this form to notify our Streetcare section about vandalism and graffiti.

Our aim is to remove offensive graffiti in 1 day and all other types within 3 days from areas where the Council has the authority to remove.

If the graffiti is on private or business properties and is up to 2 square metres then the Council will remove this free of charge.
