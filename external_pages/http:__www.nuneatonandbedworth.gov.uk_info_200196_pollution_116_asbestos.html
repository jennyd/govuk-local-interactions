Procurement tender opportunities and guidance

Current contract opportunities

Suppliers and contractors can find out about current and upcoming council opportunities via;

  • TED (Tenders Electronic Daily) - OJEU contract opportunities
  • CSW-JETS eTendering Portal - Coventry, Warwickshire and Solihull contract opportunities
  • Contracts Finder - UK Public Sector contract opportunities

Applying for opportunities

In line with procurement policy, where ever possible contract opportunities are now made available exclusively as electronic tenders meaning all documentation will be available for immediate download. Documents are to be completed electronically and uploaded back through the tendering portal.

We use CSW-JETS portal to run our eTenders. It's free to register your organisation and submit tenders electronically.
