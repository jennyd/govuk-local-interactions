Housing Options

The Housing Options Team will be able to offer you advice and assistance if you are:

  • a tenant and have been given notice to leave by your landlord (private, council or housing association)
  • living with friends or relatives and are being asked to leave
  • living in accommodation which you now find unsuitable (for example as a result of a disability)
  • a homeowner being threatened with repossession
  • someone living in fear of violence from a partner or other household member
  • or have any other housing advice query

We give comprehensive housing advice to enable residents to make informed decisions about their housing options and to, where possible, prevent homelessness. The Housing Options Team also manage the Choice Based Lettings scheme and deal with incidents of homelessness.

To access the service:

Many enquiries can be resolved by telephone. Please call the Customer Services Team on 0116 288 8961. We also offer appointments by prior arrangement with Housing Options Advisors. To make a booking, please visit the customer services centre at 40 Bell Street, Wigston or call 0116 288 8961.

In an emergency:

Please call and discuss your situation with a member of the Housing Options team on any of the numbers given above. If outside normal office opening times, please visit http://www.oadby-wigston.gov.uk/pages/out_of_hours_emergencies.
