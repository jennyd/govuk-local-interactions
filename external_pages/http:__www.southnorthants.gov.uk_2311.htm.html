Dead Animals

We are responsible for the removal of dead animals from the public highway in the South Northamptonshire district.

 Report a dead animal

Farm Animals

Reports of dead livestock will be subject to an Animal By-product Order and disposal will be at the discretion of Northamptonshire County Council's Trading Standards Department

To speak to Animal Health during office hours telephone 0300 126 10 18.

As a rule of thumb, if the carcass is:

  • on the public highway, we are responsible for removing it
  • on private land, the owner of the land is responsible for removing it;
  • in a watercourse responsibility for its removal may rest with the Environment Agency (08708 506 506)
