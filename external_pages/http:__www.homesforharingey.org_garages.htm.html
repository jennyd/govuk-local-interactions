renting a garage

If you need somewhere to keep your car or van, you may be able to rent a garage near you.

When we allocate garages, priority goes to council tenants and leaseholders  living on the estate. If there is no tenant or leaseholder on the list, we will offer the vacancy to the next person on the list.

To rent a garage, simply call Customer Services on 020 8489 5611 or email customerservices@homesforharingey.org. They will send you a form and put you onto the waiting list. Alternatively, you can fill in and send the form now, by clicking the link at the bottom of this page.

When you sign the tenancy agreement for the garage, you will be asked to produce a valid vehicle registration number and some identity.

Garage measurements

Most garages are designed for a standard car and measure 4.88m x 2.44m (16’x8’), but a few are larger, and can accommodate a van. Rents are based on size and facilities, such as internal lighting.

Points to consider

  • You can only rent a garage for a motor vehicle, not for other purposes such as storage
  • You must not store materials that catch fire easily in a garage
  • You cannot keep petrol or gas bottles in a garage.

Reduced garage rent

You may be able to reduce your rent on a garage if you are a council tenant in Haringey and one of the following:

  • A pensioner
  • Disabled and receiving Mobility Allowance and/or Disability Living Allowance
