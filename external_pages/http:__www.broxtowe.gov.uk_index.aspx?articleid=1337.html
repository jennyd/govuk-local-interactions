Bulky Waste Collection Scheme

The Council provides a pre-arranged chargeable service for the collection of bulky waste or you can choose to donate your furniture item to one of the charitable organisations below.

How to arrange a bulky waste collection

  1. Read an accept the terms & conditions. Please note the service charge is non-refundable.
  2. Use the online service, contact customer services or visit one of the Cash Offices

The Service & Collection

  • The service charge is £13 per order and £7 per item of waste collected from outside the property.Please note we will not help you prepare the item for collection.
  • Collection dates will be scheduled when proceeding with the order. Items must be accessible 7:00am on the allocated collection day and at the location indicated.

Donation of furniture for re-use 

A number of charitable organisations will collect free of charge certain unwanted pieces of furniture for re-use. The donations must however meet the following criteria:

  • All items should be clean and undamaged
  • Settees, chairs and mattresses must be clean, unstained and fire labels (1988 or newer) attached
  • Donations must come from a pet free home
  • If any item contains glass, this must be labelled safety glass

Links to these organisations can be found below.
