Clinical waste is as any waste that consists wholly or partly of:

  • human or animal tissue
  • blood or bodily fluids
  • excretions
  • medicines or other pharmaceutical products
  • swabs or dressings
  • syringes, needles, finger pricking devices and lancets

Find out more about clinical waste by visiting the Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs (DEFRA) website.  

For further information on clinical waste, or to report discarded clincal waste please email waveney.info@ncsgrp.co.uk or telephone 01502 562111.
