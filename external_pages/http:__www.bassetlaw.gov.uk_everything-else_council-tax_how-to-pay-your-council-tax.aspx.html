How To Pay Your Council Tax

You can pay your Council Tax by many easy and convenient ways. These are all shown on the back of you Council Tax bill.

  • Direct Debit
  • Internet (Home Banking)
  • Post
  • Post Office
  • Pay Point

Direct Debit for Council Tax

This is the only method of payment that gives you a choice of five different payment dates for Council Tax. You can also pay weekly by Direct Debit for your Council Tax.

You can choose from 5 different dates to pay your Council Tax:

1st, 10th, 15th, 25th and 28th. Or we can offer weekly (deducted on a Friday) and you can choose to spread your payments until March (or January if you prefer).

  • This Guarantee is offered by all banks and building societies that accept instructions to pay Direct Debits.
  • If there are any changes to the amount, date or frequency of your Direct Debit Bassetlaw District Council will notify you at least 10 working days in advance of your bank account being debited or as otherwise agreed. If you request Bassetlaw District Council to collect a payment, confirmation of the amount and date will be given to you at the time of the request.
  • If an error is made in the payment of your Direct Debit by Bassetlaw District Council or your bank or building society you are entitled to a full and immediate refund of the amount paid from your bank or building society.
  • If you receive a refund you are not entitled to, you must pay it back when Bassetlaw District Council asks you to.
  • You can cancel a direct debit at any time simply by contacting your bank or building society. Written confirmation may be required. Please also notify us.

Payment using Home Banking

You can pay through your own Home Banking facility with your own Bank or Building Society. Please quote our...

  • Sort Code - 20-55-62
  • Account Number - 93367584
  • Your full 12 digit Council Tax Reference Number (Located on the front of your Council Tax Bill).

Payments by Post

For security, please send only cheques and postal orders through the post. Send your payment to the Worksop or Retford office. If you require a receipt, you must enclose your Council Tax Bill. Please quote your Council Tax account reference number on the back of your cheque.

  • Address: Bassetlaw District Council, Queen's Buildings, Potter Street, Worksop, Notts, S80 2AH.

Payments at the Post Office

All Council Tax payers can pay at their local Post Office (free of charge). Your bill has a barcode on it that can be scanned at your local Post Office.

Any cheques paid to a post office should be made out to "Post Office Ltd".

Payments at a Pay Point

Your Council Tax bill has a bar code on it that can be used to scan and pay at any pay point facility.
