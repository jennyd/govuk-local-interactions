Report dog fouling

Nobody likes to see dog excrement, especially in green areas we like to enjoy, or where children may play. If left on streets and footpaths, it can cause annoyance by getting on shoes, and creating an unhealthy and unclean environment.

Please use this form to notify the Council of dog fouling so we can keep Lincoln's streets clean.
