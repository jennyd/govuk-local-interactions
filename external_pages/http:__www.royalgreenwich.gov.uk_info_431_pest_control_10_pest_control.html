Pest control services

How to deal with pests

View pages in this section

To enquire about or request a pest treatment of your property, get in touch with our pest control contractor SDK Environment Ltd (trading as Dial A Pest). The contractor provides a dedicated service for treating a range of pests, including:

  • bedbugs, fleas and cockroaches
  • mice and rats
  • Pharaoh ants, black ants, moths and cluster flies
  • wasps. 

Read factsheets about common pests (Dial A Pest website)

Pest treatment rates and treatment requests

Select the relevant category to view the rates and make a booking.

Council tenants and council leaseholders

Pest control services are free of charge if you are a tenant or leaseholder of Royal Greenwich council-owned property. Council tenants and council leaseholders requiring pest control services should:

  • call the repairs hotline on 0800 052 1183
  • or email pestcontrol-housing@royalgreenwich.gov.uk

We help to get rid of:

  • bed bugs
  • cockroaches
  • fleas
  • mice
  • rats
  • pharaoh ants
  • wasps.

Owner occupiers

Our fees are fixed for all owner occupied properties that have up to five bedrooms. If your home has more than five bedrooms our contractor will provide you with a treatment cost following a free of charge inspection of your property. You can call 020 8921 6601 seven days a week between 8.30am and 5pm, or request a call back:

  • Request a pest treatment call (Dial a Pest website)
  • View the contractor's pest treatment terms (Dial a Pest website)

Residential landlords

The Royal Borough does not expect private or non-council tenants to pay for pest treatment services and so we do not offer a direct chargeable treatment service to private tenants. We view pest control the landlord's cost of doing business and expect private landlords to pay pest treatment fees in all cases.

The fees below are for private landlords letting a property as private tenanted accommodation, and residential social housing providers or housing associations.

Our fees are fixed for all rented properties that have up to five bedrooms. If your property has more than five bedrooms our contractor will provide you with a treatment cost following a free of charge inspection of your property.

You can call 020 8921 6601 seven days a week between 8.30am and 5pm, or request a call back:

Request a pest treatment call (Dial a Pest website)

Private tenants

The Royal Borough views pest control as a potential cost of doing business as a private landlord. We expect private landlords to pay pest treatment fees in all cases. We do not expect private or non-council tenants to pay for pest treatment services and so we do not offer a direct chargeable treatment service to private tenants.

If you are a tenant of a private landlord and you are experiencing difficulties with your Landlord refusing to undertake or meet treatment costs you can contact the Royal Borough's Residential Services Section for advice - email residentialservices@royalgreenwich.gov.uk or call 020 8921 8157.

Number and length of treatments

Treatments can be very complex and several factors will need to be taken into account to estimate how long the treatment will take such as the size of the property, the number of rooms and the extent of the infestation.

General advice

If you require general pest control advice that does not include booking treatments, email residentialservices@royalgreenwich.gov.uk.

What we don't cover

Please note the Royal Borough does not undertake any type of treatment for urban foxes, squirrels or birds. You can find some advice that may help you deal with these below.

Foxes

You can contact the Fox Helpline on 01892 826222 for advice on foxes. You can help to keep foxes at bay by:

  • ensuring that bins are securely shut 
  • not leaving rubbish sacks beside your wheelie bin 
  • never feeding foxes. 

More help with fox problems

Birds and squirrels

We recommend that you contact your landlord or a private pest control company. If your landlord refuses to take action, you may contact the Environmental Health Team on 020 8921 8157 for help and advice.
