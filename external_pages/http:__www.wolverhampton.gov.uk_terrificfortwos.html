Early Education Funding

Welcome to the Early Education Funding, here you can apply for free nursery education for 2 year olds (Terrific for Twos) or Early Years Pupil Premium. Before completing the application forms please ensure you have all your details to hand, you will need your National Insurance or NASS number to process your application.

 If you need further support or have any questions regarding applications contact Information for Families on 01902 554242 or please email: early.years@wolverhampton.gov.uk
