Eastleigh Borough Council Online Planning - Welcome

The Council is increasing the availability of documents associated with planning applications on the website. Please note the documents on this website may not represent the full extent of information available. Certain documents which contain information classed as sensitive in accordance with national guidelines may be withheld from internet publication. Copies of documents can be seen at the Council offices with a redacted version of any document classed as sensitive.

Please note that there are no details of legal agreements, decision notices pre October 1995 and applications before 1985 held in the system. Please contact the Development Control Unit for details if you require information on any of these ( Email planning@eastleigh.gov.uk or telephone 023 8068 8263/4). Information on recent Planning Appeals is shown on a separate Appeals page from the Details page and associated documents are available on the Plans & Documents page. Links to Officer reports are available on the Full Details page where an application is going to Committee. These web pages are updated overnight at approximately 3am.
