Welcome to the Bromsgrove Home Options scheme

Welcome to the Bromsgrove Home Options Plus housing advice guide.

This service will help you to find out about the range of housing options and housing advice services available to you in Bromsgrove District.

  • To access this service, simply register by clicking the link above, and entering your details.
    You can then explore your housing options.
  • For returning customers, please login and enter your username and password

 

This website is designed to give you general advice about housing options that may be suitable for you.

If you are homeless or threatened with homelessness you will need to contact Bromsgrove District Housing Trust on 01527 557557 or freephone from landline 0800 0850 160
