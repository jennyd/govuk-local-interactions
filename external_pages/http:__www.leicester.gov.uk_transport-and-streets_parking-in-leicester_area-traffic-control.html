Area Traffic Control

We're responsible for helping manage road transport systems 24/7 across Leicester, Leicestershire and Rutland.

Further information

We monitor road networks using CCTV, traffic signals and other technology to help manage traffic flow across the city and beyond.

We log problems and instruct our street engineers to resolve these. Our traffic data is also used for developing future traffic schemes.

We're on Twitter 

You can follow us on Twitter for regular updated on traffic conditions in around the city and beyond.

Our other services

We’re also responsible for the traffic information service, a central database of incidents and roadworks across the city. It also includes data supplied from the police, utility companies, public transport operators and the public.

This information is also used by BBC Radio Leicester,who broadcast daily traffic updates.
