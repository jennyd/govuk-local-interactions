Dog control

Introduction

We do not have a designated dog warden service and our stray dog service is now provided by Worcestershire Regulatory Services, but we can provide practical help and advice on most dog related issues. We are able to investigate complaints of dog fouling and barking. For further information please contact us. We will ask for your name and address as we do not take anonymous complaints, but we will always respect your confidentiality.

You can find general information relating to dogs and the Animal Welfare Act on the Kennel Club website. Reports of animal cruelty or an animal in distress should be directed in the first instance to the RSPCA either online or by phone on 0300 1234 999.

General guidance on reporting animal welfare issues can be viewed in 'Who is responsible for what in animal welfare?'.
