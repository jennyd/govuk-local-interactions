You are here: Home / Waste Collection And Recycling

Order a bin and garden waste sacks

You can order bins for refuse or recycling and garden waste sacks by calling us on 01329 236100 or emailing customerservices@fareham.gov.uk. You can also purchase garden waste sacks from the civic offices. We suggest you put your house number or name on your bins.

Refuse bins

There is a charge of £30 (including delivery) for a new or replacement green top refuse bin. If you have moved house, you will need to buy a bin if there is not one there. This applies to new-build and existing properties. Only Fareham Borough Council issued bins are acceptable and no others will be emptied.

There is no charge for bins under the following circumstances:

  • If the bin or lid was broken while it was being emptied (subject to management approval).
  • If you wish to change the size of your bin (up to the maximum size of a 240 litre bin). The smallest size bin we use is a 180 litre bin. The small 140 litre bins are no longer issued.
  • If your bin has been stolen, you should report it to the police. A bin is issued free of charge, on production of a Crime Reference Number.

You are allowed one refuse bin per property unless, as a larger household, you qualify for extra capacity. If you are a large household or have exceptional circumstances, we may issue an extra, smaller refuse bin. To do this, we must be satisfied that you are making all possible efforts to recycle. If your application for an extra bin is approved, there is a £30 charge. Please contact the Council to apply for an additional bin.

Recycling bins

We provide recycling blue lid bins free of charge. If your household creates a lot of recycling, you can ask for a second recycling bin at no extra charge.

Reusable garden waste sacks

One replacement reusable sack is free of charge if you collect it or there is a delivery charge of £5 for it to be delivered. You can collect a sack from the Civic Offices during office hours with written proof - such as your name and address on a utility bill or driving licence - that you live in the Borough. You can collect a sack on someone else's behalf as long as you bring proof of their address.

 

Disposable garden waste bags

You can buy disposable garden waste bags for extra garden waste for £23.50 for 25 bags (including delivery) or £5.75 for 5 sacks (no delivery, packs of 5 sacks are available collection-only from the cash office in the Civic Offices). You can put out as many of these on collection day as you wish or you can share a pack with family and friends. We only collect Fareham Borough Council disposable garden waste bags.


 

Home composters

Composting is an easy way to reduce the amount of waste in your bin. As well as taking a lot of waste, such as peelings, uncooked vegetables, tea bags, straw and pet bedding, a composter will make useful compost for your garden. Hampshire residents can buy composters at discounted prices .

 

New property developments

When new houses are built, the developer may buy the bins; but if not, the resident will be required to purchase the bin. Developers must buy the communal bins for new flats. Current charges for refuse bins are:

240 litre = £30 + VAT (standard bin for individual houses)
340 litre = £50 + VAT (communal bin, only permitted for flats)
1100 litre = £316 + VAT (large communal bin, only permitted for flats)

For single properties, you can order direct through us on 01329 236100. We do not charge for recycling bins. You should allow at least 10 working days for the bins to be delivered once we have received payment. There is no charge for garden waste sacks if you collect them from the Civic Offices or they can be delivered for £5. For developments of several properties, please call the Council for payment information.

For more information, contact us on 01329 236100 or email customerservices@fareham.gov.uk
