Council Tax

How much is my Council Tax? Find out your valuation band and how Council Tax is spent. Who should pay Council Tax? Council Tax is a charge on all households and pays for local services. Ways to pay your Council Tax How to make a payment. Council Tax discounts There are a number or discounts which reduces the amount Council Tax you need to pay. Moving house Let us know when you move home so that we can adjust your Council Tax bill. Problems paying your Council Tax If you are struggling to pay your Council Tax, take action before things get worse. How Council Tax debts are recovered We may take action if you do not pay your Council Tax.
