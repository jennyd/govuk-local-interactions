Victim Services

Do you know what victim services are available in Bedfordshire?

We know there is under reporting of abuse, violence and hate incidents by vulnerable members of our community. Victims are often left needing additional support following an event.

The Community Safety Partnership work with local victim services and support groups to encourage victims to report their incident/offences.

Do you know what victim services are available in Central Bedfordshire? Please view our list of services that are available (PDF 83KB).
