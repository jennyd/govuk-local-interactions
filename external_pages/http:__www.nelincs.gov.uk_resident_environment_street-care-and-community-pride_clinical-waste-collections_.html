Details of the clinical waste and syringe collection service

We operate a clinical waste collection service free of charge to domestic properties.  This includes the collection of sharps and surgical waste (i.e. cover dressings and adult incontinence items)

These collections are carried out on in Grimsby and Cleethorpes on Mondays and all other areas on Tuesdays.

All syringes/sharps must be placed in the sharps container supplied by the service.  Please ensure that the container is correctly locked (see instructions on container) prior to collection otherwise our operatives will be unable to take it.

Surgical waste must be contained in yellow clinical waste bags for collection, no other types of bags will be accepted. The yellow bags can be collected from your doctor's surgery or the hospital.

To arrange a clinical waste collection service please contact us on the telephone number opposite.  Please note we will need to agree with you a convenient collection point

Disposal of discarded syringes

If you find a discarded syringe please contact us as soon as possible, reporting details of the location, so we can arrange a safe collection.
