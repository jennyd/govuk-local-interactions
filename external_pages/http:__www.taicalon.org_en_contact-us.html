    Contact us

    Our office at Solis One is open Monday to Friday, between 9am and 5pm. You can visit, write or telephone us. Please be aware that all calls to Tai Calon are recorded for training and quality purposes.

    Directions

    The Rising Sun Industrial Estate is off the A467 Brynmawr to Abertillery road. Turn into the industrial estate and take the first left turning. Tai Calon Community Housing is on the left.

    When travelling by car, satellite navigation should be set to our post code, NP13 3JW. This will bring you to the industrial estate via the A467.
