Street parties

Guidance and forms

The Gov.uk website contains a guide to organising a fete or street party.

The council will require you to complete this indemnity form (Word, 34kb) - please download, complete and submit with your application. There are also conditions of consent (14kb, PDF) that need to be read and understood. It is recommended that all potential party organisers read these prior to making an application.

Please send your completed forms to events@warwickdc.gov.uk  or call 01926 456222/456220

Roads

To ensure that no road works are affected by the event a copy of the completed application form should be sent to;
Keith Davenport,
Traffic Manager
Environment and Economy Directorate,
Warwickshire County Council,
PO Box 43,
Shire Hall,
Warwick,
CV34 4SX

We also advise you to inform Warwickshire Police of the details of your plans by contacting roadsafety@warwickshire.pnn.police.uk .
