Pay for it

Direct Debit

Direct Debit is the most cost-effective and efficient way to make payment. All Banks and Building Societies that take part in the Direct Debit Scheme monitor and protect its efficiency and security. You are fully protected by the Direct Debit Guarantee. If you would like to know more about the Direct Debit Scheme, please visit the Direct Debit website at www.directdebit.co.uk .

 

The following services allow you to make payment in instalments by Direct Debit:

Council Tax

All payments are due on the 1st day on the month unless payment is made by Direct Debit. Direct Debit payers have the choice of 4 payment dates (5th, 15th, 22nd or 30th of each month).  If you choose not to pay by Direct Debit, you will be sent a payment card.

Whatever payment method you choose, you have the option to pay over 12 or 10 months, half yearly or annually.

To pay your council tax by direct debit, you can:

  1. Call 01329 824651 and set up a direct debit instruction over the telephone (sometimes referred to as Paperless Direct Debit). You will be sent a letter confirming the details of your call within 3 working days (or not later than 10 working days before the first collection)
  2. Download the Direct Debit Instruction (410 KB). Please print the form, complete it and return to us at the address shown on the form
  3. Email us at counciltax@fareham.gov.uk and we will send you a form to complete and return
     

National Non-Domestic Rates (Business Rates)

All payments are due on the 1st day on the month unless payment is made by Direct Debit. Direct Debit payers have the choice of 4 payment dates (5th, 15th, 22nd or 30th of each month). If you choose not to pay by Direct Debit, you will be sent a payment card.

Whatever payment method you choose, you have the option to pay over 12 or 10 months, half yearly or annually.

To pay your National Non-Domestic Rates (Business Rates) by direct debit, you can:

  1. Call 01329 824651 and set up a direct debit instruction over the telephone (sometimes referred to as Paperless Direct Debit). You will be sent a letter confirming the details of your call within 3 working days (or not later than 10 working days before the first collection)
  2. Download the Direct Debit Instruction (410 KB) please print the form, complete it and return to us at the address shown on the form
  3. Email us at businessrates@fareham.gov.uk and we will send you a form
     

Council Housing and Garage Rent

The payment date is the 1st of every month.

To pay your Council Housing and Garage Rent by Direct Debit you can email us at housing@fareham.gov.uk or call us on 01329 236100 and we will send you a form to complete and return.

How to pay by telephone (24/7 automated service)

The Telephone Payment Number is: 0845 6066876

Payments are processed automatically and you will not need to speak to a Council officer. You will be told that your payment has been authorised and you will be given an authorisation code as confirmation.

Why payment by credit card is limited to certain services

If you have read all the information on this page you will have noticed that you cannot pay by credit card in all service areas. For example, you cannot pay your Council Tax by credit card.

Bank charges for debit card transactions are charged at a flat rate per payment, whereas, credit cards are charged at a percentage of the value of the payment being made. The Council has taken the decision to limit the acceptance of credit cards simply as a measure to minimise the bank charges that it would incur, and ultimately have to pass on to the Council Taxpayer. As an indication, if every Council Tax and Business Rate payer used a credit card to settle their bill, the Council Tax would need to be increased by over 20% just to cover the extra bank charges.

If you require any further information, please contact the Council's cash office on 01329 236100, or e-mail cashoffice@fareham.gov.uk.
