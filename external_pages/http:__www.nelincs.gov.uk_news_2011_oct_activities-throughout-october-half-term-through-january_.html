Activities throughout October half term and through to January are out now!

When school’s out it’s time to access all areas with North East Lincolnshire Council’s brand new Holiday Activity Booklet.

The new booklet has been split in half with one side of the booklet devoted to children aged 0 - 12 years of age and the other half aimed at young people between the ages of 13 - 19.
School’s Out... remains in the same format that everyone has come to expect from the holiday activity booklets. It provides a single directory that parents and families can use to find the best activities taking place near them this autmn. This includes; days out, sports, crafts, fun days and many other exciting events.
Access All Areas... is the new section of the holiday activity guide and can be found by flipping the booklet over. It aims to give young people a simple and easy guide to the positive and exciting activities taking place in their neighbourhoods. A lot of these activities have been organised by partnerships between North East Lincolnshire Council and partner organisations like the police and fire brigade. These activities are designed to give young people a fun and independent set of pastimes this autumn.
You can collect a copy from your local library or online  Alternatively you can contact the Family Information Service for a copy on: 0800 18 303 17.
Booking lines for all activities are open at 2.00pm Wednesday 19 October and activities can be booked online
Alternatively, call our booking line on (01472) 323344 to make your bookings. Make sure you book early to avoid disappointment.
If parents wish to pay by card for activities, they can call (01472) 325481 after making their booking. This phone line is open between 10am and 2pm, Monday to Friday. An advisor will be happy to help.
Alternatively parents and carers can pay by cash or cheque at their local library up to three days before the date of the activity.

North East Lincolnshire Council is committed to ensuring that all of our children can access a range of activities and experiences. If your child needs additional support to access any of these activities, please advise us at the time of booking.
