Parks and green spaces

Swansea has a wealth of parks and gardens for you to enjoy

Go out and get some fresh air and play in your local park! 

From the beautiful gardens at Clyne and Singleton to an indoor tropical haven at Plantasia, there's something for everyone in Swansea's green spaces.

Swansea Prom also provides lots of entertainment - take the kids to the fun-packed playgrounds or introduce them to golf at Ashleigh Road.

Park Hire Facilities

Both the Brynmill Discovery Centre and Ty'r Blodau are available for private hire for a variety of different purposes.

Parks Watch & Useful Numbers

Parks Development aims to ensure that all our parks remain a safe and friendly place, so everyone can enjoy the range of events and activities that take place throughout the year.

Allotments

We have 15 allotment sites in total, 5 are managed by us and 10 are managed by allotment associations. There is also a privately-owned and managed allotment available.
