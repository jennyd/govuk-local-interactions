Pay your Council Tax

Please click here to pay your Council Tax, but before you continue have you thought about paying by Direct Debit instead?

 

Direct Debit is simple, quick and safe.  Just give us the details once, and we'll do the rest.  No need to remember to pay.  You can choose to pay over 10 months or over 12 months.

 

Do you want to set up a Direct Debit?

  • Not now thanks:  Continue to Council Tax Payment

  • Yes:  Set up a Direct Debit to pay council tax
