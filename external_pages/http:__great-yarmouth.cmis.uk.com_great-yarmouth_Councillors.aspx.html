Councillors

There are 39 elected councillors representing residents in the Borough of Great Yarmouth.  Each councillor represents an electoral ward and there are 17 wards in the Borough.  You are able to search for your councillor alphabetically, by ward or political party.  The find my councillor/ward facility below allows you to find the ward where you are situated and the councillor(s) that represent you, by typing in your postcode.  You can access additional information about a councillor by clicking on their name or photograph.
This additional information includes their election and party history as well as declarations of  interest and expenses.
