Why create an account?

Services available to logged-in users:

  • Council Tax: view your account details, create or change a direct debit and apply for discounts and exemptions.
  • Housing and Council Tax benefit: apply for benefit and check details of your benefit claim
  • Housing Rents: check details of your rent account
  • Reporting: receive updates on any reports you submit, and search for any reports in your area
