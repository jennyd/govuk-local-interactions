About Doing Good Leeds

Doing Good Leeds is the website for the third sector in Leeds.

The website is here to help third sector organisations access the best help and advice on running their organisation. It also aims to involve and inform people in the big issues affecting the third sector in Leeds.

Doing Good Leeds is supporting a unified and empowered third sector and aims to be:

  • Welcoming
  • Inclusive
  • Celebratory
  • Inspiring and
  • Trusted

The Doing Good Leeds website is managed on a day to day basis by Voluntary Action Leeds (VAL), but draws together the combined experience of organisations that give support to the third sector and advocate on its behalf. In particular, VAL convenes an Editorial Group, made up of people from across the sector, which comments and provides guidance on the site's content and design. The group meets every 2 months or so and anyone from the third sector in Leeds is welcome to join — contact VAL for more information.
