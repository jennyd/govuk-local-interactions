Match day zone parking permits

You will need to obtain or renew a permit if parking within the match day parking zone. This only applies on days when Wolverhampton Wanderers FC are playing at the Molineux.

Please note, Wolverhampton City Council does not run a residential parking permit scheme.

Restrictions

During match days, the following restrictions will apply in addition to any already in place:

  • only vehicles displaying a valid residents, visitor or business permit will be allowed to park in bays marked 'permit holders only during match day times'
  • areas of limited waiting will apply to all vehicles, with or without a permit
  • no vehicle may park on single yellow lines
  • no vehicle may park on double yellow lines
  • disabled badge holders must not park in bays marked 'permit holders only during match day times' unless they hold a valid residents, visitor or business permit

Days and hours of operation

The match day parking zone will operate as follows:

  • weekend and bank holiday fixtures: 12pm to 6.30pm
  • weekday fixtures: 6pm to 10pm.

Cost

Application for the permit is free, replacement permits are £10.

Replacement permits


Parking permit application

If you require a replacement permit there will be a charge of £10. This can be paid by cheque or postal order, made payable to 'Wolverhampton City Council' with your  replacement application form. [798kb]

Alternatively if you wish to pay by debit or credit card, please contact us.

Contact us

Contact us if you would like to speak to someone about your application or you require further information.
