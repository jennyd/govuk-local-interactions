Complaints: when things go wrong

If we have failed to provide one of our services to you or something has gone wrong please tell us and we will investigate your complaint.

We will always try to resolve complaints immediately but if we can’t we will:

  1. Talk to you about next steps so please give us your telephone number so that we can make contact quickly
  2. Let you have a full response with the outcome of our investigation as soon as we can, making sure we let you know if there will be any delays.

If you aren't happy with the outcome of your complaint, if you have evidence that your complaint has not been handled properly you can submit it for review by our Head of Service.

If you are still unhappy after this review, you can take your complaint to the Local Government Ombudsman here – it's a free service that looks at complaints about councils and investigates them in a fair and independent way.
