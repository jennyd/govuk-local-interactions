Application forms for Council Tax discounts

If you think you may be entitled to any of the following discounts, please download the appropriate explanatory notes and application form and once completed send it to the Council Tax section at the address shown on the form:-

  • Single Adult Discount Application Form (PDF, 16k)
  • Full Time Student/Student Nurse Discount Explanatory Notes (PDF, 19k
  • Full Time Student/Student Nurse Discount Application Form (PDF, 17k)
  • Careworker or Carer Discount Explanatory Notes (PDF, 18k)
  • Careworker or Carer Discount Application Form (PDF, 17k)
  • Youth Trainee or Apprentice Discount Explanatory Notes (PDF, 17k)
  • Youth Trainee or Apprentice Discount Application Form (PDF, 15k)
  • Severely Mentally Impaired Discount/Exemption Explanatory Notes (PDF, 20k)
  • Severely Mentally Impaired Discount/Exemption Application Form (PDF, 16k)

In the case of any other discount, please contact the Council Tax Section on 01483 755855 or email ctax@woking.gov.uk, stating which discount you are claiming.
