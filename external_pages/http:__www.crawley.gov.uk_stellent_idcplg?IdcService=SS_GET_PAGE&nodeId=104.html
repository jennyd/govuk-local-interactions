Community Safety

In Crawley the term community safety means working in partnership to reduce crime, disorder and anti-social behaviour so that people are safer and feel safer.

These pages offer local information and advice on a variety of issues and tell you who to contact for further help.

Keeping your home safe this autumn

A burglary in your own home can be a devastating experience that affects you and your family. The good news is that the risk of becoming a victim of burglary in Sussex is low after Sussex Police's initiative to tackle burglary under the banner of Operation Magpie in 2012.

However, during the winter the risk of being burgled can increase with the darker evenings once the clocks go back as it can be easier for burglars to identify homes that may not be occupied.

Check out Sussex Police's essential home security tips and stay secure at home this autumn.
