Useful Links

Child Bereavement UK

Child Bereavement UK supports families and educates professionals when a baby or child of any age dies or is dying, or when a child is facing bereavement. Every year we train around 5000 professionals at the front line of bereavement support.

Cruse Bereavement Care

Cruse Bereavement Care provides counselling and support to those who are bereaved (adults or children) or for those concerned about a person suffering from grief in the Wrexham County Borough area. Tel: 0844 561 7856,  email: northwales@cruse.org.uk.

Federation Of British Cremation Authorities

This site contains useful information about cremation practices in the UK, including cremation statistics for Pentrebychan.

Funeral Inspirations.co.uk

As well as an acknowledgement of a loss, a funeral should be a reflection and celebration of life. Our aim at Funeral Inspirations.co.uk is just that - to inspire and encourage people to take an active role in planning and personalising what will inevitably happen to us all one day.

HopeLinkUK 0800 068 41 41

HOPElineUK is staffed by professionally qualified advisers who can give support, practical advice and information to anyone who is concerned that a young person they know may be suicidal. Tel: 0800 068 41 41.

Making a Will

Making a Will provides general information on preparing a will and the consequences of passing away without having made one.

Nightingale House Hospice - Child Bereavement Support Service

We can accept calls from all families/professionals in Wrexham where they are concerned about supporting bereaved children.

The Institute of Cemetery and Crematorium Management

The site contains useful information about cemeteries and crematoria.

Survivors of Bereavement by Suicide (SOBS)

Bereaved by a Suicide? You don't have to face it alone. Those who are left behind after a suicide can be helped by talking to others who have experienced the same. Tel: 0870 2413 337.
