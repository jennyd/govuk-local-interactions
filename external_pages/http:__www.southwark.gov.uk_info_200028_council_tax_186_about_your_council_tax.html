Council tax bandings

The bandings

There are eight council tax bands (A to H) as listed in the table below.

Band Value of property on April 1 1991 (£) Southwark element (£) Greater London Authority (GLA) element (£) Overall council tax (£)
A Under 40,000 608.09 196.67 804.76
B 40,001 - 52,000 709.44 229.44 938.88
C 52,001 - 68,000 810.79 262.22 1073.01
D 68,001 - 88,000     912.14 295.00 1207.14
E 88,001 - 120,000   1,114.84 360.56 1475.40
F 120,001 - 160,000     1,317.54 426.11 1743.65
G 160,001 - 320,000 1,520.23 491.67 2011.90
H Over 320,000           1,824.28 590.00 2414.28

The Valuation Office Agency website allows you to view the banding for your property as well as others in your area. It will also allow you to submit an online appeal.

Should you wish to appeal your banding, you do not need a representative to appeal on your behalf.  The Valuation Office Agency have issued a warning that a number of customers have been charged a fee by agents for making an appeal when they could have done this for free themselves.
