School Admissions - Nursery Schools

Within Torfaen there are a range of settings available to nursery age children both maintained by the Authority and within the private and voluntary sector. 

It is the Authority's policy to admit children to it's Nursery schools and nursery units (known as maintained nurseries) from the beginning of the term following a childs third birthday. (Autumn Term is 1st September - 31st December, Spring Term is 1st January - 31st March, Summer Term is 1st April - 31st August). Places are allocated on a part-time basis (morning or afternoon sessions only) for five days per week. In certain limited circumstances, full-time placements can be offered to 4 year olds. 

If you choose for your child to attend a nursery in a voluntary or private sector which is approved by the Care Standards Inspectorate for Wales, and registered as an education provider by the LA, the Authority will fund your child's placement on a part-time basis (minimum 10 hours over 4 or 5 sessions per week). The level of funding will be comparable with that of a four year old in a maintained provision and this currently equates to £411 per term following a childs third birthday. Where a child attends a Private Day Nursery, parents will be responsible for any additional payments over and above the funding allocated by the LA. 

The closing date for September 2016 admissions was Friday 2nd October 2015 and therefore any forms submitted after this date will be classed as a late application and will be considered after all applications which were received on time. You can still download a Nursery School - Admission Form here or alternatively one can be obtained from the nursery school/unit or by contacting Kath Worwood on 01495 766915, and returned to the nursery school unit of their choice.

Parents will be informed of the outcome of the placement by Friday 27th November 2015 for September 2016 four year olds and January rising 3's and Friday 29th January 2016 for April rising 3's.

Parents/carers choosing a private or voluntary setting should take the application form to the setting of their choice, who will apply for the funding on their behalf.

Full details of the Authority's Nursery provision can be found in the Parents / Carers Information Booklet or by contacting Kath Worwood, Forward Planning Team on 01495 766915. 

Details of nursery provision can also be obtained from head teachers of nursery schools or primary schools having nursery units. 

Details of voluntary and private providers can be obtained from the leaders/managers of the playgroup and Private Nurseries or in the Parents / Carers Information Booklet.

For further details on admission to playgroups, Cylch Meithrin and Private Day Nurseries, contact the Family Information Service on 08000 196330.
