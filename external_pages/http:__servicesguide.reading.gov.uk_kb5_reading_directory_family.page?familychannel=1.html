Childcare


  • View all services in this category

Deciding the type of childcare you want for your child is an important decision, so it is a good idea to get as much information as possible before deciding what is best for you and your child. You can search for childcare available in Reading below.

There are several different options for pre-school childcare:

  • Childminders
  • Nurseries
  • Pre-schools
  • Nannies
  • Holiday Clubs/After School Clubs/Breakfast Clubs

Disclaimer: The Council does not necessarily endorse or recommend any of the links or services included in this services guide - please read our Disclaimer in full (link at the bottom of this page) before using this services guide.
