Online Registers for Planning & Building Control

The Online Registers allows you to:

  • Search for and view details of Planning & Building Control applications and Planning appeals
  • Submit comments on Planning Applications during the appropriate stage of the application process.
     

The online service provides access to:

  • Planning and Building Control applications from October 1988
  • Planning appeals from January 1993
  • Associated documents for Planning applications from December 2005
  • The service is available from 05:00 Monday to 23:00 Sunday
  • The online records are incomplete and must not be used as a substitute for carrying out a formal Land Charges Search
  • No responsibility will be taken for any errors or omissions in the Planning & Building Control information provided online
  • The information contained on this system does not constitute a formal notification of a Planning or Building Control decision, and any actions taken as a result of information on the site are undertaken entirely at the viewer's own risk
  • You can view all applications, appeals and associated documents at the main reception at the Civic Offices, during normal office hours 09:00 to 17:00
  • Under the Local Government (Access to Information Act 1985) your comments on Planning applications will be made available for public inspection at the Council's Civic Centre Office
  • We are no longer able to provide an email tracking alerts service
