Current schemes

Queens Road, Cheadle Hulme

As part of the redevelopment of Queens Road Primary School, Traffic Services have been requested by the Councils Planning and Development team to investigate traffic issues on the roads surrounding the new development. It is anticipated that there will be a change in traffic and vehicle movements and therefore key junctions and new school entrances need to be addressed to improve safety.

The request for an investigation has been discussed with your Local Ward Councillor responsible for traffic issues, who has agreed to this consultation with local residents in order to ascertain your views.
