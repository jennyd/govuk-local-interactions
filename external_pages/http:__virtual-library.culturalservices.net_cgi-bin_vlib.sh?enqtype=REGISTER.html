Joining the Library

Online Registration

Under 16? Click here!

To join Bedford Borough and Central Bedfordshire Libraries complete the form below, and click the "Join the Library" button. We will give you your Library Card Number and PIN straight away so that you can use our online services immediately.

Please be aware that if you live outside Bedford Borough and Central Bedfordshire and are unable to visit one of our libraries to collect a library card you will not be able to access The Times Digital Archive due to licence agreement restrictions.

To use the full services offered by the library please bring one form of identification to prove your name and address, e.g. driving licence, utility bill, to the library you selected. If you are under 16 you should bring proof of your parent's or guardian's name and address.
