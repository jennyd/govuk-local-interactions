Parking fines and enforcement

We are responsible for parking enforcement in Ormskirk's council owned car parks. This process is handled through a system called Chipside Lancashire.

Parking enforcement on the roads and streets within the borough is the responsibility of Lancashire County Council.

Paying or challenging a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN)

If your PCN number starts with LQ and was issued on a on a road or street in West Lancashire:

  • Pay your PCN (external link) 
  • Challenge your PCN (external link) 

If your PCN number starts with LN and was issued on a council owned car park in Ormskirk:

  • Pay your PCN (external link) 
  • Challenge you PCN (external link) 

More information

We are a member of the Parking and Traffic Regulations Outside London (PATROL)Adjudication Joint Committee. Further information about parking legislation and making a challenge against the issue of a PCN can be viewed at the PATROL (external link) website.
