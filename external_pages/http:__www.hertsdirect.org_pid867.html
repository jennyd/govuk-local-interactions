What we do with your feedback

Hertfordshire County Council Consultations

We want to know what you think about the issues that matter to you.
 
We run consultations on a wide range of new ideas, proposals and reviews so that you can have your say on the decisions that we make.
 
Sometimes consultations will take the form of face to face conversations, focus groups, is invitation only or are publically available to anyone to have their say.
 
We aim to follow up and publish a response to each consultation that summarises the wider views of the public and the decisions that they inform.

Current consultations

Consultation on Proposal to close the SpLD Base at Francis Combe Academy, Watford
The SpLD (Specific Learning Difficulties) Base at Francis Combe Academy can accommodate up to 8 children of secondary age with Statements of SEN/Education, Health and Care Plans to meet their specific learning difficulties (SpLD), along with a small number of specialist staff and an outreach service.

Consultation on Proposal to Close the SPLD Base at Barnwell School, Stevenage
The SpLD (Specific Learning Difficulties) Base at Barnwell School can accommodate up to 12 children of secondary age with Statements of SEN/Education, Health and Care Plans to meet their specific learning difficulties, along with a small number of specialist staff and an outreach service.

Hertfordshire Passenger Assistant Consultation
This consultation explains Hertfordshire County Council's proposal to remove passenger assistants from small vehicles (taxis) transporting primary aged children to mainstream schools.

Consultation on admissions arrangements for 2017/18
Hertfordshire County Council will be retaining its existing admission arrangements and schemes of coordination for 2017/18, with minor amendments to meet legislation and new guidance from the Department for Education (DfE).

Charlton Way Accessibility
Hertfordshire County Council has identified pedestrian and cycling facilities that can be introduced on Charlton Way between just south of Cock Lane to the Dinant Link Road/Essex Road roundabout that we think will achieve these aims, but we are keen to seek your views before a decision is made on whether to progress any or all of the initiatives.

Hertfordshire Domestic Abuse Partnership’s draft strategy
This consultation is asking for your views on the Hertfordshire Domestic Abuse Partnership's draft strategy for tackling domestic abuse in Hertfordshire over the next three years.

Privacy notice:

Hertfordshire County Council collects information through the use of online surveys. If you would like to opt-in to recieving a response about a consultation then you may be asked to disclose your contact information.

Scrutiny - reviewing the council's decisions

Our scrutiny committees regularly review decisions that the council makes and hold Executives to account in order to improve the services we offer to the people of our county. They also have the power to scrutinise health services.

Find out more about scrutiny including the dates for the next Overview & Scrutiny Committee and Health Scrutiny Committee.

If you would like to attend a scrutiny meeting or find out more information, contact tom.hawkyard@hertfordshire.gov.uk.
