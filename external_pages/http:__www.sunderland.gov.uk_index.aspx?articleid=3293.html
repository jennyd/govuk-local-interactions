Safer Sunderland Partnership

The Safer Sunderland Partnership is tackling crime, disorder, fear of crime and substance misuse to ensure that everyone in Sunderland is, and feels, safe and secure.

Free multi-agency training on domestic violence and abuse is now available to anyone who works with vulnerable people, children and their families.

  • Our aim and priorities for 2008-2023

    Our aim is to create a city which is, and is seen to be, safer by reducing crime, anti-social behaviour and the misuse of drugs through work on prevention, early intervention, enforcement, support and rehabilitation.
