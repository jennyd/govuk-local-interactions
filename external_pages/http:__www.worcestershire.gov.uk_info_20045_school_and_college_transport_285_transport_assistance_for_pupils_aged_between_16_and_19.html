Travel Assistance for Pupils Aged Between 16 and 19

We provide travel assistance to full time students continuing into further education provided the following criteria are met:

  • You must live in Worcestershire
  • You must be under 19 when the course commences
  • You should attend the establishment that is nearest to your home address
  • You must live more than 3 miles from the school or college

Travel assistance will only be provided to the nearest establishment to offer the desired level of course. To guarantee assessment for September, please complete your application before the end of the Summer Term.

Non-entitlement - Vacant Seat Payment Scheme

The Authority may still be able to help under the County Councilâ€™s Vacant Seat Payment Scheme, which offers parents the opportunity to buy any spare seats at a discounted rate on a school contract vehicle. Vacant seats can only be awarded once all eligible riders have been allocated their seat.

We will only offer spare seats on vehicles contracted to Worcestershire County Council, using existing routes and boarding points. (Travel is linked to specific schools, however it may be possible to offer a seat to a child who attends another school near the route. In these cases the County Council cannot accept responsibility for transporting the child when the vehicle does not operate because the specific school it serves are closed).

To apply for a vacant seat, please complete an application in the usual manner and indicate vacant seat application on the form.

We cannot guarantee that the spare seat will always be available as requirements for entitled pupils may change from time to time and on occasion, we may have to withdraw the facility. It is therefore important that you do not rely on the service and have alternative travel options available if necessary.

If assistance is approved a request for payment will be sent.
