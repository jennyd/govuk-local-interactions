The Annual Canvass

The electoral registration system changed in 2014. Previously, the ‘head of household’ was responsible for registering everyone who lived at the address, but now every individual is responsible for their own voter registration. This is called Individual Electoral Registration and means that people can register individually either online or by completing a paper form.

We must contact each household to find out if there are any changes, to identify new people and invite them to register. We also need to remove the details of people who no longer live at that address.

We are currently sending out a Household Enquiry Form (HEF) to every household to confirm who lives there or provide details of all potential electors living in the property.

If there are no changes to be made, you should confirm the details either online at www.registerbyinternet.com/croydon or by text, freephone, or post, via the contact details provided on the form. 

If the your household's details need to be changed, for example, to amend a name or add a new name on the voters list , new information can be provided online at  www.registerbyinternet.com/croydon. or by posting the amended form using the prepaid envelope provided.

An Invitation to Register (ITR) form will then be sent to anyone wishing to be added to the voters list.

If you require more detail on the electoral registration system, please visit www.gov.uk/yourvotematters
