Article: Older Peoples Housing

Images

Article Body

We have a range of housing schemes which are suitable for older people.  Follow this link to see a map.

The schemes usually provide the following facilities:-

  • Laundry
  • Communal lounge and kitchen
  • Security and safety features
  • Guest bedroom
  • District heating

A £1.25 million investment programme is currently underway in 11 of our schemes to provide scooter stores, modern entrance areas and updating communal rooms.  Follow this link to find out more..... 

What does the Scheme Manager do?

The Scheme Manager will be on site during office hours and can provide you with information and advice about social activities and events that are taking place.  The Scheme Manager is also responsible for the management of the building, and will carry out regular inspections of the communal areas. 

The Scheme Manager cannot provide any personal care, such as collecting pensions, prescriptions or doing shopping. 

If you think you might need some support to help you with your daily living tasks you can contact Adult Social Services on Telephone 01226 775656.

Will I qualify for Older Peoples housing?

Applicants must be 60 years old and have a medical need for this type of housing.

In some circumstances we will consider applicants under 60 years old with a medical need.  The Lettings Team will provide information and advice.

How do I apply for Older Peoples housing?

You can apply to join the housing register online by visiting our Find A Home Section.
