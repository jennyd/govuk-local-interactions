Becoming a Member

You can join in person by visiting any of Medway's libraries or you can join online by clicking on the link below.

On visiting the library you do not need to bring any identification with you to join immediately as a light member, which allows you to take out up to three items. If you want to use the full range of services however, you will need to bring in a current form of identification as proof of your name and address.

The following is a list of documents that will be accepted as proof of address:

  • Passport - New version that includes address
  • Photo Driving License

The following forms of identification are also acceptable if they are under 3 months old and contain details of your full name and address:

  • Welfare benefits book
  • Bank/credit/store card statement (Not loyalty cards)
  • Council Tax bill
  • Utility bill or statement - water, gas, electric, telephone
  • Pay slip
  • Medical Card
  • Tenancy agreement/rent book

If you are under 16 years old, your parent or guardian will need to provide proof of your identity.

With full membership, you can borrow up to 30 items from the library, choosing from:

  • Books
  • CDs
  • Audio books on cassette, CD or MP3
  • DVDs
  • Console games

Books are free to borrow, but there are charges for borrowing items such as CDs, DVDs, console games and some audio books.

Books are issued for three weeks but there are shorter loan periods for DVDs and console games.

When it is time to bring the items back, you can return them to any Medway library. Alternatively, items may be renewed up to four times either in person, over the telephone during opening hours or online via this website.

When renewing items over the telephone please have your library card number to hand.

To renew your books online you will need a personal identification number (PIN). To find out how to get a PIN ask at your local library or contact us by telephone, fax or email.

Overdue charges are made for the late return of items.

If you change your name or address, or lose your card, please notify your local library immediately.

Full details of services available, library regulations and fees and fines charged can be found by visiting

http://www.medway.gov.uk/leisurecultureandsport/libraries.aspx

Joining Online

If you are over 16 you may join the library online by clicking on the button below, after you have read and accepted the membership conditions.

Full details of library services available, library regulations and fees and fines charged can be found by visiting

http://www.medway.gov.uk/leisurecultureandsport/libraries.aspx

Conditions of Membership

When you join online, you will be given a temporary borrower number and PIN (Personal Identification Number) that are valid for 28 days. These will let you make three online reservations and create a profile of your interests. Before the 28 days expire, you will need to visit a library to collect your membership card.

The first time you visit one of our libraries you will be asked to confirm your details, by showing library staff a current form of identification as proof of your name and address (Please see the list at the top of the page for acceptable forms of identification). If you are 16 or 17 we will also need to see proof of your date of birth. You will then receive your library card and have full access to library services.
