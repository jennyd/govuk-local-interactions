Car Park Season Tickets

Car park season tickets are available from the web-link below, by calling Sedgemoor Direct on the number above or writing to Customer Services at Bridgwater House, King Square, Bridgwater, TA6 3AR

Season tickets can have a maximum of two car registration numbers on them. If there is any change to the registration numbers the ticket must be taken to reception to be updated.

Season tickets should be displayed on the car windscreen or dashboard.  Only the original season ticket is valid and any car displaying a copy will receive a penalty charge notice. The Council reserves the right to withdraw a season ticket if the holder does not adhere to the conditions of use.

Please note that season tickets will not be issued prior to 7 days before the start date and are only valid for the following long stay car parks:

Bridgwater

  • Eastover Shoppers (Barclay Street)
  • Market Street
  • Mount Street (East & West)
  • Northgate

Burnham-on-Sea

  • Discount car park (Berrow Road)
  • High Street (Lynton Road)
  • Oxford Street
  • Pier Street (East & South)

Cheddar

  • Church Street
  • Cliff Street

Highbridge

  • Bank Street

Reserved Space Season Tickets are available for the following car parks in Bridgwater:

  • Mount Street East
  • Mount Street South
  • Queen Street 

Vehicles are parked entirely at the owner's risk and subject to orders and regulations.

Cost of Season Tickets

  • Annual £650
  • Quarterly £170

Residents Season Tickets

Residents living within 200 m of a long-stay car park can buy a residents season ticket for the long-stay car park nearest to their home.   This type of season ticket can only contain one car registration number. Applicants need to show they are the registered keeper of the vehicle and it is registered at their address.

Cost of Residents Season Tickets:

  • Annual - £325
  • Quarterly - £85
