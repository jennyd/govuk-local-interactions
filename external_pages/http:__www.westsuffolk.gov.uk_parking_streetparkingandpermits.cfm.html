Street parking and permits

Residents living in the parking zones listed below can buy parking permits online or print off and post an application form using the links shown. Some zones allow two permits per household where space allows. Senior citizens who do not have a car can apply for a free permit for their visitors. Disabled badge holders are eligible to a free permit.

All ticket costs are within the terms and conditions of ticket use in the links below.

Temporary permits for contractors

  • Tradesmen and contractors can apply for a temporary parking permit online

Pocket car parks in Newmarket

The car parks listed below have parking spaces for residents, view a location map

  • All Saints Road
  • All Saints Road (snooker)
  • Friendship House, Granby Street
  • Granby Street (small)
  • Queen Street (garage site)
  • Queen Street

A parking space costs £150 plus VAT per year and is subject to availability. You will need to pay a deposit for a key fob for use in the pocket car parks. Key fobs are refundable and are £30 for All Saints car park and £20 in all others. To apply for a space, download and complete the application form for a Newmarket pocket car park space, the return address is on the form or you can email it to: property.services@westsuffolk.gov.uk
