Bereavement


Walsall Council Bereavement Services aims to provide a sympathetic, prompt and caring burial and cremation service for bereaved families of all religious and cultural backgrounds within the community. We are also responsible for the maintenance of Cemeteries and Crematoria under the control of the authority to an acceptable standard.

The facilities of the Bereavement Service are delivered by an experienced team of dedicated staff who take pride in their work and deliver the services in a professional though friendly way, always remembering that they are dealing with the public at a most vulnerable time.

Our workforce are responsible for day to day contact with Funeral Directors and members of the public. These front line employees often have to explain to distressed members of the public what the legal requirements are to enable funerals to take place. Although funerals are, more often than not, organised by Funeral Directors, families may make arrangements themselves and deal directly with us.

Rules and Regulations consultation 2016

A consultation is being held from 1 February to 31 March 2016 on the revised draft of the Rules and Regulations for Bereavement Services.  More information can be found on the consultation page.

Office locations

The management and administration for the Bereavement Services Division is based at Willenhall Lawn Cemetery .

The team there have expert knowledge in dealing with all matters relating to burials, cremations and memorialisation. They can also give help and advice to members of the public on a wide variety of bereavement issues.
