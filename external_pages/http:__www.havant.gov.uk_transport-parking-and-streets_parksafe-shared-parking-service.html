Parksafe shared parking service

Parksafe Shared Parking Service is operated jointly by both Havant Borough Council and East Hampshire District Council.

This includes parking in council-owned off-street car parks as well as the enforcement of on-street restrictions such as single and double yellow lines, loading restrictions and bus stop clearways, including proposals for new restrictions.  This overall management of parking both on and off street comprises the ParkSafe scheme, aimed at making the streets safer for all users.

Online Payment is not possible for PCNs issued in East Hampshire due to a technical issue. We apologise for any inconvenience whilst we work to resolve this issue.

Please call the 24 hour automated payment system on 0845 130 4466 if you would like to make payment via telephone.

Alternatively payment can be made by post: 

  • Post: Cheques or Postal Orders should be made payable to East Hampshire District Council and crossed "A/C payee" and posted to:

Parking and Traffic Management, PO Box 263, Petersfield, GU32 9FD

Again, we apologise for any inconvenience caused.
