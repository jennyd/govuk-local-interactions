Hazardous Waste Collection

Hazardous Waste – free disposal:

Some types of hazardous waste are accepted free of charge at the Household Waste Recycling Centres in Suffolk.

Private householders, educational establishments and charities can take the following items only to any of the HWRCs in Suffolk, free of charge:

  • Engine oil and cooking oil
  • Fire extinguishers (But not BCF or Halon types)
  • Fluorescent tubes and energy-efficient light bulbs
  • Gas cylinders (Calor and Flo gas.) Other makes should be returned to the manufacturer.
  • Household batteries
  • Car batteries
  • Refrigerators / freezers
  • Televisions and computer monitors
  • Water based paint if it has been dried out (can use soil or sand)

Empty containers of paint or chemicals can be put into your black (refuse) bin for disposal.

Containers of water based paint that have completely dried out can also be put into the black bin for disposal.

How much does it Cost?

Hazardous Waste – chargeable disposal (£45.13):

Most types of hazardous waste can be collected by the Council for a charge of £45.13 per collection. 

Please note we only collect from domestic sources (households).

This includes items such as:

  • DIY products e.g. varnishes, paint, paint stripper
  • Flammable liquids e.g. white spirit, paraffin, wood stains.
  • Garden chemicals/pesticides e.g. weed killers and insect killers
  • Household cleaning products and chemicals e.g. toilet / drain cleaners & bleach
  • Poisons e.g. rat or mouse poisons
  • Motoring products e.g. antifreeze, brake/clutch fluid, petrol, diesel

*Very large amounts of the above items may incur additional charges.

Bonded Asbestos (£75.13):

(Such as corrugated sheets, insulation board (this could be attached to a door housing a boiler), soffit boards, guttering/downpipe, water tanks, flue pipes and asbestos pieces)

We can collect a maximum 50kgs of bonded asbestos for a charge of £75.13, providing it is from a domestic source and has been removed by the householder and not by a contractor.  Weight depends on the thickness of the material, but a rough guide for 50kg is:

  • Corrugated sheets / insulation board = 3 sheets (6ft x 3ft)
  • Downpipe / Guttering = 3 or 4 pieces around 2m in length
  • Cold water tanks - up to 40kg depending on size
  • Broken pieces of asbestos = 2 sacks with a max 25kg (have to be manageable)

We require residents to be accurate when requesting a collection, we cannot accept approximate amounts or sizes.  To request an asbestos collection for up to 50Kgs, please call the Waste Services Team on 01449 778678.

For the collection of larger quantities of bonded asbestos sheets/pieces as well as dismantled bonded asbestos sheds, garages and outhouses, please contact East Coast Insulations on 01493 745111 for a quote.

We are NOT able to collect the following: 

Fireworks/yacht flares (please contact firearms section of Suffolk Constabulary, Martlesham Heath), explosives, radioactive waste, returnable gas cylinders, clinical waste, helium gas cylinders, flammable film stock (cellulose acetate), construction and demolition waste, fibrous asbestos, fluorescent tubes, and water or dry powder fire extinguishers.

We will not collect any of the hazardous items that can be taken to the Household Waste Recycling Centres unless by prior arrangement.

How to book a collection:

To book a hazardous waste collection we will need full details of the items that you would like to be collected such as the size/number of sheets, length, diameter and height.  We will also require your full contact details including a day time telephone number. Standard hazardous collections, (not including Asbestos) are charged at (£45.13) and can be booked online.

Once you have completed a collection request form and made payment you will be contacted by our contractor to advise you of the collection date. Collection will be made within 5 weeks of receipt of the collection request.
