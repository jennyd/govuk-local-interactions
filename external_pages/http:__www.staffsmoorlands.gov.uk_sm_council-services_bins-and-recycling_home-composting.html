Home composting

Home composting is the best way to deal with your organic waste

You can compost your garden and food waste at home, easily creating a good soil conditioner for your garden.

More than a quarter of your rubbish, such as fruit and vegetable peelings, teabags and eggshells, as well as all your garden clippings can be composted at home.

The attached document shows what can and cannot be composted in a home compost bin.

We are part of a Staffordshire wide home composting scheme and have teamed up with Get Composting to provide reduced cost home composters, wormeries and food digesters. 
      
For further information on getting started with home composting visit Recycle Now
