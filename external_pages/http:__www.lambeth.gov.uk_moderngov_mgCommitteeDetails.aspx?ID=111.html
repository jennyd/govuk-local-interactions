Purpose of committee

The Environment & Community Safety Scrutiny Sub-Committee examines the planning, provision and management of environment and street services in Lambeth (including refuse collection and recycling; road care and repair; climate change and sustainability), the borough’s leisure and culture facilities (parks and open spaces, sports centres, libraries), and community and public safety matters. The committee also has responsibility for scrutinising the work of the Safer Lambeth Partnership.
