Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support

Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support is available to help people who are on benefits, or who have low incomes, to pay their rent and Council Tax. It is paid by local councils. This section explains how to apply for Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support.

To find out if you will qualify for Housing Benefit and/or Council Tax Support please use this online calculator (External Link)

If you would like more information please contact the Council's Benefit Office on 01782 715500 or call into one of the following Council Offices:
