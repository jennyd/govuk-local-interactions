Residential Parking Permits

There are residential and business on-street parking permit schemes in place designed to provide on-street parking facilities in those neighbourhoods near to Ashford Town Centre which suffer from parking and congestion problems.

If you hold a resident’ or business permit you may park in the spaces provided in the appropriate zone the times indicated within the relevant scheme.

All residents living in Ashford town centre may apply for a yearly Residents off-street Parking Permit Zone B. Subject to successful application, this permit allows a resident to park his or her vehicle in Dover Place Car Park seven days a week, 365 days a year without the need to purchase pay and display tickets.

Temporary visitors parking permits can be purchased by residents and some businesses for £1 each. These can be purchased from Ashford Gateway Plus, Church Road, Ashford, Kent, TN23 1AS Monday to Friday 9am to 4pm.

When applying for a Resident or Business permit please allow time for your application to be processed. As a guide, following receipt (whether by post or delivered personally to the Civic Centre) of your correctly completed application form, the necessary documentation and payment of the relevant charge, your permit will be posted to you within five working days at the most. However, it is more likely that we will be able to put it in the post to you, the next working day.

Notice of variation to charges paid in connection with Residents Parking Permits

The council intends to vary Residents Parking Permit charges as described in The Kent County Council (Various Roads, Borough of Ashford) (Waiting Restrictions and Street parking Places) Consolidation Order 2015 and its subsequent amendment Orders.

The council’s Residents Parking Permit administration charges have remained the same since 2000/2003.  To continue to provide the level of service to our permit holders it has become necessary to increase the administration charges.  With effect from Monday 1st June 2015 Residents Parking Permit charges will be varied as follows:

 

Residents Parking Permit Zone A – annual charge from £100.00 to £120.00

Residents Parking Permit Zone B – annual charge from £40.00 to £50.00

Residents Parking Permit Zones D, E, F and G – annual charge from £25.00 to £30.00

 

If you wish to comment or have a query, please contact Parking Services by email at parkingcustomercare@ashford.gov.uk. Please provide your permit number, no later than Friday 24th April 2015, quoting reference ‘RPP/Charges/2015’.
