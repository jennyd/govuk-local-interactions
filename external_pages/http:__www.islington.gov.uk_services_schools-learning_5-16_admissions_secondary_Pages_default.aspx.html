Secondary school admissions

Page Content

Starting Year Seven in 2016

If you are an Islington resident and your child was born between 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005 they will be due to start secondary school in September 2016. You will need to apply for a place for your child by 31 October 2015.

You must apply for a school place using an Islington application form, even if you are applying for a place in another local authority.

If you do not live in Islington, you must apply using your home local authority’s form (i.e. the council to whom you pay your council tax).

Key dates

  • 1 September 2015 - online applications open
  • 31 October 2015 - application deadline
  • 1 March 2016 - offer day. We will send you a letter to inform you of the result of your application. You can also view the results of your application online if you registered and applied online.

How to apply?

Firstly, read the Islington secondary school admissions brochure for step-by-step guidance. You can view or download the secondary admissions brochure by clicking on the link found at the bottom of this page in Related documents.

Please read the brochure carefully as some schools (church schools) require you to complete a supplementary information form.

Once you have read the brochure and you have decided which schools you would like to apply for, click on the following link which will take you through the online application process.

Apply Online

Find My Nearest School

 

Note:

  • Make sure you apply on time as the chances of getting one of your preferred schools is likely to be affected if your application is late as it is not processed until all other applications have been considered.
  • You can apply for up to six schools. Try to use all six preferences.
  • If you cannot apply online, then please contact the School Admissions team for further advice.
  • If you apply online, please do not submit a paper application
