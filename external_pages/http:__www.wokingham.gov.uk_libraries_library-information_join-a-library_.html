Join a library

Join a library

You can join if you live, work or study in Wokingham borough, or are a member of any other library.

There is no charge for joining a library. To join:

  • Fill in an application form at your nearest library
  • Produce identification showing your name and current address such as driving licence, bank statement with bank card or utility bill less than 3 months old 
  • Under 18s need a signature from a parent or  guardian
