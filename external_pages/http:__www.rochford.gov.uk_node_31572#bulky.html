Reuse

All furniture collected using the Council's bulky waste scheme will be taken to landfill, so please consider whether items can be reused before you book a collection.

Most items can have a second life.

By reusing items you are reducing the amount of waste you produce, some of which may have ended up in a landfill site in Essex.

Hopeworx Reuse Centre

Hopeworx Reuse Centre opened in 2012 and works in partnership with The Lighthouse Furniture Project Ltd and is a registered charity. The centre sells used furniture and electrical goods and also take donations of items that can be reused or sold.

Hopeworx collect reusable items free of charge from households if they meet the standards required.

Hopeworx is located at:

Anchor House

33 Brook Road

Brook Road Industrial Estate

Rayleigh Essex SS6 7XJ

Tel: 01268 743232

Please see the related links for more information and to arrange a free collection by Hopeworx.

Bulky waste

The charges for the collection of bulky waste (household furniture) and electrical household items are :

• £12 for the first item, additional items are charged £6 each

All items must be booked at the same time.

Please note, bulky waste collections do not take place on Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Year’s Day, Good Friday and August Bank Holidays.

The furniture collected under the bulky waste scheme will be taken to landfill so please consider if the items can be reused before you book a collection. If items are in a good condition, contact Hopeworx Reuse Centre, please see the related links.

If your item is not reusable and wish to arrange collection and payment please call Customer Services on 01702 318111 during the hours of 8:30am to 5:30pm, Monday to Thursday and 8:30am - 5pm on Fridays.

Payment can be made over the phone with a credit or debit card. Please note there will be a 2% surcharge for credit card payments.

Payment can also be made by cheque (made payable to 'Rochford District Council') and by cash at the Council Offices in South Street, Rochford and the Civic Suite, Hockley Road, Rayleigh.

Furniture is collected on the day after your normal wheelie bin collection day. Furniture needs to be presented by 7am, on the boundary of your property on this day for collection. Payment must be received by 3pm on the day before collection is due.

Below is an example list of bulky items we do and do not take; however this is not exhaustive. If there are any items not listed here that you need collecting, please call us on 01702 318111 or contact us using this form to check we can collect these items for you.

  • Blinds

  • Radiators

  • Stools

Electrical Items (any item with a plug)

  • Extractor fans

  • Fridges

Items we do not collect

  • Concrete, soil & turf
  • Fencing
  • Garage doors

  • Any refuse/rubbish/non-recyclables in bags or sacks

If the item you want to dispose of is not covered by the above you will need to organise your own disposal - remember to check for an official Waste License carrier. You can search for a licensed skip firm through comparison sites found in related links below.
