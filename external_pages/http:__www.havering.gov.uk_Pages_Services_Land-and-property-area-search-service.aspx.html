Online planning searches

Intro Text
Our online search facility allows you to look at current and previous planning applications, and take measurements from the drawings.

Page Content

To view weekly lists of planning applications submitted, visit the applications received web page. You can also view the weekly list at our planning reception desk in the Public Advice and Service Centre within the Liberty Shopping Centre, Romford weekdays between 9am and 11am, except on the last Wednesday in every month.

Forms of online search

We offer three forms of online search:

Application search Information on all applications received since 1990, whether they are current or already decided. Copies of documents such as application forms and drawings are viewable only for applications received since January 2003.
Decision search This search provides information only on applications that have had a decision issued since 1990. You can also view any conditions or reasons that have been included with the decision and view the actual decision notice for applications decided since January 2003.
Appeals search You can search for any planning, listed building or advertisement appeals that have been lodged since 2003.


 Please note: The information available through our online facility does not constitute a full planning history for a site, and must not be used as a substitute for carrying out a formal Local Land Charges search.

No responsibility will be taken for any errors or omissions in the planning information provided online, and no liability is accepted for loss or damage arising from use of planning information obtained from this site whether foreseeable or otherwise.

If you experience any problems viewing plans or documents online please be patient and try again later, the problems are usually only temporary. If you continue to experience problems, it may be due to the settings on your computer.

Have your say

Please visit our Planning consultations page for further information about commenting on a current application.

Copyright

Plans, drawings and material submitted to Havering Council are protected by the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 (Section 47). You may only use material which is downloaded and/or printed:

  • For consultation purposes
  • To compare current applications with previous schemes; and
  • To check whether developments have been completed in accordance with approved plans

Further copies must not be made without the prior permission of the copyright owner. In using any of the online planning systems on this page, you are agreeing to adhere to the above legislation.

Image quality and file size issues

The images on this website are either PDF files or scanned versions of paper originals, and may not appear to scale on your computer screen. Dimensions, where they appear on drawings, are the only reliable method of obtaining measurements from these images. If you wish to obtain accurate dimensions of a proposal please contact us.

Images may take a short time to appear on your screen depending on their size and the speed of your Internet connection. For this reason, this online service is best used with a broadband connection.
