How do I use it?

You can report the flyposting or graffiti using our online form. You can also report it by calling us on 01823 356356.

What happens next?

We will investigate the graffiti and take appropriate action.

What is covered?

We only remove graffiti from public areas or council properties.

We will only remove graffiti from private property when we have permission from owner, and there is a charge for this service, however we may remove grafitti that is discriminative (for example racist grafiti).

How long does it take?

We aim to remove offensive or racial graffiti from public buildings within 24 hours of receiving a report.

We aim to remove fly posting from public areas and street furniture within 5 working days.

How much does it cost?

There is a charge for removing graffiti on private land. We can inspect the area and give a free quotation . We will only remove graffiti with the consent of the property owner.
