Community meals

The Community Meals service provides either hot or frozen meals direct to people's homes, this service is provided by I-Care. You can find out more by contacting I-Care directly telephone 01543 275015.

The service aims to provide vulnerable people, who often live alone and cannot cater for themselves, with nutritional meals. The service is available to older people and anyone with a physical disability and/or sensory impairment living in our area.

Many supermarkets and other retailers have online shopping services, where you can order goods - including groceries and fresh food - on the internet and have them delivered to your door. Other stores have a home delivery service, which allows you to go to the shop to choose what you want without having to carry it home.

You can find out more by looking up your supermarket or other retailer online.

Pub Lunch Clubs provide a chance to socialise and meet friends old and new.  Throughout the area, people who are over 60 are meeting together in their communities to enjoy each other's company and good food. Each club meets at least once a month at the same local venue to enjoy a two course lunch with tea or coffee for a reduced price. One provider of these are Age UK.
