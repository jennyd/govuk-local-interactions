School Clothing Grants

A clothing grant is available for all year 7 pupils who are entitled to Free School Meals at the beginning of an academic year. The grant is currently £105.00 (subject to review). 

Pupils entitled to a grant are identified from existing records and no application is required.

For further information on School Clothing Grants please contact 01495 766930.

Alternatively, for information on Free School Meals please contact 01633 647715.
