Planning

 

The Development Management Section is responsible for determining your planning applications. These pages enable you to interact with the service; obtain necessary information and guidance and view/comment on the planning applications themselves.

Our planning applications list is updated each week and made available online via our Public Access for Planning service, where you can also view, track and comment on applications, view decision notices and view associated documents.

Building work may also require an application under the Building Regulations.

To contact the Development Management Section of the Council, please refer to the phone, fax or email details under the 'Contact Us' section on this page.

Make an application through the Planning Portal

Gateshead Council's Development Management service is registered with the Planning Portal - the government's online service for planning. This means you can apply for planning permission over the Internet, as well as submit and track an appeal online.

After registration, you will be able to complete and online application form, add attachments, submit your application electronically and pay online.

This service is:

  • Free of charge to use;
  • Open 24 hours a day 7 days a week;
  • No printing or postage required - cost savings;
  • To register and submit applications is simple;
  • Online feedback on individual applications.
