Report a problem on a pavement or cycle path

 

We are responsible for maintaining the highway including pavements and footways. Trip hazards on pavements are a key concern at all times whilst the problems of weeds, standing water and ice tend to be more seasonal.

You can report problems with highway kerbs, cycleways and footways using the button below. 

Report now

When contacting us please provide the following information:

  • Your name and telephone number, in case we need to contact you
  • The location, including the road name and town or village
  • Details of your concern 

When you report a defect relating to a footway or cyclepath we will inspect it within 3 working days. The repair response time will depend on the type of defect reported and the location. Repair will be in accordance with our Somerset Highway Safety Inspection Manual. 

When determining priorities for footway maintenance we aim to ensure that opportunities are taken to aid social inclusion particularly improving accessibility for older and disabled people and also the use of prams and pushchairs.

This will include the provision of dropped kerbs in suitable locations and textured paving adjacent to crossing points.

General information

We aim to promote walking and cycling as an alternative to using transport by the provision of a safe and convenient network of footways and cycleways.

We aim to provide safe footways and cycleways of satisfactory appearance to encourage increased usage by the public.

Manhole Covers
Please contact us to report a manhole cover that is broken, collapsing, missing, very low or protruding above road level that might be dangerous to highway users. We will assess the cover and arrange remedial work as appropriate.

It is useful to know who the owner of the cover is (e.g. Telephone, Gas, Water etc.), as it can speed up the process of repair or replacement. If the manhole is owned by a Utility Company we will request that the Utility Company fix the problem within 7 days. Alternatively, you can call the Utility Company directly to report a defect.
