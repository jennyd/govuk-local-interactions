Text Us

Step 1

Save the number 07800 00 24 25 to your phone, you never know when you will need to contact us.  If you notice an area that is in need of attention, simply send us a text message.

Step 2

Please be as descriptive as possible in your message, for example 'where can I get a Gold card?', 'abandoned car vandalised on Eastmoor' or 'when is Sharley Park Leisure Centre open?'

Step 3

Send to 07800 00 24 25

Your message will go directly to our Customer Services Team who will arrange for the appropriate action to be taken.  A text message will be sent back to let you know what this action will be.

How much will my text message cost to send?

The cost of sending a text message depends on your mobile phone tariff, but we don't make any additional charge.

Different Format or Language?

We want everyone to be able to understand us and be able to read our written materials. We are committed to breaking down communication barriers to enable you to read and talk and write to us. We will produce materials in a format which is as accessible as possible.

If you need any information provided on these webpages in a different language please use our Talkback Interpreting service free of charge.

If you need any of our information producing in a different format please contact our main customer service line number listed below.
