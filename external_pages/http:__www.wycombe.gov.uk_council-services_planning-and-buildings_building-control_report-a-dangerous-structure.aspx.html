Report a dangerous structure

Building Control monitor and control all dangerous structures within the district, under the Building Act 1984 ensuring a building or structure is safe to members of the public.

Our surveyor will examine the level of danger and may have the structure demolished or made safe depending on the severity. The owner of the building or structure is liable for the cost incurred by the council.

How to report a dangerous structure

Please call Building Control on 01494 421 403 or email building_control@wycombe.gov.uk.

If your report cannot wait until the next working day, please call our out-of-hours emergency number: 01494 463 890

When reporting a dangerous structure, please let us know the exact location, describe the damage to the property or the structure. Please also leave your contact details.
