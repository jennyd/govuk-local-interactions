Severe Weather, Flooding, Cold Snaps and Snow- Service Updates

During severe weather, especially cold snaps or snowfall, many council services can be affected. This section contains some quick links to important information, and details of any East Herts Council services that have been changed. - Updated:

Refuse and Recycling

All collections are being made today.  If this changes we will update you through the website.

Car Parks

There are no current alerts.

Please Note: Road Gritting / Salting

Although East Herts Council Street Cleansing and Grounds Maintenance teams often assist; Road Gritting/Salting, Pavement Gritting/Salting and the provision/re-stocking of public gritting bins is undertaken by Hertfordshire County Council.  Please visit the  Hertfordshire County Council web pages for more details.

Roads

For the most up-to-date information on road closures, visit:  HCC - Emergency Road Closures

Weather

View Winter Weather (GOV.UK website) which brings together winter preparedness material (floods and extreme weather, power outage) and news.
