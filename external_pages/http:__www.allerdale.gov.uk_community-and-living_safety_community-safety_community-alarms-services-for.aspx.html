Community Alarms Services for the Elderly

(Age Concern Northwest Cumbria is now known as Age UK West Cumbria)

Aid-Call is a personal SOS system provided by Age Concern. It is simple to use system that uses a waterproof emergency button worn on the wrist. Pressing the emergency button, if you have had an accident or feel ill, will connect you to the Aid-Call emergency response centre 24 hours a day. The button will work from anywhere in your home or garden.

There is a charge for the Aid-Call service - charges are kept as low as possible and any profits go to support Age Concern.

For more information and to arrange a free demonstration, please call Age Concern on 0800 77 22 66.
