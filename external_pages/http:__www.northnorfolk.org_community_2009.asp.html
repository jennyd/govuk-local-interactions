Community Safety

  • Anti-Social Behaviour

    The North Norfolk Operational Partnership Team (OPT) co-ordinates the response of the Norfolk Safer Communities Partnership in tackling anti-social behaviour (ASB) across the district.  The North Norfolk OPT is made up of representatives from North Norfolk District Council, Victory Housing Trust and Norfolk Constabulary as well as the Partnership Anti-Social Behaviour Coordinator.

  • Designated Public Place Orders

    The purpose of a Designated Public Place Order is to restrict anti-social drinking in designated public places.

  • Domestic Violence

    Practical information for those suffering from Domestic Abuse.

  • North Norfolk Safety Advisory Group

    The Safety Advisory Group helps "promote the health, safety and welfare" of all those involved with public events.

  • Norfolk Police and Crime Panel

    Representatives of all the District Councils sit on the Norfolk Police and Crime Panel, which scrutinises the work of the Police and Crime Commissioner.

  • Vehicle ASB

    Did you know Anti-social Behaviour can also relate to vehicles?
