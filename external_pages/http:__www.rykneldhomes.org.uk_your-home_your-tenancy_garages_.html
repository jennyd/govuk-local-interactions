Garages and Garage Plots

If you would like to rent a garage or garage plot, you have to complete a Garage Application Form.  This is available to download from this website or you can request a copy from your local Area Housing Office or One Stop Shop or your Neighbourhood Support Officer.


You do not have to be a current tenant of Rykneld Homes to rent a garage or garage plot.  You can apply if you are a:

  • Council tenant
  • Owner Occupier
  • Lodger
  • Tied Tenant
  • Housing Association tenant
  • Private tenant

 

You may not be eligible to rent a garage or garage plot if:

  • You have committed a criminal offence or engaged in criminal or anti social behaviour
  • You owe North East Derbyshire District Council or any other council or landlord current rent arrears, former tenant's arrears or any sundry debts
  • You are or have been in the past, subject to any formal notice to seek possession of your home.


You will have to supply details on your application form.


Where are the garages and garage plots situated?

Garages and garage plots are available throughout the district on many of our estates.  You can find out where they are situated by clicking on an estate in the 'Your Estate' Section or look at the list found within the Garage Application Form.

How do I apply?

Complete the Garage Application Form available to download on this page or you can pick a copy up from your local Area Housing Office or One Stop Shop.  Please complete the form in full and return to your local office of post it to us:
