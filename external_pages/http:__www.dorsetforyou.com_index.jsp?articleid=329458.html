Christchurch Borough Councillors

Christchurch Borough Council has 24 councillors who are elected by the public every four years. They represent 11 different wards of Christchurch.

The political composition of the council is presently:

  Conservative 21 Members   
  Independent 2 Members   
  UKIP 1 Member   

The Conservative Group have overall control of the Council.


The next borough council elections will be held in May 2019. See recent and previous election results for Christchurch Borough Council.
