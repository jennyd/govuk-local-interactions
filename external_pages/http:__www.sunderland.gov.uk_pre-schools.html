Pre-schools

  • Nursery education

    In addition to the nursery schools and classes offered by the Local Authority (LA), there are also a number of private nurseries in Sunderland.

  • Free early education entitlement

    The free early education entitlement is available to all children aged three and four years old, and children become eligible the term after their third birthday. In addition, many families across Sunderland can benefit from free childcare places for two year olds.

  • Private nurseries - nursery grants

  • School - nursery inspections

  • School - education in hospital

    All children and young people with medical needs should have their educational needs identified quickly and receive the appropriate support they require as soon as possible, including any stays in hospital.

  • School governors

    This page will help you if you are interested in becoming a school governor.

  • School - assessment and performance

    Various agencies are responsible for publishing school performance tables. The links on this page help you find the relevant school's performance table.

  • School curriculum

    Information on the National Curriculum is available on the Government's GOV.UK website.

  • School - development plans

    Performance and assessment reports help schools look at their strengths and weaknesses, compare their performance with other schools and develop plans to raise their standards.

  • School - exclusion of pupils

    Exclusion means to exclude a child from a Maintained School on disciplinary grounds, (according to the Education Act 2002).

  • School - attendance

    Being in school is important for many reasons.

  • School term and holiday dates

    Information about school holidays and term dates

  • School meals

    Information about school meals, the menus and how to access free school meals.

  • Disclosure, barring and vetting

    The Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) has access to personal records held by the Police, the Department of Health, and the Department for Education ensuring the best possible scrutiny of applicants.

  • School - closures

    For the latest information on unavoidable school closures, please contact us. This page provides further details of other sources of information about school closures.

  • Roads - school crossing patrols
