Allotments

Allotments are a great way of providing low cost, homegrown food for you and your family and for keeping fit!

They also contribute to our green spaces and provide habitat for all kinds of wildlife, with more information available on the following links:

Who administers my local allotment?

The borough's 12 allotment sites are administered by a combination of Gedling Borough Council, independent Allotments Associations and local Parish Councils.

Gedling Borough Council

We offer 60 plots at three sites around the borough, with all having access to running water. Please use the following external links for site maps and directions to the allotment sites we administer:

  • Killisick Allotments- Hawthorn Crescent, Arnold
  • Howbeck Road Allotments- Howbeck Road, Arnold
  • Rookery Gardens Allotments - Rookery Gardens, Arnold

Allotment Associations

The following sites are administered by independent Allotment Associations, with the external links below providing site maps and directions to each allotment:

  • Gedling Grove Allotment Association- Gedling Grove, Arnold.
  • New Robin Hood Allotment Association- Cavendish Road, Carlton.
  • Stoke Lane Allotment Association- Stoke Lane, Gedling.
  • Chandos Street Allotment Association- Chandos Street, Netherfield.
  • Leapool Allotment Association - Mansfield Road, Redhill.  

Parish Councils

The following allotments are administered by local Parish Councils, with site maps and locations included on the external links below:

  • Trent Lane Allotments- Trent Lane, Burton Joyce.  Contact Burton Joyce Parish Council on 0115 931 4084.
  • Bonner Lane Allotments- Bonner Lane, Calverton. Contact Calverton Parish Council on 0115 965 4560.
  • Collyer Road Allotments- Collyer Road, Calverton. Contact Calverton Parish Council on 0115 965 4560.
  • Foxwood Lane Allotments - Foxwood Lane, Woodborough.  Contact Woodborough Parish Council on 0115 983 0231.

For more information, please contact the relevant Parish Council using the details provided above.

How much does a plot cost?

This can vary depending on who administers the site and the size of the plot, but an average price is between £30-£50 per annum.

How to apply

To apply for allotment sites administered by Gedling Borough Council or the Allotment Associations, you can complete our application form or contact the Allotments team.

To apply for allotment sites administered by local Parish Councils please contact the organisation directly.
