Report hazard on the road

 

You can report a hazard on the road using the button below.

Report now

When contacting us please provide the following information:

  • the road name 
  • is it outside a specific property? 
  • is it in the road or the footway? 
  • if hazardous waste do you know what the waste is? 
  • is it near a watercourse (for example river/stream) or a drain?  

We are responsible for co-ordinating and arranging the clearance and disposal of hazards that are causing a danger or obstruction on the highway in conjunction with the police and/or appropriate specialist waste contractor and the Environment Agency. 

Examples of hazards can include a tree branch, hazardous waste, traffic cones. blown tyre or rubbish.
