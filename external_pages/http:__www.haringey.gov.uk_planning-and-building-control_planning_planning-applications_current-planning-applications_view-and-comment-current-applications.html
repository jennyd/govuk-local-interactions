Personal searches

If you want planning history and policy information on a property, you can now save yourself a trip to the planning office (and the £26 fee).

To view the planning history for a site, use the view planning applications facility. To find out whether the property is affected by any planning policies by way of its location, please see our Planning Policy pages.

Development Management Forums

Development Management Forums are used to facilitate the discussion of large scale or contentious planning applications. Its purpose is to allow participants to raise issues of concern and obtain answers to questions about the particular application from the planning applicants. The aim is to allow early discussion by Councillors and members of the public on planning issues related to these planning applications. More information about Development Management Forums can be found in our guide in the attached files section.
