Citizenship ceremonies

Every applicant for British citizenship is required to attend a citizenship ceremony to swear an oath of allegiance to the Crown and make a pledge to uphold the values and laws of the UK.

If the Home Office approves your application for British citizenship, they will send you an invitation letter which will tell you who to contact to book your citizenship ceremony. This must be completed within 3 months of the date stated in your letter.

The ceremony

Solihull ceremonies are performed in the Council Chamber at the Civic Suite, unless stated otherwise. An access guide for the Civic Suite is available at DisabledGo.

This group event starts at 7.00pm and finishes at 8.00pm on the following dates:

  • 9 March 2016
  • 13 April 2016
  • 15 June 2016
  • 13 July 2016
  • 10 August 2016
  • 14 September 2016
  • 12 October 2016
  • 9 November 2016
  • 7 December 2016

How to book

Once you have received your home office letter you can:

Apply online

You can also download a Citizenship application form and return to:

Solihull Register Office
Homer Road
Solihull
B91 3QZ

If you wish to cancel or amend the date of your booking please view our citizenship fees.

What does the ceremony involve and who can attend?

The ceremony takes the following format:

  • you will be welcomed into the community by the Registration team
  • you will be required to swear the oath or take the affirmation of allegiance, and the pledge of loyalty
  • you will be presented with your Certificate of Naturalisation
  • the National Anthem will be played

Photographs are permitted throughout the ceremony and a member of the team will assist if you have not brought any guests but have a camera. Following the ceremony you will have the opportunity to take further photographs.

Every adult applicant may bring two guests to the ceremony.

How do I arrange a private ceremony?

If you would prefer a more intimate ceremony, then please telephone 0121 704 8002 and this can be arranged for you.

Individual ceremonies will take place during office hours at the Register Office and last approximately 15 minutes. There is an additional fee for this service.

How do I become a British Citizen?

You should contact the Home Office who will be able to advise you, please telephone 0845 010 5200 or email nationalityenquiries@ind.homeoffice.gsi.gov.uk

Further information can be found on the Home Office website.

If your preparing for your UK Citizenship test our virtual reference library can help. GoCitizen is an online learning resources you can use at home with your Solihull library card - it's free to join!
