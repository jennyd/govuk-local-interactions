Roads and highways

First Priority Routes in Gwynedd, including main roads, bus routes and roads to hospitals are gritted when there are forecasts of snow and/or ice.

  • The priority 1 gritting routes are viewable on our interactive maps

When we expect hard ice, or when snow has fallen, other roads will be treated after we ensure Primary Routes are clear.

Waste and recycling

Please put your waste and recycling out as usual during snow and ice. We will make every effort to collect it.
