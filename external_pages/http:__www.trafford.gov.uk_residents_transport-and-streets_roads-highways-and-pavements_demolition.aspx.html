Demolition

When intending to demolish a building, notice must be given to the Local Authority at least six weeks prior to work commencing.

You need to submit a letter of intent which states the location of the building together with a location plan of the building and adjoining streets.

The Council makes a fixed charge of £214 for the provision of this service. Please enclose a cheque for this amount with your letter and location plan. If a cheque is not enclosed with the application an invoice for this amount will be sent out.

Letters of intent and associated documents should be posted to the following address:

Structures Section
Waterside House
Sale Waterside
Sale
M33 7ZF

Statutory Undertakers should also be notified in order that they may arrange for their services to the property to be sealed.
