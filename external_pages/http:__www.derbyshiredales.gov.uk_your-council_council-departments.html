Wednesday opening time: The Town Hall opens half an hour later on Wednesdays - at 9.30am - to facilitate staff training

Our District Council Town Hall main reception is open Monday - Friday between 9.00am and 5.00pm (except Wednesdays 9.30am - 5.00pm)

Our customer service telephone line - 01629 761 100 - is open Monday - Friday between 9.00am and 5.00pm
