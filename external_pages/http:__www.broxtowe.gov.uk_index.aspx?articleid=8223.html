Severe Weather

Latest news on severe weather conditions and updates via social media.

Public transport and commuting

Adverse weather and hazardous driving conditions may seriously affect local transport links. Commuters are urged to contact their provider for the latest updates before travelling.

School closures

The latest news and information regarding school closures in the borough is available on the link provided to Nottinghamshire County Council's school closures page.

Gritting

Nottinghamshire County Council has responsibility for gritting highways in the borough, with details of the County Council's gritting routes available on the link to the County Council's Gritting and Salting routes web page provided.

Refuse collections

Broxtowe Borough Council refuse collections will continue as scheduled wherever possible during severe weather, unless further updates are provided below.

If we are unable to collect your bin due to the weather, please leave it out for collection and we will return as soon as possible.

Social media

Broxtowe Borough Council uses Facebook and Twitter to issue the latest news and information during periods of bad weather.

Latest news will be issued direct to the Broxtowe Borough Council Facebook page allowing local residents and businesses to receive updates straight to their desktop or mobile device.

Alternatively, you can also get regular updates by following @broxtowebc on Twitter.
