Dogs

Please contact us if you find a stray dog. We try our best to reunite lost dogs with their owners. Please check our found dogs page, if you have lost a dog

Our Dog Warden Service Policy can be viewed here:   Dog Warden Service Policy [71kb]
Found Dogs We try and reunite all found dogs with their owner. This page lists the details any dogs found within the last seven days that we have been unable to trace their owner. After seven days, dogs not claimed by their owner are re-homed if possible. If no dogs are listed, we are not currently holding any dogs. Find out more
Lost Dogs Check our list of dogs that have been found within the last seven days. If your dog is not listed, you can put their details on the DogsLost website at www.DogsLost.co.uk Find out more
Fees If we seize a stray dog fees may be incurred for kennel costs, veterinary charges and possibly a Fixed Penalty Notice depending on the circumstances. Find out more
Control Orders and Dog Free Beaches We enforce Dog Control Orders to stop problems caused by dogs in public places. Find out more
Out of normal office hours If you find a dog in the evening or weekend you can call our out of hours service with the details. Find out more
Guide for responsible dog owners Most dog owners act responsibly, there are just a small minority who create a problem for others. Here are some tips on being a responsible dog owner. Find out more
