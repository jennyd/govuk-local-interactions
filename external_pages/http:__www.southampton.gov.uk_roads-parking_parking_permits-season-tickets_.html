Permits and season tickets

You will need a permit to park in a residents permit zone. Season tickets are available for city centre parking.
If you change your vehicle you will need to apply for a new permit.
If you change your address, you will need to return your permit for a refund for each full unexpired month and apply for a new permit.

Renew a residents permit

If your permit is coming up for renewal and you want to apply in person, please make sure you book an appointment well in advance.
If you park in a restricted area without a valid permit, you may receive a penalty charge notice and a fine.
To renew an existing resident’s parking permit by post you will need to sign the statement on your reminder letter and return it with your payment.

Apply or renew in person:

All permits, parking cards and season tickets are issued in person at Gateway by appointment only. To book your appointment call our Customer Services team on: 023 8083 2005.

  • if you are applying for a Resident Permit for the first time, please provide your V5 and Proof of Residency
  • for renewals please provide your reminder letter
  • for decrementing and season tickets please see evidence as required on the forms

We aim to process you permit application or renewal within 2 working days of your request.

To help you decide which parking permit you require select the description which best describes your needs below:

Business Permits
For businesses with an address in a Resident’s Permit Zone, and trades people working in a Resident’s Permit Zone.

City Centre Permits and Season Tickets
For residents or anyone wishing to park in the city centre bounded by the pay and display zone.

Decrementing Card
Prepayment parking card for use in barrier controlled multi-storey car parks.

Medical Practitioners and Carer Permits
For medical practitioners and carers to park in Residents Permit Zone whilst giving medical attention or home care.

Residents and Visitors Permits
For residents and their visitors to park in a Resident’s Permit Zone outside the city centre pay and display zone.
Find your residents parking zone number

Suspended pay and display bays
To reserve a pay and display bay for sole use for 24 hours.
