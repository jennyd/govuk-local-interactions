Pay your council tax

Pay by direct debit

Sign up online to pay by direct debit. Most people pay by direct debit. Alternatively we can take your details over the telephone on 01926 456760. We have a choice of payment dates available: 1st, 5th, 10th, 15th, 20th and 25th of the month.

Pay online

Make a payment online

There is a surcharge of 2% when paying by credit card. There is no additional charge for paying by debit card. Full details and security features.

Pay using telephone or online banking

Warwick District Council bank details
HSBC Bank PLC, Royal Leamington Spa
Sort code: 40-27-06, account number: 71023381

Please ensure you quote your council tax account reference on your payment to ensure it reaches your account.

Pay by telephone

Freephone 0800 028 3377. Payment line available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.*
*Payments made by credit card using website or freephone will incur a 2% charge. There is no charge for payments made by debit card.

Pay in person

Take your bill and payment to any Post Office or PayPoint outlet.

WDC payment card

By using your plastic payment card at any Post Office or PayPoint Outlet. Payments made by Cheque or Credit/Debit Card at PayPoint are at the discretion of the retailer and may incur a charge.

Where to pay your bill

You can pay your council tax at the following locations using the barcode on you bill or by using your Allpay card:

1. At all Post Offices
2. At PayPoint outlets

Electronic billing

Sign up for E-billing : E-Billing Sign up form.

Please note that none of the council offices have facilities for taking payments.

Contact us

If you have any questions or would like to change your existing payment method please call 01926 456760 or email ctaxenquiries@warwickdc.gov.uk.

Help to pay your bill

If you are in receipt of Income Support, Job Seekers Allowance, Pension Credit or on a low income, you may qualify for a reduction in your council tax. This is called council tax reduction. You may also get help if you are of pension age and share your home with another adult, or adults, who are on a low income. This is called second adult rebate. This is not available to those who are not of pension age. If you think you may qualify contact us straight away. Any delay could lose you benefit.

Contact the benefits department

Telephone (01926) 456760
Fax (01926) 456059
Email wdcbenefits@warwickdc.gov.uk
