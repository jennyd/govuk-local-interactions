Children's Services

Welcome to Children's Services

The Council of the Isles of Scilly is committed to providing excellent services to children, young people and families.  Please follow the links to find out more about what we may be able to help and support with; if however you can’t find the information you’re looking for, please feel free to get in touch with us directly, contact details at the bottom of the page. 

Our strategic vision and current areas of priority are set out within our Children & Young People’s Plan.  We also have a range of plans and policies that underpin many of our services, some of which are available on this website.

The Council of the Isles of Scilly doesn’t work in isolation when planning and delivering services for children and young people, we work very closely with a variety of island and mainland based organisations to ensure that multi agency working is at the heart of all we do.

To stay in touch with the activities we’re involved with follow us on Facebook or Twitter.  You can also sign up to receive our regular emails that promote what is going on.  Whether you’re on Scilly for just a few days or a resident we may have something taking place that you or your family would like to take part in.

We welcome comments, compliments or complaints so please feel free to bring your views to our attention so that we can factor these into our future plans.
