Parking Permit - Residents

Online - Resident On Street Parking Permit

You will require a valid credit or debit card and copied of relevant evidence to upload or

Resident’s permits are issued to assist residents to park their vehicles, within restricted zones situated throughout the Borough of Poole.

Cost: - Resident's parking permits are currently £55 a year.

Please note - supporting documentary evidence is required with each application, including renewals.

You will need to produce:

Proof of residence:

  • Council Tax Bill (authorisation to check Council Tax records is on application form)
    or Tenancy Agreement
    or Current Bank Statement (only if your name is not on Council Tax records, along with copy of Council Tax bill)

Proof of vehicle ownership:

  • Registration Document V5 pages 1 and 2 (the vehicle must be registered to the property and applicant)
  • Schedule of Insurance, showing name and address (only if vehicle is new to you or you have just moved to the area)
  • Company vehicles  - (a letter is required from the company to confirm the resident is the keeper of the vehicle, as well as registration document or lease agreement)

If you are unable to provide the required documentary evidence or are unsure about what to supply please contact the Parking Administration Section on (01202) 634240 for advice.

To apply:-

Online - Resident On Street Parking Permit. You will require a valid credit or debit card and copies of relevant evidence to upload or post.

Post - Complete the Resident On Street Permit application form and enclose your cheque/postal order, made payable to the Borough of Poole, together with photocopies of the supporting documents as set out on the form and post the application to:

Parking Administration Section, Borough of Poole, PO Box 5038, Poole, Dorset, BH15 2WG.

Parking Administration will not accept personal callers.
