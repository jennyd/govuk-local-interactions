Register to vote

You can register to vote for future elections by going to www.gov.uk/register-to-vote. 

You will need to enter your national insurance number and your date of birth as you register. 

If you wish to find out information about the Electoral Register, or opt out of the Open Register please click on The Electoral and Open Registers.
