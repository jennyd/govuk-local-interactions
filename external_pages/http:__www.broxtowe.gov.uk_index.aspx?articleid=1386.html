Pest Control

Broxtowe Borough Council has a legal duty so far as it is reasonably practicable, to keep their district free from Rats and Mice. This may entail making land owners and occupiers take action to control Rats and Mice on their land.

Reporting a Pest Control Issue

You can now report this using our mobile app or online.

As a Broxtowe resident, the Pest Control Team are here to advise and in certain cases, treat the pest problems that you may have in your home. So, whether you are a homeowner, private tenant or Council tenant we can advise and help you on what to do.

Quite often its the cleanest homes which are most at risk. Wherever we live, pests can be a year-round problem and are not restricted to rural or urban areas. Modern, centrally heated homes can offer an especially inviting shelter during the cold winter months; open windows during the summer invite other pests to investigate possible food sources

From insects to mammals, pests come in all shapes and sizes; some look unpleasant, most however are harmless. However there are a few pests that can cause more than a nuisance and these should be dealt with because they can pose a risk to your health.

As of 1st April  2011 the following will apply:

Broxtowe Borough Council offers free treatment for domestic premises for the following:

  • Mice and Rats inside domestic properties. 

Broxtowe Borough Council offers a chargeable service for the treatment in domestic and commercial premises for the following:

  • Cockroaches
  • Bedbugs
  • Fleas
  • Pharaoh Ants
  • Rats outside
  • Wasp nests

See Pest Fees and Charges for current prices.
