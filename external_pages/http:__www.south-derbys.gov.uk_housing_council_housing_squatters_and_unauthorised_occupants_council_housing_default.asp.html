Empty council properties are usually awaiting repair, redecoration or re-letting and we are keen to ensure they stay empty so that someone on the waiting list has the opportunity to find a home.

Outline

The laws on squatting are quite complex and the council cannot simply evict a person who is squatting in one of our properties.  

However, if any tenant becomes aware of unknown or suspicious occupants in a property that they know should be vacant, we would be grateful if the tenant would advise us so that we can commence the action.  

We cannot be expected to know what is happening in all the properties that we own and we greatly appreciate the information we receive from our tenants.

What to do

If you see anyone suspicious or who appears to be squatting in a property please use the contact details below so that we can resolve the matter quickly.  Any contact will be strictly confidential.
