Insurance claims

This page provides the information required by us should you need to make an insurance claim against the Council.

The Finance Section deals with the insurance function of the Council. If you wish to make an insurance claim against the authority you will need to put the claim in writing and forward it to the address below.

Finance Section
Oadby and Wigston Borough Council
Station Road
Wigston
Leicestershire LE18 2DR

Email: insurance@oadby-wigston.gov.uk

Telephone: Leicester (0116) 257 2891

Fax: Leicester (0116) 288 7828

You will need to include a detailed description of the incident together with witness details and any photographic evidence available. All claims will be passed to Zurich Municipal Insurance who will then contact you to acknowledge your claim within 21 days.

If you are claiming for an incident relating to a defective road or pavement surface you should direct these to the Highways Department at Leicestershire County Council by calling Leicester (0116) 305 0001.
