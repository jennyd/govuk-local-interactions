Pest control

Pest Control Operatives Treating an Infestation

Our fully trained professional team responds to pest complaints (including rats, mice, fleas and cockroaches) in both domestic and commercial properties, call us for more details.

To request a pest control service:

  • Call 01524 582935
  • Email environmentalhealth@lancaster.gov.uk

Please see our downloadable advice sheets for common pest issues.

You can follow us on Twitter @lcstr_unbugged.

We aim to provide a prompt response in all cases. Requests are dealt with in order of priority, with rats, cockroaches, fleas and bedbugs generally treated more urgently.

Annual agreements for businesses

Instead of waiting until you’ve already got a problem before you call us out, why not take out an annual agreement with us for a set fee.

Contracts consist of a number of programmed visits covering rats, mice and common insects, plus any interim callouts as required by the customer. There are NO HIDDEN EXTRAS and agreements start from as little as £120 per year.

Customer satisfaction

We aim to provide an efficient and effective pest control service.

If you have a complaint, please contact us and we will advise you of the procedure to follow.  We would also like to hear from you if you think we have done a good job, and any suggestions for improving the service are most welcome.
