Estate Management

Our Housing and Support Officers carry out monthly estate inspections.  The purpose of them is to ensure that any issues developing on estates are identified and dealt with.  This could include issues such as fly tipping, graffiti or overgrown gardens. 

We measure the performance of the estate inspections and report this to tenants in the quarterly Homing In newsletter and in the Performance section of this website.

At the end of quarter 1 2013/14, the proportion of estates meeting visual standard of excellent or good was 95%.
