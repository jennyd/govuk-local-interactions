Kettering Borough Council Grant Schemes

The aim of Kettering Borough Council's Grant Scheme is to support voluntary and community groups in carrying out activities that enhance the quality of life for local residents and increase their involvement within the community.

Please read through the guidelines carefully before completing the online application.

GRANTfinder

Kettering Borough Council have access to a flexible searchable database GRANTfinder which is the UKs leading grants and policy database and includes details in excess of 8,000 funding opportunities. If you would like to find out what funding may be available please complete the online form.
