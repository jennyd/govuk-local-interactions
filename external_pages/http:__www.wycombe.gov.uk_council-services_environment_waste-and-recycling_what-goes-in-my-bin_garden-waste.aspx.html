Green bin

 Green wheeled bin or green reusable bags - free fortnightly collection

We will collect and compost the following:

Most properties can use the existing green wheeled bin

For households with limited space and restricted access for our larger collection vehicles, we will provide two green reusable bags.

We cannot collect from your garden waste container:

  • food waste
  • paper or cardboard
  • rabbit, guinea pig or chicken bedding
  • large branches or timber
  • soil, turf or rubble
  • plant pots or plastic bags
  • noxious weeds like Japanese Knotweed
  • plants containing toxins such as but not limited to Rhododendron, Yew, Ragwort and Hemlock
  • plants treated with agricultural chemicals

You cannot use plastic, paper or corn starch bags to line the whole or part of the bin. Plastic, paper and corn starch bags are not accepted at the compost facility. Ensure that the correct materials go into appropriate bin or bags. Bins or bags contaminated with the wrong material cannot be emptied and a tag will be left to explain.

Please be careful:

  • not to overload your green bin or compress too much green waste into it
  • overweight bins cannot be emptied.
  • we can only be able to empty the green bin once the weight has been reduced

You can also take garden waste to your local household waste recycling centre (external website).
