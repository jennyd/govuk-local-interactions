PIN - (Personal Identification Number):

As a member of the library you can obtain a unique PIN, which will allow you to access your account details, place a reservation; check your loans and renew them online. If you don’t know your PIN, please visit your local library, taking your library ticket with you, or phone 0345 155 1001 (8am - 8pm weekdays, 9am - 1pm Saturdays).
