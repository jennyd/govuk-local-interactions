Report a pothole, road or pavement damage

Before you report this problem:

  • Is the road or pavement private, unadopted or owned by an organisation like a supermarket?
    If so, do not use this form. Report the problem to the owner of the road instead.

  • Is it an immediate and serious threat to safety?
    Get more advice first

Otherwise report the problem now.
