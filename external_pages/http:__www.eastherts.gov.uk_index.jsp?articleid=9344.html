Council Tax - Discounts

If you think you should have got a discount - but didn't, you can write to East Herts Council explaining why.

We will tell you what evidence you will need to provide in support of a claim. East Herts Council has two months to make a decision.

If you still disagree with us or if we have not acted within the two month period, you will be able to appeal to a Valuation Tribunal.

You should continue to pay your original bill whilst your appeal is outstanding.

Find out more about:-

  • Apprentice
    A person who is learning a trade, business, undertaking a programme of training etc
  • Carer (I)
    A person who provides care or support for a disabled person for at least 24 hours per week and receives payment for that care of less than £44.00 per week.
  • Carer (2)
    A person who is resident in the property who cares for a person with a disability who is receiving one of certain allowances or disablement pensions.
  • A Monk, Nun or member of a similar Religious Community
    This is someone who depends on the community for their material needs and has no personal income or capital apart from a pension from a former job.
  • Nursing Homes etc
    People living in residential care homes, nursing homes, mental nursing homes and hostels providing a high level of care
  • Reductions for People with Disabilities
    You may be able to get your bill reduced if your home has certain features which are essential, or of major importance, to the well-being of a person with a disability, whether an adult or child, who is resident in the dwelling.
  • A Person who is Severely Mentally Impaired
    Where someone has severe impairment of intelligence and social functioning which appears to be permanent.
  • Single Occupant Discounts
    The full Council Tax bill assumes that there are two adults living in a dwelling. If there is only one adult resident the bill is reduced by 25%. This is called a Single Person Discount.
  • Student
    You will not be counted if you are a student (or an overseas student) on a full-time or qualifying course of education.
  • Youth Training Trainee
    Explanation of qualifying conditions for Youth Training Trainee.
  • Foreign Language Assistants
    Foreign language assistants registered with the British Council
