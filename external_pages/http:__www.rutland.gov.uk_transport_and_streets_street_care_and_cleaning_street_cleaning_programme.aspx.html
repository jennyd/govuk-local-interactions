Street Cleaning Programme

The Council is responsible for keeping the County clean and free of litter.

Frequencies of Street Cleaning

  • Oakham High Street and Uppingham High Street East and West (Daily).
  • Main street Cottesmore, Ketton and Whissendine (Weekly).
  • Main Street Greetham (Fortnightly).
  • All villages are cleaned on a monthly basis except Ayston, Barrow Belmesthorpe, Egleton, Hambleton, Little Casterton, Lyndon, Normanton, Pickworth, Teigh, Tickencote, Thisleton, Thorpe-by-Water, Tixover, Stoke Dry and Wardley which are cleaned every 3 Months.

We will:

  • Continuously clean town centres.
  • Clean most roads once a month.
  • Remove accumulations of rubbish from Council land.
  • Empty litter bins before they are full.
  • Help with your locally organised clean-ups.
  • Respond quickly to requests for service.

You can help us by:

  • Being tidy with your litter, chewing gum and cigarette ends.
  • Using the free civic amenity sites in Cottesmore and North Luffenham.
  • Having waste collection contracts for your business.
  • Not leaving your dog's mess for others to clean up.
  • Not 'Fly Posting' - it's untidy and illegal.
  • Not parking where you want us to clean.
  • Reporting your concerns to us - use the form below:
