Short breaks for carers of disabled children

Short breaks support carers to continue to care more effectively for their children at home.

  • 10 Sea View Road West - a guide for children and young people

    10 Sea View Road West is where disabled children and young people can have a short break away from their families. It's a great place to relax, make new friends and try new things!

  • 10 Sea View Road West - information for parents and carers

    10 Sea View Road West provides short breaks, away from their families, for up to seven disabled children and young people at a time.

  • Short break caravan

    We have an accessible caravan at Whitley Bay Caravan Park, for use by families with disabled children.

  • Short breaks for disabled children

    Find out more about how short breaks support carers to care more effectively for their children at home.

  • Children and young people - respite care

  • Shared care for children

  • Services for disabled children in Sunderland

    Sunderland Disabled Children's webpages provide information, advice, support and guidance to disabled children, young people and their families living in Sunderland.

  • Do you have Autism or care for someone who does?

    Between June and August the Local Autism Working Group and Sunderland City Council asked people with Autism and their families about their experiences of living with Autism, the services and support people use, what is good about them and what needs to improve.

  • Getting a break from caring

    Sunderland Care and Support provides short break residential services for adults aged 18 to 65 who have learning or physical disabilities.

  • Complaint in relation to Children's Services

    Complaints about Children's Social Care, including complaints about Social Workers, are classed as statutory complaints.
