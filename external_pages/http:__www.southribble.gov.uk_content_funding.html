Funding

Every year, the council allocates 'core funding' to each My Neighbourhood Forum. Details of this year's funding will appear on this page shortly.

My Neighbourhood Forums also try to bring external money into their areas, by bidding for funding on projects, or by supporting community groups with their own funding bids.  Forums also have an influencing role in how money paid to the council by developers is spent, which must be used to improve the local area when building work has taken place, like a new housing estate or a supermarket.
