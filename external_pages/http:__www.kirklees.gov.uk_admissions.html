Supplementary information forms (SIF's)

  • Supplementary information forms

    • Some Academies, free schools, voluntary aided Catholic or voluntary aided Church of England schools require parents to complete a supplementary information form (SIF) in addition to the online application for a school place. This is so they have all the information they need to assess your eligibility against their admission criteria. Failure to complete the SIF could result in your child not gaining a place at your preferred school.
