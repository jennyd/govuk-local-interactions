£5,000 Property Level Flood Resilience Grant

The property level flood resilience grant scheme of up to £5,000 can be applied for by owners of properties which were flooded during storm Desmond and storm Eva. The grant is for flood prevention measures and can be combined with grants awarded to neighbouring property owners if required. Read more about the Property Level Flood Resilience Grant Scheme.

Appleby Flood recovery drop-ins and meetings

  • Property (Household and Business) Flood Defence Advice Day on Monday 22 January between 10am and 4pm in the Public Hall, Boroughgate, Appleby-in-Westmorland CA16 6YA

  • Multi Agency Post Flood Meeting - Monday 29 February at 7pm in the Public Hall, Boroughgate, Appleby in Westmorland

  • Drop-ins on Tuesdays and Fridays at the Hub, Chapel Street, Appleby, CA16 6QR - 9.30am to 12.30pm

£500 Government funding for households flooded in December 2015 still available

Households flooded in December 2015 can still apply for a fixed payment of £500. Council Tax payers who pay by direct debit will see a £500 payment from Eden District Council on their bank statements. If you do not pay your Council Tax by direct debit, please telephone 01768 817817 or email: counciltax@eden.gov.uk to let us know so your claim can be processed. Find our more about available funding.

Clean for the Queen from 4 to 6 March 2016

We are supporting the Clean for The Queen campaign launched by Country Life magazine in partnership with Keep Britain Tidy to clear up Britain in time for The Queen’s 90th birthday in 2016. Read more about the Clean for the Queen campaign.

Free Microchipping on Wednesday 23 March

At the Evergreen Hall, Bluebell Lane, Penrith CA11 7LH between 11am and 3pm. Provided by the Dogs Trust in partnership with Eden District Council. Dogs MUST be microchipped by 6 April 2016. Read more about microchipping on the Chip My Dog website.
