Norfolk Winter

Norfolk Winter

This winter you can follow Norfolk's winter updates on Twitter and Facebook, where Norfolk County Council will be adding regular updates about gritting, details of disruptions to services, such as libraries or day centres, information about winter and festive events and activities across Norfolk.

Updates on Twitter will carry the hashtag #Norfolkwinter, and the Norfolk Community Facebook page will also host Norfolk County Council's school closures feed.

You can now also follow the latest winter updates on the new Norfolk Winter blog which will feature stories, news, interviews and advice from a range of local contributors.
