Animals and Pests

Dog Control

Our Neighbourhood Warden Service respond to, investigate and resolve complaints and enquiries relating to stray dogs and dog fouling. They also have the power to enforce relevant legislation when necessary. We do not operate a dog registration scheme.

  • Dog Fouling
  • Dangerous Dogs
  • Stray Dogs

Pest Control

We can treat rats, mice, fleas, cockroaches and bedbugs if they are inside the property. We can advise on a number of other pest species, including rats (outside), foxes, moles, wasps, ants, squirrels and biscuit beetles.

If you have insect, bed bug or flea infestations that you believe need identification, please bring them to the Civic Centre in a sealed jar. We are unable to accept them in envelopes.
