Housing Benefit & Council Tax Support

  • Submit - Supporting Documents


Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support Average Processing Time: 6 Weeks

Applying for Benefits

Information on who can claim Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support in Torbay and how to apply.

Council Tax Support

Information about Council Tax Support which has now replaced Council Tax Benefit.
 

Changes to Housing Benefit

Find out about important changes to Housing Benefits that have taken place and that are coming into affect from July 2013.

Change of Circumstances

It is very important that you report any changes in your circumstances immediately as you may receive too little or too much benefit.
 

Local Housing Allowance (LHA)

The LHA scheme is a different way of calculating housing benefit entitlement for tenants renting from a private landlord.

Landlords Information

Housing Benefit is paid by the Council to help people on low incomes with their rent. Find out how this affects you as a landlord.
 

Benefit Appeals

What to do if you think the decision about your Housing or Council Tax Benefit claim is wrong.

Housing Benefit Overpayments

If your circumstances change and you are entitled to less benefit, if you do not tell us straight away, you will be overpaid benefit.
 

Universal Credit

Universal Credit is a single monthly payment for people in or out of work, which merges together some of the benefits and tax credits that you might be getting now.
