Community support

Practical information, tips and policies for community groups to help start, fund and run a local community group.

  • Sign up to receive the communities newsletter, monthly funding newsletter or information about jobs, training, events and to be involved in consultations
  • Access voluntary and community grants
  • Check the diversity calendar to help you decide when to hold an event
  • The United Nations calendar of world awareness dates
  • Hire the community minibus
  • Existing community groups you can join
  • Tenants' associations - Work Clubs community-based support for unemployed people

Keeping in touch with Southampton communities:

  • Southampton communities Facebook page
  • Southampton communities on twitter
  • RSS feed for Southampton's communities
  • Community jobs information
  • Southampton Community jobs on twitter
  • Southampton Communities email: communities@southampton.gov.uk

Templates, policies and guides for community groups:

  • Community group templates
  • Community group guides - on starting, running or funding your community group
  • Community group policies - Legal and statutory policies to help you run your local community group

Other useful organisations that provide community support

  • Charity Commission – starting and running charities.
  • Community Matters – resources and support for groups, registration is required.
  • Southampton Voluntary Services – local organisation providing support, training and information on volunteering to people and other organisations.
  • National Council for Voluntary Organisations - publications on governance for the voluntary and community sector (charges apply for some).
  • Business in the Community (South East) - business-led charity focused on promoting responsible business practice.
  • Southampton Connect - working together to improve Southampton.
  • Training for Work in Communities - develop community work skills and have learning accredited and to gain qualifications, some courses are free.
