SUBMISSION VIA PAPER ONLY:

Please note that at the moment we are only accepting paper submissions however please revisit this page regularly as we are working towards electronic submissions in the near future.

If you have any questions regarding the forms, you can contact the Duty Building Standards Surveyor.   Duty service operates 9:30am - 1pm on weekdays.

For more detailed discussions, users of the duty services can also make an appointment to meet with a surveyor.
