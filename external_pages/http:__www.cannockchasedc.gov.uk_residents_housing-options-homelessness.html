Housing Options / Homelessness

The Housing Options Service aims to prevent people becoming homeless by providing advice and assistance to help people find a solution to their housing problem.
The Housing Register is available to anyone 18 years of age or over who wish to apply for a property. However, due to a shortage of social housing, availability is usually limited to those who are in the most need.

What does the Housing Options service do?

Help you keep your existing home
Help you explore your housing options
Give advice on the council's statutory duties

We can help with

Negotiating with landlords
Advice about paying your mortgage
Fast track claims for Housing Benefit and LHA
Assistance if affected by domestic violence
Neighbour nuisance
Unfit properties
Referral to tenancy support services
Referral to supported housing schemes
Help to find accommodation in the private sector
