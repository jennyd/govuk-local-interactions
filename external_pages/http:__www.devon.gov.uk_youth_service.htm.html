What we do

We offer more than you’d think!

  • Open access youth work from 8 youth hubs across Devon
  • 1:1 bespoke support for young people
  • Alternative education packages
  • Support our communities through training, The 9th Hub and our community workers
  • Duke of Edinburgh (DofE) support to thousands of young people
  • Specific group work in the following areas:
    • LGBT
    • Young carers
    • Children in care
    • Care leavers
    • Young music leaders
    • Mental health

Not all services are available in all areas. Please contact your local Hub for information.
