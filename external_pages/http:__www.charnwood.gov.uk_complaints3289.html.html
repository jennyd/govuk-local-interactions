Have your say about council services

There are a number of ways you can have your say about the Council's services and these are listed below:

Online

Send a comment, compliment or complaint using our on-line Feedback Form

In person

By visiting the Customer Service Centre, Charnwood Borough Council, Southfields, Loughborough, Leicestershire, LE11 2TR

In writing

  • To the relevant department at Charnwood Borough Council, Southfields, Loughborough, Leicestershire, LE11 2TR - (for a list of services visit our website at www.charnwood.gov.uk/atoz)

By phone

  • (01509) 263151 (switchboard) and ask for the relevant department or by direct dial if you know the number you want

By email

  • To the relevant department or information@charnwood.gov.uk or in the case of complaints to complaints@charnwood.gov.uk

In any language or Braille

  • Phone (01509) 634560

What happens next?

If you have sent us your comments we will try to use what you say to help us improve what we do.

If you have sent us a compliment we will pass it on to the people concerned.

If you have sent us a complaint we will follow our complaints procedure. Find out more about how we deal with complaints.
