Holiday Activities

 

CORBY EAST MIDLANDS INTERNATIONAL POOL:

  • For changes to the pool programme during the holiday and details of all the activities taking place - click here
  • All junior loyalty card holders can swim for £1.00 per session during the holidays.

Teen Fitness - A great way for young people aged 12 - 15 years to use the Gym at the Pool and Lodge Park Sports Centre.  Booking is advisable.   The equipment available includes Treadmills, Cross Trainers, Bikes, Rowers and much more.  £3.20 per session or sign up for a Junior Leisure Membership click here for further details. 

Surf n Turf - Wednesdy 17th February 11am - 1pm £4.50 per session:  This is an instructor led class that will engage your child in fun games and activities that aim to improve their co-ordination, teamwork and social skills.  The session consists of 45 minutes of dry side activities in the studio and 45 minutes of pool based activities in the swimming pool.

Family First Aid - Thursday 18th February 1 - 3pm £3.50 per person: For adults and children (6 yrs+), covers CPR, bleeds, choking and common illnesses.  Must pre-book and pay to secure your place onto this course.

Disability and Additional Needs Session - for children and their families on Saturday 20th February 5 - 6pm (new session trial period) £1 per person (1 carer free, siblings welcome).  Fun Pool, Floats, Aquatube*, 20m Pool* (* subject to availability).

Various courses are available during Half Term and throughout the year - click here for more information.

 
 

SPORT & PLAY DEVELOPMENT

Cheerleading Club - Learn skills/cheers and chants, improve flexibility, complete stunts, perform at various events throughout the year, and lots more! For more information click here

Disability Sports Club - First session FREE.  Sports include: Archery, Boccia, New Age Kurling, Tennis, Parachute Games, Cricket and much more - click here for more information.

To book any of the above (where booking is necessary) telephone 01536 464047 or 01536 464611; Email sports.development@corby.gov.uk
 

WEST GLEBE PAVILION

Pay and Play football sessions on the astro-turf: £1.50 for U18s' and £2 for over 18's (free to those with valid junior membership cards)
Monday 15th, Tuesday 16th, Wednesday 17th and Friday 19th,  1-3pm.
 

EAST CARLTON COUNTRY PARK, LE16 8YF

On Sunday 14th February there will be a Valentines Walk and the Cafe will be set up with candles and red table cloths.  Also, there will be a coffee and cake deal.  There will be paper love hearts for sale so that loved ones can put their messages on and pin them to our Valentine Board.  All proceeds from the Paper Love Hearts will go to the British Heart Foundation.  (#CorbyCanLove).  Come down for a walk in our beautiful park and look around the craft workshops.  Cafe is open from 9.30am - 3.45pm everyday.
 

LODGE PARK SPORTS CENTRE

  • Get your friends together for a game of football on our 3G Astro Turf Pitch - £10 per pitch per hour, booking times available 9.30am - 4.30pm, Monday - Friday.
  • Tots & Tiddler's Pre School Gymnastics sessions on Thursday 18th February, 1 hour session.  Crawling - 3 years 10am - 11am, 3 - 5 years 11.15am - 12.15pm.  £4.00 per session.  Please note: older siblings can attend the session but will have to either observe on the bench or pay £4.00 to take part.
  • Racket Sports - Table Tennis, Short Tennis or Badminton.  Come and play for just £1 per person between 9am - 4pm Monday - Friday  (equipment available to hire from reception, charges apply).  
  • Teen Fitness - A great way for young people aged 12 - 15 years to use the Gym at the Pool and Lodge Park Sports Centre.  Booking is advisable.  The equipment available includes Treadmills, Cross Trainers, Bikes, Rowers and much more.  £3.20 per session or sign up for a Junior Leisure Membership click here for further details.

Our offers are too good to miss so make the most of your time off and keep active!
