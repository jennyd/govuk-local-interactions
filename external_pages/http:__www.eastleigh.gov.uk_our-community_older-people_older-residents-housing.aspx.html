Housing for Our Older Residents  

Sheltered Accommodation

If you wish to apply for sheltered accommodation in the Borough you only need to complete one application form for Hampshire Homechoice.  You are able to apply to join the Register through this website or, alternatively, contact us and we will arrange to send you a form in the post.
 
Not all the Housing Associations working in the Borough providing sheltered accommodation are part of the combined register and therefore you will need to contact them direct.

Staying at Home

If you feel that you can no longer cope on your own and need help with day to day tasks in your home there are services to help you. Care options available if you wish to Stay at Home.

Residential, Nursing and Extra Care

If you are finding it difficult to manage at home, there are now more choices available to help make your life easier.

Extra Care Housing offers the opportunity to have your own front door, but with care and support on hand 24 hours a day, every day. You could also have help to stay in your current home, or move into a residential or nursing care home if this is right for you.

Help for Owner Occupiers

Disabled Facilities Grants help people who are, or could be, registered as disabled and who would otherwise be unable to live independently.

Private Home Improvement Funding The Home Trust Loan is a unique, low cost, home improvement service and lending scheme offered in partnership between Parity Trust and Eastleigh Borough Council.
