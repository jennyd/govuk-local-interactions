Paying your parking ticket 

 

Pay your parking ticket

 
Online payments can only be made 48 hours after the parking ticket (Penalty Charge Notice) has been issued.

Alternative ways to pay your penalty charge notice can be found on the back of the parking ticket.

Challenging a parking ticket

If you think that you shouldn't have been issued with a parking ticket and you have received it within 14 days, you can appeal.

We receive a large volume of mail on a daily basis, however, once your challenge has been submitted and entered into our system your notice will be placed on hold at the current charge until a response can be sent. We will endeavour to respond as soon as possible.

 

Challenge a parking ticket
 
 

Alternatively, you can write to Blackpool Council at the address provided on the parking ticket stating why you believe it was incorrectly issued and we will write back to you with our decision.

If you wish to appeal further after hearing our decision, you have the right to appeal to the Traffic Penalty Tribunal which is an independent organisation.
