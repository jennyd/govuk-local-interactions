In addition to fact and fiction books for all ages and access to a range of audio books and DVDs your local Flintshire library provides :

  • Free Computer and internet access (Pdf 16KB new window)
  • Community information
  • Help with reference and local history enquiries
  • Large print books
  • Ticket booking service for some local theatres
  • Many of the larger libraries hold cultural events, such as visits from authors.

The following activities take place regularly at some libraries :

Children's summer holiday story and craft activities : larger libraries
Reading Group meetings : Flint, Hawarden, Holywell, Mold

Keeping track of your loans:

Please be aware that Flintshire Library and Information Service does not send out reminders about overdue items to adults or children. To avoid paying fines you can renew your items online, by telephone or mail. We do not charge fines on items that are borrowed on a child's card.
