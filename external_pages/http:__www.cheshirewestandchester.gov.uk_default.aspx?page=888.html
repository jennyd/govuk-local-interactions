Emergency Planning

Cheshire West and Chester Council plays a central role in the management of major emergencies that happen within the borough.

Why we plan for emergencies - the Civil Contingencies Act

Cheshire Resilience Forum

Emergency planning is the responsibility of a range of organisations in Cheshire West and Chester and the wider area.

These have teamed up to create a Local Resilience Forum which help co-ordination and co-operation between responders at the local level. More information is available on the Cheshire Local Resilience Forum website.

Flooding

Find out if your house is in a flood zone on the Environment Agency's Flood Map.

Community risk register

The community risk register for Cheshire, Halton and Warrington highlights potential hazards in the county and how local services would respond in the event of an emergency.

It is available to download from the Cheshire Fire Service website
