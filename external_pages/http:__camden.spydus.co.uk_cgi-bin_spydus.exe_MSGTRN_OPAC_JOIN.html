Joining Camden library service

You can join at any Camden library by showing proof of your name and current address. You should also bring proof of concessionary entitlement if you are eligible. There is no minimum age for library membership, but under 18's will require a signature from their parent or guardian.

If you already have a Camden library card you can logon with your library card number and library PIN.

Joining Camden library service online

You can join Camden libraries online by clicking on the button below after you have read and accepted the conditions. You will be issued with a temporary library membership number.

Conditions of Membership

  • To borrow items you must have your library membership card.
  • Items can be borrowed from and returned to any Camden library.
  • Overdue fines are charged for the late return of items.
  • Some services and loan types will be charged for.
  • All costs incurred by Camden Libraries due to items lost or damaged whilst in your care must be reimbursed.
  • Report lost cards to the library immediately. There will be a charge for replacement membership cards.
  • If you change your address, name, phone number or email address, please notify us immediately.
  • You understand that the personal information you have supplied will be held and processed according to the current data protection legislation.
  • You agree to abide by Camden library service rules and bye-laws.

Acceptance

Click the button below to accept these conditions and proceed to the registration form.
