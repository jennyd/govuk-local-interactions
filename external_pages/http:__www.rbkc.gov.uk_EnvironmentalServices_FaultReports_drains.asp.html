Fault Report: Drains

The Council only maintains rainwater gullies or storm drains which are usually located on the carriageway just by the kerb. If the drain you want to report is a mains drain, please contact Thames Water on 0845 920 0800.

To report a blocked drain in the street please fill in this form.

Required fields are marked with an asterisk *.

Where is it? Please type the name of the street or road.
