It's quick and safe to pay your PCN online.

Note: If you are paying more than one fine online, you will have to complete the form for each PCN e.g. if you are paying three fines, you must complete the form three times entering the relevant PCN number.

Other options

By Phone

Automated payment line (24 hour service). 

Note: If you are paying multiple parking or bus lane fines, please contact us. 

By cheque

Payable to Liverpool City Council. Post to: Liverpool City Council, PO Box 981, Municipal Building Dale Street, L69 1JB. Please write the PCN number on the back of the cheque.

Pay in person

The cash office in Municipal Building’s One Stop Shop.

PCN and bus lane charges

The higher parking PCN charge is set at £70. The lower charge is set at £50. However, if you pay within 14 days of issue, this will be reduced to half the amount, which is £35 and £25. Check the contravention codes to see if your offence falls into the higher or lower charge rate.

For a bus lane PCN you will be asked to pay £60 within 28 days of issue. However, if you pay within 14 days this amount is reduced by half to £30. If you choose to pay after the 14 day discount period, you will still be liable to pay the full £60.
