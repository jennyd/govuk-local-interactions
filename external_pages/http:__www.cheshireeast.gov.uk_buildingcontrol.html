Building Control

Cheshire East Council's Building Control service has been re branded as Civicance Ltd, a wholly owned company by Cheshire East Council.

For more information about Civicance Ltd or for any enquiries relating to Building Control in Cheshire East, including application forms and site inspections, please visit www.civicance.co.uk

Civicance delivers a premium building control service with all the benefits of an established local authority service together with the flexibility of the private sector. Working with you proactively to ensure that your projects are a success, offering same day site inspections, competitive quotes and a fast turnaround on decisions.

Revised charges scheme

With effect from 1 February 2016 the Council will adopt a revised Local Authority Charge Scheme in accordance with The Building (Local Authority Charges) Regulations 2010. The scheme can be viewed over the next 7 days at the Municipal Buildings, Earle Street, Crewe, CW1 2BJ.

The services offered by Civicance encompass development through a full life cycle from planning support, building control and address management through to local land charges.
