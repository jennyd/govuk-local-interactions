Community centres

There are lots of community centres across Derby for you to use, including over 20 council owned centres which are run by individuals and community groups.

Each centre has a programme of activities for you to take part in, with some offering volunteering opportunities to help run the centres. Please contact each centre to find out their activity programme and any opportunities available.

Activities

Activities available in the community centres include:

  • pre-school groups
  • fitness and wellbeing classes
  • dance classes
  • martial arts and self-defence classes
  • weight-loss groups
  • over 60s bingo
  • dog training classes
  • craft and hobby groups
  • local interest groups
  • faith groups.

Hiring for an event or club

Our community centres are available to hire for a one-off party or event, or a regular booking for your club or group. Please contact the individual community centres for more information.
