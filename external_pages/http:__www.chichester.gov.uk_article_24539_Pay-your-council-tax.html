Pay your council tax

Council Tax is normally due on the 1st or 15th of each month from April to January. However, you can ask to pay twice a year instead (on 1 April and 1 September) or just once a year (on 1 May). You can pay your account in a number of ways:

  • Online
  • Direct debit
  • Debit card by telephone
  • By post
  • Payment card
  • Bank payment
  • Extended instalments to March 2016

Online

Pay council tax online

Direct debit

The most convenient way of paying your Council Tax is by Direct Debit. The advantages include:

  • Payments can be made monthly, or once or twice a year.
  • Time is saved (there is no need to write and post cheques).
  • Queues are avoided.
  • You don't need to worry about forgetting to pay.
  • You don't have to carry cash.
  • You don't need to buy stamps.
  • You don't have to notify your bank/building society if the amount or date changes.
  • It's easy to cancel.
  • You have a money back guarantee and you are protected by the the Direct Debit Guarantee, which monitors the efficiency and security of the scheme.

Please fill in the form and send it to the district council offices.

  • Direct debit mandate for Council Tax [pdf 91kb]

Debit card by telephone

Payment can be made using your debit card. Please call our fully secured automated telephone payment line on 01243 534880 to make a payment. Please ensure you have your Council Tax reference number as shown on your latest bill ready to quote. Please note you cannot pay your Council Tax by credit card.

By post

Cheques and Postal Orders should be made payable to 'Chichester District Council' and crossed. Please ensure that your Council Tax account number is written on the back of the cheque. If you would like a receipt for your cheque, please provide a stamped addressed envelope with your payment. Post dated cheques cannot be accepted.

Please send your payment to:

District Treasurer
East Pallant House
1 East Pallant
Chichester
West Sussex
PO19 1TY

Payment card

Payment can be made using an allpay payment card. This can be used at the following locations;

  • Post offices - cash, debit card & cheque (made payable to Post Office Ltd)
  • Paypoint outlets - cash only

For details of your nearest allpay outlet please visit the allpay website (see offsite links).

If you would like a payment card ordered please contact the Council Tax office on 01243 534501.

Bank payment

Please use the following Chichester District Council bank details:

Sort code 40-17-16,

Bank account number 71466135.

Please ensure you quote the Council Tax account number as shown on your latest bill.

Extended instalments to March 2016

You can request that your Council Tax instalments are extended to March 2016. If you make this request then all instalments payable up to the date of your request must be paid in accordance with your previous bill. Please complete the request form and return to the district council offices. Alternatively the form can be emailed to us.
