Premises licences

To provide late-night refreshments and regulated entertainment, and sell alcohol, you need a licence from the local authority if you are in England and Wales.

If you are applying for a new premises licence or club premises certificate you must ensure that your business is registered with us as a food business.

Apply for licence

Online

Through our partnership with EUGO you can make and pay for your application online. When you make your application this way you only have to complete one set of forms and we will pass these on to all other responsible authorities for you.
