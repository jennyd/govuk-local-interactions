Family Information Service

The Family Information Service - working to support local families with information and choices

The Family Information Service (FIS) Information Team will answer your enquiries about a range of family related issues, childcare, parenting support, benefits, schools and much, much more. 

Contact FIS 

  • By telephone on 01225 395343 available Monday to Thursday 8.45am to 4.45pm and Friday 8.30am to 4.30pm.  There is an answerphone outside of these hours. 
  • Email: fis@bathnes.gov.uk 
  • Text: 07980 998 906 
  • Find FIS on Facebook: www.facebook.com/bathnesfis 
  • If you know what you are looking for the 1 Big Database childcare search will help you identify a range of childcare choices
  • For families with a child or young person with special educational needs and disabilities or an additional need search our Rainbow Resource for local and national organisations that provide information for families.
  • Have a look at Information for parents - lots of useful information for parents with babies, young children and much more

Not all of the information is kept on this website - so contact us using the details above, our online form, or visit us at one of our outreach sites, so we can provide you with the most up to date and relevant information.

We have information on local parent and toddler groups, after school activities and details of leisure activities and support groups for you and your family. We can offer support around special educational needs and disabilities or additional needs.  FIS has access to a range of leaflets and booklets about parenting support, family issues, positive activities and safe lifestyle choices for teenagers and lists of registered childcare, including childminders, nurseries, pre-schools and out of school clubs.

The Outreach Team promotes the work of the Family Information Service across the Bath and North East Somerset area and you will see them regularly at venues such as Children's Centres, One Stop Shops and Schools. They visit organisations that work with families, go along to parenting and play events, post natal groups, toddler groups and family fun days.  If you are having difficulty finding childcare, or a service to help you with your family, the Outreach Team can meet with you to discuss your options and give you impartial information.

We also provide an Information & Advice Service, working closely with families who do not have a regular wage or income, offering one to one appointments and drop in sessions at local Children's Centres.  At an appointment a family will be able to check that they are claiming all the benefits they are entitled to and be supported to access debt and housing advice, free school meals, specific benefits information and appropriate legal services.

Our Information & Advice Officer for SEND is happy to talk with parents and carers by telephone, meet for one-to-one discussions and can signpost to local and national helplines, support groups, leisure activities, holiday playschemes and community organisations and services.

If you are interested in setting up a childcare business such as an Out-of-School Club, Nursery or perhaps becoming a childminder, please visit the page Setting up a childcare business.
