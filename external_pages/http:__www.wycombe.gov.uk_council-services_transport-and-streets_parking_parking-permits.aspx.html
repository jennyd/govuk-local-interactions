Parking permits

Parking permits are available for a number of our car parks. See our car parks listing pages for details.

Permits are available for two, four, six or twelve months. They always start on the first day of the month. Part months are not available.

  • Bucks CC issues on-street parking permits (external website).

Parking permit benefits

  • Convenience and flexibility; if your preferred car park is full, you can try a nearby alternative.
  • Reduced parking rates may apply when using a permit.
  • No change required - permits are paid for in advance.

Please note, the issue of a permit does not guarantee the availability of parking spaces.

Apply for a parking permit

We issue a limited number of parking permits for each car park. Sending us an application form does not guarantee the availability of a permit.

  • Phone or email us to check availability.
  • Download and complete the parking permit application form below. Complete and post this to us with your payment.
  • We aim to process applications within three working days. Your permit will then be posted to you.

Payment

You can pay by:

  • Cheque (payable to "Wycombe District Council")
  • Credit/debit card

Please note that payments made by credit card will be subject to an additional card transaction charge.

Check the charge amount before you confirm your payment. You can cancel the transaction at the confirmation stage.

There is no transaction charge for debit card payments.

Terms and conditions

When applying for and using a parking permit, please read the terms and conditions provided with the permit application form or available on request. If you don't follow the terms and conditions you may be issued with a Standard Charge Ticket.

Lost or stolen parking permits

We charge an administration fee to replace lost or stolen parking permits.
