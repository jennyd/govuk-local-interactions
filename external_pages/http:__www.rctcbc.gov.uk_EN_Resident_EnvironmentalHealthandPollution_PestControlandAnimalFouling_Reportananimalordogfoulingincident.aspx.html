Report a dog or animal fouling incident

You can report a dog or animal fouling incident to us online
Report dog or animal fouling

Please give us as much information as possible including the following:

  • where is the dog fouling?
  • do you know the person who allowed the dog fouling?
  • are you prepared to help us to prosecute people who allow dog fouling by agreeing to make a statement about the incident?
