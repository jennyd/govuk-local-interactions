Apply for a waste or recycling container

Remember - it costs less if you contact us online

Please read the what goes where guide before making your request.

Large families may qualify for an extra black bin at a cost of £25. Unfortunately, we do not offer extra green wheelie bins.

Depending on your address and circumstances, we may not be able to give you all the containers. We will contact you if we can't give you what you've asked for and offer alternatives where possible.

We also provide garden compost bins for £15 each, which can be collected from all of our sites. Please allow five working days before you come to collect it.  

The personal information you provide will be processed in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
