Street name signs

The Council is responsible for ensuring that street name plates and road signs are provided, fitted in suitable positions and maintained.

Clear and up-to-date street and road signage is important for navigational purposes and, also, so your street can be located quickly by the emergency services.

The Council is also responsible for repair and maintenance of street name plates and road signs when this becomes necessary either due to accidental damage, vandalism or normal wear and tear.

To report damaged, missing or illegible street name plate or road sign please use the contact details below

Email: customerservices@rhondda-cynon-taff.gov.uk
Tel: 01443 494700
Fax: 01443 494888
