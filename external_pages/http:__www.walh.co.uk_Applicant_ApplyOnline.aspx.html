Apply for housing online

Applying for housing or allocations

A housing application form should be completed by new applicants who wish to apply for Council or housing association properties in the Wigan Borough.  There are several online forms available on the right hand side of this page. You must save online forms to your computer/device before filling them in or they will not work properly. When you have completed an online form please return it to the email address shown on this page.

A housing register change of address form should be completed by applicants who are already on the housing register but their housing circumstances have changed. e.g. change of address.  

For Council or Housing Association tenants who wish to advertise their homes for exchange with another tenant the mutual exchange application form should be completed. 

Unless you are a Wigan Council tenant, two references should be supplied before your application can be accepted. If you are, or have been a tenant in the past, at least one reference should be from your former landlord. The reference form document should be completed.

We aim to process applications within 10 working days of receipt. Once we have processed your application, you will receive a letter informing you of your application status.
