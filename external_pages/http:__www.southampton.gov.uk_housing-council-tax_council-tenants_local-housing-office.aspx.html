Local housing office

Your local housing office can give you a range of help, advice and information on your council home and the services available to you. Such as:

  • neighbour nuisance
  • tenancy queries
  • abandoned vehicles
  • empty property management
  • apply for a council owned garage

Rent and arrears

Please contact the income team on 023 8058 5565 or email income.services@southampton.gov.uk for all queries relating to rent and arrears. Below you will find the address, contact details and opening times for each office.

Office opening hours

Offices open at the following times. If you would like to speak to a member of the local housing services team please phone for an appointment first. Thornhill local housing office can no longer take payments and Central local housing office is open by pre-booked appointment only.

Wednesday 10am to 4pm CLOSED 2pm to 4:30pm
Thursday 8:30am to 4pm 8:30am to 4pm CLOSED
Friday 8:30am to 4pm 8:30am to 4pm 9:30am to 12pm


All local housing office phone lines are open from 8:30am to 5pm Monday, Tuesday and Thursday, 10am to 5pm Wednesday and 8:30am to 4:30pm on Friday.
