 Secondary Admissions 

  

Primary to secondary transfer  for September 2016

If you are a Hackney resident and your child is in year 6, born between 1st September 2004 and 31st August 2005 you should have applied for a secondary school place by 31st October 2015. You can still apply using the on-line application form on eAdmissions site, your application will be considered as an 'Late' application. If you have good reason for your late submission and want your application to be considered as 'on-time', you will need to provide an explanation.

As part of this process, Hackney Academies and  secondary schools use banding. Year 6 children attending primary schools outside Hackney will need to take the cognitive abilities tests (CATs).  These tests will take place on Saturdays 28 November, 5 or 12 December 2015 (am or pm session) and Monday 14 December (am session only) at various secondary schools in Hackney used as testing venues. These are the only dates available so it is important that your child attends on the date/venue allocated to you. Parents will notified of date and location of the test via letter in mid November 2015. If your child is not tested this will affect your application.

Hackney’s Secondary Schools 2016 Admissions Guide for Parents’ contains all the admissions arrangements for secondary schools located in Hackney as well as how the process works.

  • Download the Hackney Secondary School Admissions Guide 2016 

Parents who have not yet submitted their proof of address, please bring your documents (2015/16 Council Tax Bill and recent Utility Bill ) to the Hackney Learning Trust, 2nd floor reception desk by 6 November 2015. Parents will be notified of the outcome of their application on 1 March 2016.

Please contact the Admissions Team if you require further information by emailing Y6sectransfer@learningtrust.co.uk or call 020 8820 7247 or 020 88207489.

Children with Special educational needs & disabilities

There is a separate transfer process for Year 6 pupils who have a statement of special educational needs or an educational, health and care plan. Do not complete the on-line form, a separate application form will be sent to you to list the schools you prefer for your child. If you have not received this form, please call Barbara Carpenter on 020 8820 7326 who will then direct you to the correct person to speak with or go to Special Educational Needs section on this website.

If your child attends a hackney primary school, your child can take the cognitive abilities test, so that they can be placed in the appropriate band for the secondary school allocated. Further details about tests and banding is in the above guide.
