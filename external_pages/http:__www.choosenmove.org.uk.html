                              Welcome to choose'n'move 

 

 

choose'n'move is the way to find an available social rented property, you are in control.

When available council and some housing association properties become empty, we will advertise them online  and at our council offices. If you are a member of choose'n'move you will be able to tell us which properties you would like to be considered for. Properties that are available for rent will be advertised each week.

Use the menu options on the left hand side of the screen to find out more about choose'n'move.
