How much does it cost to subscribe to the Garden Waste Collection Service?

Debit/Credit Card

The cost to subscribe to this service is £50.00 which includes a one-off £5.00 delivery fee. For this you will receive a fortnightly collection of your garden waste and the hire of a 240 litre brown bin for the subscription period.

Direct Debit

The cost to subscribe to this service is £50.00 which includes a one-off £5.00 delivery fee. For this you will receive a fortnightly collection of your garden waste and the hire of a 240 litre brown bin for the subscription period.

How do I subscribe to the brown bin scheme?

You can join the Garden Waste Subscription Scheme by making a payment using any of the following methods:

Online - To subscribe to this service online, please use the following link: New Brown Bin Subscription. 

Telephone - To subscribe to the Garden Waste Subscription Service, please call the waste services helpline on 01449 778678.

Please note this number is extremely busy, therefore this option should only be used if all other methods are unsuitable.

*Payments made using a credit card will be subject to a 1.5% surcharge.

How do I renew my brown bin subscription?

To renew your brown bin, you will require your customer and invoice number which can be found at the top of your renewal invoice. You can make a payment by any of the following methods:

Online - Paying online is quick and easy and is the most efficient way to renew your brown bin subscription. To do this, please use the following link: Brown Bin Renewal

Automated Telephone System - To make a payment using method, please call 0845 372 4112 (24 hours a day). This call will cost you 3pence per minute plus your phones companies access charge.

Please have your invoice and card to hand before calling:

Press 2 - To make a payment to Mid Suffolk District Council, and Press 6 - Sundry Debtors.

Telephone - If you wish to make a renewal payment by speaking to a member of the Waste Services team, please call 01449 778678.

Please note this number is extremely busy, therefore this option should only be used if all other methods are unsuitable.

*Payments made using a credit card will be subject to a 1.5% surcharge.

How do I find out my collection day?

To find out on what day your brown bin is collected, please check our brown bin calendars.  If you are unsure of the collection day for your area, please check the collection schedule.

Are there any discounts available for the Garden Waste Collection Service?

There are no discounts when subscribing to this service.

Can I subscribe for more than one brown bin?

Residents can have as many brown bins as they like, but the same annual subscription fee applies to each bin. 

Do I own the brown bin?

No, the brown bin remains the property of the Council at all times. You are subscribing to the service not buying the bin. See our Terms and Conditions for further information.

What happens if I move house?

If you move to another property within the Babergh or Mid Suffolk districts you can take your bin with you, but you must inform us of your new address so we can transfer the service.

If you do not let us know, the crews will not be authorised to empty the brown bin from your new address.
