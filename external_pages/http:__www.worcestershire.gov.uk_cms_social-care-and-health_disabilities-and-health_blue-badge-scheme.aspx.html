Blue Badge Parking Scheme

The Blue Badge Parking Scheme (parking badges for disabled people) provides parking concessions both nationally and in Europe, for people with a permanent and substantial disability, whether the person is the driver or the passenger. The scheme does not apply to off-street car parking.

Apply online for a Blue Badge

or via mail: Blue Badge Application Form

Note that the service level for posted forms is 6 weeks in contrast to 3 weeks if it is completed online.

Take our Customer Satisfaction Survey

Cost

On receipt of your application, an assessment of eligibility will be completed. Should your application be successful you will be required to pay a fee of £10 before your badge can be ordered. We will contact you following the assessment to inform you of the outcome and request payment of the fee by credit or debit card.

No payment will be taken until the badge has been approved. 

When will I receive my pass?

The delivery times below are following receipt of your application and all supporting evidence.

Once you have completed your application and all supporting evidence has been received you can expect to receive your Blue Badge within 15 days if you have applied online.
