Colleges and universities

If you decide you want to go on to Higher Education, you can search the University and Colleges Admissions Service (UCAS) for courses and apply online.

Colleges and universities in Medway:

  • MidKent College
  • The University of Greenwich
  • University of Kent 
  • Canterbury Christ Church University
  • University for the Creative Arts (UCA)

Your Choice in Medway gives students aged 14 – 25 advice on making choices about learning, financial support and applying to colleges and universities online.

If you go to university, you can also contact Prospects after you have left for advice on your next move.
