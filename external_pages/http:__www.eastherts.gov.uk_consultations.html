Consultations

View details of current consultations

Draft Environmental Crime Enforcement Policy

The Draft Policy is now open to public consultation until 24 January 2016 and we'd like to have your comments.  View  Environmental Crime and Enforcement  for further information. 

Public Spaces Protection Orders - Consultation Autumn 2015

We are inviting local people to have their say on our plans to introduce a Public Spaces Protection Order (PSPO) in April 2016 to enhance dog control and anti-social behaviour powers in East Herts. The formal public consultation period will run  midnight on Sunday 24 January 2016.

For further details view the Public Spaces Protection Orders page.

Draft Housing and Health Strategy 2016-2021

The  Draft Housing and Health Strategy 2016 - 2021 [488kb] is available for public consultation until the 12th February 2016.

If you have any comments you would like to make about the Strategy please e-mail them to Louise.Harris@eastherts.gov.uk

Draft Fuel Poverty Strategy 2016

Your comments on the Council's  are welcome and should be emailed to david.thorogood@eastherts.gov.uk  or, alternatively, please write to the Environmental Strategy and Development Manager, East Herts Council, Wallfields, Pegs Lane, Hertford SG13 8EQ. If possible comments should be submitted before 1st February 2016.

Draft Taxi Licensing Convictions Policy

East Herts is reviewing all of its taxi licensing policies.

Following comments from the licensed Hackney Carriage and Private Hire trade we have started with a comprehensive review of the convictions policy. 

For further details view the Draft Taxi Licensing Convictions Policy.

Planning Applications

Planning applications are subject to ongoing consultations.

You can search for current applications using the East Herts Online Planning Tool

You may also wish to have your say on Planning Applications.  View How do I object to, or comment on a planning application? for details.
