Weighbridges

For the location of weighbridges in Angus contact ACCESSLine on 03452 777 778.

Weighbridges are mainly used by heavy goods vehicle operators, but caravaners can use them for checking that their caravan is not overloaded. A small charge is made by the weighbridge operator for each weighing.

Our trading standards service test weighbridges and issue a certificate before public weighings can be carried out. You can apply online for a public weighbridge operator's certificate.
