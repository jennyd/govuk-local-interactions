Apply for a transfer

If you are an existing Waltham Forest Housing tenant and wish to apply for a transfer, you can now complete the following online application form.

If you previously saved an incomplete transfer application, you can also log in to complete it.

Start a new transfer application or complete an unfinished application


Please note that demand for a transfer is very high with over 2000 tenants currently registered. The rules about who gets priority for a transfer are set out in the Council’s Allocations Policy.

We do not have a ‘waiting list’, but have a Housing Register for anyone wanting to apply for a transfer. The Housing Register ranks applicants in order based on their level of housing need which is assessed against the criteria set out in the Housing Allocation Policy:

The majority of homes are let to applicants that have ‘additional preference’

Less than one in ten homes are let to applicants in the 'reasonable preference' band

If you have ‘no preference’, you will not be housed or bid for advertised vacancies


Unless your housing need meets the criteria for 'additional preference' we advise that you to consider other housing options. Before you apply for a transfer, please take some time to read about the other options available to you: 

  • Affordable home ownership (external website) 
  • Supported and sheltered housing (external website)

 

If you are not currently a Council tenant and would like to apply to go on the housing register, please complete the housing application on Waltham Forest Council's website.

Homefinder

Homefinder UK is a national housing mobility scheme that offers housing opportunities to social tenants and households who have been placed in temporary accommodation by councils.

By working in partnership with social landlords Homefinder helps households who want or need to move to find a home that better matches their household size and personal and financial circumstances.
