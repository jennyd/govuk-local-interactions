Your wheeled bins

Green wheeled bin

Changes to the garden waste collection service 

The current garden waste collection service (green wheeled bins) will stop from 4 April 2016. Read more about the current green bin service.

How to subscribe to the new garden waste collection service

Subscribe now to the new green garden waste collection service. Harborough District Council is using Charnwood Borough Council to take payments for the garden waste collection service on our behalf. You will be directed to the Charnwood Borough Council website to make your payment for your subscription. There is a fee of 2.2% for using a credit card.

You can choose to join the garden waste collection service at any time during the year for a fee of £40 per bin (there are no discounts, refunds or reductions on this fee). We will add your address to our collection schedule for the remainder of the collection period for that year.

You can setup a subscription for up to 5 garden waste green bins to be collected from your property. There is a fee of £40 per garden waste green bin that you would like collected.

If you do not currently have a green garden waste bin at your property, or would like to request additional green garden waste bins unfortunately this cannot be completed online so call 01858 828282 to setup your subscription to the garden waste collection service.

New garden waste collection service

From 4 April 2016 a new chargeable garden waste collection service will be introduced.

Residents in the Harborough District can subscribe to the garden waste collection service to have their garden waste collected for an annual £40 subscription fee per bin.

From the 4 April 2016 we will only empty green garden waste bins from households that have opted-in to the garden waste collection service. Your bin will be collected fortnightly by the same trusted teams as before and where possible you will have the same collection day as you do with the current green bin service.

When you opt-in you will receive a bin sticker which you will need to stick on the body of your bin: at the rear, below the handle. This subscription covers the financial year with:

  • Fortnightly collections from April to November 2016
  • No collections during December 2016, January and February 2017
  • Fortnightly collections during March 2017

From January 2017 you will be able to resubscribe for the following financial year â€“ subscriptions are for 1 year and are not automatically renewed.

Current green bin service

Your green wheeled bin is for the following items:

  • Loose green waste
  • Grass cuttings
  • Leaves
  • Flowers
  • Clippings

Please do not place the following into the green bin:

  • Food waste
  • Soil
  • Turf
  • Animal bedding or animal waste
  • Cardboard

During the Christmas period real Christmas trees can be placed in the green bin, however large trunks and branches will not be accepted.

All other materials should be disposed of via:

  • Blue lidded bin - for recyclable materials
  • Black bin - for non-recyclable rubbish
  • Household recycling and waste sites - for items that cannot be placed in the wheeled bins

Bin not collected

If you believe that your bin has been missed and not been collected you can report a missed bin collection.

Replacement for missing or damaged bin

A charge will usually be made to replace lost, missing or damaged bins. If a bin has been damaged during collection a charge will not be made for a replacement bin.

Make a request for a replacement bin.

Help with collections

We can help with bin collections for residents who are elderly, infirm or disabled and have no one else at the property to help. Request help with bin collections.

Different size of bin

It may be possible to request a second green bin (240 litre size) or to exchange your current bin (240 litre size) for a smaller bin (140 litre size).

Make a request for a second bin or a smaller bin.
