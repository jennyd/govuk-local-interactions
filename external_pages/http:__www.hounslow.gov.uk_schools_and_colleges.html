Schools and colleges

In this section of the website you will find information about Hounslow's schools and colleges. Information such as contact details, admissions policies, truancy policies and term dates are all available here.

You can also find information on educating your child at home and on educational support for looked after children.

Hounslow has 54 primary schools, 14 secondary schools and 5 special schools.
