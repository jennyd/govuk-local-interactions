Compliments, Suggestions and Complaints

We aim to provide you with a high-quality service. If you have a suggestion or complaint about any service we provide, please let us know. We will do our best to put the matter right and make sure we improve the service we provide. We would also like to know if you are pleased with the service provided. We can inform our staff and use the information to improve our services.

If you have a complaint

In the first instance you should advise the member of staff who has been dealing with you. However, if you do not want to do this, ask for the name of their manager.

If you are unhappy with the way your complaint has been dealt with, you should write to the chief executive. Your complaint will be investigated further and we will let you know the outcome as quickly as possible.

How to contact us

  • By letter addressed to the relevant manager at the Council Offices, London Road, Saffron Walden, CB11 4ER
  • By filling in the form at the top right of this page
  • By telephone: 01799 510510
  • By fax: 01799 510550

How quickly will the council respond?

Where possible your complaint will be dealt with fully within 10 working days. If we need further information we will contact you within three working days.

What happens if I am not satisfied with the council's response?

If, when the council's complaints procedure is completed, you are still not satisfied and wish to pursue the matter further, please tell us and we will send you the appropriate form to enable you to take the matter to the Local Government Ombudsman. His/her role is to deal with complaints of maladministration. A leaflet is available at the Council Offices. If you wish to contact the Ombudsman direct you may call 0300 061 0614, send a fax to 024 7682 0001 or write to: Local Government Ombudsman, PO Box 4771, Coventry, CV4 0EH. Further information can be found on the Making a complaint page on the Local Government Ombudsman website.

And remember: we also welcome your views on how to improve the service we provide.
