Council tax - how to pay

There are three easy ways to pay your council tax.

All you have to do is choose the one that suits you:

Direct Debit - Please complete a direct debit form here

Win a life changing £25,000!

Most of our customers pay by direct debit as this is the most convenient and safest way to pay. It’s the easiest way to spread the cost across the year and to enjoy the peace of mind of never being late to pay. So why don’t you make the switch? Do it now and you’ll be entered into our free prize draw to win a life-changing £25,000!

Pay online - using your debit or credit card

Phone – call our 24-hour automated service on 0845 30 10 210 (Please note: this call will cost you 3p per minute plus your phone company's access charge).
