Council Tax

All the information you need to know about Council Tax including: how to pay, reducing your bill (discounts and exemptions), appealing a decision, finding your property banding...

Information you need to know about your Council Tax and how to pay.

There may be help available to pay your Council Tax bill or you may be entitled to a discount or exemption. If you're on a low income, you may be able to apply for Council Tax support.

Remember, you can use our online form to see how much Council Tax and Housing Support you could claim, and also to apply for it.

If your situation changes, please contact the Council as soon as possible.
