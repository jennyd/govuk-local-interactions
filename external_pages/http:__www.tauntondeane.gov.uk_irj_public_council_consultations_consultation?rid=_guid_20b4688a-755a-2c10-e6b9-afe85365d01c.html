Consultations

Why do we consult?

The purpose of consultation is to inform the decisions taken by the Council, to help make the best decisions based on the views of the community and the wider information available to the Council. Ultimately, responsibility for making effective decisions rests with Members of the Council.

Consultation can:

  • Provide invaluable information about improving services, plans and partnerships across Taunton Deane.
  • Allow us to determine which priorities to focus our time and resources on.
  • Help to generate ideas, issues and solutions, and identify key people to be involved in the planning process.


Much of our consultation is a statutory requirement.

How do we consult?

We consult using a variety of methods, which include public meetings, forums, surveys, and focus groups.

Within the Council the Strategy Unit maintain a forward plan of consultation. This is to help ensure consultation is accessible and representative of those we need to hear from, and where possible, avoids members of the public suffering from "consultation overload"; being invited to take part in a number of different consultation exercises around the same time.

If you have queries about any consultation being carried out by the council please contact the Strategy Unit;

Email: strategy@tauntondeane.gov.uk 

Phone: 01823 356312
Each consultation is also governed by the  Consultation Policy which sets out the values to which Taunton Deane Borough Council is committed.
