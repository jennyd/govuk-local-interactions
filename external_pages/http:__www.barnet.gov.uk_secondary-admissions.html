Admissions for entry into secondary school in September 2016

If your child was born between 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005 you will need to apply to your home council for a secondary school place for the academic year 2015/2016.

You should submit your application online using the eAdmissions website

  • the application process opens online on 1 September 2015
  • the closing date for applications is 31 October 2015
  • the outcome of your online application online results will be sent by email during the evening of 1 March 2016
  • if your first choice school cannot be offered, an outcome letter will also be posted to you on 1 March.

Making your application - important points to remember

  • you can list a maximum of six schools in the order you prefer them
  • you can select schools within and outside your home borough
  • there is no advantage in listing only one school
  • make sensible choices to improve your chances of securing a place at a named school
  • attend the Open Evenings of the schools that you are interested in (dates and times below)
  • if your child has an Education Health Care Plan (EHCP) or a statement of special educational needs (SEN), you must not apply using this process
  •  if your first choice school cannot be offered, you will  receive information on how places were allocated, the waiting list procedure and how to appeal against the decision not to offer your child a place at your chosen school(s)
  • after 31 March 2016, the council will retain waiting lists for the oversubscribed schools
  • further offers will be made from the waiting lists as and when places become available (please remember that only a small number of places are expected to become available after 1 March 2016)
  • if your child has not been offered a place at any of your preferred schools and you do not wish to accept a place at the alternative school offered, you will become responsible for securing suitable education for your child.
