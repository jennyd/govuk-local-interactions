Register for council housing

Who can join the Housing Needs Register?

The Housing Needs Register is open to all people who wish to live in the Wokingham Borough. People who own their own home, or apply from outside the Borough, will be given a lower priority on the Housing Needs Register than people who live in the Wokingham Borough already.

  • For more details read the Housing Needs Register eligibility and guidance (PDF document.)

Medical re-housing

Do you or someone living with you have a health problem, or has a physical, mental, learning or sensory impairment which restricts or prevents you living in your home? Are you getting older? Your housing needs could be changing. If you have answered yes to the above we may be able to help you. Contact us for advice.
