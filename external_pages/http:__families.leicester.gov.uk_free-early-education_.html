Free Early Years Education

Children playing with blocks

All 3 and 4 year old children are entitled to access up to 15 hours of free early education each week for a maximum of 38 weeks a year.   

Some 2 year olds are also entitled to free early education, this is dependent on meeting a set criteria.

 

For more information on free early education and how to access it, select the relevant age range.
