Pest control

The council provides a pest control service for rats, mice and wasps which is available to all residents in Bristol. 

At busy times, particularly during the Summer, you might have to wait up to four weeks before we can begin dealing with your pest problem.

Council pest control services

Pests we treat

We control rats, mice and wasps for all residents in Bristol.

If you're a tenant of Bristol City Council and you live in a flat, we also deal with bed bugs and cockroaches.

Costs and timings

Although our prices are often less than private companies,  you might have to wait up to four weeks before we're able to begin dealing with your pest problem.

  • Rats: £25
  • Mice: £64.80
  • Wasps: £64

Some jobs that require another member of staff will cost an additional £64 to £82.

Bristol City Council tenants who live in a flat will not have to pay for the control of rats, mice, bedbugs or cockroaches.

Reductions for people on benefits

The rat service is  free for people getting Income Support, Income Benefit Job Seekers Allowance, Pension Credit Guarantee, Personal Independence Payment (was called Disability Living Allowance) or Housing Benefit.

People getting Income Support or Pension Credit Guarantee pay  half price for the treatment of mice and wasps. 

You must show proof that you get these benefits to our pest control officer before the treatment is carried out.

Book an appointment

It might take up to four weeks to get an appointment with a Bristol City Council pest controller at busy times, although we're currently able to offer appointments within one week.

  • Call 0117 922 2500 to book an appointment. Please only call this number if you want to make a booking.
  • Email: pest.control@bristol.gov.uk
  • Fax: 0117 971 6001
