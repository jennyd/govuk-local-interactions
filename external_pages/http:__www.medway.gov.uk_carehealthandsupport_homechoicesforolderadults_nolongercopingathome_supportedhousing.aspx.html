Supported housing

If you can no longer live at home by yourself, supported housing units can provide you care, while allowing you to live independently within a community.

What is supported housing?

  • a studio flat, one-bedroom flat or bungalow
  • fitted with Telecare, a 24-hour community service alarm
  • overseen by a scheme manager for your safety.

How to apply for accommodation

Full details of how to look for a home, apply and bid can be found on the HomeChoice website.

Or phone 01634 334466 to find out more.
