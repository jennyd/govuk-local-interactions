Help to buy or part-buy a property

Listen

You must register with a HomeBuy agent - for Spelthorne this is BPHA .They act as a one-stop-shop for assessing eligibility and providing information on all aspects and options for home ownership.

Please call BPHA (Options to Buy) on 0845 456 6757 or visit the BPHA website.
