Adoption

Changing a child's life forever

Last updated: 26/10/2015
Email page
Print page

In Cornwall we are looking for people with patience, humour and energy to become adopters. Becoming an adoptive parent can be fulfilling, life changing and make a real difference to the life of a child. On average we successfully place 40 children a year in loving adoptive families, and we continue to have children waiting for a family.

Find out more.

Watch a short film of one of our adoptive families.


We hold regular information meetings, contact us if you would like to attend one using the contact information to the right of this page.

What is adoption?

Adoption is a way of giving a child the love and security of family life when they cannot live with the family they were born into.
