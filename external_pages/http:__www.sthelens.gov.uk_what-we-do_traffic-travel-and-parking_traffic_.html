Traffic and UTC Consultancy

St Helens Council is the local Highway Authority for the Borough of St Helens. Good traffic management contributes towards safety for all road users and enables the safe passage of vehicles on the highway. The Traffic Section is part of the Environmental Protection Department and is available to provide advice on the following matters:

  • Requests for Traffic Data
  • List of Traffic Schemes (Capital)
  • Traffic Application Forms

Parking Issues

Residents Parking; Disabled Bays; Restrictions; H Markings; Bollards; Civil Parking Enforcement (CPE)

Road Safety Issues

AIP Schemes; Speeding Traffic; Traffic Calming Road; Road Safety; Accident Reduction

Lining and Signing

Road Signs; Directional Signs; Tourism Signs; Diversion Routes; H Markings; Electronic Signs; Children on Holiday Signs
