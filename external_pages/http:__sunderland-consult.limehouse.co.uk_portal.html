What are you looking for

Welcome to Sunderland’s Consultation Calendar

Sunderland City Council works with partners such as the police and health authority to provide a range of opportunities for local people to get involved in and influence decisions about the local area and city. This is to help us make sure that our services and plans meet the needs of local people.

Consultation

One of the ways in which you can get involved is by taking part in consultations. This might include for example, taking part in surveys, discussion groups, online forums or attending local events, meetings and exhibitions.  All open consultations are listed below. 

The consultation calendar helps you to find out about open consultations, register your interest in future consultations and see what has happened as a result of past consultations.  You may be able to take part in the consultations online and in some instances, you may need to register.  To search for consultations, you can use the filter options below.

E-Petitions

Another way in which you can get involved is by creating or signing a Petition.  To create or sign a petition and to view the Council’s Petitions Scheme, which contains information on how your petition will be responded to, please click the link to the left of this text.  Guidance and instruction videos are also included.

Visit ‘Help’ above for more tips on how to use these pages.
