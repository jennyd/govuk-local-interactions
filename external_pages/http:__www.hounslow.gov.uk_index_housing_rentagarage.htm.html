Rent a garage

From as little as £9 per week you could rent your own lock up garage. Hounslow Council has over 2,500 garages on 160 sites across the London Borough of Hounslow.

You can rent a garage from us if you are a tenant, leaseholder or private resident of the London Borough of Hounslow.

Complete a garage rental application form to apply, or for further information contact us on 020 8583 2000.

You can also visit your local housing office to pick up a form or ask for further details.

Please return your completed form to: Repairs Call Centre, Civic Centre, Lampton Road Hounslow TW3 4DN

Please be aware that some areas are very popular and you may be added to a waiting list.

Important information about renting a garage

Garages can only be used for the storage of motorised vehicles and for no other purpose.

Private residents applying for a garage will be asked to pay four weeks rent in advance on commencement of the garage rental, and monthly by direct debit thereafter.

If you are a council tenant or leaseholder you cannot rent a garage if you have rent or service charge arrears. If your account goes into arrears your garage will be repossessed. You will be able to re-apply for a garage when your arrears are cleared.

Some areas are very popular and you will be added to a waiting list if there are no garages available. We can add you to more than one list if there is more than one location that is suitable.

You can report repairs to your garage to the Customer Service Centre on 020 8583 2000
