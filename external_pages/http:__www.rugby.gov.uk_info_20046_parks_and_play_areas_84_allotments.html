Allotments in Rugby

With the rise in popularity of 'growing your own,' we have long waiting lists for allotments at all of our sites.

On average, residents can currently expect to wait at least a year before being offered an allotment.

Our allotment sites

We currently have six allotment sites in the borough: 

  • Addison Road - CV22 7DA
  • Clifton Road - CV21 3SD
  • Eastlands - CV21 3RY
  • Freemantle Road - CV22 7DH
  • Newbold Glebe - CV21 1AY
  • The Kent - CV21 4NG
