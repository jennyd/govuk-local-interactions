Sandwell Register Office

Sandwell's Register Office is based in the beautiful Grade II listed Highfields House in High Street, West Bromwich - a five minute walk from West Brom town centre.

You can get married or hold a civil partnership ceremony at our Register Office any day of the year (except for Christmas Day and Good Friday). 

The Sandwell Suite can hold up to 85 guests. Our smaller ceremony room caters for up to 16 people (including the happy couple).

Contact us and find out about our fees and charges.
