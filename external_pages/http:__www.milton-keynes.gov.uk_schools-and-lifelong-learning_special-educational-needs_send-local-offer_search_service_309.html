Home To School Transport

The qualification criteria applies to all children, including those with special educational needs, a disability or mobility difficulties and post 16 students with a special educational need or disability.

Parents and carers have a legal duty and a responsibility to ensure that their children attend school regularly and to make any necessary arrangements to ensure that they attend. In certain circumstances the Council has a duty to provide free home to school transport.

http://www.milton-keynes.gov.uk/schools-and-lifelong-learning/information-for-parents/home-to-school-transport

Organisation Details

The qualification criteria applies to all children, including those with special educational needs, a disablility, or mobility difficulties. Special educational needs, disability or a mobility difficulty do not give an automatic right to free home to school transport, even if the school attended is a special school.

However, there will be exceptional circumstances where a child is unable to walk to school due to their specific needs. Where parents and carers feel there are good reasons why free home to school transport should be provided, there is a process for making an application on exceptional grounds.
