Request an extra bin or change the size of your bin

Does your household struggle with the amount of rubbish you produce? Or would you like to change your bin for a different size? If so, please contact us and someone from our Sustainability and Climate Change team will respond to you.

Please note that we charge a £10 contribution towards each new bin.

However, some citizens are exempt from paying the replacement bin charge include those receiving:
  • Guaranteed Pension Credit
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Income Support
  • Housing Benefit
If you would like to order a new bin, please telephone the Neighbourhood Services Call Centre on 0115 915 2000.
