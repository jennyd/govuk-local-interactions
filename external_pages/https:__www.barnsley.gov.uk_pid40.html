    Home to school transport for children with special needs

    If your child has a special need, disability or mobility problem that prevents them walking to school, they may be entitled to free school transport.

    How to apply for free transport for your child

    If you think your child may qualify for free home to school transport, please complete the special needs transport application form and email it to schooltransport@barnsley.gov.uk.  You can also send it by post to the school transport section at the address given below.

    Our Transport Panel will consider your application and we’ll write to you to let you know the outcome.

    If your child qualifies for free travel, please get in touch with us to let us know what your child’s travel needs are.  Once we have this information, we’ll arrange transport for your child as soon as possible and write to you to tell you who the transport provider is and, if your child needs an escort, who they will be.

    We'll normally provide a free taxi or mini bus to pick your child up and drop them off at the school door.  Sometimes, parents take their child to school themselves and we reimburse their travel expenses. In other cases, we provide a free travel pass for your child to travel to school on public transport if this is appropriate for their needs.

    Whatever we do, we’ll always write to you to confirm the arrangements we’ve made for your child’s travel.

    If the Transport Panel refuses your application, you can ask for your case to be reviewed. Our Transport Review and Appeals Procedure explains more about the appeals procedure.
