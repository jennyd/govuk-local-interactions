Licensing Act 2003

Personal licences

A personal licence allows an individual to authorise the sale or supply of alcohol.

Premises licences

Premises that cater for activities such as the sale of alcohol, late night refreshment and the provision of entertainment, music and dancing

Club premises certificate

A club premises certificate authorises a members' club to carry out licensable activities.

Live Music Act 2012

More information on the changes to the Licensing Act 2003 and live music in licensed venues.
