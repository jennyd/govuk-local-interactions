Report a problem

This form will allow you to report any environmental problem affecting council-owned property such as roads, pavements, parks and buildings.

Please click the map to tell us the exact location of the problem. This will create a pin to mark the spot. If you cannot see the location on this map, please click on ⌖ to zoom into your location. You may need to switch on your device's location services. You can also type the nearest street name in the search box below before choosing the exact location on the map.
