Applying for a place on our Farepayer Scheme

You can apply online for a place.

What is the Farepayer Scheme?

The Farepayer Scheme offers any spare seats on our school buses to school pupils and post-16 students who live in West Berkshire, and who are not eligible for free transport.  There is no guarantee of a place as we don't provide extra seats for farepayers; these are spare seats on existing services, offered on a discretionary basis.

Applications received after seats have been initially allocated are only approved subject to availability; please get in touch if you'd like to discuss.

Pupils should read the  behaviour code [445kb] as they'll be expected to follow this when using our transport.

Post-16 Students should also read  Post-16 Transport Statement [46kb] and the  16-19 Travel Guide [742kb].

For full details and prices please read the  Fare Payer Scheme guide [79kb]
