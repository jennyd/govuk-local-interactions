Application for a skip permit

This form is for use by registered skip operators to apply for a permit to place a builder's skip on a public road or highway. We aim to process your application within 5 working days of receiving it. We will send you your permit by email.

There is a non-refundable charge of £19. This covers administrative costs for processing this application and a permit for one week. Additional weeks, or part thereof, cost £12.50 each. A permit cannot be granted without payment. You will be asked to pay online when you have completed this form.
 

Please read the conditions for placing a skip on a public road or highway before completing this form.

Items marked (*) must be completed

No skip permits can be granted without a valid skip operator licence number issued by Bracknell Forest Council.
