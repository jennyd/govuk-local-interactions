How to pay a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) 

You can pay for Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) online, by telephone or by post.

Please read the information on your PCN to see which Council you should pay.

Penalty Charge Notices issued AFTER February 2015

If you have been issued with a PNC with a reference number of 800 or above after February 2015 payments should be made directly to Cumbria County Council either:

  • online on Cumbria County Council's Parking Portal
  • over the phone please call 0300 111 7766
  • cheque payment by post see the information stated on the PCN.

Pay or challenge a PCN issued BEFORE February 2015

  • Online: Payments can be made online by completing the Car Parking Penalty Charge Notice (Fine): Payment form
  • By Post: Postal payments can be sent to: Eden Parking Services, PO Box 258, Carlisle CA3 8YE
  • In person: Payment can be made in person at the Town Hall in Penrith (cheques made payable to Eden District Council).
