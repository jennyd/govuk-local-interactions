Make an online payment

Using this online service, you can pay Council Tax, Business Rates, Business Improvement District (BID), Housing Rents, Benefit Overpayment, Parking Penalty Charge Notices and Invoices.

Did you know…
You can also pay your regular council bills such as Council Tax, Housing Rent, Business Rates and some regular invoices by Direct Debit, to save time and make life easier. Find out more on our Direct Debit page.

If you are in a public place we strongly advise you make sure you are not overlooked as you enter your details to prevent them being misused by somebody else.

You can pay your Ipswich Borough Council bills using any of these cards:

Note - Minimum payment is £5.00 GBP and if you use a credit card to pay, a surcharge of 1.5% will be made (except if paying a Parking Penalty Charge Notice). This reflects the bank's charge to the Council for accepting credit card payments.

Please select the type of payment you wish to make. You can add more than one payment before deciding to process them.
