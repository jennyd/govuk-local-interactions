  • Carers & Family Support Hampshire County Council offer a wealth of knowledge and support for carers
  • Childcare Hampshire County Council offers a range of information about services for young children.

  • Community Right to Bid The Community Right to Bid is a government policy that gives local communities the opportunity to recognise and 'pause' the sale of land and buildings considered to be of importance to the community.
  • Community Safety and Neighbourhood Services The Neighbourhood Services Team work towards creating a safer, cleaner and greener Winchester, engaging with the local community and responding to issues.
  • Community Venues A map and information about community centres and facilities available around the District.

  • Cultural Grant Scheme Due to the high volume of responses, this grant is currently closed to new applications. Please check back regularly for updates. The grant is a funding opportunity for festivals, major cultural events or arts activities taking place in the Winchester District.

  • Find a play area Find a play area including the four natural playgrounds in the District were part of the Big Lottery Funded, It's Okay to Play project.
  • Grants Find out how we can help your organisation with a grant.
  • Health & Wellbeing The Winchester District Health and Wellbeing Partnership Board has been established to lead and oversee all health and wellbeing issues across the district.
  • Insulate Hampshire Scheme Insulate Hampshire offers FREE cavity wall and loft insulation (for a limited period) helping residents to make savings of over £300 a year.
  • Jobs Our recruitment process is managed via the HIOWLGA (Hampshire and Isle of Wight Local Government Authorities) Recruitment Portal.
  • Launch of a Water Vole Trail to Winnall Moors On Saturday 24 May 2014 Hampshire & Isle of Wight Wildlife Trust celebrates the completion of a five-year Heritage Lottery funded project to restore wildlife habitats on its Winnall Moors Nature Reserve and to improve access for visitors.
  • Markets Winchester market is situated between Middle Brook Street and often along the High Street in the City Centre
  • Moving to Winchester Winchester is just an hour south west of London, surrounded by rolling countryside of the South Downs National Park and idyllic Hampshire villages. All you need to know if you move to Winchester or within the District.
  • Parish Connect e-newsletter sent out to Parish Councils and subscribers towards the end of each month.

  • Public toilets A list of Council maintained public toilets

  • Sports Pitches/Courts We have three sports pitches available for hire as well as facilities in our two leisure centres.
  • Tours & Trails Winchester City and District is perfect for exploring on foot combining breathtaking scenery and countryside.
  • Voluntary Organisations We work in partnership with voluntary organisations to support local communities.
