Report a damaged pavement

Use this form to report a damaged pavement.

Before you start

Any fault relating to a Red Route (A316 or A205) should be reported directly to Transport for London.

If you notice the damage outside of office hours and feel it cannot wait until the next working day, please call our out of hours contact number: 020 8744 2442.

You will need

  • The location of the damaged pavement. You are able to mark this on a map.
  • To describe the type of damage.

What happens next

The area will be inspected within the next 3-5 working days and anything reaching our criteria will be repaired on a priority basis.

We can update you on any action to be taken if you provide your contact details.
