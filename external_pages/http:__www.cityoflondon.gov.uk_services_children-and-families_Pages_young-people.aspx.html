Get active, get creative, get involved

Lots of fun and educational activities with City Gateway, a charity working in partnership with the City Corporation, providing support for young people aged 10 to 19 living in the Square Mile.

Here's a taste of what you can do to take part:

At Portsoken and Artizan Street community centres

  • Play dodgeball, pool and table tennis, learn about cooking, get help with homework, discover your artistic side with activities like photography and music and video production, streetdance, graphic design and more...
  • Get the best tips for writing your CV, application form and preparing for job interviews.

Bridwell Youth Theatre

Learn and develop through performing arts and a safe space to express yourself and have their voices heard.

Volunteer Police Cadet

Learn about police work and help support your community.

Employment opportunities with the City of London

Apprenticeship Programme

University is not the only option. There are plenty of career options to choose from and the apprenticeship programme is set up with your future in mind, matching candidates with leading employers.

City Talent

City Talent offers you the chance to visit a City firm, a week of work experience in the Square Mile and four months of career focused mentoring from a City worker.

City Opportunities

City Opportunities highlights career opportunities available in the City through series of workshops led by City firms on roles available and the skills needed to work in the City.
