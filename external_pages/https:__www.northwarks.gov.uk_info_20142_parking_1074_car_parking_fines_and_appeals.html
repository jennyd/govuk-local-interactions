Car parking fines and appeals

Paying a parking fine

Our Fixed Penalty Charge (FPC) Number starts with NW0 - Fixed Penalty Charge Notices that do not begin NW0 have not been issued from North Warwickshire and you will still have to pay the issuing Authority.

Payments to North Warwickshire Borough Council, as set out in the Fixed Penalty Charge Notice, are:

Pay a parking fine
  • Within 7 days - £25.00
  • After 7 days - £50.00
