Supporting the voluntary sector

We recognise the vibrancy, diversity, and range of services provided by the voluntary and community sector to the people of Nottinghamshire.

We support the voluntary and community sector through:

  • grant aid
  • Councillors divisional fund
  • contracts
  • support in kind.

We have an annual grant aid budget of approximately £1.6 million and support a range of organisations each year.

You can find out more about grant aid, including how to apply on our grant aid pages.

For more information about how we work with the voluntary sector see the Council and the voluntary sector pages
