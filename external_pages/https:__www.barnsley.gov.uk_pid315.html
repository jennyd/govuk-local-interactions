Help with meals

Information about community meals services and luncheon clubs.

Meals delivered to your door

The council does not provide a meals on wheels service.  In Barnsley there are a number of private providers of meals services, that can deliver either a hot meal to your door, or a frozen meal, for you to reheat.  The meals are nutritionally balanced and special dietary requirements can be catered for.

You can visit our new online marketplace for social care and support services Connect to Support  for details of meals providers or contact Adult Social Services on (01226) 773300.

Luncheon Clubs

There are luncheon clubs across the borough, where you can have a hot meal and spend some time socialising with other people in your community.  Sometimes activities for example Bingo are included.  Many of them are run by voluntary organisations like Age UK, who work closely with local churches and communities to start new clubs. For further information contact Age UK or telephone number (01226) 776820.

Help to live independently at home

If you need some other support to live at home you can contact us on (01226) 773300 for help and advice.  You can find out more about what help is available from adult social services here.
