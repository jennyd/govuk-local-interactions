Volunteer Awards Ceremony 2015

Over 100 local people came together on the 4th June 2015 to celebrate Community Links Bromley’s eighth Annual Volunteer Awards ceremony, held at Bromley Central Library. The event celebrated volunteering and voluntary activity by recognising the valuable input volunteers make to their local communities. 

Over 25 people received awards from nine categories in recognition of their contributions. The categories included Supporting the Work of an Organisation, Trustee Award, Supporting an Individual, Making it Happen, Outstanding Contribution, Supporting a Community, Young People, Team of Volunteers and Volunteer Manager.

Welcome to Community Links Bromley (CLB)

Welcome to the new website of Community Links Bromley (CLB). This website is always being updated, so if there is anything you cannot find, please email Alexandra.  

We are a membership organisation that supports, develops and promotes the voluntary sector in the London borough of Bromley.  We provide free advice and guidance to local organisations on issues from finance and fundraising to HR and governance. We provide a detailed training and development programme and we are able to provide tailor made support including a number of chargeable services including training courses.
