Fault Report: Street lights

To report a problem concerning street lights please complete the online form below.

Alternatively, you can phone in a report using 020 7361 3001.

Note: This form does not cover all types of street lighting in the Royal Borough:

  • Traffic lights - for problems with traffic lights, please contact Transport for London at: http://www.streetmanagement.org.uk
  • Red routes - for problems with lighting along red routes , please contact Transport for London at: http://www.streetmanagement.org.uk
  • Housing estates - for problems with lighting on housing estates, please contact the Tenant Management Organisation (TMO)

If your problem is not listed above, please use the form below.

Required fields are marked with an asterisk *.
