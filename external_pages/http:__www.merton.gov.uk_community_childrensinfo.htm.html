Disability/Special Needs Database

The Merton disability/special needs database is a voluntary register of children and young people with a disability or special need in Merton. All children and young people with a disability or special need on the database will receive information regarding services and provisions available to them in the borough.

The database also helps the London Borough of Merton and other organisations to be aware of the needs and demand for services in the borough

If you register with the database, you will be entitled to apply for an M-Card, the Merton identity card for children and young people with disabilities or special needs.

If you would like more information or to register on the database, please contact the Disability Database Coordinator by calling 020 8274 5830 or via email at fiag@merton.gov.uk
