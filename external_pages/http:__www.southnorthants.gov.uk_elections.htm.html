Elections

What we do

The Democratic and Elections team are responsible for administering the electoral process in the district of South Northamptonshire. This means that we:

  • prepare and maintain a complete and accurate register of electors 
  • organise elections and referendums which take place in the district 
  • promote public awareness of, and participation in, the electoral process
  • provide a high standard of service to all local residents

An altered register of electors for the district was published on 2 March 2015.
