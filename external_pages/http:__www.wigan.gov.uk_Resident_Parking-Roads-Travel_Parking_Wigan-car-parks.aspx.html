Wigan car parks

This page lists the car parks in Wigan, we also have a list of car parks in Leigh.

  • Wigan town centre car parks map

Galleries multi-storey car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 1QX

Situated in Wigan town centre, the Galleries multi-storey car park has 625 spaces including dedicated disabled and parent and baby spaces. Access to the Galleries Shopping Centre is via lifts which serve all floors and this is a pay on exit car park.

  • Open from 7am to 7pm on Monday to Saturday
  • Sunday 10.30am to 5.30pm
  • Height restriction applies - 6ft 6inches - 1.98m
  • Spaces 625 - disabled spaces 29
  • Disabled parking - charges apply

Water Street multi-storey car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 1UP

Situated within a short walk of the town centre, the Water Street car park is ideal for users of Gala Bingo. This is a pay on exit car park with dedicated disabled and parent and baby spaces. Lift access is available to levels 3, 5, 7, 9 and 11. Operates as a pay on foot facility.

  • Open from 6.30am to 7pm Monday to Saturday inclusive; 10.30am to 5.30pm Sunday
  • Height restriction applies - 6ft 5inches - 1.95m
  • Spaces 470 - disabled spaces 6
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Credit/debit card payment facility available
  • Electric vehicle charging point available

Permit charges for Water Street multi-storey car park (subject to availability)

  • Annual - £670
  • Six monthly - £360
  • Quarterly - £190

Chapel Lane car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 1DL

Situated at the junction of Darlington St and Chapel Lane, this car park is just a 5 minute walk from the town centre.

  • Permit holders only Monday to Friday
  • Pay and display on Saturdays only
  • Height restriction applies (6ft 6inches - 1.98m)
  • Free parking on Sunday
  • 24hr access
  • Spaces 253 - disabled spaces 2
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Pay by phone & credit/debit/wave & pay card payment facility available

Harrogate Street car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 1BL

Situated just a five minute walk to the town centre, Harrogate Street is a surface pay and display car park.

  • 24hr access
  • Pay and display Monday to Saturday
  • Free parking on Sunday
  • Spaces 107 - disabled spaces 2
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Pay by phone & credit/debit/wave & pay card payment facility available

Mona Street car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 1AA

Situated near to the bus station this surface pay and display car park is ideal for those wishing to gain access to Wigan College and the town centre.

  • 24hr access
  • Parking is free on Sunday
  • Spaces 81
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Pay by phone & credit/debit/wave & pay card facility available
  • Electric vehicle charging point available

Permit charges for Harrogate Street and Mona Street surface car parks

  • Annual - £515
  • Six monthly - £290
  • Quarterly - £165

Civic Centre car park

Saturday and Sunday public parking only

Situated directly behind the Civic Centre, the pay and display car park is within a few minutes' walk of the town centre and Town Hall.

  • Public pay and display parking Saturday and Sunday only
  • The car park gates are locked overnight from 11pm to 6am
  • Spaces 48 - disabled spaces 2
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Pay by phone & credit/debit/wave & pay card payment facility available

Sovereign Road and Pepper Mill Lane car park

Postcode for sat nav: WN1 3AE

Situated near to the Magistrates Court they are surface pay and display car parks Monday to Friday, offering value for money at just £1.50 all day.

  • 24hr access
  • Parking is free on Saturday, Sunday and Bank Holidays
  • Height restriction applies 6ft 8inches on Sovereign Rd car park
  • 36 spaces for Sovereign Road, 30 spaces for Pepper Mill Lane
  • Disabled parking - charges apply
  • Pay by phone payment facility available

Permit charges for Sovereign Road and Pepper Mill Lane surface car parks

  • Annual - £360
  • Six monthly - £195
  • Quarterly - £110

Haig Street and Pennyhurst Street surface car parks

Postcode for sat nav: WN3 4AZ

Situated near to Wigan Pier and a 10 minute walk to the town centre, Haig St and Pennyhurst St offer value for money at just £1 all day.

  • Pay and display Monday to Saturday
  • Free on Sundays and Bank Holidays
  • 47 spaces - 2 disabled for Haig Street, 100 spaces for Pennyhurst Street

Permit Charges for Haig Street and Pennyhurst Street surface car parks

  • Annual - £120
  • Six monthly - £65
  • Quarterly - £35

Wigan on-street pay and display charges

Situated around the town centre, Wigan has 324 pay and display spaces with tariffs ranging from 1 to 3 hours.

  • ½ hour - 80p
  • 1 hour - £1.10
  • 2 hours - £2.10
  • 3 hours - £3.20

Motorcycle stands

There are motorcycle parking spaces with secure chain locks and the facility to display a pay and display ticket in both Wigan and Leigh town centres. Two pay and display motorcycle stands are located on New Market Street, Mona Street car park and Harrogate Street car park.
