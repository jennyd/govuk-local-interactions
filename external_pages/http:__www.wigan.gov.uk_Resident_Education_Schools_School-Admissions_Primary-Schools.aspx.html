Primary school admissions

Applying for reception class for 2016

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012, they will start reception class at the start of the new school year in September 2016.

Before you apply

You must read:

  • Applying for a place at primary school
  • Oversubscription criteria for community and voluntary controlled primary schools

Note: If you have previously registered an interest at a Wigan primary school this is not an application for a school place.

Make an application

The deadline has now passed. If you have not yet applied for a primary school place, or you wish to change your mind about the preference or the order of preferences after the closing date, you must complete a late application form.

  • Primary school application form (late applications)
  • Or get a printed application direct from the primary school, or by contacting us

If any of the schools you want your child to go to is a Wigan voluntary aided primary school, the governors will need extra information to consider your application against their faith criteria. If you do not give this information your child may be refused a place. You are strongly advised to complete a supplementary information form for each voluntary aided school you name as a preference.

  • Download a church school supplementary information form

Alternatively contact us for a printed copy.

Deadline

The closing date for applications was 15 January 2016.

As the deadline has passed all applications received will now be classed as late. We will only look at late applications after we have dealt with the applications we receive on time. This means that you are less likely to get a place at the school you want.

Need help?

If you need any help using the online service please contact us.
