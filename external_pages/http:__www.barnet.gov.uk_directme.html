Awareness for parent/carers

As the parent or carer of a child or young person, it is your responsibility to ensure your children’s safety when taking part in activities or events carried out by organisations and individuals listed in this directory. We recommend that you contact the organiser to check they have proper safeguarding policies – to include health and safety measures- in place.

Including your organisation on Directme

If you are an organisation or service and would like to be included on Directme, please submit details of the services or activities you offer.
