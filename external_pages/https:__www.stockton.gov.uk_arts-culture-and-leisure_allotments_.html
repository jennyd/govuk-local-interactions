Allotments

The Council owns many allotment sites throughout the Borough, Thornaby Allotments on Thornaby Road and Quarry Road Allotments in Eaglescliffe are managed by us. The remainder are managed by allotment associations and Town and Parish Councils.

In Thornaby the average allotment is approximately 1/16 of an acre which you can rent from Stockton Council for £28 per year. Costs to rent an allotment within the other self-managed sites vary.

A number of allotment sites, including Thornaby and Quarry Road have a water supply.

If you would like to apply for an allotment you can complete the online form.
