Sheltered Accommodation for the Elderly

The Planning Policy section of Planning Services has published a list of sheltered, warden controlled housing association and nursing home accommodation for the elderly in Chiltern District. The document relates to information as at 31st December 2005. It has not been possible to update the document after that date.

During 2009 a new way of allocating social rented accommodation came into operation. It is called Bucks Home Choice and is a partnership of Aylesbury Vale, Chiltern, South Bucks and Wycombe District Councils, together with the Paradigm Housing Group and the Vale of Aylesbury Housing Trust. Housing applicants (home seekers) and existing tenants wanting a transfer can use Bucks Home Choice to look for a new home.

For more details go to the web site  Bucks Home Choice
