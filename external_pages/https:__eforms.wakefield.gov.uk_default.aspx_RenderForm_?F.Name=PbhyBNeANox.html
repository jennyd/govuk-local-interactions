Request a Bulky Refuse Collection

 
Under our improved system, when you request this service you will be given an appointment advising you of the collection date.

You will need to tell us on this form what you need collecting. The item(s) for collection need to be placed to the front of your premises before 7.15 am on your appointment date, or alternatively where your wheeled bin is emptied - however it must not cause any obstruction.

Please note that all items must be suitably tied or bundled.
 

We charge £15 to collect and dispose of a maximum of three bulky items per collection. We can visit 3 times in any one day, i.e. for a maximum of 9 items per day (£45)

For further information about payment methods, please go to the How to pay Council bills page.
 

We do not collect fixtures and fittings as part of the bulky collection service.

Please Note
As a general rule, if you wouldn't take it with you when you move house then we won't take it in this service.  However if you require such items removing we will remove them for a charge.
