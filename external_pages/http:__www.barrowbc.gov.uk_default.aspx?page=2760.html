Planning Public Access

Planning Public Access

Viewing Planning Applications on the Planning Portal

 

  • Online Register of Planning Applications (includes the following)

  • Search for applications

  • View forms and drawings
  • View decision notices
  • Make comments on applications. (What kind of comments will be taken into account?)
  • View Appeals
  • See progress on applications
  • View a list of people/ organisations who have been consulted on applications

  • Online Maps of Pending Planning Applications (with find my nearest application, updated daily)

  • Weekly List of Planning Applications Received and Decided

  • View dates of Committee Meetings

  • View Enforcement Notices and cases

The information shown regarding planning applications is constantly updated during the day. However, some items attached to applications are not immediately available. Decision notices usually appear within 24-48 hours, and applications forms and plans within 5 working days.

Please Note: As the Local Planning Portal accesses live information, the service is unavailable while backups occur, between 04:00 and 07:30 every Tuesday.

In person:

Applications can also be viewed at Town Hall, Duke Street, Barrow in Furness during the hours 9am - 4pm Monday - Friday.
