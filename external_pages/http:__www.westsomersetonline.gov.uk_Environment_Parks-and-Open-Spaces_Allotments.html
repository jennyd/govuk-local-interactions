Allotments

West Somerset Council does not own any allotments.

All allotments in Minehead and Alcombe are administered by Minehead Town Council (Tel 01643 707213).
 

Other villages such as Dunster, Porlock and Stogursey administer their own allotments.

Please contact the relevant Town or Parish Council for more information.
