Online forms: Report an abandoned, unwanted or untaxed vehicle

This form has 3 pages

The Council can arrange the removal and disposal of unwanted vehicles for Rotherham residents at no charge. 'Vehicles' includes cars, motorbikes, small vans, trailers, small boats and caravans.

We will also arrange removal of abandoned or untaxed vehicles.

When reporting a vehicle please give the following information:

  • Location of vehicle
  • Make and model
  • Colour
  • Registration number
  • Expiry date of tax disc (if displayed)
