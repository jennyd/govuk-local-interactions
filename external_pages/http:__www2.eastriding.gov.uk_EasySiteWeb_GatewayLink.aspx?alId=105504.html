Request a replacement bin

Use this form to request the delivery/removal/repair of refuse wheeled bins from the council.

Households can request a second 240 litre blue bin for recyclables if one large blue bin collected fortnightly is not sufficient.

Only one standard size green bin is given per household. This is due to the wide range of materials that can be recycled in the blue and brown bins. If you still feel you are unable to cope despite making full use of the recycling bins, please contact wastewatchers@eastriding.gov.uk.

Only one brown bin is given per household.

Delivery will be within 10 working days of the request being made.

If you have requested a bin to be repaired or replaced, please ensure the bin is left in an accessible location for changing over.

Please provide accurate information when requesting new bins. Larger size bins will be removed if inaccurate information is found to have been supplied.

This form consists of 4 pages and will take approximately 4 minutes to complete. Fields marked with a * are mandatory and require completion.
