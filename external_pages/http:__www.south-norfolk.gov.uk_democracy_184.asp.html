Councillors

Summary

Councillors are elected to represent all residents of their ward - not just those who voted for them, and they receive allowances for the work they do. Details of all 46 councillors are given below.

Know your councillor!

These Councillors represent you and make decisions on your behalf. Make sure you know who represents you and how you can contact them - follow the links in our A-Z of Councillors below for their contact details.

The current leader of the Council John Fuller.

Find out what to do if you want to complain about the conduct of a Councillor.

You can also find out about Norfolk County Councillors, Members of Parliament and MEPs.
