Homelessness Advice and Information

If you think you are at risk of losing your home or are homeless , please contact us

Over the festive period it will close to the public at 3pm on Thursday 24 December and will reopen on Tuesday 29 December 2015 at 10am. It will also close to the public at 3pm on Thursday 31 December and reopen at 10am on Monday 4 January 2016.

The entrance to the customer contact centre is at the rear of the Civic Centre building, via the Civic Centre car park. If you are unable to use the stairs up to the Customer Contact Centre, please contact us on 01228 817200 or email customerservices@carlisle.gov.uk and we will arrange to meet you at the Carlisle Tourist Information Centre.

If you are experiencing homelessness and require emergency advice outside of office hours please ring 01228 817386 or email tammie.rhodes@carlisle.gov.uk for assistance.
