Resident Parking Permits

Birmingham has several residents parking schemes currently in operation across the city.
For more information including how much they cost and how to renew them, please read the information below.

All residents living within the permit scheme area (see application form for plan of exact area) can apply for a permit provided they can demonstrate they are a resident and own a vehicle registered at the same address. This is subject to limitations on the number of permits per household or per scheme.

To apply for a permit please call 0121 303 7616 or 0121 303 7617.

Resident permit schemes and costs

  • Inner Zone

    Annual resident permit for use in the pay and display bays - (from 5pm to 9am and all day Sunday) - £350

    Annual resident permit for use in the pay and display bays - (from 5pm to 9am and all day Saturday and Sunday) - £750

  • Jewellery Quarter (where applicable)

    All resident permits - £225

  • Gun Quarter (where applicable)

    All resident permits - £225

  • Outer Zone

    Annual resident permit for use in the pay and display bays - (from 5pm to 9am and all day Sunday) - £75

    Annual resident permit for use in the pay and display bays - (from 5pm to 9am and all day Saturday and Sunday) - £175

  • Outside of City Centre

    1st permit for each household - £16

    Additional permits £32 (subject to availability)

    Blue Badge holders resident at the property will qualify for the 1st permit free of charge. Any additional permits will be charged at £32 (subject to availability).

Frequently Asked Questions

What documentation is required?

You will need to provide the documents detailed below. It is necessary for the original documents to be seen (copies are not accepted). Do not send any documents or payment until you have received an application form.

  • Your completed application form and payment
  • Proof of vehicle ownership (V5 log book)
  • Proof of residency i.e. Council Tax/Utility bill

Can my visitors park in the resident permit area?

Visitor permits are available for some schemes at a cost of £5.50 for a book of 5 permits. Each permit lasts for 24 hours from midnight to midnight. To purchase a visitor permits book, please ring 0121 303 7616 or 0121 303 7617.

What if I change my vehicle?

If you change your vehicle you will need to replace your permit (unless your permit is for the Aston Villa match day scheme). You will need to complete a change of vehicle form and send us your current permit and supporting documentation in the same way as a new permit. Call us on 0121 303 7616 or 0121 303 717 for a change of vehicle form.

The charge for this service is £11.00

What if my permit is lost or stolen?

You will need to complete a replacement permit form and send us supporting documentation in the same way as a new permit. Call us on 0121 303 7616 or 0121 303 7617 for a replacement permit form.

There is a charge for this:

  • £11.00 if your permit is stolen (a police crime number will be required in this case)
  • £20.00 if you have lost your permit

How can I renew my permit?

You will usually be sent a renewal reminder letter about 4 weeks before your permit runs out.

How do I cancel my permit?

It may be possible to issue a refund if you need to cancel your permit. Please send your permit to us at:
