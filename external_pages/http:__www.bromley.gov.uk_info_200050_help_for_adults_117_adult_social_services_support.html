Adult social care support

At some point in our lives most of us will need help with an aspect of everyday living. This could be the result of an illness, a stay in hospital, a disability, or simply because we are getting older and need some support to carry on living independently. 

We provide information, advice and support for people for people who may need help to live as independently as possible and their carers. This includes adults who have

 

  • physical disabilities
  • sensory impairment
  • mental health needs and learning difficulties

We also provide details of organisations both voluntary and private, which support and care for people in the Bromley area.

You can also find out more about paying for care and guidance on whether you will receive any assistance from Bromley Council with any of the costs.
