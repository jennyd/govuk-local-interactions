Repairs and Maintenance

Your home

Homes in Sedgemoor is responsible for maintaining the structure of your home. This includes roofs, doors, windows and heating. As a responsible tenant, we ask that you report any minor repairs you find that may need attention. We also ask that you allow us access to your home to carry out needed repairs and servicing. In addition we also ask that you keep the interior of your property in an acceptable manner and maintain your own items such as light bulbs, showerheads and tap washers.

Reporting a repair to us

You can report your repair to us in a number of different ways. However, the fastest way is by phone, emailing or through our website. 

  •  Telephone 0800 585 360 or 01278 552400
  •  Email customer.services@homesinsedgemoor.org
  •  Visit www.homesinsedgemoor.org click on the 'report a repair' link on the front page and complete the online repairs form.

Write to us or visit us at our offices in Bridgwater House, King Square, Bridgwater, Somerset TA6 3AR.

Reporting emergency repairs

Our free phone number 0800 585 360 operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year and can be used to request emergency repairs. However, only emergency repairs should be reported outside of working hours, which are 8am to 6pm Monday to Friday.

How we will deal with your repair

Your repair will be put into one of three categories and responded to within the timeframes shown below.

• Emergency repairs - 24 hours

• Urgent repairs - 7 days

• Routine repairs - 28 days

Once you have requested your repair and it has been put in to one of three categories we will:

• Agree an appointment time that is convenient for you.

• Take into account if you or anyone in your home has a vulnerability or any special requirements.

• Aim to fix your repair on the first visit wherever this is possible.

Your repairs appointment

Our Customer Services Team will always try to book an appointment slot when you report the repair to us. You can request an AM or PM appointment slot and be selective about times. You can also request evening or Saturday morning appointments, but these slots can be limited.

It's not always possible to book an appointment straight away, especially when works will take longer to complete and may require special materials. In these cases our contractor will arrange an appointment with you directly.

If you book an appointment that you later realize you can't keep, or you no longer need, please let us know as soon as possible.

After your repair

If you have something to say about your repair, are happy with how it has been dealt with or are perhaps unhappy with the outcome, remember we want to hear your feedback. You can provide this to us using the same methods you would to report a repair to us.
