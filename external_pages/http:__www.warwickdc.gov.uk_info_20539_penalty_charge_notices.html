Penalty Charge Notices

Please ensure you pay or challenge with the correct authority.


On street PCNs

Warwickshire County Council now issues all on street Penalty Charge Notices. If the heading on your PCN looks like this go to the on street PCN page on the county council website. These PCNs all have a prefix of ZQ.


Car park PCNs

Warwick District Council issues Penalty Charge Notices for car parks only. If the heading on your PCN looks like this use the links below. These PCNs have a prefix of WQ.


If you have been issued a PCN in one of our car parks please do not ignore it. Upon receipt of a PCN, there are 2 options available â€“ pay or challenge.

  • Pay a car parks Penalty Charge Notice
  • Challenge a car parks Penalty Charge Notice

The Penalty Charge will be reduced by a discount of 50% if payment is made no later than the last date of the period of 14 days beginning with the date on which the PCN was served. If payment is received after 14 days the full amount will become due.

Penalty Charge Notices (PCNs) will be issued to a vehicle parked within Warwick District Council car parks in Leamington Spa, Warwick and Kenilworth if

  • A valid ticket, season ticket or permit is not clearly displayed
  • Your ticket has expired
  • Your vehicle is parked in a disabled/blue badge holder space without displaying a valid blue badge
  • Your vehicle is not parked wholly within a marked bay
  • Your vehicle is parked on double yellow lines, hatched lines or in a reserved space
  • Your ticket has been purchased from another car park
  • Any other contravention as specified in the Off-Street Parking Places Order
