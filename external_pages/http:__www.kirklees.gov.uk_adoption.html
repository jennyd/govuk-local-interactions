Adoption

Adoption Service (adoption@kirklees.gov.uk) Last updated - July 2015

Adoption is one of the best ways of providing a permanent family for children who, for various reasons, cannot be brought up by their birth parents.

Thinking about adopting?

At the moment we are not looking for families who wish to adopt a younger child under two. If you are thinking about over two's please get in touch otherwise we advise you contact First4Adoption.

  • www.first4adoption.org.uk

We are encouraging applications from potential adopters who could offer a home to children with complex backgrounds, medical needs, additional health/development needs, over two’s or sibling groups, as these children wait the longest to be matched with families.

We have been very successful in matching families with children given we have recruited so many adopters. The number of children in need of adoption is very low just now and we need to make you aware, whilst we can approve you quite quickly, it is expected you will wait to be matched with a child.

This situation is likely to change over time so please do get in touch if you would like some more information or advice.

Choosing to adopt could be one of the most important, life-changing decisions you ever make. At Kirklees, our experienced, dedicated adoption team has been successful in helping hundreds of people to become adoptive parents. We are here to provide guidance and advice throughout your adoption journey; from deciding whether adoption is right for you, through to supporting you and your family, post-adoption.

As well as being hugely rewarding for a parent, the benefits of adoption to a child are immense. But don’t just take our word for it; by clicking on our links, you can find out more about adoption, including inspirational stories from parents who have adopted, and about some of the children in Kirklees who are waiting to be adopted.

You can also get in touch with one of our friendly adoption team via the initial contact form, who are happy to answer your questions

    or please call us on 0800 028 3001

Our adoption information booklet and support guide are here for you to download and read at your leisure. If you would also like a hard copy of these please ask the team when you call.
