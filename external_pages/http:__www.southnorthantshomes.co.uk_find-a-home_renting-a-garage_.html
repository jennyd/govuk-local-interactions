Renting a Garage 

Garage Rent Costs

SNH have a number of garages available to let throughout the South Northants District at competitive rates.

£9.30 per week for SNH tenants

£11.16 per week for non SNH tenants

Please note some areas may have a waiting list. If you would like further information about a particular garage or location please contact the Allocations Team via email or phone 08454 606 888.

Anyone can apply to rent a garage but priority is given to SNH tenants, as long as all their SNH accounts are paid up to date.

Garages are for the garaging of a vehicle and should not be used as a store, workshop or for business purposes.

Ending Your Garage Tenancy 

If you would like to give up your garage you will need to give 4 weeks notice. To do this you will need to use the Termination of garage tenancy form below. More information can be found on this form or you can contact the Allocations Team on 08454 606 888 or via email
