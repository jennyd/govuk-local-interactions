Dog Warden Service

Reporting lost or found dogs

Wokingham residents are supported by a dedicated Animal Warden - more information is available on the Wokingham Borough Council website.

West Berkshire residents are supported by our partner organisation, SDK Environmental, which delivers our dog warden services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. 

Please note that the information below is for West Berkshire residents only.

If you've found a dog

For dog collections, please contact SDK Environmental on 03444 828344 during office hours, or 08444 828346 at other times.

  • The warden will arrange for the dog to be kennelled while we try and find the owner - if we can't do that within seven days, it will be re-homed.

All our found dogs are listed on our partner website..

If you've lost a dog

Please contact SDK Environmental on 03444 828300 during office hours to make any necessary payments and to be re-united with your dog.
You might also want to register your dog's details online to help get re-united - found dogs are also listed there.

Dog owners please note that:

  • you are legally required to ensure your dog/s have identification
  • dogs will be kennelled for up to seven days at a cost of £14.40 per day
  • upon collection of the dog from the kennels, a seizure fee of £50 will be payable in addition to kennelling costs incurred before the dog is released back to you
  • the seizure fee will be £95 if the dog is collected out of office hours
  • dogs not claimed after seven days are re-homed

We operate a non-destruction policy, so healthy dogs are never destroyed unless they present a danger to the public.

Please note that our Dog Warden cannot collect stray dogs on private land without the landowner's permission.
