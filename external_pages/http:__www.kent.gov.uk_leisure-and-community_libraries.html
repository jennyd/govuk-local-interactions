Libraries

  • Libraries consultation

    The latest information on the transformation of library, registration and archive services following the 12-week public consultation.

  • Find a library

    Find your local library and check its opening times and facilities or find out when and where our mobile libraries stop.

  • Renew or reserve a book

    Find out how to renew your library books; online, using our mobile app, over the phone or in person. You can also reserve items.

  • Online reference library

    Access to over 50 free online subscriptions including encyclopedias, newspapers and eLearning resources with your library card.

  • eBooks and audiobooks

    Browse the catalogue, check which devices are compatible and borrow eBooks and audiobooks free of charge.

  • Log in to your library account

    Log in using your library card number and PIN to check your account, renew your books or browse the library catalogue.

  • Find a book

    Search the library catalogue to find a book, CD, DVD or other item in any of our libraries or partner libraries.

  • Join the library

    Get free access to over 2 million books, eBooks, audiobooks and online resources by joining the library.

  • Computer and internet access

    Book a library computer with your library card and PIN. Find out about free online courses and get help with using computers.

  • Clubs and activities in libraries

    Meet new people and make new friends at one of our clubs or activities. Search our database to find clubs and societies in Kent.

  • Ask a Kent Librarian

    If you need help with research, homework or a library service our librarians are here to answer your questions.

  • Specialist library services

    For people with specific needs, such as music and drama collections for performing arts groups and services for disabled people.

  • Statistics for libraries, registrations and archives

    Usage statistics and how the Libraries, Registration and Archive service is inspiring and supporting the people of Kent.
