Parking permits overview

We offer a variety of permits for residents, businesses and visitors. 

View available parking permits

When you click on a permit it will display a list of car parks where the permit is valid, where you can buy the permit and the cost. To find out where each car park is, you can click on the name of the car park and it will display a map showing its location.

You can buy permits:

  • over the counter at one of the office listed on the permit page (you can pay in cash at Lynton House, all other outlets only accept credit or debit cards and cheques)
  • over the phone - call us on 01271 388279. Please have your debit or credit card ready. We can post the permit to you or you can collect it
  • in writing to Customer Services, Lynton House, Commercial Road, Barnstaple, EX31 1DG. Please include your payment. You can ask for the permit to be posted back to you or you can collect it

Unfortunately we don't currently have the facilities to take payments for permits online.

Please provide:

  • full name
  • postal address
  • telephone number
  • type of permit required
  • start date of the permit

You need to provide additional information for local residents permits, the detail of which is listed on the relevant permit page.

For more information about out parking permits, email customerservices@northdevon.gov.uk or call 01271 388279.
