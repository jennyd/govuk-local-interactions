About Walsall FIS

Hi

Welcome to the Walsall Families Information Service website.

If you are parent or carer, or a professional working with a family, then please click on the Parents link above. In this part of the website is lots of information, details of services and other sources of help that are aimed at families.

If you work in childcare, then please click on the Providers link - this part of the website is just for you! There is information relating to working in childcare, running your business, Ofsted, etc. If you are looking for a course, then please book online via the Courses page.

If you have any problems, or prefer to speak to someone, please go to Contact Us for our details.

Thanks

Walsall FIS
