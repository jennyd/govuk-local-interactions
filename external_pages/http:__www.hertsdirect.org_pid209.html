Health and Community Services online self referral



Find the help you need.

We will help you find services or support so that you can stay independent.

By answering a few questions about your situation we can guide you to the help you need. This could be equipment, other organisations or services. This will take 20 minutes to complete and you will need an email address to do this.

If the information you give suggests that you need an assessment from social care staff, we will ask for your details so that we can contact you.
