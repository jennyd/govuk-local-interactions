Youth Bank (Buckinghamshire)

Youthbank awards grants to young people's projects, which will make a difference in their community.

Do you want to be part of the team giving out money to young people? We are looking for young people to be on the award panel to meet and consider the grant applications and award grants. 

We give grants to local groups of young people or individuals aged 13 to19 years. Funding can be for new projects that young people want to start or existing projects they want to develop - you make the decisions.

What do we do?

Funded projects include: dance, drama, music workshops, community safety, volunteering, drugs awareness, independent living, special music events.

We also regularly do fundraising events and activities to add to the funds we can distribute.

How to get involved

We are always looking for new people to be involved. For young people aged 12 to 19 (up to 25 if you have a disability). Give us a shout to find out more.  

How to apply for Funding

To apply for funding complete both the Youth Bank Application Form & Youth Bank Sponsor Form. For grants over £3000 a presentation to the panel will be required. 

For more information call 01296 382583 or email yourvoicecounts@buckscc.gov.uk
