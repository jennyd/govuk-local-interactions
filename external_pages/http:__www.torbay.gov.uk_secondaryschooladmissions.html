Secondary School Admissions

  • Apply - CAF1


If your child was born between 1st September 2004 and 31st August 2005 and you have not yet applied for a place at secondary school, please do so now.
The online application process is no longer available as the closing date has passed but you can download the Common Application Form CAF 1 from this page. You can save it, complete it electronically and email it to pupil.services@torbay.gov.uk. Alternatively, you can download and print the form, complete it and take it to your child’s school or your local Connections Office. Remember to keep a copy for yourself and ask the school or Connections staff to date stamp this copy. Alternatively you can send it by post to Student Services, 4th Floor South, Tor Hill House c/o Town Hall, Castle Circus, Torquay TQ1 3DR. You can also get a paper version of the form from the Connections office or from Student Services, tel 01803 208908.
Before applying you should read the TIPS 8 booklet, which is available to download below. This contains details of the application process. The secondary supplement contains the admission criteria for each secondary school. Some schools have supplementary forms that will also need to be completed. You can download these forms below. School staff members can also download form SUPP1 if you are applying for a place at your school (this is only relevant for some schools – please see admission policy for the school). No matter what other forms you complete, you will also need to complete the Common Application Form.
Because your application is late it may not be processed until all the on-time applications have been processed. Please see booklet TIPS 8 for further information about this.
