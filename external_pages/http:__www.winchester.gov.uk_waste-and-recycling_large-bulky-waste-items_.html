Bulky waste items

Listen

Our contractors, Biffa provide a chargeable bulky waste collection service for getting rid of large household items which can't be disposed of through the domestic waste collections. There charges for this service can be seen in the table below.

Items can include:

  • Furniture
  • Televisions
  • Washing machines
  • Household fridges/freezers
  • Cookers
  • Mattresses (please keep covered and dry)
  • Carpets 

Please note: Unfortunately due to a regulation change at Hampshire County Council Transfer Stations, we are now unable to collect commercial fridges/freezers. Residents that require commercial fridge/freezer removal will have to seek an alternative company to dispose of these items.

You can book and pay for a collection by calling Biffa directly on 01962 670 706. Collections are carried out on Monday, Wednesday and Friday subject to availability in the Winchester District.

You can also dispose of large bulky items yourself, including white goods, at a HCC Household Waste Recycling Centre.
