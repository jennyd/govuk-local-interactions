Hazardous waste

Asbestos

We no longer collect asbestos so you will need to organise the disposal of it through a licensed waste management service or you can take it to the Nottinghamshire County Council facility at Freeth Street in Nottingham.  You should contact Nottinghamshire County Council on 0300 500 80 80 to make arrangements before visiting this site with asbestos.

Visit the government website for more information on asbestos.

Plasterboard

We cannot dispose of plasterboard so please take it to Calverton Household Waste Recycling Centre on Hollinwood Lane, NG14 6NR or contact Nottinghamshire County Council who will advise where else it can be disposed of.
