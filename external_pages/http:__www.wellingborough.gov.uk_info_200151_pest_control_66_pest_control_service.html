Pest control service

The council will provide information and advice on the control of pests.

Information on a range of pests is available to download, but a treatment service is only available for rats and mice.

A treatment for the eradication of rats and mice at a property are as follows:

Charge inclusive of materials £73.20

If it can be established at the first visit that a treatment is not required we will refund the fees minus an £18.48 charge

Council tax & housing benefit claimants - free of charge

No service is provided for commercial premises.

To request a treatment please contact Environmental Protection on 01933 231960 during office hours
