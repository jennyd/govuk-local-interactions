Blue Badge parking spaces

We have plenty of disabled parking spaces across the borough and Blue Badge holders can park for free in pay and display car parks as well as using bays marked for disabled people at:

  • A34 Stratford Road (service roads between Olton Road and Solihull Road)
  • A34 Stratford Road (service roads between the Saracen’s Head pub and Haslucks Green Road)
  • Balsall Common
    • Car park (behind the Library)
  • Solihull
    • Brueton Park car park
    • Church Hill Road multi-storey car-park
    • Dominion Court multi-storey car park
    • Drury Lane
    • Hobs Moat Road (service roads north and south of Ulleries Road)
    • Lode Lane multi storey car park
    • Malvern Park car park
    • Mell Square multi-storey car park
    • Mill Lane (Mell Square end)
  • Kingshurst
  • Marston Drive, outside the Library
  • Knowle
    • Near Tesco supermarket
    • Behind the Library
  • Marston Green
    • Station Road, Marston Green (service road outside Post Office)
    • Station Road, Marston Green (lay-by outside shops)
  • Shirley
    • Asda, Parkgate, Stratford Road, Shirley, Solihull, B90 3GG
    • designated parking bays along Stratford Road
