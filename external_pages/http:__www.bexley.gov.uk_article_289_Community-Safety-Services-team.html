Community Safety Services team

The team works closely with police and other organisations to tackle anti social behaviour.

While the team do not provide support for victims they continue to take action against offenders. If you are a victim of anti social behaviour please contact your local Police Safer Neighbourhoods Team or, if you occupy a property belonging to a Registered Social Landlord, your housing officer.

You can record incidents of anti social behaviour on diary sheets which will be used to assist with evidence gathering against perpetrators. This is available as a PDF under related downloads.
