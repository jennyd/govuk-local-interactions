Councillors

Wandsworth has 60 councillors (sometimes called members) who are elected to represent local people in the council's decision-making process.

Three councillors represent the interests of people in each of Wandsworth's 20 local areas or 'wards'.


Find a councillor

You can view:

  • direct email addresses, and
  • appointments
