Purbeck district councillors 2015/16

Listen

Purbeck District Council has 25 councillors, representing 13 wards.

Political composition

Following the district elections in May 2015, the council is made up of 20 Conservative councillors, 4 Liberal Democrat councillors and 1 Independent councillor. Therefore, the Conservative party has an overall majority for the 2015/16 municipal year.

Read more about the political composition of the council.

The next district council election will take place in May 2019.
