Pest control services

Rats or Mice

  • Report rats or mice  in a domestic property
  • Report rats or mice in a commercial property

Cost: Domestic removal is free, because rats and mice are considered a risk to public health.

Cost: Commercial contracts are available, the council can provide a free site survey and quote. For other commercial treatments there is an hourly charge for labour and transport of £63.80 (min charge 1 hour) + VAT, plus a charge for the amount of poisons used.

Wasps

  • Report a wasp's nest in a domestic property
  • Report a wasp's nest in a commercial property

Cost: Domestic removal £36.40.

Cost: Commercial contracts are available, the council can provide a free site survey and quote. A charge for the treatment of a commercial wasp's nest is £36.40.  For other commercial treatments there is an hourly charge for labour and transport of £63.80 (min charge 1 hour) + VAT, plus a charge for the amount of poisons used.

Moles

If you're having trouble with moles, we can visit your property to assess the extent of the problem and give you an estimate to carry out works to eradicate them if you wish.

For further information please contact (01282) 661199 who will pass your details to a pest control officer or email environmental.health@pendle.gov.uk
