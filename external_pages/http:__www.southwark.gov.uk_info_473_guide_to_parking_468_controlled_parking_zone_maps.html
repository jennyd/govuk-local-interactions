Parking maps

Interactive parking map

Finding a space to park in London, whether you are a visitor or a resident, is never simple but this page might just assist you in Southwark.

Our interactive Southwark parking maps can make finding a space that little bit easier.

Launch the full-screen version of Southwark parking maps.

With Southwark parking maps you can:

  • see the location and type of every parking bay within all of our parking zones
  • click on a bay to find out more information about how and when it operates
  • report a fault with a bay, for example if a sign is missing or unclear
  • add additional map overlays such as traffic cameras, congestion zone, public carparks or Google streetview
  • print your own tailor-made map or send a link to your visitors  

Please note that whilst we make every effort to ensure that the maps are up to date they may vary from the actual on street situation. Motorists are reminded that it is their responsibility to check local signs and lines before they park.

You can also download area-wide parking and loading guides for Rye Lane and Walworth Road.  

You can also download a spreadsheet that contains the location of all the bays shown on the above map with their respective coordinates.
