You have tried to find a page that either: does not exist any more,  has been renamed, or possibly moved to a new section.

Why have we moved things around?

We know this is very annoying for individuals who have existing bookmarks or are familiar with the old site,  We have not carried out a restructure lightly,  based upon real statistics of how the old site was used we now prioritise the top most used services. We also wanted to make your browsing less confusing by keeping all services together in the same section as much as possible, avoiding being bounced around multi sections.   A description of how the site is structured now is displayed on this page.. 

How to use this page.

  • Closest match: if we can find a similar page for your query we will display a link to any pages that match;
  • Search: use the excellent search tool in the header to find the service you want:
  • A to Z: use the A-Z tool bottom of this page to find services by a letter.


We firmly believe that once you begin to use our site you will agree that is easier to find and remain within those important services.  However, If you think something is really missing , email webmaster@carlisle.gov.uk  and we’ll take a look.

How the new site is structured
