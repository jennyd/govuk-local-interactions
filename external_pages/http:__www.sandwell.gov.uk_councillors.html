    Vacancies on the Council’s Independent Remuneration Panel

    We have vacancies for someone with an active interest in local affairs and local government, to serve on the Council’s Independent Remuneration Panel.

    The Panel’s role is to make recommendations to the Council in respect of allowances to be paid to Councillors.

    If you would like to know more about the position, please contact the Council’s Members Services Assistant on 0121 569 3043 or email Robert_Hevican@sandwell.gov.uk 

    You can also download an application form, together with full details of the skills and criteria required for this post.

    The closing date for all applications is 2 October 2015.
