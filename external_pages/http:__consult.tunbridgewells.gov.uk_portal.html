Who Said What?

What are you looking for

Welcome to Tunbridge Wells Borough Council's Consultation Portal

Listed within this welcome page are all consultation events which are being carried out by Tunbridge Wells Borough Council. These can be filtered by selecting an option from the 'Show Consultations for' box below.

Consultations can take the form of documents, questionnaires/surveys or forums on a given topic. You can search the entire consultation Portal using 'Search' box above. You can also search for Comments, Consultees and Agents by clicking on 'Who Said What?' above.

In some instances, you may be asked to register or log in before being able to participate in a consultation event. To register please click on the 'Register' link within the 'Login' section.

Representations can be made against a chapter, section, policy or even on individual numbered paragraphs (depending on the document under consultation).
