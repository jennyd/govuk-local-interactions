Help with your garden

Garden Tidy Scheme

We can assist elderly and disabled tenants who are struggling to look after their gardens, but have no relatives or neighbours who can help out.

If you are, or know someone who may need a helping hand maintaining gardens, please contact us. If eligible, we will add you to our scheme and cut your gardens and hedges once a year.
