Popular services in this section


Pay or appeal parking fines

If you pay within 14 days of it being issued, you'll be able to pay it at the reduced rate of 50%.



Roadworks

Find out what roadworks are going on in the borough and if any affect you.



Public transport

Plan your journey on public transport with timetables, routes and ticket price information.



Find a car park

Details of on-street parking and car parks.  



Report street lights, potholes...

Report street lighting, potholes, problems with pavements, manhole covers, spillages and more.



Gritting and winter service

Keep up-to-date with services over the winter period. You can also subscribe to keep up-to-date with the latest changes.


More services


Apply for a dropped kerb

Planning permission is not usually required. Find details of how to apply including what to consider before applying.



Traffic Regulation Orders

View restrictions to manage traffic.



Rights of way

Public rights of way are open to everyone. They can be roads, paths or tracks, and can run through towns, countryside or private property.



Apply for a resident parking permit

Parking permits ensure residents who don't have access to off street parking can park their vehicles close to their houses.



Concessionary travel pass

Free off-peak travel on buses, trains and trams for older people.



Blue Badges

If you meet the qualifying criteria, you may be exempt from certain parking regulations.



Road safety

Report road problems and help us make our roads safer. 



Street naming and numbering register

Make a request to rename or number a property or street.



Dead animal removal

If you find a dead animal in a public area, let us know and we'll arrange to remove it.



Temporary Traffic Regulation Orders and traffic management

Apply for the use of traffic management controls.
