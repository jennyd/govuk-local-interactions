Local Transport Plan

The third Greater Manchester Local Transport Plan is a statutory document prepared under the Local Transport Act 2008. It is a 5-year strategy for the management, maintenance, development and monitoring of the County's transport system and is put together by Transport for Greater Manchester (TfGM) in co-operation with the ten City and Metropolitan Borough Councils and with the input of local stakeholders. It provides a basis for bids to Central Government for the resources needed to implement the strategy.

The Third Local Transport Plan (LTP)  which was submitted in March 2011, covers the period up to 2011/12 to 2015/16. Progress on the implementation of the Plan will be extensively monitored.

To see the plan in full and the supporting documents visit the Third Local Transport Plan page on the TfGM website
