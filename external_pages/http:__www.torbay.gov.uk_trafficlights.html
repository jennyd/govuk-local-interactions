Traffic Lights

Traffic lights may be installed:
  • Because of possible traffic hazard
  • To manage traffic flow from new developments, schools, leisure centres. (In some cases developers will finance new signals)

Fault Reporting

Report - Highways Faults

Please be specific about the problem.  A list of possible faults is shown below:
  • All lamps out
  • Red to traffic lamp failed
  • Amber to traffic lamp failed
  • Green to traffic lamp failed
  • Green filter arrow lamp failed
  • Red man lamp failed
  • Green man lamp failed
  • Wait lamp failed
  • Long delays (specify which approach)
  • Permanent pedestrian demand (changing to pedestrians all the time when nobody is there)
  • Signal head alignment - Head turned by vehicle strike or vandals
  • Miscellaneous vandal damage (please specify)
  • Timing fault, such as short greens or long greens to approaches where there is no traffic)
  • Lamps dim
  • Pole leaning
  • Other fault (please specify)

Temporary Traffic Lights

In order to use temporary traffic lights within Torbay (Accredited Contractors only) you must notify the Council.
