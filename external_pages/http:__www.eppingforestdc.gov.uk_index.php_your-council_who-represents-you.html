Who represents you?

Epping Forest District Council has 58 elected members who each represent a ward area.

They are elected by thirds which means 3 years out of 4 there will be a third of the seats on the district council up for election. In the fourth year the Essex County Council elections are held. It is their responsibility to represent you. You can see them in action from ourÂ Watch the Council page.
