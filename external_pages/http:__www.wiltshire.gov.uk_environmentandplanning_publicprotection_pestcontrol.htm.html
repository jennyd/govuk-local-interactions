Pest Control

Customer notice

We are currently updating our website with a cleaner, mobile-friendly design. This page will be updated with the new design soon. Thank you for your patience.

Please tell us what you think about our website, we welcome your feedback.

We offer a high quality pest control service. Officers will visit you and carry out a full assessment of your pest problem. Our fees include all materials needed to carry out the treatment required, we do not have hidden costs. We can also offer you free advice as part of your treatment to prevent further pest infestations

In a customer survey carried out during June and July 2014, 98.4% of people using the pest control services said that officers were knowledgeable and that 97.9% would recommend our pest control service to others.

All officers will visit you wearing a Wiltshire Council uniform and carrying identification. Our officers are highly experienced and all have a BPCA/RSPH (Level 2) Certificate in Pest Control or the equivalent. If you would like a visit from a member of the pest control team, please contact us.
