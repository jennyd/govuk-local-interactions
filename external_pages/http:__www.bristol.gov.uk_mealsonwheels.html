Meals on wheels from Bristol Community Meals

About the service

We deliver hot, tasty and nutritious meals to anyone in the Bristol area. Our caring, friendly team also carry out vital welfare checks while we are there.

Who can use the service

Anyone can apply to receive our mobile meals and all ages can apply for the service. You don't need a referral from social services or your GP.

How to request the service

Arrange meals delivery for yourself or for a relative, friend or neighbour. You can start receiving them the following day.

Arrange a hot meals delivery service

or call the Bristol Community Meals team on 0117 903 1522 and answer a short questionnaire over the phone.

If you have questions or need advice please contact us.

Details of the service

  • We deliver a hot meal between 11am and 2pm seven days a week, which is ready to eat straight away. 
  • If needed, we can deliver a teatime meal - sandwiches or salad with a cake - at the same time, for later that day.
  • You can choose from a wide menu offering a range of choices.
  • Most diets can be accommodated, including diabetic, vegetarian, low fat, and gluten free. We also cater for religious and cultural beliefs. Any particular dislikes can also be removed from your personal menu.
  • The person delivering your meal will encourage you to eat and can help with small tasks such as fetching cutlery, removing lids and cutting up meals. We'll also check that you're comfortable and secure. We do this every time we visit, and if there is a problem we can contact your family or your doctor if you need them.

How much does the service cost

  • Hot meals including pudding £4.90.
  • Tea meal £2.91.

We'll send you, or the person paying, an invoice for payment every month.

If you're not going to be in

If you’re going out, let us know by calling 0117 9031520. If you phone before 9.30am we can cancel your meal for the day without charge.

Please always call us to let us know - if our drivers can't get an answer, they will contact others to check you're ok.

Our driver will not leave your meal on the doorstep for you to reheat later - they must see you when they call to check that you're well and eating the food hot. If you allow it to cool and then reheat, you are at risk of food poisoning.
