Request an assessment of needs

A care assessment with a care worker will establish:

  • your needs and the support and services you require

  • the needs, support and services required by anyone who supports you in your day-to-day life

  • whether you meet the eligibility criteria for care services

  • whether you have savings and capital over £23,250

Before requesting an assessment of needs, have you checked the eligibility criteria and paying for care services information?

Online

Request an assessment of needs using our enquiry form
