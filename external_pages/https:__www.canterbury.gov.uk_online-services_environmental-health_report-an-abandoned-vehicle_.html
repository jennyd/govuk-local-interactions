Abandoned vehicle report form

If a car appears to have been abandoned for longer than a month, then you can report this to us to us and we will investigate. 

If there is an emergency, for example spilt petrol or live electrical wires call the Police on 101.

For security reasons you have 60 minutes to complete each page of this form. On completion you will receive a confirmation email.
