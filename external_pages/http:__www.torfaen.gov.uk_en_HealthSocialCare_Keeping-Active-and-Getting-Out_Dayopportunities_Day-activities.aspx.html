Day activities

In Torfaen, we believe that people who use social services are individuals who have the right to decide how they spend each day.

Our day activities programme offers employment training schemes, educational opportunities, leisure, sports and social activities.

For those with high level needs, there is one to one support to help people engage in the activities they choose.

The information contained in this folder covers just some of the activities that our service users are currently enjoying.

If you want to know more about any of Torfaen’s day activities, please talk to your Care Manager or contact the Principal Officer for Service Provision and Development on 01495 742261.
