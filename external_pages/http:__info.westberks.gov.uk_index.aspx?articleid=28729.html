Paying for your Care

Not all social care is funded by West Berkshire Council.  Sometimes an individual sources and with no involvement from ourselves, or the National Health Service may fund care depending on individual circumstances. 

When we do provide care, this is because someone meets the eligibility for care services - we'll financially assess someone under these circumstances to see if they are required to pay, and how much.
