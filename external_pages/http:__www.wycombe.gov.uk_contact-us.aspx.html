Find out online

You can use My Wycombe to look up an address and find your

  • Bin collection dates
  • Council tax band and annual charge
  • Local councillors
  • Parish and ward
  • Planning applications and planning history

My Wycombe offers information and maps about council services and community facilities in your local area.

How to contact us

For service contact details please see our A to Z of services.

If you can't find a relevant service contact, you can:

  • Use our online contact form
  • Email: info@wycombe.gov.uk
  • Phone: 01494 461 000
  • Visit: How to find us
  • Write: Wycombe District Council, Queen Victoria Road, High Wycombe, HP11 1BB

Council tax and benefits 24-hour telephone line

We offer a 24-hour telephone line for council tax and benefits that allows you to contact us when it's convenient with you.

For council tax call: 01494 412 226 or for housing benefit call: 01494 412 227 then use the following shortcut keys for the service you want:

  1. Make a payment
  2. Set up a direct debit
  3. Difficulty making a payment
  4. You've received a reminder, a final notice, a summons, a liability order notice, a special arrangement reminder or a bailiff's notice
  5. Report a change of address
  6. Information on single person's discount
  7. Other council tax enquiries including, exemptions, changes of circumstances, or you believe your bill is incorrect
  8. Information about housing or council tax benefit and reporting a suspected fraud
  9. Request a benefit application form

Service organisation charts
