Free childcare places for 2, 3 and 4's

All three and four year olds are entitled to up to 15 hours of free early education a week during term times as well as 40% of two year olds. Don't let your child miss out on their free early education place! More info on 3 and 4s | More info on 2s 

Primary school applications

If your child was born between 1st September 2011 and 31st August 2012 you will need to apply for your child's school place for September 2016. You can apply for your child's place online now. The deadline for online applications is Friday 15th Janaury 2016. More info here..

Applying for secondary school

If your child was born between 1st September 2004 & 31st August 2005 you will need to apply for your child's secondary school place for September 206. You can apply for your child's place online now. The deadline for online applications is Saturday 31st October 2015. More info here...

Local SEND Offer | Redbridge

The Redbridge Local SEND Offer is now online! If you are a parent/carer of a child with a special educational need or disability (SEND) then please visit the Informed Families pages - which has details of all services and support available to children and young people with SEND. More info...

Worried about a child's welfare?

If you have concerns about a child's welfare or suspect that a child is being neglected, abused or sexually exploited, please contact the Child Protection and Assessment Team.  More info...
