Camden schools which are not following the above 2016-17 term dates

Eight secondary schools and 13 primary schools have told the Council that they have set different term dates for 2016-17. Those schools which have informed us of this are listed below – please check their websites for details. If in doubt about holiday dates, please check with your child’s school. The lists below are not necessarily comprehensive i.e. other schools may have set different dates but not yet told us.

Secondary schools which have told us they are following different 2016-17 term dates: Haverstock, Hampstead, Maria Fidelis, Acland Burghley, Parliament Hill, La Sainte Union, William Ellis and Regent High School.

Primary schools which have said they are setting different 2016-17 term dates: Abacus, Beckford, Christ Church NW1, Eleanor Palmer, Fitzjohn's, Hampstead Parochial, Hawley, Holy Trinity and St Silas, Kingsgate, St Dominic's, St George the Martyr, St Michael's and St Paul's.
