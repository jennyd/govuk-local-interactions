Garden maintenance

We operate a Garden Maintenance scheme to help people with their gardens, if you have a condition which makes you physically unable to look after your garden yourself and you do not have any family or friends within the High Peak who could help.Â 

To find out if you qualify for assistance, contact your Neighbourhood Co-ordinator for a leaflet and an application form, call 0845 129 8075, click on ''Meet The Team'' or email us at enquiries@highpeak.gov.uk
