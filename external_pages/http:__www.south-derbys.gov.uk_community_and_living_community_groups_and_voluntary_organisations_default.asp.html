The Derbyshire Directory

The Derbyshire Directory is a 'one-stop shop' of information about thousands of local community clubs, organisations, charities and care services and can direct you to regional and national contacts where a local one is not available.

Community groups

An online directory of voluntary groups, organisations, social clubs, charities, social enterprises all based in - or working within - Derby and Derbyshire can be found here:  http://www.communitydirectoryderbyshire.org.uk/
