Welcome to our new website

We've improved the site so you can easily:

  • Report things to us - like dog fouling, graffiti and fly tipping
  • Pay for things - like your Council Tax, business rates and fitness class bookings
  • Request things - like a new wheelie bin or extra recycling containers

We've also improved the information on our website to make it easier for you to find what you're looking for. 

Please take some time to look around the site and see what you can now do online with us.
