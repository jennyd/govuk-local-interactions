Getting Started

Are you completing this assessment on behalf of yourself or someone else? (Required)

As you are completing this assessment on behalf of someone else please answer the questions from their viewpoint.

This postcode is currently out of our catchment area. Please contact the Local Authority which covers your postcode area to see if you may be eligible for social care funding.

Supporting You in Explaining Your Situation

Do you have communication difficulties?

Communication may be limited by physical disability, learning disability or sensory impairment.

Do you have any difficulties with understanding and/or retaining information?

This may include remembering or recalling information.

Do you have any difficulties making decisions and/or understanding their impact?

There is no limit to the amount of words you can use but please be as brief as possible

Based on your answers it may be beneficial to complete the assessment with an advocate/carer present. Visit http://sid.southampton.gov.uk/advocacy if you need help finding advocacy support.

Safeguarding

Do you have any concerns about how others treat you?

Communication may be be limited by physical disability, learning disability or sensory impairment.
