Bulky Rubbish

There are three different types of collection, Standard, Special & White Goods. Items need to be placed in your front or back garden or on your driveway from 6:30am. Electrical goods and furniture must be kept dry and be accessible throughout the day for us to collect until about 5:00pm. We are sorry but we cannot collect items that are left out in rear alleyways and we are unable to arrive at a specified time.

If you want the items collected from the back of your property please ensure our crew can get along the rear access and it is not overgrown. The crew have a key to open alleygates.

Standard Collection 
£15 for 3 Items (+£5 per any other item)

Cancel your Bulky Collection

Cancel your appointment, by emailing contactcentre@sthelens.gov.uk. Please give us at least 2 workings days' notice as we usually plan collections at least 24 hours in advance.

Large Items
Please make sure items are not bigger than 6ft x 4ft or they will not fit in the back of the vehicle and may not be collected. You will need to dismantle or break into manageable pieces heavy settees that cannot be lifted by 2 people into the back of the wagon before we can collect it.
Please Note
We do not carry out house clearances or collect from business premises and we reserve the right to contact you about large amounts. If you have any questions about this service please phone the Contact Centre on 01744 676789 for advice.
