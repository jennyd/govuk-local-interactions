Home meals service

Pages in Home meals service

  1. Home meals service
  1. Additional home meals providers

Coventry's home meals services is delivered by the ICare group.

The group serves more than 6,000 people a day across the country and provides hot meals that cater for special dietary needs and cultural and religious requirements delivered to your door.

It means that for the first time people who do not meet the council's home meals eligibility criteria will be able to access the service privately.

For more information, download the ICare brochure, visit the ICare website, telephone 0121 233 2973 or e-mail coventrymeals@icaregroup.co.uk. Opening times are 8.30am to 4.30pm. Out of hours please leave a message and an ICare representative will get back to you.

If you wish to have an assessment to see if you are eligible for Home Meals please contact Adult Social Care Direct for an initial telephone contact assessment.
