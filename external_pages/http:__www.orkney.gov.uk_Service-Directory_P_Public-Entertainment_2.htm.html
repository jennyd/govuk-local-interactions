Public Entertainment Licence

A Public Entertainment Licence under the Civic Government (Scotland) Act 1982 is required for the use of premises as a place of public entertainment. This means any place where members of the public are admitted or may use any facilities for the purposes of entertainment or recreation.

You can find all the information you will require to apply for a Public Entertainment Licence in the 'Related Downloads' section of this page.
