Fostering

All the time, foster carers from all walks of life are playing a vital role in the lives of tens of thousands of young people and children who come into local authority care for a wide variety of reasons.

Foster carers give them the secure, caring family base they need to build their lives upon. Depending upon the circumstances, fostering can involve caring for children for a very short time, such as an overnight stay, perhaps a few days or several months. Sometimes it can mean fostering a child for many years until they are able to live independently.

Fostering provides stability and support for children from a wide range of families. It is not always easy. It can sometimes be demanding, even stressful. But it can also be great fun and extremely rewarding.

Foster Carer Awards 2015

Some of Bexley's Foster Carers were recently recognised for their work with children and young people across the borough.

The Mayor of Bexley, Cllr Sybil Camsey, gave special awards to the longest serving carers and to those who were nominated for 'Promoting Looked After Children Education', 'Achieving Stability' and 'Outstanding Work with Children with Special Needs'.

Carers that had put in five up to 40 years were also thanked for their time with the Borough and for all they do for looked after children in Bexley.

Meet some of the special award winners at Foster Carer Special Award Winners 2015.

Some children have special needs, and so require additional support. Others may have suffered abuse, or been caught up in a family crisis. Foster carers allow families the time they need to rebuild their lives, and give each child the best possible chance to develop the confidence and life skills they need for their future.

In 2011 Ofsted judged the London Borough of Bexley's Fostering Service to be 'Outstanding' following an in depth inspection. The document is available as a PDF under related downloads.

If you are interested in knowing more about fostering for Bexley and would like the opportunity to talk to a member of the fostering team, we are out and about in the community at a number of events throughout the year. Please visit Fostering events to see where we will be in the coming weeks.

If you think you could foster for Bexley, just give us a call on our freephone number 0800 783 7699, contact us by e-mail adoptionandfostering@bexley.gov.uk or complete the Online form.
