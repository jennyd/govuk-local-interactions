The Borough of Havant has a population of 120,700 and covers a total area of 30 square miles, including the communities of Havant, Bedhampton, Langstone, Purbrook, Waterlooville, Cowplain, Leigh Park, Hayling Island and Emsworth.

Havant Borough Council's vision is for a cleaner, safer, more prosperous borough.
