Pest control

If you have any issues or concerns regarding pest control in a domestic property please contact us using the details below.

  • Tel: 0345 2000 103
  • envirolink@northtyneside.gov.uk
  • Report a pest control issue online

Please note there are no out of hours or weekend emergency cover and we do not treat commercial premises.

Appointments

  • Morning appointments are 08:00 to 13:30
  • Afternoon appointments are 11:30 to 17:00.

Please note no specific times can be given.

Repairs and maintenance

No repairs will be carried out or arranged by the attending officer, the
tenant / landlord will be responsible for making any arrangements for repairs
required to prevent further infestation.

We deal with the following pests for which a charge is payable on booking an appointment

Rats - £20 treatment charge inc. VAT

  • We treat rats within domestic properties including the garden area, along with allotments and public open spaces.

Mice- £20 treatment charge inc. VAT

  • We treat mice within domestic properties but do not undertake treatment of mice outside of the property in allotments or public open spaces.

Rats and mice (Prior to a pest control visit)

  • Refrain from feeding the birds
  • Clear any possible harbouring areas where the rats or mice can take refuge.

Advice

  • Clear any debris from gardens
  • Fill any visible holes inside and outside of the property
  • Check drainage for damage
  • Sterilise all surfaces that rodents have been in contact with
  • Use a shop bought treatment (read instructions carefully)

Fleas - £20 treatment charge inc. VAT

  • We carry out treatments for fleas within the property. A pest control officer will spray the infected area with water based insecticide which dries in to a fine powder which cannot be seen.

Fleas (Prior to pest control visit)

  • Pets to be treated for fleas prior to visit
  • Vacuum all floors and upholstered furniture paying particular attention to borders and skirting boards and dispose of vacuum contents in the outside bin
  • If possible move furniture away from the walls
  • Remove any aquatic or reptile life from the infected room.

After the treatment has been applied do not vacuum for at least two weeks

Advice

  • Treat pets first and foremost
  • Treat house
  • Wash all bedding
  • Use a shop bought treatment (read instructions carefully)
  • Remove any aquatic or reptile life from the infected room.

Bed bugs and Cockroaches - £20 treatment charge inc. VAT

  • We will carry out an initial inspection to ascertain the extent of the problem and undertake the appropriate treatment.

Bed bugs (prior to pest control visit)

  • Vacuum infected room(s)
  • Remove bedding and treat on a hot wash
  • Move furniture off walls if possible
  • Remove any aquatic or reptile life from infected room.

Advice

  • Follow the 4 steps as shown above
  • Use a shop bought treatment (read instructions carefully).

Cockroaches (Prior to pest control visit)

  • Clear all floors and benches of equipment.

Advice

  • Clean and sterilise all of the infested areas
  • Store all food in sealed containers
  • Use a shop bought treatment (read instructions carefully).

Wasps - £40 treatment charge inc. VAT

  • In addition to the pests shown above and subject to appointments/availability, we can provide treatments for wasps at a cost of £40 including vat.

Advice

The general advice we offer is that householders can purchase suitable insecticides at garden centres or hardware stores (read instructions carefully and carry out the treatment later in the evening). Or the services of a private pest control company can be used (these services will be at your own expense)

For insects/pests other than those shown above we can offer free advice and identification. Please contact the councils Envirolink contact centre on the details shown above to arrange for an officer to contact you.

Ants - £40 treatment charge inc. VAT

In addition to the pests shown above and subject to appointments/availability, we can provide treatments for Ants at a cost of £40 including vat.
Advice

The general advice we offer is that householders can purchase suitable insecticides at garden centres or hardware stores (read instructions carefully before carrying out the treatment). Or the services of a private pest control company can be used (these services will be at your own expense). The insecticide should be used to treat cracks and crevices inside and outside of the property where Ants can be seen or a suspected nest is located.

Bees

We do not undertake treatments for bees unless there is a significant risk to a person's health and safety. If a treatment is undertaken then a charge of £40 will be payable prior to the treatment being undertaken.
