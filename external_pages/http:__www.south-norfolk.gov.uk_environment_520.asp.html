Animal welfare

All animals are protected in law under the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981. There are further levels of protection for particular species. To find out more about wildlife laws, how you can help to protect the wildlife and the environment, and about individual acts protecting certain species, visit the RSPCA website.

Please contact our stray dog collection service if you have a lost dog. It may well be in our care - see our page on found/stray dogs.
