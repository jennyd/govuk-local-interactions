16+ travel ticket

16+ students can pay £60 or £100 per term, or pay for their journey daily to get to school / college. (PLEASE NOTE: If the student is travelling on a designated school bus, the option to pay daily does not apply)

The 16+ Travel Ticket is available to students who live and study within Gwynedd (including Coleg Menai Llangefni) and who are 16 years old and under 22 years old on 1 September.

Before you apply for your Travel Ticket, make sure a service is available to and from the location you wish to travel, at the time you wish to travel. All journeys are not available to you.

Apply for new ticket / change details / Replace a lost or damaged ticket / contact us

  • online: log in to your account / register for an account                                    The application must be in the name of the person who will use the ticket.

  • phone: 01766 771000


Apply

To apply you will need:

  • debit / credit card. You will pay online by using the Capita Payment Portal secure system.
  • photo (school / Bangor University students only)
  • ID number (college students only: this is given when you register and is on your student card).
  • The application must be in the name of the person who will use the ticket.


Ticket price

For £60 per term students can travel:

  • to the local secondary school
  • between homes in Dwyfor and Meirionnydd schools catchment areas and Coleg Llandrillo Dolgellau and Pwllheli
  • between homes in Arfon schools catchment areas and Coleg Menai Parc Menai, Bangor, Llangefni and Caernarfon
  • between anywhere in Gwynedd and Coleg Llandrillo Glynllifon
  • to any special school in Gwynedd

For £100 per term students can travel between their home and any school or college in Gwynedd which is part of the scheme.

The scheme does not provide transport for Coleg Llandrillo Glynllifon residential students to get there at the beginning of the week. The College is responsible for this.

If you leave the school / college you will not get a refund.


Change details

You can change your details free of charge. If the change results in a different ticket price, this will apply from next term.


Replace a lost or damaged ticket

A £10 fee will be charged for the replacement ticket.


Empty Seat Ticket

This is for students who live within 3 miles of school / college or are over 19 years old on 31 August and under 22 on 1 September, or on a degree course and under 22 years of age on 1 September.

If this is relevant to you, you can make an application in the usual way, but these differences will apply:

  • if a service becomes overcrowded, your ticket will be withdrawn and you’ll be refunded
  • you won’t be able to get a taxi service to the bus stop
  • (over-19s / degree courses only) the ticket will cost £100.
