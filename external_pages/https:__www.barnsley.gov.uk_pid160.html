Adoption

Fifty children in Barnsley are counting on us to find them a forever home. If you live within 30 miles of the borough and can provide a child with a nurturing, stable and safe environment, we want to hear from you.

Concerned about your age, marital status or living arrangements? Don’t be – we know our children come from all walks of life – as do our adoptive parents!

Worried it might take years to adopt?  Don’t be – it’s quicker than you think.  In fact, you could be approved in as little as six months and have a child placed with you within three months after that.

If you could offer a child a permanent, loving home and a better future, all you need to do is come along to one of our information evenings or email us to find out more about the children counting on you.

Barnsley is one of the local councils and voluntary adoption agencies that make up ‘Being Family – Yorkshire and Humber’s network that works together to help and support prospective adopters.

Together, we’re a ‘family’ of people – adoption social workers, support workers, experienced adopters, foster carers and others – who are on hand to share our own experiences, offer advice and provide any help you need. We’ll make sure you never feel alone and that the right person from this family is there to support you at each stage of your adoption journey.
