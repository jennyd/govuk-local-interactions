PCN Challenge

Please complete all sections of the form which contain (*)

Please use this form to challenge a Penalty Charge Notice served within the last 28 days. If your challenge is submitted within 14 days service of the charge , the discount rate payable will be re-offered if you are unsuccessful. If your challenge is received after 14 days the Council is not obliged to re-offer the discount rate for payment of the charge.
If you have already received a rejection of your challenge, you cannot challenge further until a Notice to Owner has been sent to the registered keeper of the vehicle.
Due to character limit if your challenge is more than 3,500 characters, please attach detailed information as a word document.
