Housing benefit and Council tax support

These pages contain information about Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support. We may be able to provide assistance for people on a low income to help pay their rent and council tax.

Please note that we cannot help with mortgage payments for owner-occupiers, contact Jobcentre Plus for help with mortgage costs.
