Blocked Gullies/Drains Report

If you are concerned that a public highway gully or drain is blocked then you can report it using this online form.

If the flooding is not connected to a blocked gully on the public highway please view the information on the Flooding web page.

The large sewers that are normally situated under the road are usually maintained by the sewage undertaker and are known as public sewers. United Utilities are the sewage undertaker for Stockport.  If a flooded road is caused by a ruptured water main / sewer then United Utilities are responsible for repairing the damage.

To contact United Utilities visit their website at www.unitedutilities.com.
