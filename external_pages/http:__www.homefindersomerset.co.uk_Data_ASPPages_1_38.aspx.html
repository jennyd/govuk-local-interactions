Login

 

If you are already registered with Homefinder Somerset you can login here.  Simply enter your unique reference number and your PIN (which will be your date of birth in the format DD/MM/YYYY including the forward slashes) in the boxes provided below.

You will then be taken to a new page and asked for your password (if you have already set one).  This is shown as the bottom of the page - you may need to scroll down the page to see this.

If you havent already set a password  for your account you will be prompted to set one up before you can login to your account. Please note that your password is different from your PIN. 

Please note that the password  must between 6 and 10 characters long and have the following format:

  • It must have two upper case (capital) letters, two lower case letters and two numbers
  • Symbols and punctuation marks are not allowed
  • Upper case/Capital  examples are A, B, C
  • Lowercase examples are  a,b,c

If you are not registered with Homefinder Somerset please click on the 'click here to register' link below. Once you begin this process you will be given a unique reference number and you can use this and your date of birth to access any saved information and complete your application.

Applications must be completed within 28 days once you have begun the online process. After this time incomplete applications will be deleted from the system.

Before you begin an online application please read our guidance note  - this will help you to understand the documents that you need to have available and how the online application form works.
