How can I find out if a school is closed, for example due to bad weather?

In severe weather conditions, the decision about whether to close a school is made by the headteacher. Their decision is based on local conditions, and often depends on whether enough teachers have been able to get to the school to ensure pupil safety. Some might be able to open, others nearby might be forced to close. Schools usually have their own arrangements for notifying parents.

We provide details of school closures as reported to us during severe weather or other circumstances affecting multiple schools in Reading. For details about individual school closures in other circumstances (eg. polling or staff training), please contact schools directly.
