Nursery classes

The grant-funded early years education scheme offers parents a choice in the type of setting their child attends.​

The council offers the following facilities:

  • reception classes in infant and primary schools
  • two nursery classes attached to infant and primary schools

Children are not legally required to attend school until they are of compulsory school age, i.e. the term after their fifth birthday. Information about other nursery and pre-school providers, please contact us.

Also please see our pages free childcare for 2 year olds and free childcare for 3 and 4 year olds.
