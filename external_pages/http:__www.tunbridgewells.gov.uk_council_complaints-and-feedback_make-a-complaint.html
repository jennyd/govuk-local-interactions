Complaints, Feedback & Suggestions

We value your feedback and would like to hear any comments you have about our services. If you have a complaint please tell us so that we can put things right as this will help us to improve our services for the future and ensure we are providing good quality customer service.

We aim to:

  • Handle all complaints fairly and consistently
  • Try to resolve complaints on the spot where possible
  • Improve the services we have to offer by listening to your feedback
Comment, compliment or make a complaint online

 

Complaints about Councillors

For advice and information relating to complaints about Councillors, please visit our councillor code of conduct page.

Complaints about Freedom of Information Requests

If you are unhappy with the way we have dealt with your request or disagree with the decision, you can request a review under our complaints procedure. We will respond to your complaint within twenty working days and if you remain dissatisfied at this stage you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office.
