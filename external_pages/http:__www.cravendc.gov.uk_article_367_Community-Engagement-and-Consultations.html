Get Involved

There are many different ways you can Get Involved in your local community. On the following pages you will find information about how you can take part in projects in your local area.You will also find information about how you can Have Your Say in decisions that are made locally and affect local people.

  • Craven Youth Council

    Craven Youth Council

    The aim of Craven Youth Council is to empower young people aged 13 - 19 to influence and inform the decisions that affect their lives. We support young people to get involved in their communities and democracy locally, nationally and internationally, making a difference as volunteers, campaigners, decision-makers and leaders.

  • Residents Feedback Panel

    Residents Feedback Panel
