Herefordshire Archive and Records

We hold archives for the county of Herefordshire, the city of Hereford, the diocese of Hereford, as well as other local bodies and individuals.

Although we do not currently have an online archive catalogue, some of our catalogues are available on The National Archives Discovery website.Â 

We do have an online index to our wills and archival collections which is part of an ongoing project. Access the indexes from our Archive collections page.

If you have any further questions please email us at archives@herefordshire.gov.uk

We are open Tuesday to Friday 9.15am to 4.45pm and the second Saturday of every month.
