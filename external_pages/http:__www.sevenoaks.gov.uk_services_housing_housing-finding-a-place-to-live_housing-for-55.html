Housing for 55+

In the Sevenoaks District, retirement housing consists of bedsits, flats and bungalows.

Some schemes have communal lounges and laundry areas. Usually a housing assistant calls on tenants at least once a week and the scheme is linked to a 24 hour call centre for additional security in case of an emergency.

Housing assistants for retirement housing provide the following types of support:

  • Managing your home
  • Personal administration
  • Income and benefits
  • Bills and budgeting
  • Social networks and relationships
  • Referrals to health and welfare services
  • Accessing employment/training/volunteering opportunities
  • Accessing cultural and religious services

To apply you need to join the Sevenoaks District Housing Register.

Download a factsheet with the types and locations of retirement housing offered by the Sevenoaks District Housing Register partner organisations.

For more information, visit the Rockdale website and EAC Housing Care.

Extra care housing

Accommodation with a higher level of care may also be available.

Download our extra care factsheet with details of housing offered through the Sevenoaks District Housing Register.

For further information, view the information for two extra care schemes ar Hollybush Court and Emily Court.

Specialist Advice leaflets for people aged over 55+

The leaflets provide tailored advice and information on different housing and support options for people aged over 55. The information covers what help is available if you want to move home and also what help is available to enable you to remain in you own home for as long as you want to.

Additional sources of information 

Which online provides free independent and practical advice for older people and their relatives across the UK.
