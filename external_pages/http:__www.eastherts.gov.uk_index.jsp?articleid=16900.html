Brown Bin Collections

Information and guidance about your Brown Bin (Organic) service.

East Herts Council currently operate an alternate refuse and recycling collection service.

This means your brown wheeled bin is collected fortnightly on your recycling week.

You can use your brown bin to recycle food waste and garden waste only.

East Herts Council provides each household in the district with one brown bin.

We can also provide a green kitchen caddy to help you collect the food waste, before transferring it to the brown bin.

Please use your blue lidded bin for cardboard; you can no longer place cardboard in the brown bin.

Please Note: We will  now accept caddy liners featuring the seedling logo in your brown bin.

Bags without this logo should not be used and will prevent your brown bin from being emptied.  For more information about bag types and suppliers view Caddy Liners - Information and Suppliers .

  • We offer a boundary collection service; this means your bin must be presented with the lid closed at the boundary of your property by 7am.
  • The boundary of your property is where the front of your property / garden meets the public highway (pavement or road)
  • Operatives will not step onto your property.
  • Please do not overfill your brown bin.  If it is too heavy it may not be collected as our vehicles have weight restrictions.

If you are not physically able to place your waste on the boundary you may qualify for an Assisted Collection (Exemption).

Find out your Brown Bin Collection Dates here.

If you have followed the above guidelines but your brown bin has not been collected please report a  Missed Collection.  You must report this within 48 hours of your collection day.

If you do not have a wheeled bin or kitchen caddy and would like to request one, please complete our online Replacement / Lost Bin Request Form.

Contamination

If your brown bin contains items which are not acceptable it will NOT be emptied as this is considered contamination. You must remove the contamination and the crew will collect it on your next scheduled collection date. We will not return for contaminated bins.

Please Note: Cardboard is no longer accepted in the brown bin, and will be considered contamination if it is put in there. Please place it in your blue lidded recycling bin.

Brown bins may also be rejected if they are too heavy for the loading equipment or deemed too heavy by the loading crew. You will need to reduce the amount of waste presented in the brown bin and we will collect it on your next scheduled collection date. We will not return for overweight bins.

Home Composting

You can recycle your kitchen and garden waste at home and make rich compost to help your garden bloom, visit Get Composting or call 0844 571 4444 for more information and discounted offers.

If you have any queries please refer to the service leaflet or contact our helpful Environmental Services Team for further information via email: operations.admin@eastherts.gov.uk or call 01279 655261 ext: 3507.
