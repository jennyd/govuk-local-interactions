Somerset Community Meals Service

 

The Somerset Community Meals Service is for older people and people with disabilities who we agree need help to make sure they can have regular cooked meals. This is provided at a discounted rate menu by Wiltshire Farm Foods.

The Service delivers you frozen meals every two weeks. The frozen meals must be kept in a freezer and can be reheated in a microwave, special steamer or ordinary oven. This means that you can choose what you eat and when you eat it and can have a cooked meal every day. Your dietary, religious or ethnic needs can be catered for.

If you need help to get your meal ready or to eat it, this can be arranged after an assessment of your needs.

If you already have a social care worker, ask them about it. If not, phone us on 0300 123 2224.

We will ask you some questions about what things you can do on your own and what you need to have help with.

What happens next?
After we have agreed that you can use the meals service, we will pass your name on to Wiltshire Farm Foods. They will arrange to deliver your first order and any equipment you need. The delivery driver will show you how to use the equipment and will explain how to store and heat your meals.

You will need a microwave or a steamer to re-heat your meals and a freezer to store them. If you don't have the equipment to store or re-heat your meals Wiltshire Farm Foods will lend you what you need for as long as you are buying at least ten meals from them every two weeks.

If you can't heat or eat your meals on your own, we can make arrangements for you to have some help with this after an assessment of your needs. Phone 0300 123 2224 to arrange for an assessment. 

What is covered?
You can still have frozen meals without being assessed by us. Wiltshire Farm Foods is a private company that provides a service to many parts of the country. You can buy meals from them privately but this won't be at a discounted rate. You can find out more about Wiltshire Farm Foods by clicking the link in Information and resources, by phoning them on 0800 773773, or by contacting us on 0300 123 2224.

How much does it cost?
Each meal provided as part of the Somerset Community Meals Service costs £3.85, and includes a main course and a dessert. There are some more expensive meals that you can buy if you want to.

If you borrow equipment from Wiltshire Farm Foods there is no charge for this, as long as you place the minimum order of ten meals every two weeks.

If you have help to heat or eat your meals you may have to pay something for this. This charge will depend on your circumstances.
