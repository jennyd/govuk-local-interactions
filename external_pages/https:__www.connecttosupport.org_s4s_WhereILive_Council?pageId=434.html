Telecare

Older and vulnerable people, particularly those living on their own, need to be able to call for help in an emergency. Telecare equipment can be a range of automatic sensors around the home that raise the alarm in an emergency, or an emergency alarm system worn round the neck.

  • Telecare equipment
    We can provide a range of telecare equipment that can ensure an automatic early warning when someone is at risk, giving customers, their family and carers peace of mind.

    Telecare equipment

  • Emergency alarms
    These are available in the form of a pendant worn around the neck or as a wrist band during the day and kept close at hand during the night and are used to summon help in an emergency.

    Emergency alarms
