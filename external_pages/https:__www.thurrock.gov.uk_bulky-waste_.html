Disposing of bulky waste

To deal with bulky items you can either:

  • dispose of household bulky waste for free at the Linford Civic Amenity Site

  • find free options using our A to Z of waste and recyclable items

  • pay for us to collect household or commercial bulky waste

Arrange for us to collect your bulky waste

Bulky items will be picked up from the same place as your usual wheeled bin collection.

We charge:

  • £27 for up to 3 items
  • a further £7 for each additional item

We will only collect the items that you have asked us to collect. We will not take away any items that do not match those you have paid us to take. Loose waste such as rubble or foliage must be bagged securely.

How to book a collection

You must arrange your collection online through our My Account service. You can book a date for collection but we cannot reserve a time slot for you, morning or afternoon. Items must be left out on the collection date before 6am.

Bookings are often made well in advance so you should book early for the best chance of getting your preferred date.

You must make your payment at the time of booking. You can pay by credit card or debit card – there is an additional 2% fee for payment by credit card. All payments are non-refundable.

Your bulky waste collection cannot be added to or changed once it has been booked, so it's important to think carefully about all the items you might like us to collect.
