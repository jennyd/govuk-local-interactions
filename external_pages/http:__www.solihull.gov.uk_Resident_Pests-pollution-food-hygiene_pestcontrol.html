Pest control

We can remove certain pests or offer professional advice.

We can help with:

  • mice
  • rats
  • ants
  • bedbugs
  • beetles (not carpet beetles)
  • cockroaches
  • fleas
  • squirrels
  • wasps and hornets

How much does it cost?

See our services and fees. You can pay by credit/debit card or over the phone.

How long does it take?

We aim to visit within 5 working days, although it can take longer during the summer. For rats indoors, we visit within 24 hours.

Book an appointment

Call 0121 704 8000

Solihull Community Housing (SCH) tenants

Call 0121 717 1515

Commercial contracts

For a quotation:

  • email pestcontrol@solihull.gov.uk
  • call 0121 704 8178

We do not deal with:

  • badgers
  • bats
  • bees
  • carpet beetles
  • cluster flies
  • foxes
  • moles
  • psocids (booklice)
  • pigeons
  • starlings

Try a private pest control service (look online or in the phone book) or one of the following organisations.

Other sources of help

The British Pest control Association - the UK trade body listing advice, information and accredited pest control services

Badgers - Warwickshire Wildlife Trust: 01905 754919

Bats - Bat Conservation Trust: 0845 1300 228

Bees - Bee keepers association: 02476 696679

Foxes - RSPCA: 0300 1234 999
