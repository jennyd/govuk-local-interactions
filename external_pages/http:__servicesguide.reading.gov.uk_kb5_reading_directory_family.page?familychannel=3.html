Welcome to Reading’s Local Offer

What is the Local Offer?

The Local Offer is part of the reforms that are happening as a result of the new Children & Families Act 2014 and the new SEN reforms. The Local Offer contains information about Education, Health and Social Care services that are available in Reading for children, young people with SEND and their families read more about SEND Reforms

The Short Breaks/SEN/D Information Service is a free service provided by Reading Borough Council for families with children and young people who have special needs aged 0- 25 years (inclusive) living in the Reading area. 

For people who do not have access to the internet, this information can be found in a number of other ways. We offer a 9am-5pm telephone service 0118 9373777 (opt 2), we offer an outreach service and attend events. You can also visit us at the Civic Centre as we are based just behind the reception area.

Reading's Local Offer will continually grow over time to ensure it provides a genuinely valuable resource for our children, young people and their families.  Details on how to get involved and inform future Local Offer developments will be publicised on this site.  If you have any ideas on how we can improve and enhance this site in the meantime we’d love to hear from you, at disc@reading.gov.uk
