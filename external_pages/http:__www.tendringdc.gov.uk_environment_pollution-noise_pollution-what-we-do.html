Pollution - what we do

Listen

 

 

 

 

 

 

The Pollution Team investigate complaints from the public concerning commercial, industrial and domestic pollution problems and public health nuisances including: 

  • noise
  • bonfires
  • general nuisance such as odours
  • rubbish accumulations
  • pests
  • artificial light

They also undertake the:

  • authorisation of certain prescribed processes that have the potential to cause pollution
  • approval of furnace installations, arrestment plant and chimney heights
  • monitoring of local air quality
  • investigation of contaminated land
  • enforcement of standards for rural and mains drainage systems
  • testing of private water supplies e.g. boreholes and wells 
  • provision of assisted funerals

You can report problems with noise, black smoke from bonfires, rubbish accumulations etc. using our complaint/enquiry form, a simple form which you can complete and submit online in the useful forms area above.
