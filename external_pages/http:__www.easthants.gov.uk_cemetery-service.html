Cemetery Service

What to do if someone dies

You can find advice and guidance on gov.uk.

It gives help on everything from how to register the death, and how to arrange a funeral.

How to find East Hampshire District Council Cemeteries

The cemeteries have graves for burials and cremations. They are always open to visit.

  • Alton Old Odiham Road GU34 2EB (opened in 1856)
  • Catherington Catherington Road PO8 0TH ( opened in 1966)
  • Petersfield  Ramshill GU31 4AP (Opened in 1857)
  • West Liss Church Street GU33 6JY (Opened in 1954)
  • Whitehill Holywater Road GU35 0AH ( Opened in 1987)

Green Burial Sites

There are two green burial sites in East Hampshire. These are run by private companies.

  • South Downs Natural Burial Site The Sustainability Centre, Droxford Road, East Meon GU32 1HR  Telephone 01730 823425
  • GreenAcres Woodland Burials Heatherley Wood, Grayshott Road Grayshott, Hampshire GU35 8LA  Telephone 01428 715915

Memorials and Headstones

All stonemasons who work in our cemeteries must be registered with either:

  • The British Register of Accredited Memorial Masons
  • National Association of Memorial Masons 

Please search them for a stonemason to suit your needs.
