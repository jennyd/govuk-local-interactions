Adult library membership

To join a library please visit any library, or complete a library application form online. All you need is proof of your address. Normally one of the following is all you need:

  • Driving licence
  • Benefits / pension books
  • Bills: gas, electricity, council tax, water rates or similar
  • Other documents such as your medical card (typed) or council rent book (typed)

Other identification may be OK, but please ring the library before you visit to check. This will save you time when you join.

We will give you a library card - if at a later date you change your name, address or phone number please tell us so we can keep our records up to date.

How much can I borrow? 

There are different loan periods for different items. There are also limits to the amount of items you can borrow at any time:

  • Books - 4 weeks, up to 20 at one time
  • DVDs - 2 nights or 1 week, up to 4 at one time
  • Compact Discs - 1 week, up to 8 at one time
  • Talking Books - 4 weeks, up to 8 at one time
