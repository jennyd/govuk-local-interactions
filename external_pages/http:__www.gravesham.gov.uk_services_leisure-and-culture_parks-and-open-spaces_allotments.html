Allotments

There are 14 allotment sites in the urban area of Gravesham.

Most but not all of these sites have fencing and a water supply. There are six sites in Northfleet and eight sites in Gravesend.

The cost for an allotment plot varies depending on how many square rods (one rod = 25.3 sq mtrs) the plot has.

Most of our allotments have water and the cost is £6 per square rod. Making a yearly total of £30 for a 5 square rod plot. For sites without a water supply the cost will be £4 per square rod, making a yearly total of £20 for a 5 square rod plot. These prices are correct from 1 April 2015.

If you would like to be added to a waiting list for any of the sites listed below please contact our allotments team by email; allotments@gravesham.gov.uk.  You can go on the list for up to four sites so please tell us which sites you prefer.
