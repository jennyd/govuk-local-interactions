Gritting

We treat our roads and pavements in a priority order depending on how busy they are. During winter we monitor our roads and pavements around the clock, taking action when needed.

Please see our road gritting priority and footpath gritting priority maps for information in your area.

If you have an enquiry about our treatments, please contact us
