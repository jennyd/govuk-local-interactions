Your district councillor

Councillors 2015 -19

Suffolk Coastal District Council is now represented by 42 councillors following the elections on 7 May 2015 - thirteen fewer than previously. Those councillors will serve for the next four years and will represent eleven single-member wards, fourteen two-member wards and one three-member ward across the district - 26 electoral wards in total.

The Conservatives have control of the Council with 37 councillors, there are 2 Lib Dem councillors, 2 Independents and 1 Labour councillor.
