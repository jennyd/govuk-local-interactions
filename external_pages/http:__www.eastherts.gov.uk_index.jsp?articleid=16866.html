Refuse and Recycling Schedule Street Index

Use My East Herts to find out your next collection day.

To download a complete calendar please select the letter your road starts with from the list. The table will show you the day of the week your refuse and recycling is collected, and which rotation week of the alternating schedule you are on; either Week 1 or Week 2. There will also be a link to a calendar you can print off or save to your computer.

This will take you to a list of streets starting with that letter. Scroll down until you find your street and your collection day.

If you cannot find your details listed, please e-mail operations.admin@eastherts.gov.uk or call us on 01279 655261 and we will be happy to advise you further.
