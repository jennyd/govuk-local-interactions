Housing Benefit is a benefit to help you with your rent. It is paid by Sefton Council based on rules set by the Government.

To claim Housing Benefit you must be aged 16 or over, on a low income and pay rent. This includes people in full time or part time employment, unemployed or retired.

You cannot claim Housing Benefit if:

  • you have savings of £16000 or above (unless you are receiving Guaranteed Pension Credit).
  • you are a close relative of your landlord, who also lives with you.
  • you are claiming Universal Credit.

Housing Benefit normally cannot be paid on a property you previously owned, in the last 5 years, unless you had to sell the property in order to continue living there.
