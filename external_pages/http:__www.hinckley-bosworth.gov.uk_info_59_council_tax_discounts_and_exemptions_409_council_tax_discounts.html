Council tax 25% discount

When this discount applies

If only one adult lives in a property, the council tax charge will be reduced by 25%.

If one adult lives in the property with someone who falls into a category below, the council tax charge can also reduced by 25%:

  • Full time students
  • Student nurses
  • Apprentices
  • A single parent claiming child benefit 
  • Members of religious communities (monks and nuns)
  • Care workers working for low pay, usually for charities
  • People who are severely mentally impaired
  • Patients resident in hospital
  • People being looked after in care homes
  • Members of visiting forces and certain international institutions
  • People staying in certain hostels or night shelters
  • 18 and 19 year olds who are at or have just left school
  • People caring for someone with a disability who is not a spouse or, partner or child under 18
  • People in prison (except those in prison for non-payment of council tax or a fine)
  • Diplomats and their spouses

You would need to apply to get this reduction.

Download and complete the form that is relevant to your situation to apply for a 25% discount.
