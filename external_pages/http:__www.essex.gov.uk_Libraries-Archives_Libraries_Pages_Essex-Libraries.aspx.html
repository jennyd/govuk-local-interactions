5 February 2016

Page Content

In celebration of National Libraries Day this Saturday (6 February) we would love if you could share a photo of yourself in your local library.

 

  • On Twitter, please tag @EssexLibraries and include the hashtag #whatididinthelibrarytoday.
  • If using Facebook, please post on our page www.facebook.com/essexlibraries
  • On Google+, please tag +Essex Libraries and include the hashtag #whatididinthelibrarytoday

 

If you do not wish Essex Libraries to use your photo please opt out by stating “I do not wish this photo to be used on Essex Libraries social media channels”. Otherwise by posting the photograph it is understood you consent to Essex County Council Libraries using your image on its social media accounts (Twitter, Facebook, Google+) to raise awareness of National Libraries Day and to promote the library service afterwards.”
