Simple to install

Our telecare lifeline alarms are simple to install needing just a mains socket and a telephone line. Options are available that work through the mobile phone network if you don't have a landline.

One of our friendly installers will fit your alarm and show you how easy it is to use and will help you to make a test call through to our monitoring centre.
