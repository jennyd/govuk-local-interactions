Graffiti & vandalism

Summary

If you know of any graffiti or land or premises that have been vandalised, you can report it to us. Who you report it to depends on whether it's council-owned land or property, or private property. If the problem is with council-owned property or land get in touch with us using the contact details below - or fill out our online form if the problem is with one of the public toilets we manage (see our page on public toilets for the form).

If the problem is on private property you can fill out an anti-social behaviour report form, send it to us, and we will investigate the problem. Find out what to do on our anti-social behaviour page.
