Pay your Penalty Charge Notice online

Welcome to the Brighton and Hove Councils Penalty Charge Notice payment system. Before proceeding please ensure that you have:
  • The notice number.
  • Your vehicle registration number.
  • A valid credit or debit card.
This service will accept Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, Electron and American Express.
