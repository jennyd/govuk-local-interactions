Recycling

It is everyone's responsibility to dispose of their rubbish correctly and help protect their local environment.

By using the schemes provided by us, you can recycle up to half of all the rubbish you produce. Recycling is important but, where possible, you should also try and reduce and reuse your waste. This section gives you some useful tips and ideas around how to recycle more and waste less.
