Pest control services

  • Pest control for residents

    We treat a variety of pests in domestic properties for all residents of the borough. There are discounts for council tenants and Dacorum Card holders.

    If you have seen evidence of pests in your home or garden and would like to book a treatment, please phone our Customer Service Centre on 01442 228000 and ask for Pest Control.

    We will process your payment over the phone (you will need a credit or debit card to do this) and make an appointment for one of our officers to visit your property to carry out the treatment. We can offer appointments between 8am and 4pm, Monday to Friday. We are unable to carry out treatments during the evenings, weekends or bank holidays.

  • Pest control for businesses

    We provide cost-effective pest control services to businesses, which cover a variety of rodents and insects. Our service is tailored to the needs of your individual business.

    Contact us for more information and to book a free survey of your premises. Email pestcontrol@dacorum.gov.uk or phone us on 01442 228543.
