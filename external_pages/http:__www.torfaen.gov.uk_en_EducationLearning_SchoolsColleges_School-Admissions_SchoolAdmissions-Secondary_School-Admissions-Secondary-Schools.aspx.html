School Admissions - Secondary School

Children normally transfer from primary to secondary school at the start of the school year in which their twelfth birthday falls (i.e. 1 September to 31 August inclusive).

As you may be aware the closing date for the Secondary Admissions 2016 was 30th November 2015 at 4pm. However if you still wish to apply you can download a copy of the Secondary School Admission Form here or obtain a paper copy from either your child's current Torfaen primary school or by contacting the Torfaen Admission Team on 01495 766915 / 766921. Please note that any applications received after this date will be processed as a late application and may affect your application.

If you are not a Torfaen resident you will be required to contact your Home Authority to seek advice on how to apply.

If you wish for your child to attend a Voluntary Aided School, you will need to contact the school direct for the necessary admission arrangements.

Full details of the Authority’s Admission arrangements and related issues such as the provision of school transport can be found in the Parents / Carers Information Booklet.
