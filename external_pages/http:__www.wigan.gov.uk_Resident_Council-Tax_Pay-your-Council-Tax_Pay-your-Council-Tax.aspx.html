Pay your Council Tax

Bank account change →
Our bank account details for receiving payments changed on 1st April 2015. Please update any standing orders, and use the new details below for any payments after this date.

Via MyAccount

To pay your Council Tax log in to your MyAccount.

If you don't have one already simply go to register for your MyAccount.

Direct Debit

Direct Debit is the easiest way to pay your bill. We offer customers a choice of five dates each month when they can pay. These are the 1st, 6th, 15th, 21st and 27th. You can also ask to pay over 12 months instead of 10, or however many months remain in the year.

You are also protected by the Direct Debit Guarantee, which promises that you will get your money back straight away if anything goes wrong.

  • Set up a Direct Debit to pay your Council Tax by logging in to your MyAccount. 

Internet

You can pay your Council Tax bill online with a debit or credit card, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

  • Pay your bill online  - Please note: There is a 1.5% charge when making payments using credit cards, and if you are using a debit card from a foreign bank. No charges for debit cards from UK banks.

Telephone

You can pay your bill by credit or debit card 24 hours a day by phoning the automated bill payment hotline 01942 489006. Have your card and reference number ready when you call. Payments cannot be made with American Express cards.

Telephone and online banking or standing order

To pay your bill via telephone or online banking, or by standing order with your bank you will need our bank details:

  • Our bank account number is: 63553698
  • Our sort code is: 20-55-41
  • You must quote your 12 digit billing reference shown on the front of your bill as the reference for your payment.

Post Office and PayPoint

You can pay your bill by cash, debit card or credit card at any Post Office or by cash at any PayPoint outlet nationwide. You will need a bill with a barcode on it to pay. If you already have one of these simply take it along and make the payment.

Post

Make cheques payable to Wigan Council and post to:

Customer Services, Wigan Council, PO Box 100, Wigan, WN1 3DS.

Cheques should be crossed 'A/C Payee' and please write your account number and the type of bill you are paying on the back of the cheque. If you need a receipt you must enclose your bill and a stamped addressed envelope. We cannot accept cheques that are post-dated.

Chip and PIN

You can also pay your bill with a debit or credit card using chip and PIN machines at any Life Centre.

Please note you will not be able to pay your Council Tax bill by cash at any of our offices. Please use your local Post Office or PayPoint outlet to pay by cash.
