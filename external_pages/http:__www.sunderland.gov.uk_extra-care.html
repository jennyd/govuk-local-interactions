Extra Care housing

Extra Care housing provides independent living for people over the age of 55.

  • About Extra Care housing

    Care and support is available on site and is flexible to meet the needs of each household if required.

  • Beckwith Mews, Seaham Street, Silksworth

    This scheme is owned and managed by Housing 21. It opened in March 2009.

  • Bramble Hollow, Four Lane Ends, Hetton le Hole

    This scheme is owned and managed by Housing 21. It opened in June 2010.

  • Cherry Tree Gardens, Houghton le Spring

    This scheme is owned and managed by Gentoo. Opened in November 2011.

  • Dovecote Meadow, Fordfield Road, Ford

    Dovecote Meadow is a stylish extra care development, located in Ford Estate which is owned and managed by Housing and Care 21. This scheme opened in June 2014.

  • Haddington Vale, Doxford Park

    Haddington Vale is being developed by Gentoo. It is expected to open at the end of 2014.

  • Keelboat Lodge, Silver Street, Hendon

    Keelboat Lodge is being developed by Housing 21. It is expected to open in March 2015.

  • Seafarers Way, Moor Terrace, Hendon

    Seafarers Way provides extra care accommodation for people with Alzheimers and dementia. This scheme opened in September 2014.

  • Springtide Cove, Dock Street, Roker

    Springtide Cove opened in September 2014 and provides 53 one and two bedroom apartments for rent and sale located close to Roker sea front and Marina.

  • Woodridge Gardens, Columbia, Washington

    This scheme is owned and managed by Housing 21. It opened in December 2009.

  • Enabling independence

    Sunderland City Council is engaged within an ambitious project to deliver supported accommodation to meet the needs of our current and future communities.

  • Housing advice

    Here you will find advice on all housing issues, including how to prevent homelessness and what to do if you are homeless.
