Garden waste - Green Club

The collection of garden waste is managed by Biffa.  You can sign up for the Green Waste Club online.  This is a chargeable service.

 Credit card  £46  £92  £138

Sign up for the Green Waste Club

Green waste is usually collected on the same weeks as your brown bin. If you would like to check this please contact Biffa direct:

  • Email - gwc.mbc@biffa.co.uk
  • Phone - 0800 0858286
