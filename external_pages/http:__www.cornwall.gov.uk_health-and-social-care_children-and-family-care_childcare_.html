Childcare

Last updated: 16/09/2015

How do I find childcare?

The Family Information Service (FIS) is the only official source of all Ofsted registered childcare in Cornwall.

It can provide you with details on:

  • Childminders
  • Day nurseries
  • Pre-school playgroups
  • School nursery units / classes
  • Breakfast clubs / after school clubs
  • Holiday schemes

To search for childcare, visit the FIS website or to request help contact a FIS advisor. 

Childcare Sufficiency Assessment

The Childcare Sufficiency Assessment (CSA) helps Cornwall Council establish the supply and demand of childcare within the county.  Visit the CSA page to view results of the latest survey.

Two, three and four year old childcare funding options:

  • 2 Year Old Funding
  • 3 and 4 Year Old Funding

Other childcare funding

Details of support available to help pay for childcare can be found on the FIS website. 

Contacts

For more information on any childcare or funding options, please contact the Family Information Service on 0800 587 8191, email fis@cornwall.gov.uk or visit the FIS website.
