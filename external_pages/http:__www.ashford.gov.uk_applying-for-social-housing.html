Applying for Social Housing

If you live or work within the borough and you have a housing need that you cannot meet from your own resources or if your current home does not meet your housing requirements then you may wish to apply to join the council's housing register.

To make an application for social housing within the borough you need to complete our online application form which is available on the Kent Homechoice website.

If for any reason you are unable to complete the form online, please either phone 01233 330688 or email us at housing@ashford.gov.uk.

Once you have completed the form you will be required to submit some supporting documentation which you can do by post or via the Gateway Plus. Please do not send us in original documents such as passports, a photocopy is sufficient.

Once you have submitted your documents it can take up to six weeks for us to make an assessment of your application and to contact you with details of your application priority and how you can express an interest in available properties.

For further information please view the council's current Lettings Policy 2013 (Revision 2015) [pdf] 412KB.

If you wish to ask for a review of any of the decisions made about your application, you can do so here by making a Stage One Review Request, if you are still unhappy with the decision you can submit a Stage Two Review Request.
