How to apply for sheltered housing

You need to complete an online application form so that your circumstances can be considered.


Apply online:

  • Apply online (takes you to the Canterbury City Council website)
  • You can also email housingadvice@canterbury.gov.uk if you need to request a form and are not able to access the service via the internet.

East Kent Housing is not responsible for the Housing Need Register or lettings; this remains a function of the Council.
