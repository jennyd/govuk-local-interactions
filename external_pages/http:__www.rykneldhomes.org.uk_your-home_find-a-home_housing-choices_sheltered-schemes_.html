Sheltered Schemes

Sheltered accommodation is usually intended specifically for people over the age of 60.  There is typically an alarm system for use in emergencies and also either floating support or a Scheme Manager/Warden living on the premises or nearby. 

Some schemes are designed specifically for disabled people and may have specialised facilities and trained staff to provide support.

Sheltered accommodation tends to be self-contained flats which often have a communal area.

Within North East Derbyshire there are a range of sheltered schemes - click on the links on the left of this page.
