Pest, Noise, Air Pollution, Smoke, Light and Drainage Information Request

This form will get you free advice from our specialist team regarding any of the subjects mentioned below.

Air pollution Control - Including responding to complaints about smoke, odour, grit & dust and licensing industrial air pollution control processes

Pest Control - Including Treatment for rats, mice and cockroaches in houses. (There is a fee for this service.) and Smoke testing to find the source of the problem.

Noise Control - Including responding to complaints of noise from neighbours, industrial processes, construction sites, pubs and clubs.

Light Pollution - Including responding to complaints about glare from excessively bright or poorly aimed lights.

Drainage - The Council is now able to offer a drain clearance service for blocked private drains and sewers. (There is a charge of £85 for this service).

Smoke - We deal with smoke nuisances from bonfires and chimneys serving residential, commercial and industrial premises. In addition we enforce legislation controlling dark/black smoke emissions from bonfires and chimneys serving any trade/industrial premises or activity (eg demolition and construction work) carried on by way of a trade or business.

Nuisance is where the smoke interferes with another persons comfortable and convenient enjoyment of their property (including gardens) or causes physical discomfort.

Dark/Black smoke has a specific definition in law and the circumstances would need to be determined by the investigating officer before coming to a decision on whether an offence had been committed.
