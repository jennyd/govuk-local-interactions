Changes to traffic movement in Aldgate area:

  • St Botolph Street is open to two-way traffic
  • You can now turn right from Whitechapel High Street into Middlesex and St Botolph streets
  • Bevis Mark and Dukes Place has returned to their original working, with traffic moving from south-east to north-west.
  • Aldgate and Aldgate High Street are closed in both directions until 30 April.
  • Lloyd's Avenue is reversed to run north-east,
  • Fenchurch Street, Jewry Street, Leadenhall Street are closed at their junctions of Aldgate,
