Child Employment Permits

Children of compulsory school age can work from the age of 13 onwards, provided that:

  • They have a work permit from the council in the workplace's location
  • They do not work before 7am or after 7pm 

Child Entertainment Permits

All children who perform on stage or in television, films, commcercials, work in paid / professional sport or who work as models have their welfare and safety protected by the following legislations:

  • Children and Young Persons Act 1933
  • Children and Young Persons Act 1963
  • Children (Performances) Regulations 1968
  • Children (Performances) Regulations 1998
  • Children (Performances) (Amendment) 2000
