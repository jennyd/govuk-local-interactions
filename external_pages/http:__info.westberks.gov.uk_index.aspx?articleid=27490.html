Activity Team West Berkshire

Outdoor activities, in partnership with the Adventure Dolphin charity, based in Pangbourne

Activity Team West Berkshire provides outdoor activities for the residents of West Berkshire and the surrounding area at The Dolphin Centre, Pangbourne and Waterside Centre, Newbury.  Activities can also take place at other locations across the UK.

The Dolphin and Waterside Centres have a wide variety of well-maintained outdoor equipment, allowing the team to offer and support a broad spectrum of outdoor activities and expeditions, from an expedition to a remote wilderness area, to a day spent canoeing or team building.

The team offer introductory and development sessions to schools and groups during term time and holidays, and have extensive programmes for individuals and groups from the age of eight upwards. These programmes include specialist courses in both land and water based activities, at novice or a more advanced level.
We pride ourselves on offering high value training at competitive prices.

Full details of the current available programme can be found in the  Activity Team brochure [1Mb]

Learning Outside the Classroom Quality Badge

In 2015, Activity Team West Berkshire (ATWB) earned the  Learning Outside the Classroom (LOtC) Quality Badge [688kb].
The LOtC Quality Badge is a nationally-recognised award that shows we work to high standards in teaching and safety.  Only organisations that pledge to take part in an ongoing process to make sure learning outside the classroom is of a high quality gain this recognition.

To find out more, take a look at the LOtC Quality Badge website.
