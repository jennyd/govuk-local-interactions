Housing

Popular Links

Advice for Homeowners

The Somerset West Landlord and Tenant Services (SWeLT) can offer advice and financial assistance in a number of ways if your house is in need of improvement, adaptation or repair.

Advice for Landlords

The Somerset West Landlord and Tenant Services (SWeLT) is committed to working with private landlords and agents to encourage a vibrant, affordable private rented sector with decent properties for all.

Advice for Tenants

We offer a wide range of housing advice for everyone who needs it, providing advice and assistance on housing related issues including renting privately, affordable home ownership, homelessness, illegal eviction, registering for social housing.

Homefinder Somerset

Homefinder Somerset is a way of finding a home to rent which lets you select the home you would like to be considered for.

Somerset HomeLet

Somerset HomeLet the one stop site for private rented lettings – free for landlords, free for home searchers.
