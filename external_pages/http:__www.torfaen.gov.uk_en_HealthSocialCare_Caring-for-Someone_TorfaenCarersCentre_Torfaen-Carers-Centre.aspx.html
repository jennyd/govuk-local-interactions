Torfaen Carers' Centre

Torfaen Carers Centre is located in Pontypool town centre and offers support and advice for carers living across the borough.

The centre was established by Pontypool Mencap and Torfaen Carers Group in March 2005 and provides a one stop shop where carers can obtain information about a whole range of issues, including respite care, transport and personal care for the person they care for.

Carers are encouraged to call in for a coffee, chat and information when they are passing.

The Carers Centre has a full itinerary of activities. It is open Monday to Friday from 11.00am to 3.00pm.

The Carers Centre is affiliated to The Princess Trust, which employs a Carers Health Development Worker and a Carers Outreach Worker who are based there.
