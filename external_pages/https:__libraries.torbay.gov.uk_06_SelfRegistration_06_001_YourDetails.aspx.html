Join Torbay Libraries

It’s easy, it’s free and you can join at any age

To join the library online please complete the form below. This facility is for new members only - please do not fill in if you already have a card. Please visit your chosen library to collect your membership card within 14 days of joining online.

All fields marked with an * must be filled in.

If you are under 16 you need your guarantor's borrower number or details to complete the form. The guarantor is responsible for the ticket and is usually a parent, guardian or carer at the same address.
We will need to check that your guarantor is happy for you to join when you collect your card.
