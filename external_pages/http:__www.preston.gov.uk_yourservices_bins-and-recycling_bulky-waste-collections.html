Bulky waste collection

What to do with old furniture, beds, white goods and other bulky waste

You can arrange for the collection of bulky items of waste that do not fit inside a grey wheeled bin through our bulky waste collection service.

Please note: this service is for residential waste only. For business waste visit special business waste collections.

Who can use our collection service?

The service is available to Preston household residents only. Areas such as Penwortham and Longridge fall under Ribble Valley and South Ribble Borough Council - bulky waste collection.

To find out if your area is covered by Preston council please visit the GOV.UK - Find your local authority.

How do I book and pay for a collection?

You can book and pay for a collection in the followings ways:

  • online – complete bulky waste collection form 
     
  • telephone – contact Preston Wasteline on 01772 906904
     
  • in person – visit Argyll Road Depot or our contact centre


Please note: we accept credit/debit cards and will only collect once payment has been received. In the event of cancellation, this charge is not refunded.

How much will my collection cost?

The table below explains how much your collection will cost:

Number of Items Cost for collection Cost for those in receipt of housing/council tax benefit
up to 3* £18.50 £13.20

* A charge of £6.70 per item will be added to collections over 3 items. A maximum of 10 items can be collected

When will my items be collected?

Items will be collected on your arranged day between 7.00am and 3:30pm Monday - Friday. We do not collect at the weekend.

Items for collection must be left outside the property in an easily accessible place. E.g. your front gate, back alley/walk way, outside the alley gate or near the bin area (if you live in a flat).

Please place your items outside either the night before or morning of the collection. Ensure all items are together in one place. We will only pick up the items you have specified.

What can and can't be collected?

Our service covers most items such as suites, settee’s, mattresses and old bed collection; basically any household item that you no longer need.

We can collect rolls of carpet.  However please note that our collections are carried out by a crew of two.  Excessive amounts of carpet which cannot be easily manoeuvred by a two person crew may be classed as more than one item or may not be possible to collect.  Please ask for more information at the time of booking if you are unsure about the quantity of your items.

We will not collect:

  • asbestos
  • gas cylinders
  • clinical waste
  • windows (broken glass panels)
  • rubble

You may be able to take some of these items to your local recycling centre, though please check what they accept before making a journey.
