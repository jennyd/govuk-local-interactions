Customer feedback

Listening and learning

We aim to provide high quality services that meet our customers' needs. By telling us how we are doing, you can help shape these services in the future. Your feedback is something that we value highly and can learn from.

What is feedback?

Feedback can be a comment, compliment or a complaint.

  • A comment is any thought, suggestion or good idea on how we could improve the services we provide to our customers.

  • A compliment is where you have been very satisfied with a service you have received and you tell us about it.

  • A complaint is where you feel we have not treated you fairly or politely and/or not done something we should have done and/or done something badly.

If we’ve done something wrong please tell us as soon as possible as we may be able to sort it out straight away.

How to give feedback

  • Use our online form

  • Use the smiley/sad faces on the right hand side of each webpage and submit a comment

  • Email Customer Services

  • Contact us on twitter

  • Send a letter to Customer Services, East Northamptonshire Council, Cedar Drive, Thrapston, NN14 4LZ

  • Speak to us in person at our Customer Service Centres at Thrapston, Rushden or Oundle

  • Telephone us on 01832 742211

  • Speak to your ward councillor. Find out who s/he is

Our Customer Feedback and Complaints Policy is available as a download.
