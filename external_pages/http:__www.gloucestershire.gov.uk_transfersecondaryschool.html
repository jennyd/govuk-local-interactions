Apply for a secondary school place

Transfer to secondary school & middle to high school places for September 2016

The online transfer to Secondary School and Middle to High School for September 2016 application service has now closed.

You can make a "Late" application using the late common application form (see downloads) or by sending an email application stating your child's name, DOB, address (if different please include your address), your name and telephone number and the schools of choice in preference order (please include any further information if appropriate, i.e. siblings, medical, public care, etc)

Please be aware that as a late application it will not be considered until all the on-time applications have been processed; and in some cases a school place may not be allocated until after our re-consideration process which means that you may not be notified of your child's school place until after 1 April 2016.

Please read the admissions booklets for 2016/17 (on the left) which provide important information about our admissions procedures and policies.

Key Dates

  • From Monday 1 June 2015 - midday on Friday 3 July 2015 - Online registration period for grammar entrance test. Now Closed. If you require information regarding late testing arrangements please contact the Grammar Schools direct using the link below.
  • September 2015 - Letters sent out via a child's primary school explaining how to apply for secondary school/academy places. If your child attends an independent school or an out of county school a letter will be sent directly to your home address. If you are a Gloucestershire resident and you still have not received a letter after 14 September 2015 please contact coordinated admissions, see below link for contact details.
  • From 7 September to 31 October 2015 online secondary school transfer and middle to high school application facility open.
  • Saturday 12 September 2015 - Grammar entrance test day.
  • By Monday 12 October 2015 - Grammar test results sent to parents.
  • Saturday 31 October 2015 - Closing date for all applications
  •  Tuesday 1 March 2016 - Allocation Day.
  • Tuesday 15 March 2016 - Closing date to return reply form accepting the school/academy place allocated or requesting reconsideration.
  • After Monday 4 April 2016 - Letters sent out advising outcome of reconsideration
  • Tuesday 12 April 2016 - Closing date for return of reconsideration reply forms
  • May - July 2016 - Appeals

Transfer to Secondary School places for September 2015

Applications for a transfer to secondary school place for September 2015 has now closed.  To enquire about or apply for a year 7 place from September 2015 you now need to apply through in-year admissions.

Applying for a school place during the school year (in-year applications)

To apply for a school place if you move to the area during the school year or your child needs to move school for other reasons go to our in-year admissions page..

Need help?

We cannot make decisions for you or recommend a particular school but we can guide and advise you about: school admissions policies, facilities, pupil numbers etc. We can also help you fill in the application forms. Contact the Co-ordinated Admissions Team with your enquiry.
