What is Links? 

Links is the short title of "Links: the Chesterfield and North East Derbyshire Council for Voluntary Service and Action Limited". (CVS) 

 

Links provides support to voluntary organisations and community groups operating in the local government districts of Chesterfield and North East Derbyshire. Links also leads projects and works in partnership across a wider area of Derbyshire.
