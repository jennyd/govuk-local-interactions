Grants for voluntary sector organisations

We offer the following grants to voluntary organisations and community groups, including town and parish councils.

  • Capital Grants (for community projects) - NOW OPEN!
  • Young Achiever Awards - closed until 2016-17
  • Revenue Grants - closed until 2016-17

Other types of council and partnership funding

  • Flood grants for businesses and homeowners - funding for those affected by the floods in 2013/14
  • Disabled Facilities Grants - funding for home adaptations (please contact the Disabled Facilities Grant team on 01235 520202 for more information)
  • Community loans - loans to registered charities and parish councils
  • Supporting our town centres - helping support the vitality of our town centres
  • SOBusiness - supporting our local businesses
  • Business rates relief - how to apply for a reduction in your business rates
  • Energy saving grants - helping you make your home more energy-efficient
  • Home improvement loans - support for changes to your home

Funding advice - finding other sources of funding

We help fund Oxfordshire Community and Voluntary Action (OCVA), who support voluntary and community sector groups and organisations based in Oxfordshire and offer all kinds of advice, including sources of funding and getting fit to fund.

We recommend any organisation looking for funding talks to OCVA before applying to our schemes, you can contact them on 01865 251948 or at admin@ocva.org.uk This link will take you to an external website... to discuss potential funding options for your project or service.
