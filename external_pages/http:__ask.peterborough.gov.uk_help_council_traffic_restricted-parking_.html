Can I buy a permit if I live in a parking restriction zone?

Yes, you can apply for a resident parking permit which allows you to park your car/s in a residents marked bay in the street or zone where you live. Permits are available to purchase for 6 or 12 months. Download, completed and return the parking permit application form to the cash office at Bayard Place.
