Councillors

This is where you can find out detailed information about each of the Councillors.

You are able to search for your Councillor alphabetically or by Ward or by political party by selecting one of the links below.

You can also view all elected Members on a given date by using the Elected Member Filter.

You can access additional information about a Councillor by clicking on their name or photograph.

The political make-up of the Council is:

  • Conservative 27
  • Liberal Democrat 20
  • Labour 9
  • Highwoods and Stanway Independent 3
  • Vacancy 1

The Leader of the Council and the Cabinet

The Leader of the Council is elected annually at the Council’s Annual meeting. The Council is currently run by a joint administration consisting of the Liberal Democrat Group, the Labour Group and the Highwoods and Stanway Independent Group. The Council Leader is Councillor Paul Smith (Liberal Democrat).

The Cabinet is made up of eight Councillors, each of whom is responsible for a specific area, known as a Portfolio. The political composition of the Cabinet is 5 Liberal Democrat members, 2 Labour members and 1 Highwoods and Stanway Independent member. Further information about the membership of the Cabinet and Portfolio Holder responsibilities is available here.
