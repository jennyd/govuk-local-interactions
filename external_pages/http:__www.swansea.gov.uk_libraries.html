Libraries

Have you heard about our BookMyne library app? If you are using a mobile or tablet you can renew and reserve items on the BookMyne library app. The app is available from the Apple iTunes storeOpens new window and the Google Play storeOpens new window for Android.

Libraries in Swansea Find out more about your local library including opening times, events and facilities.
Renew and reserve library items You can renew and reserve books and DVDs online through a computer or using our app.
Online library resources As a library member you can access lots of online information resources that we subscribe to (external link).
Library events Find out the events and activities that are going on at your local library.
eBooks, eAudiobooks and eMagazines Library members can download eBooks, eAudiobooks and eMagazines for free.
Library Line information service The library line information service can help you with any queries you may have including where to find information.
Joining the library and membership information Sign up to become a member for free online or at any of our 17 libraries.
Library charges Find out charges for reservations, overdue books and more.
Children's library service Children of any age are welcome to join the library.
