Funding enquiries

Important information about the Youth Opportunity Fund and Youth Capital Fund.

Following announcements from Central Government about the reduction in funding we would be receiving this year, the Youth Opportunity Fund and Youth Capital Fund are no longer available. Hillingdon Council is committed to supporting local projects for young people and will continue to invest in initiatives through the Young People's Fund.

Why will the Youth Opportunity Fund not exist any more?

At the moment, the Government have not been able to give councils as much money as they have in previous years so we are having to be more careful about what we spend.  This means that some funds, like the Youth Opportunity Fund can no longer be available.  Hillingdon Council thinks that being able to provide youth facilities and activities is very important and so young people, and those who work with them, will still be able to apply for funding from the council through the young People's Fund.

Will I still be able to get funding for my project?

If you have a group, project or activity that you think could benefit from funding then you will still be able to apply to Hillingdon Council for funding - just not through the Youth Opportunity Fund or Youth Capital Fund.  As with all funding you will have to fill out an application form and a decision will be made as to whether funding can be offered.  The council cannot offer funding for every single application it receives, but it does wherever possible try and help as many young people as it can.

How do I contact the council if I want to get funding?

You can contact the council by calling 01895 277816 and asking for an application form to be sent through to you.
