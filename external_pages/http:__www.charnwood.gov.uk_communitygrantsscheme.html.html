Community Grants Scheme

What do the Hathern Band and the Citizens’ Advice Bureau have in common? The answer is that they’ve both benefited from Charnwood Borough Council’s Community Grants Scheme.

So far this year Charnwood Borough Council has given out over £25,000 in Community Grants to Charnwood based voluntary organisations including Parish Councils, play schemes and even musical outfits.

Cllr Max Hunt is a keen advocate of the scheme, he said: “Community Grants are a fantastic resource. It can be hard for small, voluntary organisations to raise funds in order to further themselves and we want to give them as much of a helping hand as we can because often these organisations are the ones that can really make a difference to a community.”

Last year 29 applications were made to the Council for Community Grants and Charnwood Borough Council supported 27 of them. Applications can be made through the website – www.charnwood.gov.uk – or by phoning 01509 634556 and can be made all year round but will only be considered early in the months of July, September and November of this year.
