Street cleaning

Street cleaning

We clean litter from adopted pavements, pedestrian areas and council-owned car parks within the district. Our teams are based in Barnstaple, Ilfracombe, South Molton and Lynton and Lynmouth, each following an agreed programme of works. 

South West Highways are contracted by Devon County Council to maintain and clean highways and roads. 

Commercial street cleaning

North Devon Council offers a commercial street cleaning service. This includes small, private car parks to large industrial or factory areas. Our service is flexible to suit your needs and we can offer regular contracts to ensure your site stays clean and safe, as well as one-off cleans, for example, after an event. 

If you would like to enquire about a private street sweep and the costs of this service, please email tradewaste@northdevon.gov.uk or phone 01271 318509. 

Dead animals on roads

We will arrange for the collection of dead dogs and cats that are reported to be on a public highway or pavement. However, this does not apply to animals on private land, for example, a public footpath running across private land.
