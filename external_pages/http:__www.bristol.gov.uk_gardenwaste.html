Garden waste

You can order a green garden waste bin or buy garden waste sacks.

We can collect your garden waste. This service is only available to domestic properties. 

Order a green garden waste bin

If you would like regular weekly collections you should order a green garden waste bin from us. We won't collect bins that have been brought from other council areas.

We charge £21 for a 240 litre Bristol green garden waste bin, plus £32 annual service charge. 

If you get income support, pension credits, housing or council tax benefits we charge £10 for the bin and £18 for annual service charge.

You get a £3 discount off the annual service charge for paying by Direct Debit.

Contact us on 0117 922 2100 to order.

You are responsible for your bin’s security, and any repairs needed to it. To order a replacement bin, call us on 0117 922 2100.

We may not collect garden waste from a damaged bin.

We only collect from Bristol city council bins.

If you move house or want to cancel your collections

Contact us on 0117 922 2100 or waste.services@bristol.gov.uk.

If you move outside of the Bristol boundary, we can’t give you a refund.

Garden waste sacks

A 75 litre garden waste sack costs £1. This includes kerbside collection. We’ll only collect sacks with the Bristol city Council logo on. You can buy these in bundles of 25 from us or from authorised sellers.

Buy garden waste sacks

You can also buy individual sacks from:

Other ways to buy

To buy garden waste sacks in bundles of 25, contact us on 0117 922 2100.

Arrange a sack collection

You’ll need to contact us each time you want to book a collection. To book a collection go online or call at least two working days before your weekly collection day.

Over busy periods you may not be guaranteed a booking with your next recycling collection.

Book a garden waste sack collection

You can also contact us on 0117 922 2100 to make a booking, or to cancel a collection, or email waste.services@bristol.gov.uk.

What you can and can’t include in your garden waste bin or sack

You can include:

  • Branches - up to 10cm or 4 inches in thickness
  • Flowers
  • Grass cuttings
  • Hedge prunings
  • Leaves
  • Leaves and bark
  • Plants
  • Straw or sawdust - bedding material from pets such as gerbils, hamsters, guinea pigs and rabbits, as long as fed a vegetarian diet
  • Twigs
  • Weeds - no Japanese Knotweed

You can’t include:

  • Cardboard - please put in or next to your green box
  • Cat, dog or other pet litter
  • Compost
  • Japanese Knotweed
  • Nappies
  • Plant pots
  • Soil and turf
  • Stone or rubble
  • Treated wood
  • Vacuum cleaner contents
