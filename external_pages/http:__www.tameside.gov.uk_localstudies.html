Online Enquiries

Use our on-line enquiry form to contact us or to order archives in advance of your visit. We require 24 hours’ notice to retrieve documents. Please note that all requests for archives to be produced on Saturdays need to be received no later than 2pm on the Thursday preceding your visit. We will acknowledge receipt of all requests to confirm that the documents can be produced. Please bring a copy of your acknowledgement when you visit.

Minimum charge for look-up service £5

This includes up to 2 free A4 copies (photocopy / printing / microfilm print) & postage. Additional copies 70p A4, £1.40 A3

Important: Please include your name and postal address when you send us a message as this will help us provide a full response to your enquiry.
