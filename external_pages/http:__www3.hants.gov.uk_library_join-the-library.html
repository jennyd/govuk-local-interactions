Join the library

Anyone living, working or studying in Hampshire can become a member of Hampshire Libraries. You can join online or in person at any Hampshire library. You do not need any identification to join the library.

Joining the library online is easy:

Check the terms of membership
  • What you can borrow
  • Extra membership plans
  • Reserving
  • Renewing
  • Fines and charges
  • Keeping your details up to date
Complete the registration form

If you are happy with all the terms of borrowing, and the way fines and charges are priced, you can join online now.

If you are under 16 you cannot yet join online. To register please visit any of our libraries or Discovery Centres with your parent, guardian or carer.

Start reserving titles

Once you have joined online you will be given a temporary membership number and PIN (Personal Identification Number). You can use this straight away to reserve titles from our catalogue.

Receive your library card

Your library card will be posted to you at the address you have registered, ready for use. The membership number on the card will give you access to ebooks and online resources.

Good to know

You don't need to join again if you already have a Hampshire library card

If your membership has expired you don't need to apply for a new card. Just visit your library to renew your membership.

If you’ve lost your library card please visit your library to obtain a free replacement.

If you have a library card but don't know your PIN, you can get a PIN reminder.
