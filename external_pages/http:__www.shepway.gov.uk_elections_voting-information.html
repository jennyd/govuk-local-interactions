Elections

Voting information

Information on the type of elections that happened this year, and how you can vote for the next elections

On 7th May 2015 there were three elections that took place:

  • UK Parliamentary
  • Shepway District Council
  • Town/Parish councils  

View results

How can I vote?

You must be registered to vote by the registering deadline (made public near to an election) in order to vote. It takes less than five minutes to apply online.

Due to the recent changes in electoral registration, the Elections team recommend that you register as early as possible and do not wait until the deadline.

If you are not sure if you are registered, please contact the Elections team.

 

Different ways to vote

Postal vote

Applications to vote by post must be made weeks before the election so you are included in the distribution.

Forms can be downloaded  here [194kb]

Alternatively, forms can be found on the About My Vote website

Proxy vote

Applications to vote by proxy must also be made weeks before the election date.
