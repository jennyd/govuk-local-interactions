Pay your Council Tax

 

Direct Debit

Direct Debit is the easiest way to pay your bill. We offer customers a choice of two dates each month when they can pay. These are the 1st and 16th. You can also ask to pay over 12 months instead of 10, or however many months remain in the year.

You are also protected by the Direct Debit Guarantee, which promises that you will get your money back straight away if anything goes wrong.

  • Set up a Direct Debit Online

Internet

You can pay your Council Tax bill online with a debit or credit card, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

  • Pay your bill online - Please note: There is a 2% charge when making payments using credit cards, and if you are using a debit card from a foreign bank. No charges for debit cards from UK banks.

Telephone

You can pay your bill by credit or debit card by using our 24 hour automated telephone payment system on 0300 456 2231.
Have your card and reference number ready when you call.

Telephone and online banking or standing order

To pay your bill via telephone or online banking, or by standing order with your bank you will need our bank details:

  • Our bank account number is: 71275984
  • Our sort code is: 40-16-22

Post Office and Paypoint

You can pay your bill by cash, debit card or credit card at any Post Office or by cash at any Paypoint outlet nationwide. You will need a bill with a barcode on it to pay. If you already have one of these simply take it along and make the payment.
