Local and family history

Birkenhead Reference Library holds a wealth of resources on local and family history.

A range of resources are also available at the Wirral Archives Service.

Resources available at Birkenhead Library

Obituaries

Obituaries from 1880-1896 and 2010-2012 are available to view at Birkenhead Reference Library. Make a note of the year you require from the list below, there is a 75p charge to use the reader and a 75p charge for any prints. 

  • Obituaries at Birkenhead Reference Library

Digital images

A large collection of digital images is available at Birkenhead Reference Library to view and purchase.

  • Digital image index

Wirral Remembers 1914-2014

  • Wirral Remembers materials held in Birkenhead Library

Birkenhead History Society newsletter

Issues available from 1974 to present.

Camden series (Royal Historical Society)

  • third series (1900-1963). View the index online
  • fourth series (1969-1993). View the index online
  • fifth series (1993-present). View the index online

Hard copies of the indices are available at Birkenhead Reference Library.

The Chetham Society

  • old series (1843-1883)
  • new series (1883-1947)
  • third series (1949-present)

Indices are available at Birkenhead Reference Library.

Magazines and journals

  • the Cheshire Ancestor - journal of the Family History Society of Cheshire (1987-present)
  • Family Tree Magazine. Issues available from 1997-June 2010. View the article index online
  • Your Family Tree. Issues available from 2011 onwards
  • Genealogists’ Magazine - journal of the Society of Genealogists. Back issues kept for a year, local articles kept permanently
  • Lancashire Parish Register Society. Hard copy index available for Wirral articles. View the full index online
  • The Local Historian - journal of the British Association for Local History. Back issues held for a year, local articles kept permanently
  • Local History News - magazine of the British Association for Local History. Back issues kept for a year, local articles kept permanently
  • Record Society of Lancashire and Cheshire. View the index online
  • Recusant History - journal of the Catholic Record Society
  • Transactions of the Historic Society of Lancashire and Cheshire
