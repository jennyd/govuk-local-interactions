Before you begin...you will need:

  • Any information you have been given about applying for a school place
  • Your child’s full address (including postcode)
  • Your child’s date of birth
  • Approx 15-20 minutes to complete the application (but you can save and come back to it later)
  • For security, the website will timeout if you login and do not use it
  • Do not use the Browser's Back button; use the Back button on each page

Welcome to Luton Borough Council's online admissions facility

If you are new to this site and wish to make an online application, you will need to register with the local authority. Click on 'Register' where you will need a valid email address so we can send you your password.

If you have used the online sytem before, you will need to enter the email address and password that you previously used.

Parents should use the online application form if:

  • you would like to apply for a school place outside of the normal admission rounds (in-year applications)

  • Starting school admissions round - your child was born between 1/9/11 - 31/8/12 and you would like to apply for a school place.  Please note, you can use this website to apply for a school place if you live within Luton.  If you pay Council Tax to another Council you will need to apply using their online application system.

  • your child is transferring to junior school in September 2016.  Please note, you can use this website to apply for a school place if you live within Luton.  If you pay Council Tax to another Council you will need to apply using their online application system.

  • you would like your child to transfer to middle school in September 2016.  Please note, you can use this website to apply for a school place if you live within Luton.  If you pay Council Tax to another Council you will need to apply using their online application system.

  • your child is transferring to high school in September 2016.  Please note, you can use this website to apply for a school place if you live within Luton.  If you pay Council Tax to another Council you will need to apply using their online application system.

  • you would like your child to transfer to upper school in September 2016.  Please note, you can use this website to apply for a school place if you live within Luton.  If you pay Council Tax to another Council you will need to apply using their online application system.

The benefits of online admissions are:

  • easy step by step instructions

  • preferences can be changed at any time until the closing date. Please note, applications will need to be re-submitted each time the application is edited.  Online applications for a school place outside of the normal admissions round (in-year applications) cannot be changed one you have submitted your applications

  • you will receive confirmation that your application has been submitted.

We apologise for any confusion that this may cause and the issue is currently being looked into by our software supplier, who are committed to resolving the problem with minimun delay.  Please disregard any duplicate emails that you receive relating to the application process.  This issue will not affect the submission of their online application or have any impact on the process of allocating school places.

For the starting school admissions round and the junior and high school transfers

Parents/carers will receive information from Luton Borough Council's Admissions Team detailing their child's Unique Identification Number (UID).  Once logged into the system you will be prompted to enter this number along with your child's date of birth.

For starting school applications only - once you have entered your child's date of birth and clicked on the Confirm button, you should click on the link 'I want to apply for both of the above' and click Next.

Applications for the middle school and upper school transfers

Parents/carers can apply for a middle school and/or upper school once logged into the system by inputting their child's details.  Parents/carers cannot apply using a Unique Identification  Number (UID).

The closing date for the high school and upper school transfers is 31 October 2015 and the closing date for the starting school admissions round and junior and middle school transfers is 15 January 2016.
