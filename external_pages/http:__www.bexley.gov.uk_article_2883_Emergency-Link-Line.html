Emergency Link Line

Link Line offers security and help to people at the touch of a button. It uses technology to monitor and respond to a person's well being and summons help if required.

The service is provided by an organisation called Medvivo and is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year service, enabling you to live as independently as possible in your own home.

Who is Link Line for?

Link Line is for anyone who needs it, eg those living alone, older people, those with physical or learning disabilities or issues with their mental health.

How does Link Line work?

Medvivo will install a Link Line box in your home. This is plugged into a modern telephone socket and nearby power point. It operates on your telephone landline and is linked to a computer system which holds all your information. By pressing the pendant supplied or button on the alarm box you will automatically dial the call monitoring service will be connected to a monitoring officer who will have all your details in front of them. You can talk to us from anywhere within your home via your alarm box and can let us know what assistance you need.

The supportive professional staff will work out with you what help you need, not just for emergencies, but for a variety of things, which may include phoning a nominated contact on your behalf. We can also arrange for medical assistance, despatch an emergency response worker or contact the emergency services if required.

Once your application for the Link Line has been accepted, all equipment is installed for you and the engineer will demonstrate how to operate it and will show you how to make monthly test calls.

How much does Link Line cost?

There are two levels of service:

Gold Service

This provides an installation, maintenance, full monitoring and response service. If your designated contacts are not available, we will send one of our officers if necessary. The cost of the Gold service is £21.45 per month (2014).

Silver Service

This is an installation, maintenance and monitoring service and we will contact the people who support you if there is a problem. The cost is £16.90 per month (2014). If at any time none of your emergency contacts are available to assist and it is necessary for our response officer to visit you (other than for a technical problem) a call-out charge of £70 will be made on each occasion. If possible we will discuss this with you at the time.

You can pay for your link line either by a monthly direct debit or by a quarterly invoice sent to you. If you are on a low income you may be eligible for financial support following a financial assessment.

Charges for link line are agreed by the Council and are subject to the Council's annual review of all fees and charges.

How do I apply for Link Line?

You can apply for the link line service using the online application form. To request a printed application form, or find out more about the service please contact Medvivo on 03000 111 247 or you can email the team at telecare@medvivo.com.
