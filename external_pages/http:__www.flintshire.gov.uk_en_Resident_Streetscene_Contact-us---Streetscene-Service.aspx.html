Streetscene contact us

Streetscene is about giving you an easy point of contact for all services associated with your streets, highways and open spaces. You can report a problem that affects the street scene like litter, dog fouling, recycling, faulty streetlights, problems with the road or pavement. Or you can request a service e.g. obtain a RADAR key for a disabled toilet.

Report a problem online (will open an e-form) – provide details, attach photos and mark the location on a map.

Mobile users can download the Flintshire App, provide details and attach photos. You can choose to report problems anonymously or register. Benefits of registering include receiving updates on the progress of your report, ability to see all your previous reports and give feedback.

If you prefer email, contact us using streetscene@flintshire.gov.uk

If you prefer to speak to us telephone our helpline on 01352 701234.
