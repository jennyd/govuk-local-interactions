Announcement

Properties available immediately. 'Make a house your home'

Welcome to Homefinder RCT

A new way of allocating housing association properties within Rhondda Cynon Taf.

In partnership with six housing association landlords including:

Cynon Taf Community Housing Group,
Hafod Housing Association,
Newydd Housing Association
RCT Homes,
Rhondda Housing Association
Wales West Housing

Homefinder RCT will give you access to over 15,000 rented homes in Rhondda Cynon Taf.

Homefinder RCT will also provide you with access to low cost home ownership properties via the Council’s Homestep scheme.

In addition Homefinder RCT will advertise properties available to rent with private landlords who you can contact directly without the need to make a bid.

Our aim is to give you more choice in deciding where you want to live.
