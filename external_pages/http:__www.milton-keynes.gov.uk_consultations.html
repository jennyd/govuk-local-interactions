Consultations

Milton Keynes Council is committed to providing quality public services that meet the needs of our customers. We therefore want to work more closely with residents, communities and voluntary organisations.

Milton Keynes Council actively supports and encourages involvement to ensure residents and communities have the opportunity and ability to express their views, participate in making decisions, have more control over the services we deliver and are more involved in monitoring those services.
