Dangerous structures

We deal with reports of dangerous structures across Lichfield District, South Staffordshire District and Tamworth Borough. These are usually as a result of fire, weather and or neglect.

We work with owners to make sure the structure is made safe. This sometimes includes taking enforcement action or immediate action to remove the danger, depending on the risk.
