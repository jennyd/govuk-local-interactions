Concessionary Passes for Over 60’s and Disabled Pass Holders

New National Scheme from 1 April 2008

For enquires on concessionary passes please contact Transport for Greater Manchester  on 0161 244 1050.

The National travel concession scheme for bus travel was revised on 1 April 2008. The concession changed from a local to a national entitlement.

The over 60’s and most disabled pass holders will be entitled to free local bus travel anywhere in England from 9.30am to 11.00pm Monday to Friday and all day at weekends and on bank Holidays

Concessionary Passes for Over 60’s and Disabled Pass Holders can contact TFGM on 0161 244 1050 or by visiting the TFGM Home page  for more information.
