Recycling at home

Kerbside recycling services for the residents of Sunderland make it easier to recycle their waste and make a big difference to the amount of rubbish we throw away in our household rubbish every day.

  • Reduce, reuse, recycle

    We all have a responsibility to minimise waste wherever possible.

  • Recycle

    The next best sustainable option after reduce and reuse - your items become a resource which can be remodelled or made into the same thing again and again.

  • Your blue recycling bin

    Your household blue bin makes it easier for you to recycle more of your waste from your doorstep and us to reduce the amount of waste we have to send to landfill.

  • Household garden waste (brown bins)

    Information on garden waste collection, charges and what you can put in your brown bin.

  • Composting

    Compost bins can be used within your property to manage your green waste and some raw food waste (including egg shells and peelings).

  • Recycling batteries and electrical equipment

    Battery and electrical equipment recycling facilities are available throughout the city.

  • Household Waste Reception and Recycling Centres

    Sunderland residents can use either of these Household Waste Reception and Recycling Centres.

  • Collections of large or bulky items of household waste

    There is a £15 charge for each bulky waste collection from your home, find out more.
