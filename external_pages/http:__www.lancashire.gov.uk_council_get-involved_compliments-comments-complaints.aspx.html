Send a compliment or comment or make a complaint to the council

We are committed to providing the best possible services and we welcome all your feedback, whether it is a complaint, comment or compliment.

  • How we handle your compliments, comments and complaints

You can submit a compliment, comment or complaint in a number of ways:

Online

If your compliment, comment or complaint is about:

  • Adult social care
  • Children social care
  • Schools
  • Libraries, museums and archives
  • Individual county councillors
  • Bus services
  • Quality of goods (Trading Standards)

If you want to report a street lighting, roads or highway fault use our dedicated online form.

Email

Send us your compliments, comments and complaints to complaintsandfeedback@lancashire.gov.uk.

Please ensure that your email contains:

  • Your full name
  • Your postal address
  • The date (where possible) and details of the compliment, comment or complaint
  • The service concerned where possible

Post

Comments, compliments and complaints forms for downloading and printing. Once printed, please send us your completed form to the relevant contact point.

  • Complaint form  (PDF, 88 KB)
  • Compliment and comment form  (PDF, 80.8 KB)

If you are unsure, where to send, please send it to

Complaints and appeals team
Legal and democratic services
Christ Church Precinct
County Hall
Preston
PR1 8XJ

Telephone

For social care complaints you can speak to one of our advisors on 0300 123 6720.

For complaints about any other council service please ring 0300 1236701.
