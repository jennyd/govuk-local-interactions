How to pay?

The Council Tax is to be paid monthly for ten months (unless other arrangements have been made, such as paying one lump sum, paying over 12 months, or paying for the remaining months of the financial year).

The payment must be made by the 1st of each month (except direct debit payments). 

You can pay your Council Tax by:

  • Direct debit – this is the easiest way of paying. You can choose to pay on the 5th, 15th, 21st or 28th of the month. To arrange a direct debit:
    • Download direct debit form and return to the address noted on the form.
    • phone (01286) 682701 or (01286) 682708 (you will need your bank detail at hand
  • Pay your Council Tax online
  • Phone: Call 01766 771000 (between 08:30 and 17:30 Monday to Friday)
    You can pay by Debit or Credit card (transaction surcharges apply with Credit card payments).
  • Post Office: You will need a special plastic swipe card to make a payment at a Post Office. To request your plastic swipe card please call us on (01286) 682701 or (01286) 682708.
  • Through the post: Send a cheque or postal order to: Income Unit, Council Offices, Shirehall Street, Caernarfon, LL55 1SH.
    All cheques and postal orders should be crossed 'Account Payee' and made payable to “Gwynedd Council”. Please quote your reference and name/address on the back of the cheque. Receipts will not be sent for cheque payments from now on.


Receive your Council Tax bill by e-mail

Help the environment by using less paper! You can now choose to receive your Council Tax bill by e-mail rather than through the post. For further information go to the electronic billing page.
