Extreme weather conditions

Emergencies - If you need to contact the Council outside normal working hours (8am to 5pm Monday to Friday), dial our main number 020 8303 7777.

This will connect you to the Emergency Duty Service. People who need emergency services (other than police, fire brigade and ambulance services) will be attended to by an emergency duty officer (EDO). Bexley's EDO is able to liaise with utility companies and Council officers to organise an appropriate action response to any issues.

Hot tips for cold snaps is designed to provide you with information and advice to help you, your family and neighbours during the winter months.

You can also use school closures where schools post the latest news on whether they are operating as normal, or if the weather is affecting them.

Relevant updates will also be issued via our Twitter page at @LBofBexley - so stay connected!
