Computers and Wi Fi

Last updated: 11/11/2015

Anyone can use the computers in our libraries.  The first hour is free for Cornwall Library members. Theres a small charge for longer use, visitors and non-members.

You will need to have your library card number and pin code to make a booking.  If you dont know your pin code, contact your local library.

Book a library computer

Book a library computer online

Use of our computers is subject to acceptance of our Computer Acceptable Use Policy. 

Charges for Computer Use

  • Cornwall Library members FREE for the first hour then £1.80 per hour
  • Concessions, Young Adult and Child members FREE for first two hours then £1.80 per hour
  • Other UK Library members with a valid visitors card £1.80 per hour
  • Non-members £3.60 per hour

Please note: If you owe more than £5.00 in library charges you will be charged the non-members fee.

Cancellations

  • If you need to cancel your booking, log in to our library computer booking system, find the session you have booked and select ‘delete’.

Free Wi Fi

  • Wi Fi is free to all library members and visitors with temporary membership. To access our free Wi Fi, simply show your valid membership card to the librarian and ask for the Wi Fi password.  Cornwall Council - Computer Acceptable Use Policy applies.

Free Computer Lessons

  • We provide FREE computer classes for beginners in selected branch libraries. Find out more in our leaflet, Need help getting online? or ask in your local library or One Stop Shop, or call us free on 0800 032 2345.
