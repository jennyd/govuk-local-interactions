About these dates

These dates relate to Hampshire Community and Controlled schools only, but usually Voluntary Aided & Foundation schools also subscribe to these dates.

Whilst every effort is made to ensure that these dates are accurate, there are occasions when circumstances beyond our control may result in changes.

Five days are also available for professional day closures (including inset days) during term time. These are specified by the headteacher and governors of each school. Please check with your school for further details.
