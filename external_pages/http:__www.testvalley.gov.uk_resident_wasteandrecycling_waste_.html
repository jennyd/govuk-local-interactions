Household Waste and Recycling Collection Service


The collection of household waste and recycling takes place on alternate weeks.

This means that your black wheeled bin will be emptied one week and your brown wheeled bin will be emptied the following week.

Your bin(s) should be placed at the edge of your property by 7:00 am on your collection day.  Your bin(s) should be returned onto your property as soon as possible following your collection.

Note: There will be some exceptions for flats where there are bin stores or chutes.

To check when your next collection is, please use the 'When are my bins collected' facility on the homepage.

To report a missed bin collection online, please follow this link:  My Test Valley
