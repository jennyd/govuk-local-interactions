Parking permits and season tickets

Season tickets

Temporary parking dispensations

We offer permits for vehicles to access Mansfield town centre and for those who regularly use our car parks.

Season tickets

Season tickets are available for some of our Mansfield town centre car parks.

Tickets are banded into three price ranges.

Gold - £90 per calendar month

Available for:

  • Four Seasons Shopping Centre multi-story car park
  • Walkden Street multi-story car park

Silver - £65 per calendar month

Available for:

  • Water Meadows Leisure Centre long stay car park
  • Toothill Lane car park
  • Church Lane car park
  • Victoria Street car park
  • Garden Road car park

Bronze - £40 per calendar month

Available for:

  • Robin Hill Line Station car park
  • Grove Street car park

Tickets are valid from the first to the last day of the month and available from the Old Town Hall, Mansfield Market Place, between 12.30pm and 1.30pm, Monday to Friday.

To apply for a season ticket, please contact us.

Trade parking dispensations

Temporary parking permits (called dispensations) are available for vehicles which need to access to park in the pedestrian area of Mansfield town centre to carry out a service.

The permits cost:

  • £15 per week, when five days' notice is given
  • £25 per week, when 48 hours' notice is given, or
  • £100 per year for regular service vehicles (i.e. carrying out window cleaning etc).

To apply for a temporary parking dispensation, please complete the  parking dispensation form [291kb].

Temporary permits will not normally be issued for more than 14 consecutive days. Extensions to dispensations are at the discretion of the Parking Manager.
