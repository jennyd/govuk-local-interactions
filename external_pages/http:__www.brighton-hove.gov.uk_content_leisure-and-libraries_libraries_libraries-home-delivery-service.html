Libraries home delivery service

If you have difficulty in getting to a library we offer

  • a longer loan period of six weeks
  • concessions on our fees and charges
  • collection of items by a friend or family member at any library
  • access to our online services like eBooks and other online resources
  • access to our services by telephone

Home delivery service

We offer a home delivery service to individuals who are unable to get to a library. If you have limited mobility, poor health or are a full time carer for someone who would like this service contact the home delivery service on 01273 294599 or via email at libraries@brighton-hove.gov.uk or staff at your local library. Watch a video from ITV News to find out more about the service.

Library online catalogue

You can search, renew and reserve online

You will need your library card number and PIN to make a reservation. You can also check what you have out on loan and renew items by logging in to your account. You are welcome to email your enquiries to us at libraries@brighton-hove.gov.uk
