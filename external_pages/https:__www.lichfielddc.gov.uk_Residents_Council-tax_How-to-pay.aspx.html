How to pay

You can pay your council tax in a number of ways. Find out more below:

  • By direct debit
  • By debit or credit card
  • At the post office
  • Credit and debit card payments by telephone
  • By cheque
  • Pay by instalments
Back To Top

By direct debit

The easiest way to pay your council tax is by direct debit. To set up a direct debit, call our customer services team on 01543 308900. Make sure you have your banking details and your council tax reference number to hand.

Alternatively you can fill in a direct debit payment form and return it to us by post to: Council Tax Payments Team, Lichfield District Council, Frog Lane, Lichfield, Staffs WS13 6YX.

  • Direct debit form - business rates
  • Direct debit form - council tax

Visit our ePayments section to pay.

Back To Top

By debit or credit card

Visit our ePayments section to pay with a credit or debit card.

Please note: we charge a 2% surcharge on all credit card payments for council tax and business rates. This is in line with many companies and councils up and down the country.

Processing credit card payments costs more, so the surcharge will help us to cover the cost of the additional processing. We are looking to roll this surcharge out to other e-payments soon.

We also want to discourage people from paying their regular bills such as council tax on credit card, which can lead to more debt in the district.

There is no surcharge on debit cards or other forms of payments, so you can avoid the surcharge by setting up a direct debit, using your debit card, or by paying with cash or by cheque. 

Back To Top

At the post office

You can use your payment swipe card at any post office. There is no charge for this service. 

Back To Top

Credit and debit card payments by telephone

Simply call us on 01543 308900 to pay over the phone. Please make sure you have your credit/debit card details and council tax reference number handy.

Back To Top

By cheque

Make your cheque payable to Lichfield District Council and write your council tax reference number on the reverse of the cheque. Send your cheque to: Council Tax Payments Team, Lichfield District Council, Frog Lane, Lichfield, Staffs WS13 6YX.

Back To Top

Pay by instalments

You can apply for your instalments to be paid over 12 months.

You must apply in writing to our revenues department (Revenues Department, Lichfield District Council, Frog Lane, Lichfield, Staffs WS13 6YX) no later than 10 March each year if you wish to pay in instalments between April and March.

After this date, the actual number of instalments we are able to offer will be affected by the date you apply, or from when you become liable for council tax. 

You should not withhold payments while your application is being processed. You must continue to pay your instalments on your previous bill until you are advised of your revised payments.
