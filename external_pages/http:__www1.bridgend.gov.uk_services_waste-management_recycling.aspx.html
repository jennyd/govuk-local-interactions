Recycling

The average household throws away about one tonne of waste each year – so why not use the council’s new kerbside recycling scheme to recycle the majority of your waste. The new collection service for recycling more waste was introduced in June 2010. Information on the scheme can be found below.

This recycling section provides information on other recycling initiatives in Bridgend County Borough.

The council also works closely with schools to deliver an educational waste awareness campaign concerning waste, recycling and other relevant environmental issues to children.

For further information about any aspect of recycling, please contact the recycling helpline on (01656) 643643 or email us at cleanupthecounty@bridgend.gov.uk
