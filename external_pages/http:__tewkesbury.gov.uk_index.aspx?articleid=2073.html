Council owned property

Available for lease: Yard/depot with a range of workshop/office & storage buildings Located on Lower Lode Lane, Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire. The site may be suitable for a number of uses which may include haulage yard/transport depot, vehicle hire/leasing depot.


Depot 1         
Depot 2

Lower Lode Lane is located off the Gloucester Road approximately quarter of a mile to the south west of Tewkesbury town centre.

The Gloucester Road is the main arterial route into Tewkesbury from the south and leads directly to the A38, which provides convenient access to Gloucester, approximately 8 miles to the south and junction 9 of the M5 Motorway, approximately 2 miles to the east.

A fenced and gated level site with a tarmacadam surface extending to approximately 0.372 hectares (0.92 acres). The site benefits from a vehicle washdown area with interceptor tank. There is also a bonded fuel tank above ground, and an underground fuel store with pump.

                                            
Depot 3

Three buildings are located on the site which includes a workshop/office building, a storage unit with mess room and showers and a concrete sectional garage. The property is connected to mains electricity, drainage, water and gas.

Terms/Viewing:

Terms can be discussed upon formal request. Viewing can be prearranged with the landlord please note that 24 hours notice will be required.
