Have your say

Have your say in council surveys and consultations.

Fabian Way Innovation Corridor masterplan framework consultation Comments are invited on a draft masterplan framework which will form the basis for an integrated approach to planning along the Fabian Way Corridor.
Swansea tall buildings strategy (draft revised guidance) We would like to know your thoughts on the draft strategy that has been prepared.
City centre strategic framework review The strategic framework review aim is to make the city centre an attractive destination for people to live, work and visit. Have your say on the draft report on the Swansea city centre website.
Swansea Voices Swansea Voices Citizens' Panel consists of a representative sample of 1,250 residents who are regularly consulted by the Council about its services and local issues.
