Allotments

We have currently 28 allotment sites across the borough.

All of the council's allotment sites have waiting lists due to the volume of applicants being sent in.

Please note, the waiting list for allotments in Chiswick has now been closed.

Allotments are available in:

  • Bedfont

  • Brentford

  • Cranford

  • Ealing

  • Feltham

  • Hanworth

  • Heston

  • Hounslow

  • Isleworth

Carillion, on behalf of London Borough of Hounslow, manage many allotment sites throughout the borough which are available to hire.

For information on hiring an allotment and prices please visit their website (link on right) or tel: 0845 456 2796 or email: hounslow.info@carillionservices.co.uk
