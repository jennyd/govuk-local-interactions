Pay your rent

Look at where and how to pay your rent. You can download your direct debit form here, pay online with a credit or debit card on our secure 24-hour payment service, or you can call for free on 0800 052 0173 at any time.

Report ASB

We take an uncompromising stance on ASB so that our tenants and residents can enjoy peace and quiet in their own home and feel safe and secure in the area they live.

Find a home

In this section, you can find out how to join the waiting list, how to find a home using Nottingham Homelink, and how to take part in a mutual exchange.

Accessibility

We aim to make this website accessible by everyone as easily as possible.

Report a repair

We want to help you keep your home in a good state of repair so that it’s a nice place for you to live. If you have something that needs repairing, please let us know and if it’s our responsibility, we’ll do all we can to get it fixed.

Read news

Our tenants and leaseholders get our newsletter, NCH News, four times a year – in January, April, July and October. You can read the latest copy here. You can also read our latest media releases.

Get involved

We’d love you to get involved in what we do – you’ll have fun, make new discoveries, meet new people and help to make things better for you and for everyone else.
