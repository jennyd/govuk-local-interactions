Web accessibility and usability

Listen to website
You can listen to our website through Speak IT plus, which has a clear visible toolbar and user friendly icons to make it easy to understand. Listen to website now.

You can also visit the BBC website for further web accessibility help, they have lots of detailed information enabling computer users to make the most of the internet whatever their ability or disability.

Plain English Award
Our website has been approved and awarded the Internet Crystal Mark. This allows us to display the Crystal Mark logo and we will continue to ensure we achieve this every year. For more information see the Plain English Campaign website.   

Socitm - Better Connected
Socitm - Better Connected is an annual survey of all local authority websites to identify good practice based on extensive evidence-based research.
