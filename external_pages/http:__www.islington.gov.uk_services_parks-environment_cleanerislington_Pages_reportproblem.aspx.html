Clean Streets app

You can now report dog fouling online using the Clean Islington app, which is available for free for iPhone, Android and Windows Phone via app stores.

My eAccount

You can report street issues and problems through My eAccount – an online system which allows you to manage your council accounts in the same place.

The system is a new, faster and simpler way for residents to report everything from graffiti, dog fouling, potholes, damaged paving, litter, dumped waste and much more. As well as problems on your street you do everything from view your council tax, pay for parking and request a new recycling container. Homes for Islington and Partners for Islington residents can also manage their rent accounts and estate parking on My eAccount.

Not registered yet? It’s simple, just visit www.islington.gov.uk/myeaccount
