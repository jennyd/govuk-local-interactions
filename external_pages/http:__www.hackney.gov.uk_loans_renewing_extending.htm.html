Reserving, renewing and extending loans

Library members can reserve items and renew or extend existing loans online.

Reserve or renew items online

See your books on loan, reserve or renew items. You will need your library card number and PIN.

If you need a new library card or want to know your PIN, visit your local library. We can not tell you your PIN over the phone

Reserve or renew items

Renew by phone

Call  020 8356 2539, Monday to Friday, 8am-8pm and Saturdays 9am-1pm.

  • You will need your library card number.
  • If you need a new library card, visit your local library.

Renew in person

You can renew items by visiting your local library - bring your library card.
