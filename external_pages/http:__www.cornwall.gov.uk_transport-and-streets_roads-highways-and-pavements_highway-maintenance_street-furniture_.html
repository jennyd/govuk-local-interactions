Street furniture

Last updated: 27/10/2015

The Council is responsible for providing and maintaining a range of items of street furniture. These include benches and seating, cycle racks, bollards, etc.

Unauthorised items, such as advertising A boards, placed on the highway or footway by individuals or business may cause an obstruction or compromise the safety of road users and pedestrians. Such items will be removed by the Council.

To report a problem with street furniture please use our report a problem with a highway form or use our contact details to the right.

Alternatively contact us at any of our one stop shops or write to us at your nearest group centre office:

  • Western Group Centre, Radnor Road, Scorrier, Redruth, TR16 5EH
  • Central Group Centre,Castle Canyke Road, Bodmin, PL31 1DZ
