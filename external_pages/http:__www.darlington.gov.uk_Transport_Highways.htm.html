Information and maps on the upgrades to street lights across the borough

Weare responsible for maintaining and improving the roads, footpaths, bridges (with the exception of railway bridges), street lights and traffic signals in Darlington. This includes 574km (366 miles)of roads, over12,500 street lights, and90 bridges (including culverts and subways).

A1(M) and A66

The A1(M) and A66 are maintained by theHighways Agency[external link]

Railway bridges

We are not responsible for maintaining railway bridges. ContactNetwork Rail[external link] for help and advice
