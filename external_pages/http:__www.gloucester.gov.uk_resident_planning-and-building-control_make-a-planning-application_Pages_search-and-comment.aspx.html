Search and comment using Public Access

Public Access

You can view and comment on planning applications made to the City Council via Public Access.

Features of Public Access

  • Track the Progress of an Application
  • Submit Comments about an Application
  • Save Searches
  • Search the Weekly/Monthly List for Validated/Decided Applications
  • View historical Applications
  • View Property Details by Reference of Map

We reserve the right to withhold from publication on this website any comments or parts of comments not considered suitable for public view for reasons including offensive, personal or defamatory content.

Copyright Notice

Planning application information submitted to the Council is protected by the Copyright Acts (Section 47, 1988 Act). You may only use material which is downloaded and/or printed for consultation purposes, to compare current applications with previous schemes and to check whether developments have been completed in accordance with approved plans. Further copies must not be made without the prior permission of the copyright owner. If you link to Public Access you have acknowledged that you have read, understood and agree to the copyright and other limitations.

Limitations

The online planning history contained within the site at present is incomplete and should not be relied upon in connection with a property transaction. Anyone wishing to obtain planning information for this purpose are advised to carry out a local land charges search through the City Council's Local Land Charges Section or the National Land Information Service (NLIS)​ using one of the official Licensed Channels. No responsibility will be taken for any errors or omissions in the planning history information obtained from 'Public Access'. Similarly the information contained within Public Access does not constitute in any way a formal notification of a planning decision, and as such any actions taken as a result of information displayed on the site are undertaken entirely at the viewer's own risk.

Gloucester City Council reserve the right to remove or not display certain planning application information for the confidentiality or other reasons.

Make a Comment

The Council is required to consider all comments made on a planning application as being ‘public’ and they will therefore be published on our website. We will endeavour to obscure phone numbers, e-mail addresses and signatures (but not addresses) although we cannot guarantee these will be redacted in every case. Your letter or e-mail will be scanned and presented in its entirety and will therefore be available to be viewed by other people. In exceptional circumstances the Council reserves the right to redact some or all of the comments, particularly if they are of an inflammatory, derogatory or libellous nature. To ensure your comment is valid you should include, the planning reference number, your name, your full postal address and your comments.

Unfortunately the Council cannot enter into dialogue regarding individual cases and as such your comments must be self contained, and any questions that are raised in your response will be treated as being rhetorical. For a comment to be considered valid, it should refer to appropriate planning considerations.

Comments submitted will be made available online to the public.
