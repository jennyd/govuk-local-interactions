Allotments

A growing number of people are discovering the benefits of allotment gardening to grow your own food, or for the healthy exercise.

Whether you are an expert gardener or novice, please remember you are not alone and that there is a wide range of help, advice and assistance available should you need it. Your first point of contact should always be your site secretary (on site Sunday mornings), who will have a wealth of knowledge about the allotment site.

Applying for an Allotment

There are more than 60 allotments in Rhondda Cynon Taf. A list of all allotments broken down into area can be viewed below:

To enquire about the availability of an allotment plot and to be added to a waiting list, please enquire at your local allotment. Allotment Secretaries are on site on Saturday or Sunday Mornings.

Please note: Allotments are owned by Rhondda Cynon Taf Council, Local Town Councils, Community Councils and some are also privately owned. You should seek advice from the appropriate landowners / managers of the site you are interested in.
