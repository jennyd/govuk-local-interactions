Right to Buy scheme and leaseholders

The Right to Buy scheme allows council tenants to buy their homes at a discount. The ownership of all Merton Council homes transferred to the housing association Merton Priory Homes (now called Circle Housing Merton Priory) in March 2010.

For all enquiries about ex-Merton Council homes, including lease extensions, freehold sales and deeds of waiver, please contact Circle Housing Merton Priory.

Preserved Right to Buy

If you were renting your home in March 2010, you may still be able to buy it under what is called the Preserved Right to Buy. Circle Housing Merton Priory can tell you if you have the Preserved Right to Buy.
