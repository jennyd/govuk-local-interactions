Transport Services

About the transport service

From our modern (3,000 m2) well equipped IRTE Accredited Workshop, our staff are fully skilled / Irtec licensed technicians using the latest diagnostic equipment. We provide MOT testing, services and repairs as well as being a Ford authorised repairer and and ATF for larger vehicles.

We are an accredited training centre offering Driver Certificate of Professional Competence courses to drivers and fleet operators.

  • View the Redbridge Transport show 2015 video transcript

 

View our recent video about Redbridge Transport services

 

  • View the Redbridge transport services video transcript

 

MOT testing, Services and repairs for the public

You can now book an MOT, service and repairs with us online.

Broadly speaking we can do MOT (classes 4, 5 and 7- below), routine services and inspections, supply and fitment of tyres (including wheel balancing), body work and accident repair on:

  • cars
  • vans
  • mini buses
  • coaches
  • light commercial vehicles
  • heavy commercial vehicles 

 

Technician Accreditation

We are an approved assessment centre for Institute of Road Transport Engineers Certification (irtec) which is focused on commercial vehicle, trailer and passenger carrying industries.
Accreditation is a voluntary assessment of an individual’s current competence against accepted industry standards. All of the accreditations offered have been developed in conjunction with the  sector to enable  assessment of technicians knowledge and competence at benchmark career levels.

For more information on these assessments contact Eddie Cross, transport@redbridge.gov.uk.

 

London Low Emission Zone (LEZ)

We can fit ‘approved filters’ to assist with your compliance of the LEZ. We are able to service Eminox  filters as well as undertake statutory ‘smoke test’ for your Reduce Pollution Certificate (RPC), via our Authorised test facility (ATF).

 

Ford Authorised repairer

We have the same status as any other Ford dealer such as their service repair warranty. More information on Ford Authorised repairer

 

Authorised Testing Facility (ATF) for larger vehicles

For larger vehicles (HGV & PCV) testing is done under the Authorised Testing Facility (ATF) scheme for anyone wanting to know about an ATF.

For further information or to use our services above please give us a call on 020 8708 5116 or 020 8708 5117

(Text Relay customers should dial 18001 before these numbers to access the Text Relay service)

You can also email us transport@redbridge.gov.uk.

 

Tachograph and Speed Limiters

In our workshop we are able to install and calibrate Tachograph equipment. Follow this link for Tachograph information.

We can also install or adjust the speed limiter fitted to your vehicle.

For further information or to use our services above please give us a call on 020 8708 5116 or 020 8708 5117 (text Relay customers should dial 18001 before these numbers to access the Text Relay service).

You can also email us transport@redbridge.gov.uk.

 

Driver Training 

We offer a range of Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (DCPC) training modules for both LGV's and PSV's of 16 seats and over. Our Safer Urban Driving module is currently offering funded places and so drivers operating in the Borough can access this course for FREE.  Redbridge is a JAUPT and CPC accredited training provider.

Contact us on 0208 708 5001(text relay customers dail18001 before the number) for more information on booking a place on a course

You can also email us passenger.transportenquiries@redbridge.gov.uk

 

Vehicle Hire

The Borough offer a vehicle hire service, including passenger vehicles from 16 to 74 seaters. Self drive is available on some vehicles. 

Contact us on 0208 708 5001(text relay customers dail18001 before the number) for more information on hiring vehicles

You can also email us passenger.transportenquiries@redbridge.gov.uk

 

How to find us

Ley Street Depot, Ley street, Ilford, IG2 7QZ (View Ley Street Depot on the redbridge i map)

Protection of the Environment

Transport Services is committed to protecting the environment from the vehicles that we use on the fleet and our daily operations, the details of which can be found in our ISO14001:2004 Environmental Systems Manual (PDF, 411kb).

Bus operators grant

In 2014/15 the Department of Transport awarded the London Borough of Redbridge £44,802 to support the operation of community transport services across the borough. In accordance with grant conditions, the annual report sets out how the devolved grant was spent.
