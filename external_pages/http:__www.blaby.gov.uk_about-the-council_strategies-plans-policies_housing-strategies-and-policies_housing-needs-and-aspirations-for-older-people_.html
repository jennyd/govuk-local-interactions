Housing needs and aspirations for older people

Summary

The findings of the Older Persons Housing Needs and Aspirations Study which was undertaken by Salford University on behalf of the authorities within the Leicester and Leicestershire Housing Market Area.

Downloads

Leicestershire older people report - 2010

Launch workshop feedback

Older persons needs and aspirations launch presentation (Powerpoint Document)

Further information

The study provides guidance on:

  • Meeting the housing and support needs of older people in the future
  • Focusing on the role of supported housing, including sheltered housing, retirement villages
  • The type of support required to enable older people to 'stay put' in their own homes

The research involved qualitative consultation with older people from a wide range of ethnic and cultural backgrounds.

The launch event included a presentation by the report author of the findings, and workshop discussions around the way forward and key challenges.
