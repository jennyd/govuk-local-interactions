Parking and car parks

The council operates 10 pay and display car parks and 18 free car parks in Wyre.

The operational times of all pay & display car parks is 8am to 6pm every day.

If you would like to park your vehicles overnight on any of these, you can pay after 6pm. The time on the ticket will then start at 8am the following morning for however long you have paid to stay.

Season tickets are available in some car parks.

Motorbikes and mopeds park free of charge on all Wyre Council pay & display car parks.
