Find a Place to Live

There is a very high demand for properties owned by Registered Providers in Chiltern. This means that many households will have little prospect of actually obtaining a tenancy via Bucks Home Choice. Therefore, it is important that anyone looking for housing in Chiltern looks at all of the options that are available to them. The Council provides advice and information on other housing options, including getting a private sector tenancy via the Rent Deposit Guarantee Scheme or getting help to buy a property via the Help To Buy scheme.

A Housing Options Advisor is available at Chiltern District Council from Monday to Thursday between 9.00am to 5.00pm and on Friday between 9.00am to 4.30pm. If possible, you should ring the Council in advance to make an appointment.
