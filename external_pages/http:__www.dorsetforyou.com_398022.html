North Dorset District Councillors

Listen

North Dorset District Council is made up of 33 democratically elected councillors - voted for by local people in local elections.

Find my local councillor

To find out who your local councillor is, please enter your postcode and select your address.

Tell us your address

You can set your address here

Political composition

The political make up of North Dorset District council is:

  • Conservative: 27
  • Liberal Democrats: 4
  • Independent: 2
  • The Conservative Party has overall control
