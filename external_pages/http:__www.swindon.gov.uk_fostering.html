Find out about fostering or adopting a child

Fostering could be your missing piece...

We need more foster carers of all ages for children of all ages with placements lasting anything from a few days to several years

Carers receive payment and allowances to help cover their time and expenses, as well as free ongoing training and 24/7 Help and support.

  • 01793 464329
  • fosteringrecruitment@swindon.gov.uk

Further information

For information on fostering, adoption, supported lodgings and short breaks care, including the Home and Away Scheme, please go to the Fostering Adoption with Swindon website.
