Sign In

Sign in to view your personal record, renew books, make reservations.

Enter your library card number (with no spaces) and your Personal Identification Number (PIN). If you don’t know your PIN contact any of our our libraries.or use our online contact form.

  • If you have just joined Dudley Libraries online you can use your temporary UNREGXXXXXXX library card number and the 4 digit PIN you chose when joining.
  • If you are an existing library user enter your library card number and the PIN (the last 4 parts of your postcode for example DY1 1HR= 11HR).
  • Once you have logged in you can change your PIN on the Your Personal Details page.
  • If you can not log in successfully please contact your nearest library
