Complaints Form: Level One

To make a level one complaint, complete the form below and click Submit to return this form online.

Note: Fictitious names will be treated appropriately.

Highways and traffic

Highways, traffic, footways and street lighting are dealt with by Leicestershire County Council: Please visit their web site at leics.gov.uk to pursue a complaint on these subjects.

Drainage and Flooding

Similarly, public sewers and shared drainage matters are dealt with by Severn Trent Water: Please visit their web site at stwater.co.uk to pursue a complaint on these subjects.

Please select the section from the 'drop down' list below that you wish to complain to. If you cannot find what you are looking for, please select 'Other' in the 'drop down' list.
