View a planning application or decision

All planning applications, decisions and appeals are available to view online.

 

View online register of planning applications, appeals and decisions

Our Planning online register includes:

  • planning and estate management applications
  • drawings, photographs and documents accompanying a planning application
  • the officer's report for each decided application
  • Decision Notices, including any attached conditions or reasons for refusal
  • appeal decisions
  • Comments on applications where a decision has not been made.

Some planning applications are considered and decided by Hertfordshire County Council such as planning applications for minerals and waste development and applications related to county council services.

Unsure of an application's full address

Our Interactive Map can identify the application number of recent planning proposals by area. Once you've identified the application number the online database will provide you details of the application.

Sign up to 'Alert It' for new planning applications

We offer the option of an automatic email notification of planning applications that you are particularly interested in. Register for Alert-it. UPDATE 17 December 2015 - Alert It is now back online following the migration to a new Planning IT system.  We apologise for any inconvenience caused while the system was offline.  All registered users of this service have been contacted.

Local access to computers and advice

Council offices

We have computers at our council offices which you can access to view plans (please note we no longer store paper copies of plans). A duty officer is also on hand to give advice 0900 - 1300 hours Monday to Friday.

You can also access computers at parish and town councils. However they are unable to give you advice.
