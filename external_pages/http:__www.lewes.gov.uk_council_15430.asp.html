Housing Benefit - am I entitled & how do I claim?
Housing Benefit helps people on low income pay their rent. You can claim Housing Benefit if you are working but your earnings are low or if you are receiving state benefits. Find out more about your entitlements.
Council Tax Reduction - am I entitled and how do I claim?
The scheme helps people on low income with their Council Tax. Find out how to apply.
Changes to Housing Benefit and Welfare Reform
The Government has announced a number of changes to Housing Benefit from April 2011 leading to the introduction of Universal Credit in October 2013.
