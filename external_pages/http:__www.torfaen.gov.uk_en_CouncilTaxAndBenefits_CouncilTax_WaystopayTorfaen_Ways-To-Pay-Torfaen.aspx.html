Ways to Pay Torfaen

By Internet

You can pay your council tax, business rates, housing benefit overpayments and Council invoices online via the Internet using your debit or credit card. This is a totally secure method of payment, which is quick and easy to use. If you wish to find out more visit the paying online page or alternatively you can make a payment here.

Please do not send cash through the post.

Automated Telephone Payments Service

You can pay your Council Tax, Business Rates, Housing Benefit Overpayments and Council invoices via Torfaen's Automated Telephone Payments Service using your debit card. You will hear recorded information and be prompted to enter your transaction details via the handset keys on your telephone.

The Telephone number is 08458 500855 (calls cost 3p per minute plus your phone company’s access charge).

This service is available at all times. 

By Telephone to Call Torfaen

If you prefer to speak to a Customer Services Officer, you can telephone during the hours 8am - 6pm on 01495 766002. This facility is available to pay any monies due to the Authority by debit or credit card. 

By Direct Debit

You can pay your Council Tax account, Business Rates account or Sundry Debtor invoice by Direct Debit. You can register to pay by Direct Debit or speak to the Torfaen Customer Care Team on 01495 766002.
