Single person discount

If there is only one adult living in your home you can apply for a 25% discount on your Council Tax

Students

Full-time students may get a Council Tax discount or exemption

Low income households

You could get Council Tax Support to help with the bill if you are unemployed or your household has a low income

Disability relief

You could pay less Council Tax if you or someone who lives with you is permanently disabled

Under 20 in full-time education

You may get a 25% discount on your Council Tax if you receive Child Benefit for someone living with you who is aged over 18 and in full-time education

Structural alterations or major repair 

You may get a discount on your Council Tax if your property needs or is undergoing structural alterations or major repair.
