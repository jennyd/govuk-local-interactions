Public transport

Tickets and timetable information for all local buses, trains and the Metro can be found on the Network West Midlands website.

Concessionary travel passes

You can apply for a concessionary travel pass online.

School travel passes

Some families are also entitled to help with the cost of getting children to and from school. Find out more about applying for a school travel pass.

More about public transport

  • We host an annual public transport conference where Centro and operators provide information about future plans and answer questions from the public and other organisations. For further information email transport@sandwell.gov.uk
