Report a street issue

  • Type of problem
    Highways - Potholes on carriageway or footway must be at least 20mm deep) , broken/rocking paving slabs , missing manhole covers, missing/damaged road signs, work barriers down, pedestrian guardrails damaged. Street lighting - Missing or open door on lamp column, lamp column not working, damaged illuminated board, lanterns hanging, any item damaged, belisha beacon out, streetlight on during day. Traffic lights - Any traffic light out, traffic light hit or damaged. Street Cleansing - Dead animal, fly tip on road/pavement, oil spillage, syringes or drug related problems, offensive graffiti, overflowing dog/litter bin, blocked drains. Tree - Tree or branch fallen on to road/pavement, tree of branch in danger of falling.
