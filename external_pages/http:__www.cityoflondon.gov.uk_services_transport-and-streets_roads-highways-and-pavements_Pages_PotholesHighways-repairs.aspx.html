Potholes and highways repairs

We aim to maintain City streets to a high standard. Potholes are rare but can occur following utility works or after prolonged snow and ice.

You can report potholes or other road surface damage online email highways.management@cityoflondon.gov.uk or telephone our Contact Centre on 020 7606 3030

The Pothole Fund

To help us repair potholes the Government created a Pothole fund and invited the Highways Authorities to bid for £200 million in the 2014 / 2015 financial year.

We bid for a share of this additional funding and secured £41,008 to improve our road network. You can view our application here (83KB). This document is not fully accessible. If you need it in another format please contact us on highways.management@cityoflondon.gov.uk.

We repaired 1,090sqm of our highway in addition to our planned carriageway resurfacing and you can view the completed works below.
