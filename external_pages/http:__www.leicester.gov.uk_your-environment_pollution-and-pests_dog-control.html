Dog control

Our dog warden service offers assistance with roaming and stray dogs, lost dogs, dangerous dogs, dog fouling on private property and advice on controlling your dog, including enforcement action if necessary.

Further information

Roaming and stray dogs

If a dog's owner is identified they will be advised that their dog has strayed and been removed to our kennelling provider: College Garth Kennels, Derby Road, Hathern, Loughborough, Leicestershire, LE12 5LD, Tel: 01509 842327.

Owners wishing to reclaim their dog must pay the relevant charges (kennelling/fine/vet fees) before the dog is released. Advice on responsible dog ownership will be given to owners reclaiming their dog.

The current charge for claiming a dog is £35 plus £9.60 per day kenneling. All charges are to be paid to the kennelling provider. If you have difficulty with transport please, the kennelling provider can return your pet for an additional fee.

If you find a stray dog; see a roaming dog or have lost a dog during office hours (8am to 6pm) please call customer services on 0116 454 1000. Outside office hours (6pm to 9pm Monday to Friday and 9am to 9pm at weekends and bank holidays) please call 0116 373 7770.

Dangerous dogs

The dog warden will respond to dangerous dog complaints and contact the person who complains on the same day for full details of the incident.

The dog warden will contact the dog owner (if known) to advise them of the incident and of the possible legal action that may be taken against them. We may also need to liaise with the police. The owner will then be written to regarding the incident and dangerous dog legislation.

Incidents regarding a person being bitten should be reported to the police.

Microchipping, collars and ID tags

Microchipping will be a legal requirement from April 6 2016. Please also ensure your dog is wearing a collar and disc, as this is already a legal requirement and may help you locate your dog if it is lost.
