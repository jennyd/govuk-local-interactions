Early years and childcare

Early Years & Childcare services are here to support parents, carers, families and everyone working in childcare and early years education.

High quality childcare, play opportunities and early education are crucial for young children to get the best possible start in life.

The Early Years & Childcare Service has a strong history of successfully supporting Early Years provisions across the borough. We have a strong professional team who are passionate about early years. We draw on a wealth of experience in order to offer support to you.

If you are a parent looking for registered childcare in the London Borough of Hounslow including:-

  • Free childcare for all 2, 3, and 4 year olds

  • Day Nurseries, preschool playgroups and Childminders

  • Breakfast Clubs, Afterschool Childcare and Holiday Playschemes

see the Family Services Directory
