Reserving books and other items

Jump to:

  • Key contacts
  • Related links

Inside Fulwell Library

Books, Talking Books and CDs can be reserved and made available for collection at your local library.

It's free to reserve an item.

See 'Related links', below, to access information about other library charges.

You can call into your community library and  library staff will reserve the item for you.

You can also reserve items online catalogue by using the 'Do it online' section of this page. You will need your library ticket number to use this service.

You cannot reserve DVDs.
