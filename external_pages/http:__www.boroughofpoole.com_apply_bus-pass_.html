Concessionary Bus Pass

Use this form to apply for a Connect Bus Pass. If you have any difficulty completing this online, you can download a form to complete and post back. Please note that posting the form back to us means it will take longer to process your application.

You will need to upload:

  • A passport size photo
  • Proof of eligibility
  • Proof you are a permanent resident of Poole
