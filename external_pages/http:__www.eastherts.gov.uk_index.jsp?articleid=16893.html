Clinical Waste

Healthcare waste or clinical waste is not accepted in standard domestic waste bins.

This is because it poses a threat of infection to humans and must be incinerated.

Types of clinical waste include:

  • Human or animal tissue.
  • Blood or other bodily fluid.
  • Swabs or dressings.
  • Syringes and needles.
  • Drugs or other pharmaceutical products.

NB: Incontinence wear is not considered clinical waste but you may be entitled to extra capacity or a larger black wheeled bin. Apply for extra capacity here.

Clinical waste is collected fortnightly or as and when you require the service.

If you produce clinical waste we will provide the containers or sacks and ensure the correct disposal.

Please contact our Environmental Services Team on 01279 655261 ext 3507 or e-mail operations.admin@eastherts.gov.uk
