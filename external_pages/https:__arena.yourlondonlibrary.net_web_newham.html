Welcome

From 3rd November 2014 you can only borrow items from Wandsworth via Inter Library Loans. Wandsworth library cards cannot be used elsewhere in the consortium.

Access the library catalogue and its interactive services.
You can search our catalogue without being signed in.
The simple online service can be used to reserve an item which can be collected from a library of your choice.


Click on My Loans to renew any books you have on loan or My Reservations to check the status of any items you have requested. You can also sign up for text or email reminders when your borrowed items are due for renewal or reserved items are ready for collection.


To search within your authority only, use the Advanced Search option.

You can log in with your Library borrower number and PIN or join the site by creating a username and password. This will enable you to provide readings tips, make recommendations and write reviews, amongst other things.

This site times out after 30 minutes of inactivity!
