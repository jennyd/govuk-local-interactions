Notes This Division is responsible for the maintenance and improvement of the public highway including the implementation of traffic safety schemes. High quality materials are used for all elements of the structure i.e. footways, carriageways, street lighting, signs, benches etc.

Public lighting/signage - installation and maintenance of all public lighting, illuminated bollards and signs including all lamps on Albert and Chelsea Bridges.

Drainage - installation and repair of highway gullies, and construction of new sewer connections.

Construction - project management of bridge reconstruction/maintenance and major civil engineering schemes. Implementation of traffic management and highway improvement works. Structural advice on defective vaults and cellars.


A small team liaises with utility companies to minimise inconvenience during major works by British Gas, BT etc. A proportion of utilities works are inspected and tested for compliance with safety and reinstatement specifications.


Albert Bridge and Chelsea Bridge are regularly inspected with major refurbishment and relighting projects carried out approximately every eight years.
