Extra Care Housing

Extra Care Housing is also known as 'assisted living' or 'housing with care'. It allows you to live independently with the added security of access to 24-hour support services.

Where are Extra Care Housing properties in Runnymede?

There is one Extra Care scheme at Aldwyn Place, in Larchwood Drive, Englefield Green. It's managed by Hanover Housing Association. The scheme has 56 modern, self-contained flats built to accommodate wheelchairs.

It offers:

•24-hour alarm call system
•on-site care team
•door entry system with CCTV protection
•weekly flat cleaning
•pet-friendly policy
•communal facilities including garden, lounge, dining room, library, laundry, shop for general provisions, hairdresser and a guest suite.

How do I apply for Extra Care Housing?

Please apply by joining the housing register. If you are currently have a Social Worker from your Runnymede Locality Team, then please discuss a possible referral through them.
