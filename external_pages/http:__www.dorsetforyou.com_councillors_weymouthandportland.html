Weymouth & Portland Borough councillors

Listen

Weymouth and Portland has 36 councillors who are elected by the public. They represent 15 different wards of Weymouth and Portland.

Find my local councillor

To find out who your local councillor is, please enter your postcode and select your address.

Tell us your address

You can set your address here

Political composition

The political composition of the Weymouth and Portland Borough Council is:

  • 14 Conservative
  • 13 Labour
  • 6 Liberal Democrat
  • 2 Independent
  • 1 UKIP (for the purposes of the allocation of committee seats Cllr F Drake is a member of the Independent Group)

The council has no overall controlling political party

There are 15 wards across Weymouth and Portland.  The interactive map of Borough Councillors (opens in a new window) can be used to find your local councillor and other council facilities and services.

The council is elected by thirds. See which  12 councillors and wards are up for re-election in each year (pdf, 33kb) (opens in a new window) and how to become a borough councillor in Weymouth and Portland

Each councillor is a member of Full Council and may also sit on Committees - Weymouth & Portland Borough Council.

The Chairman of the Management Committee is Cllr Ian Bruce

The Mayor of Weymouth and Portland Borough is Cllr Christine James

The Deputy Mayor of Weymouth and Portland Borough is Cllr Richard Kosior

  • Weymouth & Portland Councillor complaints

    Weymouth & Portland Borough Council has a duty under the Localism Act 2011 to promote and maintain high standards of conduct by its councillors and co-opted councillors. The council has a process for investigating complaints made about individual councillors under the Weymouth & Portland Borough Council's code of conduct.
