County Ticket

 

County Tickets are available to students attending a sixth form school or college in Somerset or a neighbouring county. They are for use on most local and cross border public bus services, subject to operator restrictions.  

You can print a County Ticket application form using the link in the Information and Resources section of this page or if you prefer we can post an application form to you. Use the 'Request now' button below to request this.

Please note: For applicants wishing to attend Bridgwater College, Bridgwater College Cannington Centre and Bridgwater College Yeovil Centre please use the Bridgwater College website link in the Information and resources section where you can download a separate application form for these colleges. When completed this form needs to be returned directly to Bridgwater College.

Request now

The County Ticket entitles the holder to travel throughout Somerset subject to the conditions of each operator and on cross border routes to and from Wiltshire, Dorset, Devon, Bristol, North Somerset and Bath and North East Somerset.

The County Ticket will not cover journeys that are wholly outside of Somerset.

For information about restrictions please refer to the questions "Is there a limit to the distance I can travel?" and "Are there Individual Operator Restrictions?" on the question and answer sheets on the 2015/16 County Ticket Application Form in the Information and Resources section to the right of the page

You can apply for a County Ticket if you will be attending a Further Education Course at a 16+ establishment, including work-based learning courses.  The course must be funded by the Education Funding Agency (EFA) the college/school will be able to tell you if your chosen course is funded.

The quickest way for you to get your County Ticket is to download the application form online and print it. Once you have read the notes and completed the form you need to post it to the address on the Contacts tab of this page.

If you prefer we can post an application form to you. Use the 'Request now' button above to request this.

For applications that relate to Bridgwater College, Bridgwater College Cannington Centre and Bridgwater College Yeovil Centre please use the Bridgwater College website and send your completed application form directly to Bridgwater College.

Somerset County Council: Transporting Somerset has negotiated a very competitive price for the County Ticket.  The cost for the 2015/16 academic year is £675.  You will be able to pay online by credit/debit card, cheque or by direct debit. 

Why do applicants need to tell us what bus service they will be using?
The way we pay bus companies for their services is based on which specific services are being used to access school/college.
