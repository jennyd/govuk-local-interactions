Tell Us

We want to give you the best possible service – whether it’s in person, on the telephone, by email or via social media and the internet.

We set ourselves high standards, and we want to hear from you, our customers, about whether or not we’re meeting those standards.

If you have anything you want to tell us, whether it’s a compliment on good service from our staff or a complaint about something we didn’t get quite right, you can do so quickly and easily.

Compliments Form

If you've had a good experience with us, or if one of our members of staff has gone above and beyond for you, please let us know. We’ll make sure that we pass it on to the member of staff concerned

Complaints Form

If you’re not happy with something we’ve done, we want to know about it – so we can look into it and put things right for you, and so we can avoid the same thing happening in future. If you feel that our services aren’t up to standard, if you feel you’ve been inappropriately treated by a member of our staff, then let us know

If you get in touch with us, we’ll:

  • Let you know that we’ve received your compliment, complaint or comment
  • Reply in writing within 10 working days to any compliments or complaints

There are, of course, some things that we can’t change – things that we’re required to do by law, or because government policy states we have to do it in a certain way. If this is the case, we will, of course, let you know.
