Bulky waste collections

We offer a waste collection and disposal service for large bulky items (over 25kg or more and can't be put into your bin).

Cost

  • £20 for up to three large items
  • £40 for up to six large items

Some items may be priced as two items due to their size and weight, for example; three-seater sofas or settees, double cookers, agas or extra long ladders.

To make a booking please phone 01752 304750.

We can take payments over the phone with a valid credit or debit card. Collections take place throughout the week but the exact date will be arranged during the booking process based on the area in which you live.

Items we can collect

  • Fridges and freezers
  • Furniture
  • Beds and mattresses
  • Electrical items

Please ensure that

  • all items must be able to be carried by two able bodied people
  • carpet must be manageable by two people, or cut into manageable pieces, rolled and tied. Carpet does not include underlay - this is a separate item
  • items should not be filled with other rubbish
  • items for collection must be easy to get to, outside and at one point on the property

Items we will not collect

  • Paint
  • Car parts and tyres
  • Chemicals
  • Asbestos
  • Building or DIY waste
  • Any items that cannot be carried by two people
  • Any commercial equipment
  • Garden waste - we seasonally collect garden waste on the same day as your green recycling collection. View the garden waste collection page for more details.

Some commercial waste can be collected through our Trade Waste Collection Service. Please call 01752 304700 or email tradewaste@plymouth.gov.uk for further details.

Before you throw your special items away, consider if you could donate them to a local charity or refurbishment organisation - view the charity shops and reuse organisations page for more information.

Free special waste collections

If you receive income support, housing benefit, council tax benefit or pension credits you are entitled to two free collections in a 12 month period. These collections must be booked separately and on different days.

Disposal of other items

Please take domestic items that we cannot collect to one of our recycling centres at Weston Mill or Chelson Meadow.
