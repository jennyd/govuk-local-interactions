Garages and Parking Standard

Garages

We will:

  • Acknowledge your garage application within seven working days
  • Offer garages to applicants on top of the waiting list who have clear rent or service charge accounts
  • Give you the key to the garage on the day you sign the garage licence
  • Ensure that your garage is secure and weather tight
  • Offer you an alternative garage if yours cannot be repaired
  • Offer a range of payment methods – direct debit, paypoint, online, telephone, post, cash and debit card
  • Repossess the garage if you fail to pay your rent/service charge for either your home or garage

 

Parking

We will:

  • Give residents a parking permit free of charge
  • Provide additional permits for members of your household at a cost of £10 each
  • Give you two temporary parking permits for visitors free of charge
  • Provide you with a replacement permit if you lose yours for £10.
