Recruitment to the Citizens’ Panel 

We are currently recruiting to Viewpoint, our Citizens’ Panel, so complete your contact details  and join today. 

Viewpoint is made up of around 1,000 North East Derbyshire residents and it is an invaluable way for residents to give their views on our services, as well as those run by Derbyshire County Council. 

Members of the panel receive questionnaires on a range of topics about 4 times each year – these can be by post (we provide a Freepost envelope for you to return it) or online if you prefer. Sometimes members are invited to events or group discussions but you only attend these if you want to. 

Being a member of Viewpoint allows you the opportunity to give your views directly to the people who make decisions about how the Council and its services are run. Have your say today.  

Get Involved

There are many other ways in which you can have your say on issues in North East Derbyshire. 

To find out about the current consultations taking place in your area visit our Ask Derbyshire online consultation site. 

If you want to become a regular member of the Viewpoint Panel then take a look at the Citizens’ Panel page. 

You can also comment on, complain about, or compliment us – for more information visit our Comments, Compliments and Complaints section.
