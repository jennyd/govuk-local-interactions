Apply for planning permission

We prefer to receive planning applications online.

The Planning Portal allows you to submit planning applications, attach plans and associated documents, keep track of applications, calculate and pay fees - all online.

To submit a planning application, select the button below:

Applications by post

If you don't know what application form you need, visit the Planning Portal - guidance website and it will take you through the process and give you the correct form.

Alternatively, if you know what application form you need, select it from the list below:
