Fly Posting and Graffiti

Fly posting is the illegal display of advertising material, such as a sign placed on a lamp post or pasted to a building. It may also include distributing leaflet on car windows. Both fly posting and graffiti are illegal, anti-social activities that create a negative impression of an area and contribute to the fear of crime in the community.

We will remove fly posting and graffiti from public buildings; if you would like any unsightly fly posting removed from council property, private property or street furniture, please telephone our Customer Contact Centre 01539 733333.
