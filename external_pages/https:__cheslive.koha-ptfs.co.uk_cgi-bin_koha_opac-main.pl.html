Sandbach Library Refurbishment

Sandbach Library will be re-opening with a limited service on Monday 8th February.  

We expect to be able to accept books at the door, but issuing stock may not be possible.  We apologise for any inconvenience.  

(published on 22/01/2016)

Crewe Library Reduced Service

Due to necessary work as part of the Lifestyle Centre move, Crewe Library will be operating as a reduced service from Monday 7th December, 2015.  

Until further notice, Crewe Library's stock will not be visible via the online catalogue, although a selection of stock will be available in branch.  To enquire about the stock available at Crewe Library during the reduced service period, please speak to Crewe Library directly.

Customers will still be able to place holds on stock available at other libraries and collect their holds from Crewe Library as usual.  We remind all customers that Cheshire East and Cheshire West and Chester library cards are valid in all of our libraries across the two authorities, and all stock can be borrowed and returned at all branches. 

(published on 02/12/2015)

Ebooks
