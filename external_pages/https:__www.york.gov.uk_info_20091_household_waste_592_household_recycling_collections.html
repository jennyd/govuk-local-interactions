How can we help you today?

Household recycling collections

Planned maintenance...

All households are provided with a kerbside recycling collection (green waste). Collections are every fortnight on alternate weeks to collection of general rubbish (grey waste).

Our collection times vary. You should put your boxes outside the front edge of your property by 7.00am on the day of collection and no earlier than 7.00pm the evening before. To find out when your recycling is collected please use the online collection calendar.

Most households have three recycling boxes with lids or a net. Please separate your recycling into these types:

  • paper and cardboard
  • plastic bottles and metal tins/cans
  • glass bottles and jars

Find out about how we undertake recycling in York, see a full list of what you can recycle at the kerbside or check the A to Z of recycling and reuse which explains where you can take many other household waste items.

Please stack or place your boxes on top of each other at the front edge of your property, making sure they don't cause an obstruction hazard to anyone walking past.

Bank Holiday Collections

We will collect your recycling and bins as normal on any Bank Holidays in 2015. Changes at Christmas and New Year will be advertised nearer the time.

Missed collections

If your recycling hasn't been collected check your boxes contain only the correct items and were  put out to the edge of your property at or before 7.00am. You can Contact us to report your missed waste collection (this must be within 24 hours of your normal collection, or else we will not return). 

Once your missed collection has been logged we will empty it by the end of the next working day.

Recycling for flats

If you live in a flat you may have shared or communal recycling bins which are kept outside in a bin store, or you may have your own individual recycling boxes. Please separate your recycling into the appropriate containers.

Recycling bins have a small flap on top which you can open to put your recycling in. Only place recycling in the bins provided and do not leave bags of recycling next to the bins.

Recycling in the city centre

St Nicks provide weekly recycling collections in the city centre using a recycling box.  Please use the online collection calendar to find out your collection day. For a full list of what you can recycle in your box please look at the St Nickâ€™s website.

Recycling for rural properties

If you live in a more remote or rural part of the city you may have a recycling collection for only paper/cardboard and plastic bottles/metal cans. To check what type of collection you have please use the online collection calendar.

Replacement recycling boxes

If you need to replace your recycling box because it has been lost, stolen or damaged you can order online. See replacement containers for further information.

If your recycling box has been damaged during collection, the crew will leave a card with details of how it will be replaced free of charge.
