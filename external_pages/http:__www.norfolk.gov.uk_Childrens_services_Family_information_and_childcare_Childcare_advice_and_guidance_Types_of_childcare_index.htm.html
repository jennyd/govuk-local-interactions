Types of childcare

Childcare can be offered by private, independent and voluntary-run providers. Many schools offer a range of childcare on-site, for example, breakfast, holiday and out-of-school clubs. Pre-school children can receive childcare and early education in a day nursery, nursery class, or at a playgroup or childminder’s, for example.

Search for childcare providers in your area on the Norfolk Directory.
