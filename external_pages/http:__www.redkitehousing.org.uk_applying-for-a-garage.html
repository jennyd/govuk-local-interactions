Apply for a garage

Whether you are one of our customers or not, you can apply to rent one of our garages by using the form at the bottom of this page.

Our current prices for renting a garage are:

  • £59.71 per month if you are not currently one of our tenants
  • £49.75 per month if you are currently one of our tenants

These prices are subject to change. If you rent one of our garages, we will contact you before any price changes take place.

There is currently a waiting list for garages, so depending on the area you ask for, there may be a slight delay in finding you one. Once we have your application, we will be in touch within two weeks to give you more information.

Please note that if you fall into arrears on your garage and have not made an arrangement to clear them, we may end your garage tenancy. If you fall into arrears with your home rent, your garage will be repossessed.

We have garages in the following areas:

  • Bourne End
  • Princes Risborough
  • Downley
  • Castlefield   
  • Stokenchurch
  • High Wycombe town centre
  • Booker, Cressex, Holmers Farm, Sands
  • Marlow
  • Hazlemere, Terriers, Tylers Green
  • Totteridge, Bowerdean
  • Micklefield
  • Loudwater and Wycombe Marsh
  • Flackwell Heath and Wooburn Green
