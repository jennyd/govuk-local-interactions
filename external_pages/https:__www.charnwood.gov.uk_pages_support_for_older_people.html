Housing advice for older or vulnerable people

Top tasks:

  • Apply for a council home
  • Charnwood Lifeline (Emergency Response Service)
  • Contact the Housing Needs team
  • Sheltered accommodation

There are a number of support and advice services available to older people within Charnwood offering assistance with housing issues, including how to remain in their homes.  


The Bridge

 

The Bridge, 38 Leicester Road, Loughborough, LE11 2AG 

Tel: 01509 260500 

Web: http://bridgehousingservices.com/  

The Bridge provides housing advice, housing related support services and accommodation options for the homeless and most vulnerably housed people in Charnwood. 

Charnwood Connect

Charnwood Connect is a partnership between Charnwood Borough Council, Charnwood Citizens Advice Bureau, Human Rights & Equalities Charnwood, John Storer House, Loughborough University, Living Without Abuse, PACE, The Bridge and Youth Shelter.

Charnwood Connect, c/o Charnwood CAB, Woodgate Chambers, 70 Woodgate, Loughborough, LE11 2TZ

 T: 01509 221 200 | W: http://charnwoodconnect.org.uk/    


Age UK

Formed through the merger of Age Concern and Help the Aged, Age UK provides information and advice on housing, health, debt and budgeting, bereavement, making a will, and going into hospital.  

Services include advocacy, befriending scheme, daybreak scheme, day care services, health advice and information, handyman service, respite service, advice on moving into residential and nursing homes, and services for people from minority ethnic communities.

19 Baxter Gate | Loughborough | Leics LE11 1TG

T: 01509 212 402 | W: http://www.ageuk.org.uk/


Elderly Accommodation Counsel

The Elderly Accommodation Counsel (EAC) is a national charity that aims to help older people make informed choices about meeting their housing and care needs. 

EAC | 3rd Floor | 89 Albert Embankment | London SE1 7TP

T: 01509 212 402 | E: info@firststopadvice.org.uk | W: http://www.ageuk.org.uk/ or http://www.firststopcareadvice.org.uk/


Meals on Wheels

The service provides meals for people who have been assessed as needing this support.  A meal is delivered to homes on the days that have been agreed.  Meals are nutritious and can cater for specific dietary needs. 

Pennine House | Leicestershire County Council | 2 Lemyngton Street | Loughborough | Leics LE11 1UH

T: 01509 26 6641 | E: AdultSocialCare@leics.gov.uk | W: http://www.leicestershire.gov.uk/service.thm?initial=M&pid=315
