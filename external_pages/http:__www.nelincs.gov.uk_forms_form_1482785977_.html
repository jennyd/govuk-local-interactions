Was it a refuse or recycling collection that was missed? *



 

Please note you must report your missed collection by 12 noon the next working day for us to return.

Your bin/box will be collected within the next 24 working hours. Please make sure the bin/boxes are left available at their collection point.
All containers need to be placed out by 7am on the day of collection and retrieved to within the boundary of your property as soon after emptying as possible. Containers must not be left out over night as this would be an infringement of the Highways Act, under “obstruction”.

For more information on our assisted collection service please visit http://www.nelincs.gov.uk/resident/recycling-rubbish-waste/household-collections/assisted-refuse-recycling-collections/
