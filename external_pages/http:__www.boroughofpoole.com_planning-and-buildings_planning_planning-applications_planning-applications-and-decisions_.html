Publication of Documents on the Website

Applications and Decision Notices may not be available for viewing until up to 7 working days after they have been administered by us. 

Planning Decisions

You can now view and run your own decision list by week or month within our Planning Application Search. You can also search by Ward and Date. 

All correspondence and documents submitted about Planning Applications received from 2005 onwards can now be viewed in their entirety via the online planning applications viewer.

Historic Planning Applications going back to 1948 are available. The applications since 1997 include application forms, reports, decisions and comments (if less than 5 years old) made on the application whether verbal, written, email or submitted online. Applications prior to 1997 have been scanned but may only contain application form, plans and decision notice if available at the time of scanning.

If you have any concerns regarding the publication of personal data please see our privacy policy.

 We work in partnership with The Planning Portal to provide information and services about the planning system.

  • Downloadable Planning Application Forms can be found within our Apply For It section. Please note that only 2 copies of forms, drawings and other documents are now required and not 4 as  stated on guidance notes and forms.

  • Details of Planning Committee Agenda, Minutes and Reports are also available
  • Details of Appeals lodged with and decided by the Planning Inspectorate can now be viewed

You can search for details of planning and treework applications and also submit a comment on undetermined applications when the normal consultation period allows.
