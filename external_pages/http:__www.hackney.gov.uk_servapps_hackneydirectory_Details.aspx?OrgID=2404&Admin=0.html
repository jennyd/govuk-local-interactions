Hackney Community Transport Ltd

Address: Ash Grove Bus Depot
Mare Street
London
E8 4RH
Tel: 020 7275 2400
Fax: 020 7275 2450
Email: info@hctgroup.org
Web www.hctgroup.org
Main Aims: HCT is a large, versatile organisation and registered charity offering a wide range of services. We are one of the UK's best known and successful social enterprises delivering a series of innovative public and community transport and training services. We retain our original mission and have successfully developed the business into the award winning organisation that it is today.
Extra Information: Our services include:

Group Transport - a low cost accessible minibus service for community and voluntary groups.

Capital Call - a Door 2 Door service provided by Private Hire vehicles for users with mobility difficulties.

ScootAbility - an award-winning mobility scooter home delivery service.

Leisure Mobility - a new way to access electric mobility scooters and powered wheelchairs.

PlusBus - a local fixed route bus service for those who have difficulty accessing mainstream transport.

Door 2 Door - an individual transport service for Hackney residents provided by volunteers.

Integrated Transport Solutions - a “one stop” shop service, provided by our Transport Co-ordination Centre, accessing a whole range of transport modes from taxis to coaches.
Publications: Annual report and posters in several different languages. Periodic newsletters.
Opening times: MON to FRI: 8am - 5.30pm.
Fees: Variable.
Referrals: Not necessary except with travel training.
Practice Boundaries: Nationwide.
Disabled Access:
  • TOILET with access for disabled people
  • Building has LEVEL or RAMPED ACCESS via the main or side door
