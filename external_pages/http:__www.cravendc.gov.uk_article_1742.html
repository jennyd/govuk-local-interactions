Report fly tipping

Fly tipping is illegal and an eyesore. The Council can take action to require the removal of noxious, toxic or verminous refuse, which has been dumped on privately owned land.

If you spot a fly tipper in action please note as many details as possible including the date, time, location, registration number of any vehicles involved and the type of waste being tipped and report to the Environmental Health team.
