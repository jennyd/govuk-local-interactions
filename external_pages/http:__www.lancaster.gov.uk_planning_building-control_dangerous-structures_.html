Dangerous structures

Lancaster City Council will deal with dangerous and dilapidated structures upon receiving notifications. These notifications arise from a variety of sources including neighbours and members of the public.

Our building surveyors will generally ensure that appropriate action is taken by building owners to ensure that the danger is removed safely. In the absence of the owner action may be taken on their behalf.

For further information, or to report a dangerous structure, please contact us using the details below.

Garden walls

Structural defects of garden walls and the associated collapse are the most common cause of injury to members of public. It is important that these structures are periodically inspected by property owners. Guidance for the inspections and general maintenance of garden walls is available on the planning portal:

www.planningportal.gov.uk/permission/commonprojects/fenceswallsgates

If you think a structure is dangerous, please report it to us using the contact details below.
