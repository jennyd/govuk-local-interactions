Garden Waste

What Can I Do With My Garden Waste?

  • Home composting is a great way of dealing with your garden waste and vegetable peelings
  • You can take your garden waste to one of the Household Recycling Centres
  • We provide a chargeable collection service using wheeled bins - see below to register your interest
  • We provide a chargeable collection service using sacks (for households where bins are not available) - see below


Garden Waste information leaflet

Garden Waste (Wheelie Bin) Collection Service - Expression of Interest

The service is specifically designed for residents who generate a lot of garden waste but don't have sufficient home composting facilities. So to help keep your garden tidy and do your bit for the environment register your expression of interest here. The cost of the service is £44 per year by direct debit.

Please Note: If you have already registered an interest in the garden waste service you do not need to take any further action, we will contact you in a few weeks time.

You can put all your compostable garden waste in a special 240 litre brown wheeled bin which is collected every fortnight throughout the year. We continue collecting throughout the winter months on the normal fortnightly schedule, weather permitting.

How To Pay

New Customer (Payment by Direct Debit only)

The cost of the service for one year is £44 by Direct Debit.

Existing Customers

If you are an existing customer and have received your invoice you can pay on-line here

For the future, if you would prefer to pay by direct debit, please complete the Direct Debit Form and send it back to us and we will set this up for the following year.

 

Garden Waste Sacks

Sacks can be purchased if garden waste bins are not available in your area or your household is exempt from wheelie bins

Garden sacks are sent for landfill disposal so are collected on the grey landfill week, not on the green recycling week. The sacks should be put out for collection with your grey wheelie bin or black sacks.

Sacks can be purchased at £2.20 each from our Community Contact Centres in Abbey Road, Evesham, Victoria Square, Droitwich Spa, the Civic Centre, Pershore or Broadway Library. Alternatively, we can post them to you:-

Disabled Residents

Disabled residents on income support qualify for half price garden waste sacks and can be purchased at £1.10 each. Evidence of entitlement will need to be shown by producing the Department of Works & Pensions award letter. Each household is limited to fifty sacks per year.
