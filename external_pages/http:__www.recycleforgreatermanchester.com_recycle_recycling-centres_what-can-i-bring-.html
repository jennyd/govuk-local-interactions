Recycling Centres

The Transfer Loading Station and weighbridge on sites will remain open as normal for vehicles over 2 metres in height.

We provide a network of 20 Recycling Centres for local residents to recycle waste from their home. 

The network of Recycling Centres allows you the flexibility to use your local Recycling Centre or one that's on route to work.

To note: Items cannot be removed from any of the Recycling Centres by members of the public due to Environment Agency legislation.

Using the map

Please use the map for details of the Centres most convenient to you.

Simply click on the recycle symbol for information on the centre including a location map and a list of what you can recycle at the centre.

Everything you need to know for your visit to the centre, opening times, vehicle height restrictions you can find below.

Due to maintenance and site improvements, the Household Waste Recycling Centre at Chester Road, Stretford will close from Wednesday 2nd March to Wednesday 23rd March.  It will re-open on Thursday 24th March 2016.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

What Can I Bring?

Our Centres can accept up to 23 types of material for recycling, from your home. See our map above for a comprehensive list of the things you can bring to each centre.

Below we have provided further information about the more problematic materials you may need to dispose of.

Asbestos

Asbestos can be taken to all our HWRCs.  We no longer accept unwrapped asbestos.  Any resident wishing to dispose of asbestos at our facilities will need to bring it double wrapped in heavy duty plastic wrapping.  You can also use household rubble sacks that can be bought from supermarkets and other outlets, as long as the asbestos is contained and sealed within the bags.

If you have to remove and handle Asbestos from your property there are some essential guidelines you should follow and can be found here
