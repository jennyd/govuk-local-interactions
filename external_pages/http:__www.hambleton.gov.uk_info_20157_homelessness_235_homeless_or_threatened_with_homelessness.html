We assess claims of homelessness from people who are homeless, or threatened with homelessness. If you meet the criteria, we will help you find permanent accommodation (either council housing, with a social landlord or in the private rented sector)

Housing Options operate an appointment service from 8.45am until 5.15pm Monday to Thursday, and 8:45am until 4.45pm on a Friday. If you are not homeless tonight please contact the Housing Options Team on 01609 779977 to make an appointment or emailinfo@hambleton.gov.uk

Homeless tonight?

If you are homeless tonight, please go to customer services at the the Civic Centre and ask to see a Housing Advisor. You will be seen at some point of the working day but you may have some waiting time.

What to expect at the interview

At your interview we will provide you with an action plan listing what we will do and what you need to do.

We need to consider the following five points when we assess an application

  • are you eligible for accommodation or assistance under the homeless legislation?
  • are you homeless or threatened with homelessness within the next 28 days?
  • do you have a priority need?
  • are you intentionally homeless?
  • do you have a local connection with Hambleton?

You will be asked to sign the homeless application, authorizing us to make the necessary enquiries, for example we may need to contact doctors, solicitors, and landlords etc. You will be notified in writing within 33 working days of the decision.

Out of hours contact

If you need help outside of the working day, an emergency service is available on 01609 767267

Please do not give false information in order to obtain council accommodation - you may be prosecuted

Further information

You can view our Homelessness Strategy for a breakdown of the Council's key principles, priorities and the actions we are taking to combat homelessness in the district.
