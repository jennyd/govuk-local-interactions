What is Playmapp?

Playmapp is a directory of playgrounds and play spaces featuring hundreds of free places you can take children, including skateparks, paddling pools, forest parks and games areas across Devon, with the rest of the South West (coming soon!)

How to use Playmapp?

Find a play area using GPS

Start by clicking on the 'Use current location' in the search form below to active Playmapp's in build GPS function (for PC users who are using the 'Find my location' option, look out for the popup at the top of your browser to grant access to your location). Once your laction has been found, you have the option to select how far away from your location you would like Playmapp to look for play areas or just click the search button.

Find a play area by postcode

Type in a postcode for your area of interest. You can also select how far away from your location you would like Playmapp to look by using the select distance option. And finally, for those who would like to specify a larger area, the Choose Area option will allow you to search specific areas in Devon and the South West

Once you have submitted your GPS or postcode search, you will be taken to a list of play areas in your chosen area. If you have used the 'Find my location' option, the results will be in order of which play area is nearest to you. Further filtering options are also available if you are looking for specific facilities.

Find on a map

Your final option is the map search, just click on your region of interest to see all play areas on a map.

About Playmapp

When Playmapps founder, Claire, had her twins, she discovered how essential playgrounds would become to keeping two energetic toddlers entertained. There was just one problemfinding the play areas, especially when away from the home patch. So Playmapp was started to help everyone locate a playground, wherever they may be. It's an ambitious project - there is no database of play areas in the UK, so we are starting from scratch building up the 'play map'.
