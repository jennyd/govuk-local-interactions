Lost and found dogs in East Northamptonshire

What to do if you have lost your dog

If you lose a dog in East Northamptonshire please call 01832 742000 to report it.  We will record the details of the dog and check if it looks like one that we have collected or a stray that has been reported to us. Alternatively you could complete the report lost/found dogs form with as much information as you can .  If later on you get your dog back please contact us so we can update our records. 

What to do if the council has collected your dog

When we collect a dog it is taken to Forest Lodge Kennels, Forest Road, Quinton, Northampton, NN7 2EQ.  Telephone: 01604 862239

Please contact the kennels to arrange the collection of your dog. You will have to pay a straying fine along with a kennelling charge for every day (or part day) they have the dog.  For further information on these charges please visit our fee and charges page.

Out of hours

If you have lost a dog outside of office hours please call 01832 733530.

Depending on where you have lost your dog it may be worth calling some of our neighbouring councils in case one of them has collected your dog.  This map shows the boundary of East Northamptonshire and nearby council areas (PDF 3.39MB).

We only collect dogs that have been found in East Northamptonshire.  If you lose or find a dog outside East Northamptonshire please contact the appropriate council.  Links to nearby and neighbouring council websites and their telephone numbers are listed below:
