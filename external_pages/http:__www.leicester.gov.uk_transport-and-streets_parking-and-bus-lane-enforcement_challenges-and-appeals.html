Challenges and appeals

If you have received a fine and want to challenge it, you need to put your representations in writing to us via our online form or sending us a letter.

Quick links

  • Pay or challenge a parking fine (PCN)
  • Pay a bus lane penalty charge notice (PCN)

Further information

You should write to the below address or alternatively complete the online form linked above:

Penalty Charge Representations,
PO BOX 8459
Parking Services,
Leicester
LE1 8AW

or email us. 

What happens next?

If your challenge is rejected and you wish to challenge the decision further, a formal representation can be made at the next stage by completing the Notice to Owner form. To make a formal representation, you should use the Notice to Owner form we will send to you and post it to the above address.  Alternatively, you can scan the Notice to Owner form and email it to us.

If you are unable to write or email, please call us on 0116 454 1000 quoting the PCN number, the vehicle registration and your address in all communications.
