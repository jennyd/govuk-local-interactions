Garden waste

Garden waste collection

From 4 April 2016, we’re introducing an annual charge for garden waste collections. If you want us to continue to collect your garden waste after this date you need to sign up for this optional service.

How it works

You will receive a 240 litre brown wheelie bin when you subscribe. You can also opt to use two 90 litre sacks if this is more convenient for you to handle and store.

You will receive fortnightly collections of garden waste for a year from your subscription date. You can also opt to share the service with a neighbour. Talk to your neighbour about sharing the bin and how you will split the cost. Only one of you should apply.  

What it costs

Households not in receipt of council tax support: £50 per year

Households in receipt of council tax support: £25 per year.  

Households who subscribe before 29 February will receive a ten per cent discount.   

I want the service

Click here to sign up and we will call you back to take payment. You can also call our dedicated garden waste phone line on 020 8583 2060. This is open between 5-7.30pm, Monday to Friday  until 4 March. You can also call 020 8583 5555 during office hours. (We recommend that you use the dedicated line to avoid prolonged waiting times.)

Discounts and offers

  • Households signing up to the service before 29 February will receive a ten per cent discount. Normal charges apply thereafter.   

  • If you receive council tax support, provide your claim reference number to receive a 50 per cent discount. This can be found on any letter sent to you from the benefits department.

  • Apply for a FREE composter, worth around £20, and compost some of your garden waste and food waste at home. This limited offer is on a first come, first served basis. To request your free composter visit www.getcomposting.com or call 0844 571 4444.

Alternative services

You can take your garden waste free of charge to Space Waye Reuse and Recycle Centre. You can bring a van in for free as long as it has not been used before and you show proof of identify and a copy of your council tax bill.

Questions about the service

For more information about the changes visit our frequently asked questions page.
