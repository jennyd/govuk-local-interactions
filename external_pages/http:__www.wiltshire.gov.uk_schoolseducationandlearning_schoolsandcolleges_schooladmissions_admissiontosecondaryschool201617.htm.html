Shortcuts for applying for a secondary school place in 2016/17

Related information

If your child was born between 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005, they will start secondary school in September 2016. The deadline for applying for a place is the 31 October 2015.

Apply online

You can still apply after this date but it will be considered a late application. The timetable below provides details of how this will affect your application.

You cannot apply online after 31 October 2015 deadline day, instead you will need to complete a paper application form paper application form 50kb and you can read our Guidance notes on how to complete the secondary application form 2016/17 guidance notes on how to complete the application 23kb.

Timetable

Date Action
September 2015
  • Please read A parents guide to applying for a secondary school place in Wiltshire 2016/17 A parents guide to applying for a secondary school place in Wiltshire 2016/17 2mb before making your application.

  • Some foundation and aided schools with religious admissions criteria will ask you to complete a supplementary application form. These forms are available from the schools directly and must be returned to the school by the closing date and not to Wiltshire Council. Completing a supplementary form does not mean that you have made an application. You must still make a formal application to Wiltshire Council.
31 October 2015
  • Deadline - All paper and online applications must be received by 31 October 2015.

  • Applications received after the deadline without exceptional reasons are not considered until all the applications received on time have been allocated. You are less likely to secure a place at your preferred school and you will not receive an offer of a place until late April 2016. It is important that you apply on time to avoid disappointment.
November 2015 - February 2016
  • The school admissions team liaises with schools and other authorities to make sure that, where possible, parents get a single offer for the highest preference school able to offer a place.
1 March 2016
  • Offer day - Letters are posted out to all parents who applied by the 31 October deadline offering them a place at one school. Parents who apply online will be able to log back on to the online system on national offer day to view the outcome of their application. You will require your username and password which was created at the time of application. It is important that you keep these details safe as the school admissions team are not able to send duplicate emails to you confirming them. All online applicants will also receive confirmation of their offer by letter
15 March 2016
  • Deadline for parents to accept the place they have been offered. If you do not respond by this date, we will assume that you do not want the place and it will be offered to another applicant.
  • If you have been refused a place at your preferred school you can appeal.
  • Waiting lists are set up.
From 29 April 2016
  • We offer spare places and any places which have become available after the 1 March offer day.
June/July 2016
  • Appeals are heard.
31 August 2016
  • Deadline for receiving any late applications and for putting names on waiting lists. Any applications received after this date will be considered as in-year transfer applications.
September 2016
  • Your child starts secondary school.
22 July 2017
  • Waiting lists are closed.

Key points

  • It is the responsibility of the parents/ carers to apply for a school place.
  • You can apply for up to three schools, putting the school you want the most as your first preference. We strongly advise you to use all three preferences.
  • We advise you to enter your catchment area school as one of your preferences so that there is more chance of your child being offered a place at one of your chosen schools.
  • If you live in Wiltshire and want to apply for a school place outside the county, you must still make your application to Wiltshire Council. We will pass on your application details to the relevant council where it will be considered alongside the other applications for that school.
  • If you do not live in Wiltshire and want to apply for a Wiltshire school, you must make an application to your own local authority. They will then pass on your application details to us. You will not be able to use the Wiltshire online application system or Wiltshire's paper application form. The only exception to this would be applications from children of UK service personnel.
  • We will acknowledge receipt of your application. If you do not receive acknowledgement within 15 school days of returning your completed application, please contact Customer Services at Wiltshire Council on 01225 713010.
  • All online applicants will receive immediate email confirmation of registration and once the application has been submitted a separate comfirmation email will be sent.
