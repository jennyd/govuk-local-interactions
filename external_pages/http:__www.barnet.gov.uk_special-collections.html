Large and bulky waste collections

  • Rubbish, waste and recycling

You can have most large or bulky waste collected from outside your property using the Special Collections service.

Special collection charges

  • white goods/electrical items are charged at £44.85 for the first item and a tiered charge per additional item
  • other items are charged at £56.60 for the first item and a tiered charged per additional item

These charges are payable in advance and can be made by credit/debit card or cheque.

Please contact us to arrange a special collection using the contact details below.

Cancelling a Bulky/Special Collection

Please note that there is a 48 hour cancellation period required to cancel any booked collections. Any collections cancelled after this time will not receive a refund regardless if the collection takes place or not.

Items that we are unable to collect

We are unable to collect the following items, although you can dispose of most of these at the Summers Lane Reuse and Recycling Centre:

  • asbestos (please see Hazardous Waste pages)
  • batteries
  • floor standing boilers
  • gas bottles/canisters
  • glass
  • glazed window frames
  • heavy cast iron items, e.g. car engines
  • items over 10ft long
  • liquids
  • metal baths
  • plasterboard
  • sharp or heavy items
  • soil or building materials
  • steel water tanks
  • tree trunks or thick branches
  • tyres

Alternative options for re-use or recycling

If your unwanted furniture could be used by someone else, please consider giving it to the Barnet Furniture Centre. Please do not leave items outside of the centre outside of the opening hours.

A free collection service is offered by ReStore Community Projects for old electrical goods including fridges, PCs, printers, and fans. Anything with a battery, plug or charger can be reused or recycled. To request your free electrical collection please call ReStore Community Projects on 020 3457 5681. Items will be collected by ReStore Community Projects, where they will be tested and then sold on at a low cost to people or families in need.

You can also offer unwanted items for re-use on the Freecycle website.
