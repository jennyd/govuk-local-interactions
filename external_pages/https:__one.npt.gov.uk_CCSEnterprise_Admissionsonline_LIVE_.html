Before you begin...you will need:

  • Any information you have been given about applying for a school place
  • Your child’s full address (including postcode)
  • Your child’s date of birth
  • Approx 15-20 minutes to complete the application (but you can save and come back to it later)
  • For security, the website will timeout if you login and do not use it
  • Do not use the Browser's Back button; use the Back button on each page

Welcome to Neath Port Talbot County Borough Council on-line application process for School Admissions. The following screens allow you to make an electronic application for a school place for September 2016. Please not that the on-line application process will open on 9th October 2015.

Please note the on-line facility is only available for admission to reception class (Infant / Primary), transfer from Infants to Junior School (Year 3) and admission to Secondary School (Year 7), including St Joseph's RC School and 6th Form.

For admission to Alderman Davies CIW Primary School, Bryncoch CIW Primary School, St  Joseph's Catholic Infant School, St Joseph's Catholic Junior School , St Joseph's Primary School ansd St Therese's Catholic Primary School please contact the  relevant school direct.

For information relating to nursery admission or in year transfer please click on the Education Websites link below. The screens explain the information needed by you at each stage. Please read the narratives carefully in each section before entering your details. Further information about the admissions process is available via our website please click on the link below.

                                 The closing date for applications is

                        Midnight on the 27th November 2015

Please click on Register or if you have already registered enter your email address and password in the boxes opposite.

Your application will be secure. The system has a series of security features that will prevent others from seeing your information.

You can make changes to this form at any time until the closing date. If you make changes to your application you must ensure that you re-submit the application.

Please note: by making an online application you are choosing to accept communication from us by email. You should be aware that the automated emails produced by our admissions software will include the child's name and date of birth. You should consider taking appropriate measures to ensure the location from which you access your email information is secure. If you have spam filters set up on your email account please make sure you change your settings to allow emails from admissions@npt.gov.uk. Please refer to your email providers help instructions for information on how to do this.
