Pay a parking fine (PCN)

You can make a payment in the following ways:

  • Pay online using the parking partnerships secure online payments system
  • By phoning the Parking partnerships' automated payments line on 01206 507275.

Pay within 14 days to get the discounted rate

If you pay within 14 days you will only have to pay the discounted amount shown on the notice.

Can I challenge a parking fine?

  • Challenge a parking fine

You can challenge a parking fine online by filling out the parking partnership online form. All challenges must be made within 28 days of the parking ticket being issued. If you make your challenge within 14 days of the date it was issued and your challenge is rejected, the discount period will be reset for a further 14 days.

What can I get a parking ticket for?

Parking fines are also known as Penalty Charge Notices (PCN) and you may receive a penalty charge notice if you:

  • Park in areas where loading/unloading restrictions apply
  • Park for longer than the time paid for.
  • Park in a resident's parking space without displaying a valid permit.
  • Park in a reserved bay e.g. disabled bay, taxi rank unless authorised to do so.
  • Park in a bus stop or bus lane during the prohibited hours.
  • Park not wholly within a bay.

These are just a few of the most common contraventions.
