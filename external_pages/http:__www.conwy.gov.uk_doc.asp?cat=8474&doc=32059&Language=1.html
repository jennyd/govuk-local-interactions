Secondary School Admissions 2016

Education preference form for Secondary School Admissions September 2016

Please find below a Parent/Guardian/Carer preference form for you to complete to register your child at one of Conwy's Secondary schools.

A list of Conwy schools together with the admissions policy is attached below.

Conwy's Schools and Education Services Information Document is also attached below and a copy of this document is also available in all our schools and libraries. If you would like a hard copy of the document please contact education@conwy.gov.uk.

Please return the completed form to your child's primary school by 20 November 2015. You will receive notification regarding your request for a place at the school by 1 March 2016.
