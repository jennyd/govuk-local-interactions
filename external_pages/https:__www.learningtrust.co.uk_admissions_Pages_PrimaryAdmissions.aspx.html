 Primary Admissions 

  

Awaiting a school place in a reception class

If you have not yet secured a reception class place and your child’s name is on the waiting list for your preferred schools. You can contact us to check your child’s waiting list position at anytime during the school year, but  we urge you to consider the available schools as your child must be on the roll of a school from the term following their 5th birthday. Your child's name will remain on the waiting list until 20 July 2016.  If you would like to discuss this matter further, please get in touch with the Admission Team by emailing inyear@learningtrust.co.uk or calling 020 8820 7397 or 020 8820 7398.

Reception class admission process for September 2016

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012, they will be due to start the reception class next year and you will need to start thinking about the primary schools you would like to apply for.

In order for you to be fully informed and prepared, the leaflet below will provide you with some basic information. 

Please refer to the relevant time-table dates. Information about the number of applications and places offered at each of the Hackney primary schools can be found in the guide below.

Our Primary Schools 2016 Admissions Guide for Parents contains information about the admissions arrangements for primary schools located in Hackney.

  • Download the Hackney Primary School Admissions Guide 2016  

To apply online, go to the eAdmissions website link on the right of this page, the closing date for on-time applications was 15 January 2016. If you have not yet applied you can still use the on-line application form to make a 'Late' application, if there are good reasons for your late submission and you want your application to be considered as 'on-time', you will need to provide an explanation. If you have not yet  submitted proof of address, please either bring your documents (2015/16 Council Tax Bill, proof of child's date of birth and recent Utility Bill ) to the Hackney Learning Trust, 2nd floor reception desk or post to; Admissions Team, Hackney Learning Trust, 1 Reading Lane, E8 1GQ. The notification date of outcome is Monday 18 April 2016.

Please contact the Admissions Team if you require further information by emailing reception.class@learningtrust.co.uk or calling 020 8820 7245 or 020 8820 7401.

Children with Special educational needs & disabilities

There is a separate process for pupils starting reception class who have a statement of special educational needs or an educational, health and care plan. Do not complete the above on-line form, a separate application form will be sent to you to list the schools you prefer for your child. If you have not received this form, please call Barbara Carpenter on 020 8820 7326  who will then direct you to the correct person to speak with.
