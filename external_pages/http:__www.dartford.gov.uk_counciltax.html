Council Tax Homepage

Welcome to the Council Tax Homepage for Dartford Borough Council. Below you will find all you need to know about council tax.

The pages include how to pay your council tax, where your money goes, how much to pay and information on discounts and reductions. You can also tell us about a change in your circumstances.

We hope you find these pages useful and we would welcome any feedback to help us improve our service.

  • How do I pay?

    The ways in which you can pay your Council Tax.

  • How much do I pay?

    Council Tax bands and charges, discounts and reductions and what the money is used for

  • My Council Tax

    Check your balance, view your bills, set up a Direct Debit, tell us about your move

  • Tell us about a change

    Tell us if you move or if people leave or join your household

  • Council Tax Reductions

    Ways you may be able to get money off your council tax bill

  • What if I can't or don't pay?

    Advice if you can't pay and the steps the Council can take to recover unpaid Council Tax
