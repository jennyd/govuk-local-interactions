Street Cleaning Request

This form allows street cleanliness issues such as overflowing bins, fly tipping and graffiti to be reported to Colchester Borough Council.

We can no longer take Street Cleaning requests that are made anonymously.

Your details will never been passed to anyone outside of the Council and will only be used by the Council if more information is required or to provide you feedback about your request.
