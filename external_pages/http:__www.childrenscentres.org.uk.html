Welcome to Ealing Children's Centres

Here you can find your nearest children’s centres – where they are and what they do. Each centre offers a range of activities and services for young children and their families (from pregnant mums to children under the age of five). Services offered range from childcare, leisure and learning to family support and family health. There’s something for everyone so why not pay them a visit?
