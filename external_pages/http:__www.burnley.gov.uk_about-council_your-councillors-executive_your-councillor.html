Your Councillor

Content

This is a debugging block

Your local Councillors have been elected by voters in a ward in the borough, and represent that ward. They are the key link between local residents and the way your services are run.

Councillors take part in the decision making Full Council meetings and are usually members of particular committees. Some Councillors have specific and more responsible roles: as Members of the Executive, or as a Chair or Vice Chair of a Committee.

Find out details about your representative below by clicking on their name/photo, or use the search box.
