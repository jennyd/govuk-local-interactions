Apprenticeships

In simple terms, an Apprenticeship is 'employment' with training which allows you to earn while you learn, gaining a real qualification and a real future. Currently, more than 100,000 employers in England offer Apprenticeships in 200,000 locations, covering over 170 industries and 1500 different job roles so there are plenty of opportunities out there.

Carry On Learning

There are many post 16 options out there if you want to carry on learning, whether it is to gain the qualifications needed for a specific career path or to continue with education while you decide what you want to do with your future.
