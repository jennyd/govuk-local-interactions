Large Community Grants

'Large Community Grants' provide a potential source of 50% grant funding of between £1,001 to £8,000 to 'Not for Profit' organisations delivering community projects in East Lindsey.

The grant scheme can support a various activities with 'capital' and 'revenue' grant funding, eligible activities includes but is not limited to: Community buildings, Sports and recreation projects, Heritage projects, Community arts projects, Community events and 'Support and advice' type projects.


The grants are awarded twice yearly through 2 'Funding Rounds' which take place during Spring and Autumn each year.For more information regarding who and what we can fund please download a copy of the 'Community Grant Information Guide' that also contains details regarding the smaller 'Councillors Community Grants'.

Background

We have provided 'Large Community Grants' for organisations delivering community projects in East Lindsey since 2009. Over the last 6 years more than £770,000 has been awarded through the grant scheme to a variety of projects occurring across the district. Our  map illustrates how much Community Grant funding has been awarded [190kb]

Who Can Apply?

The grant scheme is open to formally governed 'Not for Profit' organisations delivering community projects within East Lindsey. Eligible groups include but are not limited to:

  • Town & Parish Councils
  • Constituted Community Groups
  • Sports Clubs
  • Registered Charities
  • Social Enterprises
  • Parochial Church Council's

For additional information regarding who can and cannot apply for a Large Community Grant, including other eligibility criteria, please refer to the 'Community Grant Information Guide'. 

How much Funding is available?

The 'Large Community Grant Scheme' provides grants of between £1,001 and £8,000. Grants can not exceed 50% of the total project costs and so applicants will need to show where the remaining funds will come from. For information regarding funding ratios and 'In-kind' support please refer to the  'Community Grants Information Guide'. [7Mb]

Information Workshops

The Community Grants Team holds a series of workshops across the district each year to provide information and advice to groups interested in applying for a Community Grant (Including Councillors' Community Grants and 'Large Community Grants'). Unfortunately, the calendar of 14 workshops during 2015 has now completed. More workshops are planned to be held during March and April 2016.

For further information regarding the Community Grants Scheme please contact the Community Grants Team on 01507 613040 or 613073. Alternatively you can email us at community.grants@e-lindsey.gov.uk
