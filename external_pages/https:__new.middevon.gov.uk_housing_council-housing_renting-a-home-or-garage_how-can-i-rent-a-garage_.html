How can I rent a garage?

We own over 1,000 garages across Mid Devon. Please see the list of Vacant Garages to Let and list of Garage Location List

You can rent a garage by completing an online application form. This will ask for your personal details.  You will be able to choose up to three areas in which you wish to rent a garage. We give priority to tenants living within those areas.

Who can apply for a garage?

Anyone over 16 years of age who is eligible may apply. Persons under the age of 18 will need an adult to act as a legal guardian for the tenancy until their eighteenth birthday.

Businesses may also apply for a garage for storing any vehicle used for business purposes. However, they cannot use the garage for any other purpose.

Registered charities can rent garages at a reduced amount as long as we see proof of charitable registration.

Who is not eligible to apply for a garage?

Anyone who has the following may not apply to rent a garage:

  • MDDC tenants who are in rent arrears or breach of tenancy conditions
  • Committed acts of anti-social behaviour within the last two years
  • Allowed a council property to be used for illegal purposes
  • Serious damage or neglect of a council property
  • Intentionally misinforming us about information that we would need
  • Threatening behaviour or being violent or abusive towards council staff within the last two years
