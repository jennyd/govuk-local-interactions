Dead animal removal

It can be upsetting to see a dead animal on the roadside, whether it's a domestic animal or a wild animal such as a fox. If you come across one, contact us and we'll remove it.

Report a dead animal (Fix My Street)

What happens next

We try to remove dead animals from the public highway within 24 hours of being told about them.

If you've lost a pet

If you're missing a family pet and suspect it may have been in an accident, let us know. Details of where your pet was last seen and any distinguishing marks or collars will help us answer your queries.
