Welcome to the Rother District Council 24 hour secure Internet payments service. You may make a variety of payments using this secure site with most major credit or debit cards (not Diners Card or American Express).

NOTE :- Payment Using A Credit Card Will Incur An Additional 2% Fee. No Additional Charge Is Made For Paying Using A Debit Card.

ALL OTHER PAYMENTS includes :- 

  • Car Park Notice To Pay,
  • Planning Advice/Enquiry Fees,
  • Rother District Council Building Control Fees 
  • Hastings Borough Council Building Control Fees
  • Garden Waste Collection Service 
What may I pay?
You may pay for any of the items shown below by clicking on the selected line.
 

Please select from the following list:

  • Council Tax
  • Business Rates
  • Housing Benefit Overpayment Invoice
  • Other Invoices
  • ALL OTHER PAYMENTS

Security Information
Your transaction is now taking place within a secure payment system. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, a standard supported by most major Internet browsers. All of your personal and card details are protected using 128 bit encryption, when transmitted over the Internet.

Your card details are stored on a server behind a secure firewall. Only authorised personnel may access this information.
