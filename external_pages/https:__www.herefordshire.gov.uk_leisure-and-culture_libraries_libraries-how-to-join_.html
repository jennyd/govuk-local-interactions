How do I join the library?

You can join the library in any branch or if you are 16 or over you can join the library by completing the online membership form. Your joining pack, including your library card, will be posted out to you within a few days providing you with instant library membership. You will be able to borrow two books, use online resources and public computers.

For an upgrade to full membership, call into a library with identification showing your name and current address. This will give you access to all library services and if used regularly, your card is valid for life. 

To join the library please complete our membership form.

To update your details please complete our update details form.

Is there any charge for library services?

  • Books are loaned free of charge
  • Books not in the Herefordshire catalogue can be requested and a charge is made for this
  • There is a hire charge for music CDs, DVDs, talking books and PlayStation and Wii games
  • Fees are charged for overdue books depending on the borrower card type
  • There are replacement charges for lost or damaged items (these do not apply for under 5s)
  • Payment can be made by cash or cheques at any library, and some libraries can take card payments - for more details, see the library charges (PDF document).

For more information, you can also download the Herefordshire Library Byelaws.

How do I renew books and other loans?

  • You can renew books twice in person, on the telephone or online provided that they are not already requested
  • If one of your items has been requested by another reader you can contact the library for an additional 48 hours to return the item before incurring any charges. This runs from the date you make this loan extension
  • DVDs, games and talking books cannot be renewed online or over the telephone. Repeat hire charges are applicable for each subsequent loan period or part of a loan period, and are payable on return
