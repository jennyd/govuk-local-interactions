Disposing of bulky household items

A to Z of recycling - browse or search for information about how to reduce, reuse, recycle, compost, or safely dispose of everyday household items.

Disposing of items in usable condition

Before disposing of items such as furniture and household appliances that are in usable condition, consider whether you could offer them to friends or family, or to a charity shop.

Alternatively, why not try Freecycle or Freegle to recycle your unwanted items? Both are online groups for matching people who are giving and receiving free unwanted items in their local communities. Follow this link to find Freecycle groups and Freegle groups in Suffolk.

There is a community-run furniture project locally, which is a charitable organisation that arranges for the reuse of unwanted furniture and other household items:

Framlingham & District Furniture Project

Framlingham & District Furniture Project accepts donations of unwanted furniture and good quality household items. These are then passed on to families and individuals in the area who are in need. They are usually able to collect items from most areas of east Suffolk.

Donating unwanted items to St Elizabeth Hospice

St Elizabeth Hospice has a number of shops in East Suffolk. Donated goods can be taken directly to their retail centre in Ipswich or to any of the shops.

  • Donating goods to St Elizabeth Hospice

The Hospice will collect furniture donations, for free, six days a week. If you have any furniture that you would like to donate, call 0845 259 0319 to arrange collection. To comply with trading standards regulations, all upholstered furniture must have an intact fire safety label.

Suffolk Coastal Norse bulky items collection service 

Suffolk Coastal Norse offer a collection and disposal service for bulky items, charging £43 for the first item plus £16 for every additional item. If you would like to use this service please call 01394 444000.

The sorts of items collected include:

  • Three piece suites
  • Wardrobes
  • Carpets
  • Mattresses
  • Household furniture
  • Plastic baths
  • Washing machines, tumble dryers
  • Fridges, freezers, cookers, microwaves, TVs etc

Heavier items such as cast iron baths, central heating boilers, garage doors, oil tanks, storage heaters or an assortment of household clearance items or garden waste can be quoted for. Call Suffolk Coastal Norse on 01394 444000 or email scs@ncsgrp.co.uk for a quotation or further information.
