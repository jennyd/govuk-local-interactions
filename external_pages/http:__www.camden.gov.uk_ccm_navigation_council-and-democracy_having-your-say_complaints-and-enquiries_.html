Complaints and Enquiries

At Camden Council we treat complaints seriously and constantly seek to improve our services.  If you are dissatisfied with the service you have received, we want to know about it.  Similarly, if you are unhappy with an issue that the council is unaware of (e.g. anti-social behaviour, noise problems or reporting a repair), please let us know so that we can try and put matters right. If however you want to raise a formal complaint about the service you have received, kindly choose the option below to access our complaint form.

For more information about Camden’s complaints process please click here


Let the council know


  • About a housing repair
  • Make a parking appeal
  • About a planning enquiry
  • More...

Let the council know and we will respond to your enquiry within 10 working days, choose this option if you require action to be taken, e.g. to arrange a missed bin collection.


Make a complaint


Make a complaint about a council service and we will respond to your complaint within 20 working days, choose this option if you have experienced a service failure, e.g. poor staff conduct. You can also make a complaint about:

  • Children, schools & families
  • Adult social care
  • A councillor
