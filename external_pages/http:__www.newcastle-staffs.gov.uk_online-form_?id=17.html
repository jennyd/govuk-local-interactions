Request a Replacement Waste or Garden Waste Bin

Use the form below to request a replacement waste or garden waste bin

  • Please note that the bins remain property of the council at all times and additional garden waste bins can be chargeable.
  • If you do not need a standard 180L bin, or find it difficult to store, a smaller 140L bin is available upon request.
  • Households with 6 or more permanent residents are entitled to a larger 240L bin upon request. Please note that a formal application has to be made.

Please complete the form below and a Customer Services Agent will contact you to discuss the details of your request. Alternatively, you can call us on 01782 717717.
