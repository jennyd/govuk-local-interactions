Pest control

We provide an effective and affordable pest control service to residents and businesses within South Norfolk. Our professional service can help you if you have problems with; ants, mice, rats, carpet beetles, flies, fleas, hornets or wasps. 

Unfortunately, we are unable to treat for bees, moles, bats, birds, squirrels, rabbits or other feral animals.  Details of organisations who may be able to offer assistance on these can be found at the bottom of this page.

 

To book a pest control visit for any of those listed below, click here.

We are unable to carry out pest treatments for some species. These include birds, bats and bees and rabbits. For advice on these, please see below:

Birds
Many bird species and their nests are protected by law. If you have a problem with birds we advise that you contact a private pest control company. More information about laws protecting birds can web found on the Norfolk Constabulary website.

Bats
Bats are not a considered a pest, they are a protected species. It is an offence to harm bats or their roosting sites. If you have bats on your premises, please contact Natural England.
 
Bees
The Norfolk Bee Keeping Association can give you advice and the names of beekeepers. They will be able to advise you about dealing with the nest and in many situations, can remove the colony.
If you're unsure whether the nest in question is a bees nest or a wasps nest - have a look at this fact sheet and see what best describes your pest problem. If you think it's a wasps nest, visit our pest control treatment page.

Rabbits
We are unable to offer a treatment for rabbits. If you have a problem with rabbits we advise you contact a private pest control company
