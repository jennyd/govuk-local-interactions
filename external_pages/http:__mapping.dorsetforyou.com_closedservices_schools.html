Closed Schools in Dorset

Check your school's current status using the table below.

You can also listen to local radio stations for updates or click on your school's name to go to its own website. You should also check to see if there is any disruption to school transport.

All schools will automatically revert to 'no disruption' at 4pm each school day. After 4pm, the status shown refers to the following school day. Parents should check the page in the morning.

If your school is marked as 'partially open', some classes may be running as normal. Please check with your school for more details.

You can also subscribe to the RSS Feed to receive updates whenever a Schools status is changed. (What is an RSS feed?)
