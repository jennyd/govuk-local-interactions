Introduction

Information sessions

We hold monthly open evenings that give you the chance to speak to our social workers in person. Please do come along to meet our team and hear about the fostering process in Camden and the support we can offer you.

Seeing a child enjoying life and looking forward to the future is one of the greatest rewards of fostering, especially when the child may have experienced neglect or abuse in the past. As a foster carer, you will have to work slowly and patiently with any child placed with you to help them come to terms with their experiences and difficult feelings about separation from their families. We understand the challenges our foster carers face, and we will be with you every step of the way.

These pages will help you to decide if fostering is right for you, but please do call us for an informal discussion on our freephone number 0800 0281 436.

We are currently also recruiting foster carers who can help in the assessment of a parent and child in your own home. We will offer you specialist training and you can earn up to £36k per annum. To find out more, please email fostering@camden.gov.uk or call 0800 0281 436.

North London consortium

Camden is also a member borough of the North London Fostering Consortium

We are currently looking for remand foster carers. Have you got what it takes to foster a young person in trouble with the police? Help us turn their life around by caring for them in your own home. To find out more, please email info@fosteringnorthlondon.co.uk

Guides for children and young people

As part of their legal duty, Camden’s fostering service has produced two written guides for children who are looked after by Camden to describe what it’s like to live in foster care. There are two versions:

  • The children’s guide to fostering for six to 12 year-olds
  • The young person’s guide to fostering for 13 years upwards.

The guides explain what foster care is, how foster carers look after the children who live with them, what social workers do to help children and looked after children’s rights.

Copies of the guides are available from the Camden fostering service
