Already getting Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support?

Don’t forget to tell us about any changes in your circumstances or those of any other people who live with you. You can now do this online using our online change in circumstances form or download a copy of the change in circumstances form (PDF, 184KB) to fill out by hand, sign and return.
