Refuse & Recycling Collection Search

Enter your full postcode and find out what day your, recycling, refuse and green waste will be collected.

A list of properties with your postcode will appear below. Please select your property for details of the collection days.

Search

Whilst using the search if you can not find your property or your property details are incorrect, please use the 'Address Search Details' online form to inform us of any errors.

Further Information

Recycling, refuse and green waste should be out by 7am on collection days, at the front edge of your property where it can be easily seen, and no earlier than the night before. Please be careful not to obstruct pavements or roads.

Collections normally continue until 4pm. If put out correctly and your waste has been missed, you can report a missed waste collection by using the Report a missed collection button below.

Report a missed collection


Somerset Waste Partnership have issued guidance on refuse and recycling collection arrangements in the event of severe weather on their website. If collections are disrupted, further updates will be issued, including on SWP's Facebook page and Twitter feed.

If you have any queries regarding your collections, please contact Customer Services customerservices@westsomerset.gov.uk or by telephone on 01643 703704.
