Waste and Recycling

Rubbish and recycling collections in South Northamptonshire are made over a 4 day week by our in-house collection team. Your collections could happen any time between 7:00am and 5:00pm so please make sure your bins are ready for collection by 7:00am. We collect as normal on Good Friday and you will be notified of any changes to collections over Christmas and New Year.

The majority of households have alternate weekly collections of mixed recycling (blue bin), garden waste (green bin) and rubbish (black bin). In addition, most households have a weekly food waste collection from silver caddies. If you live in a flat we provide shared bins for rubbish and recycling.
