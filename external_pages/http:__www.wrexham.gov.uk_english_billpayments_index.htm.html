Ways to Pay

Wrexham County Borough Council is committed to making services more user-friendly and accessible.

As part of our on-going development programme we offer a range of payment methods for Council Tax, Business Rates, Housing Rents and Sundry debts securely to the Council.

Please note that a fee of 2.5% will be payable on all Credit Card payments.

  • Direct Debit
  • Online Payments
  • Internet Banking
  • Telephone Payments
  • Pay Online

Direct Debit

The safest and most convenient way for you to pay is by Direct Debit. You save time and there are no cheques to write, no cash to pay, no posting letters or queuing at counters.

You can always be assured with Direct Debit that you are guaranteed an immediate refund in the unlikely event of a mistake being made.

You can get a Direct Debit mandate in one of the following three ways:

  1. Pick one up at Contact Wrexham, 16 Lord Street, Wrexham.
  2. Phone up and request one from the appropriate section.
  3. Print one off, complete and sign it, and drop it off at one of our offices or post it to us. The forms can be completed electronically but you will need to print them out and sign them before returning.

Online Payments

This online payment facility allows you to pay bills directly, and securely, to the Council for:

  • Council Tax
  • Business Rates
  • Housing Rent
  • Housing Benefit Invoice
  • Debtors Invoices
  • Council Mortgages
  • School Fines (Fixed Penalty Notices)

From 1 April 2015 we are no longer able to accept payments for Dee Valley Water or the Credit Union.

Please note that a fee of 2.5% will be payable on all Credit Card payments.

By using this payments facility you agree to our website Online Payments Terms & Conditions.

Due to the level of security required with online payments your web browser should be either Firefox, Internet Explorer at version 6 or above.

You can also order and pay for:

  • Apply for a licence or permit
  • Bulky Household Waste Collection
  • Car Park Season Tickets
  • Order a Water Butt to collect and use rain water
  • Penalty Charge Notice (commonly known as parking fines and parking tickets) external link
  • Request a Pest Control Service
  • Online payment Frequently Asked Questions
  • Online Payments Terms & Conditions
  • View our Privacy Policy

Internet Banking

You can pay via your own internet banking page by quoting our bank details which are sort code 20-25-69 and number 43626725. You must ensure you quote your payment reference number.

Telephone Payments

You can make payments to Wrexham County Borough Council, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, by using the digital telephone payment option.

By telephoning 0300 3336500, and following the simple instructions, payments can be made using your credit or debit card by keying in the appropriate numbers.

This payment facility allows you to pay bills directly to the Council for:

  • Council Tax
  • Business Rates
  • Housing Rent
  • Debtors Invoices
  • Housing Benefit
