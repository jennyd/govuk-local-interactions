Online services/Payments/

main content Payments

Welcome to the London Borough of Bromley 24 hour secure Internet payments service. You may make a variety of payments using this secure site with most major credit or debit cards (not Diners Card or American Express).

What may I pay?
You may pay for any of the accounts shown below by clicking on the selected item. Please note that only debit cards are accepted for Council Tax and NDR payments.

Additional services will be added as we continue to develop this facility.


Please select from the following list:

  • Council Tax
  • NDR (Business Rates)
  • Trade Refuse
  • Debtor and Domiciliary Care Invoices
  • Miscellaneous
  • Fixed Penalty Notice (Not Parking Fines)
  • Business Improvement District Levy
  • Summer Parks Programme

Security Information
Your transaction is now taking place within a secure payment system. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, a standard supported by most major Internet browsers. All of your personal and card details are protected using 128 bit encryption, when transmitted over the Internet.

Your card details are stored on a server behind a secure firewall. Only authorised personnel may access this information.
