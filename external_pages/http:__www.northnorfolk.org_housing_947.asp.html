Adaptations for the disabled

Disabled Facilities Grants

If you, or someone you are living with, are disabled, you may be entitled to a Disabled Facilities Grant to help pay for adaptations to your home.

What work does the grant cover?

Disabled facilities grants cover work like widening doorways, installing ramps, improving access e.g. to the bathroom, and adapting heat and light controls among many other works.

  • How much is available?
  • Eligibility


How to claim

The first step is to contact North Norfolk District Council by calling 01263 516060.

Once your needs have been assessed, and a recommendation for adaptations has been made, you will be contacted by a grant officer.

Your grant application must be approved before you start any work.  If you are in receipt of benefits, they will not be affected by any grant awarded to you.

More information can be found at the GOV.UK website. Alternatively, download the Disabled Facilities Grant booklet.
