Homelessness

  • Preventing homelessness

If you're homeless - or about to become homeless - we're here to help. 

Come and speak to us as soon as possible. Ideally we want to stop you becoming homeless in the first place - and help you find an alternative home if we can't.

Get in touch and we'll arrange a housing advice interview.

There are a number of rules about homelessness, and we have to understand your circumstances before we work out how we can help you. 

Our legal responsibility to you

By law (the 1996 Housing Act, as amended by the 2002 Homelessness Act) we have a legal responsibility to find you a home, where we're satisfied a household has a local connection to North West Leicestershire and is:

  • Homeless
  • Eligible
  • In priority need
  • Not intentionally homeless.

Find out more about how we prevent homelessness, and also the rules under which we work. 

Homeguide

Before you come and talk to a housing advisor either in person or on the phone, we may ask you to register on Homeguide self-service housing advice system.

This system allows you to build up a profile tailored to your specific needs and circumstances so that you can access the right advice for you.  You can use they system via any internet connected device at any time.

Supporting homeless young adults aged 18-24

Ambition East Midlands is an innovative ‘housing first’ support project focussing on homeless young adults who are unable to access existing services. Launched in January 2015, this three year project will provide intensive support to ensure vulnerable 18–24 year olds have a secure home from which to build their aspirations for employment and learning.

Eligibility criteria

Ambition will work with young adults who meet all of the following criteria:

• Aged 18 to 24 (care leavers must be 21+)
• Not in education, employment, training
• Single homeless not in priority need
• A strategic priority for local authority support but unable to access supported accommodation, for example; because of previous difficulties, security issues, complex needs or lack of specialist provision.

More information about the project can be found on the Ambition East Midlands leaflet on the related documents tab below or by logging on to their website.

If you know someone who would benefit from the project and meets the eligibility criteria please contact Housing Choices on 0800 952 0079
