Welcome to Swansea Libraries

You can use these pages to search our library catalogue and access online content. Once logged in you can then renew and reserve books and DVD's online. See below on how to login.

Every Child A Library Member

The Every Child A Library Member initiative aims to improve the reading literacy and communication skills of children across Wales by introducing them to the wealth of material available at the local library – from a range of books, comics, audio-books, computers and magazines - and encourage reading for pleasure. This is a short film to celebrate the ECALM project as it is rolled out to all local authorities in Wales.

BookMyne Library App

If you are viewing this website on a mobile phone or tablet, BookMyne is a mobile app which you can use to search the Library catalogue, renew a book or see what you have on loan. It's available for Apple and Android devices and can be downloaded from your app store.


Wi-Fi

All of our Libraries (except Bonymaen) have Wi-Fi access. To access our Wi-Fi (and other IT resources) you must be a member of Swansea Libraries. If not yet a member and would like to join, check out our Joining the Library page to find out how.

Log in to this site

You can find the option to log in at the top of the page. Use your library card number as your username (including the SW prefix) and enter your Password/PIN to log in.

Having problems logging in?

Having problems logging in? Call us on 01792 636464.
