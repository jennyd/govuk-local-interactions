You do not have the item I want, what can you do for me?

If we haven't got the item you want and we are unable to buy it, we may be able to borrow it from another library authority. We will do our best to get your request but can make no guarantees, items can take up to eight weeks to be delivered or may be unobtainable. You can request an inter-library loan online or at your local library and there are charges for this service; unless you have a visual impairment.Â 

The charge is added to your account when we receive the requested item. We will notify you when your request is ready for collection and keep the item for two weeks. If you don't collect your item we will return it to the home library authority but you will remain liable for the charges. Inter-library loans are non-renewable.
