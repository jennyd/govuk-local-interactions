School Term and Holiday Dates

The following are school terms and holiday dates for St Helens schools and academies based on a pattern of 195 days. Each school sets 5 training/occasional days within the pattern and these are normally taken at the start or end of a term. You can view individual school holiday dates for 2015-16 by clicking on the link below.

Autumn half term Friday 23 October 2015 Monday 2 November 2015

Easter Bank Holiday Thursday 24 March 2016 Tuesday 29 March 2016
Easter break Friday 1 April 2016 Monday 18 April 2016

Academic Year 2016/17
HolidaySchools CloseSchools Open

Autumn half term Friday 21 October 2016 Monday 31 October 2016
Christmas Wednesday 21 December 2016 Tuesday 3 January 2017
Spring half term Friday 10 February 2017 Monday 20 February 2017
Easter break Friday 31 March 2017 Tuesday 18 April 2017
May Day Bank Holiday Friday 28 April 2017 Tuesday 2 May 2017
Summer half term Friday 26 May 2017 Monday 5 June 2017
Summer Friday 21 July 2017  

PLEASE NOTE: The Deregulation Bill 2015 grants all maintained schools the right to determine their own school term dates.  St Helens Local Authority will be recommending that schools follow the above dates for 2016 -17  - these dates are based on the standard school term and holiday pattern previously agreed by local authorities in the Merseyside area. However, it advises parents to check with the relevant schools before booking any holidays.
