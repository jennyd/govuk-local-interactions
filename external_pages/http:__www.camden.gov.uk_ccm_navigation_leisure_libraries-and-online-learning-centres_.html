Libraries and online learning centres

On this page you can manage your library account, renew your books, borrow using our elibrary and find out information about your local branch.

What’s happening in Camden’s libraries?

Camden future libraries – formal consultation report

The future libraries formal consultation is now closed. The consultation ran for 12 weeks from 15 July–6 October 2015.

Camden’s Cabinet agreed a new library strategy on 16 December 2015, which we plan to start to implement from September 2016. Further details on this new strategy can be found in the December 2015 Cabinet report

  • download: Camden future libraries consultation findings – independent analysis from University College London (PDF)

Your online library account 

You don’t need to visit a library reserve a book, film or DVD or to manage your account or renew your library items. You can do this online:


Your library account


  • Join Camden libraries
  • How to renew your library loans
  • Renew library loans online direct from our catalogue 
  • Manage your library account
  • View the library catalogue and search for books, films and music 
  • Camden elibrary

If you are already a member your account is automatically set up for you and can be used straight away.

Please note that Ancestry and Lexis Nexis Law Library can only be accessed from a library computer. You will need a library card to use this free service.


Your digital library


  • Download free ebooks
  • Download free audiobooks
  • Read ebooks in your browser 
  • Online reference information – a range of information resources you would normally pay for, but which are free with your library card
  • Online newspapers from PressDisplay
  • Online magazines from Zinio – please note that Zinio will be undergoing maintenance on Tuesday 9 February, 11am-12pm. You may experience a short disruption in service (for approximately one hour). During this period you may not be able to checkout, read online or download emagazines on the Zinio or libraries site through the app or your web browser.
There’s a wide variety of online information available from our digital library pages, whether it’s research using newspaper archives, art libraries, family history, literature, the Times Digital Archive, biography and using the Oxford English Dictionary – all of these and more can be accessed online for free. 

Hire a Library Space

Did you know you can hire our libraries and library rooms managed by Camden Council for events and activities? Events include seminars, workshops, meetings, children’s parties, social gatherings, exhibitions and more. Restrictions apply on time of use.

Email the events team at events@camden.gov.uk or call 020 7974 5633.
