Community Support Shop

The Community Support Shop is based at Alexandra Place and Ashwani Gupta (Community Development Worker) welcomes local residents to drop in to discuss needs, issues and concerns.

  • Supports the South East Woodley Community Association
  • Small courses are run from the shop (e.g. Henna Course)
  • Works in partnership with Alexandra Place, Emmanuel Church and Christ Church

Acorn Community Centre

The Acorn Community Centre provides services and activities for people in the community. The Acorn Cafe provides a wonderful, relaxing place to have a drink and enjoy views over the Emmbrook Walk.

The following facilities are available for hire:

  • A multi-purpose main hall with a terrace
  • 3 varying sized meeting rooms
  • Sensory and art rooms

The Acorn Community Centre is shared with Optalis who provide a learning disability Day Service, which offers:

  • Arts and Crafts
  • Interview Skills
  • Practical Life Skills
  • Sensory Experiences
  • Self Awareness
  • Many More!

Community Centre: Community House, Finchampstead

The Community House is in the heart of Finchampstead. The Community Development Worker for Finchampstead is Catriona MacMillan. Let Catriona know if there is anything that you would like to get involved with, or just pop along for a chat.

Events and activities regular schedule:

  • Monday: Housing Surgery - 9.30am to 11am
  • Tuesday: Tea and Tales
  • Wednesday: Finchampstead Community Trips - Leaving at 9.30am. Returning at 2pm approx.
  • Wednesday: Women's group - different activities each week 9.30sm to 12.30pm
  • Wednesday: Evening opening from 7pm to 9pm - drop in to raise issues, have a cuppa or do some craft

Shinfield Rise Community Flat

The Shinfield Rise Community Flat opened it's doors in November 2012. Since then we have been running a basic programme, which includes Housing Surgeries, local Have Your Say Meetings, and Drop-In Surgeries with local Borough and Parish Councillors. 
We also run several events and activities.

We really need ideas and suggestions from the community, as to what they want to see happening moving forward, all suggestions are welcome as we want to deliver the right services and activities to suit the local needs of residents in Shinfield Rise.

Community Centre Winnersh: Rainbow Community Centre

The Rainbow Community Centre serves the whole spectrum of the Winnersh community.

The community building is maintained by Wokingham Borough Council and managed by a Community Development Worker, Rose Green. Many events and activities are arranged by WBC, Winnersh Children's Centre and Centre Forward Community Action Group, a voluntary organisation run by local residents.  

All activities can be viewed through the monthly Rainbow Community newsletter below.

The Rainbow Community Centre is also the venue of the Winnersh Children's Centre.

  • Darts Club
  • 3 sessions of Short Mat Bowls per week
  • Bumps and Babes
  • After School Football, Dance & Tag Rugby
  • Days out
  • Life Long Learning courses
  • Craft Club (Fridays)
  • Plus a whole range of Children's Centre activities, including Play & Learn, Messy Play, Parent-led sessions, Speech Therapy, Well Baby Clinic, Paediatric First Aid and Cooking on a Budget classes</p>
