Find it

Use these directories to find a range of local services and facilities, get help and advice, and keep active.

  • Find your nearest...

    Your local councillor, MP, MEPs, dentists, schools, doctors, bin collections, recycling sites, leisure facilities and pharmacies based on your postcode or address.

  • Community service directory

    Here you'll find a range of local services, which may be provided by individuals, voluntary organisations, private companies or Sunderland City Council.

  • NHS Choices

    Here you'll find information about hospitals, GPs and dentists, information about health issues and treatments, along with details of registered care homes, nursing homes and care at home providers.

  • Wellbeing info Directory

    Advice and information about how to keep your mind and body healthy and where to find help if you have a problem.

  • Sports club and activity finder

    Use Tyne and Wear Sport's Club and Activity Finder to find the sports club or activity of your choice.
