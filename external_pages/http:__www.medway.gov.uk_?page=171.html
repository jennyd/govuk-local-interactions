Clinical waste disposal

Collections

We provide free collection of clinical waste to homes where residents are undergoing Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD). Residents do not need to apply directly to us for this service. The hospital should contact us on the residents behalf to arrange collection.

Our clinical waste collector SITA collects the waste each week from the property in specially provided sacks. A new supply of sacks will be provided each week at the same time the waste is collected. Ensure your waste is placed out for collection by 7am.

To ensure safe handling, transportation and disposal it is important that the sacks are not overfilled (not filled more than two thirds full) and are securely tied.

Note that the clinical waste collection service should not be used to dispose of incontinence pads; these should be double bagged and placed into the residual black sack waste.

Holiday arrangements

The scheduled collection day for CAPD waste is Monday for Medway residents. As this falls on bank holidays, SITA will contact you with an alternative day to collect your waste.

Missed collection

Any missed CAPD waste collections will be collected if reported within 24 hours. Report a missed collection.
