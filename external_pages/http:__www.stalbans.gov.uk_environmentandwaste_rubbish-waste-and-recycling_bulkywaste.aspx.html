Bulky items collection

Printer-friendly page

We offer a bulky waste collection service.

Large household items may also be taken to household waste recycling sites (tips/dumps).

Bulky waste collection service

The cost of this service is £25.50 (including VAT) for up to 6 items including white goods (i.e. refrigerators, freezers, washing machines, tumble dryers, spin dryers, cookers, dishwashers) or items such as TV sets, PC monitors, sun beds, fluorescent tubes and all electrical goods. THIS IS A NON-REFUNDABLE SERVICE AND ANY CANCELLED COLLECTIONS WILL STILL BE CHARGED FOR.


Not included in the £25.50 service are

  • items weighing more than 100kg

  • items where alternative collection and disposal arrangements need to be made.

A separate estimate may be provided on request at the discretion of our contractors e.g. a piano can currently be collected and disposed of for a fixed price of £58.75.

NB. Fridges and Freezer units, once collected, are dealt with separately, ie. they are taken away to have the CFC gasses removed prior to scrapping. If a unit is in good condition, however, it is reconditioned and resold.
