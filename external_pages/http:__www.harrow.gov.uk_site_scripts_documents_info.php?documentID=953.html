School travel assistance grant: for students aged 16 - 19

Introduction

  1. Introduction
  2. Apply
  3. Further info
  4. Contact us

Financial assistance for students

The Local Authority has a responsibility to prepare and publish a Post-16 Transport Policy Statement specifying the arrangements for young people of sixth form age receiving education or training. The Post-16 Transport Policy Statement is subject to the council's Corporate Complaints Policy.

Eligibility for school travel assistance grant
For full details of the eligibility process, the assessment, financial assistance and how to apply, please download our 16-19 Transport Policy Factsheet.

Apply for school travel assistance grant
To apply for school travel assistance, please go to the apply section.

Safe and sustainable travel in Harrow
Harrow actively encourages the use of safe and sustainable travel within the borough.  At primary and high schools, including those with sixth forms, this is done by providing professional traffic management expertise, funding and resources.

For further information, please see the borough's Sustainable Mode of Transport Strategy Summary.
