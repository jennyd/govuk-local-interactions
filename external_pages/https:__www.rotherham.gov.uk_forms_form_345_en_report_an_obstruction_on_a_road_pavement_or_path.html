Online forms: Report an obstruction on a road, pavement or path

This form has 3 pages

Please tell us about inconsiderate parking or other obstruction of a public highway or footpath, such as:

  • Builders' skips and materials
  • Scaffolding or hoardings
  • Temporary works, including traffic lights
  • Overhanging trees, hedges, bushes or plants
  • Mud or debris on the road
  • Mixing concrete or mortar on the highway
  • Unauthorised street traders
  • Intrusion on highway boundaries, where ownership of the highway has been unlawfully claimed
  • Discharge of water onto the highway
  • Blocking public rights of way
  • Unauthorised signs

The Council has powers to remove any obstruction, and can take court action against those responsible.
