Request Cleaning of Graffiti

Use the following form to report graffiti that needs to be removed.

Location of the Problem

Use the Street Search facility below to select the general location of the graffiti. Please use the 'graffiti details' text box to provide more specific location information.

Enter the street name below and click 'Search'.



Please wait...

 

Graffiti Details

Please include details about:

  • the size of the graffiti,
  • the material used (for example; paint or pen),
  • the surface affected (for example; brick, concrete, or glass),
  • the message if the nature is offensive, and
  • the specific location of the graffiti.

Your Details

Please provide your contact details. We need to know your name and contact details in order that an officer can contact you to discuss your complaint and to be able to obtain further information that may be helpful.

Contact Phone Number*

Please provide at least one contact phone number.
