Rent a garage

If you want to rent a garage or garage site, please contact your local area housing office or complete our online form. There may be a waiting list.

As with your council home, you must pay your garage rent on time so you don’t fall into arrears and risk repossession.

Complete our online survey and you could win £30 worth of Morrisons shoping vouchers.

The Council Housing Service is carrying out a review of all garage sites and plots in the city to better understand what customers want from the service.

Your opinions and ideas are important to us, and the feedback we receive will feed into the development of the service provided for letting garages,

Please take the time to complete the survey which can be completed via the link below.

This is your opportunity to have your say, and to ensure your priorities are considered as part of this service review.

All completed surveys will be entered into a prize draw and one winner will receive a prize of £30 of Morrison’s vouchers.

The closing date for completing the survey is Friday 15 March 2013.
