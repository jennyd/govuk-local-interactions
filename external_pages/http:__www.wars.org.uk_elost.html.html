Did you know we are also open for dog walking from 3pm?

Did you know that we are open for dog walking twice a day? We invite any inducted dog walkers to come to walk our dogs between 10am and 2pm and also 3pm and 4.30pm. The afternoon walks are just a gentle, post dinner stroll, to help settle the dogs before bed time. We are always looking for help for these walks, so if you have been through a Health & Safety induction please come along to help.

About Us

Worcestershire Animal Rescue Shelter opens it’s doors to abandoned, unwanted and mistreated animals in need of urgent care. Our main aims are to give shelter, medical treatment and care, and, of course, to rehome.

The Shelter is run by dedicated staff and volunteers wanting to ease the distress of animals who, through no fault of their own, are either abandoned, unwanted or mistreated.

Our task is enormous. It is essential we sustain our income, we receive no government funding and rely solely on kind and generous donations to meet running costs and, most importantly, the welfare of the animals in our care.
