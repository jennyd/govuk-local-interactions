Abandoned Vehicle Report

 

From 1st November 2014 tax discs are no longer issued. Please check if a vehicle is taxed before completing this form.

You can check if a vehicle is taxed by clicking here and entering the vehicle's details.

Colchester Borough Council cannot remove vehicles that are legally on the road. Any issues regarding obstructions etc must be referred to your local police station.

We can no longer take Abandoned Vehicle reports that are made anonymously.

Your details will never been passed to anyone outside of the Council and will only be used by the Council if more information is required or to provide you feedback about your report.

The Council cannot remove a vehicle solely because it is causing an obstruction. If this is case the Police may be able to take action and you should contact them on 101 or 01245 491491.
