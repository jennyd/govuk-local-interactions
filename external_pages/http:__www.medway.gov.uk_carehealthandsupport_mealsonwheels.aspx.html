Community meals

If you're finding it difficult to cook for yourself, you could benefit from community meals (formerly known as 'Meals on Wheels')

What are community meals?

It's a way you can choose to have a wide range of good food delivered to your home.

How you can get meals at home

You'll need to be assessed for eligibility first.

Simply phone 01634 306000 and we'll arrange for someone to visit you at home.

How to pay for your meals

If you're assessed as in need of community meals, we can help support you with up to £2.60 towards the delivery cost of your meals. 

Depending on your financial circumstances, you can also get an assessment to help with the cost of your meals by direct payment.
