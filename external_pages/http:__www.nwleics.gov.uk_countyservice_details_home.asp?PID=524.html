From this page you'll find lots of information and fun facts about reducing, reusing and recycling in North West Leicestershire.

Have a look around and feel free to contact us with any questions or queries.

Thanks you your hard work, we have a recycling rate of 47 percent - which is above the national average, and helps keep your council tax down by earning us more than one million pounds a year. 

We have different containers for different materials to make sure the quality of what we collect is always the best - so we can get the most for it.

All of our material goes to reprocessors in the UK, so there is less travelling for the materials before they are recycled - saving both CO2 emissions and money. Find out what happens to your recycling. 

For the latest news check out our Facebook  and Twitter pages, or our recycling news.

Frequently Asked Questions (1)

How do I get some new recycling containers or bins?

We deliver extra or replacement recycling containers every week. We look to deliver them in 7-10 working days. Just give us a call, visit the website or email to request yours. We will happily supply you with extra recycling containers to make sure you can recycle as much as possible.

Visit our site for more details or to apply on-line.
