Parking permits

Parking permits are for areas of Medway where on-street parking is limited. The scheme helps to improve parking availability for residents and businesses. To keep Medway moving and provide enough parking we offer many different types of permits.

Report a lost or stolen permit  or phone 01634 33 22 66.

 

Resident permits

If your address is in a resident permit area within Medway, you can buy a yearly resident parking permit. This will allow you to park in a resident permit space.

  • Apply for a Resident Permit (doc 27KB)
  • Resident permit terms and conditions (doc 27KB)

Visitor permits

Residents in a permit area can buy one annual visitor permit per property. This can be used on any vehicle and displays the expiry date, postcode and the badge area the permit may be used in. It will allow any visitor who comes to your property to park in a resident permit space nearby.

  • Apply for a Visitor Permit (doc 24KB)
  • Visitor permit terms and conditions (doc 44KB)
Business permits

Any business in a parking permit area can buy annual business permits for staff or company vehicles. This will allow you and your employees to park in spaces within the specified badge area.

  • Apply for a Business Permit (doc 16KB)
  • Business permit terms and conditions (doc 30KB) 
Carer permits

These permits are for professionals who need to park in any Controlled Parking Zones to provide help and care in the community. Restricted to a maximum stay of 2 hours, this permit is not valid for parking at the business address where it is registered.

  • Apply for a Carer Permit (doc 28KB)
  • Carer parking permit terms and conditions (doc 30KB)

 
Late night permits

These allow the permit holder to park in a single car park between 7pm and 9am. You can only use the car park specified on the permit.

  • Apply for a Late Night Permit (doc 8KB)
  • Late night parking permits terms and conditions (doc 29KB)
Special business permits

These permits are for businesses who are required to park within Medway's Controlled Parking Zones so they can carry out their day-to-day business.

  • Apply for a Special Business Permit (doc 9KB)
  • Special business permit terms and conditions (doc 30KB)
Essential user permits

These permits are for professionals who provide important services to a community and its residents. It's ideal if you are making home visits to the sick and housebound or even if you are carrying out duties in connection to the health and safety of the community. The permits are also ideal for people carrying out works to properties or highway maintenance works. The permit is not valid for parking at the business address where it is registered.

  • Apply for a Essential User Permit (doc 27.5KB)
  • Essential user permit terms and conditions (pdf 31KB)
Daily visitor
vouchers		 Daily visitor vouchers allow your visitors to park in the controlled parking zone where you live or your business is located. Find out more about daily visitor vouchers.

Can I get a full or partial refund if I stop using a permit?

Once a permit has been issued, a refund cannot be given. However, you are able to get a refund with season tickets.
