Public health pests are best dealt with by professional pest controllers because successful treatments require a high degree of knowledge and skill. All our Pest Control Officers have up to date qualifications and knowledge of the laws relating to pest control. The staff are trained to the highest standard: The Royal Society of Health - Pest Control (R.S.H.P.C)

 

We are unable to provide a service for pigeons, squirrels, foxes and bees.

The Pest Control Service will undertake treatments for rats, mice inside the home, ants, cluster flies, wasps, bed bugs, moths, beetles and cockroaches in domestic and commercial premises.

We also offer a Pest Management Service for businesses and our portfolio of commercial contracts include food producers, farms, nursing homes and schools.

Pay online

You can pay right now using our secure payment system. Make sure to select the correct service from the left hand side.

The Cost

The charges for domestic treatments are as follows:

Domestic charges per treatment
rats outside   £60 (including VAT)
rats inside   £60 (including VAT)
mice   £60 (including VAT)
ants   £42 (including VAT)
fleas   £60 (including VAT)
cluster flies   £60 (including VAT)
wasps   £42 (including VAT)
bed bugs   £180 (including VAT)
moths   £60 (including VAT)
beetles   £60 (including VAT)
cockroaches   £60 (including VAT)

Advice- single visit offering an investigation of the problem and advice to the householder as to how best to deal with the situation

Advice visit  £20 (including VAT)

All charges are subject to VAT at the current rate.

Payment can be made when you request the treatment using a credit or debit card or you can request an invoice which will cost an extra £10 + VAT per hour.

Commercial premises will be charged £60 + VAT per hour.

Contact us

Please ring us on 01609 779977 to report a pest problem. When you ring us please have the following details to hand:

  • name
  • telephone number
  • email address
  • address of where the pest problem is
  • type of pest
  • either your Council Tax Reduction number or a debit card / credit card (please note that there is an extra charge for payment by credit card)
