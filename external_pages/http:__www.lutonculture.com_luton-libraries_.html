Do it online

Access a wide range of library services from home or on the move.

  • Use the library catalogue to renew your books, reserve or request items.
  • Download an eBook from our Digital Media Library,
  • Take a course online with Universal Class
  • Learn a new language with Transparent Languages
  • Browse a newspaper with PressDisplay
  • Read a magazine online with Zinio

Borrow E-Books

Library members can borrow eBooks free of charge from our Digital Library.

Kindle readers won't work, but the Kindle Fire tablets will, as will iPhones and iPads.

There are thousands of titles to choose from, and a great selection of children's and young people's books too. For more information and tutorials, go to:

Digital Media Library

Newspapers

Check out our new online newspaper service, from PressDisplay.

When you click on the link, you will need your library card number to log on.

 http://library.pressdisplay.com/

 

Magazines

Library members can now read magazines online free of charge.

Zinio Magazines – a selection of some of the most popular magazines, including BBC titles, such as Good Food, Top Gear, Cosmo, Gardener’s World, Men’s Health and TV Times. These can be checked out and viewed on your laptop, PC or tablet. When you click on the link you will be prompted to create an account, which you will use to log in each time. 

https://www.rbdigital.com/luton/zinio
