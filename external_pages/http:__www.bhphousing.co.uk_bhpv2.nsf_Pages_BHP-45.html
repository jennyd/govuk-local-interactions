Estate parking and garages

Estate parking 

 

BHP operates parking control on many of our estates. This means that there may be restrictions on where people can park. Vehicles parked on the estate without proper authorisation will be subject to parking enforcement measures.

 

If parking control operates on your estate, please make sure you have applied for a resident's parking permit. Where visitor's parking is available on your estate, you must apply for the appropriate visitor's parking permit by applying through Wing Parking.

 

Please ensure that you have the following documents to ensure that your application is approved.Vehicle registration document (V5), Vehicle has to be registered to where you live.

 

  • Valid MOT certificate
  • Valid Road tax
  • Proof of purchase
  • Utility bills.

 

If you wish to discuss any parking issues, please contact estate.services@bhphousing.co.uk or 0208 937 2750.

Garages  

 

BHP manages council owned garages and there are some council garages available for rent at competitive rates.  Garages are for personal private use only and not for commercial activities and they are intended for the storage of a motor vehicle only! They are not sufficiently weather tight or secure for the storage of any other goods. You should not store petrol or any other inflammable materials in your garage and you should not sublet your garage to anyone else. Finally you will not be offered a garage if you have rent arrears.    You can request a garage by competing the following:
