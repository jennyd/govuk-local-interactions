Adoption

North Tyneside Fostering and Adoption Service

We are seeking adoptive parents from across the North East, to provide homes for children aged from birth to nine years.

More adoptive parents are needed especially for older children and sibling groups. We currently have children waiting to be adopted.

You can contact the Fostering and Adoption Service on (0191) 643 2540 or email fosteringandadoption@northtyneside.gov.uk.

Special Guardianship

Special Guardianship Orders (SGOs) were introduced to address the need for permanency for children and young people in care where adoption is not the right plan.  

These orders are designed to provide greater security and permanence than the alternatives offered for the child or young person and to give parental responsibility to their carers without ending the child’s legal connection to their birth family.
