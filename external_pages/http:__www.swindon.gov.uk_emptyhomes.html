Report an empty property

Empty homes can attract squatters, vandals and burglars. Neglected gardens can become overgrown which has an impact on neighbouring properties. In extreme cases, the property becomes dangerous and a risk to public safety.

The council will try in the first instance to help an empty home owner bring their property back into use; however there are also various enforcement options that are available to the council that we will take in extreme circumstances.

How to report an empty property

If you know of an empty residential property that you would like to report, please either email emptyhomes@swindon.gov.uk or call on 01793 463275.

If you own an empty property

If you own an empty property our Empty Homes Officer can give you advice and assistance on getting the property back into use. There are grants, loans and other schemes that you may not be aware of that can help you renovate your property and save you money.
