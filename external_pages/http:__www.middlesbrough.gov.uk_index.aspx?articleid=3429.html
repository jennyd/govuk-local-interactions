Myplace Middlesbrough

Myplace is a state-of-the art £4.3 million venue and activity centre for young people in Middlesbrough

Visit the new Official Myplace Website: http://myplaceboro.co.uk/

" For young people, by young people"

About Myplace...

Myplace is a state-of-the art £4.3 million venue and activity centre for young people in Middlesbrough. It has amazing facilities and opportunities for young people including a theatre, TV and recording studio, Internet café, alcohol free nightclub, sensory room and amphitheatre. We officially opened on 30th June 2012.

Myplace is your place...

Everything from the modern, colourful interior to the range of activities, leisure and support on offer, has been decided by the young people for the young people. From the initial grant application to the doors opening, a group of over 800 young people have been involved with every decision - and you can get involved too!

Myplace is open for everyone aged 9-19 years (or up to 25 with special needs).

There are many ways in which you can enjoy Myplace it's up to you!

  • Pop down for a coffee or game of pool with some friends
  • See what's going on, or sign up for a future event, activity or group
  • Check job vacancies, volunteering, training courses or get confidential advice, information or guidance from a range of services

Become a member of myplace for just £5 a year.  You can take part in planned sessions for 50p or £1 for non members. Sessions currently run from 7pm to 9pm Tuesday, Wednesday & Friday and on Thursday from 6pm to 8pm. All sessions currently run for 2 hours.

Also we are open on Saturday's 10am to 4pm.

What Middlesbrough's young people say about Myplace:

  • Nancy Harker, aged 13: "Myplace is my inspiration. My place of freedom and my place of fun."
  • Bobby-Leigh Howard, aged 15: "Myplace, Middlesbrough's place, our place. That's what Myplace means to me!"
  • Dion Howard, aged nine: "Myplace is fun. I really like it and everybody is friendly."
