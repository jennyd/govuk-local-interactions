My housing tenancy

'My Housing Tenancy' is an online service where you can access the details of your housing tenancy, including such things as:

  • Rent payments
  • Balances on your account
  • Repairs pending

To use the system you will first need to register and you will need to know your tenancy/rent reference number to do this:

Register for My Housing Tenancy online

Once you have registered you will be able to access the My Housing Tenancy service online. If you forget your username or password, please re-register using the above button.

Access My Housing Tenancy online

If you have any problems logging onto the system, or wish to update any of your personal details, please contact:

E-mail: housing@swindon.gov.uk
Tel: 01793 445503 (Monday to Friday, 9.00am-5.00pm)

For rent payment options, please go to the Pay your Council Rent webpage.
