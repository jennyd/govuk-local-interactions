Slough post-16 travel 2015-16

The post 16 reduced fare cards scheme for 2015-16 will open on 20 August 2015 with application forms being available from this webpage or from St Martins Place Reception from this date.

Slough LA has its own transport policy statement for students aged 16-18 in further education and for continuing students aged 19 and over.

Please note, eligibility is income assessed.

  • Slough post 16 transport policy 15/16
  • Student reduced fare card application form 15/16

The Department for Education has a bursary scheme for 16-19 year olds which is administered by schools and colleges themselves. If you are eligible for access to this bursary scheme, any funding you receive can be used towards transport costs. For further information relating to financial support, contact the student finance/welfare offices of your school or college.

For further information relating to Slough's post 16 travel policy, please contact the Home to School Transport Team on 01753 875729 or email educationtransport@slough.gov.uk.
