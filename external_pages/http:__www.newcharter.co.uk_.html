Home

 

New Charter Group is a social landlord with 19,500 homes. We have a turnover of £100 million and a workforce of around 1,000 people.

The Group consists of New Charter Homes, based in the Greater Manchester area, Gedling Homes in Nottingham and Aksa Homes in Oldham. We have our own New Charter Building Company, and one of our most recent partnerships is with Threshold, based in Oldham. We are proud to sponsor the New Charter Academy, in Ashton-under-Lyne, and we have recently sponsored the Silver Springs Primary Academy and Copley Academy in Stalybridge.

Our mission is to provide GREAT Homes, GREAT Neighbourhoods and GREAT People

You can read more about us in our Annual Report 2014-15.
