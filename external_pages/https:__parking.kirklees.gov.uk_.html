PCN Details

You can use this site to pay for a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), to find out more information about the contravention, or to appeal.

PCN details
 
 
Penalty Charge Notices will not be available on this system until the next administrative working day after issue.
 
Should you experience any problems when making a payment DO NOT attempt to make the payment again. Please send an email enquiry to: highways.carparks@kirklees.gov.uk
