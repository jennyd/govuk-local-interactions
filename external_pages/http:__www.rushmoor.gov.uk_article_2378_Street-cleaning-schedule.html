Street cleaning schedule

We manually clean our residential roads every four weeks and with our road sweepers every 12 weeks.

Our main roads and some busier roads are cleaned more often.

Coning off streets for cleaning

We have to cone off some of our streets before we clean them. To find out if we need to cone off your street, see our Coning off streets for cleaning 2015 page.

To find out when your area is due to be cleaned, find your road on the a to z list below.
