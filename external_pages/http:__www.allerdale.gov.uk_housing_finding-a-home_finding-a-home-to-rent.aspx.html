  • View information on private renting

Renting from a Housing Association

Housing Associations (Registered Providers) have been building and providing homes for many years. To make sure that rents are kept to an affordable level, they receive financial help from both the Government and Councils. The easiest way to rent from a housing association is to use the Cumbria Choice service.
