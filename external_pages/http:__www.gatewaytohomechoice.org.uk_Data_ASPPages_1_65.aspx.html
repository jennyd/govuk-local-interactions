Welcome to the Gateway to Homechoice website 

Gateway to Homechoice is a choice based lettings system, advertising Council and Housing Association properties in the local authority areas of Babergh, Braintree, Colchester, Ipswich, Maldon, Mid Suffolk, Suffolk Coastal and Waveney.

  • This website provides information on the Gateway to Homechoice scheme. 
  • For information about the scheme and how to register, please click on the relevant links on the left hand side of the page.
  • Below, you can also find useful information on the following:

    - Updates and news relating to the Gateway to Homechoice scheme
    - Information about our online housing options service
    - How to access mutual exchange opportunities 
    - Details of current shared ownership opportunities
    - Latest information from our partners (for example, details of new housing developments and consultation events)

Information from our partners

Get extra help towards your rent and/or Council Tax

If the amount of Housing Benefit or the housing costs element of your Universal Credit you receive does not cover your full rent you may be eligible to apply for a Discretionary Housing Payment (DHP) to help you pay the shortfall.

If you need extra assistance towards your Council Tax you may qualify for help under our Local Council Tax Reduction Scheme/Council Tax Support Scheme.

To apply for either a Discretionary Housing Payment or financial help towards your council tax go to your Local Authority website.
