Craven Community Safety Partnership

Craven Community Safety Partnership (CCSP) is a multi agency partnership working in Craven to reduce crime and disorder, and the fear of crime.

Craven Community Safety Partnership is made up of the following:

Executive Board

This group sets the strategic vision for the Partnership. It is made up of senior representatives from public and voluntary organisations.

Please see the CCSP Executive Membership  page for more information.

Key Area Task Groups

These groups develop and deliver actions towards local priorities in the area. They are made up of people who have direct involvement, interest or specialist knowledge of the issues being addressed.

Please see the Vision and Priorities  page for more information, including the actions of the Task Groups.

Partnership Plan

The Partnership originally developed a three year plan for 2008-2011. A refresh has now been produced to cover the period 2010-11.
The Partnership Plan provides a common set of priorities for our crime and disorder partners to work towards.

Please see Craven Community Safety Partnership Plan 2013 - 2014 [892kb]  for more information.

Immobilise

To register your property for free and improve your chances of getting it back if it is lost or stolen please visit the Immobilise website.
