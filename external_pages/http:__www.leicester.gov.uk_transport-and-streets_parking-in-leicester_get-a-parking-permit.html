Get a parking permit

A parking permit scheme is in operation in some residential streets in Leicester. A resident parking permit costs £25 and lasts for 12 months. Applications for residents' permits can be made online

Quick links

  • Apply for a permit online
  • See what supporting documents you need to provide

Further information

Where are the permit zones?

You can download a list of eligible addresses at the bottom of this page. You'll also find guidance notes on applying for the permits in each of the areas. 

Other types of permit

Each household can apply for five free visitors permits every year. You can also buy 15 visitor parking permits (scratchcards) per week at a cost of £1 per permit. These are free of charge for over 60's.

Charities, community centres and religious organisations are entitled to three permits a year, at a cost of £25 each. To apply contact us on 0116 454 6300.

Application forms for business permits can be made online. Applications for maintenance permits can only be made by post or in person. Both forms can be downloaded at the bottom of this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions with regards to permits, please email us.
