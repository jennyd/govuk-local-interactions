Pest control

There are three main areas of activity. Please select the most appropriate area.

  • Residential services: Dealing with pests in residents’ own homes and gardens

  • Enforcement services: Dealing with pests reported at premises other than your own

  • Commercial services: Pest control and pest protection services available to businesses within the London Borough of Hounslow

Residential services

Services are provided to residents to deal with certain household pests in their homes and gardens. These services are all charged for although reduced prices may apply for certain services if the househoulder is receiving a qualifying benefit. If you are reporting pest problems at locations other than your home or in a communal area then please follow our Pests at other premises link.

Charges

The current rates, including concessions, for pest control treatments in domestic premises can be downloaded from this page.

Payment will be accepted on or before the first visit and may be made by cash, cheque, or credit or debit card.

Pests treated

Do reduced prices apply?

Rats, fleas, bedbugs, cockroaches, Pharaoh ants

Yes. See the information below on claiming reduced price services

Mice, squirrels, wasps, garden ants

No. Charges apply in ALL cases. Further information on charges can be downloaded from this page

Claiming reduced price services

Services to control fleas, bedbugs, cockroaches, and Pharaoh ants can be provided at a reduced rate of approximately 80% of the full rate, where the householder receives one or more qualifying benefits at the time of service delivery. Please note that the householder is generally taken to mean the person registered for council tax at the property.

Services to control rats can be provided free of charge where the householder receives one or more of the qualifying benefits or Disability Living Allowance.

The full list of qualifying benefits is:

  • Income Support

  • Housing Benefit

  • Pension Credit

  • Council Tax Benefit (not rebate)

  • Employment Support Allowance

  • Job Seeker's Allowance

  • Universal Credit

Other concessions

Young residents who do not qualify for means testing are able to access concessionary rates if one of their parents or guardians are in receipt of means-tested benefits.

Local community groups with an annual turnover less than £15k may also be offered concession rates.

Limit on free pest control services

Any household receiving a free rat treatment (due to receiving one of the qualifying benefits above), will only be able to claim one free rat treatment in any twelve-month period. If your pest problem recurs, within this period then a charge of 80% of the full rate applicable at the time will be required. You can reduce the risks of pest re-infestation following treatment by following all the advice given by our pest control officers and that contained in any leaflets given to you during treatment.
