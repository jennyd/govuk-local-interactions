Pay your Penalty Charge Notice online

Welcome to the Dacorum Borough Council Penalty Charge Notice payment system.

Please note if you receive a ticket and pay it, you cannot contest it later.

Before proceeding please ensure that you have:
  • The charge notice number.
  • Your vehicle registration number.
  • A valid credit or debit card.
This system will accept Visa, Mastercard, Switch/Maestro and Electron.

Security Information: A padlock displayed on your browser indicates you are using a secure, encrypted connection.
