Message for tenants

To all those who’ve received the latest issue of our Door to Door newsletter, we’d like to correct the information on page 3 of the newsletter and in your rent statements regarding council tax support.

The council is not reducing council tax support in 2016.

The newsletter and rent statements make reference to a consultation about changes that could happen in the future. However, nothing’s been decided yet.

There’s no need for you to take any further action at this point and your council tax bill for this year will not be affected.

We’re sorry for the miscommunication.
