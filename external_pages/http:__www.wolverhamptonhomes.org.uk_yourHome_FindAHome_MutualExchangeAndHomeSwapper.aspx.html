Mutual Exchange

If you're already a secure tenant and you're looking for a new home - a mutual exchange or house swap can be a quick and easy way to move to a property that suits you better. You don't need to be on a waiting list or have a housing need. 

You just need to find someone who wants to swap with you, let us know so we can do a few checks and if all is well you can sign the exchange paperwork then move into your new home.

How Do I Find a Swap?

  1. Register with Swap and Move - it's a free exchange website where you can advertise your home, search for a swap by postcode and apply online when you've found a swap. Make sure you upload lots of nice photos to show off your home to possible swappers.
    Swap and Move also have an app so you check your exchanges on the go: Download the app.
  2. There are Facebook groups and pages which you can use to advertise your home to other tenants. This is a great way to find a swap but please be careful about giving your address and contact details to strangers. Here are a few links:
  • Council Exchange Wolverhampton
  • Wolverhampton Serious Homeswappers
  • WV14 & DY3 Council and Housing Association Home Swap
  • UK Homeswap
  • Council House Exchanges

If you have any rent arrears, or other housing debts, and want to move - contact us as soon as possible.

Top tips for swappers:
1. Upload photos to your advert to show off your home to possible swappers.
2. Make sure you opt-in to receive emails from Swap and Move for your new matches.
3. Share your advert with as many people as possible - share it with your friends on Facebook and Twitter and share it on our Facebook page so we can share it for you.
