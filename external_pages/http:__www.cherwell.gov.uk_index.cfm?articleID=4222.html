Disabled facilities grant

How do you rate this information / service?

What we do with your feedback

Disabled Facility Grants (DFGs) are used to help people who are registered disabled with Oxfordshire County Council (OCC), or eligible to be registered.

What the service is for

The purpose of the grant is to ensure that disabled people can properly access and use the facilities in their homes. DFGs are typically used to provide:

  • ramps
  • wider doors
  • stairlifts
  • level-access showers
  • through-floor lifts
  • low level facilities.

Through our in-house home improvement agency (HIA) we can also provide help with:

  • selecting contractors
  • getting prices
  • administering works.

There is a modest fee for this particular service, but it can often be included as part of the grant.

How you can access the service

Before you can apply for a grant your disability must be assessed by an Occupational Therapist (OT) employed by Oxfordshire County Council. The OT will tell us if they think a DFG is the right way to meet your needs and what sort of adaptations they think are suitable.

We then decide if the proposed adaptations are 'necessary and appropriate' and 'reasonable and practicable'. Our Disabled Facilities Grant policy (which can be viewed and downloaded on the right) explains how we set about making those decisions.

More information about the DFG process and how to request an initial assessment is given in the Adaptations Booklet (which can also be viewed and downloaded on the right).

Details required

If you are eligible for adaptations to be made to your home we need to carry out a means test to see how much you need to contribute to the cost of the work. The assessment will determine whether:

  • you contribute nothing
  • you must contribute a certain amount towards the work
  • you must pay for all the work to be carried out.

We will guide you through this process and help you with any form filling.

We must give you a decision in writing within six months of receipt of a valid completed application form and any other information we may require.
