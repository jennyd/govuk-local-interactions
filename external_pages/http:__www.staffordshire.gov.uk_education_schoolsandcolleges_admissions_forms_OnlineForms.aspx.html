Paper Application Forms

Anyone with parental responsibility can apply.  We expect parents to agree on school places before an application is made, and may demand evidence from you that this is the case.  The local authority is not in a position to intervene in disputes between parents over school applications and will demand that these are resolved privately.

Admission to 14 - 19 establishments (UTCs) September 2016

You should apply when your child is in Year 9 and either complete an online application or complete the specific application form for the Academy.

Each academy will recruit students at age 14 for admission into Year 10 and again at age 16 into Year 12.

 

The JCB Academy

The JCB Academy are focused on delivering high-quality engineering and business education. Applications will close on 31 January 2016.

The JCB Application Form (597KB)

The JCB Application Form (312KB) 

Burton and South Derbyshire University Technical College

The Burton and South Derbyshire UTC offers a ground-breaking new concept in education for 14-19 year olds, providing academic and specialist education with work-based skills.  Applications will close on 31 January 2016. 

To apply please view the college website.  

The West Midlands Construction University Technical College

The West Midlands UTC is for students from Year 10 to Year 12 who want to make their mark on the world around them through construction and the application of ITC in the built environment.  Applications will close on 31 October 2015.

If you live in Staffordshire and wish to apply for a place at a 14 - 19 academy that is not any of those mentioned above, you should either complete an online application or the 14-19 Academy Application Form above.

If you do not live in Staffordshire then you will need to apply directly to the Local Authority in which you live.

 

Admission to 13 - 16 Rural Enterpise Academy September 2016

The Rural Enterprise Academy enables young people from the age of 13, to gain a broad education with traditional and essential qualifications such as English, Maths and Science, as well as the opportunity to link learning, wherever possible, to rural enterprise, environmental sustainability and land-based subjects. You should apply when you child is on Year 8 and either complete an online application or the paper application form on their website.  Applications will close on 31 January 2016.
