Appeal against a decision

If you disagree with our decision on Housing Benefit

You can either: 

  • ask for an explanation 
  • ask us to look again at the decision 
  • appeal against the decision – this can only be in writing. 

If you appeal against the Housing Benefit decision your appeal will be heard by an independent tribunal administered by the HM Courts & Tribunal Service.

For more details, please contact us.
