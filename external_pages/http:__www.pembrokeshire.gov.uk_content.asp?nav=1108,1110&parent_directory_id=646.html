Applying for a home

The way that Council housing and housing association properties are allocated has changed. Homes are now let through ChoiceHomes@Pembrokeshire scheme.

Are you looking to apply for a home with any of these housing providers?

Pembrokeshire County Council
Pembrokeshire Housing Association
Cymdeithas Tai Cantref

For an application form, and guidance notes, visit www.choicehomespembrokeshire.org (opens new window). Alternatively you can e-mail CBL@pembrokeshire.gov.uk phone one of the housing providers (numbers below).

When you have returned the form you will receive a membership pack explaining how to apply for properties that are available to let.
