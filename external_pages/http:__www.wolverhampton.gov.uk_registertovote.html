Register to vote online

The system for registering to vote changed in June 2014. The new system is called 'Individual Electoral Registration'.

Voting is easy - find out how to vote with this quick guide, it will provide you with all the information you need to know.


Register to voteRegister online

  • you can now register online at www.gov.uk/register-to-vote
  • everyone is now responsible for registering themselves
  • you need to provide a few more details to register - including your national insurance number and date of birth

Do I need to do anything?

Most people who are already registered to vote will be registered automatically under the new system. They do not need to do anything. However, some people will need to take action to join the new register. We are writing to people to tell them whether they need to take action.

Annual canvass

Every year we will write to each household within the city to ensure that we hold the correct details on our Electoral Register. This is also know as the annual canvass. Find out .
