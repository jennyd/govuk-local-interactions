Current School Closures

Last updated: 03/02/2016

We recognise that the decision to close a school in the event of an emergency (e.g. snow, flooding, no water or heating) significantly impacts on parents and guardians.  As such we have produced School Emergency Closures - A Guide for Parents and Guardians.

Information for Schools

School Emergency Closure procedures for schools can be found within School Messenger direct from the home page.  Please note that you will need to login to this system.
