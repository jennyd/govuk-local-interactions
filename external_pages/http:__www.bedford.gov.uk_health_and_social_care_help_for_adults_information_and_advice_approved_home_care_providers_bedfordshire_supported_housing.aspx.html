Francis House Homecare Service is a specialist domiciliary care agency providing care to people who have a serious and enduring mental health problem including dementia. This includes those who may also have a history of non-engagement with services, pose an increased risk to themselves or others. We also provide a service to people with a learning disability, physical health needs and substance misuse problems.  

 

Our aims

We aim to support individuals to live their lives how they choose. One of our core principles is flexibly delivering the agreed number of hours at times which suit the service user. 

 

Example of services    

  • For people with challenging behaviour we can provide 24 hour support to enable them to live in their own home.
  • Support with medication management
  • Monitoring of the service users mental health and help to manage symptoms
  • Effective management of the symptoms associated with psychosis such as self-neglect, an inability to self- motivate, self-isolation and anti-social behaviour associated with serious mental health problems.
  • Assistance with personal care such as dressing, bathing and eating.
  • Support with social activities, accessing community resources, practical activities including paying bills etc.
