Council Tax Discounts

The full council tax bill is worked out on the assumption that there are two adults over 18 years of age in a dwelling.

 

Single Person Discount - 25%


If only one adult lives in a dwelling as his or her main home, the bill can be reduced by 25%.  More information is available here.

  

Other discounts


Certain people are not counted when looking at the number of adult residents in a property if they meet certain conditions. For example, people who are:

 

  • full time students, student nurses, apprentices and youth training trainees;
  • 18- and 19-year olds who are at, or who have just left, school or college;
  • patients whose main residence is in hospital;
  • people being looked after permanently in care homes;
  • people staying in certain hostels or night shelters;
  • care workers working for low pay, usually for charities;
  • people who care for someone with a disability who is not their husband or wife, partner or a child under 18;
  • members of visiting forces and certain international institutions;
  • foreign diplomats;
  • members of religious communities (monks and nuns);
  • people in detention; and
  • the severely mentally impaired. 

 

If someone else over 18 moves into the property please contact us straight away as this may affect the discount.  If you falsely claim the discount it is a criminal offence and you may have to go to court and be fined.

 

What is Council Tax Reduction Scheme?


Council Tax Reduction Scheme is a reduction on your council tax bill if you are on a low income. More information is available.

 

Important - changes to council tax for 2014/15


From 2014/15 properties that are uninhabitable or are currently unoccupied and unfurnished no longer qualify to receive any discounts on Council Tax bills. An empty premium on vacant properties has been introduced, meaning owners of properties left empty for two years or more will pay their Council Tax bill plus an additional premium of 50%. A discount for annexes is also available. View the leaflet for more information about these changes.

 

Conditions apply to each category noted above. For further information please download the Council Tax Discount Explanatory Leaflet or the appropriate application form .
