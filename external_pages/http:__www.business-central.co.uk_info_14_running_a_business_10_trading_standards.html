Trading Standards

It is our role to ensure that businesses trade safely and fairly

We have joined forces with the Environmental Health and the Licensing teams to form Worcestershire Regulatory Services (WRS), a service to advise and inspect on Food Safety, Food Standards, Trading Standards and Health & Safety at Work.

Contact us

To contact the WRS team, call 01905 822799 or email the team at wrsenquiries@worcsregservices.gov.uk or go to the WRS website.

Food Safety and Food Standards

For information on your legal obligations, go to the Food Standards Agency.

National Food Hygiene Rating scheme

Go to the Food Standards Agency FHRS

Health & Safety At Work

  • To find out how the legislation affects you as a business owner and/or employer, go to the HSE (Health & Safety Executive)
  • For health and safety advice regarding inspections, accident investigations, demolition and dangerous structures, go to your district council
  • If you're an employer hoping to boost productivity by improving the health and wellbeing of staff, go to Worcestershire Works Well

Business Companion - A One-Stop Shop for Regulations

For more advice on Food Safety, Food Standards, Health and Safety, and Trading Standards, go to Business Companion
