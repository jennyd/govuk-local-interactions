Car parks - locations, number of spaces and prices

Short stay car parks

View short stay car parks on a map

  • Church Walk - LE10 1DW. There are 82 standard bays, seven disabled bays, one motorcycle bay and three reserved bays
  • Mansion Street - LE10 0AU. There are 17 standard bays
  • Rear of Castle Street, off Stockwell Head - LE10 1RE. There are 23 standard bays and 10 reserved bays
  • St Mary's Road - LE10 1EQ. There are 73 standard bays, six disabled bays, one motorcycle bay and three reserved bays
  • Stockwell Head - LE10 1RE. There are 42 standard bays and one motorcycle bay
  • Trinity Lane East - LE10 0BL. There are 45 standard bays, one small bay and two disabled bays
  • Trinity Vicarage Road - LE10 0BU. There are 88 standard bays and four disabled bays

Reserved bays are by pre arrangement with the Borough Council. These are not for use by season ticket holders.

Current car parking charges

  • One hour: 50p
  • Up to two hours: £1
  • Up to three hours: £1.50
  • Up to four hours: £2.60
  • Over four hours: £4.80

Parking charges apply from 8am until 5.30pm Monday to Saturday. Access to car parks is available at all times.
