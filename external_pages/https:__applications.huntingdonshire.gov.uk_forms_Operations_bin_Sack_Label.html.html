Additional Domestic Waste

Each household is entitled to one domestic waste bin. However, you may be entitled to an additional bin in some circumstances, such as:

  • there are six or more permanent residents in the household, and excessive waste cannot be recycled
  • there are five permanent residents in the household including children in full time nappies
  • there are four permanent residents in the household with more than one child in full time nappies
  • a resident within the household has any special circumstances creating an unusual amount of waste to be produced on a regular basis
  • any resident who can prove that after using all possible measures (recycling and composting as much as possible, determined by an officer carrying out a waste audit), they still have more waste than can be contained in a standard 240ltr wheeled bin.






***Please do not continue with this form. Instead you need to complete this application form so we can assess your eligibility for an additional domestic waste bin.***
