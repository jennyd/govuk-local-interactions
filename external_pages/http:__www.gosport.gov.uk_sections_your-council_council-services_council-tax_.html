Council Tax

  • Your guide to your council tax 2015/16 leaflet [pdf / 2.06MB] PDF

Gosport Borough Council is responsible for collecting the Council Tax. The main portion of this is distributed to Hampshire County Council, Hampshire Police and Crime Commissioner and Hampshire Fire and Rescue Authority, the remainder is kept by Gosport. This money helps to pay for the services provided throughout the Borough.

 

You can pay your Council Tax online by clicking here, a Change of Address form for Council Tax is available, this needs to printed, signed and returned to the supplied address. A Direct Debit form is also available here.

 

There is a charge for each domestic property based on its Valuation band. The person liable to pay is usually the owner or tenant.

 

The Valuation Office Agency (VOA) have an online facility to find out which Council Tax Band your property is in - www.voa.gov.uk/council_tax
