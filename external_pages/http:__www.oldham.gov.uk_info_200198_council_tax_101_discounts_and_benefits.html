Council Tax discounts

You may qualify for a Council Tax discount. For example, if you are on a low income, if only one adult lives in your home, or if you're disabled.

  • Carers
  • Disabled people
  • Low income - This is called Council Tax Reduction
  • Prisoners
  • Property in need of major repairs
  • Religious community members (such as monks or nuns)
  • Residents in care homes, certain hostels or long term hospital patients
  • Second homes
  • Severely mentally impaired people
  • Single people (adults living alone)
  • Students
  • Visiting forces or international headquarters and defence organisation members
  • Young people
