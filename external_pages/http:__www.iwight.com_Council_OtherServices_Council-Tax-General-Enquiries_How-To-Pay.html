How To Pay

12 Month Instalment Plan

From April 2014 you can pay your Council Tax over 12 monthly instalments from April - March inclusive, instead of the current 10 monthly payments.  To request this option and complete the form please click on the following link 12 month instalment form.

Any delay means your request may not allow sufficient time to start the new instalment plan in time for your annual bill for 2014/15.

The annual council tax bill that we send you will show you how much Council Tax you have to pay and will also provide you with your account reference. All council tax payments should be received on or before the date they are due.

Direct Debit Payments

Direct Debit is the easiest way to pay your Council Tax. We offer a choice of payment dates – 1st, 10th or 20th of the month or ½ yearly (2 instalments 1st April & 1st September).

  • Reasons to change to Direct Debit
  • It is a more convenient way to pay.
  • It helps you budget for the future.
  • It saves you money on postage and stationery.
  • It avoids travelling expenses and queuing.
  • You do not have to complete a new form every year, unless you change Bank or Building 
  • Society or move house.
  • You will be notified at least ten working days before an amount is debited.
  • You can cancel a Direct Debit at any time.

All payments are governed by the Direct Debit Guarantee.

Direct Debit Payments

You can download a Direct Debit instruction form here (PDF 63.75KB, 1 page) to complete and post to us. You can also telephone us to set up a Direct Debit, our contact details can be found in the 'Contact Information' tab.

Internet Payments

You can pay via the internet with your Debit Card. Please access: Online Services - Pay Online by clicking here to utilise the 24-hour payment facility.

Telephone Payments

You can pay by Debit Card when using the 24 Hour Automated Telephone Payment system.
Please call 0845 045 0076 to access the 24 Hour Automated Telephone Payment system.
You will need to quote your Account Reference and the amount you wish to pay. Your account reference number can be found on your council tax bill.

Online Banking

You can pay your Council Tax over the Internet, you will need the Council’s Bank Details: Sort Code 54-10-34, Account Number 47672552 and to quote your account reference number. Your account reference number can be found on your council tax bill.

By AllPay 

Download the App from App Store /Google play using an Apple or Android Smartphone wherever there is a mobile signal.  Use the 19 digit payment reference number from the bar code on the front of your council tax bill to pay your instalments.

You can pay in cash only at any PayPoint retailer. You must take your bill or payment card with you when you go to pay. To request a payment card click here

If you have been issued with an Allpay payment card you can now make your payment to the council direct from your mobile telephone. All you need is a registered mobile phone and a debit card, then please log on to www.allpayments.net/textpay/login.aspx

Post Office

You can use your payment card at any Post Office free of charge by cash or debit card.
