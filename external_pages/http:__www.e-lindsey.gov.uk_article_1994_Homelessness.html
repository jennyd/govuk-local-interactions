Homelessness

If you are faced with homelessness it is important to contact us as we may be able to prevent you from losing your home or find you alternative accommodation.

If you have nowhere to live or are faced with losing your home please contact us immediately as we may be able to prevent you from losing your home or find you somewhere else to live.

If we are unable to help you keep your home or find alternative accommodation, a homelessness interview will be carried out. This will help us assess your individual circumstances and needs and establish how we can help you further.

If you become homeless outside of office hours please contact us on the Emergency Helpline 07766 776447 (5pm to 9am Mon to Thurs and 4:45pm Friday to 9am Monday)
