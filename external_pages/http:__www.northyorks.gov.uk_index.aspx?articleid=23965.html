Supermobile library

Our supermobile library carries around 3,000 items of stock, including books, DVDs and audiobooks.

Disabled access is provided and access to the internet is available via our satellite link. The supermobile calls at 21 locations on a rolling fortnightly timetable (see below) and stays for two hours in each location.

Consultation on changes to North Yorkshire's library services

We recently consulted on proposals to change how library services are run across North Yorkshire. The consultation has now closed but the information is still available for reference purposes.

You can also read a  Supermobile library information sheet [102kb] which gives key facts and figures about this library.

Joining the supermobile library

You can join the library service by visiting the supermobile at your nearest stop.

Borrowing items

The supermobile provides access to the full range of materials held by the library service. You may request items from the supermobile as you would from a library. If you miss a supermobile visit and you need to return borrowed items, you can return them on its next visit. You can also return items to any library. There are also other ways of renewing your books and other items, including online and over the telephone. For more details, see the loan renewals page. Charges do apply on the supermobile.
