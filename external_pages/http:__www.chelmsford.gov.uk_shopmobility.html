Shopmobility

     

    What is Chelmsford Shopmobility?

    Chelmsford Shopmobility is a free service, provided by us, which helps anyone with a mobility problem to visit Chelmsford. The problem can be temporary or permanent, and the visit can be for any purpose, from conducting business, enjoying Chelmsford’s visitor and cultural attractions, or shopping.

     

    Where is it?

    Chelmsford Shopmobility is in Market Road, Chelmsford. This provides easy access to all the city centre has to offer including the Cathedral, a great variety of shops, our lively retail market and other tourist and leisure attractions.

     

    For access information, please visit the link under the "External Links" section of this page.

     

    How much is it?

    Chelmsford Shopmobility is free, but you do need to apply for membership to the scheme. If you would like to apply, or want more information about the scheme, please call 01245 615800 or email shopmobility@chelmsford.gov.uk. You can also write to:

     

    Chelmsford Shopmobility
