Looking to rent a garage in Norwich?

The council has garages located throughout Norwich and a small number of lockable parking bays.

Anyone can apply to rent a council garage or parking space with a lockable post. Please note that you may only apply for those currently being advertised and applications must be received by the deadline date quoted. No other applications will be considered.

Advertised garages to rent

  • Go to mynorwich to see garages to rent nearest to where you live or

  • See the full list of garages and parking bays available to rent  (1.2 Mb pdf)

Garages are to be used to park motorised vehicles in only. They are not let as storage or workshop facilities.

Please note that you will be required to provide your driving license, as well as proof that you have a vehicle to park in the garage (your V5 registration document), if your application is successful.

Garages are allocated according to the following order of priorities:

  • Blue (disabled) badge holders.
  • Norwich City Council tenants.
  • Norwich residents who pay council tax to Norwich City Council.
  • Non-city residents and commercial users.

Click here for a guide to council garages and car parking. (51 Kb pdf) 

Rent prices

This varies depending on whether you are a council tenant, Norwich resident or a non-city resident and whether the garage is in a high, medium or low demand area.

Monthly charges 

Garage/parking bay Norwich City Council tenants Norwich residents Non-city residents
High demand garage £33.50* £57.55 £93.80
Normal demand garage £33.50* £52.30 £93.80
Low demand garage £24.13* £32.45 £36.20
Parking bay £17.25* £41.85 £52.30

*Please note that for council tenants, only the first two tenancies will be charged at the lower rate. Any extra garages or parking bays, from the third onwards, will be charged at the “Norwich resident” rate.

How to apply

Click on the link below to see all garages and parking bays currently available to rent. The list is updated every two weeks.

See the full list of garages and parking bays available to rent (1.2 Mb pdf)

You can apply for any of these but they will be allocated in accordance with the priorities list shown above. Where more than one person qualifies for a garage or parking bay, it will be allocated to the applicant who lives closest.

If you would like to apply for a garage or parking bay complete the application form from the link below and then email it to us as an attachment to garages@norwich.gov.uk.

Please note that you can only apply for a garage or parking bay that is currently being advertised.

Application form (300 Kb pdf)

You can also request a form by calling 0344 980 3333 or pick one up at City Hall and send it to Garages, Norwich City Council, City Hall, Norwich, NR2 1NH.

You will need to apply by the deadline shown on the top of the list.

What happens with your application

If you are successful in your application we will contact you on the telephone number or email address you have provided on the form.

You will need to visit City Hall by the deadline date given to sign your tenancy agreement and direct debit form, and collect your keys. Please remember to bring with you your driving license and your V5 as proof of vehicle ownership.

If we do not hear from you we will assume that you no longer wish us to proceed with your application and the garage/parking post will be offered to the next eligible applicant.

How to pay

Rents must be paid monthly by direct debit.

Click here for a direct debit form (43.4 Kb pdf)

General information: 

  • Tenancies run from Monday to Sunday.
  • Garages are all inspected and considered suitable for letting at the time they are advertised. Tenants are encouraged to report any issues or concerns immediately, or no later than one week from the date the tenancy begins.
  • You are supplied with keys to the garage or parking bay. If you choose to replace a lock or padlock, this will become the property of the council when you give up the tenancy and should remain in place.

Ending your tenancy

If you want to give up your garage or lockable parking bay you will need to give us notice. Please complete the form below, which also includes our terms and conditions.

Notice of ending a garage/lockable parking bay tenancy (373 Kb pdf)

You can either print the form and send it to the address on the form or complete it online and email it as an attachment to garages@norwich.gov.uk
