Have your say

Canterbury City Council is committed to delivering excellent customer service. In order to achieve this we need to listen to the views of our customers and understand if we have met their expectations or if there are ways we can improve our services.

The links below set out the various ways you can give us your views on council services. Thank you in advance for taking the time to give us your feedback.

Make a complaint

Make a complaint to the council

Consultations

Have your say on current proposed changes to the way your services are run.

Speaking at public meetings

Attend and speak at council meetings.

Customer satisfaction survey

Provide feedback on a service you received from the council

Petitions

We welcome petitions as one way in which you can let your concerns be known.

Contact your councillor

Find your ward councillor.
