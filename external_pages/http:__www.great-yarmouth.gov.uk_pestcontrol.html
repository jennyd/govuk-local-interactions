Pest control

GYB Services, our operational partner, offers some services to control pests in domestic premises.

Collapse allReveal all

Can you help me get rid of rats or mice in home?

GYB Services will visit your home and assess the size of your rodent problem. For £35 they will visit up to three times, provide poison and advice on any structural amendments that could help.

Fill in our Pest Control Form to arrange this service.

If a survey shows no treatment is needed you will be charged a £25 cancellation fee.

Can you help with insect infestations?

GYB Services provide pest control services to clear homes of:

  • Wasps
  • Hornets
  • Carpet & Furniture Moths & Beetles
  • Fleas
  • Woodworm

If you have an infestation of brown-tailed moths you should follow the advice in our  information sheet [136kb].

Can you help with a bee swarm or nest?

Honey bees are protected. If you have a swarm or nest you can contact Norfolk Bee Keepers Association to arrange for collection.

To arrange for the removal of bumble or tree bees' nests phone or email GYB Services on 01493 742200 or gybs.group@ncsgrp.co.uk.

If you are unsure what type of bee you have, you should refer to our  bee identification guide [1Mb].

What can be done about problem seagulls and pigeons?

If you are having problems with gulls or pigeons you should make your property unwelcome to them, by:

  • Restricting their access to food
  • Bird-proofing the building

It is a criminal offence to kill wild birds without a licence. Licences can only be obtained for certain species under certain circumstances.

Other pests

If you are having problems with bats, red squirrels and snakes you can get information on gov.uk.

The Fox Project offers advice if you are having problems with foxes.

Contact DEFRA Wildlife Administration Unit on 0845 6014523 for help with grey squirrels.

GYB Services offer commercial customers a full pest control service. Please complete the Pest Control Form for a no-obligation quotation.
