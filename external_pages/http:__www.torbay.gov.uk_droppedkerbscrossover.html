Dropped Kerbs and Crossovers

With increased problems of parking, many residents now wish to park their vehicles off the highway and within their own property. To do this you require the construction of a vehicle crossing to allow you to cross over the pavement.
For the Council to allow you to have a vehicle crossing and park on your property the following requirements must be met:
  1. The consent of Torbay Council Highways and Engineering Department must be obtained prior to the construction of a vehicle crossing; this is processed through a licence. No works are permitted on the Highway without a relevant licence.
  2. Torbay Council’s Planning Department must be consulted, and planning permission obtained if required, for any works to be undertaken within the boundary of the property to be served by the footway crossing. If the property was at any time Council owned, then the Riviera Housing Trust must be consulted.  Then the Sanctuary Housing (formerly Riviera Housing Trust) Must be contacted.
  3. There must be an area of at least 6.0 x 3.2m available for parking off the Public Highway.
If you require advice on the above requirements please contact 01803 701310
Once you have gained all necessary consents you will require a suitably qualified contractor to carry out the works. It is recommended that your nominated contractor obtain the required licence to construct the footway crossing on your behalf. Torbay Council’s Direct Services and Waste division can provide this service and the number to contact for a quotation is listed below. Alternatively, any private contractor can be used provided Torbay Council has assurance in writing before work starts that the property owner and the contractor will comply with conditions outlined below: -
For quotes from Direct Services and Waste on 01803 701310
The Highways and Engineering department must receive the licence at least 7 days before works commence on site.
Every licence must be completed in full with payment and all relevant and current details supplied, licences not meeting these criteria will not be authorised and immediately returned
