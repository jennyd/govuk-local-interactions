Allotments

Allotment Sites - Leek Town Council

Leek Town Council is responsible for seven sites that consist of 260 full plots and 40 half-plots.

The main sites are situated at:-

  • Cheadle Road
  • Beggars Lane
  • Peggs Field
  • Parrs Field
  • Brow Hill/Badnall Street
  • and two sites at the Crescent

If you are a resident of Leek you qualify for an allotment, but due to the current popularity of allotments, there is a waiting list for all sites.

If you would like to be added to the waiting list, please contact the Leek Town Clerk.

Mrs. Julie Taylor
15 Stockwell Street
Leek
ST13 6DH

Tel: 01538 381271 or email: julie.taylor@leektowncouncil.co.uk

The Leek Allotment Holders and Gardeners Association meet regularly at the Blue Mugge in Leek and provide help and advice on maintaining an allotment. Contact Gaynor Tatton at: leekallotments@gmail.com
