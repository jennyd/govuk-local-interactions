Book a Computer

To book a computer you will need your library card and PIN. You can book online, at a library or by phone on 020 8303 7777 (option 4). If you are a visitor to the area, it may be possible to use a computer without a library card. Please ask library staff for more information.

All of our libraries offer free wireless internet access. Sidcup Library and Welling Library have coverage in all public areas, although at Welling Library you can't plug your laptop into the power supply and can only use it on battery power.

Central Library has two WiFi zones available, one on the first floor and one on the ground floor. In the first floor WiFi/Study Zone you can plug your laptop in to the surge protected mains sockets. If you need help in connecting to the internet please ask library staff for an instruction leaflet or download it here.

Adults owing more than £10 in library charges will not be able to book a computer. If you owe more than £10 on your library card, you will need to make a minimum payment to library staff of 50p for 30 minutes use until the debt is paid off or reduced to below £10.
