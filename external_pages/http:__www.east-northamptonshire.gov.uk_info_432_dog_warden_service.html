Dog warden service

The role of our dog warden service is to enforce dog control order legislation and promote responsible dog ownership.

The council not only deals with complaints but can provide information on a range of issues including barking dogs, stray dogs, dangerous dogs, dog fouling and the enforcement of dog control orders.

Please use the dog waste bins that have been provided. You can be issued with a Fixed Penalty Notice of £75 if you do not clean up after your dog.
