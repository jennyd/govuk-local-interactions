Pay your council tax with a credit or debit card

View live account information and register for e-billing

Rugby Borough Council keeps less than ten pence in every pound of council tax collected. The rest goes to Warwickshire County Council to pay for expensive services such as highways and social care, to Warwickshire Police, and to your parish council or town area special expense.
