Council Tax Exemptions

If your property falls into one of the following categories you may receive a full exemption from Council Tax. Some exemptions require you to complete an application form, in which case please click on one of the links below where provided:

  • Class B, Charity Owned

    An unoccupied property (owned by a charity) which has been unoccupied for less than 6 months and was last occupied by a charity.

  • Class D, Detained

    An unoccupied property where the owner or tenant is detained in a prison, hospital or other place of detention.

  • Class F, Deceased

    A property left unoccupied by a deceased person where no grant of probate or letters of administration have been made, or less than 6 months have elapsed since such a grant was made.

  • Class G, Prohibited by Law

    An unoccupied property where occupation is prohibited by law.

  • Class H, Vicarages

    An unoccupied property which is held for being available for a minister of religion as a residence from which to perform their duty.

  • Class I, Receiving Care Elsewhere

    An unoccupied property where the taxpayer now has his/her main home in another place where he/she receives personal care for old age, disablement, illness, mental disorder or alcohol/drugs dependence.

  • Class J, Providing Care Elsewhere

    An unoccupied property where the taxpayer now has his/her main home in another place for the purpose of caring for a person who requires such care because of old age, disablement, illness, mental disorder or alcohol/drugs dependence.

  • Class K, Student Owned

    An unoccupied property owned by a qualifying student.

  • Class L, Repossessed

    An unoccupied property which has been repossessed by the mortgage lending company.

  • Class N, Students Residence

    A property which is occupied by students, school or college leavers only. Please complete the form for Full Time Students.

  • Class O, Ministry of Defence

    A property which is occupied and consists of armed forces barracks, messes or married quarters owned by the Ministry of Defence.

  • Class P, Visiting Forces Residence

    A property which is occupied by a person who is a member of a visiting force.

  • Class R, Pitch or Mooring

    A dwelling consisting of a pitch or a mooring which is not occupied by a caravan or a boat.

  • Class S, Under 18 Residence

    A property which is occupied solely by a person or people under the age of 18.

  • Class U, Severely Mentally Impaired

    A property which is occupied only by people who are severely mentally impaired and would normally be the liable person for Council Tax. Pleae complete the form for Severely Mentally Impaired.

  • Class V, Diplomat

    A property which is occupied by a diplomat or a member of an international organisation headquartered in the UK.

  • Class W, Occupied Granny Annex

    An annex or similar self- contained part of a property, occupied by an elderly or disabled relative of the residents living in the rest of it, and subject to a planning condition. Please complete the form for a Granny Annexe Form.
