Grassroots Community Allotment on the Rochdale Canal

The Grassroots allotment is a place for people with a disability to engage in horticulture and animal welfare, and creates a relaxed and secure atmosphere to socialise and learn (the allotment is also open to the wider community).

Come along and check out the community room, large fruit orchard, vegetable growing areas, flower beds, chickens, ducks and bees!

Find out what's on, and where to find the allotment, on the Grassroots website.
