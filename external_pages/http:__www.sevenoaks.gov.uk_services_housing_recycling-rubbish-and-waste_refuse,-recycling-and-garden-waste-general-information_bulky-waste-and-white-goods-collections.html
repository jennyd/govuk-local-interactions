Bulky waste and white goods collections

Our fortnightly bulky waste collection service can dispose of large household items like furniture, beds and mattresses.

We also collect large white goods, including cookers, hobs, ovens, dishwashers, freezers, fridges, fridge-freezers, tumble dryers and washing machines.

These items are taken to Kent County Council for recycling and disposal.

Generally white goods can be collected for £17 per appliance. Large double-door or American-style fridge-freezers are charged at £40 per appliance.

This service is only available to households. Please note that we cannot enter your home or collect from your rear garden. All items must be out at the property boundary by 7am on the collection day.

For additional prices and more information download our bulky waste guidance.

To book a collection, please call us on 01732 227000.

Alternatively, you can donate your good reusable item to a charity.
