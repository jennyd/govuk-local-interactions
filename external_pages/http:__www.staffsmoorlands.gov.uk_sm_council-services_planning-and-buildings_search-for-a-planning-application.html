Search for a planning application here

Supporting, objecting or commenting on applications via the search facility

If you comment on an application, please note a scanned copy of your comments will be available to the public, including the applicant, as it is placed on the website with the other documents relating to this planning application. As your proximity to the proposal is a consideration you should include the full postal address of your property. If you do not do so, the Council will not be able to inform you of appeals or other applications. Please note that for transparency, your name and address are likely to appear on the website. We will try to remove signatures, phone numbers and email addresses, however we cannot guarantee to do so on every occasion.

Search for applications using an interactive map

Launch the map to check the status of planning applications in your local area, and to view the planning history of selected buildings. You can also find out if your property is in a conservation area, close to protected trees, or at risk of flooding.
