Pay your Council Tax

  1. Pay online (You are here)
  2. Pay by direct debit

Pay online

Before you pay online, you will need your debit or credit card and your Council Tax reference number.

Please note, a processing fee of 1.75 percent applies to all credit card payments. There is no charge for debit card payments.

Pay your Council Tax

Paying like this keeps your costs down, but it means you have to remember when to pay.

If you register for 'Your Account' you can set up a direct debit. There's no need to remember when to pay and you can spread your payments over the year. You can also use 'Your Account' to access other Council services.
