Report problems with roads

To report any problems with roads in Gwynedd, contact us by calling  01766 771000 or use one of our online forms.

 

For out of hours emergencies only, please use the following telephone numbers:

  • Problems in the Arfon area: (01286) 677067

  • Problems in the Dwyfor area: (01766) 810382

  • Problems in the Meirionnydd area: (01341) 422592
