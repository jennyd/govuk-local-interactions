Pay a parking fine

You will need your reference number (also known as PCN). You don’t need to enter your vehicle registration.

Pay parking fine online

Please note you can't challenge a parking fine once you have paid it.

You can't pay online if:

  • you have received a bailiff’s letter – please contact the bailiff directly
  • you are trying to pay part of the full debt - payment online must be the full value of the Penalty Charge Notice.

Cards we accept

We can accept payments made with the following debit or credit cards:

  • Visa
  • Visa Delta (Debit)
  • Visa Electron
  • Mastercard
  • Domestic Maestro
  • Solo
  • UK Debit Mastercard.

We do not accept American Express or Diners card.
