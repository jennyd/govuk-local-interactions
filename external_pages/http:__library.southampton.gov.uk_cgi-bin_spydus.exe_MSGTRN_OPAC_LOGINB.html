Login

Welcome to Southampton City Libraries online services.

To search our catalogues please select a link on the left.
To check your account, renew items or access our digital resources please type in your membership number (found on your library card or bus pass) and your personal PIN.
Not a member, click here to join now.

The library catalogue will remember your login information for this session only. To protect your privacy please make sure you Logout when you have finished using the library catalogue.

Tip: Your Borrower ID is the barcode from your library card, or your Smartcities account number, or your Southampton University ID card number preceded by a U.
