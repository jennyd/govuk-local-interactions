Housing Needs

Housing needs, option and advice

We can help you with general enquiries, try to to prevent homelessness where possible, or provide options for rehousing.

Before you look for alternative accommodation, you might want to try:

  • Local Authority Mortgage Scheme (LAMS)
  • Help to buy

Preventing Repossession

  • Mortgage Rescue Scheme
  • What if I have to go to Court?
  • Support for Mortgage Interest
  • Repossession Prevention Scheme

The Gov.uk website has advice on dealing with debt problems and preventing repossessions.

If you're living in private rented accommodation our Housing Standards section may be able to help in resolving notices to leave, disrepair and illegal eviction issues.

If you feel that you require some support/advice regarding domestic violence, there are a number of agencies who are able to help. 

Pendle Domestic Violence Initiative are a charity set up to offer advice and assistance so that you are aware of all your legal options. Pendle Domestic Violence Initiative or Housing Needs may also be able to help you access a refuge should you require it.

Where it is not possible to prevent homelessness, there are a number of options for rehousing and support in and around Pendle:  

  • Applying for a Housing Association Property through B-With-Us
  • Supported Housing
  • Housing advice for older people
  • Private Rented Sector Housing
  • Bond Guarantee Scheme
  • Homelessness
  • Advice Agencies in and around Pendle
  • Calico
