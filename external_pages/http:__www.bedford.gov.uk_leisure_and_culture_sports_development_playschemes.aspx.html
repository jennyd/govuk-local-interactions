2015

Play & Games Schemes 31st July to 21st August 2015 

These schemes offer a wide range of sports, arts, crafts, games, trips, competitions, free play and much, much more!

 

CLICK HERE TO VIEW OUR NEW SUMMER PROGRAMME

Registration

A parent/guardian/carer will need to complete a registration form for each child before they can join the schemes. We recommend that you download this by clicking HERE. Complete this form and bring it with you to the first sessions. 

If you are unable to download this form, some will be available at each site so that you can complete one per child at the first session.

 

Cost

There is a charge of £2 per child per session payable at the door.

You cannot pre-book a place, we work on a first come, first served basis and attendance in the morning doesn't guarantee your child a place in the afternoon.
