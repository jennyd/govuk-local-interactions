Household Waste (Refuse) Collection

SMS Text Reminders

Receive SMS text reminders the day before your recycling collection or for notifications of changes to your refuse or recycling collection day

 

Household waste collection

All bagged household waste must only be placed out for collection on the designated collection day and left on the kerbside at the front of the property or we will assume you do not need a collection.

Residents who are unable to comply, can apply for an assisted service. The criteria for claiming is as follows: Housebound and/or elderly or infirm which prevents you from placing your refuse and recyclables on the kerbside and there is no other able bodied person living at home with you.

Please complete our Environment - Assisted Household Waste Service on line form to apply, stating the reason why you require assisted service and where you wish to place your bin i.e. front of property, side of property etc. Once your application is received it may be necessary for an officer from the Council to visit you to ensure the bins are accessible for collection.

All domestic household properties in the Brentwood area receive a weekly refuse pick up. To find your Refuse Collection Day please use the refuse, food waste and recycling collection day finder

 

Where to place your refuse for collection

Your household waste needs to be placed out for collection on the specified day. 

 

Refuse must be available from 7.30am (collection times may vary) DO NOT place refuse sacks on the kerb side prior to your collection day due to animals scavenging the black sacks and causing litter issues.

Refuse must be left on the kerbside at the front of your property or we will assume you do not need a collection..

Wheeled Bins: Residents that choose to present their general waste in a wheeled bin must use standard size refuse sacks and not for example carrier bags or wheeled bin liners.

Wheeled bins will not as a rule be mechanically tipped, however, it may be necessary for the bin to be tipped on occasions due to unforeseen circumstances. If this is necessary Brentwood Borough Council shall not be liable for any loss or damage as a result of tipping your wheeled bin.

Dry co-mingled recyclables or mixed glass presented in wheeled bins will not be collected.

No change of service for residents with ‘permission to tip' stickers previously issued by Brentwood Council

NB – Permission to tip stickers are no longer available.

Recyclable items

Please Note: Food Waste must be presented in the external caddy provided, Glass must be presented in the red box provided, other dry recyclables must be presented in the orange sack provided and garden material must be presented in the compostable sacks provided or in the Council's leased garden waste container.

 

Larger items

Larger household items can be collected for a small charge. To arrange for a bulky household collection online, please go to the The Bulky Household Waste Collection page. 

For further information, please call 01277 312500 or if items can be reused then please contact the Lighthouse Furniture Project for information 01277 222050

 

Christmas tree recycling

For information about recycling Christmas trees, please see the How to do I recycle...? page.
