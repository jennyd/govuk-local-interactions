Hazardous waste

Residents can dispose of:

  • weed killer

  • bleach

  • garden fertilizer

  • other small household and garden chemicals

  • gas bottles - enter through gates 1 or 2, pull up at the public unloading area,  tell the site attendant that you want to dispose of gas bottles and they will then take it to a safe storage area on site

Trade can dispose of:

  • fridge/freezers

  • televisions

  • monitors

  • plasterboard/gypsum

  • gas bottles - enter through gate 3 and report to the weighbridge and, if accepted, you will be charged the full trade waste rate.

We recommend you contact the gas bottle manufacturers directly or a specialised disposal company to dispose of your bottles.

We reserve the right to turn away traders who try to dispose of commercial gas bottles.

We do not accept

  • industrial chemical waste

  • large volumes of hazardous trade

  • tyres

  • asbestos

  • large amounts of gloss paint (very inflammable)

  • accelerants

You will need to contact a registered hazardous waste carrier and make arrangements for the correct, safe disposal of your waste.

Disposal of other hazardous materials

Residents can have asbestos, paint and other hazardous chemicals collected by the City of London Hazardous waste collection service. Make sure you double wrap these items.

Call 020 7332 3433

Need more information? Call the Environment Agency on 0845 9333 111.

Asbestos guidance

Do not to touch anything that may contain asbestos.

Please contact a specialist contractor to have it safely removed.

However, if you choose to dismantle cement bonded asbestos yourself, you can either arrange for this to be collected or take it to a site licensed to accept it.

Nearest sites

West London Waste Site

This is in Brent, Abbey Road, London NW10 7TJ

  • asbestos needs to be double wrapped and sealed in heavy duty plastic

  • you will need to show two items with proof of address in the borough for access

  • contact the site in advance on 020 8965 5497 to ensure sufficient capacity is available

  • there may be a charge depending on the amount you want to dispose

The City of London Hazardous Waste collection service will collect asbestos on our behalf

To arrange collection by call 020 7332 3433 or complete their online form

A list of authorised contractors is available from ARCA - Asbestos Removal Contractors Association.
