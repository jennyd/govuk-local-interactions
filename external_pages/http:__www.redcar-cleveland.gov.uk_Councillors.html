Contacting your Councillor:
The Councillors details include telephone numbers and e-mail addresses where possible. You can also write to your Councillor at Redcar & Cleveland Leisure and Community Heart, Ridley Street, Redcar TS10 1TD.


Councillors Code of Conduct:
The Members Code of Conduct forms part of the Council Constitution (page 228) which is available to download in the Council Documents section. Alternatively you can download the relevant extract from this link: Members Code of Conduct.


Local Code of Conduct Complaints Process:

If you believe that a Councillor has breached the Code of Conduct you should let us know by completing the attached form, which you can download from the link below. This form can also be obtained from Governance Services, Redcar & Cleveland Leisure and Community Heart, Ridley Street, Redcar TS10 1TD or emailing it to corporate_complaints@redcar-cleveland.gov.uk
