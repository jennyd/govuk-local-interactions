Secondary school admissions

To be able to apply for a place in a Bury School

  • You must be resident in Bury. If you live outside Bury, you must apply through your home authority.
  • This is for children due to transfer to secondary school in September 2016 and born between 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005.
     

Important dates to note:

  • September 2015 - Information on how to apply is sent to parents.
  • 31 October 2015 - Deadline for applications. Applications received after 31 October 2015 will be treated as late applications.
  • 1 March 2016 - Emails will be sent after 2pm. for those that applied online. Letters will be posted for those that have applied by application on 1 March 2016.

Transfer to secondary school place application 

The online system is available during the application period each year for children in Year 6 children transferring from primary to secondary school.

What you need to know before applying

Read through the Transfer to Secondary School Information Guide 2016 from the downloads below which guides you through the admissions process and should give you all the information you need.

Have a read through our frequently asked questions for information on the following topics:

  • How do I know when to apply
  • Criteria for offering school places
  • Applying for a school place
  • Offer of a place

Once you've decided which 3 schools you wish to show preferences for, you can apply online

Important Information

These pages are intended as a guide to the policies and procedures of the Local Authority (LA). Please note that:

  • Bury Council does not fund places in independent schools.
  • Details of secondary schools in Bury can be found from the Secondary Schools list.
  • Certain schools need additional information to apply their admissions criteria and may require you to also complete a Supplementary Information Form from the school's website.
  • Examination results are published in the school's prospectus. The Office for Standards in Education publishes Reports OFSTED - Inspection reports for each school.
  • School achievement and attainment tables for Bury Local Authority are available free from the Department for Education. The Local Authority number is 351. The telephone number for the Department for Education is: 0870-000-2288.
  • Details of  School Performance which can be searched on by school or area from the Gov.uk website.
  • Organising travel to and from school on public transport can be done via the  Transport for Greater Manchester  website.
  • Information on tram services is available on the Metrolink  website or at main Metrolink stations.
  • If your child(ren) need care before and/or after school, you can use the Childcare Search - Bury Family Information Service
  • Links to neighbouring authorities can be found below:  
    • Bolton Council - admissions
    • Lancashire County Council - admissions
    • Salford City Council - school admissions
    • Rochdale City Council - school admissions
