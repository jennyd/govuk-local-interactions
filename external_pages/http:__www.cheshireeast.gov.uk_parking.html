Free After Three

Parking on seven car parks across the Borough is free of charge from 3pm each day. This is to support businesses and encourage more people to visit their local town or village centre.

Free After Three car parks:

  • Whalley Hayes, Macclesfield
  • Oak Street, Crewe (as from 15/09/14)
  • Snow Hill, Nantwich
  • South Street, Alderley Edge
  • Back Park Street, Congleton
  • Spring Street, Wilmslow
  • Princess Street, Knutsford
