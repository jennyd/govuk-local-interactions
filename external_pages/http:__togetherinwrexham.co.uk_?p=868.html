Enabling Grants Applications Open

  • Posted On 18 Jan 2016
  • By webmaster
  • In News

The Together in Wrexham enabling grant scheme (the scheme) was established by the Council as a result of our commitment to support communities to come together to actively participate in delivering services.

The resource available for the scheme is finite, with monies set aside in a reserve. An annual review will determine the total amount available in the scheme fund for the following 12 months.

The panel will meet every three months to consider applications, following the schedule below:

2016
Deadline for applications: Panel will meet during third week of: Applicants notified of outcome during third week of:
Mon 29 February 2016 March April
Tue 31 May 2016 June July
Wed 31 August 2016 September October
Wed 30 November 2016 December January 2017

 

All submissions will be considered on merit and with reference to the criteria specified below.

It is expected that any successful project will:

  • Offer something new. Financial support is not available to fund ‘business as usual’ activities
  • Be able to demonstrate tangible benefits to the wider community
  • Support the volunteer ethos
  • Actively encourage community cohesion
  • Be self- sustainable, i.e. continue without further financial backing, once the grant/loan money is spent

The awarding process will take all five of the above criteria into account in equal measure; these will form the basis of the scoring matrix that will guide both Officers and Members of the Council when deciding upon awards.

All applications must be supported and countersigned by the most appropriate Community Council. Successful applicants must satisfy the Community Council that grant / loan funds have been expensed appropriately.

Awards by Wrexham County Borough Council

Awards from the scheme by the Council can take one of two forms:-

  • A grant up to the value of five thousand pounds (£5,000) OR
  • An interest-free loan to the value of twenty thousand pounds (£20,000) repayable over a period commensurate with the nature of the scheme, and the benefits to be derived from the funding. It is expected that any payback period would not normally exceed five years, however this may not be strictly enforced where it can be evidenced that to do so would render the proposal unfeasible
  • Together in Wrexham can accept only one successful application from any single organisation in any 12 month period

If you need advice on completing the application form, or on any elements of the submission process, please contact together@wrexham.gov.uk.
