School Place Allocation Summaries

 

School place allocation summaries provide information on how places were allocated at each school or academy where it was not possible to offer places to all children who applied.

If the allocation summary for the school you require is not available please check the school website or contact the school direct. If you have been refused a school in another Local Authority area, you will need to contact them direct to request a copy and to discuss the outcome.

If you are looking for a school that is not on the list, this is because the school was not over-subscribed as of national offer day.Â
