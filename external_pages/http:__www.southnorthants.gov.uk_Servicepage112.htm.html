Homelessness

If you are homeless or threatened with homelessness it is important that you contact us as soon as possible. The Housing Options Team is here to provide you with advice and assistance and we may be able to prevent you becoming homeless if you contact us at the earliest opportunity.

In some circumstances we will provide homeless households with temporary accommodation, although usually only when we have a legal obligation to do so. We will always try to prevent homelessness.

  • Contact the Housing Options Team during office hours call 01327 322374 or email us at: housingoptions@southnorthants.gov.uk. At all other times there is an out-of-hours service in operation, telephone 0800 160 1022.
  • If you cant easily access a phone or email, you can call into our offices at The Forum, Moat Lane, Towcester. You can use the free self-service phones to speak with the Housing Options Team duty officer, who will give you advice and information relevant to your situation. The phones are in the reception area, on the desks with the computer screens. You can use the computers free of charge to access the councils information and services.
