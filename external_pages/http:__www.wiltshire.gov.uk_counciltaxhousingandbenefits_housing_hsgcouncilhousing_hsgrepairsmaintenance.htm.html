How to report a repair

Phone us

Telephone: 01722 434724  or 0300 456 0106

Out-of-hours telephone: 01722 434724 or 0300 456 0106

Text us

You can report a non-urgent repair by texting us on 07788 915 718. We will reply to let you know the works order number and target time (normal call charges apply to this mobile number)

Online

You can report a non-urgent repair online using the iHousing portal once you have registered to use this service.

Email us

HousingRepairs@wiltshire.gov.uk (please do not use this email address for reporting any emergency repairs)

Write to us

Housing Management (Repairs),
Wiltshire Council,
P.O. Box 2281,
Salisbury,
Wiltshire,
SP2 2HX.

Letters can also be handed in at the housing reception: The Council House, Bourne Hill, Salisbury, SP1 3UZ

In person

You can report it in person at the housing reception at The Council House, Bourne Hill, Salisbury, SP1 3UZ between 9am and 5pm Monday to Friday.

If you have an urgent problem out of hours you may be asked to make safe the situation until the next day, for example if you have a bad water leak you may be asked to turn off the water until a plumber can get there in the morning.

When reporting a repair it is important that you provide as much information as possible. This will help us to ensure that we arrange for the right person to carry out the repair and to be able to identify the right equipment and parts required for the job. It will also help us to decide how quickly the repair needs to be carried out.

When reporting a repair you will need to provide:

  • Your name and address
  • A contact phone number
  • Access times
  • Details of the repair required

The Housing - repairs report guide for tenants Wiltshire Repairs Reporting Guide 1mb provides further information on repairs. It also provides some handy hints on dealing with common repair problems such as blockages, resetting an electrical trip, condensation and smoke alarms.

Please remember

We will answer your call as soon as we can. However, we are often busier than normal on a Monday morning and Wednesday and Friday afternoons. To avoid waiting, try calling us at our less busy times.

If you are calling about a repair you have already reported, please let us know the works order number.
