Graffiti

    The Council aims to provide a response-based graffiti removal service.

     

    • Graffiti is any writing or drawing scribbled or scratched on a wall etc.
    • We will try to remove all graffiti on street furniture and Council property, i.e. bus shelters.
    • We will assist residents in removing graffiti from their properties. This service is free of charge.

     

    The Council does not remove graffiti from wooden fences as the results are usually poor and make no improvement.


    Damaging property is a criminal offence and is dealt with by the Police.


    If you would like to report graffiti please use the link to the right of this page.


    If you are interested in clearing a small area of graffiti in your neighbourhood yourself, we can loan you a Graffiti Removal Kit by clicking the link to the right of this page.
