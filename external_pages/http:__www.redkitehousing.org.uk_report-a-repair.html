Report a repair

You can report repair issues online – any day, any time!

Simply use the handy pictures and questions to tell us about technical issues - you can even add some pictures of your own to make sure we know exactly what you are referring to. Please note that if you leave the screen for too long, your session will time out.

If you have any questions about an existing repair, go to our contact page to find out how to get in touch with us.

To report an issue with your gas central heating system or servicing, please call 08454 349686.

For emergency repairs only, please call 01494 463690.

If you'd like to get in touch with us via email regarding a repair, you can do so by sending an email to repairs@redkitehousing.org.uk. When you report a repair, please give as much information as possible.
