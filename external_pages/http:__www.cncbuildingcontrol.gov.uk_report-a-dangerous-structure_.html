Dangerous Structures

If a structure poses a potential threat to public safety we can undertake works to protect and keep them safe, if the owners are unable to carry out the work themselves.

Our experienced building surveyors are on call 365 days of the year. We aim to inspect reported dangerous structures within 2 hours of notification if there is a perceived risk to public safety.

If you need to report a dangerous structure between 8am and 5pm please call:

Freephone: 0808 168 5041

Or outside of these hours please call:

South Norfolk and Norwich: 01603 412180

Broadland: 01603 705121

King's Lynn and West Norfolk: 01553 771891

Fenland: 01945 581850

The out of hours numbers go through to a dedicated call centre who will contact the duty surveyor.
