Lighting - street lights

We are responsible for maintaining street lights on public land.

You can report a street light not working to Hounslow Highways using their online form here.

Services offered on council maintained roads

  • The addition or removal of street lights

  • The maintenance of lamp columns, bollards, centre island lamp columns, flashing beacons at zebra crossings, school flashing lights and illuminated sign lights

Street light maintenance frequency

There are eight set night patrol routes. In the summer there is one night patrol (of a certain area of the borough) every week and during the winter there are three patrols every two weeks in different areas of the borough.

Report a street light issue on a housing estate

Report a street light issue directly to the housing services team:

Tel: 0800 085 6575
Email: housing.repairs@hounslowhomes.org.uk  or contact the relevant estate manager.

Privately maintained roads: Contact the contractors, landlord or appropriate housing association with column location.

Repair times

3 working days.

Defective lamp column in an alleyway - 48hr response (not including weekends).

Vandalised column (inspection cover off, lantern damaged) - Attended within the hour and rectified then as long as there is not a cable fault or cut-out damage in the column.

Request the removal of a street light

To request for the removal of a street light, contact us in writing with reasons for the removal.
