North Warwickshire Allotment Federation

The North Warwickshire Allotment Federation was set up in 2008 to support the allotment community in North Warwickshire. The Federation is run by a group of volunteers from different allotment sites across the Borough. The group hold an AGM every January as well as activities and events throughout the year.

North Warwickshire Allotment Federation Website

The Borough Council aims to support the provision of allotment sites through the North Warwickshire Green Space Strategy and has set out a framework to do this in an Allotments Policy Statement.
