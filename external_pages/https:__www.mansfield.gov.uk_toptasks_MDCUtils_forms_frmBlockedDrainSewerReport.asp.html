blocked and defective drain or sewer reporting form

If the drain serving your property is blocked or defective, you can report it to us so we can investigate the cause.

Please note, if the drain or sewer is on private land, the street/highway, or serves multiple properties, you should report the issue directly to Severn Trent on 08007 834444.
