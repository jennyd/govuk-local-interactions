Online

Did you know you can now contact the council all day and all night, all year long, at a time that suits you?

The answer you are looking for is most likely here on our website. Just a few examples are:

  • Pay your Council Tax or rent
  • Report missed bins, fly-tipping, graffiti and more
  • Apply for housing benefit, council tax discounts and much more
  • Join the garden recycling scheme

In order to provide the best services resources allow the council wants you to access our services here online. Did you know that to resolve an query face to face it costs the council approximately £30? It costs £3 to resolve a query over the phone, but only 30p to do so online?

We are always looking to improve information available on our website. Therefore if there is something you cannot find, please contact us.

Telephone

Revenues and Benefits - 01233 330227

Since April 2013 there have been several changes to council tax and the way that the benefits system works. Ashford Borough Council has been as proactive as possible in helping everyone as the changes have been introduced and will continue to work hard to give you the best advice. In light of this the council have introduced a 24 hour interactive telephone service for revenues and benefits enquiries, frequently asked questions and more.

Phone 01233 330227 and when you are asked for the "hot-key" number select from the numbers below:

  1. For payments
  2. For Reminders/Summonses
  3. For all other council tax enquiries
  4. For Housing Benefit enquiries
  5. For all business rate enquiries
  6. Council Contact details
    Alternatively wait for more options

General enquiries - 01233 331111

Gone are the days of needing to wait in a queue, being put on hold or having to comply with opening times. You can now get information even when we are closed! With general enquiries now being dealt with via this method, this means that more staff are free to deal with more complicated enquiries. So don't worry, you will be transferred to a member of our customer service team if you do need to speak to someone.

Telephone 01233 331111 (for Ashford Borough Council enquiries) and take advantage of the options available such as press 1 to make a payment. If you know the name of the officer/department you want to talk to, then you can use our automated switchboard to make contact. The automated switchboard can be accessed through the normal contact number or by dialling 01233 330838 directly.

Face to face

NEW - Seeing us just got easier! Download the Appointment Service Information [pdf] 6MB

Busy schedule and no time to wait in a queue, or you have a complex query? Book an appointment to see us at a time that suits you.

All our face-to-face enquiries are now by appointment only. Monday to Friday from 9am to 3.30pm.

To make your appointment either call us on 01233 331111 or email us at appointments@ashford.gov.uk and one of our Customer Services Advisors will be contact you to arrange an appointment.

Our fast track desk is also available if you simply want documents copied.

Don’t forget most of the information our customer service advisors give you can also be found online on our website.

If your query relates to revenues and benefits, or council tax, you also have the option of using our self-service phone number - 01233 330227.
