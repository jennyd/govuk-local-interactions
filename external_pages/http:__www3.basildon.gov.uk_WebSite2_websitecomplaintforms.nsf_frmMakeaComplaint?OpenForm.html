Complaints Representation

If for any reason you are unable to make your own complaint, somebody else (such as a friend, family member or local political representative) can act on your behalf to do this for you. But, before we can look into your complaint, we will need your written permission confirming that you want them to act on your behalf.

If somebody asks you to make a complaint for them we will have to contact that person to check that they have given permission for you to do this.
