Equipment to help you

Specifically designed equipment for people with a disability, such as raised toilet seats, walking frames and mobile hoists can be requested through the Council health and social care department.

An assessment of need will be undertaken by a member of the Cheshire West and Chester Occupational Therapy Service.

  • Occupational Therapy Service

Equipment can be provided by prescription redeemed at an accredited retailer and will belong to the service user, or larger equipment can be provided on loan from the Community Equipment Service. There is no cost unless you want to upgrade from the equipment normally provided

Help with walking

If you are finding it difficult to walk around inside your home there are a number of walking aids available. For example a walking frame.

If your only difficulty is walking then please ask your GP to refer you to the Physiotherapy Service.

Alternatively you may wish to purchase your own walking equipment.  To receive suitable advice, please contact your local Independent Living Centre to make an appointment.
