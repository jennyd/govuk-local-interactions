Parking permits

In order to keep the city moving and provide access we offer many different types of permits.

Types of permits

  • Resident parking Apply for a resident permit of you live in a controlled parking zone
  • Visitor parking Permits for people who are visiting you
  • Business, trader and essential working parking Permits for businesses, traders and essential services such as gas or electric companies
  • Essential carer parking Permits if you provide care in the community or to a relative or friend
  • Other permits Hotels, schools, window cleaner, electric vehicles, club car permits
  • Match and event day parking Permits for Residents and Businesses in the Coldean or Moulsecoomb areas

To find our more about the zones please see our pages on zone information.

Change of Details

There is a £10 administration fee to change your zone and/or your vehicle for your Parking Permit.

Please let us know as soon as possible if any details for your permit need to be updated.
