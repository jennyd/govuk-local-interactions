Welcome to the Wrexham Libraries' Online Catalogue

We apologise that we have been unable to resolve the problems with the online system and we are unable to offer our customers transactional facilities such as renewals and reservations. We have now purchased a new system and currently working towards implementation. We do not, as yet, have an implementation date but we will keep you informed as work progresses. We apologise for this situation.

In the meantime, please use one of the following methods if you wish to renew books or to reserve items:

  • telephone your local library or Wrexham library on 01978 292090
  • e-mail your local library or library@wrexham.gov.uk

The online catalogue gives you details of the complete stock of Wrexham Library and Information Service. The catalogue will tell you what we have, where it is and whether it is available or out on loan. There are details of books, recorded music, videos, DVDs, software, talking books, printed music etc.

To search the catalogue click on one of the options at the top of the screen. You can search the full catalogue or a particular format, such as books or DVDs.

Anyone can browse the catalogue but to reserve an item that you wish to borrow you must have a current membership with Wrexham Libraries, with a membership number and PIN. If you don't already know your PIN please ask at your local library where staff will be able to advise you.

Joining is easy and free - see our main library site!
