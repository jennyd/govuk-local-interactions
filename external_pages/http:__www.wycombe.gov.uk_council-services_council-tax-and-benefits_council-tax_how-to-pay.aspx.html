How to pay your council tax

Pay online

Pay by direct debit

For your security we will only discuss details about direct debit payments with individual(s) named as liable on your council tax bill.

Monthly payments

All council tax payments are due on the first of each month (unless payment is made by direct debit, in which case you have a choice of four payment dates):

  • 1st
  • 10th
  • 15th
  • 25th

It is your responsibility to ensure payments reach us by the due dates.

If you want to pay later in the month, at the start of the council tax year you should pay April's instalment at the end of March, May's instalment at the end of April and so on. This will ensure your account does not fall into arrears and will prevent recovery action.

For more about ways to pay, see our payments page.

Contact

To talk to us about council tax payments, please phone 01494 412 226 and select help key 1.
