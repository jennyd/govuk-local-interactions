Maintenance and Improvement to Council Properties

Find information regarding day to day repairs, home improvements, planned maintenance and septic tanks. Please remember before carrying out alterations to your home you must gain Landlords permission.

  • Day to Day Housing Repairs

    Day to Day Housing Repairs

    South Holland District Council provides a comprehensive property repairs service to all our tenants. Repairs are normally carried out by the council's own in-house Construction Services Unit, which is based in Spalding.

  • Home Improvements

    Before carrying out alterations to your home you must gain Landlords permission. Permission will not be refused unreasonably and all permission should be requested and will be given in writing.

  • Planned Maintenance and Improvement Work

    Every year the Council supports a rolling programme of works to its housing stock to ensure that each home meets or exceeds the Decent Homes Standard.

  • Blocked Drains and Septic Tank Clearance

    Blocked Drains and Septic Tank Clearance

    The Council operates a free drain clearance and septic tank clearing service to Council tenants or property owners who pay their sewage charge to South Holland District Council.
