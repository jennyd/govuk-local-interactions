Privately owned empty homes

Often there is a good reson why a property is empty, for instance when it is undergoing renovation by the owner.

However, properties that remain empty for a long time can attract vandalism, and other problems affecting the whole community. They can also become a burden on the community and the owner. 

If you have information about a privately owned empty property which has been empty for a long time, we would like to hear from you. We can then investigate what is happening and try to bring it back into use.
