Eligibility and applications

Allotments in Torbay are available to all members of the public and are becoming increasingly popular.
The allotments managed by TOR2 have waiting times of around two to three years (longer on some sites).  If you would like to place your name on the waiting list please the following form and return it to TOR2

Allotment Waiting List Application Form

As soon as we receive your form, your details will be recorded and placed on the waiting list for the sites you have selected.
The waiting list is managed in strict date order and if you are next on the waiting list, an Allotment Offer Letter will be sent to you, confirming which plot and site is available and the cost. We ask that this letter is replied to within 10 days to confirm whether you would like to accept or decline the offer. On confirmation that you would like to accept the plot, a lease agreement is then issued to be signed and witnessed. The new plot holder can then start cultivating their allotment.
Please note the following information:
  • The details you provide will be kept on file until an allotment is offered to you.
  • If you do not respond to the offer of a plot within the stated time period, your details will be removed from the waiting list.
  • Please inform this office of any name or address changes as we may not be able to contact you to offer you an allotment plot. You can write to us or send an email to .
  • To keep up with demand, we only allow one application per person

Fees

For all fees please contact TOR2 or the relevant Allotment Association directly using the contact information above.
