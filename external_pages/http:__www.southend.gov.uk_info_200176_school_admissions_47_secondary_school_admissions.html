Secondary School Admissions

All transfers from year 6 to year 7 for residents living in the Borough of Southend-on-Sea are coordinated by the School Admissions Team at Southend-on-Sea Borough Council. All in-year admissions for Secondary schools in the Borough are to be made directly to the schools.

Secondary School Admissions 2016/17

Applications for a September 2016 transfer to year 7 must be submitted between 1st September 2015 and 31st October 2015. The full admission criteria and further details can be found using the links below. Please note the Secondary Admissions Booklet is available here and a hard copy has been provided to all current year 5 pupils in a school in Southend-on-Sea. 

The online service is now closed, however parents can apply late using our paper form. 

Applications can only be submitted to Southend-on-Sea Borough Council by those who pay Council Tax to Southend-on-Sea Borough Council and have children resident in the Borough.
