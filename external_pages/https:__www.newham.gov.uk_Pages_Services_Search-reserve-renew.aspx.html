Search, reserve and renew library items

  • twitter
  • facebook
  • print
Intro Text

Search the library catalogue, reserve items and renew the ones at a time and place that suits you.

Page Image
Image Caption
Page Content

Go online

With an library card and PIN you can:

  • search for items including books, DVDs, CDs and talking books
  • reserve items and choose which library you would like to collect them from
  • renew your loans up to five times
  • find out what you have borrowed in the past
  • check what items you have on loan

on any computer, tablet or smartphone. If you do not know your PIN, call or drop in at your local library.

Got to our online library to get started.

Note, if you are a Newham resident with a Newham library card, it is free to reserve library items.
 
Back to top
 

By phone

Call our 24-hour automated line, 0115 929 3388 to renew and reserve items. Make sure you have your library card number handy.
 
Back to top
 

At a library

You can also renew your items and reserve new ones at your local library, as long as you bring your library card with you.
