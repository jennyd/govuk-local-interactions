Welcome to the Test Valley Borough Council consultation portal

These pages will provide links to all our current ‘live’ consultations giving you the opportunity to get involved, as well as giving you access to details of the feedback from previous consultation.

Consultations can be questionnaires, surveys or forums on a specific topic. Please check this page regularly for new consultations and regular feedback. If you have any queries please contact consultation@testvalley.gov.uk or telephone 01264 368123.
