Homefinder Somerset

In March 1999 all 'council housing' stock was transferred to South Somerset Homes, now Yarlington Housing Group and other housing associations in the area. Therefore, there is no 'council housing' in the area.

Homefinder Somerset

Choice Based Lettings - Homefinder Somerset

South Somerset District Council is part of a scheme called Homefinder Somerset.

What is Homefinder Somerset? 

Homefinder Somerset is a way of finding a home to rent which lets you select the home you would like to be considered for. Almost anyone can apply to join the scheme including existing tenants who would like to transfer.

It is a partnership of local authorities and housing associations who are working together to make the process of finding a home simple and transparent for all applicants across Somerset.

Partners are:

  • West Somerset Council
  • Mendip District Council
  • Sedgemoor District Council
  • South Somerset District Council
  • Taunton Deane Borough Council
  • Housing Associations in the Homefinder Somerset area
     

How the scheme works
 

Anyone looking for social housing anywhere in Somerset now only has to complete one application form and their needs will be assessed against the same clear set criteria no matter where they live.

Applicants choose which social housing vacancies they wish to be considered for by placing a 'bid' - expression of interest NOT a money bid - on properties which they are eligible for by size. Applicants can express an interest on a maximum of three properties per week.

How to apply

Complete an application online at www.homefindersomerset.co.uk

If you are already registered and do not have your login details, please contact the Registration team on 01935 462938 or email - HousingRegister@Southsomerset.gov.uk including your full name, date of birth and address.

Once you apply, your application will be assessed and placed into one of four bands:

  • Gold
  • Silver
  • Bronze
  • Emergency - in a very small number of cases
     

What to do if your circumstances change
 

If you move or your circumstances change you will need to contact the housing team for advice. It is very important to keep us informed of any changes which affect your application. Failure to do so could lead to you not being made an offer even if your bid is successful.

Where to find details of vacancies
  • Via the website www.homefindersomerset.co.uk at home or using computers available in the library or your local council offices.
  • Looking at a weekly property Newsletter at your local housing office or council office if in Somerset.
  • By ringing the Property Hotline to hear details of vacancies - Tel. 0845 2415948.

How to express an interest for homes

  • Via the website www.homefindersomerset.co.uk at home or using computers available in the library or your local council offices.
  • Via the automated telephone bidding line - Tel. 0845 2702524.
  • In person at your local housing office or council.
  • With the help of a support worker.

The advertising cycle for properties on Homefinder Somerset starts on a Wednesday and ends on a Sunday. Please note that you CANNOT search or bid for properties on a Monday or Tuesday.

If you have a question about your application you should contact the Housing Options Team on (01935) 462938.

If you are UK National Returning or arriving for the first time, we will require additional information from you to ensure that you are eligible to go onto the Housing Register.
