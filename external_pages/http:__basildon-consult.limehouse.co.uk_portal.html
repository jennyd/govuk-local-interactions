What are you looking for

Welcome to Basildon Borough Council's Local Plan Consultation Portal

Listed within this welcome page are all recent Local Plan consultations. These can be filtered by selecting an option from the 'Show Consultations for' box below.

Consultations can take the form of documents, questionnaires/surveys or forums on a given Local Plan document or topic. You can search the entire Consultation Portal using the 'Search' box above.

In some instances, you will be asked to register or log in before being able to participate in a consultation event. To register please click on the 'Register' link within the 'Login' section.
