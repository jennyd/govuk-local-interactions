The Electoral Register and Individual Registration

The Electoral Register

The Electoral Register is a list of all the people in England who are registered to vote

The Electoral Register is managed regionally by Local Authorities, under the control of the Electoral Registration Officer. The Electoral Registration Officer is personally responsible for the Electoral Register in his or her area. The day to day managing of the electoral register in Rother is delegated to the Elections Team at Rother District Council

The Elections team have a legal duty to be proactive about keeping the register up to date*. This is why we will write to you if we think you could register to vote *Section 9 of the Representation of the People Act 1983
