Street care & cleaning

Streetscene Services ensure Middlesbrough's public spaces are clean, tidy and attractive

The work undertaken by Streetscene Services plays a vital part in the life of Middlesbrough. The Service is responsible for looking after Middlesbrough's public spaces and ensuring that they are clean, tidy and attractive and operates a wide range of services from refuse collection and maintenance of the highway through to the provision of flowerbeds and waste education services. We believe that what we do is important to the present and future of Middlesbrough and we are working hard to deliver with the citizens of Middlesbrough a clean, green and attractive town.

To report a problem regarding street cleaning and litter removal please call 01642 726001 or email envservices@middlesbrough.gov.uk
