Council Tax

Stratford-on-Avon District Council is responsible for the collection of council tax. Your overall council tax bill includes charges made by ourselves, your parish or town council, Warwickshire County Council and Warwickshire Police.

The following information is available online;

  • How to pay your council tax
  • Council tax charges
  • Council tax bandings
  • Council tax benefits
  • Single occupier discount
  • Discounts and Exemptions
  • Inform us that you are moving home

Any information that is provided to the Council in respect of Council Tax may be used for other purposes. Please see the attached notice for further information.

For further information please contact the Enterprise, Housing and Revenues Division on 01789 260990 or by email at revenues@stratford-dc.gov.uk or by fax on 01789 260969.
