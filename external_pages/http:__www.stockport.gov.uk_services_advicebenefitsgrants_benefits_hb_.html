Housing Benefit - Help towards your Rent

Housing Benefit (HB) is a national scheme to help you pay your rent.

It is worked out and paid by your local Council but the rules regarding entitlement are set by government.

You can claim if you have a low income of any type. This includes people who work for an employer or who are self employed.

Housing Benefit is available for:

  • People who rent their home from the Council or a Housing Association
  • People who rent their home from a private landlord which is assessed under the Local Housing Allowance Scheme.
