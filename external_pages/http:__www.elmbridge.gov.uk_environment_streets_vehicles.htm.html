Abandoned Vehicles

|Report it

Our commitment

  • We aim to resolve any reports of abandoned vehicles within 21 working days.
  • We will investigate, and if appropriate arrange for the vehicle's removal and disposal.
  • |Any enforcement action by the Council will follow these principles.

Please report any vehicle that you think has been abandoned. We will need to know

  • Registration
  • Make, model and colour
  • Location of the vehicle

The Enforcement team will investigate the vehicle but please note that it can take some time to determine whether a vehicle is really abandoned and it may be necessary to seek permission to remove it.

Vehicles we are unable to deal with

  • Taxed vehicles. You can |check the status of a vehicle on the government website.
  • Vehicles causing an obstruction or parked in a dangerous position - please report to the police
  • Vehicles on private land - please report to the owners or managers of that land

If you suspect a vehicle is untaxed, then you can |find out online what you need to do before reporting it to the DVLA
