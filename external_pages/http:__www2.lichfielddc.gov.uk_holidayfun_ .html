Holiday Fun

We run fun activities for children and young people over the school holidays. From coaching courses at Burntwood, Friary and King Edward VI leisure centres, to fun activities, events and trails at Beacon Park, we have plenty to make sure you have a holiday of fun. To keep up to date, sign up for our Holiday Fun e-newsletter.

Booking by phone for leisure centre activities is now even easier! We can now take bookings for all activities at any one of our leisure centres. For example, you can book activities at Burntwood Leisure Centre and Friary Grange Leisure Centre by calling just one leisure centre. To book, please call the centre of your choice on 01543 308810 (Burntwood LC), 01543 308842 (Friary Grange LC) or 01543 308782 (King Edward VI LC).

Bookings for park activities To book activities in the Beacon Park, call 01543 308867 (everyday 9am – 4pm).

Call me back please If we’re busy, or you’re browsing this page outside of normal centre opening hours, and you would like us to call you back at a time that’s convenient to  you, please fill in our online call back form.

Take a look at the calendar below before each school holiday to see what’s on offer.
