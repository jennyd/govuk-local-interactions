Welcome to the Your Voice, Your Views database. The database holds information about consultation activities undertaken by Bedford Borough Council and our partners. The database helps to co-ordinate consultations, and enables us to communicate outcomes of the consultations.
The database allows you to search for consultation projects based on previous, current and future projects. An advanced search function is also available which allows you to search for consultations based on a range of criteria.
If you have any questions or comments about the Your Voice, Your Views database please contact us.
