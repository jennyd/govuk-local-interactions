Crematorium and Cemeteries

Taunton Deane Crematorium opened in 1963. It was built by local builders using stone from local quarries. The Crematorium was presented with a Civic Award in 1965 and the buildings were listed for architectural interest in 1998.

There are several cemeteries that are administered by Taunton Deane Borough Council in the area.

Who we are

Taunton Deane Crematorium and Cemeteries are publicly owned facilities run by Taunton Deane Borough Council. We charge for our services to cover the costs of running the crematorium.

What we do

We offer a respectful and helpful service for people at a difficult time. We perform cremations and burials. We also provide a Chapel for funeral services and we have a number of cemeteries in the Taunton Deane area.
