Dog fouling – penalty for not cleaning up

If you are walking your dog, you should always carry a poop scoop, plastic bag or other suitable equipment to clean up after your dog.

If you fail to clean up after your dog you may be liable to an on the spot fixed penalty. Further informtaion can be found on the dog control orders page.

 

How to dispose of dog waste

You can dispose of dog waste in the following bins (on the condition that the bag is biodegradable and tied securely):

  • Public waste bins
  • Dog mess bins
  • green wheelie bin
