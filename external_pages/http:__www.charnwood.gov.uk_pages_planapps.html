Anyone can make comments on a planning application. All comments we receive will be considered, but only comments which raise material planning considerations can be taken into account, alongside the planning policies of the Council and central government, when an application is decided.

All submitted planning applications are available to the public to view or comment on using Planning Explorer online. You can also comment by email or send your comments by post. Please note that any committee meeting dates shown in Planning Explorer may be subject to change until five working days before the meeting.
