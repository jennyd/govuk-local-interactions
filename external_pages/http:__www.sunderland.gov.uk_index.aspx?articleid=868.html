Street care and cleaning

How you may get a problem in your area sorted.

  • Dead animal removal

    Sunderland City Council will collect dead pets on the day of your request. We may also pick up dead animals from the Highway.

  • Drainage - help and advice

    Find out who is responsible for drainage within the boundary of your property.

  • Environmental projects and campaigns

    The council works with partners, residents and local businesses to improve the environment.

  • Flyposting - reporting and removal

    Find out how to report flyposting.

  • Fly tipping

    The council will investigate incidents of fly tipping and where appropriate, remove and dispose of illegally dumped rubbish from footpaths, highways, pedestrian areas and other areas of council owned land within three working days of a request.

  • Graffiti - reporting and removal

    Find out how to report graffiti.

  • Grass cutting

    Grass is maintained within the city on varied frequencies from once per year up to 30 times per year depending upon the site and growing conditions.

  • Markets - cleaning

  • Public toilets

    Sunderland City Council maintain the public conveniences around the city.

  • Vandalism - prevention measures

  • Litter bins

    Sunderland City Council supply and maintain litter bins throughout the city.

  • Dog and animal fouling

    Dog fouling is unpleasant and may cause disease and illness and, therefore, needs to be controlled.

  • Road safety - spillages

    We will remove spillages where possible, from our roads and footpaths. It is important to do so, to prevent the likelihood of an accident.
