Abandoned vehicles

If you suspect a vehicle is abandoned please first check online if the vehicle is taxed. You will need the vehicle registration and make to do this.

North Warwickshire Borough Council has agreed a protocol with the Police and Warwickshire County Council to deal with abandoned vehicles. Please report abandoned vehicles to Car Clear.

Please report dangerously parked or suspected stolen abandoned vehicles to the police on 101.

Abandoning a vehicle is a crime. If you have a car you no longer want please call our contractor on 01827 714782 and request for it to be removed. You must be the owner of the car and you will need to have the keys and/or vehicle logbook.
