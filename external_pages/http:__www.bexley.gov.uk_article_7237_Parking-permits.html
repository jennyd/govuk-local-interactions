Parking permits

You can apply online for resident and business parking permits, visitor scratch cards, car park season tickets and parking dispensations and suspensions.

When making your application, please ensure your vehicle registration is entered correctly and be careful not to mix up the letters 'I' and 'O' with the numbers '1' and '0'.

For further information and guidance please read the Methods of application section below and follow the simple steps. If you are looking at renewing your permit, (if your permit was previously issued by the Council and you have received the unique PIN number through the post), please select Permits Online and follow the guidance - Online permit renewal is simple and the quickest way to renew.

This authority is under a duty to protect the public funds it administers, and to this end may use the information you have provided on this form for the prevention and detection of fraud. It may also share this information with other bodies responsible for auditing or administering public funds for these purposes.

For further information, see National Fraud Initiative.

Social Care Permits

Individuals or organisations (other than medical practitioners) providing a local service of essential care or support to Bexley residents can apply for a social care permit. A GP Declaration confirming that the resident is receiving care must be provided alongside applications for this type of permit. Applications and completed GP declarations can be submitted through Permits online.

The GP Declaration is on page 2 of the social care permit terms and conditions which can be found under related downloads.

Two tier season tickets

There are two tiers for season ticket tariffs; the 'local' tier is for applicants who are employed locally within the London Borough of Bexley. The 'other' tier applies to all other applicants.

Employees applying for the local tariff must provide supportive evidence confirming they are employed by a business located within the borough. This may be in the form of a letter on headed paper from their manager, a wage slip, or anything else that confirms their employment is within the borough.

Methods of application for new parking permits or season tickets

On-line: Complete an application form through Permits Online. This will generate the permit number which must be quoted in all correspondence.
Then either:

  • Email your supportive evidence to permits@bexley.gov.uk
  • Take your supportive evidence quoting your permit number to a Council library or the Contact Centre
  • Post it to London Borough of Bexley, Contact Centre, Permit Section, 2 Watling Street, Bexleyheath, Kent, DA6 7AT*

If you make a "Pay Online" application and submit your supportive evidence by email or through the post, you will be contacted to confirm approval, and your e-permit will then be valid from the date specified in your application.
