The council tax leaflet sets out where our money comes from, including how much you contribute through paying council tax. It also explains what we are proposing to spend that money on this year, and how we are trying to make it go as far as possible as we continue to be committed to making Wellingborough a place to be proud of.

Council tax support scheme 2016-17 consultation

Some residents on low incomes are entitled to a discount on their council tax bill. This is called council tax support.

We are proposing some changes to our council tax support scheme and would like your views before any decisions are made. These proposals do not affect people of pensionable age.

Please take part in our consultation before 30 November 2015.
