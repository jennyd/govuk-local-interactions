Dog warden service

Dog warden service in Sandwell

To report a stray dog in Sandwell, or to find out if your dog has been picked up by the dog warden within Sandwell, contact us:

Email: contact@sandwell.gov.uk
Call: 0121 569 6625
Opening hours: Monday-Friday 8am-8pm, Saturday 8.30am-1.30pm.

To report a stray dog in Sandwell, please call 0121 569 6625.

We will only collect stray dogs within Sandwell. If you see a dog straying in another local authority area, you will need to contact the relevant council.

We will not deal with vicious dogs, or dogs seen as aggressive. If you believe that a dog is vicious or aggressive, please contact your local police station by calling 101.

Lost Dogs

If your dog has gone missing you can check the K9searchuk website at any time.     

Any stray dog picked up in Sandwell by the Sandwell dog warden is checked for an identity chip and details are registered on the K9SearchuK website as soon as possible (usually within 4 hours).  The dog is then taken to our contracted kennels.

Fees

If your dog is picked up by our dog warden, you will be charged a fee when you claim your dog. This fee will need to be paid before your dog is released back to you and will include daily kenneling costs.

Dogs are kept at the kennels for seven days. If a dog is not collected by its owner after seven days, it can be re-homed.
