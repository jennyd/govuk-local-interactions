Islington Citizens Advice Bureau (CAB)

Page Content

Islington CAB offers residents free independent and confidential advice.

Its trained and friendly staff will give you information and help to sort out a range of problems including:

  • Debt
  • Benefits
  • Housing
  • Employment
  • Goods and services
  • Family
  • Education
  • Discrimination and many more issues

How to Get Help

  • Call Islington CAB on 03444 889 626. Advice line opening hours: 
  • Monday - Friday, 10am - 1pm and 2 - 4pm
  • Closed Bank Holidays
  • If you are calling from a mobile, after dialling the above number you will be asked to give a local number, please use 020 7527 5259 (please don't call this number directly, it will not be answered).  

You will be able to get advice by phone or email or an appointment to see a CAB advisor in Islington.

Advice and information is also available anytime from the www.rcjadvice.org.uk/islington-cab/ website.

Funded by Islington Council

Your local CAB is funded by Islington Council but remains totally independent and impartial. 

It is a registered charity affiliated to Citizens Advice.
