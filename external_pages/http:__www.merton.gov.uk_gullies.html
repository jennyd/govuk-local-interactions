Drain and sewer problems

What to do if a drain or sewer is blocked or flooding.

Drains

We are responsible for maintaining road drains up to the point where they connect to the main sewer, which is the responsibility of Thames Water. The rainwater drains at the side of the road are called gullies.

To report a problem with a drain or gully at the side of the road, please use the Street Scene Enquiry Form and select "Drains and gullies" from the list of services. You can also use this form to request an additional gully.

Thames Water - Blocked Drains and Sewers

Sewers

Sewerage drains which connect from your property to the main sewage system are not the responsibility of Merton Council. Either the owner of the property or Thames Water are responsible for unblocking or repairing the drain.

Public sewers are the responsibility of Thames Water.
