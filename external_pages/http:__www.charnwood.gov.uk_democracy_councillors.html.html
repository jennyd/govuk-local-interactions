Further Details for Councillors

Contact details for councillors are available on their individual web pages.

Committee membership can be found on the committee pages. Those with lead responsbilities can be found under Cabinet.

The Conservative Party are currently in control and the political make up of the Council is as follows:

  • Conservative

      There are 41 Conservative Councillors in Charnwood

  • Labour

      There are 9 Labour Councillors in Charnwood

  • Liberal Democrat

      There is 1 Liberal Democrat Councillor in Charnwood

  • Independent

      There is 1 Independent Councillor in Charnwood.
