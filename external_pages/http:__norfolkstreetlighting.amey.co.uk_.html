Norfolk Street Lighting

Amey is working with Norfolk County Council to provide your public lighting service.

Please use the form below to report problems with streetlighting and other illuminated street furniture, e.g. an illuminated sign/bollard or a vehicle activated sign.

  • If it is an emergency please call 0344 800 8008 (24hrs)
  • Some street lights are owned and maintained by District, Borough, Parish or Town councils. If the street light is maintained by another organisation, then we will pass this information to them on your behalf.
  • For other highways problems, such as potholes or street cleaning, please use the following page Direct Link or visit the Norfolk County Council website.
