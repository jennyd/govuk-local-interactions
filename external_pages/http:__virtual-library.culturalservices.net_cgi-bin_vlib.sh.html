Major Changes to Stock Sharing

Luton Libraries have decided to withdraw from the current Joint Working Arrangement with Bedford Borough and Central Bedfordshire Libraries. This means that from Monday 1st February:
Bedford Borough and Central Bedfordshire members will not be able to request or borrow Luton titles
Luton members will not be able to request or borrow Bedford Borough or Central Bedfordshire titles.
We apologise for the inconvenience that this may cause.
