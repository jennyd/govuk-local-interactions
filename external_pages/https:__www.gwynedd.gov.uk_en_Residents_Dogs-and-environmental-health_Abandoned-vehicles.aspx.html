Abandoned vehicles

If you come across an abandoned vehicle in Gwynedd, please report it by calling

  • 01766 771000


We will aim to deal with your complaint as soon as we can to avoid any negative effects on the community and the environment.


What is considered to be an “abandoned vehicle” ?

A vehicle that has been left by the owner without any intension of returning.

You shouldn’t report a vehicle to abandoned just because of the following reasons:

  • the vehicle isn’t taxed
  • the vehicle is taking up a parking space
  • the vehicle is in a bad condition and is an eyesore in the area.

If a vehicle is obstructing an entrance or highway, or is parked dangerously, please contact the police.


For further information, call 01766 771000
