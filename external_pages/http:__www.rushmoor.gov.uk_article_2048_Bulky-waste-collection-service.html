Bulky waste collection service

Arrange a collection of larger items of household waste from your home.

How much the bulky waste collection service costs

  • Up to three bulky items for £38 (one item would still cost £38)
  • Four to six bulky items for £76
  • Seven to nine bulky items for £114

How the bulky waste collection service works

Once you have booked a collection, we will give you a collection day and we will collect your bulky waste between 7am and 3pm.

You must put your bulky items outside your property for collection.

To check which items we can collect, please see the table below.

How to book a collection

By phone:

Call our Customer Services team using the contact details on this page. We accept all major debit and credit cards (excluding American Express).

In person:

At the Council Offices, Farnborough Road, Farnborough. You can pay with all major debit and credit cards (excluding American Express).

If you want to amend or cancel your booking, please contact our team by 10am, the day before your collection is due.  We can not give refunds if we receive your request after this time.

Discounts

If you receive any of the following, your first three items a year (April - March) will be reduced to £19:

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance (mobility component at higher rate)
  • Employment Support Allowance
  • Guaranteed Pension Credit
  • Income Support
  • Job Seeker's Allowance
  • Personal Independence Payment
