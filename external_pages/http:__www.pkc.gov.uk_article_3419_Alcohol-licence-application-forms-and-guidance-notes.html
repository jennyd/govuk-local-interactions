Alcohol licence application forms and guidance notes

Sideways scrolling

Please refer to the application notes before completing any applications. Note:- The Policy Licensing Statement requires that to allow time to consult the Police and for objections to be made, applications for occasional licences should be submitted as far in advance of the event as possible. Applications submitted, less than 7 weeks prior to the date sought may not be capable of being processed in time for that date. This does not apply to an occasional licence for an unplanned event such as a Funeral.
