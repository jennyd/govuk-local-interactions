New builds and conversions

All new bins for new builds and conversions are £30 each including blue and green recycling bins.

Brown recycling sacks are provided for free.  

New resident 

If you have just moved into a property and the bins are missing there is a charge of £15 for a grey lidded bin.  

Replacement blue and green lidded recycling bins and brown recycling sacks will be delivered for free.

Replacement

Lost or missing bins often reappear before your next collection so we ask that you wait 2 weeks before reporting a missing bin.

If you require a replacement grey lidded bin one will be issued for a charge of £15.

Replacement blue and green lidded recycling bins and brown recycling sacks will be delivered for free.

 

Apply for a brown recycling bag 

 

Additional recycling containers

Where possible, we will provide additional or larger replacement recycling bins or sacks for free.

Additional grey lidded bins

If you feel your allocated grey lidded bin isn’t sufficient then you may be allowed an additional or a larger replacement bin depending on the number of people in your household or if you have babies in nappies or any medical factors that affect the amount of waste you produce.

You will need to complete an application form and a waste officer will need to conduct a waste audit at your home. If your application is successful an additional grey lidded bin will be issued for a charge of £30

 

Request a family waste assessment 

 

All additional bins must be authorised by Blackpool Council's application process and any unauthorised bins presented for collection will be removed.

Applying for bins

Please call Customer First on 01253 477477 to purchase a bin or apply for an additional recycling container. Or you can request delivery for a brown recycling sack online . All bin charges are non-refundable.
