  • Berkhamsted Town Council by-election 2016

    A by-election for the Berkhamsted West ward will be held on Thursday 18 February. All residents in the ward who are on the electoral register will receive a polling card before election day.
    Read more...

  • Don't miss out on discounted insulation

    We’re working with Aran Services to give energy company-funded discounts on loft and cavity wall insulation to home owners and private tenants. All residents were sent a letter with more information about the discounts available in December.

  • Could you be a Business Ambassador?

    The recently launched Hemel Hempstead Business Ambassadors programme will recruit economic champions to bring together the knowledge, networks and influence of businesses, organisations and individuals as a powerful force to help put Hemel Hempstead on the map.
    Read more...
