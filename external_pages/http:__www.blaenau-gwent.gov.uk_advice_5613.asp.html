Family Information Service

Welcome to Blaenau Gwent Family Information Service.

Blaenau Gwent Family Information Service provides free, quality, confidential and impartial information and guidance on the full range of services available to Children and Young People in the local borough of Blaenau Gwent.  

We offer advice on choosing childcare, children's clubs, baby groups, leisure and sports activities, health issues, parenting support and much more. 

We can also provide impartial advice on training for a career in childcare, setting up as a childcare provider and how to access grants.

Blaenau Gwent Family Information Service is based at The Heart of the Valleys Children's Centre,Old Blaina Infant School, High Street Blaina Blaenau Gwent NP13 3BN  

We are available from Monday - Thursday 9.00a.m. - 5.00p.m. and Friday 9.00 a.m. – 4.00 p.m.

An Answer phone facility is available for an out of hour service, if you leave your name and number, someone will get back to you.
