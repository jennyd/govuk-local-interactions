      RCBC Sure Start West
      West Locality covers the Sure Start Childrens Centres Grangetown, South Bank, Ormesby, Normanby and Eston
      RCBC Sure Start Central
      Central Locality covers the Sure Start Childrens Centres of Redcar, Marske and Dormanstown
      RCBC Sure Start East
      East Locality covers the East Cleveland, Skelton, Saltburn and Guisborough

      Redcar and Cleveland Sure Start Children's Centres offer a wide range of services and activities for families with children aged under 5 who live within the borough.

      The range of activities covers the following themes:

      Come and Play, Next Steps.
      Offering you and your child a range of fun and exciting activities including crafts, singing, dancing, story telling, outdoor play and much more.

      Training and Employment Services
      Offering a variety of training courses and employment advice to enable you to gain new skills and confidence, improve your job prospects and gain nationally recognised qualifications.

      Parenting Programmes
      Offering you and your family a range of programmes developed to enhance your knowledge, skills and confidence as a parent/carer.

      Health Drop-In, Baby Social and Baby time
      Activities designed specifically to help support you and your family during pregnancy and in the first few months of your new babys life.

      Healthy Lifestyles
      Offering a range of activities specifically designed to introduce fun and exciting ways of enjoying a healthy lifestyle for you and your family.

      Early Support and Portage
      Offering support to parents/carers and their children under 5 who have complex additional needs.

      Advice and Information
      Offering the opportunity to discuss the issues and challenges facing families with children under 5.
