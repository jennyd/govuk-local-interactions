Register to vote

Register to vote online

How do I register to vote?

1. You can register to vote online.

2. Fill in your name, address, date of birth and a few other details. You'll also need your national insurance number, which can be found on your national insurance card or in official paperwork such as payslips or letters about benefits/tax credits.

3. Look out for a confirmation to say you're registered.

Other registration information

If you want to use your right to vote at elections, you first need to have your name on the Electoral Register. It is also much harder to obtain credit if your name is not on the register.

You should note that registering for other Council services or paying Council Tax does not automatically put you on the register.
