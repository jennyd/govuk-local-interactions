Garden Waste Services

GREENbin Deliveries

Due to really high demand for the GREENbin service, it is taking up to four weeks from receipt of application to delivery of the actual bin. 

Although payment for the service will be taken before the bin is delivered, the invoice for next year's payment will be taken one year after the bin has been delivered and not the date the application is received so you will not be financially disadvantaged.

  

GREENbin Deliveries

Due to really high demand for the GREENbin service, it is taking up to four weeks from receipt of application to delivery of the actual bin. 

Although payment for the service will be taken before the bin is delivered, the invoice for next year's payment will be taken one year after the bin has been delivered and not the date the application is received so you will not be financially disadvantaged.

  

There are several options for disposing of your garden waste such as composting at home or having a regular collection by signing up to our GREENbin service.

Garden waste should not be put in with your general rubbish for collection. If rubbish sacks/bags are found to contain garden waste they will not be collected.

Please see the options below to find out how you can dispose of your garden waste:

Home Composting

Home composting is the most economical and environmentally sustainable option for dealing with green garden waste. Please visit the Home Composting page to find out how to start your own compost bin and details on how you can purchase a composter from as little as £13 (plus £5.49 delivery).

GREENbin collection service

The GREENbin provides a cost-effective, convenient way of collecting garden waste, reducing the amount of waste that is sent to landfill.

The service is an optional, chargeable wheeled bin service where your garden waste is collected from your household every two weeks for nine months of the year.

To find out more information about the service, please visit the GREENbin page.

Crawley Household Waste Recycling Site (HWRS)

Crawley Household Waste Recycling Site located along Metcalf Way accepts green garden waste: hedge cuttings, leaves, grass cuttings, twigs and branches free of charge. Please place your green waste loose in the specially designated skips/containers/bays provided at the sites and ensure that it is free from any plastics or metal. 

Further Information

Purchasing Compost

Green waste collected via our GREENbin collection service or taken to the Household Waste Recycling Site is composted by Olus and turned into a certified soil conditioner compost product.

This product sold under the Revive brand meets the highest quality standard on the market, including the Compost Quality Protocol (CQP) and the British Standards Institution (BSI) Publicly Available Specification (PAS) 100, the leading industry accreditation for compost quality.

You can buy the soil improver locally from the Crawley Household Waste Recycling Site at Metcalf Way for £4 per 40 litre bag or 3 for £10.

The compost can also be purchased in bulk for either household or business use from the composting facility direct.
