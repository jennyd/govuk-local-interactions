Whitley Housing Needs Survey 2015

Tuesday, 17 November 2015

Selby District Council’s Rural Housing Enabler is completing a survey in Whitley Parish to identify if there are local people with a local connection in housing need. In order to assess this it is important to hear from people who live, work or may have previously lived in the Parish and would like to move and/or remain/return to Whitley.

A paper copy of the questionnaire has been sent to all residents in the Parish and the form can also be completed on-line using the link below.

The closing date for responses is 23 December 2015.
