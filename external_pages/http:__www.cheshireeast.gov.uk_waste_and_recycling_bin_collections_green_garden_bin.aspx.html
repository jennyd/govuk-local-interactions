Garden waste collection service

Winter suspension 2015/16

The garden waste service will be suspended from the week beginning Sunday 6th December 2015.

The service will resume the week beginning Sunday 14th February 2016. As garden bins are collected every fortnight, please check the online calendar for your last collection of 2015 and your first collection of 2016.

For more information see the 2015/2016 Garden waste collection winter suspension page.

We offer a garden waste collection service every two weeks to all residents, bins may be a green or brown colour. The waste is taken to local farms to compost, where it is shredded, composted in large rows and finally spread onto the farmland.

To arrange for a garden waste bin to be delivered to your home, please complete the online bin request form.

  • Tea, tea bags, coffee grounds and filters*
  • Egg shells, stale bread and crusts*
  • Feathers, pet straw, sawdust and wood shavings

* We cannot accept kitchen organic waste because of Foot and Mouth regulations. All waste collected from the Garden Waste Service goes back on to farmland, and we have to ensure that there is a break in the food chain to prevent any future outbreaks. However, if you do wish to dispose of kitchen organic waste, you can do so by home composting.

Collection information

  • Put your bin at the edge of your property, adjacent to the public highway, for collection
  • Ensure the lid is closed and your bin is out for 7am on your collection day
  • Return your bin to your property as soon as reasonably possible after collections have been made
  • Additional garden waste left next to the bin, will not be collected
  • If you are elderly or disabled and will have difficulty moving the bin, please contact us on 0300 123 5011 to be considered for an assisted collection service.

If you are experiencing problems with your waste collection please go to our problems with collection page.

When is my bin collected?

You can check out when your garden waste bin will be collected using the online My Collection Day app.

Additional garden waste bins

We offer one free garden waste bin to all residents. However, we do realise that some households have larger gardens and find it easier to use more than one garden bin for their needs. We offer additional garden waste bins at an annual charge of £45 each. To apply for an additional garden bin please complete the online bin request form.
