Community wardens and local crime

Reporting crime and anti-social behaviour

Read our report crime and anti-social behaviour page to find out how to report a problem.

Contact your Police and Crime Commissioner

If you have any concerns about community safety, email your Police and Crime Commissioner (Anthony Stansfeld) at pcc@thamesvalley.pnn.police.uk or send him a tweet. Find out more about Anthony Stansfeld on the Thames Valley Police and Crime Commissioner website

Community wardens

Our team of community wardens patrol the Borough in pairs. Each team is responsible for a specific area. Wardens are not a substitute for the Police or the emergency services, but they complement them.   

Join our Junior Warden Scheme

Our Junior Warden Scheme encourages young people to contribute to the community through a variety of projects, such as litter picking, bike marking and public speaking. Past projects include a Saturday morning cinema group, a community garden, a carol service for older people, and charity fund-raising activities. Contact us to find out more.
