Have you got the energy and passion to change the world for the better? Are you up for lobbying MPs and other decision-makers to make change happen? Stand for UK Youth Parliament and represent young people from Hackney on the national stage.

The UK Youth Parliament provides opportunities for 11-18 year olds to use their elected voice to bring about social change through meaningful representation and campaigning.

Elections for UK Youth Parliament will be taking place on 29 February 2016. Anyone aged 11-18 can stand – as long as you live, work, or go to school in Hackney. You will need to submit a manifesto statement of 200 words before the deadline of Thursday 25 February (5.00pm). If you are interested in running as a candidate fill in the form below email info@hackney.gov.uk or call 020 8356 7650 (10.00am – 5.00pm weekdays).
