Welcome to Wakefield Online Admissions

The online system is closed for new applications and will be opened on the dates below, for those who applied online to view their result.

Transferring to Secondary School

01 March 2016

Admission to full-time School (Reception)

18 April 2016

Transferring from Infant to Junior School

18 April 2016

Please note that if the offer day is on either Saturday or Sunday, you will be able to see your result by logging into your account after midnight on that date. 

In order to see the result of your application, you will need to log into your account using your username and password.

Please note that you can accept or decline the offer of a school place online. In doing so, you DO NOT need to return the acceptance slip enclosed with your offer letter.

If the decision reads 'Place offered', this is the school that you have been offered a place at.

If the decision reads ‘Preference discarded,' this means that you could have been offered a higher preference and therefore the decision on this preference has been discarded. 

If the decision reads ‘Refused’, this means that there were more applications for the school/academy than there were places.

If you have been refused a place at a school of a higher preference to the one you have been offered, your child will be placed on the waiting list for that school for the 2016/2017 academic year.

PLEASE NOTE, YOU ARE NOT ABLE TO SUBMIT AN APPEAL ONLINE.  APPEAL PAPERS WILL BE SENT IN THE POST TO ANYONE WHO HAS BEEN REFUSED A PLACE AT A HIGHER PREFERENCE SCHOOL.
