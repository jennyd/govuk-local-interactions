Contact Us

We want to make access to our services as easy as possible for our customers. The information below details the various ways to contact us, our current opening times and office locations.

 

 Twitter

 

Follow @WestNorfolkBC to send us tweets and receive live updates on a range of services

 

 Online Form

 

Use the online electronic form: General Enquiry eForm

 

Once you have submitted the form, we aim to respond within 24 hours. If you include your email address, you will also receive an automated email confirming the same details.

 

 Email

 

For all enquires please email us on contact@west-norfolk.gov.uk

 

 Community Information Points

 

There are a number of Community Information Points located in rural areas where council services can be accessed via the Internet.

 Office Opening Hours

 

There are Council Information Centres in King's Lynn, Hunstanton, and Downham Market. Please check below for the opening times of each office.

 

At any of our Council Information Centres you can:

  • Get advice and information
  • Arrange delivery of any council service
  • Make a payment for council services - no cash accepted 
  • Submit applications along with supporting documentation or evidence
  • Get assistance and advice in completing application forms
