Local parks


There are over 200 parks and open spaces within the borough of Walsall covering approx 950 hectares.  Links to the right of this page are for the larger parks and open spaces found within the borough.

Whilst all the parks and open spaces are generally free to visit, sometimes there will be a charge to attend an event or participate in an activity. A small entrance fee may be incurred when events are taking place within the park.

For further information please email cleanandgreen@walsall.gov.uk
