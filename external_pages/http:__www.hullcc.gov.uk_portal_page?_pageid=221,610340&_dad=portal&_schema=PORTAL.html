Brown bin and kitchen caddy

Below is a list of things that can you can put in your brown bin. With your brown bin you will also receive a kitchen caddy - a small bin you can use in your kitchen to collect food waste in before putting it in your brown bin. Your kitchen caddy also comes with compostable liners.

What goes in your brown bin

Yes please to -

  • Garden waste, for example -

grass cuttings

weeds

small shrubs

cut/dead flowers
twigs small branches

hedge cuttings

Remember, please don’t put your garden waste in bags - it must be loose.
  • Food waste, for example -

fruit and vegetable peelings bread cheese meat
plate scrapings leftovers bones tea bags
fish coffee grounds egg shells


  No thanks to -

soil rubble stones
wood old plant pots

How to use your kitchen caddy

  • line the kitchen caddy with a compostable liner
  • put leftover food in the caddy
  • lock the lid
  • tie the liner up when it’s full
  • put the liner into your brown bin

When using the compostable liners please remember to -

  • replace the liners every three to four days
  • let hot food cool before putting it in
  • keep the bags away from young children
  • only use the compostable liners provided by us

Collections

Your brown bin is emptied every two weeks. Please put your bin out by 7am at the boundary of your property and return it as soon as you can after it's been emptied. We empty bins between 7am and 4.30pm.

Use our postcode checker to find out your collection dates

Assisted collections

If you need help moving your bin, you can request assisted collection by telephoning 01482 300 300.

Replacements and additional bins

You can request up to two additional brown bins or a replacement kitchen caddy using the online form below -
 Request recycling bins and food bins

Compostable liners
There is a tag towards the end of the roll of compostable liners delivered to you. Please attach the tag or a liner to your brown bin and the collection crew will give you

more liners. Rolls can also be picked up from customer service centres, household waste recycling centres and Longhill and Bilton Grange information centres.

How to use your kitchen caddy and brown bin

Line the kitchen caddy with compostable liners Put leftover food in the caddy Lock the lid
Tie the liner up when full If you need more liners, simply attach the tag in the roll or a liner to your brown bin and we'll deliver Do your gardening - cut the grass
Do your gardening - trim the hedges Put your bagged food waste and your garden waste into the brown bin Put your bins out by 7am
We'll do the rest!
