Comments, complaints, questions

At the London Borough of Bexley we are committed to putting you first, but we need your help to ensure we do that.

We want you to tell us how we are performing, so we can recognise staff who deliver excellent customer service and, most importantly, put things right when they've gone wrong.

So, whether it's a comment, compliment or complaint, please use our online feedback form to tell us about it.

We promise to:

  • Take feedback seriously
  • Use your comments to help us improve our services
  • Investigate complaints thoroughly and fairly

Complaints:

We pride ourselves on delivering high quality, value for money services in Bexley and are committed to providing what you need, in the way you want and to the standard you expect. We believe that dealing effectively with complaints is essential to providing good services.

All of the information you should need about all aspects of our complaints procedure can be found on Bexley Complaints Procedure.

Have you had reason to contact the Council recently or used one of our services?

If so, we would be grateful if you could take the time to complete our Customer Satisfaction Survey to give us your views on the speed and quality of the response you received.
