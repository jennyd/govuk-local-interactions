How do I pay a penalty charge notice?

There are many ways to pay your penalty charge:

Online

Pay using our online payment service

By phone

You can pay over the telephone using the automated system which is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Please call Allpay on 0844 557 8321 (local rate), have your debit or credit card, and this Notice, ready as you will need to quote the reference number found under the bar code on the front of the notice.

In person at any Post Office or shop displaying the PayPoint sign

You may pay at any Post Office or shop displaying the PayPoint sign. You must have the notice with you when making the payment as the bar code has to be scanned.

By post

Send a cheque or postal order to Herefordshire Council, PO Box 224, Hereford HR1 2XW.

Write the penalty charge notice number on the reverse of your cheque or postal order and complete the slip on the bottom of the notice. Cheques should be made payable to Herefordshire Council. No receipts will be given for remittances paid by post unless a stamped addressed envelope is provided.

When a penalty charge notice is issued the amount payable will be either £70 or £50 (the amount will be stated on the PCN). The notice is payable within 28 days of being issued.

If payment is received within 14 days of the issue date, we will accept a discounted payment of 50 per cent as full and final settlement.
