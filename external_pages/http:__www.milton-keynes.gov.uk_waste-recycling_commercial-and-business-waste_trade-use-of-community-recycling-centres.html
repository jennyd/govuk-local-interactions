Trade use of Community Recycling Centres

To assist small traders who have limited disposal options, Milton Keynes Council provides a commercial and industrial waste area at our Newport Pagnell Community Recycling Centre (North Crawley Road, Newport Pagnell).  The Trade Waste area is operated on the council’s behalf by H W Martin Recycling Services and is available between 8am and 5pm Monday to Friday and from 8am to 1pm on a Saturday (the trade site is closed Saturday afternoons, Sundays, Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Years Day and only open until 1pm on Christmas and New Years Eve).

A weighbridge is installed so that business vehicles can be charged according to the type of waste and by weight – green waste is less expensive to dispose of so this is reflected in the price. Materials which are more expensive to dispose of, such as plasterboard, are charged at a higher rate.

The site is only suitable for commercial vehicles up to 3.5 tonnes.

Prices listed are for cash payments. Payments by cash, cheque or credit card are accepted (Credit Card payment facilities are only available Monday - Friday 9:00am to 16:45pm). Credit accounts are available to high frequency users. For further details please contact the site manager.

  • We recommend traders pre-sort their waste. Mixed loads will be charged at the highest rate - so mixed green waste and plasterboard will be charged at £144 per tonne.
  • Please note We are unable to accept asbestos or tyres from commercial sources.
  • Alternative privately operated disposal sites can be found in your local directory listings under "waste disposal".
  • Failure to dispose of your waste at a licensed disposal point could result in a fine of £50,000.
