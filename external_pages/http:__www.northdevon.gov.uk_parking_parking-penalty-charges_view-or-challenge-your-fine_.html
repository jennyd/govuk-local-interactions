North Devon Council

PCN Details

North Devon Council as from the 1st April 2014 will only be enforcing off street car parks. If your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) number begins with the prefix DX or DQ, please use this facility to view your PCN. However if your PCN begins with the prefix YD, please contact Devon County Council.

You can use this site to pay for a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), to find out more information about the contravention, or to challenge. If you are making a formal NTO representation, please press the Appeal button after entering your PCN details and use the PIN number supplied with your NTO. Please remember to use the bottom back button on each page when navigating back to the main parking page.
