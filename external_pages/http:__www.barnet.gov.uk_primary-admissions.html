Starting school in Barnet

Starting school is an exciting time for both parents and children. You should be thinking about your child starting reception class in the September following their third birthday.

Full details of the application process can be found in our Primary School Admissions Guide 2016. The guide includes:

  • school admission arrangements
  • how to choose a school
  • the application timetable
  • contact details for Barnet primary schools.

Admissions for entry into school in September 2016

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012 you will need to apply to your home council for a place in a primary school for the 2016/2017 academic year.

  • you should submit your application online using the eAdmissions website
  • the online application process opened on 1 September 2015
  • the closing date for applications is 15 January 2016
  • online results will be available in the evening of 18 April 2016
  • you must not apply using this process if your child has special educational needs (SEN), with an Education Health Care Plan (EHCP).

Making your application – important points to remember

  • you can list a maximum of six schools in the order you prefer them; there is no advantage in listing only one school
  • you are advised to include at least one local community school as one of your preferences to ensure that your child has a better chance of securing a school place for 2016/17
  • you can select schools within and outside your home borough
  • make sensible choices to improve your chances of securing a place at a selected school
  •  if your first choice school cannot be offered, you will receive information on how places were allocated, the waiting list procedure and how to appeal against the decision not to offer your child a place at your chosen school(s)
  • we will retain waiting lists for the oversubscribed schools and make a second round of offers from the waiting lists at the end of May 2016 (please remember that only a small number of places are expected to become available at oversubscribed schools, after 18 April 2016)
  • after May 2016, further offers will be made from school waiting lists as and when places become available
  • if your child has not been offered a place at any of your preferred schools and you do not wish to accept a place at the alternative school offered, you will become responsible for securing suitable education for your child.
