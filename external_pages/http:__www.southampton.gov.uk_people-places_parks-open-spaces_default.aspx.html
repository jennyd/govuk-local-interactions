Activities and events

Get active in the fresh air with park activities.

Lunchtime walks in Central Parks
Lunchtime walks have finished now for the winter and will start again in early spring.

Forest Schools Day Camps: Our day camps allow children to experience and enjoy a range of fun activities to help build confidence, self-esteem, independence and give encouragement and support to assess risk. We welcome children between the ages of 4 to 10 years old, but children under 6 must be accompanied by an adult.

We usually run Forest School day camps during school holidays and half term breaks. Find out about the next Forest Schools day camps and how to book.

We are having a free open session on Saturday 30 January to discover why our day camps are so much fun.  You can join in at any time between 10am to 3pm and take part in a Forest School style activity, and find out why our day camps are so popular. This is a drop in session, so you don't need to book, but children must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

If you would like information about future Forest School camps and similar activities, please email hawthorns@southampton.gov.uk.

Orienteering is a fun way to explore Southampton Common. There are three courses to suit all ages and abilities, all starting from outside The Hawthorns Centre. You don't need any special equipment, just download the map and bring a pen or pencil. The course is checked regularly but if you find a control point missing, please let us know.

The Pitch and Putt at Riverside Park, Woodmill Lane is now closed for the winter.

Pitch and Putt, Weston Shore 
This is managed by an independent operator and therefore any enquiries or comments need to be directed to the kiosk at Weston Shore.

Opening hours 10:30am to 7:30pm (Weather permitting)
Last tee off 6:45pm

  • Adult £4 for 9 holes
  • Under 18 £2.50 for 9 holes
  • Family of 4 £12 for 9 holes

Hire of clubs an extra £1.00 charge, for 3 clubs, Putter, Wedge, Sand Iron, or 7,8,9 Iron.
This includes 1 Golf Ball, 1 tee and a score card

Deposit of £10 required for the hire of clubs - either cash or Credit/Debit Card
