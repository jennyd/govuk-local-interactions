Health and Social Care enquiry form

Fill in this form for help and advice about adult social care. We will usually reply by the next working day, to give you an answer or to find out more about your situation.

Do not use the form in an emergency or if your enquiry is urgent. During office hours, you should telephone 0161 909 6517, text 07970 134894 or minicom 0161 909 6527. At other times contact the emergency service on 0161 794 8888.

If you are worried that someone is in immediate danger of being abused call 999. Further information is available about safeguarding vulnerable adults.

If your enquiry is about welfare rights (benefits) or about debt advice, you can contact our welfare rights and debt advice service.

If your enquiry is about a child, you should contact our Referral and Initial Assessment Team.
