Premises Licence

To provide late-night refreshments and regulated entertainment, and sell alcohol, you need a premises licence. 

Apply

You can apply for a premises licence online.

A copy of your application must be submitted to local responsible authorities.

We advise you to read the guidance notes before applying.

Fee

View our premises licence fees. 

Premises licensees

If you currently hold a premises licence you can apply for the following services online:

  • application for a provisional statement
  • application for need for a designated premises supervisor to be disapplied
  • application for a minor variation to a premises or club licence
  • application to vary a premises licence
  • application to vary a premises licence to specify an individual as designated premises supervisor
  • application to transfer a premises licence
  • notification of an interest in premises under section 178
  • interim authority notice
  • consent to transfer
  • consent to be designated
  • notification of change of name or address
  • request to be removed as designated premises supervisor
  • make an annual payment for a premises licence
