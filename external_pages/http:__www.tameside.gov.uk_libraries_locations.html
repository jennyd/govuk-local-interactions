Tameside Local Studies and Archives Centre

Old Street, Ashton OL6 7SG

Location Map of Local Studies and Archives Unit 
Car parking: There are several spaces behind the Library next to the entrance to Tameside Local Studies & Archives Centre; Disabled Parking Bays are available next to the ramp at the side of the building on Old Street; The nearest pay and display car park is at Cotton Street.

Ordering archives in advanced is recommended as retrieval on demand cannot be guaranteed. Saturdays- please ensure your order reaches us by 2pm on the Thursday preceding your visit at the latest.
