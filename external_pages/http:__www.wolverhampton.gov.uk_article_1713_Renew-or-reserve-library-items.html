Renew or reserve library items

Renew or reserve library items online, by phone or in person.

If you haven't finished with your books, CDs or DVDs, then you can renew or reserve them in a number of ways.

Online

You can renew your book online. Please note, you will be taken to an external website.

You will need your borrower number (located on your library card) and PIN number to renew or reserve library items online. If you do not have a PIN number you can request a PIN number.


Renew or reserve library items new

Phone

You can renew items by telephone 24 hours a day.

When calling, you will need the barcode number from your library ticket:

  • Telephone: 01902 552500

In person

You can renew your library items at any Wolverhampton library, regardless of which library you borrowed the item from.

Reserve an item from another library

If your local library does not stock the particular item you want, or you can't find the item in the library catalogue, they can try to buy it or borrow it through the Inter-Library Loan scheme.

The Inter-Library Loan scheme also allows you to request out of print items, photocopies from periodicals and serials.

Find the costs here

You can only borrow items through this scheme by visiting your local library.
