Homelessness - Advice and Support

People become homeless for all sorts of reasons. If you are at risk of losing your home, can't stay where you are, or are worried that you will have to move out soon, we may be able to help you. We can give you advice and support around the options that may be available to you by clicking on the link below:

Aberdeen City Council Housing Advice Service

Below is a list of other help that might be useful: 

  • Homelessness Out of Hours Helpline - Tel: 01224 620610.
    This emergency service operates from 5pm to 8.30am Monday to Friday. During weekends and public holidays we provide a 24 hours service.
  • Housing Support and Resettlement Service
  • Shelter Scotland
  • Free food: Times and Places to Collect Free Food
  • Health: NHS Grampian - Marywell Health Centre
  • Drugs/alcohol: Integrated Drug Service 
  • Drugs:  Drugs Action
  • Alcohol: Addaction
  • Alcohol:  Alcohol_Support
  • Housing/Debt: Civil Legal Assistance Office
  • Low cost furniture, carpets, etc:  Instant Neighbour
  • Free help: furniture – Somebody Cares
  • Financial help:  Grampian Credit Union or St Machar Credit Union - Credit Unions are a not-for-profit financial co-operative that is owned and controlled by its members. They can best be described as groups of people who save together and lend to each other at reasonable rates of interest, thus allowing each other the opportunity to gain greater control over their financial affairs.
