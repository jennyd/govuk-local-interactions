Mobile library

Service announcements

  • Wednesday 3 February - no evening service
  • Thursday 4 February - no service, all day
  • Monday 8 February - no evening service
  • Tuesday 9 February - no evening service
  • Wednesday 10 February - no evening service
  • Thursday 11 February - no evening service
  • Saturday 13 February - no service, all day

The mobile library visits each stop every week and has a lift for disabled access.

If you already have a ticket for the Central or any community library you can borrow items from the mobile library. If you want to join for the first time, please bring some proof of your name and address with you. 

The mobile library has a large collection of books, including fiction and non-fiction as well as large-print books. A variety of CDs and talking books is also available.

A small selection of Urdu, Punjabi, Bengali, Gujarati and Hindi material is carried. There is also a wide variety of books for children and teenagers.

If the book you want isn't on the mobile library, just ask for it and we'll try to get it for you.
