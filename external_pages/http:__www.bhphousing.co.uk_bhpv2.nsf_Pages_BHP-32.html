Repairs

How to report a repair?

 

You must tell us, without delay, if there are any faults in your home which we are obliged to repair. 

 

You can report a non urgent repair online.


You can also contact the Customer Response Team by:

  • Phone: 020 8937 2750 (select option 1)
  • Email: repairs.centre@bhphousing.co.uk

 

Please remember the Customer Response Call Centre is open between the following hours:

 

  • Monday to Thursday 8am to 6pm and Friday 8am to 5pm.

 

Emergency out of hours repairs

 

If you need an emergency repair outside office hours you should call 020 8937 1234.

 

South Kilburn

 

If you need to report a repair and you live in South Kilburn, you can visit the South Kilburn Housing Office at:

Community Resource Centre
William Dunbar House
Albert Road
Kilburn, London
NW6 5DE

Opening hours Monday - Friday 9am to 5pm, closed for lunch from 1pm to 2pm
