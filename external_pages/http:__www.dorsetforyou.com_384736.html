Bulky waste reuse and recycling collections

Listen

Get big household items like old fridges, sofas, TVs, beds and mattresses collected.

If you want to get rid of big household items like fridges, TVs, washing machines, sofas, beds and mattresses and can't take them to your local household recycling centre, you can book a bulky waste collection.

Reuse and recycling to help the community

The Dorset Waste Partnership (DWP) is working with local charity Dorset Reclaim (opens in a new window) to provide a bulky waste collection service for Dorset households.

Dorset Reclaim is a furniture reuse project offering electrical and household goods donated by local people and businesses to households on modest incomes.  They aim to reuse or recycle most of the items they collect.

How much does it cost?

The price is £30 to collect a single item and £6 for each subsequent item.  However, if an item is good quality and can be reused, Dorset Reclaim may collect it for free.

You can book a bulky waste collection by calling Dorset Reclaim on 01202 679350.

Opening hours

Dorset Reclaim are open Mondays to Friday, 8am to 6pm.

For more information, visit the Dorset Reclaim website (opens in a new window).

Other ways to reuse and recycle bulky waste

Household recycling centres

If you have your own transport, you can take bulky household items to your local household recycling centre.

Commercial waste services

The bulky waste collection service is only for household items.  If you are a business, the DWP also provides a range of commercial waste and recycling services.

Furniture reuse groups

As well as Dorset Reclaim, there is a range of furniture reuse groups across Dorset that collect unwanted furniture and household items.
