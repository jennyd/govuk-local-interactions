Services for Older People

The Department's services are provided by its specialist division, Social Care and Health for Older People and include a wide range of domiciliary, day care and residential support that may be commissioned from in house provision or independent agencies.


Eligibility for services is determined by an assessment of an individual's needs undertaken by a member of one of the Department's Care Management Teams. Sheltered/Supported Housing Sheltered housing has a warden who may be on duty for part of the day, tenants can access community provision i.e. personal care if required or careline, but this has limited options for those with greater need.


Extracare housing has a support team on duty 24 hours a day who would meet each tenants specific needs. This allows people with higher needs to live in tenancies where previously they may have gone into residential care.
