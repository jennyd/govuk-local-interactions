Comments, compliments and complaints

We aim to provide high quality services to anyone who lives, works or visits here. If you are happy with the work we do, or if you want to complain about a service or our response to a request, you can register your feedback with us. You might find our  Complaints Policy [140kb] and  Management Structure [511kb] documents useful.

Collapse allReveal all

How do I give my feedback?

You can fill out our feedback form or send us an email. We have a separate feedback form if you are not signed up to My Account. You could also:

  • Telephone us at 01493 856100
  • Write to Complaints, Member Services, Town Hall, Hall Plain, Great Yarmouth, NR30 2QF
  • Visit us during office hours at the Town Hall, Greyfriars House or the Novus Centre
  • Contact your Borough Councillor

How do I make a complaint about a councillor?

All councillors are subject to a code of conduct. If you think your councillor has breached this code, you can fill in our code of conduct complaints form. The independent person whose job it is to review these cases will recommend whether the action needs investigating.

What happens if I don't like the Council's response?

You can ask for a review of our response. If you still want to take the matter further, you can contact the Local Government Ombudsman.
