Shortcuts for applying for a primary school place in 2016/17

Related information

If your child was born between 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012, they are due to start school in September 2016. The deadline for applying for a school place is 15 January 2016.

Apply online

You can still apply after this date but it will be considered a late application. The timetable below provides details of how this will affect your application.

There is no automatic entry from a nursery into a school, and there is no automatic offer of a sibling place (a place for a brother or sister). Parents of children transferring from an infant to junior school must also apply for a school place.

You can still apply after this date but it will be considered a late application. The timetable below provides details of how this will affect your application.

You cannot apply online after 15 January 2016 deadline day, instead you will need to complete a Paper application form for a primary school place in 2016/17 paper application form 49kb and you can read our Guidance notes on how to complete the primary application form 2016/17 guidance notes on how to complete the application guidance notes on how to complete the application 23kb.

The above deadline also applies if your child is currently attending an infant school and you are applying for them to transfer to a junior school from September 2016. You can apply online for a junior school place up until 15 January 2016 online and after this date by submitting a paper application form paper application form 49kb and you can read our guidance notes on how to complete the application guidance notes on how to complete the application guidance notes on how to complete the application guidance notes on how to complete the application 23kb.

Timetable

Date Action
1 September 2015
  • Please read our A parents guide to applying for a primary school place in Wiltshire 2016/17 Parents guide to applying for a primary school place in 2016/17 4mb before making your application.
  • Some foundation and aided schools with religious admissions criteria will ask you to complete a supplementary application form. These forms are available from the schools directly and must be returned to them by the closing date. Completing a supplementary form does not mean that you have made an application. You must still make an application to Wiltshire Council.
15 January 2016
  • Deadline - All paper and online applications must be received by midnight. If you apply after this time your application will not be considered until all those received before the deadline date have been processed. This means that the outcome of your application may not be decided until the end of June 2015.
January - April 2016
  • The School Admissions Team will liaise with schools and other authorities to make sure that, where possible, parents get a single offer for the highest preference school able to offer a place.
18 April 2016
  • Offer day - Letters are posted to all parents who applied by the 15 January 2016 deadline offering them a place at one school. Parents who apply online will be able to log back on to the online system on national offer day to view the outcome of their application. You will require your username and password which was created at the time of application. It is important that  you keep these details safe as the school admissions team are not able to send duplicate emails to you confirming these details. You will also receive confirmation of the offer by letter.
3 May 2016
  • Deadline for parents to accept the place they have been offered. If you do not respond by this date, we will assume that you do not want the place and it will be offered to another applicant.
  • If you have been refused a place at your preferred school you can appeal.
  • Waiting lists are set up.
20 May 2016
  • We offer spare places and any places which have become available ater the 18 April offer day. These are known as second round offers.
June/July 2016
  • Appeals are heard.
31 August 2016
  • Deadline for receiving any late applications and putting names on waiting lists. Any applications received after this date will be considered as in-year transfer applications.
September 2016
  • Your child starts primary school.
22 July 2017
  • Waiting lists are closed.

Key points

  • It is the responsiblity of the parents/ carers to apply for a school place.
  • You can apply for up to three schools, putting the school you want the most as your first preference. We strongly advise you to use all three preferences.
  • We advise you to name your catchment area school as one of your preferences so that there is more chance of your child being offered a place at one of your preferred schools.
  • Some primary schools in Wiltshire operate as a single school but have two geographical bases. These are known as split site schools. For admission purposes, these sites are treated as separate schools therefore on your application you should name the school site which you are applying for. If you would like to apply for both school sites, you must name each school site as a preference.
  • If you live in Wiltshire and want to apply for a school place outside the county, you must still make your application to Wiltshire Council. We will pass on your application details to the relevant council where it will be considered alongside the other applications for that school.
  • If you do not live in Wiltshire and want to apply for a Wiltshire school, you must make an application to your own local authority. They will then pass on your application details to us. You will not be able to use the Wiltshire online application system or Wiltshire's common paper application form. The only exception to this would be applications from children of UK Service personnel.
  • We will acknowledge receipt of your application. If you do not receive acknowledgement for your paper application within 15 school days of returning it please contact customer services at Wiltshire Council on 01225 713010.
  • All online applicants will receive immediate email confirmation of registration and once the application has been submitted a separate confirmation email will be sent.
