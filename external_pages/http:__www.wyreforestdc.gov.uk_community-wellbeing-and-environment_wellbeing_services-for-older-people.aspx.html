Services for Older People

Below you will find links to other organisations and schemes aimed at improving the quality of life for older people. While we make every effort to ensure the links are up to date we cannot be held responsible for external website. If you do notice a problem or if you are part of an organisation that has not been included please contact us with the details.

Download our Activities For Older People leaflet.

Disabilities

 

North Worcestershire DIAL
A free and confidential information and advice line for anyone regarding impairment or disability

 

Disability Action Wyre Forest
A group who meet to discuss and act on issues affecting those with a disability, generally relating to access.

Health and Wellbeing

 

Adult Social CareInformation on care, independance and paying for care from Worcestershire County Council.

 

Home care
Also known as home support or domiciliary care. Services arranged by Adult Services and Health where someone comes into your home to help with practical and personal tasks such as getting up; washing and toileting; organising meals; shopping; collecting pension money.

 

Dental Services
Provides NHS dental care for patients who need additional support.

Arthritis Care
Learn more about managing your arthritis

 

Stroke Association
Provides advice, information and emotional support for stroke survivors, their families and carers.

 

Prostate Cancer Support Group
Help and support for men with prostate cancer and those who care for them.

 

Early Intervention Dementia Service

Service available to people of any age, registered with a GP in Worcestershire who are experiencing memory problems and do not have a formal diagnosis of dementia.

 

Diabetes UK
Support for people affected by or at risk of diabetes.

Care24
