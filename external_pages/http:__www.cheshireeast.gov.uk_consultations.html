The essence of Cheshire East’s Landscapes – National Trust survey

The National Trust, working alongside Cheshire East Council, is conducting a short survey to better understand the value of our landscape to residents and people within Cheshire East. The area being asked about covers the Northern half of the borough, from Congleton up (a map of the area is given within the survey).

Knowing what you feel is special or important about this area, and understanding how it plays a role in your lives, will help the National Trust and partner organisations  manage this landscape more effectively.

Cheshire East children’s centre consultation

Cheshire East council is proposing to de-designate 4 of the current 11 children’s centres in Cheshire East. De-designation means not having registered children’s centres in the specified buildings funded by the council, although this does not prevent the delivery of children’s centre services, or related early childhood services, from the specified buildings by other means. The children’s centres that we are proposing to de-designate are Broken Cross in Macclesfield, Knutsford, Nantwich & Rural and Sandbach.

More information about  children's centres consultation.  You can respond to this consultation via any of the following means:

  • Complete the Cheshire East children's centre consultation survey
  • Write to us at Research and Consultation, Cheshire East Council, Westfields, Sandbach, CW11 1HZ
  • Email us at RandC@cheshireeast.gov.uk.

This consultation will close on Friday 12th February 2016.

Pre-budget report

The Council has issued its Pre-Budget Report setting out proposals for change for 2016/17. This report provides stakeholders with the opportunity to understand the strategy behind the 2016/2017 Budget proposals. The report contains an update on the Budget Setting process, as well as providing a summary of proposed changes to the current budget, and how this may affect services in the future.

  • More information about the Pre Budget Report
  • Provide feedback on the Pre Budget Report

This consultation will close on Monday 15th February 2016. All feedback is presented to Cabinet and Council to inform the final decisions made on delivering these proposals and the overall Budget.
