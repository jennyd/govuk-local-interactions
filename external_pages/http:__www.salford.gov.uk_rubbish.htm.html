Rubbish and recycling

Find out when your household bins will be collected

Please enter your street name or full postcode. Once you have selected your property a list of collection dates will be shown for the bins below:

  • Food and garden waste (pink lid and outdoor food bin)
  • Paper and cardboard (blue bin)
  • Glass, cans, plastic bottles (brown bin)
  • Household waste (black bin)

Business waste customers

To find out when your business waste bins will be emptied, please online enquiry form for details.

These pages provide information on arrangements for rubbish and recycling facilities in Salford:

  • Recycling - find out more about your kerbside recycling collections and what you can and cannot recycle
  • Domestic refuse - find out more about your household waste collection service
  • Business waste - find out more about our waste collection and recycling service for businesses
  • Bulky items - find out how to dispose of large items such as old sofas or washing machines

Need more compostable liners?

Simply tie an empty liner to the lid of your pink lidded bin or outdoor food bin and place out for collection as normal. We'll then deliver some more liners for free.

Need extra recycling bins?

If you need additional recycling bins or an indoor kitchen caddy (including compostable liners) you can order them free of charge using our online reporting form.

Revive your garden
