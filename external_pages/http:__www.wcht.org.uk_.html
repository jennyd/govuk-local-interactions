Welcome to Watford Community Housing Trust

We are a housing association providing homes and services to over 20 local communities throughout Watford and parts of Three Rivers. We work closely with residents and surrounding communities to ensure we deliver high quality homes and services. We own and manage nearly 5000 affordable homes and are regulated by the Homes and Communities Agency. 

We hope you will find everything that you are looking for on this website but if not please email us at enquiries@wcht.org.uk
