Homeswapper Scheme

 

Swap and Move - Helping tenants swap homes

 

Are you looking to swap and move homes?

 

If you are an existing tenant of Ashfield District Council, any Housing Association or another Council, you may be able to exchange your home with another tenant. A home exchange can be a quick and easy way of finding a more suitable home; it may mean that you do not have a lengthy wait on our home transfer list. More information about the Swap and Move Scheme can be found by clicking this link

 

Whether you want to downsize, live nearer to family, find a larger home, or move closer to your new job, use swapandmove.co.uk, the online service helping social tenants to swap their homes locally and nationally.

 

It’s easy to...

 

Swap and move

  • Visit www.swapandmove.co.uk
  • Enter details of your current home and the home you want
  • See your possible swaps!

 

Please note that when registering you will be asked for your email address and telephone number. By submitting your details you will be providing this information for display on the Swap and Move website and you may be contacted by parties interested in your home.
