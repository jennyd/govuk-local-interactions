Report a dog issue

You can report the following dog related issues online:

Report dog mess

  • Report full or damaged dog mess bins (or litter bins)
  • Report nuisance barking

Find out more information about our campaign to tackle dog fouling.

Dog warden service

We employ a dog warden to collect any lost dogs.

You can also report dog issues by calling or emailing us:

  • Email: contact@sandwell.gov.uk
  • Call: 0121 569 6625 Monday-Friday 8am-8pm, Saturday 8.30am-1.30pm.
