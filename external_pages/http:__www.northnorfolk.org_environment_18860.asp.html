Abandoned vehicles

Abandoning a vehicle is a criminal offence and is dealt with by the Environmental Protection team. Use our online report form, below, to let us know about an abandoned vehicle.

We are not responsible for untaxed vehicles; they should be reported to the DVLA.

Once a vehicle has been classed as abandoned, a DVLA check will be carried out to identify the last registered keeper. A letter will be sent to the keeper advising that unless a response is received the vehicle will be collected and destroyed.

Please note - if the vehicle is parked on private land we can only accept reports from the landowner.
