Direct Debit is the easiest way to pay and is safe and secure.

Set Up a Direct Debit

Changing a Direct Debit
If you need to change any details with your Direct Debit then let us know by filling out the online form again. From this, we'll then be able to update your details.

You can pay monthly, pay the full amount in one go or pay half yearly. You will automatically be given monthly instalments. To change this, call 01622 602003.

Pay Online

Paying online is quick, easy and secure.

You can pay by debit card. We accept Visa Debit and Maestro.

You can pay monthly, pay the full amount in one go or pay half yearly. You will automatically be given monthly instalments but if you would like to change call 01622 602003.

You can pay using our automated payment line.

Call 01622 602544.

To make a payment, choose option 1. You will need to have your Council Tax reference number ready and we will need to know how much you want to pay.

You can pay by debit card. We accept Visa Debit and Maestro.

You can pay monthly, pay the full amount in one go or pay half yearly. You will automatically be given monthly instalments but if you would like to change this call 01622 602003.

You can pay at the Maidstone Gateway in King Street.

You can pay by Standing Order.

Our bank details are:

Bank: Lloyds Bank
Account Name: Maidstone Borough Council
Sort Code: 30-00-00
Account Number: 02390027

Please also quote your Council Tax reference number (this starts with a 1).

The payment must be with the council by the tenth of each month.
