Contact Pollution Control

Service requests, enquiries, comments and complaints can be sent to the Pollution Control Team using this form

Complaints about certain potential statutory nuisances and general enquiries about air and land pollution issues may be made using the form below.

The potential statutory nuisances investigated by Pollution Control:

  • Smoke emitted from premises
  • Fumes or gases emitted from (private/domestic dwellings)
  • Dust, steam, smell or other effluvia arising on industrial, trade or business premises
  • Any accumulation or deposit (but not dumped rubbish)
  • Noise emitted from premises or equipment in the street 
  • Light (e.g. domestic security lighting, but not street lighting or reflected sunlight)

Domestic Noise Nuisances

If the noise is from a domestic property you should report the incident on the Police non-emergency number 101. Noise arising from domestic violence incidents or other violent confrontations must be reported to the Police on 999.  Please do not call 999 except in an emergency.

If the noise is from a commercial, industrial or licensed premises or activity please contact Pollution Control on 0115 915 2020.

Process

Enquiries and complaints alleging statutory nuisances will usually be responded to within 5 working days or sooner.

However, please be aware that the process of investigating a complaint alleging a statutory nuisance, through to resolving the complaint, may take from a few days to several weeks, and in cases where legal action has to be taken, sometimes up to six months.

Please note that we are generally unable to deal with anonymous reports and complaints alleging statutory nuisances.
