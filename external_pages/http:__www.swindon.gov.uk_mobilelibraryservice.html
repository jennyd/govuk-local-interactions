Find out about the mobile library service

Due to essential servicing, the Mobile Library will not operate on Wednesday, 10 February 2016.

For details of the mobile library routes, please refer to the timetable below:

  • Mobile library routes 2016 (PDF)

If you have any queries regarding this please contact the Strategic Manager

Tel: 07884 270448
E-mail: lbolton@swindon.go.uk

The Mobile Library provides a comprehensive service to outlying rural and urban areas of the Borough of Swindon including:

  • Books (adults' and children’s)
  • Large print items
  • Audio books
  • A request and information service
  • OWL ®Micro Energy Monitors to borrow

The service covers many different routes and serves multiple locations including:

  • Public stops
  • Schools
  • Sheltered accommodation

Accessibility

The mobile library has full wheelchair access.
