Vehicle access - kerbs

Share this on:
  • facebook
  • twitter
  • google+
  • email

Jump to:

  • Key contacts

We can construct vehicle access over pavements (crossovers) at the request of residents, this page explains how to apply.

Provision of crossovers may also include 'access protection markings', which are white 'H' shaped lines painted on a road, in front of an access to brin dropped kerbs to other road users attention. There may be a charge payable for this service and in some locations, such as on major highways, planning permission may be required.

Further information may be requested by contacting City and neighbourhoods using the contact details on this page.
