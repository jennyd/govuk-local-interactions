Adopt a future

Children need to be adopted when they can no longer be cared for by their birth parents. Most have had a difficult start in life and need the stability of a permanent, loving home.

You can make a difference

Children need parents to love and nurture them; help overcome their individual background difficulties, understand who they are and ensure they grow up in a safe and secure environment.

This is where you come in.

Through adoption you become the child's legal parent; taking on all the legal and practical responsibilities and the joys this entails, throughout their childhood and beyond.

When you adopt you provide a child with a forever family in which they can grow, develop and have the best chance of fulfilling their potential.

Your family will be supported along the way by our lifelong adoption support service, who are there whenever a little extra help is needed.

You don't need specialist qualifications to adopt a child or children and you can be single, older, gay, not a homeowner and already have children living at home. If you have enthusiasm and patience, you could be the one!

If you think you could give a Suffolk child the care they dream about, contact the Fostering and Adoption Team or attend one of their drop-in information events happening around the county.
