Find a Home

Follow this link to HomePoint if you would like to rent one of our homes
Mutual exchange? Follow This Link to Homeswapper

 

If you are interested in becoming one of our tenants, you must be registered with Shropshire Homepoint. Follow this link to the Shropshire Homepoint website.

We do not hold a waiting list for our properties. HomePoint is a Choice-Based Lettings agency that has replaced the old council waiting lists.  The purpose of Choice-Based Lettings is to give you more choice in the properties you are interested in and the areas in which you would like to live.

If you are registered with Shropshire Homepoint and your circumstances have changed you must contact them direct.
