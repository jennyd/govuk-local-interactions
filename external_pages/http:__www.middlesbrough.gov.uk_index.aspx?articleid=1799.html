Preparing for emergencies

The preparations in place should there be a major emergency or disaster

The chances of being caught up in a major emergency or disaster in Middlesbrough are low.  However, emergencies are usually the result of a series of improbable events occurring in sequence which cause a major incident.

Some of the emergencies we might have to deal with in Middlesbrough are:

  • Flooding
  • Adverse weather e.g. heavy snow, high winds etc
  • Large chemical fires, toxic gas releases or explosions from the industrial activity in Middlesbrough.
  • Power failures
  • Town centre evacuation
