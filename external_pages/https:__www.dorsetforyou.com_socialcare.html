Adult Social Care Annual Report - 'The Local Account' 2014/15

The local account is a means by which residents hold their local authority adult social care department to account. It is an annual publication that sets out how we have done, where we need to improve, including details of what we have spent, what we plan to spend and what our future plans and priorities are.
