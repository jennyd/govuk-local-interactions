Allotments

Allotment gardening is one of the healthiest pastimes anyone can become involved in.

You can grow good food, it's good exercise and you get to meet new people.

We have some allotment sites with toilet facilities.

Some allotments have raised beds for less able gardeners and some have improved roads and paths that allow for wheelchair access.

Some sites have been set up as Community Agriculture Projects.

There is no barrier to allotment gardening in Sandwell - you don't need any particular knowledge or skill. Tips on getting started with an allotment are available.

To find out more about allotment gardening in Sandwell please email amy_robinson@sandwell.gov.uk or call 0121 569 3440.
