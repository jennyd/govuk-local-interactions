Council housing

Herefordshire Council do not own or manage any homes in the county. All affordable housing is owned and managed by housing associations. The main housing associations which own stock in Herefordshire are:

  • Bromford Housing Group
  • Fortis Living Housing Association previously Festival Housing
  • The Guinness Partnership: Affordable Housing Association
  • Herefordshire Housing Limited
  • Sanctuary Housing
  • Shropshire Housing Group
  • Stonewater previously Marches Housing Association
  • Two Rivers Housing
  • Kemble, part of the WM Housing Group

If you would like to apply to register for housing in Herefordshire, visit the Home Point website.
