Parks and open spaces

There are many parks, play areas and open spaces for your to use and enjoy around the District.

Our interactive map can be used to show you their locations:


Park Banner Image

The Council manages a number of pitches and open spaces that are available for football, commercial and community events, they are as follows:

  • Ayscoughfee Hall Museum and Gardens, Churchgate, Spalding, Lincs PE11 2RA
  • Castle Sports Complex Playing Field, Albion Street, Spalding, Lincs PE11 2AJ
  • Monks House Lane Playing Field, Spalding, Lincs PE11 3LH
  • Sir Halley Stewart Playing Field, Winfrey Avenue, Spalding, Lincs PE11 1DA

Our Events and Football Bookings (link) gives further information together with advice on Event Planning.

For more details please contact the Community Development Team on 01775 761161 or email: community@sholland.gov.uk
