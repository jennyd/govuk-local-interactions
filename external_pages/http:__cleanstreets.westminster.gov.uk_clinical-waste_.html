Clinical waste

Clinical waste like syringes, needles, bandages, medication etc from clinics and health centres should never be disposed in the same way as general commercial waste. Apart from the obvious risks that this type of waste poses to public health, clinical waste presents a significant hazard to staff handling waste and recyclable materials.

If you require a clinical waste collection from your business please call us on 0800 3580514

Email: pestcontrol@westminster.gov.uk
