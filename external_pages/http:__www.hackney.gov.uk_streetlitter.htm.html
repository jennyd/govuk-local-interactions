Litter and street cleaning

Clean streets improve the image and atmosphere of a road. Although it is impossible to guarantee that there will be no litter at any time, we aim to ensure that all our streets and markets are kept as clean as possible.

Report litter

Report a litter problem

We have over 1000 litter bins and spend over £1 million a year cleaning the streets. You must not drop litter. You can be fined up to £2,500 for littering.

Businesses' responsibilities

Business owners should ensure their customers and employees do not drop litter within 100 metres of their premises. You should display posters about environmental awareness or provide more bins around your premises. You could be fined up to £2500 if litter is being dropped within 100 metres of your premises.
