Parks, Open Spaces and Countryside Sites

The Land of the Fanns Landscape Partnership is a project developed in conjunction with the councils of Havering, Barking and Dagenham, Thurrock, Essex and Brentwood, as well as the Forestry Commission and Thames Chase Trust. Supported by the Heritage Lottery Fund, the project aims to promote and restore the "Land of the Fanns", an area that extends from Dagenham to Stanford-Le-Hope and from Brentwood to the Thames, and restore the landscape and highlight the area's important natural and historical features. As part of this, a short questionnaire has been developed to help understand local people's relationship to the landscape. If you would like to help and give your thoughts,please complete this survey: Land of the Fanns Survey.
