Licensing

Introduction to licensing

Please note that it is an offence knowingly or recklessly to make a false statement in connection with an application and the maximum fine for which a person is liable on summary conviction for the offence.

Important information relating to publication of personal data

In respect of any applications, representations, objections or other documents received by the Council that are published on this website from 6 August 2008, the Council will take reasonable steps to remove personal (but not business) telephone numbers and email addresses, and signatures, before publication.

Planning applications have been edited in this way since May 2006, but other documents published before 6 August 2008 have not been. Anyone who wishes their signature, personal telephone number(s) or personal email address(es) to be removed from documents published before 6 August 2008 (or planning documents before May 2006) should contact the Council's Assistant Data Protection Officer on 023 8028 5588 or email lisa.clark@nfdc.gov.uk.

New Forest District Council Licensing Services is responsible for the licensing of:

Registers and Pending Applications,
Licensing Fees,
Licensing Act 2003 (Licensing Act 2003),
Temporary Event Notices,
Proprietary and Private Members Clubs,
Busking,
Restaurants,
Theatres,
Cinemas,
Late Night Refreshment Premises,
Gaming, Betting, Lotteries, (Gambling Act 2005)
Casinos,
Bingo Halls,
Betting Office,
Arcades,
Gaming Machines,
Lottery Licence,
Sex Establishment Licences,
Taxis,
Hackney Carriage Vehicle,
Private Hire Vehicle,
Drivers,
Private Hire Operator Licence,
Taxi Ranks,
Wheelchair Accessible Vehicles,
Boatmen,
Pleasure Boats,
Street Collection,
House to House Collection,
Scrap Metal Dealers and
Animal Premises Licensing

Licensing Online - EU Services Directive - As part of the EU Services Directive, you can now apply online and pay for many licences we offer.

Licensing Services work closely with New Forest District Council Environmental Health and Environmental Protection.
