Wolverhampton Youth Service Programme Fund 2016

Apply for the Youth Development fund here.

What is the Wolverhampton Programme Fund 2016?

From January 2016 to April 2016 the Council is offering the opportunity for youth organisations to apply for funding of up to £2,000 to undertake projects with young people in additional to the current open access youth provision in their local neighbourhoods and communities.  Funding of £60,000 is available to local community and voluntary organisations during this period.

The fund is available for youth organisations to deliver additional activities and projects in areas where there is a current lack of provision or a particular need.  It is also available for working with young people who would particularly benefit from open access youth work opportunities.  The fund is intended to provide young people with the ability to be involved in the planning of projects as well as the application and decision making process.
