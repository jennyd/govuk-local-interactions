2 Year Old Free Early Learning and Childcare

Information about free early learning and childcare for 2, 3 and 4 year olds.

Over 1,200 two year olds in Stockport are eligible for up to 570 hours per year of FREE childcare at nurseries, pre-schools or childminders across the borough.

If you receive certain Benefits or have an annual household income below £16,190 then call in to your local Sure Start Children’s Centre and the friendly staff will check if a free place is available to you and help register your child. Or you can telephone 0161 217 6028. 

For more detailed information, please see 'In This Section'.

You can also find Information about early education and childcare for your 3 or 4 year olds.

Their school or setting may be entitled to £300 per year to improve the education of your child? For more information visit the Early Years Pupil Premium page.
