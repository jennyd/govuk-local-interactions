Car Parking Charges and Permits

Prices

Payment can be made for tickets at the machine by cash or by card using a mobile phone.

  • Up to two hours - 20p
  • Up to three hours - 60p
  • Up to four hours - 80p
  • More than four hours - £1.60
  • Weekly tickets (valid for seven days) - £6.00
  • Permits are available - see below for prices and how to apply

 

The machines are open from 7.00 a.m.  Early customers wishing to park in to the charging period are advised to purchase a ticket by phone or, if a regular event, to buy a season ticket. 

 

Please note that disabled parking is free for any period of time in any bay providing that a valid blue badge is displayed.

Permits

To apply for a permit please call APCOA Services Group on 01452 521284 or e-mail admin.gloucester@apcoa.com for an application form.

Please note it will take up to five working days for your permit to be processed.

Permit Prices

Monthly permit£10
3 monthly permit£30
6 monthly permit£60
Annual permit£120
Overnight permit£50 per annum (only for use between 5.00 p.m. and 9.00 a.m.)

Monthly payment by direct debit is available.

All prices are inclusive of VAT and correct at the time of publishing.
