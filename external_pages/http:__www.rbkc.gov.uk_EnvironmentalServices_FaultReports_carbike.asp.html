Fault Report: Abandoned cars and bicycles

To report an abandoned car or bicycle in the street please fill in this form:

Required fields are marked with an asterisk *.

Problem Type

Please select one of the following for the type of problem you wish to report.

* I wish to report an:

Location

Where is it? Please type the name of the street or road.

To make sure we find the car or bicycle, please tell us the nearest landmark, ie if it is close to a particular house, shop, or road junction.

Further details

In order to help us deal with your report quickly, please fill in as many details as possible.

Please give the time and date that you first noticed the abandoned car or bicycle.

Your details

You do not need to give us your name but it would be helpful to the Council to have a contact telephone number or email address in case we need further information to follow up this report.

Thank-you for taking the time to report this problem. If you wish to be kept updated about progress on this matter, please choose yes from the options below.

* Keep informed

Data Protection

Any information you provide will be used by the Council for the purpose of dealing with this report and will not be disclosed to the public. This information may be provided to organisations working on behalf of the Council to rectify the problem.
