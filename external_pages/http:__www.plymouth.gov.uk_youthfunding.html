Funding opportunities for youth groups in plymouth

Targeted Youth Work Fund

Targeted Youth Work Fund supports projects aimed at reducing vulnerability and risk taking behaviour within Plymouth geographical areas.

Applications are welcomed from organisations that:

  • Support young people leaving care
  • Work with young offenders
  • Support young carers
  • Projects that are created to address unemployment and worklessness
  • Support to young people who are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless

The maximum amount of funding that can be applied for is £7,500.

All applications are scrutinised by a young people's assessment panel and successful applicants will be expected to provide monitoring and evaluation reports.

Plymouth Aspirations Trust

The Plymouth Aspirations Trust aims to assist individual young people in Plymouth who have been recognised by a supporting organisation as having an aspiration to achieve higher and greater things towards recognising their wider aspiration to include sporting, musical, artistic, vocational or academic endeavours.

Applications are welcomed from any Young Person aged 11 to 19* (up to 25 with additional needs) who has been in continuous residence in Plymouth for three years preceding the date of the award.

*Younger ages may be considered in exceptional circumstances.

Applications will be reviewed up to four times a year.

There are four stages for your application as follows:

  1. Submission of application form and any additional supporting information
  2. Review by Young Peoples Panel with recommendations to Trustees
  3. Review and Approval by Trustees
  4. Communication of decision to applicant

A panel of trained young people from across Plymouth will be supporting the Trustees in making decisions on all applications. Typically bursaries will range from £100 to £1000.

If you feel that any of these funding streams will support your project with young people, please contact Youth Services on 01752 306596 or email youth.admin@plymouth.gov.uk.
