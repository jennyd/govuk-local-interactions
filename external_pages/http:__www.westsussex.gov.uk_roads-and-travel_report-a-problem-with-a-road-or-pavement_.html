Report a problem with a road or pavement

Report issues such as potholes, road flooding, overgrown vegetation, street lighting and damaged pavements.

  1. Report problems online
  2. Street lights and traffic lights
  3. Rights of Way
  4. Emergency reports
  5. General enquiries
  6. View and track online reports

1 Report problems online

Report faults or problems with roads, pavements and verges online using our Love West Sussex website. You can report problems such as:

  • potholes
  • non-illuminated bollards
  • road flooding
  • overgrown vegetation
  • damaged pavements.
Report a problem online using Love West Sussex (external link)

Smartphone users – download one of our free mobile apps

Please note any emergency reports must be reported by phone. 

All reports received online are not monitored at weekends or on bank holidays.

2 Street lights and traffic lights

Problems with street lights or traffic lights should be reported to our contractors:

  • Report street lighting problems to SSE
  • Report faulty traffic lights to Telent

3 Rights of Way

To report problems with West Sussex Public Rights of Way (such as footpaths and bridleways):

  • use our Public Rights of Way feedback form

4 Emergency reports

Phone 01243 642105 to report emergency issues such as:

  • dangerous potholes
  • fallen trees that are blocking a road or pavement
  • any other obstruction or spillage blocking a road or pavement
  • drain/inspection covers that are missing.

All reports received online are not monitored at weekends or on bank holidays.

5 General enquiries

Complete our online form to request information or raise a general enquiry.

Send a general enquiry

6 View and track online reports

You can track reports made online using Love West Sussex to see if the work has been completed:

  • View the status of reports you have logged online (sign in and then click 'My reports' from the menu bar)
  • View all public reports logged by anyone
  • View a map of reported problems
