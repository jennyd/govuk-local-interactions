Pay your Council Tax

Online

Pay your Council Tax online here

*Please have your account reference number handy which you will find on your Council Tax bill.

Paying by direct debit

You can pay by direct debit which is the easiest and most secure way of paying your Council Tax bill.  As well as helping us to keep the Council Tax down you can be guaranteed that you will not miss a payment.

You have a choice of when you pay.  Direct debits can be paid over 10 or 12 months on either the 1st, 15th or 30th of the month, whichever suits you best.

You can change your instructions whenever you wish.  If at any time in the future you want to stop paying by direct debit, contact us and your bank to cancel your direct debit instruction.

If you wish to set up a direct debit, complete our online form and one of our customer advisors will contact you to set this up over the phone. Alternatively, please call us on 0345 6053011 to set up a direct debit or request a mandate.

Making cash payments

Payment by cheque, credit card, debit card can be made at any of the One Stop Shops in Leek, Biddulph or Cheadle.

Visit our 'contact us' section for details of the latest opening times

We provide a plastic swipe card for making payment at specified Paypoint outlets cash cannot be accepted at council offices.

Click here to order a payment card

You should pay the instalments on or before the dates shown on your Bill / Special Payment Arrangement Letter.

Over the phone

You can make payment using a debit card (Visa Debit, Visa Electron, Maestro) or credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Eurocard) by phoning 0345 605 3016 (24hr phone line).

There is a charge of £2.50 for each payment by credit card.

Standing Order

Ask your Bank/Building Society to make payment to the council’s account using the following details:

Bank NatWest
Branch Derby Street, Leek
Sort Code 57-92-67
Credit Staffordshire Moorlands District Council
Account Number 00000000

It is essential that your Bank/Building Society quotes your account reference number, as shown on your Bill / Special Payment Arrangement letter, with every payment.

By post

Cheques should be made payable to ’Staffordshire Moorlands District Council’ and posted to Staffordshire Moorlands District Council , PO Box 136, Buxton, SK17 1AQ.

Please write your account number on the reverse of your cheque. Post-dated cheques cannot be accepted.
