Pay or Appeal a Parking Ticket (PCN)

You can use this site to pay for a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), to find out more information about the contravention, or to appeal.

Please note that the online appeal system does not allow supporting evidence to be added. If you wish to supply supporting evidence (e.g. copy of ticket/both sides of disabled badge) in relation to your appeal then please either send your appeal in writing to the address quoted on the reverse of your Penalty Charge Notice or via email to parking.appeals@stratford-dc.gov.uk. If you wish to supply supporting evidence with a Notice to Owner please complete the form and return to this office only.
