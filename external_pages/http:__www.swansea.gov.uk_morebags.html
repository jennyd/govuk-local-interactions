Need more recycling bags?

We want to make it as easy to recycle as possible. To help make sure you always have enough bags and boxes these are available in a number of places.

Green, pink and food bags

More recycling bags can be requested at the kerbside by using the re-order tag inside each roll. Place this on your bag on collection day as you start running low and the collection crews should then deliver a replacement roll or pack for you.

'Reorder packs' of green and pink bags and replacement food waste bags can also be collected from a number of locations, with a full list available below. Re-order packs contain 6 bags and a re-order tag for you to place out to request more from the crews. 

Garden waste bags

Reusable garden waste bags are available for a small cost from the Civic Centre, all district housing offices and libraries. Weighted bags which are less likely to blow away are available for £1.50 each or non-weighted bags for 75p each.

Writing your address on your bag will help crews return them after emptying. A maximum of 10 garden bags can be placed out for any single collection.

Food waste bins

Large and small food waste bins are available to collect from all district housing offices and libraries.
