Online Graffiti Reporting Form

This form is for reporting Graffiti issues only. If you would rather make an enquiry or suggestion, please do so using the website feedback form.

Once we have received your request we will remove offensive/hate graffiti on Council owned property within 4 working hours and other graffiti within 5 working days. Where we are not able to remove the graffiti we will inform you of what is happening within 5 working days. If the graffiti is on private land will offer to remove it within 5 working days

Please complete the sections below by entering information into the appropriate boxes, and press the SUBMIT button to send your information.

The areas marked by * are mandatory, and must be completed.

We ask you to complete these areas so that we can contact you if we are unable to process your request or if the information supplied is not clear or complete.
