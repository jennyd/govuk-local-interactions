Animal welfare

Web Development (web.development@kirklees.gov.uk) Last updated - June 2014
On this page
  • Animal welfare,
  • Dead animals,
  • Keeping chickens or animals in the garden,

Animal welfare

  • We investigate allegations about the welfare of animals on farm premises, complaints about farm animals in transit or at livestock markets and reports of dead farm animals.
  • We investigate allegations about animals kept in or on premises licensed by Kirklees such as dangerous wild animals, animals in pet shops, riding establishments, catteries and boarding kennels.
  • We check on the welfare of animals which are part of visiting circuses.
  • We also offer advice on setting up a smallholding and on the movement of farm animals.
  • Contact the Animal Welfare Team:
    • Email: animal.welfare@kirklees.gov.uk
    • Telephone 01484 226440

Dead animals

  • We provide a free service to remove dead animals from all public accessible areas.
  • More information:
    • Email: waste@kirklees.gov.uk
    • Telephone 01484 414700

Keeping chickens or animals in the garden

  • There may be something about keeping animals on your house deeds or tenancy agreement, in which case you should consult a solicitor or your landlord.
  • The animals must not cause a nuisance from either smell or noise.
  • If a neighbour's animals are causing a nuisance, contact Pollution & Noise Control:
    • Email: pollution@kirklees.gov.uk
    • Telephone 01484 414739
