Garden waste collections

Last updated: 07/02/2016
 
Live chat:

 

You can use our fortnightly garden waste collection service for things like grass clippings, weeds, leaves and prunings.  There is a charge for this service.

What else can I do with my garden waste?

You can also take garden waste to our household waste and recycling centres for free or compost it at home.  We can’t collect garden waste with your household rubbish.  

Buy a composter or green Johanna

How do I sign up for or renew garden waste collections? 

You need to buy a bin or bag from us and pay for the collections.  If you’re renewing, you can keep using your existing bin or bag and just pay for the collections.

Please read our garden waste containers terms and conditions and garden waste service terms and conditions before signing up.

  • Leaves or organic sweepings
  • Tree, shrub and rose prunings
  • Branches (up to 30cm long and 2.5cm diameter, e.g. broom handle width)
  • Cut flowers
  • Weeds

When will my garden be waste collected?

Garden waste collections are fortnightly.  Your garden waste will be collected on same day as your rubbish, on the alternate week to your recycling.

View garden waste collection days

How do I report a missed garden waste collection?

You can report a missed collection online.

Can I get a refund during the winter or when I’m on holiday?

No. the service runs for the whole year.  We no longer offer six month subscriptions.

Where can I get a water butt?

Water butts and other water saving devices are available from South West Water.
