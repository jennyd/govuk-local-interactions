Street care and cleaning

We offer a range of services to help keep the Wycombe district clean and tidy.

Please note: Transport for Buckinghamshire (TfB), on behalf of Buckinghamshire County Council, maintains roads in the district. See Buckinghamshire County Council's Roads, highways and pavements maintenance page.

Report dumped rubbish→

We remove rubbish dumped on public land, also known as flytipping.

Report an abandoned vehicle→

We remove vehicles abandoned on public land.

Report a dead animal→

We remove dead animals found in public areas.

Public toilets→

We maintain council owned public toilets.

Report street litter→

We clear up litter in the Wycombe district.

Report graffiti→

We remove graffiti and flyposters from council property.
