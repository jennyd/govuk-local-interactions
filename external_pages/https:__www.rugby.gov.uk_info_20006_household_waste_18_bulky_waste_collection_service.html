Bulky waste collection service

  • Check your next bin day
Print this page

The council offers a bulky waste collection service, for large items that cannot be collected in the bins provided. We cannot collect broken glass.

Pay for bulky waste collection  

The first 3 items are charged at £7.00 per item, and each subsequent item is charged at £4.00.

An example is shown below:

1 Cooker £7.00
1 Armchair £7.00
1 Mattress £7.00
1 Lawnmower £4.00
TOTAL COST: £25.00

However, if you have a significant quantity of bulky items or garden furniture, sheds or other miscellaneous items please call (01788) 533332 before booking a collection. It may be possible to provide you with a quote for the work.

Book bulky waste collection

If you have any queries regarding the service or you wish to book a collection please contact (01788) 533332.
