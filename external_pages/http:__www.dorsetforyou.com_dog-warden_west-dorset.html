Dog warden service in West Dorset

Listen

The West Dorset Dog Warden gives advice, educates and may take formal action, including the service of fixed penalty notices and prosecution in appropriate cases. Dog fouling, stray and lost dogs can be reported in West Dorset online.

How to contact the Dog Warden

Please call us on 01305 251010 for the most immediate response or complete the short online form to report:

Found, lost or stray dogs

Dog fouling

Unreasonable and excessive levels of dog noise

Attacks from dogs on other animals

Breach of Dog Control Orders

Please see the District Council's Stray Dog Charges (pdf, 140kb) (opens in a new window) for this service.

Where and how to walk your dog

Lyme Regis beaches map (pdf, 4Mb) (opens in a new window)

West Bay exclusion order map (pdf, 3Mb) (opens in a new window)

West Bay dogs on leads order (pdf, 22kb) (opens in a new window)

Mistreatment or neglect of dogs

The Dog Warden is not responsible for the investigation of mistreatment or neglect of dogs. If you are concerned about the treatment of a dog you should contact the RSPCA (opens in a new window) on 0300 1234 999.

Further advice

The Dog Warden is happy to speak to members of the public on all dog related issues, if you have any queries please contact the Dog Warden on 01305 251010. Further information can be found in the  District Council's Dog Owners Guide (pdf, 2Mb) (opens in a new window).
