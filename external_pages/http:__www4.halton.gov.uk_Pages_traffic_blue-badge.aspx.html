Blue badge and parking bays

Badge issue fee and validity

  • The Blue Badge issue fee is £10. This will apply to all types of new badge applications and renewals.
  • Subject to entitlement, badges are normally issued for a period of three years.

Apply for a blue badge

  • You can Apply or Renew online for a blue badge​ via www.gov.uk
  • You can apply in person at any One Stop Shop, please download and complete form from this page. Forms can be returned by email to blue.badges@halton.gov.uk or returned by post to any One Stop Shop.

Before your badge can be ordered we will also need:

  • A recent photograph which shows your face clearly and is of passport quality (approximately 3.5cm x 4.5cm).  Photographs will be added to the badge in digital format and returned to you.  Alternatively you may provide us with a digital image that meets the required standards or attend any One Stop Shop office where a photograph will be taken.
  • 2 forms of personal identification – one should be a photograph form of identification and one must show your address.
  • Proof of your eligibility, if you are applying without further assessment – an original letter of entitlement dated within the last 12 months or your annual uprating letter.
  • £10.00 fee payable by cash/cheque at any One Stop Shop or by telephone on 0151 907 8309

Eligible without further assessment

You can get a blue badge under this criteria if: 

  • you receive the Higher Rate of the Mobility Component of the Disability Living Allowance (HRMCDLA) or 8 points or more for the ‘moving around’ part of the Personal Independent Payment
  • you are registered blind (severely sight impaired)
  • you receive a War Pensioner's Mobility Supplement (WPMS)
  • you have been awarded a lump sum benefit at tariffs 1-8 of the Armed Forces Compensation Scheme and certified as having a permanent and substantial disability which causes inability to walk or very considerable difficulty in walking.

If you think you would qualify under this criteria please complete the application form.

Eligible subject to further assessment

You may also be eligible for a blue badge under this criteria if:

  • you drive a vehicle regularly and have a severe disability in both arms and unable to operate, or have considerable difficulty in operating, all or some types of parking meter
  • you have a permanent and substantial disability which causes inability to walk or very considerable difficulty in walking

If you think you would qualify under this criteria please complete the application form 

Application for a child under 3 years of age

You may apply for a child below the age of 3 under this criteria if:

  • on account of a condition, the child must always be accompanied by bulky medical equipment which cannot be carried around without great difficulty
  • on account of a condition, the child must always be kept near a motor vehicle so that, if necessary, treatment for that condition can be given in the vehicle or the child can be taken quickly in the vehicle to a place where such treatment can be given

If you think your child would meet this criteria please complete the application form.

Organisational eligibility

  • An organisational badge may be issued to establishments whose responsibility includes the care and transportation of disabled people who would themselves meet the eligibility criteria for a badge should they apply individually.
  • The badge should cater for an adequate number of people with the required degree of disability. Where relatively few people meet the eligibility criteria for a badge in the organisation, it would be preferable for the disabled people themselves to apply for badges, rather than have one issued to an organisation.

If you think your organisation would meet this criteria please complete the application form.  The application should be returned with the following information:

  • Covering letter on headed notepaper
  • Copy of CSCI registration. If a voluntary organisation then a copy of constitution or statement of purpose will suffice.
  • Copy of company stamp or logo (to be inserted onto badge in place of photograph)
  • Photocopy of tax disc(s) as proof that the organisation has vehicles licensed under Disabled Passenger Vehicle (DPV) class or alternatively a declaration on the organisations headed paper to confirm that they are an organisation concerned with the care of disabled people and that they will be using the vehicle solely for the purpose of transporting those people.

Blue Badge Renewals

You can renew your badge up to two months prior to the expiry date shown on your current badge. To avoid being without a badge it is recommended that you apply as soon as possible, particularly if you are applying under the ‘subject to further assessment’ criteria.

If you require any further assistance please email us at blue.badges@halton.gov.uk

Disabled Parking Bays

In order to be eligible for an advisory disabled parking bay you must:

  • hold a blue badge
  • have a vehicle registered at the address, and the designated driver must also live at the address
  • be in receipt of higher mobility allowance
  • In addition, there should not be an alternative means of off-street parking or space within the property boundary to provide off-street parking for the applicant

If you wish to apply for a disabled parking bay, please complete the Application for a disabled parking bay Form.
