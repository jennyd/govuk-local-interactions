Council Housing - services for tenants

Guildford is situated within the South East of England and is surrounded by areas of outstanding natural beauty.

We manage approximately 5,150 properties across the borough. Area managers work in partnership with community safety wardens to create successful and attractive neighbourhoods where people want to live.

To apply for a council home in Guildford you need to join the housing register.

Services for tenants

How to report a repair for your council home

  • Report online
  • Email us - repairs@guildford.gov.uk
  • By phone - call 01483 444222 (please call this number if you have an urgent or emergency repair)

Tenant involvement

Our neighbourhood and housing management team offers a wide range of services and resources for our tenants, including tenant involvement, adapted housing, garage hire, mutual exchanges and anti social behaviour.

We also keep our tenants up-to-date with our newsletter,  Contact Point [3Mb], and a Facebook page. You can also read our annual newsletter for council leaseholders and equity share owners:  Leasehold Link. [2Mb]
