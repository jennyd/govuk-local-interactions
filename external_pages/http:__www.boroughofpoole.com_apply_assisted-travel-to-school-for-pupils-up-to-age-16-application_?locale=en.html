Assisted Travel to School for Pupils up to Age 16 Application

Transport assistance is provided for pupils living within the Borough of Poole who meet the Home to School Transport criteria. If you meet these criteria please submit an application form. Please note there is a separate application form for grammar schools.

You can also read the Home to School Transport Policy.

Grammar Schools

Please note that following changes in legislation and guidance (Education and Inspections Act 2006), the Borough of Poole no longer has a policy of providing discretionary transport assistance to grammar schools.

The secondary school transport assistance criteria also apply to grammar schools. However, there is also the option to purchase a subsidised bus pass for grammar schools if you live more than 3 miles away from the school, as measured by our Geographical Information System. Please complete the Grammar school application.
