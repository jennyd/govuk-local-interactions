Children's Services (Family Information Service)

  • Search - Torbay Directory


Welcome to Children's Services. We hold a wide range of information for families in Torbay who are either accessing our services or looking for available groups, events, activities, advice and other support available.
The information we hold is managed by the Family Information Service Team, much of which can be found on the pages listed below. Other methods of communication include the Torbay Directory , Facebook, Twitter or in person at the Family Information Reception on Union Street, Torquay opposite Connections.
If you would like any assistance in finding information on a specific service, you can also contact us via our freephone telephone number 0800 328 5974 or by using the contact form at the bottom of this page.

Childcare

Information on the various types of childcare available in Torbay which includes nurseries, pre-schools, childminders and out of school care.

Education

Information on education and learning services within Torbay for children, young people and adults.
 

Early Help

The Early Help Service guides and assists children and families and professionals through the process of getting the right help to meet need.

Adoption & Fostering

Information on the care of children and young people in Torbay. Including details on Children Looked After and Torbay Virtual School.
 

Integrated Youth Support Service

Support provided to help young people to grow and develop to reach their goals and aspirations as they enter adulthood.

Services & Support

Services, support and information available for children and their families in Torbay.
 

Strategies & Policies

Documents that outline the current business priorities for Children’s Services and Partners and describe our operating process and governance structures.

Safeguarding Children Boards 

The Devon & Torbay Safeguarding Children Boards brings together all the main organisations who work with children and families in Devon & Torbay.
 

Social Work Careers

Information and advice on working in Social Work for Torbay Council.

Children's Service Feedback

We believe everyone has the right to express their views and raise concerns. Providing your views will help us to shape and improve the service we deliver.
