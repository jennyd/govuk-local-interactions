Housing Grants and Loans

The Council's policy for helping home owners and tenants improve, repair or adapt their properties is set out in the Private Sector Housing Investment Policy (pdf 372Kb)

 

The types of assistance available as follows:

  • Mandatory Disabled Facilities Grant (DFG)
  • Adapt Grant
  • Additional Adaptation Assistance – Discretionary
  • Emergency Repair Grant - Discretionary
  • Home Repair Assistance - Discretionary
  • Safety & Security Grant - Discretionary
  • Careline Grant - Discretionary

Please note that all assistance identified as discretionary is provided subject to funds being available. For this reason provision of this assistance may be limited or discontinued at any time.

 

Disabled Facilities Grant (Mandatory)

 

A grant of up to £30,000 is available to help people meet the cost of adaptations to a disabled person’s home. We are required to ascertain that the works are necessary and appropriate for the needs of the individual and that it is reasonable and practical to carry out the works having regard to the age and condition of the dwelling.

 

Adapt Grant (Discretionary)

 

A grant of up to £6,000 (subject to conditions pdf 161Kb) which is a simplified version of the Disabled Facilities Grant suitable where the applicant is not subject to a test of resources. The grant can provide funding for measures otherwise not covered by the DFG such as the provision of disabled equipment or funding for relocation where deemed more appropriate.

 

Additional Adaptation Assistance (Discretionary)

 

Assistance by way of a loan, to help fund measures associated with adapting the home of a disabled person, where the nature of the works falls outside work funded by the mandatory DFG of if the works exceed the DFG funding limit of £30,000 (subject to conditions pdf 160Kb).

 

Emergency Repair Grant (Discretionary)

 

A grant of up to £250 to assist owner occupiers deemed to be a vulnerable household with emergency repairs to alleviate the most significant hazards (subject to conditions pdf 154Kb).

 

Home Repair Assistance (Discretionary)

 

Assistance by way of a loan, up to £15,000 to help owner occupiers deemed to be a vulnerable household alleviate the most significant hazards (subject to conditions pdf 161Kb). Assistance is only available where no other sources of self funding are available. Further details are contained within the Private Sector Housing Investment Policy (pdf 372kb).

 

Safety & Security Grant (Discretionary)

 

A grant up to £200 (subject to conditions pdf 155Kb) to fund measures that improve the safety and security of vulnerable owner occupier households in accordance with the Private Sector Housing Investment Policy (pdf 372kb).

 

Careline Grant (Discretionary)

 

A grant up to £200 (subject to conditions pdf 155Kb) to fund measures to allow vulnerable households to live safely and independently in their own homes in accordance with the Private Sector Housing Investment Policy (pdf 372Kb)

 

Help and advice and/or application forms for the above can be obtained from Care & Repair West Norfolk.
