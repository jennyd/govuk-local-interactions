Holiday collections

During holiday periods, there may be changes to your fortnightly waste collection.

More information about holiday collections

Bin tags

If a residents bin hasn't been emptied, a tag will have been attached to explain why. It could be that a resident has put their waste in the wrong bin or that the bin was overfilled or heavy. If you have received a tag on your bin, please contact us if you would like to discuss any problems you may be experiencing with your recycling and waste.

Waste and recycling pack

Recycling and waste information packs were dropped through letterboxes in September which included -

  • calendars with your new collection days and dates
  • a resident's pass to the city's household waste recycling centres

Request recycling bins or food bins

To help residents recycle more, you can also order extra free blue and brown recycling bins or food bins using our online form below.
 Request recycling bins and food bins (online form)

Please note that additional black bins are only provided at properties where there are more than six residents and when the recycling services are being used to full potential. For more information please telephone 01482 300 300.

Collections

Please make sure your recycling and refuse bins are put out by 7am on your collection day. We will empty bins up to 4.30pm.
