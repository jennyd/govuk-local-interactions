Abandoned vehicles

What is an abandoned vehicle?

The following list can help you to determine whether a vehicle is abandoned.

  • Has the vehicle been parked without moving for two weeks or more?
  • Is the vehicle untaxed? (To find out if it's taxed or not, go to www.gov.uk/report-untaxed-vehicle)
  • Are there any flat tyres or removed wheels?
  • Is the windscreen or any of the windows broken?
  • Is the vehicle mouldy or rusty?
  • Is there waste inside the vehicle?

If you can answer yes to two or more of the above, then it is possible the car has been abandoned.

What is not classed as abandoned?

A vehicle is not classed as abandoned just because it is in poor condition and/or out of Tax. In these cases further enquiries would be needed. Vehicles that are illegally, obstructively or dangerously parked; irrespective of whether it is taxed or not, must be reported to the Police on telephone number 101. 

How to Report it

To report a suspected abandoned vehicle

  • Go to our Report It section to report it online
  • Email: councilconnect@bathnes.gov.uk
  • Call Council Connect on 01225 39 40 41
  • Text (SMS): 07797 806545

A vandalised vehicle

Call the Vandalised Vehicle Hotline on 0800 0835 115 

A vehicle without a valid tax disc

Vehicles no longer need to display a valid tax disc but you can find out if a vehicle is taxed and report an untaxed vehicle by going to www.gov.uk/report-untaxed-vehicle
However, an untaxed vehicle is not necessarily abandoned and we have no power to remove them.

What’s the council doing to help?

  • Investigation, removal and destruction of all reported abandoned vehicles.
  • Automatic £200 fine issued to owner.
  • We run a collection service for old vehicles, which costs £80. Your vehicle log-book and proof of identity is required. Call 01225 39 40 41 for more details.

We are also part of the Car Clear Scheme, which is coordinated by Avon and Somerset Arson Task Force. Please click the link Car Clear Scheme to take you to the Avon and Somerset Police Websitefor more information.
