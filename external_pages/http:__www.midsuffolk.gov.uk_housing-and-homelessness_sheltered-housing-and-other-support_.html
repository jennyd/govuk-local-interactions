How do I apply for a community alarm?

As you may be aware Mid Suffolk and Babergh District Councils have joined many of their services together. During this process many of those services have been reviewed.

Following the review of the above services it is with regret that both Councils have decided it is not possible to run these services effectively themselves in these times of fast moving technological advances and financial constraints.

With the knowledge that this vital service helps to maintain people’s independence in their own homes both services are to be transferred to Stafford & Rural Homes (who currently monitor the alarms for both Mid Suffolk and Babergh Councils) to be effective from 1st July 2015.

Stafford & Rural Homes are a well-established not for profit Registered Social Landlord.

Stafford & Rural Homes can be contacted on 0808 168 7794

How do I apply for sheltered housing?

It is simple to apply for sheltered housing:

  1. Register with Gateway to Homechoice (opens new window) online
  2. Call: 01449 724762
  3. Visit the Council offices, 131 High Street, Needham Market, Suffolk, IP6 8DL

I would like to know more about the services that Orbit East can offer me. Where can I find more information?

Orbit East provides two services for older people:

1. Orbit East HomeWorks HandyHelp Service (opens new window) 

The Orbit East HomeWorks HandyHelp service offers assistance with minor repairs or odd jobs at your home if you are a homeowner or private tenant and:

  • are over the age of 60 or
  • are disabled or vulnerable and
  • live in Babergh, Forest Heath, Ipswich, Mid Suffolk, Suffolk Coastal District Council, St Edmundsbury or Waveney local authority areas

2. Suffolk Home Improvement Agency (also known as Care and Repair) (opens new window).

The Suffolk Home Improvement agency provides advice, support and practical help to older homeowners and private tenants and people of any age with a disability who need help to repair, improve or adapt their home so that they can continue to live comfortably, safely and independently.
