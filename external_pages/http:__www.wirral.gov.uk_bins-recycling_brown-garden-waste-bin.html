Brown garden waste bin

There is a charge for the brown bin garden waste collection in Wirral.

Subscribe today

Charging period

The charge covers collections for 12 months from 1st June through to 31st May only.

Please allow 15 working days for your permit to be posted out to you.

Cost

  • £35 for the first bin to be collected (£30 if you subscribe online)
  • £20 for each additional bin to be collected
  • £37 for a new or replacement brown bin

Collections

Bins are collected every other week. Check your bin collection date.

Items allowed in your brown bin

  • grass mowings
  • hedge cuttings
  • pruning
  • leaves
  • weeds
  • dead flowers

Please ensure all of your garden waste fits inside your bin.  Chop up hedge cuttings and pruning if necessary.
