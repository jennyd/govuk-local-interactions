Application for a Bus Pass for Blind or Disabled Persons

Please use this form to make an application under the application for a bus pass for blind or disabled person. 
  
A bus pass would not normally be issued to persons unable to take advantage of bus travel. 
  
People in receipt of the higher rate mobility component of DLA (including wheelchair users) WILL NOT be entitled to a concessionary bus pass. This is because the higher rate mobility component is paid to them to assist them in meeting the costs of their travel. 
  
Men and women aged 60 to 65 (the current ages for receipt of the senior citizens half-price travel pass) have the choice of either applying for a DMBC Disabled Persons Bus Pass or applying for the Centro Senior Citizens Half Price Travel card. However, persons over 65 must apply for the Senior Citizens Concessionary Pass which gives them the same travel benefits as a younger person with a Disabled Off-peak Pass. 
  
Please ensure you have a Centro photo card/permit before proceeding with your application for a bus pass for a blind or disabled person.

In the event of any query relating to the issue of a bus pass please contact your local Social Services district office at Brierley Hill Health & Social Care Centre, Venture Way, Brierley Hill, DY5 1RU.
