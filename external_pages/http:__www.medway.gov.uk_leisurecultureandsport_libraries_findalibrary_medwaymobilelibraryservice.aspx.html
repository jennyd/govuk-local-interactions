Medway mobile library service

Bringing you books and more   

Mobile libraries carry a wide range of material including:

  • fiction
  • non-fiction
  • books for children of all ages
  • books in large print
  • spoken word CDs.

You can ask the mobile library staff to try to obtain items not immediately available on the vehicle. They can also help with your general enquiries.

Once you have joined the library, after producing evidence of name and address, you can borrow up to 30 items. You may keep them for up to one month. Although fines are not charged on items which are returned late to the mobile library, borrowers are asked to request a renewal of the loan period. 


Timetable

The Community Mobile Library visits each stop on a two week rota.

To find out when the Community Mobile Library is visiting a specific location on this rota please contact  01634 337799 or view the calendar below

  • 2016 Community Mobile Library rota dates (pdf 43KB)

To use the Community Mobile Library rota dates file you will need Adobe Acrobat Reader. If you do not have it on your computer, please use our advice page.

Booking a mobile library for your event

If you would like to book a mobile library to attend your weekend or school holidays event then please fill in the mobile library booking form and return it to Medway Libraries' Headquarters, 32 Bryant Road, Strood, Rochester, Kent, ME2 3EP.
