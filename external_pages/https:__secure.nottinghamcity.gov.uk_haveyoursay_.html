Submitting the Form

The information given on this form will be processed and stored in accordance with the principles of the Data Protection Act by Nottingham City Council. We need you to give permission for this and other information relating to this matter to be shared with other relevant people and organisations for the purpose of addressing this issue and for service improvement.


We will use the information you provide for the purposes stated on this form or in pursuance of any other legitimate interest held by the Council. Your personal information may also be processed to facilitate the provision of services in respect of any of the Council's activities or for the prevention and detection of crime and fraud. Under the Data Protection Act 1998 Nottingham City Council is the Data Controller for the information you have given us. For more information visit http://www.nottinghamcity.gov.uk/3212

When you press submit we will give you a reference number and tell you what happens next. There will also be an equalities information form that we would like you to complete.

Your IP address will be sent with your communication. In rare cases where abuse or criminal activity can be shown to have taken place this may be used by the authorities to trace you.
