Kirklees Allotments (julian.faulkner@kirklees.gov.uk)

Make a payment

  • Pay online

    Make a payment by debit or credit card.

Rent an allotment

    • There are about 1,874 allotments in Kirklees on around 100 sites.
    • The majority are about 200 square yards.
    • Most allotments have access to a water supply.

  • Where's my nearest allotment?

    Map of all allotment sites in Kirklees.

  • Application for tenancy of an allotment

    • If there is a vacant plot you will be given the contact details of the site secretary who will show you the vacant plot and outline the rules and regulations.
    • If there are no vacant plots your name will be added to the waiting list for that site and you will be contacted when a plot becomes available.
