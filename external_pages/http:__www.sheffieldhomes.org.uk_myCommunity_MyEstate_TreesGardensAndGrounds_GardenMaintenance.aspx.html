Garden maintenance

Garden scheme for the elderly or disabled

We have a garden scheme for elderly or disabled Council Housing Service residents.  There is currently no charge for the service, which is limited to a single visit each year.

We are reviewing how we provide this service and also what types of gardening jobs we can offer as part of it.

We will be collecting the views of those people already on the scheme and in homes with gardens but would like to hear from anyone who wants to share their views too. Please follow the link to complete our online survey.

New Applicants

In order for us to carry out our review, we have had to close the service to new applicants at this time. We are creating a waiting list of new applicants, however the criteria is likely to change once the review has taken place..

The current criteria is that you must:-

  • Be aged 75 or over or,
  • Have a disability which prevents you from gardening and,
  • There should be no-one else under the age of 75 at home - or any friends, relatives or neighbours - able to do the work

You can apply:

  • In person at your nearest Housing Office or First Point
  • By telephone on: 0114 293 0000 - North Call Centre or 0114 205 3333 - South Call Centre
  • In writing to Council Housing Service, PO BOX 483, M33 0DH
  • By using our online form

Things to remember

The current service is free of charge and you will not be asked for any money. Always ensure you check the identification of anyone coming to do the work.
