Thinking about fostering? 

Call us on 0300 123 6723 or complete our enquiry form and a member of the team will be in touch.

Why foster?

Becoming a foster carer is one of the most emotionally rewarding things you can do. Hear stories from our foster carers.

Support you'll receive

As well supporting you on every step of your journey we also provide practical benefits and financial support.

Information evenings

We host a range of information evenings across the county to help you find out more about fostering in Lancashire.

Can I foster?

We will consider foster carers regardless of age, marital status, gender, sexuality, disability or employment status.

Types of fostering

Children of all ages need fostering, from babies to teenagers, and for different lengths of time and reasons.
