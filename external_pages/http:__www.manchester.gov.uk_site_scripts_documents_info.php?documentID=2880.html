Social care & support Help and Support Manchester

Family services in Manchester

You can use the Help & Support Manchester site to run a customised search for the kind of service you need, in a location convenient to you. For example, children's centres, childminders, nurseries and playgroups in your local area. It also hosts the Local Offer for families of children with disabilities or special educational needs.

Go to Help & Support Manchester

We want to provide the most up to date and useful information possible about services that are available, so if you spot any mistakes or something that's missing please contact us at: hsm@manchester.gov.uk
