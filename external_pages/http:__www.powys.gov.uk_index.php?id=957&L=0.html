Castle Views on Offa

Castle Views on Offa took place at Montgomery on Saturday 13th July 2013. The walk was led by Dave Owen and Ginny Cooke from Powys Countryside and Sam Bibb from Tourism came along to take Photographs.

The Walk started at Montgomery Public car park at 10:30am. Everyone who turned up was then led around Montgomery with "King Offa" learning more about the history of the town, the castle and ridge and furrow farming. The weather was very hot on the day so many stops had to be taken for water breaks and shade.

The walk lasted around 3 hours and was about 3 miles in length.
