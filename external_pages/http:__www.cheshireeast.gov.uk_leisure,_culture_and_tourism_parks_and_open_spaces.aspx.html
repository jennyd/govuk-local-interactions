Parks and Open Spaces

There is a wealth of recreational opportunities across the whole of Cheshire East just waiting for you to explore and the good news is, you could be a healthier, happier, fitter person if you do! You'll find parks and smaller neighbourhood play areas, open spaces of all sizes and, of course, our playing fields and sports facilities. There will be something for you, whatever your age.

Well behaved dogs welcome.

Please remove any mess, please keep your dog under control, no fouling on play surfaces please.

Cheshire East parks strategy

Cheshire East is drafting a parks strategy and we would like users of the parks to get involved.

Please read the draft strategy (PDF, 698KB) and then complete this  short survey about the draft proposals.

Queens Park, Crewe

Dogs should be on a lead please. We are Dog Friendly and seeking to introduce a Dog Control order, enabling everyone to enjoy their lovely Park, so would be grateful if you could please complete and return Dog Control Orders questionnaire (MS Word, 134KB)  – it will only take about 30 seconds to complete!
