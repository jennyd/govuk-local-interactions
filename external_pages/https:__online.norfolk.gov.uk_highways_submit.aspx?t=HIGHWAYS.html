Highways Enquiry Tracker Report Form

Use this form to report problems with roads, pavements, cycle paths and public rights of way
Your Progress:   1. Your details  |  2. Enquiry details  |  3. Completion  | 

The form below can be used to report problems with roads and pavements in Norfolk with three exceptions:

  • If the problem is on the A11, A12 or A47 please contact Highways England. Highways England is responsible for these roads within Norfolk.
  • Please contact Norwich City Council if within the City.
  • If the problem is with a streetlight, please use our streetlighting web form.

If you are not sure who to contact, please complete the form and we will pass on the details to the right organisation if necessary.

The Council works in partnership with other organisations to provide highway services - we may share the information you provide on this form with them.

Fields marked with  * are mandatory.
