Planning application search

A search facility to query the applications that the Council have on its register. You are able to search for applications based on various criterion including address details, date received and date determined.

This online service is unavailable during daily backups which commence at 11:00 pm and complete by 6am. You can comment by email to planning@ealing.gov.uk or alternatively in writing to the planning services team.
