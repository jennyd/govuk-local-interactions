Housing options and advice

The Council's housing options team is able to provide help and advice on many housing issues - depending upon your personal circumstances.

To discuss you circumstances with a Housing Options officer, please contact us:

  • call 01494 421212 between 8.45am to 5.15pm Monday to Thursday and 8.45am to 4.45pm on a Friday
  • email: housingoptions@wycombe.gov.uk
  • emergency out of hours: 0800 999 7677

We can provide assistance where, for example you:

  • are living in privately rented accommodation and your landlord is asking you to leave
  • are living with family/friends and are being asked to leave
  • have become, or think that you are likely to become homeless
  • are experiencing domestic abuse
  • are experiencing harassment from you landlord /illegal eviction and want to find out what your rights and options are
  • are having difficulties paying your mortgage or rent
  • would like information regarding accessing accommodation

We can also refer you to a specialist elsewhere for more detailed advice.

If you would like information on how to apply to the housing register, please see our apply for housing page.

If your situation is an emergency and you are faced with homelessness, please see our homelessness page.
