Library charges

Where to find help

  • Find: your local library

Borrowing

You may borrow 20 items at any one time. This could include:

  • up to 20 books
  • eight multimedia items
  • except CD-ROMs, computer software and games and DVD box sets where the maximum is two items

CDs, DVDs and console games are loaned for one week. Everything else is loaned for three weeks.

Items on loan can be renewed if not reserved by another borrower. Items can be renewed online or by telephone, email, letter, fax, or visiting a library. You can also renew your library loans using the 24 hour telephone renewal line on 0845 148 0148.

Reservations

  • Items from within Cheshire - £1 (55p CLCH*)
  • Items for children from within Cheshire - free of charge
  • Items from a UK library outside Cheshire - £3.60
  • Books from the British Library - £13.30
  • Renewal - £4.65
  • Journal articles from the British Library:
  • Electronic copy sent by email: £5
  • Scanned print copy sent by email: £8.25
  • Standard print copy sent by post: £9.40
  • Items from a library outside the UK - £18

If an item is obtained from outside Cheshire and isn't collected by the customer they will still be liable for the reservation charge which will be added to their library account.

People who have difficulty reading print or handling books can reserve talking books and language courses free.
