Can't find an allotment in your area?

Then why not sign up to Landshare? Landshare is an exciting new project, fronted by Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall of River Cottage fame, and aims to link people who have spare land with people who want allotment space. Visit the Landshare website to find out more.
