Getting out and about

If you find it difficult to get around, there are a number of different travel options and services.

 

  • Disabled parking

    Find out about disabled parking in West Sussex including how to get and use a Blue Badge or request a disabled bay.

  • Free older persons' bus pass

    Apply for, renew or replace a free bus pass for older people.

  • Senior railcard

    Apply for, renew or replace a railcard offering a third off the cost of rail travel to those of state pension age.

  • Free disabled persons' bus pass

    Apply for, renew or replace a free bus pass for disabled people.

  • Disabled persons' railcard

    Apply for, renew or replace a railcard offering a third off the cost of rail travel for disabled people.

  • Travelling to hospital and healthcare facilities

    How you can get help with travelling for medical purposes.

  • Community transport organisations

    Find contact details for organisations providing travel assistance, such as specialist bus services for older and disabled people.

    Find activities and groups in your local area.
