Pest Control

Our Animal and Pest Control Team have all the necessary qualifications and equipment to carry out the services that we offer, and know the Torfaen area very well, which helps them to understand the problems that may affect local residents.

We offer a range of pest control services, including: 

  • A low cost public health pest service for Torfaen domestic premises 
  • Advice and leaflets on a range of other pests 
  • A sewer baiting programme to control rats

Public Health Pests

There are a number of pests that can affect homes. Whilst some of these have public health implications, most do not cause disease and many are relatively harmless.

Torfaen County Borough Council offers a pest control service for householders to treat public health pests: The costs for our pest control service are as follows:

  • Rats and Mice in domestic premises: £30
  • Bedbugs and Cockroaches in domestic premises: £40

These fees include at least one revisit to help ensure that the pest problem is sorted.

If you need one of these services, you should contact our Pest Control Service on 01633 647621 / 647622, so that we can arrange to visit your home to carry out any treatment needed.

Non Public Health Pests

Torfaen County Borough Council no longer provides a service to treat non public-health pest infestations or problems, such as wasps, fleas, ants and flies, unless they are in a public place where they may cause a public health problem.

If you have a problem you feel we should be dealing with, or need general advice, you can contact our Pest Control Service on 01633 647621 / 647622.

There are a number of local pest control companies that can treat pest infestations and problems, and these can be contacted through Yellow Pages and other business directories.
