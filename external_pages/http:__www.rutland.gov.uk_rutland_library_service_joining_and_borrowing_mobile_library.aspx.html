Rutland Mobile Library

Our mobile library provides a fortnightly
service to the villages of Rutland,
bringing books and information to
all parts of the County.

 

The library van can also be found at community events such as Rutland Day at Rutland Water.

 

 

Mobile Library routes and dates

There is a printable Mobile Library Timetable (new timetable from 4th January 2016) listing villages in alphabetical order and a separate insert with date details.  There are no deliveries on Bank Holidays.

 

Please note that the Mobile Library does not operate on Bank Holidays and the week between Christmas and New Year.

 

Services provided

 

Books to borrow for adults and children, including fiction and non-fiction
Books in Large Print

Talking Books

DVDs for hire

Request service for items not available on the Mobile e.g Wii games

Wheelchair access via a vehicle lift

Your Library Account

 

You can check your account, renew books or reserve an item online by Logging In to our Online Catalogue. You will need a library card and PIN number to do this. To obtain a PIN number, please contact your local library who can arrange to have one posted to you, or you can visit the library with your Service Card and a PIN number will be issued to you over the counter.
