School terms and holidays

These term and holiday dates apply only to community schools in Merton. For voluntary aided (CofE or Catholic) schools, academies, free schools or independent schools, please contact the school directly or check their website.

In addition to the holidays below, community schools are closed to pupils for five separate days for staff training. These days vary between schools, and parents are notified of the dates by the school. Please see the school's own website (see List of Schools) or contact the school directly if you are unsure of any of the term dates.

For the latest information from your school see School Closures or visit your school's website.
