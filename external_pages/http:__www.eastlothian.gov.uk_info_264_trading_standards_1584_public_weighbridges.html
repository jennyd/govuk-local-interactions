Public weighbridges

Public weighbridges in East Lothian

A public weighbridge is a weighbridge operated by a company, usually primarily for their own use, but which they will also allow anyone to use for a small charge. 

The following weighbridges in East Lothian are available for public use.

Please contact the operator of the weighbridge you want to use to confirm the price and opening hours prior to visiting.
