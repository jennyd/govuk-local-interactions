Pay a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN)

You can pay your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) in one of the following ways:

  • Online - make a payment now
  • By telephone
  • By post
  • In person.

Online

You will need to enter the PCN number, starting with PL and the vehicle registration of the vehicle. You can't pay your PCN online until the day after (working day) the ticket has been issued. Please note that all the boxes must be completed in order to make a payment, therefore if you would prefer not to provide your personal details then please insert fictitious information.

If you have received a warrant you must pay for your warrant directly to the bailiff company.

Telephone

Call our fully automated payment line on 01752 546220, 24 hours a day.

By post

You can post a cheque or postal order made payable to ‘Plymouth City Council’ with the penalty charge number (the number beginning with 'PL') written on the back and post to: Admail 3313, Plymouth, PL9 7BF.

In person

Make a payment at the self-service kiosk at 1st Stop.

Bailiff cases

Visits from bailiffs can cause distress, and we will only use them where we have no other way of getting payment. We would have sent three items of correspondence at various times with the amount outstanding to the owner/keepers address before passing the PCN to a bailiff company for collection.

Once a PCN has been passed to one of our bailiff companies, we are unable to deal with any payments or correspondence. If your case has reached this stage it will have been passed to the following bailiff company:

JBW
