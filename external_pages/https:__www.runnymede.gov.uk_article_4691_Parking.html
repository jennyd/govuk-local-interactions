Parking

Pay parking fines and find details of Runnymede car parks and parking schemes. The Borough Council carries out on-street parking enforcement in the Borough and manages and enforces parking in the Borough operated car parks.

  • Pay a parking fine

    Paying a parking fine online is quick and easy.

  • Phone and Pay parking

    The phone and pay service in Runnymede Borough Council car parks will be suspended on Sunday 31 January.

  • Runnymede Borough Council car parks

    Car parks are situated around the Borough. A majority of the car parks operate under a pay and display basis although some, particularly away from the town centres, are free to use.

  • On street parking enforcement

    Our civil enforcement officers regularly patrol all parking restricted areas of the Borough and issue penalty charge notices to violators. This reduces dangerous parking, improves traffic flow and helps to keep designated disabled parking bays available for those who need them.

  • Residents on street parking schemes

    Vehicles need to show a valid permit when they park in residents' bays during restricted times and are not allowed to park on the yellow line waiting restrictions.

  • Parking suspensions and waivers

    In some circumstances we may allow you to park in a place that is normally restricted by offering a suspension or waiver certificate.

    Some commonly asked questions about our parking schemes.
