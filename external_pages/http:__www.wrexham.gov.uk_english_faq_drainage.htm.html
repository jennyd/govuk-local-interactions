Frequently Asked Questions - Drainage

What drains are WCBC responsible for?

WCBC are responsible for the drainage of the road network. If you have a problem that relates to a ROAD that is flooding please contact us on 01978 298989, email Contact-Us@wrexham.gov.uk or in person Contact Wrexham 16 Lord Street, Wrexham LL11 1EG.

The body responsible for the main public Sewer Network is Welsh Water. If you have a problem that relates to Sewers you should therefore contact the Welsh Water contractor for this work on 0800 085 3968.

If you are a Council House Tenant, and you have drainage problems then you should contact the Housing Department on 0800 0855 808.

Who is responsible for other drains/sewers?

The body responsible for the main Public Sewer Network is Welsh Water. If you have a problem that relates to Sewers you should therefore contact the Welsh Water contractor for this work on 0800 085 3968.

If you are a Council house tenant, and you have drainage problems you should contact the Housing Department on 0800 0855 808.
