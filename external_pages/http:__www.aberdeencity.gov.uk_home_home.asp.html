    Civic leaders and the business community have welcomed an announcement that both UK and Scottish Governments have agreed the terms of a deal investing in the north-east's future.

  • Greenspace and Wellbeing Survey

    Aberdeen City Council in partnership with NHS Grampian is carrying out a short survey on the use of greenspaces and would like your views. The survey takes only 5-10 minutes to complete. Please click the title and fill in the questionnaire online. You can also pick up a paper copy at your local library or from Marischal College.

  • Text Message Scam

    Aberdeen City Council has been made aware of a scam involving text messages. The messages claim to be from the Council and ask recipients to follow a link where they can apply for a council tax refund. The Council does not send out text messages of this nature and anyone receiving one should contact the Council on 0845 6080922 or Police Scotland on 101.

  • SPECTRA Festival of Light 11  14 February 2016

    This year we are celebrating the 2016 Scottish Year of Innovation, Architecture and Design through this year's theme 'Spark of Inspiration'. The Festival has grown from its home in Union Terrace Gardens to incorporate additional city centre sites including St Nicholas Kirkyard, Marischal College and Seventeen (Belmont St), and will feature major public realm and gallery installations alongside new experimental works from the creative community.

  • Aberdeen Art Gallery redevelopment - Inspiring Art and Music

    Find out more about the £30million redevelopment project, which will see the transformation of Aberdeen Art Gallery, Cowdray Hall and Remembrance Hall into a modern centre for art and music.

Union Terrace Gardens closed for SPECTRA set up

05 February 2016

Union Terrace Gardens will be closed to the public in the lead up to Spectra 2016 - Aberdeen's festival of lights.

First householders awarded flooding grants

27 January 2016

The first grants have been awarded by Aberdeen City Council to householders in the city who were affected by the recent flooding.

Bus shelters being replaced

04 February 2016

A total of 15 bus shelters are being replaced around Aberdeen this month (Feb) using funds from the Bus Lane Enforcement surplus fund.

Volunteers needed for more gorse clearing

03 February 2016

Aberdeen City Council Ranger Service is holding the second Volunteer Workday of the year on Saturday (Feb 6) to clear gorse at Scotstown Moor Local Nature Reserve.

Bridge of Don Academy to reopen tomorrow

02 February 2016

Bridge of Don Academy, Braehead Way, Aberdeen will reopen to pupils and staff tomorrow (Wed 03 Feb).

Road closed

02 February 2016

  Aberdeen City Council has closed both ways of the C89c Chapel of Stoneywood from its junction with the A96 to Forrit Brae section of road due to deterioration caused by the recent bad weather and heavy vehicles working on the AWPR.
