Library Computers and Internet Access

  • Book - Library Computer Sessions 


Computers with internet access are available to use in all 4 libraries in Torbay.
  • Explore the internet
  • Renew your books. All you need is your membership number and PIN.
  • Give feedback on our services.
  • Browse the library catalogue
  • Check if the item you want is in stock.
  • Make reservations
  • Access your loans record.
  • Access our Community Information database containing details of hundreds of clubs and societies in the area.
  • Free to library members for the first hour's use each day
  • Non-members are charged at £2.00 per 30 minutes
You can also print from the computers. Prints are collected at the desk and are charged at 10p per A4 copy.
Note: Library computers are made available for public use according to the Terms and Conditions of the Public Access IT Service (see Related Documents).

Wi Fi at Paignton Library

Customers can visit Paignton library with their own laptops or other portable devices and link to the internet wirelessly. Library members can enjoy free unlimited access to Wi-Fi using their library membership card number and pin. Non members and visitors may also gain free unlimited access after getting logon details from staff.
