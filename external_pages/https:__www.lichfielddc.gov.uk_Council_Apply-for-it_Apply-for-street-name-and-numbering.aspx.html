Apply for street name and numbering

Please complete this form to apply for new, or changes to, naming and numbering of streets and buildings, for more information on the application and the fees please read the Street Naming & Numbering Application leaflet. To pay your fee use our online payments section, select 'other payments' then 'land and property' and then 'street naming and numbering'.

  • Application for new, or changes to, naming and numbering of streets and buildings in accordance with the Town Improvement Clauses Act 1847 and the Public Health Act 1925.
  • Address of applicant
  • Address of developer
  • Please tell us how we should contact you for your preferred method of correspondence.
    Mandatory field
  • Please tick the boxes that apply
  • Section 1
    Please complete Section 1 if your application is to number a building(s), to name a building(s), to name a street(s) and to rename/renumber existing property(s).
  • 1. Is this site/building:
  • 2. Are you the:
  • 3. Is the property access restricted (information required by the Royal Mail):
  • Section 2
    Please complete Section 2 if your application is to number a building(s) or to name a building(s).
  • 1. Please give four proposed names in order of preference:
  • Section 3: Street naming preferences
    Please complete Section 3 if your application is to name a street(s).
  • 1. Please give four suggested names in order of preference:
  • Section 4: Property name or number change details
    Please complete Section 4 if your application is to rename/renumber existing property(s).
  • Section 5: Please indicate which items have been enclosed
    Please send items to Geographical Information Management team, District Council House, Frog Lane, Lichfield, Staffs, WS13 6YY
  • Enclosures

    1. You must fill in all the relevant sections of the application form.

    2. A site plan must be included if no Planning Application is submitted or apply.

    3. If you are not the freeholder owner, please provide written approval from the freehold owner.

    4. Enclose the correct fee, shown on the reverse of this form. Cheques should be made payable to Lichfield District Council.

  • Declaration
    I/we hereby apply for street naming and numbering described in the following application and on the attached plans.
  • By checking this box I/we agree to the above declaration.
  • Notes

    Lichfield District Council (the council) is a data controller under the Data Protection Act 1998. We will use the information we hold about you in several ways, including:

    • to identify you when you contact us
    • to enable us to process your application
    • to provide you with a better level of service, by ensuring that our information about you is accurate and up-to-date
    • ensuring that public money is spent wisely and efficiently
    • to prevent and detect fraud and other criminal activity
    • to inform you about other services available from the council, unless you ask us not to
    • to comply with the law

    The council will only keep information about you for as long as is necessary and will dispose of it securely. The council may share your information (only where it is lawful to do so) with other departments within the council (including the elected members), central government departments, law enforcement agencies, statutory and judicial bodies, community service providers and contractors that process data on its behalf.

    Please note all applications for planning matters and supporting documentation are a public record and you should be aware that these records are open to public inspection, both in their paper version and in electronic media, such as the council's website.

    If you have any difficulties in completing the application form, please do not hesitate to contact us on 01543 308131 or addresses@lichfielddc.gov.uk Alternatively visit Lichfield District Council, Frog Lane, Lichfield, WS13 6YY
