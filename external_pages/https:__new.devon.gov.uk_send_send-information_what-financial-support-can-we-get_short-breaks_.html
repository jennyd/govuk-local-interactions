Short Breaks


Short breaks provide a positive activity for children or young people with disabilities while giving parents a break from their caring responsibilities. These breaks allow you to have the time to rest, take care of other children or do something you wouldn’t be able to do otherwise. Short breaks can also allow you to do something positive with your child in an environment where they are well-supported.
