Help to live independently

Living Independently

Are you looking to live independently within your community for as long as you can? Maybe you need some assistance to do this. Housing related support is provided by the Enabling Support Team.

How we may be able to help you

        
  • Referral: contact us on 01724 297979 and we will ask you to complete an easy to use form – you might want someone to help you do this     
  • Assessment: someone from the team will come and talk to you about what it is you are looking for and carry out an assessment of your needs.  You might want a friend or relative to be there with you      
  • Outcome: when we have completed an assessment with you we will let you know how we can best meet your needs and look at how we can plan the next steps  
        

The team can support you to:

  • manage your personal budget
  • support you to deal with accessing different services
  • support you to find paid work and get the most out of the money you earn
  • support you to access the Advocacy Service
  • offer sheltered housing services, including a warden system to community alarm phone
  • develop life skills in setting up and maintaining your home so that you may be able to stay at home 
  • develop your confidence and ability so that you can have choice and control and get involved in the community if you wish

People who have been supported by the team:

  • Homeless and at risk of eviction

  • Young and old at risk of domestic violence, drug and alcohol misuse 

  • Older people with support needs

  • People with physical and learning disabilities

  • People who have committed a crime

 

  • First Stop Service

  • Expand

The aim of housing related support is to enable vulnerable people to stay at home and live independently for as long as they want to, or are able to. This supports vulnerable people to live independently in the community rather than having to move into a care home.

Support includes helping people to:

  • Manage their budgets
  • Deal with different services
  • Set up and maintain a home 
  • Develop domestic and life skills

Contact First Stop – 01724 296454 and speak to the First Stop Housing Related Officer

To make a request for the service please complete the First Stop Referral form [PDF, 35 Kb]

Further Information

Place of Change leaflet [PDF, 678 KB] gives information about an accommodation based service provided by Creative Support.

Stage 3 Supported Accommodation Referral Form [DOC, 173 Kb] is the referral form for supported accommodation, including Place of Change, Conway House and Parkers Lane.

Place of Change provides the highest level supported accommodation for hard to house people to address complex issues that have resulted in them becoming homeless.

Conway House provides medium to high level supported accommodation for people with issues that have resulted in them becoming homeless. 

Parkers Lane provides a high level of support for people with complex mental health needs.
