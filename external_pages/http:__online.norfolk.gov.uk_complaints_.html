Complaints

Unfortunately there are times when things go wrong. To help ensure you get a courteous, fair and consistent response to your concerns, please fill in the form below telling us about your experience.

Please note that if you have concerns of a serious nature regarding the safety and wellbeing of a child, young person or adult, you should call us on 0344 800 8020. This telephone line is open 24 hours a day for social care enquiries.

The council sometimes works in partnership with other organisations to provide services, so please be aware we may need to share your information with these providers to respond to some complaints.

If you want to make a complaint about the conduct of a Councillor or Co-opted Member of Norfolk County Council please follow this link: Making a complaint about a Councillor or Co-opted Member of Norfolk County Council

Warning: Please do not use your internet browser's 'Back' button as this may cause your transaction to fail and you may have to start again. Please use the buttons provided on the form. Please be aware that this form will time out after 40 minutes of inactivity.

Please note your application will expire after 40 minutes of inactivity.
