Bulky Collections

Bulky Collections

Bulky household waste such as fridges, sofas, beds can be disposed of free of charge at your local household waste recycling centre. Alternatively, the council can collect your bulky waste for a charge.

What does it cost?

The cost for the service is £30 for 1 to 3 Items and £5 per additional item up to a maximum of six items from a single household.

Payment for the service will need to be received in advance of the collection. Please note that this is a non-refundable service.  Please call us on 01362 656870 to book a collection slot and make payment using a debit or credit card.

What we can and can't collect

What can we collect? What cant we collect?
3 piece suites, sofas and armchairs Fixtures and fittings
Beds, mattresses, headboards and cots Doors and windows
Carpets, underlay and lino Sheds, fence panels and gates
Cookers and microwaves Central heating boilers, immersion tanks and oil tanks
Cupboards, bookcases, wardrobes, tables and desks Brick rubble
Freezers, fridges and dishwashers Wood
TVs, Hi Fi and computers Paving Slabs
Washing machines and tumble dryers DIY waste

When can we collect?

Please enter your town or village to find out which day you will have your bulky items collected.






The items need to be placed at the front of your property, or in a position where they are accessible for collection by 7am.

What can I do with items that cannot be collected

  • Reuse it within your own house, or give it to someone who can.
  • Use a waste bag service available from DIY stores.
  • Hire a skip from a private contractor; see local advertising; look under Skip Hire or Waste Disposal Services in Yellow Pages
  • Norfolk County Council operate a Pay as You Throw Service at some of their Household Waste Recycling Centres for DIY waste
