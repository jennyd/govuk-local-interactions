Disability facility grant

​Home repair assistance is available to carry out essential repairs and improvements to homes.  Assistance is available in the form of low interest or fixed fee loans provided by Wessex Home Improvement Loans.  On behalf of the local authority they administer one-off funding provided by the government especially for this purpose.  For further information see ‘Low cost home improvement loans’. The enquiry form  includes a summary of the council’s home repair assistance policy.

Disabled facilities grant

If you need to make adaptations to your home to meet the needs of a disabled person, you may be eligible for a disabled facilities grant. Warm and Well is a project which aims to help people who may be suffering fuel poverty to keep their homes warm and comfortable by being energy-efficient.  An enquiry form can be completed to enable a provisional means test to be carried out. This will indicate whether or not you may be expected to contribute to the cost of any eligible work.

South Gloucestershire Handy Van service

The South Gloucestershire Handy Van service offers subsidised minor household repairs and free home safety and security checks to people who own their homes or are private tenants and who are either over 60 years old or disabled.

Home improvement agency

Home improvement agencies support older and/or disabled homeowners and tenants who want to repair or adapt their homes. We work with Bristol City Council, Bath & North East Somerset Council and North Somerset Council seeking views on a proposal to commission home improvement agency services across the west of England to produce efficiency savings and help improve the level of service. For more details and to give us your views please follow the link Home improvement agency.

Low cost loans

Crisis loans are available for people living on a low income and faced with emergency costs that they are unable to meet.  The loan has to be paid back but it is interest free.  Telephone 0845 608 8616.

Budgeting loans are available to people who have been getting Income Support, income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance or pension credit for at least 26 weeks.  The loan has to be paid back but it is interest free.  Telephone 0845 608 8618.

Low cost furniture/ household items

Kingswood Furniture Project, Sofa Project and Emmaus provide low cost recycled furniture to people on low incomes.

If you are a tenant in housing association property and you need repair work, you should contact your landlord.  If the repair does not get done we can help.
