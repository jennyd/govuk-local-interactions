Sustainable School Travel & School Travel Plans

                   It is the vision of Devon County Council that every child should be able to travel to school in a healthy, sustainable and safe way.  By working together and developing an achievable Travel Plan for your school we can help to achieve this.  Since 2000, over 99% of schools in Devon have developed School Travel Plans and benefited from the grants available.  Schools throughout Devon have developed Travel Plans, to improve children's health and the school environment.    This is not so for many children and travelling to school by car has nearly doubled since 1985.  Children are getting less exercise and congestion outside schools is a serious problem.

Bikeability cycle training

Bikeability is available, free of charge, for all schools in Devon (excluding Torbay and Plymouth) through Devon County Council. Bikeability is intended to be accessbile for all children and all schools - inlduding academies and special schools. For more information please go to http://www.cycledevon.info/cycle-training/cycle-training-for-children.
