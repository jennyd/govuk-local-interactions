How can I buy my home?

Buying your home

If you were a tenant with Wakefield Council and have remained a tenant since the transfer of stock to WDH on 21 March 2005 then you may qualify for the Preserved Right to Buy (PRTB) scheme.

If you became a tenant after transfer, and have remained a tenant with WDH or another social landlord for over five years in total, you may qualify for the Right to Acquire (RTA) scheme.

For further information please contact the Right to Buy team on 01977 724419 or email them at righttobuy@wdh.co.uk.
