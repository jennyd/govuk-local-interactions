Check your options for housing

Service details for Check your options for housing
Service element Service details
Responsibility: Housing Options


Whether you are looking to rent or buy, faced with homelessness or unsuitable accommodation, this page is here to help you find out what possible housing is available to you.

We provide a free housing advice service for everyone in Taunton Deane. This includes people who are looking for accommodation, tenants who rent their home from a private landlord or housing association, homeowners and private landlords.

About this service


The Housing Waiting List  

You can apply to Homefinder Somerset if you are:

  • Anyone over the age of 16
  • Resident in the UK 
  • Not subject to immigration control

Once registered they are placed in a band (bronze, silver or gold) according to their current circumstances and housing need.

To apply or to get further information on Homefinder Somerset visit www.homefindersomerset.co.uk

Taunton Deane Borough Council and housing associations advertise their vacant properties each week. Applicants can express an interest in up to three properties per week, that they are eligible for.

There is a shortage of social housing and the demand is high, our Housing Options team have close links with the private landlords and are able to assist households seeking accommodation, you will need to consider private housing as a solution.

You can find information on private tenancies and current vacancies at www.swpshp.org

Homelessness

We also provide a service to people who are homeless or about to become homeless. Options available include accommodation with Taunton Association for the Homeless or private rented accommodation. In some circumstances the Council can provide emergency and temporary accommodation to those that qualify.

For further information please visit our Homelessness page.

SomersetHomeLet is a one stop site for lettings and covers Sedgemoor, Taunton Deane, North Somerset and West Somerset.

SWeLT

We work with many agencies throughout Somerset and are partners in the Somerset West Landlord and Tenants Services scheme (SWeLT) who provide a wide range of services and products for private tenants, landlords and homeowners.

To find out more please go to the SWeLT website or contact us on:

0845 408 5328
SWELT@sedgemoor.gov.uk


The Housing Options Team here run Taunton Deane's housing waiting list, also known as Homefinder Somerset.
Homefinder Somerset is a choice based lettings scheme which is common to all the five districts in Somerset.
