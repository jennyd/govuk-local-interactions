Day time support

Looking to keep active and to make new friends Our centres have so much to offer:

  • A place to make friends and link with the local community
  • Social activities such as cinema nights and dinner dances
  • A caring and stimulating environment
  • Fully equipped for those with physical conditions or dementia
  • Help with bathing needs
  • Support for carers as well as those being cared for.

For more information please contact your local centre.
