Street care and cleaning

Last updated: 22/12/2015
Report a dead animal on the highway
Report fly-tipping on Council land

We are responsible for cleaning streets and highways and removing litter from highways, roads and Council owned land.

Streets are cleansed based on a zonal system.

Zone   Type of area

Zone 1

High public use eg, town centres and city's

Zone 2

Residential areas

Zone 3

All Other areas

Zone 4

Special Circumstances

It is required that streets in Cornwall should never fall below a grade B of the Cleanliness National Indicators as prescribed by defra.

Please contact us if you have queries or would like to report streets that need cleaning.
