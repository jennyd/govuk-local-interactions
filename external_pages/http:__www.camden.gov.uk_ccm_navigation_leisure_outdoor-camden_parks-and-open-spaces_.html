Parks and open spaces

Managing and maintaining Camden’s green spaces

The grounds maintenance contract which looks after our borough’s parks and open spaces is due to end in 2017. We are now working on our procurement strategy for the next contract and, as part of that, we have engaged with residents to explore how these spaces could be maintained differently to achieve financial savings. 

The online survey has now closed. We received 690 completed surveys as well as comments on social media during the engagement period from 17 September to 30 October. Thank you to everyone who submitted feedback. We are considering all of the responses and your comments will inform our proposal for the new way of delivering the service. This will be presented to Camden’s Cabinet early next year.  

Green spaces

Open spaces help improve the quality of life for people living in, working in and visiting the area. Camden Council manages 68 parks and open spaces, many of which are important historically, such as former burial grounds, manors, private gardens and London squares.

Report it

 If you would like to report an issue or make a general enquiry then please telephone 020 7974 4444.

Alternatively you can email:

  • allotments@camden.gov.uk if you would like to enquire about allotments or food growing in Camden
  • events@camden.gov.uk if you would like to hold an event in a park, or please call 020 7974 5633
  • info@camdenfilmoffice.co.uk if you would like to film/take professional photographs in Camden, including in parks, or please call 0207 620 0391
  • groundsmaintenance@camden.gov.uk if you would like to report a grounds maintenance or parks play and infrastructure issue
  • parks.officers@camden.gov.uk to report anti-social behaviour in a park or please call 020 7974 4444. Please also use this email address if you would like to enquire about nature conservation or have a parks enquiry that is not covered by the other options in this list
  • streetenvironment@camden.gov.uk if you would like to report rubbish, fly tipping or waste collection in a park or open space
