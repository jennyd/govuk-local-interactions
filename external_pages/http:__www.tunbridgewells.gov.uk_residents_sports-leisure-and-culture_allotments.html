Allotments

  • Allotments
  • Community Allotment
  • Parish Allotments

 

Only people who live in Royal Tunbridge Wells town are eligible to put their name down for one of the allotments listed below. All other residents should contact their local Parish Council.

We currently have vacancies at all of our sites except the Reynolds Lane site. If you are interested in one of our allotments and to find out about current vacancies please contact our parks team.

Allotment plot availability : Please use the links to the maps of each allotment to check availability. Available plots are marked on our maps in red, and occupied plots in blue. Areas of green are 'void' areas (e.g pathways).

Barnetts Wood Allotments

Attractive, slightly sloping site located in Andrew Road, High Brooms

Open map in new window

Charity Farm Allotments

Located in Albion Road (also called King George V Hill or Pigs Hill)

Open map in new window

Cunningham Road Allotments

Small allotment to the north of town located in Cunningham Road

Open map in new window

Eridge Road Allotments

Popular and well-worked site. This site is home to the active and well-established Ramslye Allotments and Gardens Association located off of Eridge Road, opposite Ramslye Road

Open map in new window

Ferrars Allotments

Small site, on a south-facing slope to the east of town located in Cornford Lane

Open map in new window

Hilbert Recreation Ground Allotments

This popular site is divided by a footpath which leads to the recreation ground and is located on King George V hill

Open map in new window

Hilbert Road Allotments

Small, attractive and quiet site located on Hilbert Road

Open map in new window

Reynolds Lane Allotments

Small, popular site with some very well-maintained allotments located top of Reynolds Lane

Open map in new window

Sandhurst Road Allotments

Large site which is on a gentle east-west slope located on Sandhurst Road

Open map in new window

Woodlands Allotments

Located next to Woodlands Park, with nice views across town

Open map in new window

Grange Road Allotments

A well-maintained site with some excellent plots located in Wickham Gardens, Rusthall. These allotments are now managed and administered by Rusthall Parish Council.

Please contact Rusthall Parish Council for more information including plot availability.

Rusthall Allotments

Large site, located near the sports field at Rusthall on Southwood Road These allotments are now managed and administered by Rusthall Parish Council.

Please contact Rusthall Parish Council for more information including plot availability.

Hawkenbury Allotments

The land is owned by the Council but managed by the Hawkenbury Allotment Holders Association. Located in Marlpitts site, Hall's Hole Road. Includes a variety of plots including raised beds for those with difficulties bending down. Contact HAHA directly for information

An opportunity to have a taster in Allotment life, you are invited to participate in the Community Allotment in Southborough managed by former members of Transition Tunbridge Wells which has folded.

This will give you an opportunity to find out what is involved in working an allotment and learn how to grow organic food by sharing skills, labour, tools and produce while you wait for your own allotment.

Find out more by phoning Philip on 01892 525280
