Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support

Overview

If you are on a low income you may be entitled to claim Housing Benefit or Council Tax Support.

Housing Benefit can pay for all or part of your rent. The amount you will get depends on your income and circumstances.

Council Tax Support (formerly Council Tax Benefit) is a reduction in your Council Tax bill based on your income and circumstances.

How do I claim it?

  • Submit an Online Claim
  • Download a  Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support Claim Form. [550kb]
  • Pick up a form from one of our Customer Contact Area Offices 

You can also use the online Council Tax Support Calculator to see whether you may be entitled to Council Tax Support.

How long will my claim take?

We aim to process your claim as quickly as possible. The quicker you provide all the information we need, the quicker you will receive your money. The New Claim Evidence Checklist will guide you through what is required.

View your benefit claim online

You can log in to e-Citizen to see

  • How much you are getting
  • How we have worked this out
  • When your next payment will be made
  • The date and amounts of payments already made
  • Letters we have sent you
  • Overpayments that have been billed to you

Other benefits you may be entitled to

To find out if you are entitled to any other benefits please use the Benefit Calculator
