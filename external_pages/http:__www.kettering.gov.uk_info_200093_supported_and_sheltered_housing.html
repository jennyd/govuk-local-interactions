Supported and sheltered housing

  • Sheltered Housing
    Sheltered housing is designed to meet the needs of older people who wish to remain in the community and live independently.
  • Lifeline
    Kettering Borough Council operates a 'Lifeline' scheme for people with poor mobility, serious health problems or those who feel vulnerable in their own homes.
