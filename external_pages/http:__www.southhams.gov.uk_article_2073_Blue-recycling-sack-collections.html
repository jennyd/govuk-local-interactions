Blue recycling sack collections

We want you to recycle as much as possible. In the blue sacks we accept paper and card. This is collected fortnightly along with your brown bin and your clear recycle sack.


Mixed paper and card
Newspapers and magazines
Books
Telephone directories

Yes please

  • Newspapers and magazines
  • Junk mail and envelopes
  • Printing, writing, shredded paper
  • Books, catalogues, telephone directories, yellow pages
  • Gift wrapping paper
  • Cardboard packaging - i.e. cereal packets, washing powder boxes
  • Corrugated card
  • Kitchen roll tubes, egg boxes
  • Greetings cards
  • Excess large brown cardboard to be flattened and placed beside blue sack

No wet or dirty paper, drinks cartons

Remember, your bins and sacks must be at the boundary of your property by 7am on collection day.
