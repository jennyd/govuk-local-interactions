Local Council Tax Support

If you are making a new Local Council Tax Support claim you can find out more information in this section.

You can also visit to find out whether you qualify for benefits by using the Benefits calculator. If you think you might qualify, you can complete your claim using the online claim form.

What is Local Council Tax Support?

Our new Local Council Tax Support scheme for people of working age was introduced on 1 April 2013. It is based on the old Council Tax Benefit scheme, but with five differences.

More about What is Local Council Tax Support?

How do I claim Local Council Tax Support?

You can claim Local Council Tax Support any time.

More about How do I claim Local Council Tax Support?

Can I get any other financial help?

Housing benefit is paid to people on low income who pay rent. People can claim, whether they are in full time work or get social security benefits.

More about Other financial help?

Who can claim Local Council Tax Support?

Anyone who has to pay council tax can claim Local Council Tax Support.

More about Who can claim Local Council Tax Support?

I'm not sure if I qualify for Local Council Tax Support?

Apply using the online claim form on this page and let us work it out for you. It's free and it could save you money.

What do I need to provide to claim Local Council Tax Support?

All claimants will be required to provide original documents to confirm the details on the form and prove their identity.

More about What do I need to provide to claim Local Council Tax Support?

When do I claim Local Council Tax Support?

Claim Local Council Tax Support as soon as you think you need help with your council tax.

More about When do I claim Local Council Tax Support?

How do we work out your Local Council Tax Support?

To work out your Local Council Tax Support, we must compare the money you have coming in each week with the money you need to live on.

More about How do we work out your Local Council Tax Support?

How do we pay Local Council Tax Support?

If you pay council tax, we normally send you a council tax bill showing your Local Council Tax Support entitlement. You then only pay the reduced amount. This means you wont actually get any money, but your council tax will be less.

More about How do we pay Local Council Tax Support?

Do we tell you how we have worked out your Local Council Tax Support?

No. But we do write and tell you how much Local Council Tax Support you will get.

More about Do we tell you how we have worked out your Local Council Tax Support?

What if I disagree with the way you work out my Local Council Tax Support?

If you think we are wrong, ask us to look at your claim again.

More about What if I disagree with the way you work out my Local Council Tax Support?

What changed for Local Council Tax Support in 2014-15?

There are some changes to our Local Council Tax Support scheme for the 2014-15 financial year.

More about What changed for Local Council Tax Support in 2014-15?

What is a discretionary payment?

If you are in receipt of Local Council Tax Support and are struggling financially, you may be able to get some additional help.

More about What is a discretionary payment?

More about What changed for Local Council Tax Support in 2015-16?
