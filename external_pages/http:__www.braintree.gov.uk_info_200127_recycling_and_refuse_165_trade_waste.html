Do I need a trade waste collection?

Braintree District Council has a statutory duty to collect refuse from domestic properties, but waste from business premises is not collected as part of business rates. If you run a business, you will need to arrange a separate contract for the removal of your business/commercial waste. 

Braintree District Council is one of many Registered Waste Carriers who can collect business/commercial waste.

Typical costs

Braintree Council can supply various sized bins to meet the needs of your business.  Below is a sample of some of the options available.  All bin prices shown are for one collection per week and cover the period April 2015 to the end of March 2016.

  • Blue Sacks: Rolls of blue sacks can be purchased for £42 per roll of 20 sacks (= £2.10/sack) from BDC Reception, Causeway House, Braintree. Minimum blue sack delivery order is £42 or 1 roll.  Boxes of 15 rolls (300 sacks) are available @ £620/box. 
  • 240 Litre 2-wheeled bin:  Holds approx 2-3 sacks. Costs £300 per year.
  • 360 Litre 2-wheeled bin:  Holds approx 4-5 sacks and costs £385 per year.
  • 770 Litre 4-wheeled eurobin:  Holds approx 8-9 sacks and costs £578 per year.
  • 1100 Litre 4-wheeled eurobin:  Holds approx 12-14 sacks and costs £767 per year.

The Council also offers a collection service for recycling.

  • Recycling Sacks: Rolls of clear trade waste recycling sacks can be purchased for £32 per roll of 20 sacks (= £1.60/sack).  Customers can telephone customer services on 01376 552525 and arrange payment via debit card or invoice.  Sacks will be delivered direct to Customer. Minimum recycling sack delivery order is £32 or 1 roll. 
  • 360 Litre 2-wheeled bin: Mixed recyclate (plastic packaging, paper, cardboard, cans). Costs £180 per year.
  • 360 Litre 2-wheeled bin: Glass (single colour only) costs £161 per year.
  • 1100 Litre 4-wheeled eurobin: Mixed recyclate (plastic packaging, paper, cardboard, cans). Costs  £438 per year.
  • 1100 Litre 4-wheeled eurobin: Glass (single colour only) costs £361 per year.

(This service is Non Business for VAT purposes - i.e. zero VAT)

For further information or a free quotation please call 01376 552525 or email Tradewaste@braintree.gov.uk
