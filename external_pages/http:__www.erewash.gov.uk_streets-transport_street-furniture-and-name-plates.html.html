Street Furniture and Name Signs

Street furniture

We deal with the maintenance of the street furniture (litter bins, seats).

We are responsible for the maintenance and cleaning of all bus shelters in Erewash, which also includes the replacement of broken panels.

Street name signs

The Council has a duty under section 19 of the Public Health Act 1925 to maintain street name signs. These signs are either installed in the footway or verge, or on a private building or wall. Street name signs on private buildings and walls

Before any work can be undertaken by the Council on street name signs attached to private buildings and walls, the property owner must first complete and return a Consent Form.
