About this service


The Building Control Manger and the Senior Building Control Officer are on call 24 hours a day to respond to all reports of dangerous structures, and are able to take emergency action to deal with them under section 77 & 78 of the Building Act 1984.

How do I use it?


To report a dangerous building or structure please call Building Control on 01823 356470, or email building.control@tauntondeane.gov.uk.

What happens next?


If the building or structure poses a potential danger to the safety of people, we take action to remove the danger. We have powers to require the owners of buildings or structures to repair the defects or direct our contractors to carry out works to make the building or structure safe. We also provide advice on the structural condition of buildings during fire fighting to the fire brigade.
