Garden Waste Services

Garden waste collection service

This service is for Uttlesford residents only and will cost £40 for 2015. 

New customers will normally be required to pay Â£20 for a new Garden Waste bin. Please note that people in receipt of council-administered benefits will be entitled to a 25 per cent discount on this charge.

Go to the Garden Waste page to apply for a continuation of your service or, if you are a new customer, to register for the Garden Waste Collection Service during 2015.

Further information on what can be put in the garden waste bin can be found in the leaflet  'Your Guide to Garden Waste Collection' [6Mb] . For any other enquiries please contact us using the number on the right of this page.

Collection Days

Your green garden waste bin is collected fortnightly. Your garden waste collection day may differ from your regular collection day so please check your letter for your collection day and week.

Please make sure that the bin is at the boundary of your property by 7am on your collection day. Please put the garden waste loose into the bin as plastic bags cannot be taken. If plastic bags are found in your bin, it will not be emptied. Information regarding what can be put in the garden waste bin and those items that you should avoid can be found in the leaflet  'Your Guide to Garden Waste Collection' [6Mb].

Please do not overfill the bin. If garden waste is too compacted it will stick in the bin and this may result in damage when emptying the bin. Placing twigs or dry leaves at the bottom of the bin will help stop the garden waste from sticking in the bin. Excess garden waste placed beside or on top of the bin will not be taken. The bin lid must be closed and all garden waste contained within the bin.

If you need more than one bin to handle your garden waste, please contact us via email to uconnect@uttlesford.gov.uk to order another (annual fee will apply plus a one off bin charge).

Compost bins

Uttlesford District Council has teamed up with Essex County Council and Recycle Now to offer you the opportunity to buy compost bins, which are made from 100 per cent recycled plastic, at bargain prices. 

Garden waste skip service

Alternatively residents who have access, or are local to Saffron Walden, can take advantage of the Recycling Centre for Household Waste to deposit their garden waste.

For residents who do not have access to Saffron Walden, Uttlesford District Council provides weekend drop-off points, at various locations and times across the district. Please note, though, that although the skips are operated by Uttlesford District Council they are the responsibility of the parish councils, so should you require any further details you should please contact your parish council. Contact details for parish councils can be found on our Local Councils page.

Details of the Parish Green Waste Skip Service for 2015 can be found on the page.

If you have any queries which are not covered by the information above, please email greenwaste@uttlesford.gov.uk and we will do our best to answer.
