Parks and open spaces

With over 750 acres of parks and open spaces, including several miles of riverside walks, Spelthorne is a very attractive place to live and work.

We have a positive reputation for the quality and horticultural excellence of our parks, recreation grounds and open paces.

Many are set in grounds boasting floral displays and natural landscapes.

We have plenty of sports grounds plus clubs offering a variety of activities.

You can find your nearest parks using My Spelthorne.
