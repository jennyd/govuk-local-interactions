Health and Social Care

If you need access to Adult Health & Social Care support or advice and information you can contact our Single Point of Access which is open 24/7 (and includes overnight, weekend and Bank Holidays) - on 01472 256 256

Adult Health and Social Care and Adult Safeguarding in North East Lincolnshire are delivered in partnership with the Clinical Commissioning Group (CCG) www.northeastlincolnshireccg.nhs.uk and Focus - independent adult social work www.focusadultsocialwork.co.uk/

Safeguarding adults can include any work or activity which aims to support vulnerable adults to retain independence, well-being and choice and to be able to live a life that is free from abuse and neglect - http://www.focusadultsocialwork.co.uk/single-point-of-access/report-neglect-or-abuse/

www.services4.me.uk is for everyone who lives and works in North East Lincolnshire. If you use adult social care, health care or other help and support services, if you fund your own support, or simply wish to find out more about what services and events are provided in your local community, you can find all the information and advice in one place.

Links to information about our health and social care priorities and children's health and social care services are available below.

The Local Safeguarding Chidren Board (LSCB) is now available at http://www.nelsafeguardingchildrenboard.co.uk/
