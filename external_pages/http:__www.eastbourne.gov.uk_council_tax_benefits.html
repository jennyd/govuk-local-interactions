Council Tax Reduction

Consultation

  • Have your say on our Council Tax Reduction Scheme 2015/17

How do I claim Council Tax Reduction?

You will soon be able to make your claim online. In the meantime, if you want to claim Council TaxReduction please download our application form below.

It is a joint Housing and Council TaxReduction application form that can be used for either or both schemes. You may also need to complete the application form for backdating benefit or reduction.

  • Find out which supporting documentsyou need to provide when you make your claim and how long your application will taketo process

Do not delay returning the form to us as you may lose benefit to which you are entitled. You can claim up to 13 weeks in advance, so if you know you are moving to Eastbourne soon or your circumstances are about to change you can claim now.
