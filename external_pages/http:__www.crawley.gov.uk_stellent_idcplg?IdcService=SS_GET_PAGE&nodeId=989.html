Severe Weather

We will try our best to provide services as usual during severe weather, but this may not always be possible. This page gives you the latest information on any disruptions and useful links for coping with extreme weather.

Don’t forget if you can’t get into the town hall, or if it has to close for any reason, you can always use this website to make payments , give feedback or apply for services.

Stay Connected: sign up for alerts

Our alerts service will keep you informed about any changes due to bad weather. Just provide your email address to start receiving updates.
