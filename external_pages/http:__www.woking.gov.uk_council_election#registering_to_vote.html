Forthcoming Elections

The next scheduled Borough elections will be held on 5 May 2015. These will be held across the whole Borough, following the introduction of new ward boundaries.

Back to top

Postal and Proxy Voting

If you are unable to get to a polling station to vote on the day of the election, you can apply to vote by post or appoint a proxy (someone to vote on your behalf), providing you and your proxy are registered and confirmed using Individual Registration.

Further information about applying for a postal or proxy vote is available.
