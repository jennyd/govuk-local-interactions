Planning decision notices

The Council issues a formal decision notice, which clearly states whether permission is granted or refused. If an application is approved it may be subject to conditions that need to be complied with. Unless otherwise stated, development can begin at any time within 3 years of the date of the permission.

Finding a decision

All decision notices are attached to each planning application and can be viewed by searching for the specific planning application (eg 2014/0001).

Copies of decision notices

You can search for and download decision notices free of charge using our application search.

Search now

 

In writing

You can request copies using the following form:

Order a paper copy

 

You can also email planning@hackney.gov.uk or write to Development Management, 2 Hillman Street, London E8 1FB. 

Charges

  • 1st 10 pages - £10 and £0.50 per page thereafter
  • Supplementary transmission costs: By post £2.50 by email £5.00

Please note: Where applicable VAT is charged at 20%. Payment must be received before your request can be processed. Cheques must be made payable to 'London Borough of Hackney'.
