Road Safety

Welcome to Torbay Council's Road Safety web pages. The aim is to promote the three main areas within Road Safety today - Education, Training and Publicity.
Torbay Council's Road Safety Team work hard to promote Road Safety education and implement safety schemes in Torbay that benefit the whole Community, giving everybody the opportunity to have access to information, training and guidance in all matters relating to Road Safety.
These pages provide important information and offers advice to parents, the general public, teachers and children.  There is access to resources and other web sites.
