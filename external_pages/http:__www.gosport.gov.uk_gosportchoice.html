Welcome to Gosport Choice

Gosport Choice is a service provided by Gosport Borough Council for the Gosport Housing Partnership.

This site is relevant to you if you are looking for a new home or garage with:

  • Gosport Borough Council (for homes and garages)
  • Housing Association Partners (for homes)

The properties we will advertise are for rent.

If you are interested in buying a home on a shared ownership/fixed equity base (buy a percentage of the property and rent the remaining percentage which is owned by the housing association) please go to low cost ownership for details.


You can use this site to search for properties, bid for properties and find out more about the Housing Register and how Gosport Choice works.

If you owe Gosport Borough Council any money, you may not be able to take up a tenancy.
