Bus pass for disabled people

If you are a disabled person living in Gwynedd you are eligible to receive a free disabled person’s bus pass.

The bus pass will enable you to travel for free on any local buses in Wales (some exceptions apply – see the application form).

You can use your bus pass as frequently as you wish, but you will need to show your pass every time you travel. 

The Welsh Government Concessionary Travel scheme will be available between 1 April, 2015 and 31 March, 2017. You can travel all year on the Conwy Valley line but you can only use the ticket between October and March on the Cambrian Coast line.

 

How much does the bus pass cost?

The bus pass is available for free.

 

Can I get a card?

You can claim a disabled person’s bus pass if you:

  • are blind or partially sighted
  • are profoundly or severely deaf
  • are without speech
  • have a disability, or have suffered an injury which has a substantial and long term adverse effect on your ability to walk.
  • do not have arms or have long term loss of the use of both arms.
  • have a severe learning disability.
  • have been refused a driving licence because of your physical fitness.

 

How do I apply?

Download the application form below and return the completed form to the address noted on the form itself.

  • Bus pass for disabled people  – download application form

To request a paper copy of the application form, please complete our online enquiry form or call 01766 771000.

When returning the form, remember to enclose proof of your eligibility (these are noted on the form itself).

 

Refused driving licence

If your driving licence has been refused for medical reasons, it is possible that you are eligible to receive a free bus pass. More information is available on the application form itself:

  • Refused driving licence   – download application form

 

I have lost my bus pass. What should I do?

Please contact us by calling 01766 771000.  There will be a cost of £5 to receive a new bus pass.

 

Is my card valid?

Your pass WILL NOT work if:

  • it has been reported lost or stolen
  • it has been used fraudulently in the past
  • you are no longer entitled to concessionary travel
  • it has been replaced by a more recent pass

Yellow Travel Card Scheme

If you require assistance when travelling on the bus or train you can apply for a Yellow Travel Card - complete the application form or contact the Integrated Transport Unit for further information by calling 01766 771000.
