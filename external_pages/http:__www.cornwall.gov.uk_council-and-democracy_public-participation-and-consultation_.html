Public participation and Consultation

Last updated: 26/01/2016

Your views are important to us, so make sure your voice is heard by telling us what you think.  Our approach to community engagement (the Community Engagement Strategy) makes a particular commitment to improve our coordination of activity.  The links below will direct you to more information on how to let us know your views, and what things we are currently consulting on.

How to have your say

  • Contact your councillor
  • Have your say at Council meetings
  • Send us a comment, compliment or complaint
  • Send a petition to us
