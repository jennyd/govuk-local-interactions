Fostering

In this section...

Thank you for expressing an interest in finding out more about how you and your family may contribute towards providing vulnerable children in Poole with a brighter future.

The following short film ‘Poole Kidz in Care’ describes in their own words the journey into care of 6 children and what it can feel like, their experiences and their views. The young people in care helped write and produce the animated video explaining the difference being fostered has made to their lives and how foster carers can help.
