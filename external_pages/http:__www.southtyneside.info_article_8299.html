Bulky waste collections

Large household items

If you have unwanted items that are too big or heavy to put in your bin, we can collect them from your home.

We can collect bulky items that you would normally take with you if you moved house, such as:

  • Furniture (beds, wardrobes, sofas)
  • White goods (fridges, freezers, washing machines)
  • Electrical equipment (computers, hi-fis)

Collections cost £20 for up to 8 items. 

Request a bulky waste collection online

Large household items

If you have 8 or less unwanted items that are too big or heavy to put in your bin and you can't take them to the Recycling Village at Middlefields we can provide a Bulky Waste service from your home.

Please note, bulky waste collections are only available for domestic properties - not businesses.

We can collect bulky items that you would normally take with you if you moved house, such as:

  • Various furniture (beds, wardrobes, sofas etc.)
  • Household white goods (fridges, freezers, washing machines etc.)
  • Electrical equipment (computers, hi-fi's, vacuum cleaners etc.)

Charges
There is a £20 charge for the removal of up to 8 bulky household items. The charge must be paid for in advance of the collection. Payment can be made over the phone or through our online form.

Collection arrangements for bulky waste items

  1. Once we have received payment we will notify you of your collection day. Put your items out on the day of collection before 7.30am and until 5pm. Do not place items out before this time as this may cause an obstruction.
  2. Place the items for collection on your property where you normally place your waste bins. Crews cannot enter your property, garage or shed.
  3. Do not overfill bags
  4. Items longer than 2 metres (6ft) may not be collected, so please cut up carpet into manageable lengths
  5. If you are putting your items in a back lane, please put your house number on the back door or wall
  6. Stack all items tidily in the same area
  7. The collection team will only remove the items stated on your request form

The information in the above checklist is intended as a general guide. There may be occasions when we will be unable to remove pre-booked items if we believe the health and safety of our employees will be placed at risk by a collection. This includes (but is not restricted to) items that are considered too heavy or dangerous to lift and items that are contaminated in some way or could be considered as hazardous.

If you are unsure whether the items you wish to book for collection are suitable for this service you should seek advice before making payment by contacting our waste team on 0191 427 7000. If you fail to do this and your item(s) cannot be collected, you may still be charged for the time and costs we have reasonably incurred in attending your property.

What happens to the items once they've been collected?

We will remove your old, unwanted items and recycle or dispose of them in an environmentally-friendly way that meets all legal requirements.

Recycling Village (tip)

Don't forget you can drop-off bulky items for free at the Recycling Village (tip).

More than 8 items - house/ garden/ business clearance

If you have more than 8 items or have fixtures or fittings for collection such as a kitchen, bathroom suite, fireplace etc. then our house clearance service might be more suited to you.

For more information visit Business and commercial waste or house and garden clearances.

Come to us for:

  • a quality and trusted service
  • competitive prices
  • a service that meets your individual needs, and
  • advice on environmental issues

Which areas do we cover?

We will collect items from across South Tyneside including South Shields, Hebburn, Jarrow, Boldon, Cleadon and Whitburn.
