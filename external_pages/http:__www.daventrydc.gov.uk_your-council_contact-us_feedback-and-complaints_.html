Feedback and Complaints

Daventry District Council strives to provide high quality services to everyone in the community. However occasionally things do go wrong and need changing and when this happens we need you to tell us, so that we can try to put things right and improve our services to everybody.

We welcome all feedback, whether it is a complaint, comment or compliment as this helps us to continuously improve the standard of services we deliver.

 

How to make a comment, complaint or compliment

* Please use the links below to send us a comment, complaint or compliment.  For all service requests e.g. reporting of a missed bin, requesting of new or replacement items (such as bin(s) or box(es)), reporting of noise complaints and other service requests please visit the online section. *


To make a complaint, comment or compliment:

 

  • Use our online Contact us form 

  • Write to us or print and complete a feedback form and send it to:


          Daventry District Council

          Lodge Road,

          Daventry

          NN11 4FP

 

  • Telephone us on 01327 871100

 

  • Speak to a member of staff in person at our One Stop Shop in Lodge Road, Daventry.
