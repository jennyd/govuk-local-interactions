Housing Register

  • Housing Register
  • Change of circumstances

Although we do not own any affordable housing, we are still responsible for the Housing Register, also known as the housing waiting list. We nominate people from the Housing Register to Housing Association properties when they become vacant.

Anyone over the age of 16 years can apply to join the Housing Register. However you may not be eligible to be included if:

  • you are subject to immigration control
  • you do not normally live in the UK

An eligible person must also qualify to be included on the Housing Register by meeting the Councils Qualification Criteria which include:

  • a local connection to South Northamptonshire
  • a housing need as defined by the Housing Allocations Policy
  • a household income of less than £60,000 
  • insufficient financial assets (capital/savings) to access other forms of tenure
  • demonstrating that you are suitable to be a Housing Association tenant

Applying to join the Housing Register

If you would like to apply to join our Housing Register, you will be required to attend our main offices so that an assessment can be made of your circumstances and housing needs. 

Housing needs assessments and applications to the Housing Register are completed by appointment only. To arrange an appointment, please contact us on 01327 322163 or email: sncallocations@southnorthants.gov.uk.  If necessary a Housing Allocations Officer will help you to complete an application.

If you have a medical condition or care and support needs, which are made worse by your current accommodation, you will also need to complete our Medical Assessment Form when you attend your housing needs assessment. Further information on accommodation for those with care and support needs can be found on our Supported Housing page.

We publish information on the accommodation that has become available every three months. See our Housing Register Statistics page for more information.

Change of Circumstances

If you are not sure whether to report a change, you should do so anyway, in person by making an appointment, by telephone, in writing or by downloading and completing the following form:

  • Change of Circumstances form (pdf)

Forms can be returned to us in person or by post.

You must tell us about all changes of circumstances which might alter your application. Changes include:

Accommodation details:

  • if you move to a new home

Household details:

  • if a child is born
  • if anyone moves in or out of your home
  • pregnancy
  • change to employment status / income
  • change of contact telephone number and/or email address
  • change of preferred area choice you wish to be considered for

If you are a Council or Housing Association tenant in another part of the country and you wish to move to south Northamptonshire our Home Swap page will tell you how.
