Pioneer Places grants

Pioneer Places grants from CosyDevon

Letters have gone out to home-owners and private rented properties in selected areas of North Devon, who could qualify for grants of up to £7,000 to help insulate their homes.

If you receive a letter and would like to book a free assessment call 0800 508 8353 Monday to Friday from 9am to 8pm and Saturday, from 8am to 4pm. Find out more in the latest newsletter or on North Devon News.
