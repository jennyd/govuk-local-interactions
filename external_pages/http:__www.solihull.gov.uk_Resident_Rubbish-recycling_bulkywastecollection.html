Bulky waste collection service

If you have a large household item such as a piece of furniture or a fridge you wish to dispose of, we offer a bulky waste collection service at a small charge.

Alternatively, if you have unwanted furniture which is in good condition, you could donate it to charity, see our furniture reuse page for more details. 

Charges from 1 April 2015 to 31 March 2016

  • collection of 1 item - £13.20
  • between 2 and 5 items - £21.05
  • between 6 and 10 items - £27.90

If you are in receipt of means tested benefits a concession of 25% is available. To receive this reduction you must book your collection by telephoning 0121 704 8000.

Book and pay

Before you request a collection please read our terms and conditions and list of unacceptable items.

Online

Complete our online booking service

By phone

Telephone 0121 704 8000

How do I cancel or change my booking?

If you wish to cancel your booking or change the items or date please telephone 0121 704 8000. Any cancellations or changes to your booking must be in line with our terms and conditions.
