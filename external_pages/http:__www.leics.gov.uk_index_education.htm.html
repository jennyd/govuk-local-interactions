Education & Learning

This has been another record breaking year for Leicestershire after youngsters collected the best ever GCSE results. Provisional results from schools show 74% of students achieved five or more A* - C grades in any subject.
Key stage 3 results (for 14 year olds) are amongst the top 5% in the country with 88% of young people reaching the level 5 benchmark in English, Maths and Science. Our primary schools are also improving with 82% of pupils achieving the benchmark in English and Maths and Science.
Leicestershire is consistently above the national average for the educational Foundation Stage and Key Stages 1 - 3 with over 200 (70%) schools being rated by OfSTED as Outstanding or Good.
School Admissions: Results for the 2014/2015 transfers will be available to view by midday on 2nd March 2015. To get your results, sign onto Online Admissions using your username and password with which you made the application, then click on the results link.
