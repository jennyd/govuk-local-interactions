Season Ticket (Parking Permits)

Season tickets will initially be available to all applicants, however space may be limited in some car park locations, therefore permits will be awarded on a first come first serve basis.

Please note: RESIDENTS PARKING PERMITS to help residents park their vehicles in a street or area where parking is controlled by a Residents Parking Scheme (Controlled Parking Zone) can be obtained from Nottinghamshire County Council.  Please use the offsite links section to 'Apply for a Residents Parking Permit'.

Season Ticket (Parking Permit) Use

The season ticket (parking permit) will allow you to park without the need for a daily payment at the pay and display machines, providing a valid permit is clearly displayed in accordance with the Terms and Conditions.

Season ticket (parking permit) parking will only be permitted in parking places where there is available parking capacity.  Many Broxtowe Borough Council car parks operate at maximum capacity and as such are exempt from the scheme (a list of available locations is detailed above).

The season ticket (parking permit) is only valid in the parking place in respect of which it was issued and up to the date of expiry shown on the permit. A season ticket (parking permit) can be requested for use in one car park only and is not transferable between car parks.

If you park in any other Broxtowe Borough Council car park you will need to pay the normal public charge as advertised on site.

A full list of the season ticket (parking permit) Terms and Conditions can be found on the season ticket application form or on the PDF document listed within the documents section.

Application and Payment

You can make a online application via the Season Ticket Request Form.

Alternatively you can complete the season ticket application form and return it with the appropriate payment (cash or cheque) for a quarterly or annual permit. Your application will be dealt with within 5 working days.

Renewing your Permit

The online application form can also be used for permit renewals.  Responsibility for applying for the renewal of a permit rests with the permit holder to whom it was issued.  The permit may be renewed within 21 days in advance of the expiry date.  Please allow a minimum of 5 working days for renewals and/or amendments.
