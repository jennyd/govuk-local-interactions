Your garden waste collection service

Recycling your garden waste

The council offers its residents a seasonal recycling collection service for your garden waste for an annual one off subscription charge. For your subscription you will receive 2 garden sacks for collecting and storing your waste, additional sacks can be purchased for a small charge. Collections run on a fortnightly basis between the period of April and October on the same day that your black bags are collected.

The type of garden waste you can recycle in this way

  • Plants, flowers and weeds
  • Grass and leaves
  • Hedge clippings

We are not able to accept

  • Kitchen waste – please use your brown food caddy instead
  • General waste – please recycle as much as possible
  • Soil, rubble or logs
  • Invasive species such as Japanese Knot Weed

For more information please contact (01656) 643643 or email cleanupthecounty@bridgend.gov.uk

Remember you can always recycle your garden waste free of charge at your nearest Household Recycling Centre along with a lot of other materials.

Good news! Your garden waste collections are here again for 2016

The one off annual subscription charge for 2016 is £27 with a concession of £23 available for pensioners. Registrations can be made from the 1 February onwards, to register all you need to do is contact the council on (01656) 643643 with your contact details and make a payment. Collections of garden waste commence on week beginning 4April and final collections will be made week ending 28 October.  

Conditions of the service

To help the service run smoothly please take time to read the following conditions:

 

The collections will take place on a fortnightly basis between the first week of April and the last week of October on the same day that your black bags are collected.
Sacks are to be placed outside at the kerbside between 7pm the evening before and 7am on the day of collection.
The service is available to Bridgend County Borough Council residents only.
The payment is a one off subscription for the period between April 2016 and October 2016 inclusive which must be made in advance of receiving the collection service. It is non-refundable.
The service cannot be transferred from one resident to another but if you move house the service will be transferred to the new address if it’s still within Bridgend County Borough. Please contact the council with your new address details.
Only permitted items may be disposed of and must be loose in the sacks and not contained in bags. Garden sacks contaminated with non – permitted items will not be collected until the items are removed and the sacks presented for the next scheduled collection.
If the sacks are regularly contaminated further advice will be provided.
The council accepts no liability for injury due to improper use of sacks.
To purchase additional sacks (£5), replace a missing or damaged sack, or to report a missed collection, call 01656 643643
