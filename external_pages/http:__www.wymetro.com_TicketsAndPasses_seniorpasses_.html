  • Travel benefits in West Yorkshire and beyond for those of pensionable age.

  • Senior Pass

    Senior Passes are available to all those of pensionable age in West Yorkshire. It can be used within the county and across England. 

    • Log in to your online account
     

  • Eligibility & when to apply

    You must be of pensionable age and a West Yorkshire resident to apply for a Metro Senior Pass.

     

    When to apply

    Benefits - what do I get?

    Free, off-peak bus travel in England and half-fare, off-peak train travel inWest Yorkshire.

     

    More Senior Pass benefits

    Your first Senior Pass?

    You can apply online, or at a Bus Station Travel Centre. Remember to take ID and proof of residency.

     

    Apply online now

    Lost / stolen / damaged

    You can replace a lost, stolen or damaged Senior Pass online or at a Bus Station Travel Centre.

     

    Replace a lost, stolen or damaged Pass

    Renew your Senior Pass

    If your Senior Pass is due to expire, you can renew it online.  You need to have your existing Senior Pass number to do this.

     

    Renew your Pass online
