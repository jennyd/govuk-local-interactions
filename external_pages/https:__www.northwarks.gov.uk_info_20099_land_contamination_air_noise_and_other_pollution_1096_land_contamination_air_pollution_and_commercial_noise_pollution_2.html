Contaminated Land

North Warwickshire Borough Council’s Contaminated Land Inspection Strategy and updates can be viewed online.

North Warwickshire Borough Council’s Contaminated Land Register currently has no Contaminated Land Sites determined under Part IIA of the Environmental Protection Act 1990. As soon as a new site is determined this page will be updated.

Details of contaminated land within 250m of an address can be obtained by requesting a detailed contaminated land search. There is a fee for the search to be undertaken which I currently £60.30 . To request a detailed contaminated land search contact Environmental Health.

If you have concerns relating to contamination of land please contact us via our pollution control online form.
