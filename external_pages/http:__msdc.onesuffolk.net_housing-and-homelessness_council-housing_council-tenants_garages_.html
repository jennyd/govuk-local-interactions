Garages

Council garages are a convenient and secure way to store your car. They are economical to rent and are available to all residents although, priority is given to council tenants of the Councils.

Council garages are intended for motor vehicles and must not be used for the storage of combustible materials or to conduct a business. No alterations can be made to garages and they cannot be sub-let or assigned without the prior consent of the Council. Garages must not be used in a manner that causes nuisance or annoyance to others. 

The Council accepts no responsibility for damage to vehicles in or near garages.

How do I apply for a garage?

Please apply for a garage online (opens new window) or call us on 01473 825757 and we can post an Application Form out to you.

Garages are not always immediately available. Your name will be placed on a waiting list if there are no vacancies at the time you apply.

Please note: tenants whose rent account are in arrears will not be allocated a garage.

Where are Mid Suffolk District Council's garages located?

Mid Suffolk District Council has garages to rent in a number of locations across the district.

Please use the link on the left for a list of Council Garage Locations.

How much does it cost to rent a garage?

The weekly cost to a Mid Suffolk District Council Tenant for the rent of a Mid Suffolk District Council Garage for 2015/2016 is £7.31 per week, there is no VAT chargeable to council tenants. The cost to applicants who are not tenants of Mid Suffolk District Council for garage rent is £8.77 (£7.31 plus VAT). 

How do I end my garage tenancy?

To end a garage tenancy two weeks notice must be given commencing on a Monday to Mid Suffolk District Council, 131 High Street, Needham Market, IP6 8DL. Please call us on 01473 825757 to request for a Termination Form, Alternatively you can complete our online garage tenancy termination form (opens new window). 

Keys must be sent back to us at the address above by the end of the two week period by Special Delivery, or delivered by hand to the Council offices where you will issued with a receipt.

KEYS MUST NOT BE SENT IN THE POST UNLESS SENT BY SPECIAL/RECORDED DELIVERY.
