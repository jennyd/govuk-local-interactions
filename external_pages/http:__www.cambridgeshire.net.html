Social care and community support options on Cambridgeshire.net

Did you know you can now find details of your local social care and support providers on Cambridgeshire.net?

More and more providers of services for older people and people with a disability will be listed, including home care providers and day services. Find out who is offering services such as live-in care and community support in your area.

You can find out more about your social care and support options by contacting Cambridgeshire County Council.
