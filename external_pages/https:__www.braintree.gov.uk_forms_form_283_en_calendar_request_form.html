Please complete this form to get a collection calendar

The calendar shows the recycling and refuse collection dates throughout the year including the Christmas and Bank holiday periods.

How long will it take for my collection calendar to arrive?

You should receive your collection calendar within seven working days. Alternatively you can download your collection calendar your self from our website.

How to download your collection calendar

Please enter your address/postcode in the address search field to the left hand side of the page. Select your address from the list that is presented, and then select "Click Here to Download calendar" that is also on the left hand side of the page.
