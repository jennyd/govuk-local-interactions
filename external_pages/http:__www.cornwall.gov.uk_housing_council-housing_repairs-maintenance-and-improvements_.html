Repairs, maintenance and improvements

Last updated: 15/01/2016

Report a repair

To report a repair you can contact us in the following ways:

If you have an out of hours emergency call 0300 1234 161.

Online form

E-mail us at info@cornwallhousing.org.uk

Call us on 0300 1234 161

  • Press option 3
  • Press option 1 or  2 depending on your location

When reporting a repair please be ready to give the following details:

  • Your name 
  • Your address
  • A day time telephone number where you can be reached
  • When someone is at home so the repair can be carried out or an inspection can be made
  • As much information about the repair as possible

Please choose from one of the subjects below for further information about repairs, maintenance and improvements:

  • Repairs appointments
  • Repairs to communal areas 
  • Common problems
  • Improving your home
  • Planned Maintenance section for scheduled repairs information

  • Mobility scooters, electric or manual wheelchairs
