Starting at a Primary or Junior school in September 2016

Primary School Admissions covers those pupils starting at either:

  • a Primary school at the beginning of the reception year
  • a Junior school at the beginning of Year 3

All applications for a school place will need to be made to your home authority - this is who you pay your council tax to. Royal Borough residents can apply for non-Royal Borough schools through the Royal Borough application process. Full details of the process are published in the Starting School Guide.

Applications can be made online from Monday 19 October 2015 to the closing date of midday on Friday 15 January 2016.  Please note that due to system issues currently being experienced, you may face some delay in receiving your registration email.  In most cases you should receive it straightaway but in other cases it may be delayed for up to 12 hours.

Paper application forms are available on request.  National Offer Day is Monday 18 April 2016.

Submitted applications can be amended up to the closing date and, following this, you may still view your application online. You can also access the online system on National Offer Day to view the outcome of your application. 

The Online application facility is not available for late applicants or those applying for an In Year Transfer. These applicants should contact the admissions team in order to receive the relevant application form.

Children can start full-time at school from the September following their fourth birthday.
