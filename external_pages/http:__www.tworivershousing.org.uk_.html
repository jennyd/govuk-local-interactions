More than just a landlord

Two Rivers Housing has provided affordable homes in Gloucestershire and Herefordshire since 2003.

We have 4,000 properties of various sizes and styles and an ambitious building programme that will increase the choice for people who wish to live in this beautiful part of the country.

However, we are more than just a landlord. We want to create happy and prosperous communities and do this in a variety of ways.

For example, we support local people and businesses through employment, we help groups to set up cafes, nature trails, lunch clubs, allotments and play areas, we advise our tenants how to manage their household budgets and we encourage children to become ambassadors for their area through our Junior Warden scheme.

Please take a look around our website to find out more about what we have achieved in the past, and our aspirations for the future.
