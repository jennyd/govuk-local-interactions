Thanet District Council operates a Dog Warden Service which is available on the following two week rotation: 

  • 1 week 10.30 to 18.00 Monday to Saturday 
  • 1 week 07.00 to 15.00 Tuesday to Friday 

For more information, or to contact the service: 

  • Email: Streetscene.Enforcement@thanet.gov.uk
  • Call (01843) 577 000*

*Call Thanet District Council on the above number outside of these times to reach the out of hours service. 

The majority of a Dog Warden’s duties fall into 4 areas:

  • Stray dogs & lost dogs
  • Noisy dogs
  • Dangerous dogs
  • Dog fouling & beach bans

 

If you would like more information please contact:

The Community Dog Wardens on:

01843 577000
