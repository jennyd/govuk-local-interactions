Information

Free advice on disability issues

Papworth Trust provides free independent advice, information and practical support on housing, disability issues, care, and finances. We carry out free home visits for older and disabled people and their families and carers.

We can advise you on:

  • Housing services so you can stay living independently at home, including repairs and adaptations.
  • Grants and loans for home adaptations and improvements. We can help you to apply for Local Authority and other grants and loans such as Disabled Facility Grants, Decent Home Grants, Home Repair Grants, Home Repair Loans and Energy Efficiency funding if you are eligible.
  • Finances including applying for allowances, charitable funding and equity release.
  • Claiming benefits. We can help you get what you are entitled to, such as Attendance Allowance, Personal Independence Payment (PIP), pension credit or council tax benefit.
  • Care and support in your own home, in specialist housing or getting out and about.
  • Home safety assessments and information about moving to somewhere more suitable.

Costs and booking

Our advice service is free of charge.

Please call us on 0116 279 0153 or email homesolutions@papworthtrust.org.uk to find out more or to book a home visit.

We also give advice over the phone or by email to anyone who lives in the UK. Please call us on 0800 952 5000 between 10am-5pm Monday to Friday or email info@papworthtrust.org.uk to find out more.

Warm homes, healthy homes

We're working with fuel poverty charity National Energy Action (NEA) to help you stay warm and healthy in your home. With funding from Leicestershire County Council, we can help you:

  • save money on energy costs
  • recommend ways to create a warmer, healthier home
  • access the right services to complete improvements to your home
  • explain grants and benefits you may be entitled to

Contact us for a free home visit on 0300 333 6544 or email hiacustomersupport@papworthtrust.org.uk

Home adaptations, repairs, improvements

If you want your home adapting to your needs, we can offer free information and advice and a full adaptation and repairs service.

We regularly install stair lifts, central heating, access ramps and wet rooms. We can also help you plan extensions and widen doors.

Costs and booking

We will let you know if we think you may be eligible for any grants or funding, and can help you to apply for them. We offer no obligation quotes which we keep as low as possible.

Please call us on 0116 279 0153 or email homesolutions@papworthtrust.org.uk to find out more or to book a free assessment.

 

Handyperson services

Our handyperson service’s friendly, experienced, Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) checked staff can help you with odd jobs around your home.

We can:

  • put up shelves or curtain rails and build flat pack furniture
  • do minor carpentry and plumbing
  • fit locks and bolts and key safes
  • hang pictures
  • fit grab and stair rails
  • fit smoke detectors and more.

Costs and booking

The service costs £18 per hour. Please call us on 0300 456 2048 or email homesolutions@papworthtrust.org.uk to find out more.

 

DIY Hub in Hinckley

At our DIY Hub in Hinckley, community volunteers learn skills to make adaptations to furniture and fittings for those in need.

We can

  • make special adaptations for people with dementia, such as signage and brightly painted furniture to help with deteriorating vision
  • adapt or build customised shelving
  • put together flat pack furniture

We’re working with Hinckley-based charity Worklink to deliver the project. Worklink supports local people with mental ill health.

Please call us on 0116 303 3232 to find out more.

Business Services

Making your business more accessible

We offer access audits to help your business or organisation meet the requirements of the Equality Act and to provide a better experience for disabled customers.

You can tailor the audits to suit your needs. Our audits can include:

  • a walk and talk advice service
  • a report-based service with recommendations
  • a report and architectural service
  • full project management service from report to facilitation of changes
  • access statements
  • accessibility audits

Costs and booking

We offer no obligation quotes which we keep as low as possible. We will let you know if we think you may be eligible for any grants or funding.

To find out more, please call us on 01279 648 408 or email homesolutions@papworthtrust.org.uk
