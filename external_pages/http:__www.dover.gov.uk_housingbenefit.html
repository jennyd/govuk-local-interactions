Housing Benefit

Housing Benefit is a scheme to help people pay their rent, it is a benefit based on your financial and household situation. To apply you will need to fill in a claim form and provide proof to support your claim.

If you are making a new claim for Housing Benefit and you rent your property from a private landlord, most claims will fall under the Local Housing Allowance Scheme.
