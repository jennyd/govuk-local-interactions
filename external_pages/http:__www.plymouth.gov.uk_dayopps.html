Day opportunities

What is the Day Opportunities framework?

We have been working with providers across the city to create the new Day Opportunities framework.

The framework is a list of 30 Council accredited providers offering over 200 different types of community activities or one to one sessions. You can view the available activities on the Plymouth Online Directory.

The selection of day opportunities are varied and tailored to individual specific interests and hobbies and range from cooking clubs, music tuition, sports sessions, support groups and many more.
