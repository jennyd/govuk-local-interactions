Adoption

Bradford Council welcomes adopters from all backgrounds who live in Bradford, Keighley and the surrounding area. We’re looking for adopters who are as individual as the children who need adopting. So whether you’re single, gay, married, in work or unemployed, don’t have children or already have children - if you’re interested in adopting we’d like to hear from you. Please email adoptionfostering@bradford.gov.uk or give us a call on 01274 434331.
