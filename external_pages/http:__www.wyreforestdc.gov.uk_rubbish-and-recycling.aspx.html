Rubbish and Recycling

Please put your bin at the kerbside by 6am for collection. Please do not cause an obstruction to neighbours, pedestrians or vehicles but make sure we can see and get to your bin easily.

For households with communal bin stores, bins will normally be collected from the stores provided they are open and not blocked by vehicles. 

If you are unsure where is the best place to put your bin for collection please contact us and we will advise you. If you need help getting your bin out for collection please request a pull out service.

Events

If you're planning an event and would like the recycling team to attend, please contact us.

We can tailor our information to focus on home composting, Love Food Hate Waste or general recycling information. We can have an informal chat to small groups of people or bring our event caravan to large events across the district (subject to access and staffing).
