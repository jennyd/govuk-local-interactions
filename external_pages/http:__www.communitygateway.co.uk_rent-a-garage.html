Rent a garage

People looking for a new home in Preston and the surrounding area have two main options:

  • Finding a new home through the Select Move service
  • Swapping homes with another tenant or tenants

 

The Community Gateway Association (CGA) owns a range of garages in Preston, which are rented out on a weekly basis. Rental prices start from as little as £8.21 per week.
