Discounts and exemptions

A full Council Tax bill is based upon two adults living in a house.

Discounts are available if:       

  • you are living alone       
  • you or someone you live with has a disability        
  • members of your household are not counted or 'disregarded' for the purposes of Council Tax

For a full list of people who are disregarded for Council Tax, visit: Anglia Revenues Partnership - discounts

For a full list of exemptions, visit: Anglia Revenues Partnership - exemptions

If you are on a low income and need help paying your Council Tax, visit: Council Tax reduction scheme
