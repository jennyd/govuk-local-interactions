Empty Properties

The Somerset West Landlord and Tenant Services Scheme (SWeLT) has been set up to assist Private Sector Landlords, Tenants and Owners across the three districts of Sedgemoor, Taunton Deane and West Somerset.

This scheme is a partnership between Councils and agencies who offer private sector landlords, tenants and owners unique access to a range of local housing products, services and support.

SWeLT services are free, and specialist teams are on hand to offer you personal advice and support covering a wide range of housing topics, such as:

  • Advice for homeowners
  • Advice for landlords
  • Advice for tenants
  • Energy efficiency
  • Empty homes
  • Enforcement
  • Disabled Facility Grants

To find out more, please go to the SWeLT website.
