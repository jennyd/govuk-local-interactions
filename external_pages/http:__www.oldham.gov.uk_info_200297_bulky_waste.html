Collection of large items

Oldham Council works with Bulky Household Collection Service to collect bulky household waste from your doorstep.

  • Up to 3 items: £16
  • Additional items: £8 each
Bagged up fabric and small electrical items (like kettles and toasters) are collected for free as part of your paid collection.

Charges go towards the cost of collection and disposal of items that cannot be recycled.

Book and pay

There are 3 ways you can book and pay for a collection:

  • Over the phone using a credit/debit card: 0161 770 6644
  • Use the freephone at a library using cash or credit/debit card: Find a library
  • At Moorhey Street depot using cash or a cheque (if cash, please bring correct change): Moorhey Street, Oldham, OL4 1JF

Picking up items

Items can be collected from:

  • Inside your property (please make sure access is possible, or be home to let us in)
  • On your property e.g front or back garden, garage or shed (please make sure access is possible, or be home to let us in)

Make sure items are:

  • Accessible when we arrive to collect them
  • Clean and neat
  • Dry and covered (if possible)
  • Clearly identifiable
  • Only those described at the time of booking. 
We cannot give refunds for non-collection if these conditions are have not been met.

Who is the Bulky Household Collection Service?

The Bulky Household Collection Service are an award-winning business who aim to re-use, recycle and refurbish at least 25% by tonnage of the items they collect.

Thousands of items of furniture picked up by the crews are made available to families on low income.
