Bulky waste collections

Last updated: 06/02/2016
 
Live chat:

 

You can use our bulky waste collection service for items that are too big for normal household rubbish and recycling collections.  There is a charge for this service.

You can also take your bulky waste to our household waste recycling centres.  Or you can donate usable furniture and household items to RE:SOURCE in Bodmin, Heartlands Furniture in Pool, GOFA in Penzance, Cornwall and Plymouth Furniture Reuse Project or charity shops.

How do I book a bulky waste collection?

Book and pay for bulky waste collection

Before committing to payment, you will be shown your bulky waste collection day. 

Please read our terms and conditions before booking. 

What does a bulky waste collection cost?

The charge depends on the items. See our lists for details:

  • Standard bulky items – £22 for up to four items, extra items £10 each (maximum of 4 extra items)
  • Small special bulky waste items – £20 each
  • Large special bulky waste items – £35 each

Are there any bulky waste items you don’t collect?

There are some items we don’t collect and we can’t take business waste.

If you have something you want collected that’s not on any of our lists, contact us using the details on the right of this page.

Where should I leave my bulky waste for collection?

On your booked collection day, leave your waste on your property as close to your normal collection point as possible.  Contact us if this is a problem.
