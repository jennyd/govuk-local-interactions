Equipment and adaptations

What type of equipment is available?

If you have difficulty getting around the house or climbing the stairs, we may be able to give you support.

Firstly, we will assess what you need and then look at how we can help you to live independently. This could be:

  • Fitting a stair lift;
  • Installing a bed hoist;
  • A level access shower;
  • A ramp to get in and out of your home.

You could also think about moving into sheltered or supported housing, which is another good way of staying independent. If you have limited mobility, this type of accommodation is specifically designed to suit your requirements. Follow the link below for more information.

Supported and sheltered housing

Who is eligible for this?

We may be able to provide large items of equipment or adapt certain areas around the house if you live in Stockport, are 18 years old or over, and require assistance because of frailty or illness, a physical or learning disability or sight or hearing loss.

When you get in touch someone from our Equipment and Adaptations Service will arrange a time and date to see you for an assessment, to see how we can make life easier for you. We could come along to your house and meet you so that you can look at the problems with you. Otherwise we can talk about your needs with you on the phone.

Following the assessment we will agree your needs with you and, according to how serious your situation is, talk about the help and support available.

How much would this cost?

It is difficult to say as every case is different and the costs will depend on the type of adaptations to your home, or which piece of equipment you need. It will also depend on if you live in rented accommodation or if you own your home.

Will I be able to receive help with the cost?

You can receive help with the costs, especially if you are on benefits. If that is the case, we will apply for a Disabled Facilities Grant (DFG) for you. To receive this you will be asked for details of your finances. The amount of money you pay towards the cost will depend on your income and any savings you have.

Get in touch

If you wish to enquire about equipment or an adaptation for yourself or someone else, please fill in our online form to contact the Equipment and Adaptations service. They will then get in touch with you to discuss your requirements and arrange a visit to talk through the help that is available.
