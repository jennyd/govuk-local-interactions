Gardening activity - New Leaf Horticultural Therapy

New Leaf Horticulural Therapy is based on an allotment site less than two miles from Newbury town centre. I have managed this project for the last seven years with the Newbury Community Mental Health Team. I have since June 2011 taken it on as a self employed service provider under contract to West Berkshire Council. The project aims to promote a feeling of greater social inclusion and self esteem through the activity of raising and tending to vegetables and flowers through the seasons. From sowing, nurturing, planting and maintaining the crops until their eventual harvesting and sharing. Throughout this cycle teamwork develops and is strengthened week in and week out by our emphasis on rest breaks, conversation  and group participation. We also have frequent soup (own produce) sessions, monthly outings, and BBQ's in the season. We also meet throughout the winter months to carry out small projects and maintenance needs and also more valuably, just to spend time together. The group size for any session is limited due to the scope of our plots (about  4 - 5) and understandably attendances can be erratic but enquiries are most welcome throughout the year.

Using shortlists

As you browse around this website you can build up your own personal shortlist of services and activities to display here. Using shortlists is a bit like using the shopping basket function on online shopping sites. Shortlists provide a good way of collecting together information about services which may be of interest to you. When you are viewing your shortlist, you can display a summary version or a detailed version of the shortlist by selecting the appropriate link. You can also send the shortlist to your printer to get a printed version of your list.

You can add items to your shortlist by clicking the 'Add to shortlist' button displayed alongside each item on your search hit lists or when viewing the details of an item. Items are removed by selecting 'Remove from shortlist' alongside that item.

Your shortlist information is stored in a temporary part of your browser, so if you close down your browser the shortlist will be cleared. You can also clear the shortlist at anytime by going to the shortlist and selecting 'Clear shortlist' from the onscreen options.
