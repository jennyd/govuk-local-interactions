"Where I Live" Help
Search for your property
Type some details from your address into the search box, above. Your postcode, house number or even road name, are generally a good start.

Results
The first fifty results from the database are then displayed. If your road has quite a common name or has a lot of houses on it, it is possible that your property will not be in the first fifty results returned. If this happens, try to narrow down the search by adding more details from your address, perhaps your postcode.

Problems finding your address
The address shown is in part formatted from geographic street data. It will therefore not necessarily be an exact match to the established Royal Mail Postcode Address File record.

Feedback
If you cannot find your address at all, please let us know through the Feedback form, telling us the address you were looking for and your email address so we can reply.

Stars and Circles
On all the maps, the red star denotes the position of the selected property. When viewing particular sections of information, yellow circles appear on the map in addition to the red star.

These yellow circles are other properties or sites which are relevant to the section being viewed, and help to show the local proximity to the property.

Stroud and External Services
The information available for each property is split into those services for which we have data, and those which are provided externally. Unfortunately we cannot correct data on external service websites; please contact them if you find any inaccuracies on their websites.
