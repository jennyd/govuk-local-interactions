Welcome to Castle Point Borough Council

This website is primarily designed for local residents, giving them an opportunity to find out more about their council and access its services. However this site also contains information to help local community groups, businesses and visitors to our borough.

The Borough of Castle Point, situated on the coastline of south-east Essex on the northern side of the Thames estuary,has an area of 17.3 square miles and a population of approximately 86,000 people.
