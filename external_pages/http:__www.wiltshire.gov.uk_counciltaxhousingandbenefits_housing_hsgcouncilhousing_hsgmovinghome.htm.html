Customer notice

We are currently updating our website with a cleaner, mobile-friendly design. This page will be updated with the new design soon. Thank you for your patience.

This section contains information for council tenants about:

  • Transfers - what are they and how do I apply?
  • Exchanging your home
  • The Right to Buy your council property
  • Ending a council tenancy
  • Our Letting Standard
  • Subletting and lodging

We have factsheets about moving home, lodging and sub-letting, and exchanging your council property.

If you are interesting in becoming a council tenant, please visit our choice-based letting website, Homes 4 Wiltshire, where you can find information about making an application for housing.
