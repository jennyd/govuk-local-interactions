Neighbourhood Watch

Neighbourhood Watch is a way of helping your street, village or area prevent crime and vandalism. 33% of households are involved in Neighbourhood Watch and this number continues to grow. 

For more information contact: nhw.darlington@durham.pnn.police.uk or 01325 406788 

Our Watch website [external link]

darlingtonnhw.ourwatch.org.uk [external link]

Police and crime commissioner

Ron Hogg is the Police and Crime Commissioner (PCC) for County Durham and Darlington. The role of the PCC is to represent the voice of residents and hold the police to account.

For more information about the PCC visit the Office of the Durham Police and crime commissioner website [external link]

Police force

Durham Constabulary [external link] is the police force which looks after Darlington.

#deaddrunk

We support Durham Constabulary's #deaddrunk campaign, which encourages pedestrians to keep safe when planning their way home after a night out.
