Privacy Policy
Personal information
All personal information asked for will only be used for the purpose indicated. It will not be used for any other purpose without first obtaining your permission and will not be kept for longer than necessary.
Use of IP addresses
IP addresses are only collected for the sole purpose of system administration and for auditing the use of the site. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable, which means that while your user session will be logged you will remain anonymous to us.
Use of cookies
We only use cookies to remember the information you have provided to us when first registering on the website. Cookies are also used if reporting a street light fault and selecting the ‘remember me’ option.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
Contacting us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement or data protection issues generally, you can contact lightsonindorset@ssecontracting.com
If you have any comments or questions about the accuracy of information on this site please contact lightsonindorset@ssecontracting.com
