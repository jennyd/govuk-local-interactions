Alcohol and Entertainment Licences

Planning on providing certain types of entertainment, supplying alcohol or being involved with club premises? You may need a premises certificate and a personal licence.

  • Temporary Event Notices

    Temporary Event Notices

    You will need to apply for a Temporary Event Notice if you want to carry out a one-off licensable activity in unlicensed premises.

  • Personal Licence Application

    Personal Licence Application

    All sales of alcohol must be made by (or under the authority) of a Personal Licence holder.

  • Licensing Act 2003 - Premises Licence and Club Certificate Reviews

    Licensing Act 2003 - Premises Licence and Club Certificate Reviews

    Apply for a review to take place and view current applications.

  • Premise Licence

    Premise Licence

    A premise licence is required for any premise (including vehicles, vessels or moveable structures) which is to be used for licensable activities.

  • Club Premises Certificate

    Club Premises Certificate

    You need to apply for a club premises certificate if you're a social, sporting or political members club.
