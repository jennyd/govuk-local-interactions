Allotments and Community Gardens

Plymouth has 35 allotment sites and nearly 1,700 allotment tenants.

Allotments and community gardens are a great way to:

  • grow your own food
  • enjoy the fresh air
  • learn about cultivation
  • observe wildlife
  • meet people and make friends
  • improve your health and wellbeing

We have listed our allotment sites, including a map, included frequently asked questions and there is an online form if you would like to apply for an allotment.

For the very best advice and to find out what to expect from having a plot please refer to The National Allotment Society website.

We have a team of friendly allotment staff who can offer advice and support. Contact us by telephone or email. Or why not talk to your allotments officer or maintenance team if you see them on site!

There is an annual allotment charge for a plot and to be a member of a community garden.
