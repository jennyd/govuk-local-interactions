Pay Online

Paying for services online

Welcome to the Cannock Chase Council 24 hour secure Internet payments service. You may make a variety of payments using this secure site with most major credit or debit cards.

To be transferred to our secure payment environment please select the payment you wish to make from the list below or click PAY IT Icon

  • Sundry Debtors
  • Housing Benefit Overpayment
  • Mortgages
  • Rents
  • Council Tax
  • Business Rates NNDR
  • BIDS
  • Penalty Charge Notices

Most credit and debit cards are accepted, including Mastercard, Visa, Switch and Solo.

Payments can also be made using an automated telephone payments system 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. By calling 0845 2340080 any time of the day or night and following the prompts, you can pay by credit/debit card at your convenience.

If you have any problems or concerns, please email counterservices@cannockchasedc.gov.uk
