Welcome to the Peterborough Families Information Service

 

Welcome to the Families Information Service for Peterborough.

We are a free, impartial one-stop-shop service providing information, advice and assistance to families or prospective families on all aspects of childcare, including available funding, and family life in general

Please click on the relevant boxes on the right to find out more, or select the appropriate tab above

Click on this link to see information about the Peterborough Local Offer

If you cannot find what you are looking for, or need more assistance please ring us on 01733 864446 between 9.00am and 5.00pm Monday to Friday or use the ‘Contact Us’ tab above

Please email any information regarding clubs, groups, events and activities to fis@peterborough.gov.uk
