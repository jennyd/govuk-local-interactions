Students with special educational needs

Students at special schools and other students who have a learning disability (including post 16 provision)

Transport may be provided irrespective of the minimum distance and other criteria in our policy, where it's accepted that transport is necessary to enable access to the education students require.

Home to school transport policy 2015/2016 (PDF, 86.5KB).

 

Students with a learning disability

Transport assistance may be provided to students with a learning disability who begin a course after their 19th birthday.

In these cases, agreement would be required Suffolk County Council. Please submit an application form on the link below for us to assess this.

Submit an application for assessment (external)

 

Learner support grants

Some colleges in Suffolk may be able to assist students through learner support grants.

You should contact your college directly to find out more.

 

Disabled persons free travel bus pass

Eligible older and disabled people are entitled to free off-peak travel on local buses anywhere in England.

Find out more information and apply for a disabled persons free travel bus pass.

 

Disability living allowance

Some students may be able to use their disability living allowance to support them with their transport needs.

Find out how to claim disability living allowance (external).

 

Further information

You can find out more information about our home to school transport policies and the services provided on this website.

You can also call us on 0345 6066173.
