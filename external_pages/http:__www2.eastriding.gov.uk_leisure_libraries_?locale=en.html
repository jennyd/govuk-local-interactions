Libraries

The Library Service has lots to offer all ages.  Anyone can join the library and it is free to join, borrow and request books and other items. 

Join online today and in addition to 23 static libraries and mobile libraries with locations all over the East Riding you can join, renew and request books from home.  You can also access the Digital Reference Library online, with a wide range of information for study and homework.
