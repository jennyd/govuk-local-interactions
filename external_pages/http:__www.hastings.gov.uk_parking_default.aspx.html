All on-street parking matters are the responsibility of East Sussex County Council, including all Pay and Display machines, permits for On-Street parking and the issue and enforcement of Penalty Charge Notices.

Visit www.eastsussex.gov.uk or telephone the Parking Shop on 0345 6801129 for further information.

NSL Services will enforce the the On-Street parking restrictions on behalf of the County Council, as they do in Eastbourne and Lewes.

All Off-Street car parks, including associated Pay and Display machines, season tickets and Penalty Charge Notices in Hastings remain the responsibility of Hastings Borough Council.
