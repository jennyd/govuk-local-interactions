Rochdale Stay Well

We can help you make healthier choices and support you when you need extra help short-term or long-term. You may need help because some of your abilities have deteriorated or because you are living with a disability or long-term condition.

Whether you just need a little bit of help to manage at home or you have more complex needs, our Rochdale Stay Well service will provide you with information and advice and details of services available to you.

You can make your own arrangements privately but, if you feel you need care and support from us, we will need to work out your needs and whether you could pay towards the cost of your support.
