Meals on Wheels

What is the Meals on Wheels Service?

The Pembrokeshire Meals on Wheels Service provides hot meals delivered to people’s homes. The service is for older people or people with a disability who have difficulty in cooking and shopping.

Who can get the service?

To receive Meals on Wheels you will need to have an assessment

Is there a charge?

There is a charge of £4.34 per day for a hot main meal and a sweet. This charge is made to all clients regardless of their income.
