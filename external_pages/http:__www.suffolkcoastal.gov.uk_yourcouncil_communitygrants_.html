Grants and funding for community organisations

Enabling Communities - is our commitment to local self-help and offers a range of support including funding - watch the video showing Enabling Communities in action.

In this section ...

  • Capital & revenue grants from Suffolk Coastal - details of Suffolk Coastal's revenue and capital grant programmes.
  • Sports and recreation funding guide - directory of funding organisations and guidance for making a funding application.
  • Outdoor Playing Space Scheme - scheme which provides funds for local outdoor recreational facilities as planning permission for new housing is issued.
  • Suffolk Armed Forces Community Covenant - the covenant is a voluntary pledge of mutual support between a civilian community and its local armed forces community. In order to support joint military and civilian community projects, the Ministry of Defence has set up a military covenant community grant scheme.
