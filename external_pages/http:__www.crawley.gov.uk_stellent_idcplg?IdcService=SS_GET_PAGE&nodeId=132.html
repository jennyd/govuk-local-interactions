Managing Crawley's Homes

Crawley Homes manages over 9,500 properties in the town. Over 8,000 rented homes and over 1,500 leasehold homes.

We have a varied role in making sure your tenancy runs smoothly, collecting rent, dealing with any problems that arise and keeping you involved and informed.

Your personal information

The Data Protection Act 1998 was introduced to protect personal data. That is data about individuals.

This data not only includes information about you but about items that are personal to you e.g. your rent account or housing benefit claim. This means that we can’t talk to anyone, including your mum, dad, friend or partner about your rent account unless you give us permission beforehand, and we would like this in writing. Please follow the link below to fill in the permission form, and send it back to us.
