Pest control

Advice

Treatments and prices

Bees

Additional services

The pest control service provides treatments for a range of vermin and will give advice on pest identification, control and treatment.

Advice

If you have a problem with insects, you can bring a sample into the Civic Centre for identification. The pest control officers will let you know what the insect is and tell you what you can do to get rid of the problem.

Please do not bring the droppings of larger pests in for identification.

Treatments and prices

We provide specialist treatments to deal with a range of pests, which include:

  • Mice or rats (inside or outside a property)
  • Bedbugs
  • Cockroaches
  • Fleas
  • Wasps
  • Ants

Prices for residents

Prices for businesses

For other pests such as pigeons, squirrels, bats and foxes, we do not provide a treatment. However, we will offer advice on how to deal with them.

Bees

We cannot treat for honey bees as they are a protected species.

If you have a problem with a bees' nest on your property you will need to contact a specialist bee keeper, which can be found online or in the phone directory.

Additional services

We offer additional services to both members of the public and commercial premises. These include:

  • drain camera surveys,
  • drain smoke testing,
  • bird proofing, and
  • feral pigeon culling.

Please contact us for further details on these services, including costs.
