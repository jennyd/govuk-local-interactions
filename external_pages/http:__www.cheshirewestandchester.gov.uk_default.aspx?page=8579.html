Children and young people


Report child abuse or neglect

Please report any concern you may have about a child's welfare, or if you feel that a child may be being abused. Remember, your call could mean getting the right help to a family and may even save a child's life.

  • Report a concern about a child or young person

Alternatively, in an emergency, telephone the police on 0845 458 0000.

Please be assured that your referral will be treated in the strictest confidence.
