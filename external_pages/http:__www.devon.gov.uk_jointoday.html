Join the Library

Be part of something BIG! With over a million items, across our network of libraries and mobiles there's something for everyone.

                                                 

Join your library online and start ordering books straight away. It's FREE and no ID is needed.

Here are just some of the benefits of your FREE library membership.

Borrow:
Up to 12 books
4 CDs (music or audio books)
4 DVDs
4 language courses
3 eBooks or eAudiobooks
e-magazines (no limit)

Order and renew online
Borrow from and return to any Devon Library

Information at your fingertips:
We have an information service second to none - just ask, we'll find.
FREE access to a great range of online reference sites including:

Ancestry Library Edition (trace your family tree!)
Oxford Reference
Oxford Dictionary of National Biography       
Times Digital Archive

Oxford English Dictionary
Newsbank UK Newspapers
Naxos Music Library

Computers:
Use ours for up to 2 hours free every day. FREE Wi-Fi access at some locations.

For Kids:

Over 5s
Free membership pack with colouring and wordsearch
Your chance to win super prizes with The Zone our website for children
Collect stickers and badges with Book Track
Storytimes and activities at your library

Under 5s
Bookstart and Bookstart Bear Club
Baby Bounce and Rhyme

Plus - access to Reading Groups, Children's Book Clubs and special author events. To find out more about what's available at Your Local Library. Simply follow the link and select your nearest library from the map or A-Z list.
