Organisations to help the bereaved

    Following a loss it can be helpful to turn to family and friends for support or to your religion of faith. Alternatively, there are many organisations that can offer practical help, emotional support and experienced counsellors to help you come to terms with your grief. Some of those organisations are listed here.

     

    Cruse Bereavement Care

    SAVS Centre

    29-31 Alexandra Street

    Southend-on-Sea

    Essex

    SS1 1BW

    Telephone: 0845 2669710

     

    A counselling service to help individuals and families through the emotions and practical problems of bereavement.

     

    The Samaritans

    12 Critchett Terrace

    Chelmsford

    CM1 2QN

    Telephone: 08457 909090

     

    Offering confidential support through emotional crisis (24 hours a day).

     

     

    Stillbirth and Neonatal Death Society - Sands

    28 Portland Place

    London

    W1B 1LY

    Telephone: 020 7436 7940


    A national charity network of bereaved parents befriending individuals or groups.

    The Essex Sands group telephone: 07552 556030
     

     

    Terrence Higgins Trust

    314-320 Gray's Inn Road

    London

    WC1X 8DP

    Telephone: 0808 802 1221


    A British charity that campaigns on various issues related to AIDS and HIV. It provides counselling for those bereaved by gay partners or friends.

    The Colchester based Terrance Higgins Trust telephone: 01206 798595
     

     

    The Compassionate Friends

    14 New king Street

    Deptford

    London

    SE8 3HS

    Telephone: 0345 123 2304

     

    A nationwide self help group for parents who have lost a child of any age, from any cause.

     

     

    Additional information covering all areas of death and bereavement can be located on the GOV.UK website.
