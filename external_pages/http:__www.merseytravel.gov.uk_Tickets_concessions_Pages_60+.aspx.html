Travel Concessions for the Over 60s

You can travel free on buses, trains and Mersey Ferries if you are over  60 and live in Merseyside.
 
When you reach the State Pension age, you will be given a new national pass, which you will also be able to use on local bus services anywhere in England. The pass is valid during off-peak hours (after 9.30am on Mondays to Fridays and at anytime on Saturdays, Sundays and Bank Holidays).
 
To check when you will become eligible for your national pass please click here.

 

To apply you can fill out the form below and take it with supporting documents to any Merseytravel Travel Centre:

Merseytravel Concessionary Travel Passes: Information for Customers

We are currently improving the way we produce concessionary travel passes. Unfortunately this means we are temporarily unable to issue replacement passes at our Travel Centres. We can still take your applications for new or replacement passes at the Travel Centres but the pass will be sent out to your home address within 5 days.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause. For further information please call 0151 236 6056.
