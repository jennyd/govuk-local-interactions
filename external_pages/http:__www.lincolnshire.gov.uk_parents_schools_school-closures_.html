School Closures

Schools can be Closed for a variety of reasons, this is a central resources for school children, parents, teachers and the media to find the latest information on the position of a particular school.

The default position for a school is Open and can only be changed once we have received notification from the school’s Head Teacher. If you feel the information on this site is incorrect you should contact the school’s Head Teacher directly and ask them to inform us of the change.
