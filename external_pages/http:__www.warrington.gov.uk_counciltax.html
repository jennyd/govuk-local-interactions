Most homes must pay council tax...

There is one bill per home, whether it is a house, bungalow, flat, maisonette, mobile home or houseboat, and whether it is owned or rented. 

Find out how much you need to pay, whether you can apply for a discount or exemption, or get more information about how your bill is worked out.

If you're having problems paying see how we can help.
