Heritage, Conservation Areas and Listed Buildings

We have a team of conservation specialists committed to conserving and enhancing historic buildings and the natural environment. Our commitment to you is that we will use our best efforts to:

  • Promote the conservation of the natural and built environment within the Borough.
  • Where resources allow support conservation and enhancement schemes with grant aid and technical advice.
  • Assist local communities to enhance their local environment.
  • Provide technical assistance and design guidance on request and as resources allow.
  • Prepare guidance notes and leaflets on all aspects of conservation.
  • Consult with local communities and landowners about the designation of new conservation areas.
  • Keep an up to date review of locally listed buildings and a ‘buildings at risk’ register.

Conservation and design advice is given on:

  • Listed Buildings (internal and external alterations and extensions).

  • Works to historic buildings in Conservation Areas (including demolition, new shop fronts and signage) and parks and gardens.

  • Identifying Local Heritage Assets.

  • Traditional methods of building repair.

  • The design of new buildings and the public realm in Conservation Areas (including roads) and areas of townscape character.

  • Archaeological potential and reports.

Key Sources for Design and Conservation Information

  • Our Streets and Spaces SPD

  • Supplementary Planning Guidance relating to shopfronts, design, the natural environment and landscape, and other relevant topics
