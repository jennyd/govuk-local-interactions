SEN and Disability (SEND) 0-25 : Local Offer

Welcome to Hertfordshire County Council’s local offer pages. In this resource you will find information about services available in your local area for parents, children and young people aged 0-25 with special educational needs and disabilities (SEND).

What do we mean by special educational needs and or disability?

We hope that the information will enable you and/or the child you are caring for, to make informed choices around childcare, social care, education and health.

You can also find information on Education Health and Care Plans and on Personal budgets and the Hertfordshire County Council SEND Strategy 2015-2018 in the Local Offer

Draft documents for our Accessibility Strategy and what the Local Authority expects Education Settings to do with the Local Offer are also available.

If you would like help to locate the information you require or would like to speak to someone directly contact KIDS HUBS

We are keen to know your thoughts about the Local Offer so please email localoffer@hertfordshire.gov.uk with any comments you may have.

You can see what comments have already been made and what we have done with these on this feedback page.

Further information

Helping families without internet access

If you know someone who would like to use the Local Offer but they cannot access the internet, ask them to contact their local library, or children's centre. Find your local library here or your local children's centre here.

If you need further assistance, you can call the customer service centre on 0300 123 4043.
