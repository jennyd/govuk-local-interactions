Council tax

  • Single Person Discount Review

    Boston Borough Council, in partnership with Northgate, are undertaking an evidence based Single Person Discount review.

  • Council Tax Support scheme

    Changes to Council Tax Benefit - is now known as Council Tax Support

  • Changes to Council Tax exemptions and discounts from 1 April 2013

    The Government proposed to allow billing authorities greater discretion over applying discounts and exemptions for certain categories of property from April 2013.

  • What is council tax?

    We collect council tax on behalf of the county council, the police authority, parish councils and ourselves.

  • Registering for council tax

    If you buy or rent you must register for council tax on your property.

  • Paying your council tax

    The council will send you a council tax bill at the beginning of each financial year (usually mid March) to notify you of the amount you need to pay on a monthly basis.

  • Moving/changes to household

    If you move or your circumstances change then please tell us.

  • Can I reduce my council tax?

    A full council tax charge assumes there are two adults (persons over 18) living in a property.
