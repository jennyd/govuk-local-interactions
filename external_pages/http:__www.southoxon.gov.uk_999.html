Emergency contacts

In an emergency

Dial 999 for the police, fire or ambulance service. For non-emergencies, you can contact the police Thames Valley Police This link will take you to an external website... on 101 and the fire service on 01865 842999.

If you are hard of hearing, the RNID typetalk emergency number 18000.

The council's emergency helpline

The council's emergency helpline on 0845 303 9727 is available after office hours (usual office hours are Mon - Thurs 8.30am - 5pm, Friday 8.30am - 4.30pm) to report a noise nuisance emergency, dangerous structure, flooding, a dead animal on the highway or a homeless issue. We can also collect stray dogs out of hours.

Health advice

For immediate and confidential health advice/information call NHS 111 NHS This link will take you to an external website... . 111 is the NHS non-emergency number. It’s fast, easy and free. all 111 when you need medical help fast but it’s not a 999 emergency.

Floods

For 24 hour advice/information on flooding and flood warnings call Floodline Environment Agency This link will take you to an external website... on  0845 988 1188. See our flooding page for further information.

Pollution

To report environmental pollution incidents call the Environment Agency Environment Agency This link will take you to an external website... on 0800 807 060.

Gas

To report a gas leak either in the house or street call the National Grid This link will take you to an external website... on 0800 111 999.

Homelessness

If you are facing eviction and homelessness, you can find information and advice in the housing section.

Thames Valley Risk Register

The Risk Register identifies all potential hazards to the area This link will take you to an external website... and their consequences, so any incidents that occur can be dealt with more effectively.

Business continuity advice

A business continuity plan will ensure that the impact of an emergency on your business is minimised.

Emergency planning

If you want to know how to prepare for an emergency you can find information in the emergency planning section.

Contact the emergency planning officer on john.backley@southandvale.gov.uk.
