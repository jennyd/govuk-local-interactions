Bulky waste collection

Listen

We are now working in partnership with the Surrey Reuse Network to collect bulky household items e.g. furniture, fridges, beds, cookers, televisions etc. Their aim is to increase the amount of reuse and recycling from the bulky waste that is collected.

This service will be provided by the Surrey Reuse Network who will collect your bulky household items from inside your home. They are able to collect from homes Monday to Saturday. Please do not leave items outside as they will not be collected.

Arranging a collection

  • call the Surrey Reuse Network on 0800 0820 180 and press option 3 (as soon as the automated voice starts talking). If there is no answer, leave a voicemail message and SRN will call you back.
  • email bulkysbc@surreyreusenetwork.org.uk providing your name and contact telephone number. Surrey Reuse Network will contact you to arrange the collection
  • arrange online from the Surrey Reuse Network web page

Cost

The cost is £44 (inc. VAT) for up to five items which can be collected from inside your property on any floor. For more than 5 items please call 0800 0820 180 for a quote as additional costs will occur.

There is a discounted charge of £35 available for those on income support or pension credit.

Items that can be collected

The Surrey Reuse Network will reuse or recycle as many items as possible. Broken items can still be collected as they have a specialist fixing team. They will also collect:

  • large household electricals e.g. washing machines, fridges, cookers
  • wooden furniture e.g. tables, wardrobes, desks
  • upholstered furniture e.g. sofas, chairs
  • beds and mattresses

Items that cannot be collected

  • gardening waste
  • building rubble
  • hazardous waste
  • fixtures and fittings e.g kitchen cabinets, windows, baths
  • pianos

Please note that due to the number of boroughs involved with Surrey Reuse Network their website is currently very generic.  This will be updated shortly with Borough specific information and costs.

Alternatively take your bulky items to the local Community Recycling Centre (tip) or contact local charities (such as the RSPCA) who often collect items in good condition for free.

If you have items that are rubbish and are not suitable for reuse and recycling please contact our Streetscene department directly on 01784 446411 for a quote.
