Disabled Facilities Grant

You can apply for a Disabled Facilities Grant if an occupational therapist has checked your needs and made a referral to the adaptations service.

Occupational Therapy Services

: 01375 652 868

The grant helps to pay for major adaptations for owner occupiers, private tenants or housing association tenants.

Council tenants will have their assessed adaptation costs met directly by us, via our occupational therapy service, without the need to apply for a grant.

The maximum Disabled Facilities Grant is £30,000.

Means testing

Most applicants will be means tested unless the work is for a child under 18 years old.

You won't be means tested if you get:

  • income support
  • income based employment support assistance
  • income based job seekers allowance
  • pension credit, guarantee component
  • housing benefit
  • local housing allowance
  • working or child tax credits and earning less than £15,050 a year
  • Universal Credit

If the grant is more than £5,000, up to the maximum of £10,000, we will place a local land charge on the property address. This charge will last for 10 years.

We work in partnership with our local home improvement agency the Papworth Trust, who help complete grant applications for us.

For further information call:

Adaptations Service

: 01375 652 482
