Advice on suitable housing for older people

FlexiCare is Staffordshire County Council's Extra Care service. Providing older people with access to lots of facilities and activities alongside care services and on site support 24 hours a day.

You'll have the option of being able to rent, part-buy or buy a property. Support or access issues will not be an issues as each property is modern - so there will be no worries about having to move.

FlexiCare is here to allow you to live at your own home rather than at a home.

Living in Stoke-on-Trent?

FlexiCare is only available in Staffordshire, but Stoke-on-Trent City Council offers an alternative. Its called Extra Care in Stoke-on-Trent.
