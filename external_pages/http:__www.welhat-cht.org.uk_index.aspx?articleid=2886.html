Right to Buy

The Right to Buy scheme allows eligible council tenants to buy their home from the council, usually at a discount.

Eligibility

To have the right to buy you must have either a secure or flexible council tenancy.

You also need a "qualifying period" of at least 3 years of relevant tenancies or periods in H.M. Armed Forces accommodation. Your qualifying period is the duration of your current tenancy plus the duration of any previous tenancies with Welwyn Hatfield Borough Council. Your qualifying period also includes any time living in H.M. Armed Forces accommodation.

Most tenants who have a secure or flexible tenancy and the required qualifying period will have the right to buy, but there are exceptions to this. To confirm your eligibility, please contact the Right to Buy team.

Discounts

Purchasers of houses and bungalows with a qualifying period of 3, 4, or 5 years may be entitled to a maximum discount of 35%. 

Purchasers of flats and maisonettes with a qualifying of 3, 4, or 5 may be entitled to a maximum discount will be 50%.

The percentages quoted above are only applicable if the discount does not exceeding the maximum discount allowed in Welwyn Hatfield.

Applications received before 26 May 2015

Applications received before this date will be assessed based on the eligibility criteria in place at the time, which will be different to those outlined above.

How to apply

If you would like to make an application to buy your home, please download and print the  Right To Buy Application Form [356KB]. You will need to submit the completed form to the Right to Buy team.

Further details

Take a look at our Right to Buy FAQ's page or contact the Right to Buy team for further information. Alternatively, details of the Right to Buy scheme and how to apply are available from Gov.uk and Communities and Local Government websites.
