Pests and Animal Nuisance

The Pest Control Service

If you are a resident of the Uttlesford area you can call our pest control provider direct to arrange for a treatment to be carried out.


Mitie LogoThe service includes the treatment of domestic premises for rats and mice along with other pests of public health significance, and is undertaken by Mitie Pest Control on behalf of Uttlesford District Council (UDC).

To arrange for a pest control treatment please call :

  • 01242 696969 (local rate) or 0844 335 0330 stating you are an Uttlesford resident.

Alternatively you can book a service request directly with Mitie Pest Control via email at:

  • mpc.info@mitie.com

The charges from 1 April 2015 (including 20% VAT) are as follows:

•
Rats£45 per three part treatment
•
Mice£48 per three part treatment
•
Wasps£37 per nest
•
Ants£37 per treatment
•
Cluster Flies£48 per treatment
•
Fleas£48 per treatment
•
Carpet Beetles£48 per treatment
•
Moths£48 per treatment
•
Squirrels£75 per three part treatment
•
Insect Identification£37 per specific call-out
•
Bed Bugs£75 per treatment - up to 3 Bedrooms
•
Cockroaches£48 per treatment

All charges are collected by Mitie Pest Control at the time of booking a treatment. Please note that you are entering into a contract for treatment with Mitie Pest control and not UDC and should refer to their terms and conditions.

A 25% discount for all treatments is available to UDC administered benefit claimants (Housing benefit or Council Tax Reduction benefit). You will need to show documentary evidence of your entitlement to these benefits at the time of the pest control visit. Please tell the Mitie Pest Control call centre operator when booking a treatment that you are entitled to a reduction.

The above charges are for treatment to private households only. Please contact Mitie Pest Control on 0844 335 0330 for details of the charges for treatment to business premises.

You can also use a alternative pest control company to get rid of pests. Be sure to use a reputable company which is a member of the British Pest Control Association (BPCA) or the National Pest Technicians Association (NPTA). You can search for the nearest member of the trade using the links below:

  • Search for a BPCA Pest Control Company Now >>
  • Search for a NPTA Pest Control Company Now >>
