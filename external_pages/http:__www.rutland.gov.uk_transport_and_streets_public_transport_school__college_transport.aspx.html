School and College Transport

 

If you live in Rutland and you are thinking about applying for transport to school or college, please refer to the following booklet/s.  These will guide you through the process:

 

Home to school Transport Application Booklet (pdf)

Home to college (Aged 16-18 years) Transport Application Booklet (pdf)

 

In view of the launch of the new booklets, applications for 2015-16 - Fare paying forms for existing fare payers, and Post 16 forms for current Post 16 year 1 students are being issued early. These can be submitted anytime between now and the deadline date.

 

All transport application forms can now be found within the relevant school/college booklet

 

View/Download: SCHOOL TRANSPORT POLICY (pdf)

 

View / Download: POST 16 POLICY STATEMENT 2015 (pdf)
