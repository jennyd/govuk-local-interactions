Find It - Planning Applications

Search for new, existing & historical planning applications within Stockport via our interactive map or database - including our weekly list of new planning applications.

The 'Planning Applications' map shows you recent and historical planning applications in Stockport.

You can also find recent and historical planning applications using our online search.

Search for Planning Applications

From 16th November 2015 the Weekly list can be searched for by using the "Search For Planning Applications" link above. There is a separate option to search for this week's Weekly List.

To zoom in and out use your computer mouse wheel or use the buttons.
To move around the map hold down the left button on your computer mouse and drag it around the map. You can also use the  arrows.
