Use this form to request a dropped kerb (vehicle crossing)

Eligibility

The location will be assessed to determine the suitability and type of dropped kerb for the property/area.

Cost

There is a cost for this service which will vary depending on size and location.

What happens next?

  • We will aim to investigate the location within 15 working days.
  • An officer will take appropriate measurements and complete a check list noting down any obstructions such as street lamps and looking to see that once the crossing is constructed it poses no visibility or pedestrian access problems. A quotation will be sent to you following the inspection.
