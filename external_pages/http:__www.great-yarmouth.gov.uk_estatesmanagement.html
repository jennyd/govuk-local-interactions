Estates Management

Our estate managers deal with all tenancy issues apart from rent queries. They are based at two area offices: South Yarmouth and Gorleston. For address and contact details visit our Area Housing Offices page.

For all rent payment enquires please contact the Rent Income Team or visit the Greyfriars Office, Greyfriars Way, Great Yarmouth NR30 2QE.
