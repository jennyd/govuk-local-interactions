Vehicle crossovers (dropped kerbs)

Vehicle crossovers, also known as 'dropped kerbs', allow vehicles to cross a footway to access a property or off-street parking. It is an offence to drive over a footway without a proper vehicle crossover.

We build vehicle crossovers at the request of residents or businesses, if certain criteria are met. There is a charge for this service and planning permission may be required.
