Libraries in Hull

Hull libraries offer access to books, music and video DVDs and CDs, newspapers and magazines, plus a wide range of other material.

We offer information about the library service in different languages, large print and Braille on request. Books written in community languages are available at the Hull Central Library and other local libraries.

  • join our libraries for free online now
  • search for, reserve and renew books online
  • download eBooks and eAudiobooks using the Bolinda Borrowbox app 
  • learn about our range of print and online reference material
  • enjoy free access to computers and the internet
  • find out about our fees, fines and photocopying charges
  • learn about our free library link service for housebound residents
  • enjoy breakfast, lunch or afternoon tea at Venue Hull cafe
