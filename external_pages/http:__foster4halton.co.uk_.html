Foster4Halton

Halton Borough Council look for potential foster parents of all ages and backgrounds. People who simply have the life skills to care for a child or children who may be confused, frightened and wondering what the future holds. By providing a warm, nurturing environment at a time when a child needs you most, you can help them to feel safe and secure – however short or long the time they’ll be with you.
