Report an abandoned vehicle

To report an abandoned vehicle, please complete the form below.

Once we have received a report, vehicles are inspected within 48 hours. If they are found in a hazardous condition or location, arrangements are made for their immediate removal and storage for 28 days whilst attempts are made to trace their owners. If vehicles are taxed and in an acceptable condition and location, a seven day removal notice is issued on the vehicle, otherwise a 24 hour removal notice applies.

If you have a car to scrap the council can arrange to remove it for you, free of charge (conditions apply) - email your contact details to fleet.management@northtyneside.gov.uk.
