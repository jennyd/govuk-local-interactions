Community Transport schemes operate in many parts of Derbyshire to provide transport for voluntary organisations, and for people who are unable to use ordinary public transport.
Schemes include Dial-a-Bus, Dial-a-Ride, Group Travel, Shopmobility and Community Bus.

Derbyshire County Council - Community Transport
