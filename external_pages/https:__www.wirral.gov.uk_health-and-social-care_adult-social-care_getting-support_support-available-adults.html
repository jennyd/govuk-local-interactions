Support available for adults

If you are an adult, aged 18 and find it difficult to perform some day-to-day tasks, you may qualify for care and support. This could be as a result of age, illness, disability or any significant change in your life.

There is a variety of support available  both from the council and from other professional care providers. You can choose from a range of services that meet your needs.

Support can be:

  • non-residential – you are cared for in your own home or parts of your home are altered so it’s easier for you to manage
  • residential – you receive round-the-clock attention in a care or nursing home

To decide on the best support for you, we must first complete a needs assessment.

To discuss the options available to you, please contact our Central Advice and Duty Team on 0151 606 2006
