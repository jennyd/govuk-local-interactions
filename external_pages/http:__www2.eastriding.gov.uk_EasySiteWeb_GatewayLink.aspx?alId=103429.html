Report a pothole

  • Use this form to notify the council of a pothole in a road or path in the East Riding of Yorkshire.
    •  
  • If a defect is considered dangerous, then a repair is carried out within 24 hours. Other defects are scheduled for repair usually within 28 days, or as part of a future programme of similar work.
    •  
  • If you have requested a call from a member of staff this will take place within the next ten working days.
    •  
  • This form consists of up to 4 pages and should take approximately 3 minutes to complete.
    •  
  • Fields marked with an * are mandatory and require completion.
