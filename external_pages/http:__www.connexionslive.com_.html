About Career Connect

Career Connect is a charity who are passionate about providing high quality independent careers advice, bridging the gap to learning and employment and better life chances for young people and adults.

We provide a wide range of career management services, some funded by Local Authorities and schools and colleges in your area.

We are also a provider of the National Careers Service for adults.
