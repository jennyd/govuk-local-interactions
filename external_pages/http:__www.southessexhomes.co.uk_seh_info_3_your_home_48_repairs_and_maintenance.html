Repairs and Maintenance

For most repairs that you are likely to ever need, our online reporting tool allows you to click on pictures rather than having to think of how to describe the repair, but you can still add your own words to the picture if you wish to.

Click here to go to our online repair finder 

You can still report repairs by telephone if you prefer, via the Customer Contact Centre on 0800 833 160.

Please also use this telephone number if you have an emergency repair out of hours. 

General repairs to your home are carried out by Kier and gas repairs and boiler servicing are undertaken by Aaron Services Ltd on behalf of South Essex Homes.
