Make a Comment, Complaint or Compliment

We aim to provide services of a high standard, and your feedback is important to us.

For more information about how the Council deals with Correspondence, Compliments and Complaints (known as "3Cs") please read our 3Cs pack. This explains how long it will take us to contact you and how the process works.  

Our 3Cs Annual Report explains how we are performing and the level of service our customers can expect if they contact us. 

  • Make a complaint

    Make a complaint

    We take complaints very seriously and will make sure that they are investigated properly.

  • Correspondence

    Correspondence

    We welcome your views or suggestions so we can improve our services to meet customers needs.

  • Give a compliment

    Give a compliment

    If you received particularly good service, please tell us. We will pass your feedback on to the team involved.

  • 3C's Policy and Procedure

    3C's Policy and Procedure

    This explains how the Council will deal with Correspondence, Compliments and Complaints and what to expect when you contact us.

  • Local Government Ombudsman

    Local Government Ombudsman

    If you are not happy with the outcome of your complaint at the end of our procedure, you may want to contact the Local Government Ombudsman. The Local Government Ombudsman is an independent investigator whose role is to investigate serious complaints against Councils.
