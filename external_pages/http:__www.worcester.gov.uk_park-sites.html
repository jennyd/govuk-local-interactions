Worcester City Parks Sites

There are a number of parks within Worcester City with a wide range of facilities being available on the premises themselves. Please use the interactive map below or click on one of the park's site links on the left hand menu to find out more.

All these places are fantastic to explore in the winter or to relax and enjoy in the summer.

Various events take place throughout the year; details are available in the Park Events section of this web site. We are committed to providing an enjoyable, safe environment and one in which people can make use of all the facilities available.

Working in partnership with local charities, organisations and the surrounding community, Cleaner and Greener Services feed off ideas put forward and manages the areas with these in mind but also with a sympathetic approach to the history of the parks and their wildlife.

Main park sites

Here is a list of the main parks in Worcester.

  • Cripplegate Park
  • Diglis Playing Fields
  • Fort Royal and Commandery Gardens
  • Gheluvelt Park
  • King George V Playing Fields
  • Riverside Parks
  • Westbury Street Community Gardens
  • Worcester Woods Country Park (managed by Worcestershire County Council)
