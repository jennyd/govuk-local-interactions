If your bin was missed

Report your missed collection via the on-line form below. We class a bin or garden waste sack as "missed" if it was placed on the edge of the highway from 6am on the scheduled day of collection. We operate a three working day policy on reports of missed refuse and recycling bins and garden waste collections:

  • We must be told about a missed bin within three working days of your collection day. For example, if your bin was missed on a Monday, you must let us know by the end of Wednesday so that we can empty it.
  • We try to remedy the situation which means we may return to empty your bin within two working days of the report that it was missed. After three working days, we ask you to take your refuse/recycling or garden waste in and re-present it on your next scheduled collection. Refuse collections will have moved on to another area of the Borough and it would be very inefficient and expensive to get a refuse collection vehicle to return for one bin.

Report it - If you feel your bin or garden waste has been missed, fill in our online report a problem form or phone 01329 236100. Please leave your bin or garden waste in the usual place for collection on the edge of the highway.      

If a hanger was left on your bin

Our collection crew leave hangers on bins or garden waste sacks to let you know about a problem with collection. Details of specific issues will be on each hanger.
