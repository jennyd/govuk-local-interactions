Repairs

Reporting your repairs to us is simple. There are four options you can choose from:

Option 1 - Customer Service Centre - 0300 111 1000

You can report a repair to the Customer Service Centre either in writing, via a fax or by telephone (all calls are recorded for the purpose of staff training and service improvements).

The Customer Service Centre is open between the hours of 8:00 am and 5:00 pm Monday to Fridays during which repairs can be raised, outside of these hours emergency make safe repairs can be raised by contacting the same phone number.

Click here to find out how to Contact us

Option 2 - Housing Offices

Service phones are available in the reception areas of all Neighbourhood Housing Offices which connect directly to the Customer Service Centre.

For details of your nearest Neighbourhood Housing Centre see Our offices page.

Option 3 - By Text Message

Your can report your repair to us via a short text message. Start your message with the word Tristar then provide the first line of your address and a brief description of the repair you require carrying out. There is no need to provide vast amounts of information as someone will ring you back to confirm the details. Please be aware that text messages will only be dealt with during normal working hours. You can still text us out of hours but the message won't be picked up until the working day begins. To text your repair request to us please use the mobile telephone number 60070, starting your message with the word Tristar. Please do not try to ring this mobile number as it is a text only line created specifically for this purpose. (Text messages will be charged at your network's standard rate).

Option 4 - Online

You can report your repair to us using our new Online Form below.
