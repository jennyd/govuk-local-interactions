Report a problem

Sometimes the easiest way to report problems is from this website.
You can use this page to report (or find out how to report) the following:

  • Abandoned vehicles
  • Adult abuse
  • Anti-social behaviour
  • Air pollution
  • Bonfire problem
  • Broken street light
  • Child or children at risk
  • Dog mess
  • Dead animal (on road or council land)
  • Dumped rubbish (fly-tipping)
  • Food complaints
  • Full litter bins or dog bins
  • Graffiti
  • Litter
  • Missed bin collection
  • Noise control (factories, pubs)
  • Nuisance barking
  • Pests
  • Potholes
  • Problems with road markings
  • Public health problems (blocked drains, rats)

If you can't find a form to report your problem in this list, please email contact@sandwell.gov.uk with the details and we will look into it.
