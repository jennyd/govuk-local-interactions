Help buying a home

HomeBuy is a government programme that helps people to own their own home. It includes shared ownership and help to buy schemes.

To help you realise your dream of owning your own home, the council works in partnership with Help to Buy South, our local HomeBuy agent. They are registered with and monitored by the Homes and Communities Agency This link will take you to an external website... , sponsored by the Government.

For more information contact Help to Buy South This link will take you to an external website... who will assess your current living situation and then list for you the HomeBuy options you are eligible for.

If you are a current owner occupier you may not be eligible for HomeBuy options, please contact the Housing Team on 01235 520 202 to discuss whether you may be eligible.
