Get involved

  • Thanet District Council
  • The Thanet Magazine
  • Get involved

There are lots of different ways to get involved and shape your local area. Just take a look to the right for some ideas of how to let us know what you think. We are keen to hear the views and ideas of local residents, business people, and the variety of partners that we work with. 

So if you want to have a say on issues that you care about, or want to influence the way the council makes decisions, participate in our consultations and spread the word amongst your family, friends, colleagues and neighbours and encourage them to get involved too.

Join our mailing list

Do you want to hear about new consultations?  Then join our consultations mailing list by emailing consultation@thanet.gov.uk providing your name, email address & postal address.
