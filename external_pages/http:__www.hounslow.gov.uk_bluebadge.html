Disabled people - parking permits - blue badge

The blue badge scheme helps people who are registered blind, or people who have severe walking difficulties, to be parked closer to shops and services they need to access.

Blue badges are issued to individuals, not vehicles, so you can use a blue badge as a driver and as passenger.

A blue badge costs £10. Lost or stolen blue badges are charged £10.

Blue badge applications can be made online by visiting the GOV.UK website, available on this page under external links or you can download the application form, available under documents for download.
