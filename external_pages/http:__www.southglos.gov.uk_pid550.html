Report an obstruction or spillage

If you notice an obstruction or spillage please tell us about it.
If the obstruction is a vehicle which is causing a danger to other road users, please report it to the police.
Please be as accurate as possible when explaining the location and nature of the obstruction or spillage as this will make it easier for us to find and faster to fix.
