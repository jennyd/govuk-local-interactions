Building Control

  • How to make a Building Regulation application

We are responsible for determining building regulation applications - and inspecting sites to make sure builders are following the rules.

We also deal with dangerous structures and demolition notices and more. 

We also process a lot of information about you property that is supplied by people you appoint such as 'competent persons', and Approved Inspectors.

And when it all goes wrong, and we can't agree an amicable solution, we also have responsibility for taking enforcement action under the Building Regulations and dangerous structure legislation.

Through LABC Consult we can offer you a vast range of services, using the best experts the industry has to offer - covering energy efficiency, acoustics, fire risk assessments and much, much more.
