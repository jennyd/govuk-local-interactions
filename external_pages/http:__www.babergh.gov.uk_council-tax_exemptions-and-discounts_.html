Exemptions and Discounts

There are many exemptions and discounts including empty properties and people living alone. If you think a discount, reduction or exemption applies to you, use our online forms to apply, or if you need more information please contact us.

Can I get a Council Tax reduction if I am living on my own?

Yes, if you are the only adult in the household, you can apply for a 25% reduction in your Council Tax. More information on Can I get a Council Tax reduction if I am living on my own?

I have a disability. Can I get a Council Tax reduction?

If certain conditions are met, we may be able to treat your property as if it were in the next band down for Council Tax purposes.This would mean you pay less for your Council Tax. More information on I have a disability. Can I get a Council Tax reduction?

I have had to move out of my house due to flood damage. Will Council Tax still be payable?

If your property is empty, substantially unfurnished, and undergoing major repair works, we may be able to grant a 25% discount on the property for up to 12 months. We may need to visit, while the work is underway, to confirm the situation. 

More information on I have had to move out of my house due to flood damage. Will Council Tax still be payable?

What does 'substantially unfurnished' mean for the purposes of Council Tax?

When there is insufficient furniture in the property so that habitation is not possible, for example, a wardrobe might be acceptable as substantially unfurnished, but a sofa or beds would not.
