Appeal your Housing Benefit decision

If you disagree with our decision, you have the right to:

  • ask us for an explanation

and/or

  • ask us to reconsider

You must make your appeal in writing within one month of the date of our decision, giving your reasons. It's helpful if you complete our appeals form.

If you're not happy with the outcome, we'll ask you if you want the independent Tribunals Service to hear your appeal.
