Garages

We have a number of garages for rent around the borough. You can rent a garage from Solihull Council even if you don't rent a council home.

**Due to extremely high demand we no longer accept applications for garages on the following roads: Lincoln Grove, Cambridge Drive and Norwich Croft.**

Cost of renting a garage and how to pay

In order to rent a garage, please fill in an application form. There is a waiting list, so you may not get a garage immediately.

Renting a garage costs £6.58 a week for council tenants and £7.89 a week for non-tenants (the rent is more expensive for non-tenants because non-tenants pay VAT whereas council tenants don't). You can pay by direct debit or using another payment method.

We have recently re-built two of our existing garage sites in Sheepclose Drive and Blair Grove. These new garages are a larger style and are available to rent at a cost of £9.87 per week for council tenants and £11.84 for non-tenants.

VAT

VAT is a tax charged on most goods and services in the United Kingdom. HM Revenue and Customs receive the money collected, not the Council.

You are liable to pay VAT on the garage rent if you do not pay rent for the property that you live in to the Council.

So, if you are an owner-occupier and do not pay rent to the Council for your home, you have to pay VAT at the standard rate, currently 20%.
