Recycling, waste and bins

Arrange collection of your large unwanted items

Arrange a collection of your large unwanted items directly from your home

You'll need to book and pay in advance to have any bulky waste collected.

Call the Customer Services Team to book and pay:

Telephone: 01303 858 660

The Civic Centre is open from 8:30 and closes at 17.00 Monday to Friday (Wednesdays from 9.30)

Alternatively, you can take your bulky waste to a council tip - it won't cost you anything.

What we collect

The council can collect the following items:

  • white goods, eg fridges, freezers and washing machines
  • cookers
  • lawnmowers
  • furniture, including bed frames and mattresses
  • carpets
  • TVs and small electrical items, eg DVD players, toasters and kettles

All bulky items must be 1.8 metres (6 feet) long or less. The bulky item should be able to be lifted by 2 people.

Please note that we only collect a maximum of five items per collection and no more than two Fridges, Freezers or combination Fridge/Freezers.

How much it costs

It costs £23.95 to get rid of your first large unwanted item, thereafter it is £6 per item up to 5 items per pick up.

You must leave items outside your property by 7am ready for the pickup. If it is not there and visible for the contractor at the time of collection a refund will not be given.   

If you need to cancel you must give us 24 hours notice of your pre-arranged collection date to receive a full refund.

What we don't collect

The council does not collect:

  • garden waste - find out how to join Shepway's garden waste scheme
  • rubble, wood, building materials or black sacks of rubbish
  • solid metals, including cast iron and baths
  • glass, including mirrors
  • sinks made of ceramic or metal

Where you will leave the items

You must leave them outside your property either on your drive or front garden if you have one. If you haven't either of these and are unsure where to leave contact us to discuss the most suitable area.
Unfortunately they can not be left on the path or road.

If you can't get the item outside of your property, we can collect them but only with your written consent via our permission form. Please let us know so we can send the form out to you. 

If you can't book a collection over the phone

You can:

  • apply by post

To apply by post, send the following information and a cheque made out to 'Shepway District Council' to Customer Services:

  • your name, address and telephone number
  • what needs to be collected
