Archives and local history

Visit Bexley's Local Studies and Archive Centre if you would like to trace the history of your family, house, famous Bexley people, landmarks or events.

Please note we will not be able to fetch material from our off-site store in Crayford until the middle of February. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Staff are always ready to help you use the resources.
