Allotments

Allotments are a great way of keeping fit as well as giving you the chance to meet new friends and grow fresh produce!

You can rent one of our 440 allotment plots across eight sites. All sites have water.

  1. Watt Close, Bromsgrove (behind Sanders Park)
  2. Stourbridge Road, Bromsgrove (next to the Princess of Wales Hospital)
  3. Rigby Lane, Aston Fields, Bromsgrove
  4. Newton Road, Aston Fields, Bromsgrove
  5. Stoke Road, Aston Fields, Bromsgrove
  6. Roundhill, Lickey End, Bromsgrove (Located off Staple Flats)
  7. Stoneybridge, Fairfields, (Located off Maderley Road)
  8. Pool Furlong, Clent.

Things to consider

  1. You must be resident in the district to rent an allotment from us
  2. You may also be able to rent an allotment off your local parish council
  3. If you've got a big load of allotment waste you can have it collected by our bulky waste collection service.

Apply for an allotment

Please complete our online enquiry form to enquire , or you can contact the team here.

Please be advised that we always have waiting lists for all our sites.

Prices

  1. Large plot (1/16 acre): £42.45 a year
  2. Small Plot (1/32 acre): £29.75 a year.

Prices include water. Existing customers are invoiced every October for the forthcoming year.

Pay for your allotment

  1. Online here.
  2. In person at our customer service centre.
  3. Over the telephone.
