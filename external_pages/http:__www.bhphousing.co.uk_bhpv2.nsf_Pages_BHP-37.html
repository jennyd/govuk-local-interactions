Anti-social behaviour

We work to improve the quality of life for our residents.

 

We can help with:

 

  • neighbour disputes 
  • noise nuisance
  • harassment
  • anti-social behaviour.

 

Our team aims to resolve complex, serious or persistent anti social behaviour, nuisance and harassment cases

 

Contact details

Anti-Social Behaviour Team:

 

  • telephone number:020 8937 2951 
  • fax facility: 020 8937 2265
  • email: asb@bhphousing.co.uk 


Neighbourhood Warden Service:

The Neighbourhood Warden Service tackles problems on estates such as graffiti, dumped rubbish or noise nuisance.

 

Residents can call a confidential hotline number 020 8937 2727 between 3pm and midnight to report an incident that is happening or any issues that concern their property or neighbourhood.
