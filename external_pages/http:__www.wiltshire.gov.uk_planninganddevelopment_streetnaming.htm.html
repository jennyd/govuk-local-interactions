Introduction

We have a statutory duty to name and number streets and to approve and register official property addresses in the county of Wiltshire. Prior to 1 April 2009 this street naming and numbering function was the remit of the district councils of Wiltshire.

Wiltshire maintains a comprehensive and high standard for naming streets and numbering or naming properties and allows the following:

  • Emergency services to find a property quickly (delays can cost lives and money)
  • Mail to be delivered efficiently
  • Visitors to find where they want to go
  • Reliable delivery of services and products
  • Records of service providers to be kept in an effective manner

Street naming and numbering policy

The purpose of the Street naming & numbering policy Street Naming and Numbering Policy 78kb is to establish the correct process and rules around the processing of:

  • Changing an existing address
  • Registering a new address on an existing street
  • Registering a new street and addresses on the street
  • Renaming an existing street or renumbering all existing properties on an existing street
  • Ensuring that all properties in Wiltshire are assigned an official address that is clear and unambiguous.  

Street naming and numbering procedure and costs

To apply, please complete the correct application form below:

  • Form 1 - Application to change a house name on an unnumbered property Form 1 - Application to change a house name on an unnumbered property 79kb
  • Form 2 - Application to allocate a house name to or change a house name on a numbered property Form 2 - Application to allocate a house name to or change a house name on a numbered property 81kb
  • Form 3 - Application to change a company name on a property Form 3 - Application to change a company name on a property 53kb
  • Form 4 - Application for registering a new property / properties on an existing street(s) Form 4 - Application for registering a new property / properties on an existing street(s) 65kb
  • Form 5 - Application for renaming an existing street or renumbering all existing properties on an existing street Form 5 - Application for renaming an existing street or renumbering all existing properties on an existing street 99kb (Please note: This request can only be made by the town/parish council)
  • Form 6 - Application for naming of new streets and numbering properties on that street Form 6 - Application for naming of new streets and numbering properties on that street 63kb

  • Form A - Authorisation from town/ parish councill Form A - Authorisation from town/ parish council 148kbl
  • Form B - consent for new streets(s) to be named after living persons Form B - Consent living person(s) 24kb
  • Form C - consent for new street(s) to be named after deceased persons Form C - Consent deceased person(s) 25kb
  • Form D - Consent for a street (s) to be renamed after living or decesaed person(s) Form D - Renaming consent: living/ deceased person (s) 27kb

  • Information on fees for street naming and numbering Information on fees for street naming and numbering 167kb

Please note: The current policy now has a pre-application stage for any developments involving the creation of a new street. The developer will need to liaise with the relevant town/parish council to agree on acceptable street name(s) before submitting the formal application to us.
