Request a repair

Please contact us to request any non-emergency repairs or maintenance on a Council home.

Request a non-emergency repair

Timescales

  • Non-urgent repairs will be given a timescale between 9 and 28 days, these are repairs that are no risk to your Health and Safety.
  • We may carry out a survey before starting any work and some non-urgent repairs may be placed on a future planned programme
  • All non-urgent repairs that will need the customer to be at the property for access, will be given an appointment.
