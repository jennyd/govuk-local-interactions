Compliments and or complaints

Whether you have a complaint or compliment we want to hear from you

What is a Complaint?

The Council has defined this as: "an expression of dissatisfaction about the service provided by the Council".

Why we want to hear from you

We want to listen to your views and respond by providing quality services which meets your needs.

We are continually trying to improve services, so we need to know when you are dissatisfied (or particularly pleased) with the service you have received.
