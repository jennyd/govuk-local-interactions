Who Said What?

What are you looking for

Welcome to Welwyn Hatfield Borough Council's Consultation Portal

Read the consultation documents and make your comments here.

Thank you for visiting Welwyn Hatfield’s planning consultation portal. Listed within this welcome page is a list of past and current consultation events.

Consultations can take the form of documents, questionnaires/surveys or forums on a given topic. You can search the entire Consultation Portal using the 'Search' box above. Individual consultations can be selected using the drop down box next to 'Show Consultations for:'

You can also search for Comments, Consultees and Agents by clicking on 'Who Said What?' above.

In some instances, you will be asked to register or log in before being able to participate in a consultation event.  To register please click on the 'Login/Register' link in the panel at the top of the screen.
