Cookies on Southampton City Council's website

We use cookies on our website to make sure you get the most out of your visit. We also collect non-essential, anonymous cookies to help us improve future visits. By continuing to view our web site without changing your browser settings, we'll assume that you are happy to accept the cookies on our site. Your cookie settings can be changed at any time.

Bin Collection Calendar

To see your bin collection days please enter your postcode or street (minimum 6 characters) in the search below. Your address will appear in a list, please select it and wait a few seconds for the calendar to appear.

We have not found any matching addresses. If your address is within the Southampton City Council boundary, please contact us using the feedback form below.

Any revised bin collection days for bank holidays will be shown on the calendar

Please Note: Garden Waste Collections will not display unless you have subscribed to the scheme.

If there is an issue with the required information not displaying then please email actionline@southampton.gov.uk who will respond to you directly.
