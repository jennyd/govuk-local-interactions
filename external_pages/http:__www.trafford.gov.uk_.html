Closure of Chester Road Household Waste Recycling Centre 

Due to maintenance and site improvements, the Household Waste Recycling Centre at Chester Road, Stretford will close from Wednesday 2nd March to Wednesday 23rd March.  It will re-open on Thursday 24th March 2016.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
