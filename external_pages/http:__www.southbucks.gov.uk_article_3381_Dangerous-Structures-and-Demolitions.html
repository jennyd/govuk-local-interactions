Dangerous Structures and Demolitions

The Building Control Services at South Bucks District Council and Chiltern District Council are now working as a joint team based as the South Bucks office.

 

** 01895 837296 ** 
 

Dangerous Buildings are controlled by the Council under Sections 77 and 78 of the Building Act 1984.

We respond to all reports of dangerous buildings, walls and structures within the Chiltern and South Bucks districts on a 24 hour 365 days a year basis.

During office hours you can telephone Building Control on 01895 837296.

For out of hours emergencies that cannot wait until the next working day, please telephone 07768 502183 or contact Thames Valley Police or Bucks Fire Police Rescue.

If the building or structure poses a potential danger to the safety of people, we take appropriate action to remove the danger. We have legal powers to require the owners of dangerous buildings or structures to remedy the defects or we may direct our contractors to carry out works to make the building or structure safe. In addition, we provide advice to the Fire Brigade on the structural condition of Buildings during fire fighting or  in the case of vehicular damage.

Demolitions Under Section 80 of the Building Act 1984 anyone intending to carry out demolition work within the Area is required to notify the Council either in writing or via email.

This should be done a minimum of six weeks prior to the demolition.

The Council then has the six week period to consult with appropriate bodies such as

  • Fire and Rescue Authority
  • Highways Authority
  • Thames Water Utilities
  • Environment Agency
  • Environmental Health Department
  • Health and Safety Executive

 

We will also issue and respond to appropriate notices under Sections 81 and 82 of the Building Act 1984.

This process normally only takes a few days but may take longer in some instances.

You can contact us via this site or by ringing the the Council on 01895 837296 during office hours for further information.
