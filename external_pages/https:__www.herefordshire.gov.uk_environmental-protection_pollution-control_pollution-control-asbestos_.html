Asbestos

For advice on disposing of materials containing, or believed to contain asbestos or information about asbestos in the workplace please contact environmental health:

  • Telephone: 01432 261761
  • Fax: 01432 261982
  • Online: Contact us form
  • Address: Environmental Health Commercial Team, Herefordshire Council, Blueschool House, PO Box 233, Hereford HR1 2ZB

Commercial disposal of asbestos

For information on the commercial disposal of asbestos products and advice on appropriate contractors and/or sites please contact waste management:

  • Telephone: 01432 260051
  • Online: Contact us form
  • Visit one of our customer service centres
