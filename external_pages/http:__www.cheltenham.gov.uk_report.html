If you suspect someone of committing benefit fraud you can report it to us anonymously.

Dangerous dogs

Roads and traffic

Report other problems

General enquiries

If you have a problem we ask for you to report it to us.  By doing this we can put things right, for you and for everyone else who uses council services.

We also welcome any compliments or suggestions you might wish to send us.

Road and pavement enquiries

Please contact Gloucestershire to report problems with pavements, footpaths, street light out, potholes, road works and maintenance.
