Applying for a place at a school nursery

This information is for parents wishing to apply for a place at a community school nursery in Merton.

If you are applying for a place at any of the Voluntary Aided (Church affiliated) school nurseries, or a place at one of the primary Academies (Benedict or Harris Primary Academy Merton) you must contact the school directly to get their application form.

You can apply for places at these schools and also apply for a place in a Merton community nursery class.

Children born between 1 April 2013 and 31 March 2014

The application process for these places began on 1 September 2015. You can apply online for a place at a Merton community nursery. Simply click on the logo below to be taken to the online admissions website.

Please see the attached completing an online application leaflet (60kB) if you require any assistance completing your online application.

The Starting Nursery 2016/2017 booklet (1.0MB) contains all of the information you need regarding the application process.

1 April 2013 - 31 August 2013 Starts Nursery from September 2016
1 September 2013 - 31 December 2013 Starts Nursery from January 2017
1 January 2014 - 31 March 2014 Starts Nursery from April 2017

The closing date for applications to these intakes is 15 January 2016. Applications will be accepted after this date but may not be considered until all on time allocations have been made. In order to maximise your chances of being offered a place at one of your preferred schools, please ensure that your application has been submitted by 15 January 2016.

Important dates

The key dates for the Nursery 2016/17 process can be found in the following table. Please ensure that you meet the date for submitting your online application. Submitting a late application could seriously jeopardise your chances of being offered a place at one of your preferred community school nurseries.

Date Activity
September to December 2015 Parents can contact primary schools to arrange visits.
15 January 2016 Closing date for receipt of online applications. Common Application Forms should be returned to your first preference school.
11 February 2016 Closing date for applications from people moving into the borough, or changing address after 15 January 2016.
March to April Applications are ranked according to admissions criteria.
6 May 2016 Offer day, letters are posted to applicants. Parents who applied online will be able to view the outcome of their application from the evening of 6 May.
20 May 2016 Date by which reply slips must be returned.

Children born between 1 September 2011 and 31 March 2013

The application process for nursery places has already been completed however you can still submit a late application. Please complete the In Year Nursery application form (0.1MB) and return it to School Admissions at Merton Civic Centre.

1 September 2011 - 31 August 2012 Starts Nursery from September 2015
1 September 2012 - 31 December 2012 Starts Nursery from January 2016
1 January 2013 - 31 March 2013 Starts Nursery from April 2016

Please be aware that nursery availability in many schools is limited. For more information on which schools may have places please see our Nursery Vacancy Checker (59kB). When deciding on a nursery, we recommend that parents consider whether they are likely to be offered a place at the school in Reception. Attendance at a nursery is not considered when Reception applications are being processed. More information on how Reception places were allocated for September 2015 can be found on the Starting Reception page.
