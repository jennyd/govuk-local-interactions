Conservation area supplementary planning guidance (SPG)

The purpose of the SPG is to provide the background to the designation of the borough's conservation areas and to list the architectural, historic and environmental elements that have formed the framework for the Council's Conservation Area Appraisals. The document is available below:

  • Conservation Areas Supplementary Planning Guidance (PDF)

Conservation area appraisals

Each conservation area is described in detail in appraisal documents, which also include information about the history behind some of the buildings and villages.
