Manholes and Drain Covers

Manholes and drain covers in Devon are owned by Devon County Council and other companies, e.g. Gas, Electricity, Water and Communication companies. Council owned manholes and drain covers, will be repaired, replaced or adjusted within seven working days, Faults to other manholes and drain covers will be forwarded by the Council, to the appropriate company.

If you would like to report a fault with a manhole or drain cover or make a claim, please complete the online fault reporting form.
