Contact our Animal Warden

Animal Warden van

What we do

We have a dedicated Animal Warden service. Our warden looks after stray, lost, stolen or dangerous dogs. We also respond to nuisance caused by dogs, such as dog fouling and barking.

Contact the Animal Warden

  • Call our Animal Warden on 01635 519 171 or
  • Email: environmental.health@wokingham.gov.uk 
  • Emergency Out of Hours Number: 0800 212 111    

Concerned about an animal?

  • In an emergency, (e.g. a dog has been left in a car on a warm day, or an animal is being beaten there and then, etc.) call the police on 999 for urgent assistance
  • If you're worried about how a pet animal is being treated but don't think it is actually suffering you can report animal cruelty on the RSPCA website on call the RSPCA on 0300 1234 999

More information

For help about animal welfare contact our Trading Standards team, who look after Animal Welfare Enforcement.
