Public transport

The Network West Midlands website provides public transport information for across Solihull.

Bus travel

Visit Network West Midlands for:

  • tickets & passes
  • bus timetables
  • travel maps

Rail travel

Visit Network West Midlands for:

  • tickets & passes
  • train timetables
  • an interactive rail map

Travel passes for older & disabled people

A concessionary travel pass allows you to travel for free at certain times of the day.

You can check if you qualify & apply for a concessionary pass at the Network West Midlands website:

  • apply for an older person's travel pass
  • apply for a disabled person's travel pass
