Allotments in Bedford

Over 300 allotment plots are administered by the Borough Council. They are located on three sites:

  • Allen Park, Old Ford End Road
  • Goldington Field, Barkers Lane (opposite Wendover Drive)
  • Newnham Avenue (entrance next to Priory Methodist Church)

 

January 2016 – There are plots available at all three sites.

All sites have a water supply (April to September) and security fencing. Access to sites is via keys supplied against a £10.00 deposit.

The cost of renting a full plot for the year April 2016 to March 2017 is £54.40 per year, with a concessionary rate of £31.90 for senior citizens, students or if you are unemployed.

Half plots are usually offered to new tenants so that as many people as possible have the opportunity to cultivate a plot.

Please click here to download a copy of the Tenancy Agreement.
