We aim to exceed the high standards people expect of our waste and cleansing services whether they visit, live or work in the City.

Waste from businesses

If you're a resident or a business in the City we can help you deal with your waste responsibly. Businesses should read more about the City's Time Banding scheme, which helps to keep the City clear of rubbish bags during office hours. Businesses that are actively helping us to keep the City clean and deal with their waste responsibly can enter the Clean City Awards.

 Littering

Our Street Environment Officers can fine you if you drop litter, including cigarette butts. Cigarette bins are widely available around the city to dispose of your cigarette. Find out how you can reduce the litter and give up smoking.

Remember, you can help us to maintain a high standard by reporting any cleansing issues that you see when you're out and about.

Body worn video

In December 2015 our Street Environment Officers started a six month trial of wearing body worn video cameras. The cameras provide an audio and visual record of our officers' activities by providing an irrefutable record of events, giving protection to both the public and officers. There are a number of significant benefits associated with the introduction of body worn video including an enhanced standard of evidence and a expected reduction in aggressive behaviour faced by officers in the course of their work.

During the six month trial period we will be reviewing its use to consider its effectiveness, which aspects of its use could be further developed and importantly to consider if its continued use would be justified. During this trial period we welcome your views. If you would like to comment or make a suggestion we would be very pleased to hear from you by email at bwv@cityoflondon.gov.uk​
