Chaperone Application Form

 

89KB

It is a legal requirement that children of compulsory school age who are taking part in performances, filming or modelling assignments are supervised by a registered chaperone, unless they are under the direct supervision of a parent or registered tutor.

The checks associated with becoming a chaperone are made by the Education Welfare Service before issuing the appropriate license.

Please follow the link on the right hand side of this page to download the Chaperone application form.

Please note you must submit the following with your application:

  • Photograph
    • Two recent passport size photographs
  • References
    • Name and address of two referees who will be asked to provide a written reference

When the Education Welfare Service receives your application they will apply for written references. Once these references have been received they will contact you to arrange an interview. If you are successful at the interview stage they will send a Disclosure Application Form for the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS form) to you. When you are completing this form please read the DBS Guidance Notes attached with the form and remember the following:

  • Section B13 must state CHAPERONE - VOLUNTEER
  • Section B14 must state NORTH EAST LINCOLNSHIRE COUNCIL
  • Section Y8 must be completed

Please allow at least eight weeks for the DBS check to be completed by the Home Office.

When Education Welfare receives confirmation of a clear DBS check they will issue a licence. License requirements will be reviewed individually.
