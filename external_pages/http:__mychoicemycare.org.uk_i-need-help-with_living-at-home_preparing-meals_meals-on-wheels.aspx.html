What is available?

Community Meals, often known as 'meals on wheels', can help ensure that people receive proper nutrition on a daily basis, whatever their circumstances.

All the meals are nutritionally balanced, cooked locally and delivered hot to the home in foil containers. We recommend that you eat the meals hot, soon after delivery and do not try to reheat them. A typical meal will consist of a hot main course of meat or fish with vegetables and a hot or cold dessert. Diabetic, low carbohydrate, low fat, soft diet, gluten free and low sodium, as well as vegetarian, Halal, Kosher, Asian and Afro-Caribbean meals are also available.

Meals on wheels

Who can get meals on wheels?

This service is for people aged 18 and over, normally older people, who are unable to prepare and cook one hot meal a day. This may be a permanent situation due to sight loss, a physical or learning disability, illness or a temporary situation such as recovering after a stay in hospital, to help you maintain your independence and quality of life.

If you or the person you care for find it difficult to get to the supermarket, Wiltshire Farm Foods (Apetito) offer a range of 250 frozen meals and desserts, developed by their team to give you a well-balanced, nutritious daily diet. Their meals can be cooked in a few minutes form the freezer in your microwave or oven making it easier for you to enjoy meals.

Our local provider Wiltshire Farm Foods also offer the following benefits:

  • Deliver throughout the whole of Lincolnshire  at least once a week. In the Lincoln Region, we deliver four times a week and Gainsborough Region, twice per week. As a local business we have a very good knowledge of the area.
  • Delivery is free.
  • All our staff undergo an enhanced DBS Police check.
  • We have our own Chefs, a Product Development Team and work closely with dieticians to constantly develop new recipe ideas.
  • We have audio CD versions of our Brochure and Special Diet Guide for customers with impaired vision.
  • Our meals are suitable for many dietary needs such as Diabetes, Gluten Free etc.
  • We have been operating this service in Lincolnshire for over twenty one years.

They offer a comprehensive, free delivery service throughout the county by DBS checked staff, and you can choose when you would like deliveries and how often based on your individual needs. Contact your case manager for more information about the possibility of this being part of your care package.

DBS:The Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) helps employers make safer recruitment decisions. It also prevents unsuitable people from working with vulnerable groups, including children, through its criminal record checking and barring functions.

A Country Kitchen is a meals on wheels service that provides hot, healthy and nutritious food to your door every day. They already provide meals for Age UK and can provide them for people who  need them in the Spalding and South Holland area. Their meals are cooked fresh on a daily basis using good local suppliers.

LincsHotMow (Lincs Hot Meals on Wheels) is a meals on wheels service that also provides locally sourced hot food and even a sandwich supper to your door in the North Kesteven area. They already provide meals to local residential homes. They can deliver to the following postcodes: LN1 LN2 LN3 LN4 LN5 LN6 NG34 NG32 NG31 and NG13 but will be happy to consider other areas adjoining these. They travel from north of Lincoln to south of Sleaford and across to Grantham covering all villages in between.

You could also arrange for a carer to come in and prepare meals for you through a private home care agency but remember to take care to choose one that is registered and inspected by the Care Quality Commission (CQC). Follow the link below for more information.

Can I get help with the cost?
