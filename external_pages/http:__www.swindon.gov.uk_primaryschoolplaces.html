Primary school admissions for September 2016

Applications for Primary School places for September 2016, can be made from Tuesday, 1 September 2015

Unless you decide to educate your child at home, your child must be in school the term following their fifth birthday. For example, if your child was born between:

  • 1 September 2011 and 31 August 2012 they can start Primary or Infant school in September 2016
  • 1 September 2008 and 31 August 2009 they can start Junior school in September 2016
