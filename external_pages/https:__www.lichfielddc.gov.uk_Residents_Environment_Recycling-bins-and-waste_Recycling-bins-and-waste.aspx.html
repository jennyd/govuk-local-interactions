Recycling, bins and waste

We work with Tamworth Borough Council to collect your waste and recycling.

To find out when your bins will be emptied and what you can put in your bins, visit www.recycleforlichfielddistrict.co.uk

If you have any questions call our customer services team on 0345 002 0022 (calls charged at local rate if calling from a landline).

For a full range of waste and recycling information visit our waste and recycling website
