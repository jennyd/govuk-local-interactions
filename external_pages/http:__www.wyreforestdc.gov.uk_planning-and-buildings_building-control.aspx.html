Building Control

Establishment of North Worcestershire Building Control Partnership

Bromsgrove DC, Redditch BC and Wyre Forest DC have agreed to share their building control resources in order to allow service improvements and resilience to be achieved, whilst at the same time being able to offer a more flexible customer focused service.

The new partnership commenced on 1stNovember 2011.

Full information on North Worcestershire Building Control including making an application, fees and all kinds of advice including design, construction, access and dangerous buildings is available on Bromsgrove.gov.uk
