Bulky Waste Collections

Book a one-off large items collection today and let us get rid of the things you can't!
We will collect your large items such as beds, sofas and fridges from outside your home. Collections cost £25 each, or £23 online, and can take more than one item.

  • Anyone who pays their council tax to Birmingham City Council can book a large items collection with us, just fill in the form below.
  • We will take up to six items for each large items collection you book. Three bin bags count as one item, so you can have up to 18 rubbish sacks/sacks of garden waste collected.
  • To complete this form you will need an online account. You can register an account here if you don't already have one.

White goods: As we take special care to collect fridges/fridge freezers/freezers and washing machines (white goods), a separate crew will collect these items. This may mean that when booking a large items collection, at no additional cost, two separate collection dates may be provided for you. This will only occur if you book any white goods in addition to another large item(s) such as a sofa or a bed. If you do have any white goods you wish to be collected, please be sure to select the ‘fridge/fridge freezer/freezer’ and/or ‘washing machine’ options when booking via the online form below. White goods must not be selected via the ‘other items’ section of the form otherwise your items may not be collected.

Your large items collection

Once you have booked your large items collection we will let you know the date of your collection.

  • Collections will normally happen between 6am and 3pm, Monday - Friday.
  • Please leave all listed items at the front of your property (not on the pavement) by 6am on the day of collection and no earlier than 3:30pm the day prior to collection.

Things we won't collect as part of a large items collection

  • Soil, rubble and building waste
  • Broken glass panes (unless boxed or wrapped and clearly marked)
  • Sheds
  • Boilers
  • Gas Canisters
  • Asbestos - report asbestos removal here
  • Pianos
  • We do collect mirror/glass panes, as long as they are no more than 1 x 1.5 metres in size

You can take all of the above, except asbestos, to your nearest Household Recycling Centre (Tip)

More information

Having problems accessing your account?

If you aren’t getting temporary password emails, it is likely that you don’t have an online account registered to the email address you’re trying. In this case, please register an online account before attempting to order your garden waste collection service for 2015. For a fuller explanation of how to get around problems accessing your online account, please visit our account help page.

Booking a large items collection for bin bags

If you have a large amount of bin bags that you need to dispose of, you can book a large items collection. Three rubbish sacks count as one item.

Donating items in good condition to a charity or re-use organisation

If you have unwanted items, such as furniture, that is still in good condition, consider donating it to those in need. For more information visit our page on re-use charities and organisations in Birmingham.

Cancellations and missed collections

Please call our Contact Centre on 0121 303 1112 to cancel your bulky waste collection or report a missed bulky waste collection. Please note: cancellations must be received at least 2 working days before the appointment date (for example, a collection due on Monday must be cancelled by no later than Wednesday of the previous week).
