Strategic Local Plan Consultation

Closing date: 19 February 2016

The Council is inviting representations on its Strategic Local Plan Publication Draft, which sets out the long-term planning strategy for the City and District of St Albans.  It provides overarching policies and principles for what can be built and where over two decades.

Further details

Netherway Greenspace Action Plan Consultation

Closing date: 22 February 2016

St Albans City and District Council working in partnership The Countryside Management Service have drawn up a Greenspace Action Plan (GAP) for the local green space at Netherway. The GAP will guide the management of the site, covering a five year period between 2016 and 2021.

Further details
