New location for decision notices

 

Decision notices and officer's reports from 16 May 2014 onwards are now available with the other application documents in the Planning Document Viewer. Please follow the link below.

 

Planning document viewer

 

Decision notices

 

Decision notices are available week-by-week from 2009 onwards.

 

You can select a specific historic weekly list from the menu to the left. If you know the application number, you can find the decision date through PublicAccess.
