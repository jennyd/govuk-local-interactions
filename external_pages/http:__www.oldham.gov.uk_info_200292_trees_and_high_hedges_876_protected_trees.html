Protected trees

Tree Preservation Orders protect trees.

There are approximately 500 Tree Preservation Orders in Oldham.

View Tree Preservation Orders

Contact the Planning Office to view the orders for free or to purchase a copy.

Undertake work to protected trees

With Tree Preservation Orders:

  • Application form (PDF)
  • Notes (PDF)
  • Checklist

In Conservation Areas:

  • Application form (PDF)
  • Notes (PDF)
  • Checklist

Nearly all applications are determined within 6 weeks.

You can meet with the Planning Officer and Aboribultural Officer on site to discuss applications and ask advice.

If consent is refused, you can appeal to the Office of the Deputy Prime Minister against a decision made by the Council (details are in the decision notice you will be sent).
