Bulky item collections

Doncaster Council provides collection services for your unwanted bulky household items, such as furniture, electrical appliances and certain DIY wastes. Please read details of the different collection services available.

To request a bulky collection you can do so online by completing the relevant form:

  • Request a bulky collection (disposable)
  • Request a bulky collection (re-use)
  • Request a fridge/freezer collection
  • Request an asbestos collection
  • Report a missed bulky collection

Requesting a bulky collection (disposable)

You can request up to four bulky collections per year, with a maximum of eight items per collection.

What can be collected:

  • bulky household items are items that you may expect to take with you should you move house, if they were in good working order (furniture, electrical goods, carpets etc) Please note that building waste and brick rubble, plasterboard, soil, garden waste, hazardous waste, liquid waste, car tyres and car parts are not removed on this service
  • you will need to put the items out for collection in an easily accessible location outside your property, (not in a garage or out-building), but within your boundary and you will need to tell us where they will be. We are not able to enter your property to collect items
  • if you leave items in your back garden make sure that they are properly contained, a manageable size and easily moved, ensuring that they will fit down the side of your house. If in doubt please leave the items in your front garden
  • if bags (bin liners) are used these should be tied so not to spill, the bags used should be no larger than an average black bin liner (sack) not a wheeled bin liner

We can only collect the exact items specified.  All items must be within the boundary of your property. If an item is collected using the bulky collection service, and it is considered that the item is reusable, that item may be resold accordingly through Doncaster Refurnish

Cost

  • the cost of a bulky collection is £25, however if you are in receipt of either Housing Benefit or Local Council Tax Support this cost is reduced to £10. You will need to provide us with your National Insurance Number, Benefit Reference Number and Post Code (so we can verify you are in receipt of a qualifying benefit)

Requesting a bulky collection (re-use)

There is no limit to the number of collections per year.

What can be collected:

  • if the items are in good working order and you think someone else could use them please request this service
  • ideally items should be presented outside for collection in an easily accessible location outside your property, but within your boundary, you will need to tell us where they will be. Please ensure the item/s are covered and kept from being spoilt
  • if you leave items in your back garden make sure that they are properly contained, a manageable size and easily moved, ensuring that they will fit down the side of your house. If in doubt please leave the items in your front garden. All items must be within the boundary of your property
  • we can collect reusable items from inside your house, if you would like us to do this you must include details on the form.

We can only collect the exact items specified.  All items must be within the boundary of your property.  An item collected using the bulky collection service, will be collected and may be resold accordingly through Doncaster Refurnish

Cost

  • your items will be collected free of charge, however, if it is considered that the items are unsuitable they will be left. Should you require the items collecting you will need to contact us to request and pay for a bulky collection (disposable), or you may wish to use one of Doncaster's Household Waste Recycling Centres

Requesting a fridge/freezer collection

  • you will need to put the items out for collection in an easily accessible location outside your property, (not in a garage or out-building), but within your boundary and you will need to tell us where they will be
  • please ensure fridges or freezers do not contain any food or drink
  • if your fridge or freezer is reusable we will be able to collect it from inside your house, if you would like us to do this you must include detail on the form
  • you will be advised of the next available collection day and will be able to book your collection for that day, however we cannot give you a specific time for the collection
  • if the collection is not completed on the scheduled day please let us know by completing the missed bulky collection form

Further information

Doncaster Council do not accept liability for any items left outside your property and do not accept responsibility if items are taken from outside your property prior to the collection date.

If you wish to cancel your bulky collection you can do so and request a refund, this must be done by informing our customer services team, please contact 01302 736000.  Refunds can only be processed up to the day before your agreed collection date. If you cancel your bulky collection after this time a refund will not be available.
