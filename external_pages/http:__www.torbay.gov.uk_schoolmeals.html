School Meals

Free School Meals

Find out if your child is eligible for free school meals and how to apply.
 
 
 
 
 
Each school has different arrangements for the school meals service and you should contact your child’s school for more details or look at the schools website. Remember that all children are entitled to a free school meal until the age of 7. Older children may be entitled to a free meal depending on circumstances. All schools are expected to provide meals that meet the School Food Standards which means your child will have a healthy and nutritious meal.
