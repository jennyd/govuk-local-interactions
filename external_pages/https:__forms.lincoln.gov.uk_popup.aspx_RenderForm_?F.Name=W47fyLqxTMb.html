Report a nuisance vehicle

The City of Lincoln Council is responsible for investigating nuisance vehicles in the city. This covers a wide range of issues such as people littering from their vehicles, a vehicle which may be abandoned or individuals selling vehicles on a public road from a business respect.

Please note that before a vehicle can be classed as a nuisance, various factors must be considered. Find more detailed information on our nuisance vehicles information page
