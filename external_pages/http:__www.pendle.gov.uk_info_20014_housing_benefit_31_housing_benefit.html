Housing Benefit

If you're on a low income and need financial help to pay all or part of your rent, you may be able to get Housing Benefit.

Housing Benefit is claimed from us and the Benefits Team will let you know how much you are entitled to.

To apply you must complete a claim form:

  • Collect one from one of our council shops or district offices

When you complete a claim form you will be told what information and evidence that you need to give us to support your claim. You will be asked to give us:

  • Proof of your identity
  • Proof of income and capital
  • Details of anyone else who lives with you

We can only accept original documents as proof of these things. We cannot accept copies.

Please do not be put off from claiming. We have a team of Benefit Advisors to help you. We can help you fill in the form and we will tell you what else you need to do. We may also be able to help you get the proof that you need. 

You can help us deal with your claim quickly by:

  • Answering all the questions on the claim form
  • Giving us proof of all your details as soon as possible
  • Replying straight away if we ask you for any more information
  • Contacting us quickly if there is something that you do not understand

If you are unable to get out easily, contact the Benefits Team and we can send a Visiting Officer to your home.

We will ask for your name, address and telephone number and why you need a visit. You will then be contacted within one working day to arrange a date and time for a Visiting Officer to call. We aim to visit you within 5 working days of your request for a home visit.  

We have a dedicated team of visiting officers and a team of benefit officers who all undertake home visits. When an officer calls at your home they will have identification to show you who they are.
