Who can we help with Housing?

There are many options open to you to help you to find accommodation in Milton Keynes but due to the demand for properties locally you will need to approach the task with patience and determination.

Housing Options advice to help you resolve your housing situation is provided online via the

Housing Options Self Help Tool

If you only require information we have provided quick access, useful information to meet a variety of situations:

  • General Housing Advice and Options
  • Homelessness Prevention
  • Domestic Abuse and Domestic Violence
  • Forced Marriage and Honour Based Violence
  • Harassment
  • 16 & 17 Year Olds
  • 18 – 35 Year Olds
  • Finding Accommodation as a Rough Sleeper
  • Neighbourhood Employment Programme - working opens up more housing choices
  • Leaving the Armed Forces
  • Finding Private Rented Accommodation
  • Issues of disrepair in non-council accommodation
  • Looking for Sheltered Housing
  • My Council or Housing Association Property is too big for me

  • Having Problems with your mortgage

  • I am being asked to leave my Private Rented Home

  • Help with Housing Costs
