How do I rent a garage from the Council?

If you wish to rent a garage from the Council, you must not be behind with rent on your home. To rent a garage please complete the garage rental application form.

Your application will be acknowledged and you will be contacted as soon as a garage becomes available.

Garages are also available to non-Council-tenants. However they will not have priority on the waiting list, they will be charged VAT in addition to a higher garage rent and method of payment must be Direct Debit.

What does it cost to rent a garage?

The current charges for renting a garage, per week, are as follows:

Garage Rent Levels:

Rent Level (£)
Council Tenants £10.10/ week
Leaseholder £10.10/ week
Private Tenants £13.04/ week, plus VAT

NB: if you fail to pay and fall into arrears of more than four weeks' garage rent, your garage tenancy will end.

Ending a garage tenancy

You must give two clear weeks' notice in writing, which starts from the Monday after we receive your notice. If garage keys are not returned on the date they are due, rent will continue to be charged to you until the keys are received. If the keys are not returned at all you will be charged for the cost of changing the locks.

Please make sure you do not leave any rubbish in the garage or you will be charged for the cost of removing it.

If you rent a garage and move home to a different Council property, the garage tenancy will be kept in your name and we will continue to charge you garage rent unless you give us written notice and return the keys.
