Waste and Recycling

The Waste and Recycling Section provides a high quality service to residents throughout the borough, working towards achieving a cleaner, greener and safer environment.

Important information regarding North A and B calendars.

There are some changes to the North A and B collection calendars, which were delivered in October 2015. Revised calendars will be sent out over a 3 week period w/c 18th January 2016.

The correct calendars are also available to download from our website (please select 'When is my Collection')

Please present your bins for collection by 7:00am.

By selecting the 'When is my collection' image above, you will be able to find waste and recycling collection information for your property.

Calendars are available online. Please use the 'When is my bin collection?' service page, which will identify which calendar you need to download.   

Information regarding our new textile collection service can be found on the Kerbside textile collection page.

When Is My Collection Video

Why not use our text messaging service

You can now text 60075 to check which collection is due that week. Start the message with 'BROX NEXT' including your postcode and house number - i.e. 'BROX NEXT NG9 1AB 32', you will be sent a message back with details of your next scheduled collection.

'Each message sent is charged at the user's standard network rate although some inclusive network plans exclude messages to short codes and therefore prices may vary.'

Beverage Carton Recycling (such as those made by Tetra Pak)

 Beverage Carton Recycling facilities can be found at the following locations:

  • Leyton Crescent Recreation Ground, Beeston NG9 1PS
  • Chilwell Olympia, Queens Road West, Chilwell NG9 1GS
  • Old Moor Lodge, Mornington Crescent, Nuthall NG16 1DP
  • Greasley Sports and Community Centre, Dovecote Road, Newthorpe NG16 3QN
  • Brinsley Parish Hall, Brinsley NG16 5BY

New - A Mandarin translated leaflet is available under documents opposite.

Love Food Hate waste, take a look at Nottinghamshire County Councils web pages for tips and recipes.

If you have a problem with your collection service please contact Waste & Recycling.

Assisted Collections

If you are elderly, infirm or registered disabled we are able to collect your bins/bags from a specified location. Please contact customer services for further information.
