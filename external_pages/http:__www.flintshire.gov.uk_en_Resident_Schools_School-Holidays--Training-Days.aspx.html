School Staff Standard Training Days

Important Notice

Please note that training days for teaching staff are held periodically. The Standard Training Dates are shown below. On training days, the school premises are open but pupils are not admitted. Full-time teachers work 195 days in a year and teach children in a classroom for no more than 190 days. 5 days = Designated staff training days.

Schools can choose to vary these dates. Please check with your school for any variations to the standard dates.

Welsh Government - School term dates - Regulations

Welsh Ministers have used the Education (Wales) Act 2014 to make legislative changes to how term dates are set in order to enable harmonisation of school term dates for all maintained schools.

These changes mean that local authorities and the governing bodies of voluntary aided and foundation schools can be directed by the Welsh Ministers on what their term dates must be if those dates are not harmonised, or if they need changing.

This consultation seeks your views on the proposed term dates and draft direction.
