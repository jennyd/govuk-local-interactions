Bulky Household Waste

If you wish to dispose of bulky household waste items, such as beds, sofas and fridges/freezers, these can be disposed of free of charge at the Nuffield Household Waste Recycling Centre

Alternatively, we work in partnership with Dorset Reclaim who, for a charge, will take away your bulky household waste. If you require these items to be collected from your property, please contact the Poole branch of Dorset Reclaim directly on 01202 679350. However they are unable to collect and dispose of:

  • asbestos
  • garden waste
  • food waste
  • commercial refrigeration

Opening hours:

Monday – Thursday 9:30 - 4:30pm
Friday 9:30 - 4:00pm
