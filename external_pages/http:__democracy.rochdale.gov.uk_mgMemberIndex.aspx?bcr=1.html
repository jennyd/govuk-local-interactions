Councillor contact information

Rochdale Borough Council has 60 elected councillors. There are currently 48 Labour, 11 Conservative and 1 Liberal Democrat. The council is responsible for appointing the Leader of the Council which is currently Councillor Richard Farnell as Leader of the Labour Group.
