Closures & Disruption to Council Services

Top

Severe weather can sometimes lead to disruptions to services, even closures. During these times we will always do our very best to get information to you as quickly on this page. 

Social Media

For all our latest updates on Twitter: https://twitter.com/BlaenauGwentCBC  & Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/blaenaugwentcbc

Winter Maintenance & Gritting

Our gritting team will react to weather warnings and will be dispatched when required throughtout the night and daytime hours. For further information regarding gritting routes and salt bins, please visit our Winter Maintenance page: http://www.blaenau-gwent.gov.uk/advice/17009.asp 

 

Last Updated: 

Recycling and Refuse 

During times of adverse weather, the Recycling and Refuse department will ensure all reasonable attempts are made to keep the service running, as effectively as possible.

We will constantly review information available e.g. the most recent weather forecast, local travel conditions and police advice on travelling, to resume collections, as soon as possible.

Last Updated: 

Meals on Wheels

All Services running as normal

Last Updated:  8.57am 15th Jan 2016

Highways Information

If snowfall is persistent, any ongoing Highways work will be temporarily suspended and staff will be assigned to snow clearing duties.

Last Updated:  15th Jan 2015

Cemeteries

In the case of heavy snowfall, all cemeteries will be kept open to fulfil the commitments of any planned funerals, please can we advise that you not visit our cemeteries unless absolutely necessary during the wintery conditions.

Last Updated:  15th Jan 2015

Street Cleansing

If snowfall is persistent, all Street Cleansing duties will be temporarily suspended and staff will be assigned to snow clearing duties.

Last Updated: 15th Jan 2015
