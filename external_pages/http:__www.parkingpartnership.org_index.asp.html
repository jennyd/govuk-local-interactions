The Parking Partnership is a council-run organisation which brings together all street-based parking services in Essex. The aim is to administer the parking rules to a fair, proportionate and consistent standard in order to provide a service in a reasonable and responsible way.

The service is a partnership between Essex County Council and twelve district/borough councils and is run in two areas; the North Partnership is led by Colchester Council and the South Partnership is led by Chelmsford Council.

Each Partnership is responsible for its area's on-street Civil Enforcement Officers ("traffic wardens"); the and enforcement process together with challenges to, and payments of, parking penalties ("parking fines") plus administration of the parking restrictions ("yellow lines") and the management and maintenance of the scheme.
