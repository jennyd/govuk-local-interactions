Transferring to a Havering secondary school in September 2016

Intro Text


If your child was born between the 1 September 2004 and 31 August 2005, they are due to start secondary school in September 2016.

Page Content

Applying for a school place

The application process for pupils starting a secondary school in Havering in September 2016 starts on 1 September 2015, and the closing date is 31 October 2015.

Information regarding the application process is published in the Transferring to a Havering secondary school brochure.

Apply online for a secondary school place
The application process for secondary schools via eAdmissions starts on 1 September 2015 and closes on the 31 October 2015.

When you will find out the results of your application

  • For children starting secondary school in September 2015 the national offer day will be Tuesday 1 March 2016

Please note: We will use the email address you supply with your application to advise you the outcome of your application. Emails will be sent during the evening of national offer day (1 March 2016).

Choosing a school

Choosing a school for your child is one of the most important decisions you will make for them. To have the best chance of getting your child into the primary or secondary school of your choice, you must understand the admissions process and start your research early.

Finding a school for your child

Start by looking online for schools in your area. You can do this by following the link to Finding schools in your area. Type in your postcode to find all the schools near your home.

Or to get a straight line distance (like the one we use to do the allocations) you can use our Interactive Mapping Software.

Instructions for using the Interactive Mapping Software

Find out more

Once you know which schools are near you, get as much information as you can about them. This will give you a better idea of which schools might suit your child most. For example, you might like to:

  • Visit the schools
  • Read the schools' most recent Ofsted reports
  • Read the schools' prospectuses
  • Read the local authority's Secondary School Admissions brochure (Transferring to a Havering secondary school)

Secondary School Statistics for the 2015 intake

You can view information about how Secondary School places were allocated for the 2015 intake, 2015 School Admissions Statistics page.

If you are not a Havering resident and you wish to apply for a Havering school you must contact your own local authority school admissions team.

If you wish to view the determined admissions criteria for Havering schools please visit the 2016-17 Admissions Arrangements page.
