Adults and Communities works in close partnership with the NHS and the voluntary sector to provide information, advice and services to help you stay as active and independent as possible. We support people who:

  • are aged 55+
  • have a physical and sensory impairment
  • have a learning disability
  • have mental health support needs
  • care for a friend or relative.

Information and Advice in Barnet

For general help and advice see our page on Advice and Support page which provides details about organisations in Barnet that can offer independent information and advice to our residents.

Social Care Connect

Social Care Connect is our online directory of information, advice and services in Barnet. You can search for local organisations in Barnet through this directory. 

Publications and leaflets

You can find all our information booklets, leaflets and factsheets, reports and strategies on our publications page. 

The Barnet Care Directory provides an overview of the range of support available covering topics such as home care, housing options, residential care and the wide range of support from local organisations.
