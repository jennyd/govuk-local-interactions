Community meals

Our traded services division offer a meal service delivered direct to your door.

This all year round service is available, upon request on 01454 865996, to anyone wishing to purchase meals, and is particularly helpful in supporting older, disabled and vulnerable people living in their own homes, who are not able to cater for themselves. If ordered by 10am on a week day, the meal can be delivered the same day.

All our meals are nutritionally balanced and our hot meals include roast dinners, casseroles, meat pie etc. Each meal comes with fresh vegetables, potatoes and a dessert, and is delivered daily up to seven days per week.

Cold meals are also available which include sandwiches, cake and fresh fruit if ordered along with a hot meal for lunch.

We can provide for most dietary needs such as diabetic, gluten free and vegetarian meals. The meals can be provided as standard, soft diet or puréed consistency.

We can also provide a variety of meals to meet religious and cultural needs, including:

  • Kosher meals
  • Caribbean and West Indian meals
  • Asian halal meals
  • Asian vegetarian meals

If you order a main meal and a tea meal they are delivered together between 11am and 2pm. We recommend that you eat your hot meal when it is delivered to enjoy it at its best. Please store hot food carefully as leaving hot food to cool could risk food poisoning.

The current charge for a main meal with a dessert is £4.08. A tea time meal is £2.95. However, these prices are subject to change. Payment can be made by direct debit or by a monthly invoice.

To cancel a meal please speak to your meals helper/driver the day before (or earlier if possible) or telephone 01454 865996.

It is important to notify us of meal cancellations to ensure you are not charged.

If you would like to discuss your dietary requirements, or you would like more information about religious or cultural meals, or if you would like to order a meals service, please telephone 01454 865996.
