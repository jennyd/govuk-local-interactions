Reporting flooding from public sewers, pipes and drains

Flooding from public sewers

Thames Water owns and manages the network of public foul and surface water sewers. The majority of public sewers are located in highways. The sewer maps are available for viewing the Council Offices. Please contact us on 020 7525 2000.

If you see a leak from a pipe in the road or footpath, please call Thames Water 0800 714 614.

Flooding from a burst water main

Thames Water is responsible for their supply up to and including the water stopcock. Please call Thames Water on 0800 714 614.

Flooding from the public highway

The council is making significant investments in gullies repair programme to ensure they are all fully functional.

To report flooding from the public highway or a blocked road gullies or gratings, contact us on 020 7525 2000 or fill in a street surface repair request.
