Adopt for Plymouth

Who are we?

We are part of Plymouth City Council and a registered adoption agency.

We provide loving, secure, permanent homes for children and young people who through no fault of their own are not able to live with their birth families. As part of the local authority we operate as a not-for-profit service, putting Plymouth's children first.

Our team consists of dedicated and experienced social workers and family support workers.

We need families for all sorts of children, but with a real need for school-age children, children with special needs and groups of brothers and sisters who need to grow up together.

We welcome enquiries from people in many areas of Devon and Cornwall. We set these priorities based on the needs of the children currently awaiting adoption in Plymouth.

If you would like to find out more about adopting a child, please download our information pack below, or please contact our team who will be happy to help you.
