Street Lighting Report

We will repair hazardous street light and illuminated signs within 1 hour of you contacting us. Non-emergency work will be done within 5 days. Faulty traffic lights will be attended to within 1 hour of you telling us and repaired within 24 hours.

If you wish to report a potentially dangerous street light or illuminated sign or faulty traffic light please ring 0300 555 2345.

For less urgent lighting defects either ring the number above or complete this form.
