Apply for a school place
Sign up to receive email updates about Primary Admissions 2016-2017
Apply for an Infant, Junior or Primary School Place 2016 to 2017 If your child lives in Nottingham city and is due to start at infant or primary school in September 2016*, or they are currently in year 2 at an infant school and will be transferring to junior school**, you will need to apply here for a school place. The closing date for applications was 15 January 2016. In-year School Places Apply here if your child needs a school place now, or wish to transfer your child from their current school. Transfer to Secondary School in Year 7 Apply here if your child should be starting secondary school in September 2016. Transfer to a 14 to 19 School, Academy or College in Year 10 Apply here if you would like your child to attend Nottingham University Academy of Science and Technology or any other 14 to 19 school. Choosing a school Information to help you decide which school you would like your child to attend. School Admissions Appeals If your request for a school place is turned down you can appeal to an independent appeal panel.
