Political control and political party make up:
Find out how many Adur councillors and wards there are

Adur District Council has 29 councillors (8 female councillors and 21 male councillors) in 14 wards. The following shows the political makeup and which political party is in control in Adur:

  • 20 Conservative (in current overall political control)
  • 6 UKIP
  • 2 Independent
  • 1 Labour

There are currently no councillor vacancies.
