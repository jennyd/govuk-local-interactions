What is Poole Lifeline?

Poole Lifeline provides an emergency contact system for people living in their own home.  It is designed to increase independence, providing assurance and peace of mind for individuals, relatives and carers.

Back to top

Who can have a Lifeline Unit?

It is available to people of all ages who would like to feel safer, more protected and independent in their own home

Back to top

How does it work?

The Lifeline device is linked to our dedicated control centre which provides a 24 hours a day, 365 days a year service to all our clients. If activated the unit will raise an alarm call to the control centre where one of our highly trained operators will quickly assess your situation and respond as necessary e.g. contacting a relative, key holder or emergency service.

Back to top

How much does the Service Cost

The cost of the service will vary depending on your needs and type of service you require.  Poole Lifeline can be from as little as £3.62 per week.

Back to top

What do I do next?

For a free no obligation demonstration, or if you would like to speak to someone further about the service you can-

  • fill in the lifeline further information form
  • call us on 01202 733255 (24hrs a day, 365 days a year) 
  • send us an email at lifeline@poole.gov.uk
