Appealing a Parking Charge Notice (PCN)

An appeal must be received within 7 days of the date on which the PCN was issued (day 1 being the date of the PCN). The decision of the Appeals Process is final. If your appeal is rejected, the discount period will be reset for a further 7 days from the date of the rejection letter.

Please note that appeals made for the following reasons are unlikely to be successful.

  • 1. I did not know it was a 'pay and display' car park.
  • 2. I could not find anywhere else to park.
  • 3. I went to get change for the meter.
  • 4. This is too much money to ask for a parking offence.
  • 5. I only stopped for a minute or two.
  • 6. I thought I was legally parked but I made a mistake.
  • 7. I am not the owner/keeper of the vehicle because I hired/leased the vehicle.
  • 8. My ticket or permit was not on display
  • 9. I forgot to display my Blue Badge
  • 10. I thought Sundays and Bank Holidays were free.
  • 11. I parked outside of marked bays.
