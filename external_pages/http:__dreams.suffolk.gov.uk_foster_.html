Foster a dream

Over 700 children are in care in Suffolk, with 400 over the age of 10. These children are unable to live with their birth parents for a variety of reasons which include neglect, abuse or more simply due to a parent’s ill health.

You can make a difference

Children in foster care need someone who can understand what they have been through, look at the world through their eyes and provide warmth, consistency and emotional support.

This is where you come in. If you are looking for a new challenge, have patience, compassion and have a spare room, you could provide a child with the secure home life they need to thrive and achieve.

It could be just for a few weeks or months, but for many it will be for the remainder of their childhood.

Through training and support we can help you to develop the skills and expertise to provide the therapeutic parenting they need to provide them with a brighter, more positive future.

An allowance is paid to cover living costs for the type of care being given.

If you think you could give a Suffolk child the care they dream about, contact the Fostering and Adoption Team or attend one of their drop-in information events happening around the county. 

An allowance is paid to cover living costs and dependant on the type of care being given.
