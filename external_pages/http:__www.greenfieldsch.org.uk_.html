Welcome

Greenfields is a Community Gateway Association based in the Braintree District, Essex.

We have launched our From Good to Great, 2015-20 Plan. In addition to keeping properties safe, secure and in a good condition, we will do lots more from 2015-20, including increasing our homes by up to 800, and spending £1.5million on improving communities.

Office closures: 24 March 2016

Our Witham and Halstead offices will close from 5pm on 24 March 2016. There will be a reception service at Greenfields House in Braintree. There are lots of ways to contact to us, including email, telephone calls, and using our Self-service Portal (allows you to view your repair history; check your rent balance, update your household details, send us a message and more). Or you may prefer to write to us or request a home visit.
 

Universal Credit - contact us

If you are making an application for Universal Credit, please contact us immediately. We can then liaise with the Department for Works & Pensions (DWP) to give them the information they need so they can complete your application as quickly as possible. Find out more about Benefit Changes.
