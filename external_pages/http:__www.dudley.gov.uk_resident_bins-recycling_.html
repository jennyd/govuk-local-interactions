What happens to our recycling

Improvements to your recycling and waste collections

Dudley Council is making improvements to your recycling and waste collections.

Your fortnightly recycling collection service will include plastic bottles and cardboard as well as the paper, cans and glass we already collect. Residents will receive a grey wheelie bin for non recyclable waste as well as two reusable bags for recycling by the spring of 2014.

If you have any problems with the new wheelie bin service you can enquire online or contact Dudley Council Plus.
