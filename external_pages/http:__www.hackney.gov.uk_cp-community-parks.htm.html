Parks and green spaces

Hackney has the largest expanse of green spaces in inner London. We have 58 parks, gardens and open spaces totalling around 330 hectares and ranging from the largest concentration of football pitches in Europe at Hackney Marshes to the beautiful and historic settings of Springfield and Clissold parks.

List of parks and green spaces

See our parks and green spaces list for information about Hackney's 58 parks. You can also find your nearest park on a map and see a list of our play areas.

News and events

Rethinking Parks

Over the next two years, Nesta, The Heritage Lottery Fund and The Big Lottery Fund are backing a small number of pioneering innovations, to try to find new business models that will enable our parks to thrive for the next century. Find out more about Rethinking Parks and the #ParkHack event.

Newsletter and What's on 

What's on in Hackney's parks lists all our events. We also have a monthly newsletter: Hackney leisure and green spaces news - July 2015 (PDF, 209KB). Subscribe by emailing parks@hackney.gov.uk. 

Barbecues in parks

You can use barbecues in the barbecue area (PDF, 529KB) in London Fields. If you use one elsewhere or fail to follow the barbecue guidelines (PDF, 202KB), it could be moved or extinguished.

Barbecues are not allowed in any other Hackney parks, unless they're part of an organised event with our permission. See our information on holding events in parks for more details. Approval for small events takes at least six weeks.

Paddling pools

Clissold Park and London Fields both have paddling pools open throughout the summer.

Lost property

If you've lost an item in one of our parks please email parks@hackney.gov.uk with the details. Please tell us when and where you think you lost it and we'll do our best to reunite you with your item.

Green Flag awards

19 of our parks have been awarded Green Flags. Keep Britain Tidy award them to the best green spaces in the country. Only parks with long term plans to maintain their high standards qualify for the awards. See the parks and green spaces list to see our winners.

Parks user groups

If you'd like to help care for and improve your local green spaces, you can join one of Hackney's 23 parks user groups.
