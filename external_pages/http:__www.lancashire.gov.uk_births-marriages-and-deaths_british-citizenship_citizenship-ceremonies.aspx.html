Citizenship ceremonies

If your application for British citizenship is successful, you will be invited to attend a citizenship ceremony. You will receive a letter of invitation from the Home Office and a letter from Lancashire County Council's citizenship ceremonies office setting out the dates and times of available ceremonies.

The citizenship ceremony is intended to celebrate the significance of becoming a British citizen and welcome you into your community.

It must take place within three months of finding out that your application was successful.
