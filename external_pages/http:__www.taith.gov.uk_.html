Welcome to Taith

From the 30th June 2014, the Taith Management Unit will cease to operate. For enquiries relating to bus funding and strategy in North Wales please contact Katie Wilby at Flintshire County Council. For matters relating to the current Regional Transport Plan and forthcoming Local transport Plan from April 2015, please contact Aled Davies at Gwynedd Council. For matters relating to Travel Planning or Sustainable Travel please contact Jessica Pritchard and Flintshire County Council. For any enquiries relating to bus services and other transport please contact your local council. Contact details are shown under the section Contact Us.
