The purpose of the Schools Admissions Team is to coordinate and allocate school places to children in the primary (4-11 years) and secondary (11-16 years) sector. The team also deals with requests for the transfer of primary and secondary aged pupils.

More information will be made available on these pages when the school admissions procedure is about to begin.

Contact us

All enquiries should be made to the school admissions on 01325 405907/01325 405909 or schools.admissions@darlington.gov.uk

School finder

School Admissions Information

For all queries regarding school admissions please contact the admissions team on 01325 405907 or email school.admissions@darlington.gov.uk

If you are seeking independent admissions advice you may wish to contact ACE (Advisory Centre for Education). Telephone 0300 0115 142 (normal opening hours Monday to Wednesday, 10am to 1pm, term time only) or visit the ACE website [external link]

If you are seeking independent support for your admission appeal you may wish to contact the Citizens Advice Bureau. Telephone 01325 256999, e-mail admin.desk@darlingtoncab.co.uk or visit the Citizens Advice Bureau website [external link]
