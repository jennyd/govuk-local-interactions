Bulky Collections

If your item is in good condition why not give it to someone who could use it?
If your items are unsuitable for re-use, you can take them to the Household Waste and Recycling Centre (HWRC) for disposal.

Collection Service

If you're not able to take large items to the Household Waste and Recycling Centre (HWRC) yourself, you can ask TOR2 to pick the items up for a small charge.
  • Yes:
  • bed bases
  • matresses
  • household furniture including wardrobes
  • sofas
  • chairs
  • white goods e.g: fridges, freezers, washing machines, cookers, dishwashers, tumble dryers
  • electrical goods
  • garden waste
  • No:
  • hazardous waste (in any form including liquid)
  • asbestos
  • flat/plate glass
  • broken mirrors/mirrors from built in wardrobes*
  • oil (cooking, car, diesel)
  • paint
  • paving slabs
  • photocopiers
  • pianos
* Mirrors on wardrobe doors/wall hanging mirrors (must be wrapped in newspaper and taped down to prevent shattering when crushed).
If you do not see an item on the list please contact TOR2 on 01803 701316 to discuss your requirements further or to book your collection.

Collection Days and Times

Bulky item collections are made between 7:30am - 4:00pm as follows:
  • Torquay - Mondays and Thursdays
  • Paignton / Brixham - Tuesdays and Fridays

About Your Collection

Items for collection should be left outside the property and preferably within the boundary of the property in a safe area.  Staff are not permitted to enter the property to remove items for collection as they are not insured to enter dwellings.
Only items that have been specifically pre-arranged for collection will be taken for disposal. If any additional items are required to be disposed of as part of this collection, arrangements must be made with us prior to the driver arriving for collection.

Book a Collection

For further details on other items not listed or to arrange a collection please contact us on 01803 701316.

Garden Waste

Collections that consist of just garden waste can only be arranged for collection on a Wednesday. For more details visit the Garden Waste page.
