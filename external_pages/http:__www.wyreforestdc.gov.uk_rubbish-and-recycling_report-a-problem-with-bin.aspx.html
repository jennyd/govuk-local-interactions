Report a Problem With Your Bin

Missed bins

Our crews start their rounds at 6am, please make sure your bin is out by then.

Rounds may change at anytime to take into account roadworks, vehicle maintenance or illness so your bin can be emptied anytime between 6am and 4.30pm.

If your bin hasn't been emptied after 4.30pm please check if your bin has had a yellow or red contamination tag put on it.
If it has, please remove the contamination and present your bin again on your next collection day. Crews will not return to empty bins that were not emptied because of contamination. 
If there isn't a tag, your bin was out by 6.00 am and there was no obstruction or access problem for the collection vehicle, please report your missed bin.

Damaged bins

If your bin is accidentally damaged we will either replace or repair the bin.  

If the lid has come off because one or both of the hinge pins have broken you can pick up a replacement hinge pin from our customer service centre (the Worcestershire Hub) at the Town Hall, Kidderminster, these just push into place. For any other damage please tell us what the problem is via our online form and we will arrange for someone to come out to you. Please make sure the bin is somewhere accessible for us but not somewhere that would cause and obstruction to road users or neighbours. You can continue to use your bin while you wait for us to come out. We aim to be with you within 10 working days.

Missing/Stolen bins

If your bin has gone missing and you can't find it anywhere in the immediate area please let us know using our online form and we will arrange for a replacement to be delivered within 10 working days.

If the missing bin was a nappy bin and your child is now 3 years of age your bin may have been removed by us as your entitlement has run out. If you have another baby in nappies please send us an up to date application form and copy of the youngest's birth certificate to renew your entitlement to the bin.

Please note that we reserve the right to make a charge for the delivery of a replacement bin. You can collect a replacement bin from our Green Street depot, please contact us to make sure a bin is available for you to collect.

Other problems

If your bin is too large for you you can request a smaller bin below. If you are regularly producing more rubbish or recycling for your bin view our page 'Too much for the bin' for the options available. 

Report a Problem With Your Rubbish or Recycling Bin

Please give us as much information as possible. If you provide us with contact details we will update you with the action taken.
