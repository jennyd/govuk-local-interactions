If you require a temporary road closure for a Military or Remembrance Parade event please complete the application form.

All other requests are dealt with by North Yorkshire County Council.

Types of road closure

You can apply for a full road closure, rolling closure or a footpath closure. A full closure is where the physical closure of the road is necessary for a substantial period of time which requires the signing of a diversion route. A rolling closure involves a moving procession to pass during the time the closure is in force, but is only necessary to interrupt the flow of traffic for a short period of time. A footpath closure is only necessary if your event is obstructing a footpath.

Guidance on completing application form:

Please note that we require a minimum of 10 weeks notice prior to the event.

  • You must attach a plan showing the proposed closure coloured red and an alternative route coloured green (if necessary).
  • You must attach confirmation from North Yorkshire Police stating they are responsible for providing the traffic management for the event.

North Yorkshire Police

For events such as Remembrance Events and Military Parades North Yorkshire Police will normally provide officers to control and direct traffic.

Other aspects to consider

If you intend to serve alcohol, play music or collect money at your event, you may need a temporary licence.  If you believe you may need a licence, please contact the Council Licensing Team for further information, depending on where the event is to be held.
