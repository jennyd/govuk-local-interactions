Council garages

  • Garage lettings
  • Garage arrears

Looking for somewhere to park your car or motorbike? We have a selection of garages available for rent throughout the borough.

How much does it cost?

The amount you will pay will depend on where the garage is located. Average rent is £14 per week.

How do I apply?

To rent a garage, please complete a garage application form.

You do not have to be a council tenant to rent a garage, but council tenants will be given priority.

You may rent a garage in a housing area other than your own, but tenants from the area where the garage is situated may have priority when there is a waiting list.

Please note: Where permission is granted you will be allowed to use the garage for the storage of non inflammable chemicals or equipment such as furniture and personal items. However we will not accept responsibility for theft, loss or damage of any property stored in the garage.
