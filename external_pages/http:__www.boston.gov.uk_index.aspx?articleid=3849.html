Bulky waste collection

We operate a pre-paid collection and disposal service for large household items which householders may find difficult to transport to the household waste recycling centre.

Standard items

1 to 2 items - £16      3 items - £21        4 to 5 items - £28

White items

Standard white
1 large white item - £25

2 large white item - £35

Any other items - carpets, sheds, wood, garden waste etc will require a quote so please call 01205 311112.

We CANNOT collect building materials, asbestos, hazardous waste, medical waste.

We do work on an area system, so to give you an idea of what likely day your items will be collected please see below:

Tuesday - Town (chain bridge to Burton roundabout to Whitehouse Lane and back)

Wednesday - South side (Wyberton, Frampton, Kirton, Sutterton, Swineshead, Wigtoft, Fosdyke and Bicker)

Thursday - North side (all roads north of Rochford Tower Lane/Church Green Road, Fishtoft, Butterwick, Freiston, Bennington, Leverton, Old Leake and Wrangle

Friday - Miscellaneous day for anything not covered in the above areas

Payment for this service can be made at main reception, Municipal Buildings, West Street. Payment should be made by cheque, postal order (made payable to Boston Borough Council) or over the phone payment. 

The items for collection should be placed at the front of the property the night before or by 6 AM on the day of collection. If this is not possible please contact us for more information on 01205 314200.

You may want to use a private company to dispose of your waste, but we do advise you make sure they are registered waste carriers with a current waste carriers licence.

Furniture reuse

If you do have furniture that is still in good condition, before calling us for a bulky collection why not call Centrepoint Outreach who is a local Christian charitable organisation. They will collect, store and re-distribute furniture and household items to those in need within approximately a 10 mile radius of Boston. Your furniture is then put to further use and we can avoid landfilling them.

To find out more call 01205 360900.
