Plot size

The average size of an allotment plot is 250 square metres (300 square yards).

Plot occupancy

Please see the document to the right of this page which shows occupation levels at all sites.  The symbols indicate if the occupancy has increased, decreased or remained the same from the previous month. The Beacon Hill figures are not available as this site was handed over to the association on January 1st 2012 as a devolved managed site.

Plot charges

The basic rate for a standard plot for the 2016 calendar year is £86.10. Tenants are entitled to a plot at the reduced rate of £48.51 if they are over 60 years of age, have been unemployed for more than 12 months, are registered as disabled or in receipt of Invalidity Benefit, or are a full time student.

If you have been unemployed for at least 12 months please complete and print the Allotment Plot Holder's Confirmation of Employment Status form to the right of this page and take it to the Jobcentre Plus office for signing.

In addition to the plot charge tenants are also required to pay for water.  Water is metered at each site and the charge distributed between all tenanted plots on that site. The charge is usually less than £20 per year.

If you have lost your allotment invoice please contact Allotment Enquiries for a copy.

Applying for a plot

You can view a list of available plots by allotment site by clicking here.

You can apply for a council allotment plot by:

  • completing the Allotment Plot Application form to the right of this page
  • writing to Allotment Enquiries, Municipal Offices, Town Hall Square, Grimsby, DN31 1HU
  • telephoning Allotment Enquiries

We will send you information about your chosen allotment site or the one nearest to you. You can then apply for a vacant plot. If all the plots are currently occupied you can add your name to the waiting list for that site and we will contact you when one becomes available, or choose another site.

Information about allotment sites not managed by North East Lincolnshire Council can be found under Other allotment sites.
