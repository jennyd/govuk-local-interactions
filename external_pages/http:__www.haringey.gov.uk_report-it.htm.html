A - Z of problems

ProblemDescription

Abandoned vehicles

Get a dumped or untaxed vehicle removed. We can only remove untaxed vehicles.

  • Report an abandoned vehicle

Abuse

If you are worried about abuse, contact us and talk about your concerns. Please don't keep your worries to yourself.

  • Report child abuse or adult abuse

Bonfire

Tell us if you're experiencing smoke nuisance from a bonfire.

  • Report a bonfire

Change of address

Let us know if your home or business address has changed, or is about to change.

  • Tell us about a change of address

Dangerous structures

Report a building, wall or any structure that appears dangerous.

  • Report a dangerous structure

Drains and flooding

Report a drain or gully that may be blocked, or flooding.

  • Report a blocked drain

Fly posting

Report posters and notices illegally posted on walls.

  • Report fly posting

Fraud

To report fraud of any kind please visit our Anti-Fraud and Corruption page.

Graffiti

Report graffiti in Haringey. Graffiti that is racist or obscene will be dealt with as a priority.

  • Report graffiti

Hazardous Waste

Report condoms, needles, syringes and other drug litter.

  • Report hazardous litter

Housing repairs

Homes for Haringey residents can report housing repairs with the Graphical Repairs Ordering system.

  • Report housing repairs

Manhole cover

Report missing, broken or loose manhole covers.

  • Report a manhole cover

Missed bin collection

Report a missed collection - you can report a missed collection up to 4 working days after the scheduled collection day. Reports received after this time will be collected on the next scheduled collection day.

  • Report a missed refuse collection or missed recycling collection

Noise

Report an ongoing problem with noise which doesn’t require an immediate response.

  • Report noise nuisance

Parking and driving offences

Report non-urgent issues such as unlawful use of bus lanes, banned turns and parking offences:

  • Report parking and driving offences

To pay or challenge a parking fine:

  • Pay your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) online
  • Challenge your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) online

Parks

Report a problem in park, such as litter or faulty playground equipment.

  • Report a problem in a park

Pavements

Report problems with pavements, such as loose paving stones etc.

  • Report a problem with pavements

Pests

Get help with pests including rats, fleas, wasps and squirrels from the pest control team.

  • Report pests

Planning issues

Report planning rules being broken, this may include satellites, double glazing and other conservations issues.

  • Report a planning problem

Refuse and recycling

Report a missed collection or request a new bin.

  • Report a refuse problem or recycling problem

Roads / potholes

Including obstructions, spillages and potholes.

  • Report a problem with roads / potholes

Road signs

Report problems with traffic and direction signs.

  • Report road signs

Rubbish - dumped

Report dumped rubbish / flytipping.

  • Report dumped rubbish

Trees, shrubs and hedges

Report a problem with trees on the street, overgrown or overhanging shrubs and hedges.

  • Report trees, shrubs and hedges

Skips

  • Report a problem with a skip

Street cleaning

Report a problem with spillages or litter on the street.

  • Report a problem with street cleaning

Street lights

Report a problem with street lights, lit signs or bollards.

  • Report a problem with street lights

Street names

Report a problem with street name signs.

  • Report a problem with street names

Street trading

Report illegal street trading, including handing out leaflets, and setting up stalls and markets.

  • Report illegal street trading

Website

  • Report a problem with the website
 

Other problems

For anything not listed here please contact customer services on 020 8489 1000 or use the online contact form here.

You can use our feedback form if you would like to make a formal complaint/give feedback about a service. If you prefer, your feedback can be left anonymously.
