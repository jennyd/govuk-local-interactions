Dog control

Lost or found a dog in Fareham?

Telephone:

Office hours - 01329 236100

*Out of hours - 0800 374 485 after 17:15 - 22:00 weekdays, 09:00 - 22:00 weekends and bank holidays

*This is for the collection of found dogs in your care and reporting of lost dogs only*

Our dog control service deals with a variety of dog related problems and information, these include stray dogs, noisy dogs, dog fouling enforcement, dangerous dogs, dogs not on leads, dog bans and responsibility for dog ownership.

Further information is available on the following:

  • Collection of strays
  • Dangerous dogs
  • Dogs on the beach
  • Dogs on leads
  • Enforcement of dog byelaws
  • Fouling
  • Keeping your pet healthy
  • Lost dogs
  • Neighbour noise problems
  • Pets or pests (toxocara canis)
  • Responsible dog ownership
  • Stray (seized) dog register

You can see any of this information in a leaflet. If you would like one or if you would like to report a lost or stray dog or make a complaint please contact us by email at regulatory@fareham.gov.uk or telephone us on 01329 236100.

You can see more information about dog related issues on these websites:

  • RSPCA  (Royal Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals)
  • Dogs Trust 
  • Cinnamon Trust  - for adults over 60 who need assistance with their pets
  • Pro Dogs Direct  - a registered charity rescuing and rehoming dogs
  • Hampshire Dog Wardens
