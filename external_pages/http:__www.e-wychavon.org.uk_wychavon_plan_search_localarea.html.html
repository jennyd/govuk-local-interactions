My Local Area

This part of our website allows you to search on properties within the Wychavon district.

Address Search

Please enter either your House Name/Number and Road OR Postcode. You do not need to enter details in every box.

 

 

About My Local Area

For each property chosen you will find details about whether it is a Listed Building or within a Conservation Area or whether any planning applications have been submitted. You will also find information about refuse collections and what Council Tax band this property is in.

Local area information is also available such as District and County Councillors and our Find My Nearest section where you will be able to view map-based information on services such as:

  • Domestic Refuse/Recycling and Garden Waste
  • Council Tax and Business Rates
  • Thermal Imagery
  • Cooling Towers
  • Hospitals
  • Planning Applications
  • Recycling Points
  • Telecommunication Masts for 3, O2, Orange, T-Mobile and Vodaphone
  • Tourist Information Centres; and
  • Wychavon DC and Housing Association Offices

To access all of this information please enter details within the form above.
