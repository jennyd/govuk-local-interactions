Holding A Safe and Successful Event

The planning and organisation of events, whatever their size, requires careful thought.

For information on holding a safe and successful event please refer to the planning pack, prepared by the East Herts Safety Advisory Group (SAG).

If you are planning on holding a public event the SAG is available to offer guidance. You can do this by letting us know about your event by completing and returning the event notification and road closure form.

Road closure requests

There are different ways to apply for a road closure depending on the sort of event you are holding. Please see the list below for what to do.

1.  If you are organising a street party, please view the following advice and contact Hertfordshire County Council ( Guide to Safe and Successful Events [1Mb]).

2.  If you are organising a public, charitable or community event which requires a road closure please complete the Event Notification and Road Closure request form. East Herts Council will then consult with Hertfordshire Highways and Herts Police about your road closure request ( Event Notification Form and Road Closure Requests [137kb])

3.  If you are holding an event which requires a road closure where there will be structures on the highway please contact Hertfordshire Highways: Hertfordshire County Council, Highways, Environment, First Floor, NW Block, County Hall, Pegs Lane, Hertford, Herts, SG13 8DN NM.East@hertfordshire.gov.uk, 0300 1234047

East Herts Safety Advisory Group (SAG)

The East Herts Safety Advisory Group (SAG) deals with safety at public events.

The SAG in East Herts enables event organisers to seek help and guidance from one central body for unregulated events. Members of the SAG include officers from Herts Police, Fire and Rescue Service, Bedfordshire and Hertfordshire Ambulance and Paramedic Service, East Herts Council (Environmental Health, Licensing and Community Safety) and Hertfordshire County Council Services (Highways).

SAG aims to provide you, the event organiser with a single point of contact for safety advice for your event. This allows you to have access to a range of expert advice in the interests of community safety, and to let all the emergency services know about your event in one go. To do this, please complete the event notification form and return it via the contacts shown. This form will then be circulated to the SAG for comment and consideration. You may then be contacted by a member of SAG with suggestions on how to improve your event in the interests of public safety. If you do not hear from the SAG you can assume partners are confident you will be holding a safe and successful event. The form also includes a section on road closures. Please note, the Safety Advisory Group does not accept any liability for events.

To complete the event notification and road closure form please download the word document to your computer, complete and email to the address on the form, or complete via hand and post using the address shown.

Hertfordshire Fire and Rescue Service - Guidance for Organised Events

Hertfordshire Fire and Rescue Service has produced a guidance document for people proposing to hold events. The guidance was constructed through working with the Chief Fire Officers Association Event Safety Group and experience gained within Hertfordshire when dealing with organised events, many of which were licenced.

The information suggests consideration for timescales of notification of an event to the fire authority, what information should be provided within an acceptable event management plan and possible conditions for a premises licence to account for fire and public safety at organised events which may include festivals, concerts and other gatherings.
