Welcome to BFC My choice

Welcome to BFC My Choice – your route to a new affordable home in Bracknell Forest.

 

BFC My Choice gives you the opportunity to:

 

  • Register for housing in Bracknell Forest if you are resident in the borough and have lived here continuously for 12 months
  • Find out about housing association properties that are available for letting
  • Explore low cost home ownership options which are available for you to purchase a home
  • Express an interest in a housing association rented or shared ownership property that you would like to be considered for by making a bid.
