Parks and Playgrounds Finder

There are parks, playgrounds and open spaces all over the District, some provided by us and some by the local parish or town council.

 

All our playgrounds are free to use and most have 'Safer Surfacing' around equipment to help cushion the impact of any slips or falls.

 

Take a look through our directory below to find a park near you.

 

The playgrounds we maintain are visited every month to check that they are safe and in good condition. We will respond urgently to reports of broken equipment, vandalism or broken glass etc. Contact our Clean and Green line to let us know if there's a problem with one of our play areas (details below).

 

If you would like to know more about our maintenance and care of parks, playgrounds and open spaces contact our Grounds Maintenance team direct.
