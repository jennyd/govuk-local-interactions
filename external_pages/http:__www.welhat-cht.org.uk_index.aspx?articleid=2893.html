Reporting a repair for your council home

We are responsible for most repairs in tenants' homes.

Most repairs are carried out on our behalf by our partner, the Welwyn Hatfield Housing Maintenance Team. Gas and hot water repairs are carried out by TSG PLC. 

How can I report a repair?

Online

Telephone

0800 111 4484

Email

Click here to email the Housing Maintenance Team

In person

Visit our Welwyn Garden City or Hatfield Housing Office and use the direct free phone

Please report all emergency repairs that occur outside office hours by telephone on 0800 111 4484.

What happens next?

When you report a repair we will send you a Repair Notice. That will give you details of which contractor will be carrying out the repair and a target date for it to be completed. The target date is not an appointment date.  

When will the repair get done?

Target times for repairs vary, depending on how urgent they are. In emergency situations, where there is a real risk of injury or major damage to property, or where a property is not secure, we will be there on the same day, as soon as possible. Other repairs will take longer, and for some we will need to inspect first. We will tell you the priority of each repair when you report it.  

Repairs we cannot do

We are not responsible for repairing any of your fixtures or fittings, or alterations made by you. See our page on tenant repairs and improvements for more information.

We will not replace items that are in poor condition because of a lack of care or regular cleaning.  We do not repair decorations or items that have been damaged or lost as a result of a repair, for example a water leak.  We advise you to take out home contents insurance to cover these things. 

Our Service Standards

We have agreed service standards with our customers for carrying out repairs.  These will be reviewed each year.
