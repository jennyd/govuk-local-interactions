Home contents insurance scheme

How would you repair or replace your personal belongings and furniture if they were stolen or damaged by fire, flood or vandalism? If you do not have a contents insurance policy to claim on, it would be out of your own pocket and could cost you hundreds of pounds. However, Wigan Council/Wigan and Leigh Homes can help. They run an affordable scheme* exclusive to their tenants.

It has great benefits such as:

  • You pay no portion of any claims paid out. 
  • You pay the same weekly rate for your chosen cover no matter where you live.
  • Cover for lost/stolen keys.
  • Cover for fridge/freezer contents if the unit accidentally fails.
  • You can pay by monthly direct debit, which is easiest way to pay and at no extra cost.

 *Applications subject to acceptance criteria. Policy terms, conditions, exclusions and limits apply.  

How to apply

Complete the home contents insurance scheme application form and send it to Wigan & Leigh Homes, Progress House, Westwood Park Drive, Wigan, WN3 4HH. The weekly rates are listed in the application form.

Already covered?

You need to ensure the amount of sums insured you have on your policy is enough to cover the value of all your personal belongings, furniture and decorating.

If you want to amend your sum insured amount or add on the full accidental damage cover if you have not got this, then you will need to complete an amendment of cover application.

Want Cover on Things Outside the Home?

We currently offer 4 ‘All-Risks’ Covers** for certain things outside your home when your out and about:

  • Personal Belongings Cover (excluding Pedal Cycles)
  • Pedal Cycles
  • Wheelchairs and Disability Scooters
  • Hearings Aids

For further information on these, please telephone (01942) 486669 or e-mail insurance@walh.co.uk

**Can only be taken if you are already on our Tenants’ Home Contents Insurance scheme.  Policy terms, conditions, exclusions and limits apply to each individual type of cover.
