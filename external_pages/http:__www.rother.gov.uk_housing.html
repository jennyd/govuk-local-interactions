Housing

Welcome to the Housing section of Rother District Councils Website

Rother District Council's Housing service gives help and advice to those in housing need, assistance to the homeless and help to those in Private Sector Housing and also maintains a Housing Register.

 

Housing Register and Homemove This page gives details on how to join the Council's Housing Register, what Homemove is and how choice based lettings works in Rother Find out more about Housing Register and Homemove
Help to Buy - Affordable Home Ownership in Rother Residents in the Rother District may be eligible to buy affordable homes with the Government backed Help to Buy scheme. Find out more about Help to Buy - Affordable Home Ownership in Rother
Contact housing Contact details for Housing Find out more about Contact housing
Do you need help to live independently? Supporting People is a government programme that pays for housing-related support services that help you to find, or stay in, your own home. Find out more about Do you need help to live independently?
Empty Residential Properties The Council is able to assist people affected by empty homes and the owners of the property themselves. Find out more about Empty Residential Properties
Home Energy Conservation Find out about saving money on your fuel bills and making your home more energy efficient. Find out more about Home Energy Conservation
Homelessness How the Council may be able to assist you if you are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless. Find out more about Homelessness
Private Sector Housing Find out more about Private Sector Housing
Housing Consultations The Council is reviewing its Housing and Homelessness Strategies. These documents set out how we will deliver our housing and homelessness services in the future. Find out more about Housing Consultations
