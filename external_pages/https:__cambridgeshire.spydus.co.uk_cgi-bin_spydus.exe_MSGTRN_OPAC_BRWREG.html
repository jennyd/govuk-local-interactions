Main Details

Only use this form if you have a contact email address.

More information on joining libraries.

Anyone who is living, working or studying in Cambridgeshire or neighbouring counties, or visiting is welcome to join our libraries.

This applies even if you just want to access our online resources.

Membership is free.

How to join online

  • Complete this online form.
  • Your library card will be posted to you.
  • If you are 15 or under you will need a guarantor.
