Bulky Waste

If you have a large item such as a piece of furniture or a fridge you wish to dispose of, we offer a chargeable Bulky Household Waste Collection Service.

If the item is in working order and/or in good condition please consider donating to charity or to one of the reuse projects operating in the county. Alternatively, you could advertise good quality items in the local paper, shops, supermarkets or on Freecycle. Make yourself some cash and sell your items at a local car boot sale or donate to friends and family.

Please help us reduce the amount of waste being sent to landfill and protect our local environment.

Bulky Household Waste Collection Service

If you would like us to collect your bulky waste then our collection service could be just what you need. For a small fee, you can get rid of your items quickly and easily - typically within a few days of making an appointment.

From 1 April 2015 the cost is £16.00 for up to two items and £8.00per extra item when booked at the same time. Payment can be made by credit/debit card. However, please note, there will be an additional 1.4% charge for any payment made by credit card. There will not be an additional charge for any payment made by debit card.

The service excludes any items that are part of the fabric of the property such as heating systems, kitchen units, bathroom suites, carpets, etc. and excludes house clearances.
The items will need to be placed in a position where they are close to the boundary and have unhindered level access for collection (we are unable to collect from inside the premises).

How to Book a Collection

For more information, or to book a collection please use the Bulky Household Waste Collection Request form which is available from the forms box on the right of this page or telephone 01799 510510.

Please note that bulky electrical items are disposed of in line with the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Directive (WEEE). Bulky non-electrical items are collected and disposed of at landfill.
