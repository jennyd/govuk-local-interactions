How can I get Council support?

Last updated 3 January 2014

On this page:

  • See also

We need to start by assessing your needs.

Please contact us so we can get this started by emailing us at wfdirect@walthamforest.gov.uk or telephoning 020 8496 3000.

Our social care staff will talk to you in detail about how you are managing and whether you are at risk living at home. If you are living at a 'critical' or substantial level of risk you will be eligible for our support.

For more information download the Council's factsheet on getting help to live at home (138KB PDF file).

We can arrange temporary support while the assessment is being done if you have a serious or immediate need.

If you just need some support to get your confidence back with managing every day things, our in-house Reablement Service may be just what you need.

For more information download the Council's factsheet on reablement (140KB PDF file)

If caring and looking after someone has a major impact on your life, you are entitled to have your needs as a carer assessed to see if you could get extra support.

For more information download the Council's factsheet on being a carer (134KB PDF file).
