Disabled facilities assistance

Last updated: 19/11/2014

If you have a disability that makes it difficult to manage in your home, there are several options which may help you find a housing solution.  Help is available.

You can discuss your situation with the Access Team in Adult Care and Support.  They are likely to refer you to the Adaptations and Advice Service who can:

  • arrange to assess your need for an adaptation
  • arrange for a home visit to discuss your housing options
  • make an initial assessment to inform your eligibility for financial help

You will be consulted on options that are discussed to help find a housing solution.  The Adaptations and Advice Service can help you explore relocation options and help with associated costs.

The Council can make grant payments for adaptations on your current property if appropriate (Disabled Facilities Grants). 

Works that can be funded by a Disabled Facilities Grant (DFG)

  • Ramps, rails and step lifts to get in and out of your property
  • Door widening to allow for wheelchair access
  • Level access showers and other bathroom alterations
  • Improvement of an existing heating system in the dwelling to meet the disabled occupant’s needs or to enable a disabled person to have full use of heating controls in the dwelling
  • Stairlifts and through floor lifts
  • And more

Cornwall Council must be satisfied that all works are necessary and appropriate for you and will only approve grant works that are reasonable and practicable. Funding is limited and we have a responsibility to be careful with the limited pot of money available so that as many people as possible can take some advantage from this grant. 

Advice

To be referred to someone who can give you advice about adapting your home, contact the Access Team on 0300 1234 131.

Further information can also be found on our home solutions web page.

If the adaptation is required for a child please contact a Paediatric Occupational Therapist on 0300 1234 101.

Further information can also be found on our Adaptations, Aids and Equipment pages.
