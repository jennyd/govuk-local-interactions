Book a clinical waste collection

Use this form to book a clinical waste collection from the council.

This is a free service but please note that a minimum of 3 boxes will be collected and bags must be tied.

This form consists of 4 pages and will take approximately 4 minutes to complete. Fields marked with a * are mandatory and require completion.

 

Before you start
