Garages

Interested in renting a garage? We manage thousands of garages throughout Wolverhampton.

Interested in renting a garage?

Anyone can apply for a garage. We give priority to disabled badge holders and council tenants who are not in arrears.

We offer a really competitive weekly rent from as little as:

• £4.54 for tenants and leaseholders within reasonable proximity (By reasonable proximity, we mean garages on your estate and within easy walking distance.)
• £5.45 for tenants and leaseholders not within reasonable proximity
• £5.45 for the third or more garages rented by tenants and leaseholders
• £7.81 for private owners and non-council tenants

(prices correct at April 2015)

Rent for garages has to be paid by weekly Direct Debit.

We understand that parking is a real issue on some estates – for this reason we rent out garages so that residents can park their vehicles with peace of mind without the hassle of having to search for a parking space. Garages must not be used for storage or for commercial purposes. If you use your garage for any other reason than to park your vehicle, you run the risk of losing your garage.

How to apply?

To apply for a garage please ask for or download our Garage Application Form and return to your local One Stop Shop.

Garage Application Form.pdf

Once we’ve received your application, we’ll contact you to let you know whether you’ve been successful.

You’ll then have five days to view and accept the garage. If you want to go ahead and rent the garage, we’ll arrange a time to meet with you to sign the agreement
