Concessionary travel

Bus passes or travel vouchers with an expiry date of March 2016 will be AUTOMATICALLY renewed before the expiry date. If you have moved since the issue of your current bus pass please call 0300 555 1376 to update your details.

Changes to Older Personâs bus passes from 1 April 2015

From 1 April 2015, Hampshire older personâs bus passes (those with a blue stripe) will be valid for free travel from 9.30am to 11pm on Monday to Friday and all day at weekends and public holidays.

The current weekday extension from 9am will no longer apply. A consultation was carried out last year to examine what was most important to Hampshire residents as the County Council has to save up to Â£1.5million from the public and community transport budget this year as a result of Government cutting its general grant to Hampshire by almost half.

The enhancement to the national concessionary fares scheme that Hampshire used to provide now falls into line with the national scheme. The exception to this will be the few rural routes where the next bus is after 10:30am, so these bus pass holders will still be able to use their free travel from 9:00am. There will be no change to the existing Hampshire scheme in respect of passes for disabled people (those with an orange stripe).

Printing Pages - PIPer (Printed Information Personalised)

A new online facility for printing information from our website. Users will be able to select web pages and add them to a printing basket. PIPer will then package up the information and the user will have the ability to save, email, self-print or have the information printed for them.
