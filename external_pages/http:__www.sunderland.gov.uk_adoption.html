Adoption

Adoption is the legal process by which a child under 18 years gains new parents. Children in Sunderland need adopters who are willing to make a commitment to them for life, and who are: patient, flexible, realistic and with lots of love to give.

  • Your introduction to adoption

    Sunderland City Council's Adoption Service helps local children who need new families to find the care they need for the rest of their childhood and into adult life.

  • Interested in adoption?

    If you are interested in adoption, we strongly recommend that you read the information pack, which can be downloaded from this page.

  • After adoption

    Adoption is not something that happens once and is then finished with. Its is a life-long continuing event for all those who are affected by it.

  • What we do

    More information about what we do, what children in Sunderland need from you and how we can help you if you want to become an adopter.

  • Sunderland's Adoption Passport

    We recognise that children who have been looked after and adopted can have ongoing needs. You and your child may benefit from additional support at some time in the future.

  • Adoption - Support for birth families

    If adoption is being planned for your child we can support you.
