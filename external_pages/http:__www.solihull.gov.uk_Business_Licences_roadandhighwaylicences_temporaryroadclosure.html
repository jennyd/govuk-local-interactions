Temporary road closure

Application for a temporary road closure.

Apply

To apply please download the following forms:

  • temporary road closure application form
  • temporary road closure guidance notes

At least 3 months notice of a closure is required.

Fee

  • £1565 (no VAT payable)

Closing the highway due to an event or street party

You can also apply for:

  • event on the public highway application form
    • event on the public highway guidance notes
  • street party application form
