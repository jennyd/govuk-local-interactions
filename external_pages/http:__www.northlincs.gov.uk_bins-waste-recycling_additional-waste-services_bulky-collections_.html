Bulky collection service

Bulky item collection service

We offer a collection service of up to three bulky household items that residents are unable to take to one of the Household Recycling Centres.

Before booking a bulky collection, consider whether the item(s) are suitable for reuse. 

There are several groups which can collect/accept items for reuse in North Lincolnshire.  For more information on these schemes go to our furniture reuse page.

Residents can have one collection of up to three items free of charge per financial year (1 April to 31 March).  Any further requests for collections in the same financial year will be charged for, please see our fees and charges page. 

Request a Bulky Item Collection

Items to be collected need to be placed outside in an accessible location on the next working day following the request, and will be collected within 14 days. 

Items we can and can't collect are listed below.

We can collect:

  • Three piece suite (three items)
  • Bed (three items - base, headboard, mattress)
  • Bicycles
  • Carpets (rolled and tied)
  • Cookers (inc. microwaves)
  • Dishwashers
  • Doors
  • Fridges and freezers (clean inside and empty)
  • Garden gates (standard)
  • Headboards
  • Hi-fi equipment
  • Ironing boards
  • Lawnmowers
  • Mattresses
  • TVs
  • Wardrobes
  • Washing machines and tumble dryers
  • Wheelbarrows  
  • and many more items...

We can't collect:

  • Asbestos
  • Bathroom suites
  • Boilers
  • Car parts
  • Cardboard
  • Construction/demolition waste
  • Excess household waste
  • Drums containing liquids, or empty drums containing the residue of hazardous substances
  • Garage doors
  • Garden waste
  • Glass, including windows
  • Kitchen units (cupboards, sinks)
  • LPG cylinders
  • Motorcycles
  • Oil storage tanks
  • Paint
  • Radiators
  • Wood, including sheds and fences

If you are unsure if your item(s) qualify please contact the Customer Contact Centre.
