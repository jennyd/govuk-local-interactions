Councillors, MP and MEPs

Find out more about your local councillor, MP or MEP

Spelthorne Council has 39 councillors:

  • Conservatives hold 34 seats and is the majority party in the Council
  • Liberal Democrats hold three seats
  • Labour holds one seat
  • An Independent holds one seat

Spelthorne is divided into 13 wards (local areas) represented by three councillors per ward. Only registered voters of Spelthorne or those living or working within the Borough are eligible to hold the office of Borough Councillor.
