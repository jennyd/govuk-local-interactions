Introducing The Homebid Employment Connections Service

HECS is now available for Homebid users. This new service brings together information on finding a training course, help with writing a CV, improving your skills for employment, as well as a job search tool. You can also use it to find information about managing money, in-work benefits and other benefits, childcare information and access to local health services.

To use this service you will need to have your Homebid User ID and Pin number handy.

WELCOME TO SOUTHAMPTON HOMEBID - EXPLORE YOUR HOUSING OPTIONS AND FIND A NEW HOME

Homebid is the allocations scheme for Council and Housing Association accommodation in Southampton, giving you choice about where you want to live.

You can use Homebid to look for properties and place bids. You need to be registered on Southampton’s Housing Register before you can bid.

The demand for general needs Council and Housing Association accommodation in Southampton far exceeds the number of homes available. We are not able to help everyone that applies. Therefore, you should consider alternative housing options in addition to Homebid, for example renting privately, living with family, renting a room, shared ownership, mutual exchange. The needs of those requiring supported accommodation for older people (60+) is more easily met.

If you are not registered, you will need to complete a Housing Register application form, available here or visit Gateway at One Guildhall Square.

The scheme covers all the available Council and Housing Association homes in Southampton, including accommodation for older people. The scheme is for existing Council and Housing Association tenants, as well as those applying for the first time.
