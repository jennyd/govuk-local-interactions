Home > Waste & Recycling > Household Waste & Recycling

We aim, with your help, to make west Norfolk a clean place to live, work and visit.  We collect rubbish, recycling, food waste and garden waste from residential properties.

 

The links below are designed to help residents find out everything they need to know about our household collections.

 

We also provide services for businesses, helping them to meet their legal requirements and manage their waste and recycling.  For more information see the Business Waste section of our website.

 

If you are unable to find the information you need, please contact us so we can try to help. Call 01553 616200 or email waste.management@west-norfolk.gov.uk.
