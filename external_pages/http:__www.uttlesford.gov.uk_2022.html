Parking in Uttlesford

Since 1 April 2011 parking in Uttlesford has been run by the North Essex Parking Partnership. The Parking Partnership is lead by Colchester Council which co-ordinates the on-street and off-street Civil Enforcement Officers ("traffic wardens"); the parking enforcement process together with challenges to, and payments of, parking penalties ("parking fines"). It also oversees the administration of parking restrictions ("yellow lines") and the management of the scheme.

Parking enforcement by the North Essex Parking Partnership

Enforcement of parking and waiting restrictions, both on street and in Council-owned car parks, is undertaken by the North Essex Parking Partnership (NEPP). The NEPP carries out enforcement across the Partnership area, helping make streets safer for all, traffic flow more freely and reduce congestion. A Penalty Charge Notice is issued to a vehicle owner where an infringement has been seen to take place.

Enforcement is carried out by Civil Enforcement Officers (CEO) and, since early 2014, the NEPP's Park Safe camera car.

The Park Safe vehicle focuses on enforcing 'no stopping' restrictions, including school pedestrian crossings and zig-zag markings, major through-routes with fast moving traffic, clearways, bus stops, taxis ranks and loading restrictions.

For more information on parking enforcement, the Park Safe car where the vehicle is patrolling now and in the coming weeks click here to visit NEPP website.
