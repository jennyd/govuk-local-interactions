Regaining your independence

Most of us would prefer to remain living in our own homes for as long as possible. We want to make sure you get all the support you need to live independently and stay in your own home. 

Are you living with a long term health condition or physical disability, a learning disability, Autism Spectrum Condition (ASC), sensory loss, dementia or memory loss or mental health issues? Or are you finding you need more care and support as a result of getting older?

There are plenty of ways you can be supported to live at home for as long as you would like to.

From care in your own home, to specialist adaptations to your home or help so you can regain your independence , there is a lot of support out there. We want to help you find whatever care and support suits your needs best. 

The Directory of Services for Older People contains lots of useful information for older people and/or their carers.
