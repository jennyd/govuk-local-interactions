Pests and Infestation

Our service

We offer a wide range of affordable pest control services for residents and businesses within Bath and North East Somerset.

How to make an appointment

To book an appointment, please contact us on the following number between:

8.30am to 5.00pm Monday, Tuesday, Thursday,

9.30pm to 5.00pm Wednesday and 8.30am to 4.30pm Friday.

Tel: 01225 396007 Email: council_connect@bathnes.gov.uk  for general enquiries

Cancelling and Rearranging bookings

Please give one full working days' notice if you need to make a cancellation or a rearrangement as charges may apply. Please read Terms and Conditions for futher information, applicable from 1st April 2015. These apply to both residential customers and business customers (excluding contract customers.)

Please bear in mind our opening times when notifying us so as not to incur a charge.

 

For details of prices, please see  Residents or Businesses

What we offer you

  • An affordable service with concessions for those on certain benefits
  • Speedy response and eradication of pests
  • Excellent standards of customer service
  • Fully qualified and experienced pest control officers
  • Local knowledge and experience
  • Individual treatments and maintenance contracts for businesses
  • Approved trader by Trading Standards 

 

 

Please note, as of 4th January 2016 we are unable to offer a service for the treatment of Bed Bugs. We are still able to offer advice and a free identification service.

Pest Identification Service

We also offer a Free Pest identification Service. If you are unsure of the pest you may have, you may bring a sample to one of the One Stop Shops in Bath, Midsomer Norton or Keynsham. They will arrange for a Pest Control Officer to collect the sample, then once analyzed, they will contact you. You should hear from them within 5 working days.

Samples must be put in an air tight container such as an empty jam jar with your contact details and any further information.

For those who believe that they have Bed Bugs, it is essential that before an appointment is made, the customer brings in a sample and has the pest confirmed.

Answers to Appointment Queries

A1.1 We require one full working days’ notice to rearrange or cancel a booking, without incurring a charge. Please read our Terms and Conditions document for further details on cancellations and charges. Please telephone us on 01225 396007

A2.1 Opening times are Monday, Tuesday, Thursday between 8.30am and 5.00pm, Wednesday between 9.30am and 5.00pm, Fridays between 8.30am and 4.30pm. Please note; we do not work at weekends or public holidays.

A3.1 No, we do not offer a weekend service for appointments or for making/cancelling appointments.

A4.1Yes, a responsible adult must be in attendance when we visit as we use poisons.

A5.1This will depend on where the property is. We cover certain areas on specified days and times of day. We are not able to be flexible with these times.

A6.1 If it is a morning appointment visits will be between 9.30am and 1.00pm, if it is an afternoon appointment, it will be between 1.30pm and 4.00pm.

A7.1Unfortunately, we are unable to specify a time. This is dictated by the officers other jobs that day and the situation when he calls to each visit.

Answers to payment and cost queries 

A1.2 If you are a Resident or Business within the Bath and North East Somerset Area  you may use our service.

A2.2 Please see Treatment charges for either Residential or Businesses for further details.

A3.2 Please see Treatment charges for Residential customers for further details.

A4.2 Please provide a letter to show that you are currently receiving the specified benefit with your current name and address on to show the pest control officer when they arrive.

A5.2 Please see Treatment charges for Residential customers for further details.

A6.2 If you are unable to pay over the phone, then you can make a booking at one of the One Stop Shops in Bath, Riverside or Midsomer Norton. You will then be able to pay by card, cash or cheque. Payments must be received in advance of the appointment.

A7.2 If paying by credit card, the surcharge for the cost of the transaction is 2.5%. There is no surcharge if paying by debit card.

A8.2 If you are a Curo tenant, then you should first talk to Curo on Tel 01225 366323 to see whether they will pay. If they have said they will not pay for the treatment, then you would need to pay for our services. You may receive a discount on the treatment charge, if you are in receipt of one of the qualifying benefits.

Answers to pest identification queries

A1.3 Yes, we offer a Pest Identification Service. You may bring a sample in an air tight container to one of the One Stop Shops. Please provide your contact details. They will arrange for a Pest Control Officer to collect the sample and once analysed they will contact you. This should be about 5 working days.

A2.3 Rats droppings are dark and bullet shaped. Mice droppings tend to be dark and rounder. Squirrels droppings are easy to crumble in your hand. If you are unsure, we offer a free identification service. You may bring a sample into one of the One Stop Shops. The Pest Control Officer will collect the sample and once analysed will contact you about it. This should take about 5 working days. There will be a charge for any subsequent treatments. We DO NOT however, treat for squirrels.

A3.3 Please ensure that any specimens are given to us in an air tight container, such as an empty jam jar. Specimens should also, not be squashed.

A4.3 No, the identification service will be free of charge. However, any subsequent treatments you undertake will be charged.
