Lifeline

Lifeline alarm service

Lifeline offers independence and peace of mind 24 hours a day. The lifeline alarm service is a way of getting help via your telephone, quickly and efficiently. It consists of a special unit which is connected to your ordinary telephone line and a small pendant which can be worn around the neck or on the wrist. Have a look at our information leaflet.

There is a small charge of approximately Â£3.25 (plus VAT if applicable) per week for this service, which is payable monthly in advance.

The cost includes:

  • hire of lifeline unit and pendant
  • installation and maintenance of the unit
  • 24 hour 7 day a week monitoring service from the control centre

Apply online

Use our online form to apply for the service.
