Planning Applications Weekly List

A series of different lists are produced by the Planning team each week containing information on various aspects of current applications.

To track or view application information - the application form, important dates etc, visit our online Planning system PublicAccess.

 

Weekly List

An Applications List (known as the Weekly List), is produced outlining all new applications validated each week by the Planning service. To view the latest list, please click on the 'View 2015 Weekly Lists' link below. The lists will appear in chronological order.

Daily Decisions List

The Daily Decisions Lists provide information on decisions reached regarding applications by day. Conditions for those applications permitted and reasons for those refused are also listed. Weekly Decision List

The Weekly Decision List provides a summary of the decisions reached by week. It does not contain any information on conditions or reasons for refusal but provides an overall summary of decisions for the week. To view the latest list, please click on the 'View 2015 Weekly Decision Lists' link below. The lists will appear in chronological order.
