Lighting - street lights

The Council is responsible for the design, installation and maintenance of streetlights, traffic signals.

How do I report a problem?

To report problems with street lights, traffic lights, pedestrian crossings or illuminated bollards or to complain about the location of, please use the link at the bottom of the page.

If you are reporting problems with temporary traffic lights, please provide relevant contact details where possible.

In an emergency or out of hours situation please contact our 24 hour call centre on 01858 464499.

The street light outside my house is out/is faulty
Please use the online form at the bottom of this page.to let us know - the more information you can give us the better (ie. light out altogether, going on/off, burning all day, intermittent etc.), include the number of the column - if you cannot see this then please state the exact location or adjacent house no.

How long will it take to repair?

Usually, outages are repaired within 5 working days. If there is a problem on the electricity cable feeding the column, notification is sent to the Utility who usually attend within 5 working days of receiving report.

The metal plate (or "door") at the base of the lamp column has been removed, how soon will you attend?
We will attend within 1 working day

The lamp column outside my house is leaning or damaged.
An Inspector will come out to assess the damage and then determine how urgent the job is

Can the lighting in my street be improved?
All changes including improvement requests will be considered by the Authority and prioritised accordingly.

We take into account age and condition of the existing installation along with the light output. However, all considerations are budget led. Please contact us for more information.
