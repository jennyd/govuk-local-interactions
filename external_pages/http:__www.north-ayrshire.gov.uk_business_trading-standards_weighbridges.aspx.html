Public weighbridges

Overweight vehicles can affect a vehicle's brakes, steering, suspension and tyres and could contribute to traffic accidents.

In 2013/14, Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency enforcement staff weighed 8,520 vehicles suspected of being overweight at roadside checks; of these 69% received prohibitions for being overloaded. 

Public weighbridges in North Ayrshire:

  • Robert Wilson & Son, Burnhouse, Beith, KA15 1LF

Please contact the operator directly to check opening times and cost, and if you have a very large vehicle that may not fit on the weighbridge.

To operate as a public weighbridge an operator is assessed by Trading Standards as to their competency under the Weights and Measures Act 1985.
