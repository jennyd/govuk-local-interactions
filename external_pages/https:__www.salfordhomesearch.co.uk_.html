Salford Home Search is an innovative way of letting properties. This scheme gives you more choice when looking for your new home in Salford. It allows you to register, express your interest in properties and make most amendments to your application.

Brought to you by Salford City Council, Salford Home Search aims to give new and current tenants greater choice.

You can currently search for homes available to rent in Salford from the following organisations via Salford Home Search:

Homes are advertised weekly, with new properties being added every Wednesday. You can search online or find out availability from the Salford Home Search shop.

You will need to register to be able to express an interest in a property, to find out more about the process visit our frequently asked questions page.

If you are an advocate or professional acting on behalf of a member of Salford Home Search, for example, wanting to assist someone in expressing interest in a property, please visit our Advice for Professionals page.

Additional properties may be available in Salford and Greater Manchester at www.pinpoint.org.uk. Pinpoint is a partnership between all Greater Manchester authorities and 13 housing associations.
