Popular services in this section


Renew your book now

Renew books and other items now.



Find or reserve a book

Search for a book and there's no charge to reserve it if in stock.



Local libraries and opening times

Find your nearest library and opening times.



Search and read eBooks and eAudiobooks

It's FREE to create an account. New books are added every month.



Events and training

Find the latest library events and access free IT and internet training sessions.



Join the library

It's free and easy to join the library. Anyone can join regardless of where you live.


More services


Family history

Trace your family tree, where to get help, find birth, death and marriage records and more.



Library charges

Find out charges for reservations, overdue books and more.



Reference and resources 

As a library member you can access lots of online information resources that we subscribe to.



Computers in the library

Book a session to use a computer and the internet for free in any of our libraries.



Books on prescription

Find books to help you with mental health problems.



Special collections at the library

Find out about the range of collections in our library catalogue, some of which are only held at certain libraries.



Reading

If you're looking for something different to read and don't know where to start, there are loads of resources available to help you find the right book.



Doorstep Library Service

If you're unable to access one of our libraries across the borough, you can become a member and use our Doorstep Library Service.



Library volunteers

Libraries are looking for volunteers to help with a variety of duties. In return, we can offer you the chance to make a positive difference in their local communities.

ROCHDALE CENTRAL LIBRARY CLOSED

There is currently no library service at Rochdale Central Library, Number One Riverside due to damage caused by the recent  flooding.  Please use one of the other libraries to borrow books, return books or use a computer - locations and opening hours of libraries across the borough. You can continue to renew books online.  

Sorry for any inconvenience.
