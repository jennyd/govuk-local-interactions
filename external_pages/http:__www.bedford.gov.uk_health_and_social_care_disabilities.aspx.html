Disabilities

People with a physical disability, are deaf or have a hearing or visual impairment sometimes need care and support to get the most out of life.

Adult Services in Bedford arranges services so that people in most need can lead a life which is as full and independent as possible. We also help those who care for people with disabilities. We always work closely with other professionals such as health services, housing agencies and independent care home providers.


Changing Place

Bedford Borough Council in partnership with Bedford Mencap has installed a ‘Changing Place’ toilet facility, to make Bedford town centre more accessible for people with a disability and their carers.   This facility is available at River Street multi-storey car park.
