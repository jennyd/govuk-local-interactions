Compliments and complaints

We want to provide you with the best possible service. So it is very important for us to know how we are doing.

Complaints

Because we provide hundreds of services to thousands of people, things can sometimes go wrong. If there is a problem, we need you to tell us so we can investigate and put things right. We want to learn from any mistakes so we can provide an even better service in the future.

Compliments

We would also like you to tell us when you are pleased with what we have done. Compliments highlight good practices which we could introduce to other service areas. Our staff also like to be told that they are doing their job well.

Make a complaint or compliment

Please read the guidance notes in our compliments and complaints booklet below
