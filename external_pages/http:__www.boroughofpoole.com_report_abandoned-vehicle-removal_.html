Abandoned Vehicle Request To Investigate


Vehicles do not have to display a tax disc. It is extremely unlikely a taxed car will be abandoned. You can check if a car is taxed on the DVLA's website.

If you have checked the car's tax, you will need the following information to complete the form before we can investigate:

  • Your contact details
  • Exact location of the vehicle
  • Details of any sticker attached to the vehicle
  • Whether or not the vehicle is burnt out
  • Details of the owner (if known)
  • Vehicle make, model, colour and registration number
  • Length of time it has been abandoned (if known)
  • Whether or not the vehicle has been vandalised
