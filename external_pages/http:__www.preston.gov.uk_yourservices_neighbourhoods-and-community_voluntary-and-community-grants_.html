Community grants and resources

There a variety of funding sources available for voluntary, community and faith sector organisations to access. As well as a range of resources on hand to help with the delivery of projects and services across the local community.

So, whether you're looking for funding advice and support, would like to develop your own neighbourhood action plan or, perhaps, require a welcoming space to promote your services, we are here to help.

Voluntary and community grants funding

We have been in consultation with the Voluntary, Community and Faith Sector (VCFS) and have developed a single and more focused theme based around financial inclusion which will be used to allocate our grant budget to fund VCFS organisations through our existing grant policy and procedures from 2016/17 onwards.

We will offer two types of grants, strategic funding supported by a small grant scheme to ensure smaller groups are not unfairly disadvantaged from the grant process.

Strategic funding will provide an opportunity for organisations to come together to develop consortia or partnership bids and our wish is to encourage this approach.

For further details please refer to the Grant Funding Policy under the documents section on this page along with the application forms for the respective grant types.
