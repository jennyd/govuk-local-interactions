We are responsible for issuing a wide range of licences, permits and registrations related to premises, various occupations and activities.

Licences are issued for many reasons - but usually they are intended to protect the public, maintain appropriate standards of public safety and animal welfare and to prevent nuisance.

We follow strict guidelines before issuing licences, permits or registrations and can impose a range of conditions.

The team regularly inspect licence holders to ensure they are complying with the conditions attached to their licence or registration are being met. We investigate complaints including those concerning premises operating illegally.

Our officers are happy to give advice regarding the full range of licences we issue. Please get in touch with us.
