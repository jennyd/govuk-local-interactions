Ordering additional bins

How do you rate this information / service?

What we do with your feedback

If you have extra recycling you can buy additional bins to help you recycle as much as possible.

The blue and brown bins come in two sizes: a 140 litre and a standard size 240 litre.

How to order

Order your bins online for £15. [ Additional bin ordering form ]

Bins can also be ordered over the phone on 01295 227003 at a cost of £18, or in person at our depot for £15 or at the Link Points.

How long will it take?:

We aim to deliver your bins within 15 working days.

Can't wait, then you can come to our Thorpe Lane Depot to pay for you bin(s) and take them away with you! We even have home compost bins for just £10 for you to take away.

Thorpe Lane Depot, Thorpe Lane, Banbury, OX16 4UT.
Monday to Friday 08.45 to 17.15

Payment methods

  • you can pay by debit/credit card using the online form on the right
  • cheque (or postal order) can be sent with the order form - no cash accepted
  • you can pay by cheque, cash or by debit/credit card at the Thorpe Lane Depot
  • you can pay by debit/credit card or by cash at one of our LinkPoints (listed under contacts to the right)
  • you can pay by debit/credit card over the phone, please call 01295 227003

Please note that there will be a 1.3% surcharge on all credit card payments.
