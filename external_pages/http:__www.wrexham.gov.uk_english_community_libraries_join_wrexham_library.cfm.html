Join Wrexham Libraries

Membership of Wrexham Libraries is free. Please fill in the details below and click send. When you visit your chosen library to collect your library card, please remember to take with you a document showing proof of name and address so that your card can be activated. Acceptable documents include: driving licence, utilities bill, pension/benefits book, letters, bank statement, doctor's prescription. You will be able to collect your library card within 2 working days.
