Have your say

This page brings together some of the current consultations the Council and other organisations are seeking your views on:

  • Identification of non-designated heritage assets - we have produced draft criteria for the identification of non-designated heritage assets and would like your views on them. Non-designated heritage assets can be a building, monument, site, place or area and could include, for example, Victorian cottages, village pumps, bridges, WWII pillboxes, or milestones. Closing date for comments is 17 August 2015.
  • Suffolk Coastal Disability Forum - next meeting takes place on Monday, 6 July 2015, from 1pm to 3pm, in the Council Chamber at Suffolk Coastal's Melton Hill offices.
  • Suffolk Speaks Citizen Panel - take part in surveys and customer focus groups run by public bodies in Suffolk.
  • Submitting petitions to Suffolk Coastal
