Street Cleaning, Litter, Dog Waste Bins, Discarded Syringes

Report that street cleaning is needed. Basildon Council is responsible for the sweeping of streets and removal of Litter from the area.

You can now report Street Cleaning issues online, see Online form - Report street cleaning needed.

Litter

The main roads in the Basildon Borough are swept continually on a three monthly cycle, but if there is a deviation in a specific location it would be actioned.

However, if the visual appearance of an area should deteriorate to an unacceptable level between cleans, it will be cleaned up within a certain time, as laid down in the Litter Code of Practice under the Environmental Protection Action 1990.

If you feel your road is not swept satisfactorily, or you have any other comments or questions, please contact Streetcare

Street Litter and Dog Waste Bins

Litter in a public place is unsightly, dangerous to animals, and an offence. The average fine for littering is around £95, but can be up to £2,500!

Litter ranges in size from a single sweet wrapper to a bag full of rubbish. The Environment Protection Action 1990 states that if a person drops, throws, deposits or leaves anything that causes defacement in a public place, they are committing a littering offence.

There are over 500 litter bins in the Basildon Borough. Each bin is emptied when it is full and can take bagged dog waste and litter.

To report a problem with a litter bin or to request a litter bin at a new location please contact Streetcare

Street Cleaning Programme

Basildon Council is responsible for keeping the Borough clean and free of litter. We will:

  • Clean the Town Centres daily
  • Clean road on a 2-3 weekly cycle
  • Remove accumulation of rubbish from Council land
  • Empty litter bin when they are full
  • Help with any locally organised clean up

You can help us by:

  • Being tidy with your litter and chewing gum
  • Using the free refuse tips
  • Having waste collection contracts for your business
  • Not leaving your dog's mess for others
  • Not parking where you want us to clean

You can now report Street Cleaning issues online, see Online form - Report street cleaning needed .
