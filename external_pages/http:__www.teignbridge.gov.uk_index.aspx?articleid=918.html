Councillors

Teignbridge is divided into 25 different areas which are known as Wards. The Council has 46 Councillors who are elected every four years and they represent these Wards. Some Councillors have political affiliations but will represent you regardless of their political beliefs or yours.

  • Chairman of Teignbridge District Council

    The Chairman of the Council for 2015/16 is Councillor Mike Hocking, Independent Councillor for Newton Abbot Bradley Ward.

    More …

  • Leader of Teignbridge District Council

    The Leader of the Council is Councillor Jeremy Christophers, Conservative Member and Ward Member for Haytor, which includes the Parishes of Bickington and Ilsington.

    More …

  • Devon County Councillors

    There are 62 County Councillors (also known as Members) elected by those eligible to vote in elections which take place every four years. Here you can find information relating to those representing the Teignbridge area.
