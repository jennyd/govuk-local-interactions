Pest Control

We can provide advice and pest control treatments for residential and commercial properties for the following pests only.  

Please click on the relevant link for further information:

  • Fleas
  • Mice
  • Rats
  • Wasps

We DO NOT provide advice or pest control treatments for:

  • Ants
  • Badgers 
  • Bedbugs
  • Beetles & silverfish
  • Bees
  • Cockroaches
  • False Widow Spiders 
  • Foxes also check thefoxwebsite.org
  • Gulls
  • Pigeons
  • Squirrels
  • Woodlice

More details for Pests - not dealt with by Borough of Poole.

If you wish to contact a private pest control company to discuss treatment of these, please go to Buy With Confidence. 

A wide range of information leaflets with advice on wildlife problems, that may occur and how they can be resolved, can be obtained from Natural England.
